Climate

The Líhmabgic Fields has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 4°C (39°F). The Líhmabgic Fields covers an area of nearly 16046 km2 (9972 mi2), and an average elevation of 2595 m (8513 ft) above sea level.
