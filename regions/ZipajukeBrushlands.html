The Zipajuke Brushlands (/ˈzipa/ /ˈʤuke/ Green Oasis [oasis] [green; raw]) are a group of subtropical brushland within the wetland of the Shattered Peninsula region. Zipajuke Brushlands are a relatively typical brushland for Shattered Peninsula, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Zipajuke Brushlands has a yearly average temperature of 26°C (78°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 25°C (77°F). The Zipajuke Brushlands receives an average of 207 cm/y (81 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Zipajuke Brushlands covers an area of nearly 24675 km2 (15335 mi2), and an average elevation of 4138 m (13576 ft) above sea level.

The Zipajuke Brushlands are overseen by Potentate Drumis Pont Damontus, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 37% of the population self-report as devout. The Zipajuke Brushlands is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Zipajuke Brushlands is noteworthy for its economic independence from Shattered Peninsula's export of Metals. Zipajuke Brushlands is particularly renound for its furniture industry. It is also has a noteworthy agriculture industry.
