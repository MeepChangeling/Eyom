The Sjeríhzvāj̼ Heathland (Sjeríhz /vəˑj̼/ Sjeríhz's Jar [Proper Noun (name of person)] [bottle; jar]) is a subtropical heathland on the coast of the Bulwark region. Sjeríhzvāj̼ Heathland is a relatively typical heathland for Bulwark, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Sjeríhzvāj̼ Heathland has a yearly average temperature of 14°C (57°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 2°C (35°F). The Sjeríhzvāj̼ Heathland receives an average of 289 cm/y (113 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Sjeríhzvāj̼ Heathland covers an area of nearly 17230 km2 (10708 mi2), and an average elevation of 4404 m (14448 ft) above sea level.
