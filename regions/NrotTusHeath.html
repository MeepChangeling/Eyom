The Nrot Tus Heath (/nrot/ /tus/ Jack's Lantern [complicated] [pumpkin]) is a subtropical heath on the coast of the Icereef region. Nrot Tus Heath is a relatively typical heath for Icereef, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Nrot Tus Heath has a yearly average temperature of 10°C (50°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -5°C (23°F). The Nrot Tus Heath receives an average of 155 cm/y (61 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Nrot Tus Heath covers an area of nearly 22703 km2 (14110 mi2), and an average elevation of 2182 m (7158 ft) above sea level.

The Nrot Tus Heath is home to 2092414 people. They break down into the following demographics: 53% Wood Elves, 29% Wareneese, 3% Wareneese, 9% Elf, 4% Imperial, and ~2% Other.

The Nrot Tus Heath is's peoples are culturally Wood Elven, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include eloquence and social expertise and submission to the collective will or culture, as well as a unique sun festival and a spring festival.

Wood Elf

The Wood Elf subculture native to the Nrot Tus Heath are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of tattoos of some cultural significance and base their sence of individual idenity on warbands or civic military service groups. They also place greater cultural emphasus on harmony with nature and existing life and zealous guardianship of their own land or holy sit.

Local Religion

The Nrot Tus Heath is overseen by Potentate Uklo Simon Koroneus, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 41% of the population self-report as devout. The Nrot Tus Heath is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Nrot Tus Heath has its economic roots in Icereef's export of Fish, but has developed its own legal industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.

The Nrot Tus Heath has an Imperial Development Index of 22. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 22 means there are 22 locations within the Nrot Tus Heath which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
