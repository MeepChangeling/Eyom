The Ásda-zrä Woodlands (/ə˞sˈda/ /zræ̝/ Deathmate [sexual] [god; idol]) are a group of subtropical woodland on the coast of the Lower Warding Range region. The Ásda-zrä Woodlands are well known for its thriving abundance of plant life.

Climate

The Ásda-zrä Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 32°C (89°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 36°C (96°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 29°C (84°F). The Ásda-zrä Woodlands receives an average of 66 cm/y (25 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Ásda-zrä Woodlands covers an area of nearly 6916 km2 (4298 mi2), and an average elevation of 11290 m (-5908 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Ásda-zrä Woodlands are overseen by Potentate Shakohr Cassandra Emer, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 35% of the population self-report as devout. The Ásda-zrä Woodlands is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Ásda-zrä Woodlands is a negative return economy zone at this time. It has an abundence of Undead, though infrastructure to this region of Lower Warding Range is not developed enough to support any major local industry.
