The Äncwe Udweht Woodlands (/ənˈkwi/ /uˈdwɪ̘t/ Sealedwrath [angry; mad] [stay]) are a group of temperate woodland within the wetland of the Northlake region. Äncwe Udweht Woodlands are a relatively typical woodland for Northlake, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Äncwe Udweht Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 19°C (66°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 13°C (55°F). The Äncwe Udweht Woodlands receives an average of 233 cm/y (91 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Äncwe Udweht Woodlands covers an area of nearly 4710 km2 (2927 mi2), and an average elevation of 1211 m (3973 ft) above sea level.
