The Anglegrass Woodlands (anglegrass /ˈwʊˌdlændz/ Anglegrass [Translation Unavailable]) are a group of subtropical woodland on the coast of the Endless Peaks region. Anglegrass Woodlands are a relatively typical woodland for Endless Peaks, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Anglegrass Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 27°C (80°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 31°C (87°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 22°C (71°F). The Anglegrass Woodlands receives an average of 69 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Anglegrass Woodlands covers an area of nearly 8009 km2 (4977 mi2), and an average elevation of 8767 m (-14186 ft) above sea level.
