Climate

The Vǐk Qěpzělě Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 28°C (82°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 32°C (89°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 25°C (77°F). The Vǐk Qěpzělě Woodlands receives an average of 219 cm/y (86 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Vǐk Qěpzělě Woodlands covers an area of nearly 18269 km2 (11354 mi2), and an average elevation of 5109 m (16761 ft) above sea level.

The Vǐk Qěpzělě Woodlands are home to 165734 people. They break down into the following demographics: 59% Constructs, 24% Fae, 2% Iron Elves, 6% Imperial, 7% Tanuki, and ~2% Other.

The Vǐk Qěpzělě Woodlands are's peoples are culturally Constructi, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include piety and devotion to the gods and exploring the unknown and discovering secrets, as well as a unique fire festival and a theater festival.

Construct

The Construct subculture native to the Vǐk Qěpzělě Woodlands are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Construct culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of tattoos of some cultural significance and base their sence of individual idenity on voluntary mutual-assistance brotherhoods. They also place greater cultural emphasus on eloquence and social expertise and unity and elimination of group differences.

Fae

The Fae subculture native to the Vǐk Qěpzělě Woodlands are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Construct culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of painted skin markings that sometimes change and base their sence of individual idenity on astrologically-determined birth groups. They also place greater cultural emphasus on harmony with nature and existing life and societal or ethnic superiority over all outsiders.

Iron Elf

The Iron Elf subculture native to the Vǐk Qěpzělě Woodlands are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Construct culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of worn weapons, tools or trade implements and base their sence of individual idenity on inheritance of an ancient body modification. They also place greater cultural emphasus on cunning and the ability to trick others and piety and devotion to the gods.

Imperial

The Imperial subculture native to the Vǐk Qěpzělě Woodlands are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Construct culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of patterned hair shaving or depilation and base their sence of individual idenity on having or lacking noble blood. They also place greater cultural emphasus on filial devotion to family and parents and humanistic reason and “rational” religion.

The Tanuki subculture native to the Vǐk Qěpzělě Woodlands are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Construct culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of role or class-specific clothing items and base their sence of individual idenity on lineages based on ancient hero-ancestors. They also place greater cultural emphasus on unity and elimination of group differences and societal or ethnic superiority over all outsiders.

Local Religion

The Vǐk Qěpzělě Woodlands are overseen by Potentate Khaglo Andra Despoibnis, a god serving within The Apprenticeship, the region's principal religion. Approximately 35% of the population self-report as devout. The Vǐk Qěpzělě Woodlands is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.
