The Northnach Brush (northnach /brʌʃ/ Northnach [Translation Unavailable]) is a temperate brush on the coast of the Northlake region. Northnach Brush is a relatively typical brush for Northlake, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Northnach Brush has a yearly average temperature of 22°C (71°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 17°C (62°F). The Northnach Brush receives an average of 298 cm/y (117 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Northnach Brush covers an area of nearly 16107 km2 (10010 mi2), and an average elevation of 1160 m (3805 ft) above sea level.

The Northnach Brush is home to 338066 people. They break down into the following demographics: 56% Wareneese, 10% Goblins, 19% Iron Elves, 9% Imperial, 4% Imperial, and ~2% Other.

The Northnach Brush is's peoples are culturally Wareneese, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include sharing wealth and goods with others and justice and fairness between people, as well as a unique science festival and a harvest festival.

Wareneese

The Wareneese subculture native to the Northnach Brush are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wareneese culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of piercings, whether minor or elaborate and base their sence of individual idenity on far-flung clans of affiliated families. They also place greater cultural emphasus on pacifism and peaceful resolution of problems and submission to the collective will or culture.

Goblin

The Goblin subculture native to the Northnach Brush are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wareneese culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of piercings, whether minor or elaborate and base their sence of individual idenity on inheritance of an ancient body modification. They also place greater cultural emphasus on industry and the ability to work tirelessly and conquest and domination of others.

Iron Elf

The Iron Elf subculture native to the Northnach Brush are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Wareneese culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of piercings, whether minor or elaborate and base their sence of individual idenity on professional guild or trade-specific group. They also place greater cultural emphasus on restoring some real or imagined glorious past and magical prowess and occult ability.

Imperial

The Imperial subculture native to the Northnach Brush are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wareneese culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of patterned hair shaving or depilation and base their sence of individual idenity on numerous castes of hereditary workers. They also place greater cultural emphasus on cunning and the ability to trick others and leadership and charisma in the group.

Local Religion

The Northnach Brush is overseen by Potentate Boslotl Mandra Eastmus, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 51% of the population self-report as devout. The Northnach Brush is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Northnach Brush has its economic roots in Northlake's export of Rice, but has developed its own machine tools industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.

The Northnach Brush has an Imperial Development Index of 16. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 16 means there are 16 locations within the Northnach Brush which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
