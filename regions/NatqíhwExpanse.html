The Natqíhw Expanse (/nat/ /qɪ̞w/ Shortnose [nose; beak; nostril] [short; low]) is a temperate expanse on the coast of the Ablative region. Natqíhw Expanse is a relatively typical expanse for Ablative, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Natqíhw Expanse has a yearly average temperature of 14°C (57°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 2°C (35°F). The Natqíhw Expanse receives an average of 150 cm/y (59 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Natqíhw Expanse covers an area of nearly 16432 km2 (10212 mi2), and an average elevation of 4282 m (14048 ft) above sea level.

The Natqíhw Expanse is overseen by Potentate Phireus Euandrodite Æðelrig, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 39% of the population self-report as devout. The Natqíhw Expanse is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
