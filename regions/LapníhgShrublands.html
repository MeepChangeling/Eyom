The Lapníhg Shrublands (/lap/ /nɪ̞g/ Isolation [tin can] [private]) are a group of subtropical shrubland on the coast of the Darkrass Plains region. Lapníhg Shrublands are a relatively typical shrubland for Darkrass Plains, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Lapníhg Shrublands has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 3°C (37°F). The Lapníhg Shrublands receives an average of 139 cm/y (54 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Lapníhg Shrublands covers an area of nearly 18431 km2 (11454 mi2), and an average elevation of 3367 m (11046 ft) above sea level.
