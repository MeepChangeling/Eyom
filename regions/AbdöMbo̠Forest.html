The Abdö Mbo̠ Forest (/ˈabdo˞/ /mbo̠/ Manyruins [civilized] [mess; rubble; debris]) is a subtropical forest within the wetland of the Aegispoint region. Abdö Mbo̠ Forest is a relatively typical forest for Aegispoint, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Abdö Mbo̠ Forest has a yearly average temperature of 17°C (62°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 11°C (51°F). The Abdö Mbo̠ Forest receives an average of 214 cm/y (84 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Abdö Mbo̠ Forest covers an area of nearly 1749 km2 (1087 mi2), and an average elevation of 1897 m (6223 ft) above sea level.
