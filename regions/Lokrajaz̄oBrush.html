The Lokrajaz̄o Brush (/ˈʤaz̚o/ Lokra's Chart [Proper Noun (name of person)] [graph]) is a subtropical brush within the temperate deciduous forest of the Galloping Plains region. Lokrajaz̄o Brush is a relatively typical brush for Galloping Plains, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Lokrajaz̄o Brush has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 23°C (73°F). The Lokrajaz̄o Brush receives an average of 137 cm/y (53 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Lokrajaz̄o Brush covers an area of nearly 23238 km2 (14442 mi2), and an average elevation of 5450 m (17880 ft) above sea level.
