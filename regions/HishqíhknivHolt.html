The Hishqíhkniv Holt (/hiʃ/ /ˈqɪ̞kniv/ Noonlays [afternoon] [right (direction)]) is a subtropical holt within the temperate deciduous forest of the Heartland region. Hishqíhkniv Holt is a relatively typical holt for Heartland, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Hishqíhkniv Holt has a yearly average temperature of 17°C (62°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 6°C (42°F). The Hishqíhkniv Holt receives an average of 138 cm/y (54 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Hishqíhkniv Holt covers an area of nearly 19085 km2 (11861 mi2), and an average elevation of 3197 m (10488 ft) above sea level.
