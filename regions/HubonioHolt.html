The Hubonio Holt (/ˈhubo/ /ˈnio/ The Preformer's [circus] [certain; sure]) is a temperate holt within the temperate deciduous forest of the Iron Coast region. Hubonio Holt is a relatively typical holt for Iron Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Hubonio Holt has a yearly average temperature of 17°C (62°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a cool 18°C (64°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 17°C (62°F). The Hubonio Holt receives an average of 142 cm/y (55 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Hubonio Holt covers an area of nearly 2515 km2 (1563 mi2), and an average elevation of 1194 m (3917 ft) above sea level.
