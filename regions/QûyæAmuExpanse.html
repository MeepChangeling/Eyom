The Qûyæ Amu Expanse (/ˈqʊjæ/ /ˈamu/ Goblinvale [boot] [lamb; virgin]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Darkrass Plains region. Qûyæ Amu Expanse is a relatively typical expanse for Darkrass Plains, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Qûyæ Amu Expanse has a yearly average temperature of 17°C (62°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 9°C (48°F). The Qûyæ Amu Expanse receives an average of 133 cm/y (52 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Qûyæ Amu Expanse covers an area of nearly 14135 km2 (8784 mi2), and an average elevation of 5283 m (17332 ft) above sea level.

People and Culture

However, as our culture lacks a taboo for classification of peoples by their biology and such information is highly important for our medical practices amongst other institutions, we have stated the racial demographics of this region. We do not do so to sew the seeds of disunity. We do not do so to belittle or bemoan any group. Such issues are why we use the term 'peoples' rather than races, cultures, or species.

In respect to this universal cultural element, we have split racial classification by culture while keeping the name of each race for our own purposes. Should you see the same race listed more than once in the following section, this is to designate a major cultural division within a race.

The Qûyæ Amu Expanse is's peoples are culturally Sylvanian, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include humanistic reason and “rational” religion and building things in service of their posterity, as well as a unique summer festival and a fire festival.

Local Religion

The Qûyæ Amu Expanse is overseen by Potentate Ityx Ælfriastan Medalaukos, a god serving within Tol's Children, the region's principal religion. Approximately 36% of the population self-report as devout. The Qûyæ Amu Expanse is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

The Qûyæ Amu Expanse has an Imperial Development Index of 11. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 11 means there are 11 locations within the Qûyæ Amu Expanse which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
