The Kren Ëo̠-nti̽t Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 15°C (59°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 2°C (35°F). The Kren Ëo̠-nti̽t Maquis receives an average of 147 cm/y (57 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Kren Ëo̠-nti̽t Maquis covers an area of nearly 10953 km2 (6807 mi2), and an average elevation of 3263 m (10705 ft) above sea level.
