The Sma Köbze Shrublands (/sma/ /ˈko˞bze/ Earthmortar [soft; quiet; gentle] [cement]) are a group of temperate shrubland within the tropical rainforest of the Kiteshield Peninsula region. Sma Köbze Shrublands are a relatively typical shrubland for Kiteshield Peninsula, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Sma Köbze Shrublands has a yearly average temperature of 13°C (55°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 1°C (33°F). The Sma Köbze Shrublands receives an average of 297 cm/y (116 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Sma Köbze Shrublands covers an area of nearly 8507 km2 (5287 mi2), and an average elevation of 3032 m (9947 ft) above sea level.
