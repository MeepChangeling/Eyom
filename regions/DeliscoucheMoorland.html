The Deliscouche Moorland (deliscouche /ˈmʊrˌlænd/ Deliscouche [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical moorland on the coast of the Grazelands region. Deliscouche Moorland is a relatively typical moorland for Grazelands, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Deliscouche Moorland has a yearly average temperature of 27°C (80°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 32°C (89°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 23°C (73°F). The Deliscouche Moorland receives an average of 138 cm/y (54 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Deliscouche Moorland covers an area of nearly 20529 km2 (12758 mi2), and an average elevation of 4668 m (15314 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Deliscouche Moorland is overseen by Potentate Etar Apollothniu Kassa, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 56% of the population self-report as devout. The Deliscouche Moorland is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
