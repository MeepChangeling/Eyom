The Pér Bāä Jungle (/pɜ́ː/ /bɑ̄æ/ Olivecreek [olive] [creek]) is a temperate jungle within the glacier of the Pressingstone region. Pér Bāä Jungle is a relatively typical jungle for Pressingstone, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Pér Bāä Jungle has a yearly average temperature of 26°C (78°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 30°C (86°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 22°C (71°F). The Pér Bāä Jungle receives an average of 71 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Pér Bāä Jungle covers an area of nearly 10530 km2 (6544 mi2), and an average elevation of 5812 m (19068 ft) above sea level.
