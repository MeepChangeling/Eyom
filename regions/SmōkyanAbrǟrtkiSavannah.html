Climate

The Smōkyan Abrǟrtki Savannah has a yearly average temperature of 6°C (42°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold -13°C (9°F). The Smōkyan Abrǟrtki Savannah receives an average of 306 cm/y (120 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Smōkyan Abrǟrtki Savannah covers an area of nearly 18667 km2 (11601 mi2), and an average elevation of 3980 m (13057 ft) above sea level.

The Smōkyan Abrǟrtki Savannah is home to 1287023 people. They break down into the following demographics: 66% Wood Elves, 19% Taurans, 9% Grey Elf, 4% Tanuki, and ~2% Other.

The Smōkyan Abrǟrtki Savannah is's peoples are culturally Wood Elven, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include personal sacrifice for one’s causes or purposes and unity and elimination of group differences, as well as a unique science festival and a storytelling festival.

Wood Elf

The Wood Elf subculture native to the Smōkyan Abrǟrtki Savannah are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of impractical or elaborate role-based clothes and base their sence of individual idenity on religious factions or faith alliances. They also place greater cultural emphasus on vengeance and execution of just vendettas and education and knowledge-seeking.

Tauran

While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of impractical or elaborate role-based clothes and base their sence of individual idenity on hereditary loyalty to a political group. They also place greater cultural emphasus on personal sacrifice for one’s causes or purposes and subtlety and indirectness of action.

Grey Elf

The Grey Elf subculture native to the Smōkyan Abrǟrtki Savannah are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of intricate hair styles or braiding and base their sence of individual idenity on identity based on their local community. They also place greater cultural emphasus on societal or ethnic superiority over all outsiders and humanistic reason and “rational” religion.

Tanuki

The Tanuki subculture native to the Smōkyan Abrǟrtki Savannah are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of role or class-specific clothing items and base their sence of individual idenity on far-flung clans of affiliated families. They also place greater cultural emphasus on submission to the collective will or culture and filial devotion to family and parents.

Local Religion

The Smōkyan Abrǟrtki Savannah is overseen by Potentate Beggyn Pria Oenos, a god serving within Covenant of Irus, the region's principal religion. Approximately 10% of the population self-report as devout. The Smōkyan Abrǟrtki Savannah is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Smōkyan Abrǟrtki Savannah has its economic roots in Icereef's export of Fish, but has developed its own legal industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.
