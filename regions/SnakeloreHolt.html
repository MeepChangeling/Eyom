The Snakelore Holt (snakelore /hoʊlt/ Snakelore [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical holt on the coast of the Cutmountains region. Snakelore Holt is a relatively typical holt for Cutmountains, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Snakelore Holt has a yearly average temperature of 32°C (89°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 37°C (98°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 27°C (80°F). The Snakelore Holt receives an average of 68 cm/y (26 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Snakelore Holt covers an area of nearly 6754 km2 (4197 mi2), and an average elevation of 11644 m (-4747 ft) above sea level.
