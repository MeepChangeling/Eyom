The Tëndën-rëvî Maquis (/ˈrëvɪ/ Tëndënswash [Proper Noun (name of person)] [soap]) is a temperate maquis on the coast of the Bountyrange region. Tëndën-rëvî Maquis is a relatively typical maquis for Bountyrange, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Tëndën-rëvî Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 20°C (68°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 12°C (53°F). The Tëndën-rëvî Maquis receives an average of 281 cm/y (110 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Tëndën-rëvî Maquis covers an area of nearly 11357 km2 (7058 mi2), and an average elevation of 2123 m (6965 ft) above sea level.

The Tëndën-rëvî Maquis is home to 531118 people. They break down into the following demographics: 67% Sylvin, 18% Hobgoblins, 7% Kobold, 6% Tanuki, and ~2% Other.

The Tëndën-rëvî Maquis is's peoples are culturally Sylvanian, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include beauty and seductive charm and prosperity and accruing material wealth, as well as a unique arcane festival and a relic festival.

Local Religion

The Tëndën-rëvî Maquis is overseen by Potentate Dredara Bacint Eadwintus, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 28% of the population self-report as devout. The Tëndën-rëvî Maquis is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Tëndën-rëvî Maquis is noteworthy for its economic independence from Bountyrange's export of Metals. Tëndën-rëvî Maquis is particularly renound for its textiles industry. It is also has a noteworthy mining industry.
