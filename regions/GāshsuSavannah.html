The Gāshsu Savannah (/gəˑʃ/ /su/ Spring [rubber; tire] [fun]) is a subtropical savanna within the wetland of the Blackstone Coast region. Gāshsu Savannah is a relatively typical savanna for Blackstone Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Gāshsu Savannah has a yearly average temperature of 20°C (68°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 12°C (53°F). The Gāshsu Savannah receives an average of 215 cm/y (84 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Gāshsu Savannah covers an area of nearly 7133 km2 (4433 mi2), and an average elevation of 2401 m (7877 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Gāshsu Savannah is overseen by Potentate Asdohr Eógaine Neopto, a god serving within The Apprenticeship, the region's principal religion. Approximately 35% of the population self-report as devout.
