The Brör Vreflö Expanse (/bro˞r/ /ˈvreflo˞/ Thundering [extreme; intense; severe; radical] [hearing]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Timberlund region. Brör Vreflö Expanse is a relatively typical expanse for Timberlund, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Brör Vreflö Expanse has a yearly average temperature of 9°C (48°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -6°C (22°F). The Brör Vreflö Expanse receives an average of 148 cm/y (58 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Brör Vreflö Expanse covers an area of nearly 12145 km2 (7548 mi2), and an average elevation of 4523 m (14839 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Brör Vreflö Expanse is overseen by Potentate Bystrix Verht Ciapoinawn, a god serving within Covenant of Irus, the region's principal religion. Approximately 35% of the population self-report as devout. The Brör Vreflö Expanse is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
