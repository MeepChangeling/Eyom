The Ze Zel Holt (/ze/ /zel/ Welcoming [available] [address]) is a temperate holt on the coast of the Anvil Badlands region. Ze Zel Holt is a relatively typical holt for Anvil Badlands, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Ze Zel Holt has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 22°C (71°F). The Ze Zel Holt receives an average of 72 cm/y (28 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Ze Zel Holt covers an area of nearly 11709 km2 (7277 mi2), and an average elevation of 6316 m (20721 ft) above sea level.

The Ze Zel Holt is home to 335971 people. They break down into the following demographics: 62% Iron Elves, 11% Dwarves, 12% Dwarves, 7% Elf, 6% Tanuki, and ~2% Other.

The Ze Zel Holt is's peoples are culturally Wareneese, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include ethnic purity of blood and culture and exploring the unknown and discovering secrets, as well as a unique religious festival and a harvest festival.

Iron Elf

The Iron Elf subculture native to the Ze Zel Holt are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Iron Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of culturally-significant jewelry or accessories and base their sence of individual idenity on warbands or civic military service groups. They also place greater cultural emphasus on membership in an elite bloodline or caste and eloquence and social expertise.

Dwarf

Elf

The Elf subculture native to the Ze Zel Holt are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Iron Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of color choices with social meaning to them and base their sence of individual idenity on dynastic lineages with cadet branches. They also place greater cultural emphasus on pacifism and peaceful resolution of problems and courage and valiance in danger.

Tanuki

The Tanuki subculture native to the Ze Zel Holt are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Iron Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of intricate hair styles or braiding and base their sence of individual idenity on numerous castes of hereditary workers. They also place greater cultural emphasus on industry and the ability to work tirelessly and harmony with nature and existing life.

Local Religion

The Ze Zel Holt is overseen by Potentate Denera Simontus Grononthus, a god serving within Fawresm, the region's principal religion. Approximately 42% of the population self-report as devout. The Ze Zel Holt is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Ze Zel Holt has its economic roots in Anvil Badlands's export of Clay, but has developed its own mercenary industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.

The Ze Zel Holt has an Imperial Development Index of 16. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 16 means there are 16 locations within the Ze Zel Holt which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..

As a courtesy for scholars and agents, the Ze Zel Holt is rated I2 by Eyoms banks. Business ventures within the Ze Zel Holt are expected to return above capitol with some volatility.
