The Kùmeedfùp Heath (/ˈkʊme/ /ˈedfʊp/ Wandering [mail] [wrong]) is a temperate heath on the coast of the Fargarden region. Kùmeedfùp Heath is a relatively typical heath for Fargarden, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Kùmeedfùp Heath has a yearly average temperature of 19°C (66°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 11°C (51°F). The Kùmeedfùp Heath receives an average of 69 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Kùmeedfùp Heath covers an area of nearly 14023 km2 (8715 mi2), and an average elevation of 2816 m (9238 ft) above sea level.

The Kùmeedfùp Heath is home to 224993 people. They break down into the following demographics: 65% Goblins, 20% Fae, 9% Tanuki, 4% Elf, and ~2% Other.

The Kùmeedfùp Heath is's peoples are culturally Goblin, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include restoring some real or imagined glorious past and excellence in one’s profession or trade, as well as a unique science festival and a theater festival.

Goblin

The Goblin subculture native to the Kùmeedfùp Heath are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Goblin culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of painted skin markings that sometimes change and base their sence of individual idenity on lineages based on ancient hero-ancestors. They also place greater cultural emphasus on harmony with nature and existing life and industry and the ability to work tirelessly.

Fae

The Fae subculture native to the Kùmeedfùp Heath are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Goblin culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of tattoos of some cultural significance and base their sence of individual idenity on professional guild or trade-specific group. They also place greater cultural emphasus on societal or ethnic superiority over all outsiders and building things in service of their posterity.

Tanuki

The Tanuki subculture native to the Kùmeedfùp Heath are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Goblin culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of impractical or elaborate role-based clothes and base their sence of individual idenity on hereditary loyalty to a political group. They also place greater cultural emphasus on magical prowess and occult ability and filial devotion to family and parents.

Elf

The Elf subculture native to the Kùmeedfùp Heath are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Goblin culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of intricate hair styles or braiding and base their sence of individual idenity on astrologically-determined birth groups. They also place greater cultural emphasus on humanistic reason and “rational” religion and raw strength and personal prowess.

Local Religion

The Kùmeedfùp Heath is overseen by Potentate Aotl Béibnis Eoghandra, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 10% of the population self-report as devout. The Kùmeedfùp Heath is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Kùmeedfùp Heath is predominantly economicly reiant on Fargarden's export of Medicine, and has no outstanding industries byond what is required for its role in that process.

The Kùmeedfùp Heath has an Imperial Development Index of 15. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 15 means there are 15 locations within the Kùmeedfùp Heath which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
