Climate

The Kivu̹zû Qizi Grasslands has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 8°C (46°F). The Kivu̹zû Qizi Grasslands receives an average of 150 cm/y (59 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Kivu̹zû Qizi Grasslands covers an area of nearly 16270 km2 (10111 mi2), and an average elevation of 6915 m (-20262 ft) above sea level.

The Kivu̹zû Qizi Grasslands are home to 617408 people. They break down into the following demographics: 57% Wood Elves, 22% Dwarves, 6% Gnolls, 6% Grey Elf, 7% Elf, and ~2% Other.

The Kivu̹zû Qizi Grasslands are's peoples are culturally Wood Elven, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include ethnic purity of blood and culture and courage and valiance in danger, as well as a unique summer festival and a wine festival.

While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of significant scent or perfume uses and base their sence of individual idenity on extended family out to cousins and like kin. They also place greater cultural emphasus on building things in service of their posterity and piety and devotion to the gods.

The Dwarf subculture native to the Kivu̹zû Qizi Grasslands are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of socially-meaningful animal motif items and base their sence of individual idenity on trade guilds specific to particular roles. They also place greater cultural emphasus on submission to lawful authority and industry and the ability to work tirelessly.

They also place greater cultural emphasus on restoring some real or imagined glorious past and personal development and limit-pushing.

The Grey Elf subculture native to the Kivu̹zû Qizi Grasslands are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of impractical or elaborate role-based clothes and base their sence of individual idenity on dynastic lineages with cadet branches. They also place greater cultural emphasus on education and knowledge-seeking and conquest and domination of others.

Elf

The Elf subculture native to the Kivu̹zû Qizi Grasslands are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of painted skin markings that sometimes change and base their sence of individual idenity on lineages based on ancient hero-ancestors. They also place greater cultural emphasus on hardiness and endurance before woes and submission to the collective will or culture.

The Kivu̹zû Qizi Grasslands is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
