The Gdo̠ Atjik Prairie has a yearly average temperature of 9°C (48°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -7°C (20°F). The Gdo̠ Atjik Prairie receives an average of 219 cm/y (86 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Gdo̠ Atjik Prairie covers an area of nearly 6951 km2 (4320 mi2), and an average elevation of 4707 m (15442 ft) above sea level.
