The Zomoeje Maquis (/ˈzomo/ /ˈeʤe/ Waterweed [surf] [bulb (plant root)]) is a subtropical maquis within the wetland of the Illa's Panhandle region. Zomoeje Maquis is a relatively typical maquis for Illa's Panhandle, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Zomoeje Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 21°C (69°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 22°C (71°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 20°C (68°F). The Zomoeje Maquis receives an average of 215 cm/y (84 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Zomoeje Maquis covers an area of nearly 11697 km2 (7269 mi2), and an average elevation of 2967 m (9734 ft) above sea level.

The Zomoeje Maquis is home to 348101 people. They break down into the following demographics: 69% Iron Elves, 13% Kitsune, 3% Taurans, 6% Tanuki, 7% Kivu, and ~2% Other.

The Zomoeje Maquis is's peoples are culturally Wareneese, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include raw strength and personal prowess and personal indulgence and luxuriant pleasure, as well as a unique relic festival and a relic festival.

While they follow the broader tennents of Iron Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of significant scent or perfume uses and base their sence of individual idenity on voluntary mutual-assistance brotherhoods. They also place greater cultural emphasus on education and knowledge-seeking and subtlety and indirectness of action.

Local Religion

The Zomoeje Maquis is overseen by Potentate Wulses Goronwyll Ethnis, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 28% of the population self-report as devout. The Zomoeje Maquis is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Zomoeje Maquis is noteworthy for its economic independence from Illa's Panhandle's export of Rubber. Zomoeje Maquis is particularly renound for its steel industry. It is also has a noteworthy machine tools industry.

The Zomoeje Maquis has an Imperial Development Index of 34. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 34 means there are 34 locations within the Zomoeje Maquis which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..

As a courtesy for scholars and agents, the Zomoeje Maquis is rated C2 by Eyom’s banks. Business ventures within the Zomoeje Maquis are expected to return market rate with some volatility.
