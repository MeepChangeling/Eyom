The Fifkîn-dëdo Holt (/ˈdëdo/ Fifkîn the Honored's [Proper Noun (name of person)] [respect]) is a subtropical holt on the coast of the Upper Warding Range region. Fifkîn-dëdo Holt is a relatively typical holt for Upper Warding Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Fifkîn-dëdo Holt has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). The Fifkîn-dëdo Holt receives an average of 67 cm/y (26 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Fifkîn-dëdo Holt covers an area of nearly 8543 km2 (5309 mi2), and an average elevation of 8505 m (-15046 ft) above sea level.
