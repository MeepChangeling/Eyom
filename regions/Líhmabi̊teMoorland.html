The Líhmabi̊te Moorland (/ˈlɪ̞mab/ /ˈi̘te/ Hank [emphasis] [leg; thigh]) is a subtropical moorland on the coast of the Abysstro region. Líhmabi̊te Moorland is a relatively typical moorland for Abysstro, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Líhmabi̊te Moorland has a yearly average temperature of 15°C (59°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 4°C (39°F). The Líhmabi̊te Moorland receives an average of 148 cm/y (58 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Líhmabi̊te Moorland covers an area of nearly 13125 km2 (8157 mi2), and an average elevation of 4659 m (15285 ft) above sea level.
