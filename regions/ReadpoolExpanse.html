Climate

The Readpool Expanse has a yearly average temperature of 32°C (89°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 36°C (96°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 27°C (80°F). The Readpool Expanse receives an average of 68 cm/y (26 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Readpool Expanse covers an area of nearly 6726 km2 (4180 mi2), and an average elevation of 17112 m (13192 ft) above sea level.
