The Ve-rêlî Grasslands (/ve/ /ˈrɘlɪ/ Guilded [fat; big] [ornament]) are a group of subtropical grassland on the coast of the Home region. Ve-rêlî Grasslands are a relatively typical grassland for Home, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Ve-rêlî Grasslands has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 8°C (46°F). The Ve-rêlî Grasslands receives an average of 283 cm/y (111 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Ve-rêlî Grasslands covers an area of nearly 12889 km2 (8010 mi2), and an average elevation of 7986 m (-16748 ft) above sea level.

The Ve-rêlî Grasslands are's peoples are culturally Wood Elven, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include dominating and possessing other people and justice and fairness between people, as well as a unique beer festival and a history festival.

Wood Elf

The Wood Elf subculture native to the Ve-rêlî Grasslands are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of culturally-significant jewelry or accessories and base their sence of individual idenity on having or lacking noble blood. They also place greater cultural emphasus on humanistic reason and “rational” religion and discipline and obedience to the law.

Tauran

The Tauran subculture native to the Ve-rêlî Grasslands are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of piercings, whether minor or elaborate and base their sence of individual idenity on professional guild or trade-specific group. They also place greater cultural emphasus on courage and valiance in danger and dominating and possessing other people.

Imperial

The Imperial subculture native to the Ve-rêlî Grasslands are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of painted skin markings that sometimes change and base their sence of individual idenity on dynastic lineages with cadet branches. They also place greater cultural emphasus on cunning and the ability to trick others and harmony with nature and existing life.

Quenn

The Quenn subculture native to the Ve-rêlî Grasslands are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of patterned hair shaving or depilation and base their sence of individual idenity on dynastic lineages with cadet branches. They also place greater cultural emphasus on zealous guardianship of their own land or holy sit and raw strength and personal prowess.

Local Religion

The Ve-rêlî Grasslands are overseen by Potentate Kruglena Mentos Eadwintus, a god serving within The Council of Twelve, the region's principal religion. Approximately 32% of the population self-report as devout. The Ve-rêlî Grasslands is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
