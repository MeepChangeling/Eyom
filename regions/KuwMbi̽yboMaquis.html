The Kuw Mbi̽ybo Maquis (/kuw/ /ˈmbi̽jbo/ Earthfort [sudden; abrupt; swift] [bunker]) is a subtropical maquis on the coast of the Pantza region. Kuw Mbi̽ybo Maquis is a relatively typical maquis for Pantza, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Kuw Mbi̽ybo Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 8°C (46°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -10°C (14°F). The Kuw Mbi̽ybo Maquis receives an average of 153 cm/y (60 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Kuw Mbi̽ybo Maquis covers an area of nearly 20354 km2 (12650 mi2), and an average elevation of 3977 m (13047 ft) above sea level.
