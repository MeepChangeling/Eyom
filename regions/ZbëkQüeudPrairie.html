The Zbëk Qüeud Prairie (/zbɛk/ /q˔ud/ Greycane [gray] [cane]) is a subtropical prairie within the temperate rainforest of the Heartland region. Zbëk Qüeud Prairie is a relatively typical prairie for Heartland, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Zbëk Qüeud Prairie has a yearly average temperature of 14°C (57°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 3°C (37°F). The Zbëk Qüeud Prairie receives an average of 288 cm/y (113 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Zbëk Qüeud Prairie covers an area of nearly 19763 km2 (12282 mi2), and an average elevation of 1896 m (6220 ft) above sea level.
