Climate

The Stièvloq Savannah has a yearly average temperature of 17°C (62°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 9°C (48°F). The Stièvloq Savannah receives an average of 313 cm/y (123 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Stièvloq Savannah covers an area of nearly 21599 km2 (13423 mi2), and an average elevation of 2443 m (8015 ft) above sea level.
