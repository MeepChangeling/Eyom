The Ha Hjeno̠z Woodlands (/ha/ /ˈhjeno̠z/ Dancelamp [mauve] [happiness]) are a group of subtropical woodland on the coast of the Foxvale region. Ha Hjeno̠z Woodlands are a relatively typical woodland for Foxvale, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Ha Hjeno̠z Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 12°C (53°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -1°C (31°F). The Ha Hjeno̠z Woodlands receives an average of 291 cm/y (114 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Ha Hjeno̠z Woodlands covers an area of nearly 15829 km2 (9837 mi2), and an average elevation of 4498 m (14757 ft) above sea level.
