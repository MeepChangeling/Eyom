The Chyijikttiz̄ Heathland (/tiz̚/ Chyijiktssong [Proper Noun (name of person)] [phonograph]) is a temperate heathland within the wetland of the Shimmering Spires region. The Chyijikttiz̄ Heathland is well known for its thriving abundance of plant life.

Climate

The Chyijikttiz̄ Heathland has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 20°C (68°F). The Chyijikttiz̄ Heathland receives an average of 202 cm/y (79 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Chyijikttiz̄ Heathland covers an area of nearly 14543 km2 (9038 mi2), and an average elevation of 6316 m (20721 ft) above sea level.
