The Glzijiqtjioji Basin (/ˈʤoʤi/ Lord Glzi's [Proper Noun (name of person)] [landlord]) is a temperate basin on the coast of the Brownmarsh region. Glzijiqtjioji Basin is a relatively typical basin for Brownmarsh, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Glzijiqtjioji Basin has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 22°C (71°F). The Glzijiqtjioji Basin receives an average of 135 cm/y (53 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Glzijiqtjioji Basin covers an area of nearly 21385 km2 (13290 mi2), and an average elevation of 5291 m (17358 ft) above sea level.

The Glzijiqtjioji Basin is home to 439378 people. They break down into the following demographics: 63% Iron Elves, 21% Iron Elves, 1% Wareneese, 7% Elf, 6% Kivu, and ~2% Other.

The Glzijiqtjioji Basin is's peoples are culturally Constructi, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include beauty and seductive charm and education and knowledge-seeking, as well as a unique music festival and a beer festival.

Iron Elf

The Iron Elf subculture native to the Glzijiqtjioji Basin are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Iron Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of role or class-specific clothing items and base their sence of individual idenity on trade guilds specific to particular roles. They also place greater cultural emphasus on leadership and charisma in the group and aesthetic beauty in material goods and architecture.

Sylvian

The Sylvian subculture native to the Glzijiqtjioji Basin are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Iron Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of painted skin markings that sometimes change and base their sence of individual idenity on hereditary loyalty to a political group. They also place greater cultural emphasus on loyalty to one’s friends, family, and own and honor and maintaining one’s integrity.

Rattu

The Rattu subculture native to the Glzijiqtjioji Basin are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Iron Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of piercings, whether minor or elaborate and base their sence of individual idenity on numerous castes of hereditary workers. They also place greater cultural emphasus on loyalty to one’s friends, family, and own and honor and maintaining one’s integrity.

Elf

The Elf subculture native to the Glzijiqtjioji Basin are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Iron Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of role or class-specific clothing items and base their sence of individual idenity on astrologically-determined birth groups. They also place greater cultural emphasus on justice and fairness between people and social progress toward some eventual utopia.

Kivu

The Kivu subculture native to the Glzijiqtjioji Basin are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Iron Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of worn weapons, tools or trade implements and base their sence of individual idenity on lineages based on ancient hero-ancestors. They also place greater cultural emphasus on piety and devotion to the gods and sexual license and wantonness.

Local Religion

The Glzijiqtjioji Basin is overseen by Potentate Nidite Damontus Xandroniu, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 40% of the population self-report as devout. The Glzijiqtjioji Basin is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Glzijiqtjioji Basin has its economic roots in Brownmarsh's export of Coffee, but has developed its own education industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.

The Glzijiqtjioji Basin has an Imperial Development Index of 24. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 24 means there are 24 locations within the Glzijiqtjioji Basin which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
