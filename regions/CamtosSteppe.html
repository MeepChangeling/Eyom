The Camtos Steppe (camtos /stɛp/ Camtos [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical steppe on the coast of the Highpeaks region. Camtos Steppe is a relatively typical steppe for Highpeaks, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Camtos Steppe has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 20°C (68°F). The Camtos Steppe receives an average of 73 cm/y (28 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Camtos Steppe covers an area of nearly 13209 km2 (8209 mi2), and an average elevation of 5397 m (17706 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Camtos Steppe is overseen by Potentate Budum Alemuireya Helwigid, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 19% of the population self-report as devout. The Camtos Steppe is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Camtos Steppe is a negative return economy zone at this time. It has an abundence of Elemental Crystals, though infrastructure to this region of Highpeaks is not developed enough to support any major local industry.

The Camtos Steppe has an Imperial Development Index of 5. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 5 means there are 5 locations within the Camtos Steppe which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
