The Sêlës-îzê Expanse (/ˈɪzɘ/ Sêlës' Countertop [Proper Noun (name of person)] [counter (flat; elevated surface)]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Greatbay region. Sêlës-îzê Expanse is a relatively typical expanse for Greatbay, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Sêlës-îzê Expanse has a yearly average temperature of 18°C (64°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 22°C (71°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 14°C (57°F). The Sêlës-îzê Expanse receives an average of 283 cm/y (111 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Sêlës-îzê Expanse covers an area of nearly 18300 km2 (11373 mi2), and an average elevation of 3308 m (10853 ft) above sea level.

People and Culture

Imperial Warning: The following section of this document deals with biological race. In deference to the traditions of Eyomic peoples, we have foregone town-specific racial census. However, as our culture lacks a taboo for classification of peoples by their biology and such information is highly important for our medical practices amongst other institutions, we have stated the racial demographics of this region. We do not do so to sew the seeds of disunity. We do not do so to belittle or bemoan any group. Such issues are why we use the term 'peoples' rather than races, cultures, or species.

In respect to this universal cultural element, we have split racial classification by culture while keeping the name of each race for our own purposes. Should you see the same race listed more than once in the following section, this is to designate a major cultural division within a race.

The Sêlës-îzê Expanse is's peoples are culturally Sylvanian, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include pacifism and peaceful resolution of problems and loyalty to one’s friends, family, and own, as well as a unique storytelling festival and a summer festival.

Local Religion

The Sêlës-îzê Expanse is overseen by Potentate Hinia Phinemun Clídnanawn, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 28% of the population self-report as devout. The Sêlës-îzê Expanse is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy
