The Schye-vrestjod Shrublands (/sce/ /ˈvrestjod/ Yearking's Palace [palace] [election]) are a group of subtropical shrubland within the tropical rainforest of the Lin Forest region. Schye-vrestjod Shrublands are a relatively typical shrubland for Lin Forest, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Schye-vrestjod Shrublands has a yearly average temperature of 15°C (59°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 7°C (44°F). The Schye-vrestjod Shrublands receives an average of 302 cm/y (118 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Schye-vrestjod Shrublands covers an area of nearly 16874 km2 (10487 mi2), and an average elevation of 2689 m (8822 ft) above sea level.
