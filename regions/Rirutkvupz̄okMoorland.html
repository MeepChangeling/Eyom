The Rirutkvupz̄ok Moorland (/z̚ok/ Rirutkvup's Zipper [Proper Noun (name of person)] [zipper]) is a subtropical moorland within the temperate deciduous forest of the Hearthcoast region. The Rirutkvupz̄ok Moorland is well known for being largely barren of plant life.

Climate

The Rirutkvupz̄ok Moorland has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 22°C (71°F). The Rirutkvupz̄ok Moorland receives an average of 143 cm/y (56 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Rirutkvupz̄ok Moorland covers an area of nearly 31705 km2 (19704 mi2), and an average elevation of 1085 m (3559 ft) above sea level.
