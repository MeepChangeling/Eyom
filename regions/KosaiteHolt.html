The Kosaite Holt (/ˈkosa/ /ku/ Blackhonor [glory; honor] [black]) is a temperate holt within the temperate rainforest of the Illa's Panhandle region. Kosaite Holt is a relatively typical holt for Illa's Panhandle, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Kosaite Holt has a yearly average temperature of 22°C (71°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Kosaite Holt receives an average of 285 cm/y (112 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Kosaite Holt covers an area of nearly 43875 km2 (27268 mi2), and an average elevation of 2862 m (9389 ft) above sea level.
