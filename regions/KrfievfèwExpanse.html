The Krfievfèw Expanse (Krfiev /fɛw/ Krfiev's Herd [Proper Noun (name of person)] [buffalo]) is a temperate expanse on the coast of the Orchard Garden region. Krfievfèw Expanse is a relatively typical expanse for Orchard Garden, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Krfievfèw Expanse has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 3°C (37°F). The Krfievfèw Expanse receives an average of 290 cm/y (114 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Krfievfèw Expanse covers an area of nearly 6686 km2 (4155 mi2), and an average elevation of 2817 m (9242 ft) above sea level.

The Krfievfèw Expanse is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
