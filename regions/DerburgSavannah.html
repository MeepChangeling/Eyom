The Derburg Savannah (derburg /səˈvænə/ Derberg [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical savanna on the coast of the Thunderfields region. Derburg Savannah is a relatively typical savanna for Thunderfields, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Derburg Savannah has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 20°C (68°F). The Derburg Savannah receives an average of 144 cm/y (56 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Derburg Savannah covers an area of nearly 18131 km2 (11268 mi2), and an average elevation of 5352 m (17559 ft) above sea level.

The Derburg Savannah is home to 335246 people. They break down into the following demographics: 55% Wareneese, 17% Taurans, 13% Kitsune, 8% Grey Elf, 5% Grey Elf, and ~2% Other.

The Derburg Savannah is's peoples are culturally Wareneese, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include excellence in one’s profession or trade and ascetic unworldliness and pious poverty, as well as a unique folk festival and a spring festival.

Local Religion

The Derburg Savannah is overseen by Potentate Nintuna Weald Galia, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 26% of the population self-report as devout. The Derburg Savannah is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Derburg Savannah has its economic roots in Thunderfields's export of Agriculture, but has developed its own rail industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.

The Derburg Savannah has an Imperial Development Index of 23. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 23 means there are 23 locations within the Derburg Savannah which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
