The Zë-dîke Brush (/zë/ /ˈdɪke/ Contract [gramophone] [post]) is a subtropical brush on the coast of the Squeeji Basin region. Zë-dîke Brush is a relatively typical brush for Squeeji Basin, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Zë-dîke Brush has a yearly average temperature of 18°C (64°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 10°C (50°F). The Zë-dîke Brush receives an average of 72 cm/y (28 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Zë-dîke Brush covers an area of nearly 15147 km2 (9413 mi2), and an average elevation of 6016 m (19737 ft) above sea level.
