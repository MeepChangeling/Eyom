The Shuschä Cÿckpha Savannah (/cikˈfa/ Shuschä's Fin [Proper Noun (name of person)] [fin (of fish)]) is a subtropical savanna on the coast of the Golden Brooks region. Shuschä Cÿckpha Savannah is a relatively typical savanna for Golden Brooks, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Shuschä Cÿckpha Savannah has a yearly average temperature of 26°C (78°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 30°C (86°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 23°C (73°F). The Shuschä Cÿckpha Savannah receives an average of 272 cm/y (107 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Shuschä Cÿckpha Savannah covers an area of nearly 18641 km2 (11585 mi2), and an average elevation of 6118 m (20072 ft) above sea level.
