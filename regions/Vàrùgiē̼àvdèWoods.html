The Vàrùgiē̼àvdè Woods (/ˈvərʊg/ /ˈj̼əvdɛ/ Stragglersbound [detention] [reluctant]) are a group of subtropical woods on the coast of the Ablative region. Vàrùgiē̼àvdè Woods are a relatively typical woods for Ablative, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Vàrùgiē̼àvdè Woods has a yearly average temperature of 14°C (57°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 1°C (33°F). The Vàrùgiē̼àvdè Woods receives an average of 145 cm/y (57 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Vàrùgiē̼àvdè Woods covers an area of nearly 12431 km2 (7725 mi2), and an average elevation of 4645 m (15239 ft) above sea level.

The Vàrùgiē̼àvdè Woods are home to 1382423 people. They break down into the following demographics: 64% Wood Elves, 20% Wood Elves, 1% Iron Elves, 8% Imperial, 5% Imperial, and ~2% Other.

The Vàrùgiē̼àvdè Woods are's peoples are culturally Wood Elven, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include leadership and charisma in the group and honesty and truthfulness in speech, as well as a unique storytelling festival and a theater festival.

Wood Elf

The Wood Elf subculture native to the Vàrùgiē̼àvdè Woods are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of role or class-specific clothing items and base their sence of individual idenity on ethnic membership. They also place greater cultural emphasus on unity and elimination of group differences and ethnic purity of blood and culture.

While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of color choices with social meaning to them and base their sence of individual idenity on warbands or civic military service groups. They also place greater cultural emphasus on ascetic unworldliness and pious poverty and humanistic reason and “rational” religion.

Iron Elf

The Iron Elf subculture native to the Vàrùgiē̼àvdè Woods are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of socially-meaningful animal motif items and base their sence of individual idenity on dynastic lineages with cadet branches. They also place greater cultural emphasus on magical prowess and occult ability and ascetic unworldliness and pious poverty.

Imperial

The Imperial subculture native to the Vàrùgiē̼àvdè Woods are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of color choices with social meaning to them and base their sence of individual idenity on inheritance of an ancient body modification. They also place greater cultural emphasus on harmony with nature and existing life and excellence in one’s profession or trade.

Imperial

The Imperial subculture native to the Vàrùgiē̼àvdè Woods are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of role or class-specific clothing items and base their sence of individual idenity on numerous castes of hereditary workers. They also place greater cultural emphasus on sharing wealth and goods with others and societal or ethnic superiority over all outsiders.

Local Religion

The Vàrùgiē̼àvdè Woods are overseen by Potentate Zinja Æðelrean Conw, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 36% of the population self-report as devout. The Vàrùgiē̼àvdè Woods is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Vàrùgiē̼àvdè Woods has its economic roots in Ablative's export of Wool, but has developed its own monster hunting industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.
