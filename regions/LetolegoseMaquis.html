The Letolegose Maquis (/leˈtole/ /ˈgose/ Picturesque [label] [gorgeous]) is a subtropical maquis within the temperate rainforest of the Equuscoast region. Letolegose Maquis is a relatively typical maquis for Equuscoast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Letolegose Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 20°C (68°F). The Letolegose Maquis receives an average of 296 cm/y (116 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Letolegose Maquis covers an area of nearly 14639 km2 (9098 mi2), and an average elevation of 1778 m (5833 ft) above sea level.

The Letolegose Maquis is's peoples are culturally Tauranian, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include harmony with nature and existing life and industry and the ability to work tirelessly, as well as a unique religious festival and a beer festival.

Tanuki

Local Religion

The Letolegose Maquis is overseen by Potentate Qhiorr Muiren Diape, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 26% of the population self-report as devout. The Letolegose Maquis is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Letolegose Maquis is noteworthy for its economic independence from Equuscoast's export of Machine Parts. Letolegose Maquis is particularly renound for its musical instruments industry. It is also has a noteworthy innovation industry.
