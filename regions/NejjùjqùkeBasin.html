The Nejjùjqùke Basin (/ˈnejjʊj/ /ˈqʊke/ Yellowglove [glove] [mustard]) is a subtropical basin within the temperate rainforest of the Substrate region. Nejjùjqùke Basin is a relatively typical basin for Substrate, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Nejjùjqùke Basin has a yearly average temperature of 18°C (64°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 10°C (50°F). The Nejjùjqùke Basin receives an average of 315 cm/y (124 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Nejjùjqùke Basin covers an area of nearly 26485 km2 (16460 mi2), and an average elevation of 1812 m (5944 ft) above sea level.
