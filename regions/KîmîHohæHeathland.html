Climate

The Kîmî Hohæ Heathland has a yearly average temperature of 18°C (64°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 10°C (50°F). The Kîmî Hohæ Heathland covers an area of nearly 14670 km2 (9117 mi2), and an average elevation of 4288 m (14068 ft) above sea level.
