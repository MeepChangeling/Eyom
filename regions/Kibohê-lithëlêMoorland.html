The Kibohê-lithëlê Moorland (/kiˈbohɘ/ /liˈθëlɘ/ Chedder [cute] [cheese]) is a temperate moorland on the coast of the Substrate region. Kibohê-lithëlê Moorland is a relatively typical moorland for Substrate, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Kibohê-lithëlê Moorland has a yearly average temperature of 21°C (69°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 13°C (55°F). The Kibohê-lithëlê Moorland receives an average of 220 cm/y (86 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Kibohê-lithëlê Moorland covers an area of nearly 19995 km2 (12426 mi2), and an average elevation of 3092 m (10144 ft) above sea level.
