The Rusg-lizgä Grasslands (/rusg/ /lizˈgɑ/ Manyfrog [abundant; lush] [frog]) are a group of subtropical grassland on the coast of the Cedarcoast region. Rusg-lizgä Grasslands are a relatively typical grassland for Cedarcoast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Rusg-lizgä Grasslands has a yearly average temperature of 28°C (82°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 32°C (89°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 25°C (77°F). The Rusg-lizgä Grasslands receives an average of 139 cm/y (54 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Rusg-lizgä Grasslands covers an area of nearly 15960 km2 (9919 mi2), and an average elevation of 4098 m (13444 ft) above sea level.
