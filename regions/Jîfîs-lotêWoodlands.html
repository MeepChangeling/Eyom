The Jîfîs-lotê Woodlands (/ˈlotɘ/ Jîfîs's Palace [Proper Noun (name of person)] [block; brick; cube]) are a group of subtropical woodland on the coast of the Indigoplains region. Jîfîs-lotê Woodlands are a relatively typical woodland for Indigoplains, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Jîfîs-lotê Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 21°C (69°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 16°C (60°F). The Jîfîs-lotê Woodlands receives an average of 224 cm/y (88 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Jîfîs-lotê Woodlands covers an area of nearly 9025 km2 (5609 mi2), and an average elevation of 6034 m (19796 ft) above sea level.

The Jîfîs-lotê Woodlands are home to 351570 people. They break down into the following demographics: 52% Wood Elves, 13% Kitsune, 20% Kitsune, 8% Elf, 5% Elf, and ~2% Other.

The Jîfîs-lotê Woodlands are's peoples are culturally Wood Elven, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include personal development and limit-pushing and purging evil and expelling the wicked, as well as a unique beer festival and a spirit festival.

Wood Elf

The Wood Elf subculture native to the Jîfîs-lotê Woodlands are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of tattoos of some cultural significance and base their sence of individual idenity on religious factions or faith alliances. They also place greater cultural emphasus on education and knowledge-seeking and courage and valiance in danger.

Kitsune

While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of color choices with social meaning to them and base their sence of individual idenity on hereditary loyalty to a political group. They also place greater cultural emphasus on zealous guardianship of their own land or holy sit and purging evil and expelling the wicked.

The Sylvian subculture native to the Jîfîs-lotê Woodlands are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. The Quenn subculture native to the Jîfîs-lotê Woodlands are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. Local Religion

The Jîfîs-lotê Woodlands are overseen by Potentate Qylborh Ælria Lóegaintos, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 30% of the population self-report as devout. The Jîfîs-lotê Woodlands is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Jîfîs-lotê Woodlands has its economic roots in Indigoplains's export of Linen, but has developed its own chemicals industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.
