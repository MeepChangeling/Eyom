Climate

The Thèhês-kêhê Heath has a yearly average temperature of 18°C (64°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 9°C (48°F). The Thèhês-kêhê Heath receives an average of 148 cm/y (58 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Thèhês-kêhê Heath covers an area of nearly 16971 km2 (10547 mi2), and an average elevation of 4803 m (15757 ft) above sea level.
