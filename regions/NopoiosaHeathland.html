The Nopoiosa Heathland (/ˈnopo/ /ˈjosa/ Common Polity [town; population] [mean]) is a subtropical heathland within the temperate deciduous forest of the Equuscoast region. Nopoiosa Heathland is a relatively typical heathland for Equuscoast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Nopoiosa Heathland has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Nopoiosa Heathland receives an average of 135 cm/y (53 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Nopoiosa Heathland covers an area of nearly 16939 km2 (10527 mi2), and an average elevation of 6093 m (19990 ft) above sea level.
