The Mír Bûzē Brush has a yearly average temperature of 32°C (89°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 36°C (96°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 27°C (80°F). The Mír Bûzē Brush receives an average of 67 cm/y (26 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Mír Bûzē Brush covers an area of nearly 11234 km2 (6981 mi2), and an average elevation of 12111 m (-3215 ft) above sea level.
