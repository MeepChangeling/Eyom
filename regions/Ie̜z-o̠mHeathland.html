The Ie̜z-o̠m Heathland (/je̜z/ /o̠m/ Channelway [path; track; direction (way)] [canal; channel (of water)]) is a temperate heathland on the coast of the Torpenvale region. The Ie̜z-o̠m Heathland is well known for being largely barren of plant life.

Climate

The Ie̜z-o̠m Heathland has a yearly average temperature of 14°C (57°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 2°C (35°F). The Ie̜z-o̠m Heathland receives an average of 152 cm/y (59 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Ie̜z-o̠m Heathland covers an area of nearly 22826 km2 (14186 mi2), and an average elevation of 3014 m (9888 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Ie̜z-o̠m Heathland is overseen by Potentate Givnja Eadwinthos Pononcho, a god serving within Covenant of Irus, the region's principal religion. Approximately 53% of the population self-report as devout. The Ie̜z-o̠m Heathland is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
