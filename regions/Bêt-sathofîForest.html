The Bêt-sathofî Forest (/saˈθofɪ/ Bêt's Kingdom [Proper Noun (name of person)] [civilization]) is a subtropical forest on the coast of the Upper Warding Range region. Bêt-sathofî Forest is a relatively typical forest for Upper Warding Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Bêt-sathofî Forest has a yearly average temperature of 29°C (84°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 34°C (93°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 25°C (77°F). The Bêt-sathofî Forest receives an average of 70 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Bêt-sathofî Forest covers an area of nearly 12449 km2 (7737 mi2), and an average elevation of 10945 m (-7040 ft) above sea level.

The Bêt-sathofî Forest is overseen by Potentate Ghulena Emerawl Æðelwinn, a god serving within The Council of Twelve, the region's principal religion. Approximately 44% of the population self-report as devout. The Bêt-sathofî Forest is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
