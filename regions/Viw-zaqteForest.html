The Viw-zaqte Forest (/viw/ /zaqˈte/ Queensword [crown] [proverb]) is a subtropical forest on the coast of the Upper Warding Range region. The Viw-zaqte Forest is well known for being largely barren of plant life.

Climate

The Viw-zaqte Forest has a yearly average temperature of 22°C (71°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 18°C (64°F). The Viw-zaqte Forest receives an average of 280 cm/y (110 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Viw-zaqte Forest covers an area of nearly 18235 km2 (11333 mi2), and an average elevation of 8570 m (-14832 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Viw-zaqte Forest is overseen by Potentate Wuldos Æthobar Phint, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 26% of the population self-report as devout. The Viw-zaqte Forest is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Viw-zaqte Forest has its economic roots in Upper Warding Range's export of Arcana, but has developed its own innovation industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.
