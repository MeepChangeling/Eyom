The Grön I̽n Holt (/gro˞n/ /i̽n/ Vista [ready] [eye]) is a subtropical holt on the coast of the Rivereach region. Grön I̽n Holt is a relatively typical holt for Rivereach, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Grön I̽n Holt has a yearly average temperature of 12°C (53°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -2°C (29°F). The Grön I̽n Holt receives an average of 77 cm/y (30 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Grön I̽n Holt covers an area of nearly 16455 km2 (10226 mi2), and an average elevation of 3966 m (13011 ft) above sea level.
