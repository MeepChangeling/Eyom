The Qathêt-voshê Expanse (/ˈvoʃɘ/ Qathêt's Vace [Proper Noun (name of person)] [vase; urn]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Upper Warding Range region. Qathêt-voshê Expanse is a relatively typical expanse for Upper Warding Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Qathêt-voshê Expanse has a yearly average temperature of 22°C (71°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 17°C (62°F). The Qathêt-voshê Expanse receives an average of 68 cm/y (26 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Qathêt-voshê Expanse covers an area of nearly 12067 km2 (7499 mi2), and an average elevation of 8535 m (-14947 ft) above sea level.

However, as our culture lacks a taboo for classification of peoples by their biology and such information is highly important for our medical practices amongst other institutions, we have stated the racial demographics of this region. In respect to this universal cultural element, we have split racial classification by culture while keeping the name of each race for our own purposes. Should you see the same race listed more than once in the following section, this is to designate a major cultural division within a race.

The Qathêt-voshê Expanse is's peoples are culturally Wood Elven, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include aesthetic beauty in material goods and architecture and restoring some real or imagined glorious past, as well as a unique rain festival and a science festival.

Local Religion

The Qathêt-voshê Expanse is overseen by Potentate Umus Euantheus Osgainemus, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 28% of the population self-report as devout. The Qathêt-voshê Expanse is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

The Qathêt-voshê Expanse has an Imperial Development Index of 10. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 10 means there are 10 locations within the Qathêt-voshê Expanse which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..

As a courtesy for scholars and agents, the Qathêt-voshê Expanse is rated M1 by Eyom’s banks. Business ventures within the Qathêt-voshê Expanse are not expected to return value in yearly terms.
