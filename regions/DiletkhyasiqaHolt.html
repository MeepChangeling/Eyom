The Diletkhyasiqa Holt (/ˈsiqa/ Diletkhya's Tunic [Proper Noun (name of person)] [blouse]) is a subtropical holt on the coast of the Illa's Panhandle region. Diletkhyasiqa Holt is a relatively typical holt for Illa's Panhandle, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Diletkhyasiqa Holt has a yearly average temperature of 22°C (71°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 20°C (68°F). The Diletkhyasiqa Holt receives an average of 286 cm/y (112 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Diletkhyasiqa Holt covers an area of nearly 20925 km2 (13004 mi2), and an average elevation of 3339 m (10954 ft) above sea level.

People and Culture

The Diletkhyasiqa Holt is home to 586842 people. They break down into the following demographics: 66% Iron Elves, 19% Dwarves, 8% Kivu, 5% Imperial, and ~2% Other.

The Diletkhyasiqa Holt is's peoples are culturally Tauranian, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include vengeance and execution of just vendettas and loyalty to one’s friends, family, and own, as well as a unique science festival and a religious festival.

Iron Elf

The Iron Elf subculture native to the Diletkhyasiqa Holt are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Iron Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of impractical or elaborate role-based clothes and base their sence of individual idenity on ethnic membership. They also place greater cultural emphasus on humanistic reason and “rational” religion and excellence in one’s profession or trade.

Dwarf

The Dwarf subculture native to the Diletkhyasiqa Holt are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Iron Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of culturally-significant jewelry or accessories and base their sence of individual idenity on having or lacking noble blood. They also place greater cultural emphasus on personal sacrifice for one’s causes or purposes and industry and the ability to work tirelessly.

Kivu

The Kivu subculture native to the Diletkhyasiqa Holt are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Iron Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of role or class-specific clothing items and base their sence of individual idenity on hereditary loyalty to a political group. They also place greater cultural emphasus on remembrance of the past and memorializing history and submission to lawful authority.

Imperial

The Imperial subculture native to the Diletkhyasiqa Holt are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Iron Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of tattoos of some cultural significance and base their sence of individual idenity on extended family out to cousins and like kin. They also place greater cultural emphasus on personal development and limit-pushing and cunning and the ability to trick others.

Local Religion

The Diletkhyasiqa Holt is overseen by Potentate Xidona Arandra Phinthos, a god serving within The Seven Titans, the region's principal religion. Approximately 51% of the population self-report as devout. The Diletkhyasiqa Holt is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Diletkhyasiqa Holt has its economic roots in Illa's Panhandle's export of Rubber, but has developed its own hardware industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.

The Diletkhyasiqa Holt has an Imperial Development Index of 17. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 17 means there are 17 locations within the Diletkhyasiqa Holt which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..

As a courtesy for scholars and agents, the Diletkhyasiqa Holt is rated I2 by Eyoms banks. Business ventures within the Diletkhyasiqa Holt are expected to return above capitol with some volatility.
