The Mbö Bök Woodlands (/mbo˞/ /bo˞k/ Bloodletter's [popular] [witch]) are a group of subtropical woodland within the temperate rainforest of the Islestand Coast region. Mbö Bök Woodlands are a relatively typical woodland for Islestand Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Mbö Bök Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 10°C (50°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -8°C (18°F). The Mbö Bök Woodlands receives an average of 297 cm/y (116 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Mbö Bök Woodlands covers an area of nearly 16840 km2 (10466 mi2), and an average elevation of 3115 m (10219 ft) above sea level.
