The Krö He Heathland (/kro˞/ /he/ Crumbling [ill; unwell] [cement; concrete (building material)]) is a temperate heathland within the wetland of the Salt Warrens region. Krö He Heathland is a relatively typical heathland for Salt Warrens, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Krö He Heathland has a yearly average temperature of 14°C (57°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 4°C (39°F). The Krö He Heathland receives an average of 229 cm/y (90 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Krö He Heathland covers an area of nearly 8775 km2 (5453 mi2), and an average elevation of 1489 m (4885 ft) above sea level.
