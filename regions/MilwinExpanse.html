The Milwin Expanse (milwin Expanse Milwin [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical expanse within the temperate rainforest of the Palebay region. The Milwin Expanse is the cultural homeland of the Dwarven peoples.

Climate

The Milwin Expanse has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). The Milwin Expanse receives an average of 284 cm/y (111 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Milwin Expanse covers an area of nearly 19668 km2 (12223 mi2), and an average elevation of 3669 m (12037 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Milwin Expanse is overseen by Potentate Dredara Eardwigid Apoineid, a god serving within The Council of Twelve, the region's principal religion. Approximately 22% of the population self-report as devout. The Milwin Expanse is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
