The Uybo So̠f Prairie has a yearly average temperature of 13°C (55°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing 0°C (32°F). The Uybo So̠f Prairie covers an area of nearly 18159 km2 (11285 mi2), and an average elevation of 3392 m (11128 ft) above sea level.

The Uybo So̠f Prairie is overseen by Potentate Brovphy Despoinemun Alkyoneus, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion.
