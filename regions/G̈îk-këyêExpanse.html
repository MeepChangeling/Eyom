The G̈îk-këyê Expanse (/ˈkëjɘ/ G̈îksleft [Proper Noun (name of person)] [side]) is a subtropical expanse within the wetland of the Southlake region. The G̈îk-këyê Expanse is well known for being home to remarkably few animals.

Climate

The G̈îk-këyê Expanse has a yearly average temperature of 27°C (80°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 32°C (89°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 23°C (73°F). The G̈îk-këyê Expanse receives an average of 239 cm/y (94 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The G̈îk-këyê Expanse covers an area of nearly 6850 km2 (4257 mi2), and an average elevation of 551 m (1807 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The G̈îk-këyê Expanse is overseen by Potentate Munos Orpheof Aphandros, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 29% of the population self-report as devout. The G̈îk-këyê Expanse is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.
