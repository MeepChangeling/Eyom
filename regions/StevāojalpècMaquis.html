The Stevāojalpèc Maquis (Stevāo /ˈʤalpɛc/ Stevāo's Sorrow [Proper Noun (name of person)] [apology]) is a temperate maquis within the temperate rainforest of the Substrate region. Stevāojalpèc Maquis is a relatively typical maquis for Substrate, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Stevāojalpèc Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 17°C (62°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 10°C (50°F). The Stevāojalpèc Maquis receives an average of 291 cm/y (114 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Stevāojalpèc Maquis covers an area of nearly 19311 km2 (12001 mi2), and an average elevation of 1994 m (6541 ft) above sea level.
