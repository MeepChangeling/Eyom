The Më-îhê Fields has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 20°C (68°F). The Më-îhê Fields covers an area of nearly 8527 km2 (5299 mi2), and an average elevation of 4865 m (15961 ft) above sea level.
