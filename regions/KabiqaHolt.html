The Kabiqa Holt (/ka/ /ˈbiqa/ Soulden [life (lifetime); life (soul)] [serial]) is a subtropical holt within the tropical rainforest of the Iron Coast region. Kabiqa Holt is a relatively typical holt for Iron Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Kabiqa Holt has a yearly average temperature of 20°C (68°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 21°C (69°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 20°C (68°F). The Kabiqa Holt receives an average of 283 cm/y (111 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Kabiqa Holt covers an area of nearly 9354 km2 (5813 mi2), and an average elevation of 1438 m (4717 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Kabiqa Holt is overseen by Potentate Nintuna Æðelespoll Lóegarmund, a god serving within The Apprenticeship, the region's principal religion. Approximately 18% of the population self-report as devout. The Kabiqa Holt is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Kabiqa Holt is noteworthy for its economic independence from Iron Coast's export of Industrial Goods. Kabiqa Holt is particularly renound for its dyes industry. It is also has a noteworthy dungeoneering industry.

The Kabiqa Holt has an Imperial Development Index of 35. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 35 means there are 35 locations within the Kabiqa Holt which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
