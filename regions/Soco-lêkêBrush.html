The Soco-lêkê Brush (/ˈsoco/ /ˈlɘkɘ/ Losergarden [loser] [garden]) is a subtropical brush within the temperate rainforest of the Silicahilands region. Soco-lêkê Brush is a relatively typical brush for Silicahilands, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Soco-lêkê Brush has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 18°C (64°F). The Soco-lêkê Brush receives an average of 270 cm/y (106 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Soco-lêkê Brush covers an area of nearly 18059 km2 (11223 mi2), and an average elevation of 6192 m (20314 ft) above sea level.
