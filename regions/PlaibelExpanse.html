The Plaibel Expanse (plaibel Expanse Plaibel [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Upper Warding Range region. Plaibel Expanse is a relatively typical expanse for Upper Warding Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Plaibel Expanse has a yearly average temperature of 31°C (87°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 36°C (96°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 27°C (80°F). The Plaibel Expanse receives an average of 66 cm/y (25 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Plaibel Expanse covers an area of nearly 15573 km2 (9678 mi2), and an average elevation of 12770 m (-1053 ft) above sea level.

The Plaibel Expanse is overseen by Potentate Budum Pardwulfsta Conthus, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 25% of the population self-report as devout. The Plaibel Expanse is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
