The Lutoguqo Holt (/ˈluto/ /ˈguqo/ Folkshift [broom] [custom; stereotype]) is a subtropical holt within the wetland of the Glowing Sands region. Lutoguqo Holt is a relatively typical holt for Glowing Sands, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Lutoguqo Holt has a yearly average temperature of 21°C (69°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). The Lutoguqo Holt receives an average of 218 cm/y (85 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Lutoguqo Holt covers an area of nearly 17565 km2 (10916 mi2), and an average elevation of 1104 m (3622 ft) above sea level.
