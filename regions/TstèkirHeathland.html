The Tstèkir Heathland (Tstè /kir/ Tstè the Councilor's [Proper Noun (name of person)] [counsel]) is a temperate heathland on the coast of the Foxvale region. Tstèkir Heathland is a relatively typical heathland for Foxvale, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Tstèkir Heathland has a yearly average temperature of 13°C (55°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 2°C (35°F). The Tstèkir Heathland receives an average of 155 cm/y (61 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Tstèkir Heathland covers an area of nearly 12896 km2 (8014 mi2), and an average elevation of 4905 m (16092 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Tstèkir Heathland is overseen by Potentate Brolena Grodelm Alentheof, a god serving within Covenant of Irus, the region's principal religion. Approximately 42% of the population self-report as devout. The Tstèkir Heathland is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Tstèkir Heathland is noteworthy for its economic independence from Foxvale's export of Steel. Tstèkir Heathland is particularly renound for its musical instruments industry. It is also has a noteworthy mining industry.
