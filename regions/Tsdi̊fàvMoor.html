The Tsdi̊fàv Moor (Tsdi̊ /fəv/ Tsdi̊'s Line [Proper Noun (name of person)] [train]) is a subtropical moor within the wetland of the Midlund Coast region. Tsdi̊fàv Moor is a relatively typical moor for Midlund Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Tsdi̊fàv Moor has a yearly average temperature of 18°C (64°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 21°C (69°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 15°C (59°F). The Tsdi̊fàv Moor receives an average of 225 cm/y (88 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Tsdi̊fàv Moor covers an area of nearly 7634 km2 (4744 mi2), and an average elevation of 1390 m (4560 ft) above sea level.
