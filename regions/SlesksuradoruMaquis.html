The Slesksuradoru Maquis (/ˈdoru/ Slesksura's Advice [Proper Noun (name of person)] [counsel]) is a temperate maquis within the temperate rainforest of the Iron Coast region. The Slesksuradoru Maquis is well known for its thriving abundance of plant life.

Climate

The Slesksuradoru Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 22°C (71°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Slesksuradoru Maquis receives an average of 284 cm/y (111 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Slesksuradoru Maquis covers an area of nearly 13336 km2 (8288 mi2), and an average elevation of 1292 m (4238 ft) above sea level.
