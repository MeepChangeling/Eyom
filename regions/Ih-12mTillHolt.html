The Ih-12m Till Holt (/till/ Ih-12m's Elevator [Proper Noun (name of person)] [elevator]) is a subtropical holt on the coast of the Hearthcoast region. Ih-12m Till Holt is a relatively typical holt for Hearthcoast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Ih-12m Till Holt has a yearly average temperature of 29°C (84°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 32°C (89°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 25°C (77°F). The Ih-12m Till Holt receives an average of 274 cm/y (107 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Ih-12m Till Holt covers an area of nearly 13040 km2 (8104 mi2), and an average elevation of 3639 m (11938 ft) above sea level.
