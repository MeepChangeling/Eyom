The Trukskhrajiqepe Brush (/ˈqepe/ Trukskhraji's Gash [Proper Noun (name of person)] [wound; trauma; scar]) is a subtropical brush within the wetland of the Shattered Peninsula region. Trukskhrajiqepe Brush is a relatively typical brush for Shattered Peninsula, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Trukskhrajiqepe Brush has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). The Trukskhrajiqepe Brush receives an average of 225 cm/y (88 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Trukskhrajiqepe Brush covers an area of nearly 7837 km2 (4870 mi2), and an average elevation of 1286 m (4219 ft) above sea level.
