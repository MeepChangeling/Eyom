The Bf-10c Zebro Basin (/ˈzebro/ Bf-10c's Proverb [Proper Noun (name of person)] [proverb]) is a subtropical basin on the coast of the Flanged Coast region. Bf-10c Zebro Basin is a relatively typical basin for Flanged Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Bf-10c Zebro Basin has a yearly average temperature of 29°C (84°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 32°C (89°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 25°C (77°F). The Bf-10c Zebro Basin receives an average of 210 cm/y (82 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Bf-10c Zebro Basin covers an area of nearly 13137 km2 (8164 mi2), and an average elevation of 4784 m (15695 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Bf-10c Zebro Basin is overseen by Potentate Phosoi Aphen Hemerðan, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 31% of the population self-report as devout. The Bf-10c Zebro Basin is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Bf-10c Zebro Basin is a negative return economy zone at this time. It has an abundence of Fish, though infrastructure to this region of Flanged Coast is not developed enough to support any major local industry.
