Climate

The Ok-mbo̠n Prairie has a yearly average temperature of 6°C (42°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant -13°C (9°F). The Ok-mbo̠n Prairie receives an average of 149 cm/y (58 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Ok-mbo̠n Prairie covers an area of nearly 17860 km2 (11100 mi2), and an average elevation of 4614 m (15137 ft) above sea level.
