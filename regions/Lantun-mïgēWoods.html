The Lantun-mïgē Woods (/lanˈtuŋ/ /mɪˈgæ̝/ Everharvest [next] [harvest]) are a group of temperate woods within the wetland of the Northlake region. Lantun-mïgē Woods are a relatively typical woods for Northlake, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Lantun-mïgē Woods has a yearly average temperature of 19°C (66°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 14°C (57°F). The Lantun-mïgē Woods receives an average of 230 cm/y (90 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Lantun-mïgē Woods covers an area of nearly 21569 km2 (13405 mi2), and an average elevation of 1277 m (4189 ft) above sea level.
