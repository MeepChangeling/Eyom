The Hudworth Brush (hudworth /brʌʃ/ Hudworth [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical brush on the coast of the Upper Warding Range region. Hudworth Brush is a relatively typical brush for Upper Warding Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Hudworth Brush has a yearly average temperature of 30°C (86°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 34°C (93°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 26°C (78°F). The Hudworth Brush receives an average of 69 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Hudworth Brush covers an area of nearly 13153 km2 (8174 mi2), and an average elevation of 12048 m (-3422 ft) above sea level.
