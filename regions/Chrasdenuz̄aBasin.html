The Chrasdenuz̄a Basin (/deˈnuz̚a/ Chrassrucksack [Proper Noun (name of person)] [backpack]) is a subtropical basin on the coast of the Brownmarsh region. Chrasdenuz̄a Basin is a relatively typical basin for Brownmarsh, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Chrasdenuz̄a Basin has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 23°C (73°F). The Chrasdenuz̄a Basin receives an average of 135 cm/y (53 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Chrasdenuz̄a Basin covers an area of nearly 15937 km2 (9904 mi2), and an average elevation of 5707 m (18723 ft) above sea level.

The Chrasdenuz̄a Basin is home to 446185 people. They break down into the following demographics: 60% Tauric, 25% Fae, 8% Elf, 5% Grey Elf, and ~2% Other.

The Chrasdenuz̄a Basin is's peoples are culturally Tauranian, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include justice and fairness between people and raw strength and personal prowess, as well as a unique arcane festival and a winter festival.

They also place greater cultural emphasus on building things in service of their posterity and personal sacrifice for one’s causes or purposes.

The Chrasdenuz̄a Basin is overseen by Potentate Drione Despon Mneid, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 37% of the population self-report as devout. The Chrasdenuz̄a Basin is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
