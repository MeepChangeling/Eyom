The Vdral Na Forest (/glaː/ /na/ Emperor's [purple] [use]) is a subtropical forest on the coast of the Icereef region. The Vdral Na Forest is well known for being largely barren of plant life.

Climate

The Vdral Na Forest has a yearly average temperature of 7°C (44°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -13°C (9°F). The Vdral Na Forest receives an average of 294 cm/y (115 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Vdral Na Forest covers an area of nearly 21563 km2 (13401 mi2), and an average elevation of 2517 m (8257 ft) above sea level.

The Vdral Na Forest is's peoples are culturally Wareneese, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include vengeance and execution of just vendettas and piety and devotion to the gods, as well as a unique spirit festival and a storytelling festival.

Local Religion

The Vdral Na Forest is overseen by Potentate Qhivx Muireanos Kassandi, a god serving within Tol's Children, the region's principal religion. Approximately 14% of the population self-report as devout. The Vdral Na Forest is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Vdral Na Forest is noteworthy for its economic independence from Icereef's export of Fish. Vdral Na Forest is particularly renound for its rail industry. It is also has a noteworthy innovation industry.
