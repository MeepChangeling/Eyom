The Yaah-selyìkhě Heath has a yearly average temperature of 27°C (80°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 32°C (89°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 23°C (73°F). The Yaah-selyìkhě Heath covers an area of nearly 14804 km2 (9200 mi2), and an average elevation of 2315 m (7595 ft) above sea level.
