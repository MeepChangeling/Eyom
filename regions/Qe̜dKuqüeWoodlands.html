The Qe̜d Kuqüe Woodlands (/qe̜d/ /kuq˔/ Picturesque [comic] [present (time)]) are a group of subtropical woodland within the wetland of the Kiteshield Peninsula region. Qe̜d Kuqüe Woodlands are a relatively typical woodland for Kiteshield Peninsula, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Qe̜d Kuqüe Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 18°C (64°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 12°C (53°F). The Qe̜d Kuqüe Woodlands receives an average of 218 cm/y (85 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Qe̜d Kuqüe Woodlands covers an area of nearly 4759 km2 (2957 mi2), and an average elevation of 3876 m (12716 ft) above sea level.
