The Witchflower Woods (witchflower /wʊdz/ Witchflower [Translation Unavailable]) are a group of subtropical woods on the coast of the Greenrange region. The Witchflower Woods are named after an old folktale, of which some evidence proving the stories both true and false has been found.

Climate

The Witchflower Woods has a yearly average temperature of 22°C (71°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 18°C (64°F). The Witchflower Woods receives an average of 141 cm/y (55 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Witchflower Woods covers an area of nearly 16941 km2 (10528 mi2), and an average elevation of 8523 m (-14987 ft) above sea level.
