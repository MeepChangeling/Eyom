The Qahî Vabi Forest (/ˈvabi/ Qahî's Daughter [Proper Noun (name of person)] [daughter]) is a subtropical forest within the wetland of the Substrate region. Qahî Vabi Forest is a relatively typical forest for Substrate, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Qahî Vabi Forest has a yearly average temperature of 20°C (68°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 12°C (53°F). The Qahî Vabi Forest receives an average of 221 cm/y (87 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Qahî Vabi Forest covers an area of nearly 15643 km2 (9722 mi2), and an average elevation of 1698 m (5570 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Qahî Vabi Forest is overseen by Potentate Udon Glaukos Oenonawn, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 17% of the population self-report as devout. The Qahî Vabi Forest is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Qahî Vabi Forest has its economic roots in Substrate's export of Agriculture, but has developed its own rail industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.
