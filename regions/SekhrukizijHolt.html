The Sekhrukizij Holt (/zij/ Sekhruki the Earthshaker's [Proper Noun (name of person)] [seismograph]) is a temperate holt within the temperate rainforest of the Equuscoast region. Sekhrukizij Holt is a relatively typical holt for Equuscoast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Sekhrukizij Holt has a yearly average temperature of 20°C (68°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 15°C (59°F). The Sekhrukizij Holt receives an average of 302 cm/y (118 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Sekhrukizij Holt covers an area of nearly 27377 km2 (17014 mi2), and an average elevation of 1076 m (3530 ft) above sea level.
