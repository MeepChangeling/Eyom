The Vyilenjinuna Maquis (/ˈnuna/ Vyilenji's Sale [Proper Noun (name of person)] [sale]) is a subtropical maquis on the coast of the Anvil Badlands region. Vyilenjinuna Maquis is a relatively typical maquis for Anvil Badlands, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Vyilenjinuna Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 22°C (71°F). The Vyilenjinuna Maquis receives an average of 72 cm/y (28 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Vyilenjinuna Maquis covers an area of nearly 10021 km2 (6228 mi2), and an average elevation of 4363 m (14314 ft) above sea level.
