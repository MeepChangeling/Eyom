The Rockbloom Holt (rockbloom /hoʊlt/ Rockbloom [Translation Unavailable]) is a temperate holt on the coast of the Pressingstone region. Rockbloom Holt is a relatively typical holt for Pressingstone, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Rockbloom Holt has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). The Rockbloom Holt receives an average of 72 cm/y (28 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Rockbloom Holt covers an area of nearly 13857 km2 (8612 mi2), and an average elevation of 7641 m (-17880 ft) above sea level.

The Rockbloom Holt is overseen by Potentate Grera Idanditana Despone, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 12% of the population self-report as devout. The Rockbloom Holt is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Rockbloom Holt has its economic roots in Pressingstone's export of Plant Oils, but has developed its own dyes industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.
