The Mo̠l-ntet Holt (/mo̠l/ /ntet/ Biscuit [pastry] [skeptic; cynic]) is a temperate holt within the temperate rainforest of the Gnashed Coast region. Mo̠l-ntet Holt is a relatively typical holt for Gnashed Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Mo̠l-ntet Holt has a yearly average temperature of 6°C (42°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold -11°C (13°F). The Mo̠l-ntet Holt receives an average of 284 cm/y (111 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Mo̠l-ntet Holt covers an area of nearly 11845 km2 (7361 mi2), and an average elevation of 4098 m (13444 ft) above sea level.

The Mo̠l-ntet Holt is's peoples are culturally Gnollish, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include ascetic unworldliness and pious poverty and subtlety and indirectness of action, as well as a unique fire festival and a history festival.
