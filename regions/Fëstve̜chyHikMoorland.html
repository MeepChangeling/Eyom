The Fëstve̜chy Hik Moorland (/ˈfɛstve̜c/ /hik/ Oddrealm [weird; strange; bizarre; peculiar] [triangle]) is a temperate moorland within the wetland of the Kiteshield Peninsula region. Fëstve̜chy Hik Moorland is a relatively typical moorland for Kiteshield Peninsula, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Fëstve̜chy Hik Moorland has a yearly average temperature of 18°C (64°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 13°C (55°F). The Fëstve̜chy Hik Moorland receives an average of 228 cm/y (89 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Fëstve̜chy Hik Moorland covers an area of nearly 3503 km2 (2177 mi2), and an average elevation of 1488 m (4881 ft) above sea level.

The Fëstve̜chy Hik Moorland is home to 86513 people. They break down into the following demographics: 52% Gnolls, 28% Iron Elves, 5% Dwarves, 6% Dwarves, 5% Grey Elf, 2% Imperial, and ~2% Other.

The Fëstve̜chy Hik Moorland is's peoples are culturally Gnollish, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include justice and fairness between people and social progress toward some eventual utopia, as well as a unique religious festival and a spring festival.

They also place greater cultural emphasus on raw strength and personal prowess and loyalty to one’s friends, family, and own.

Local Religion

The Fëstve̜chy Hik Moorland is overseen by Potentate Tuxta Orphrona Damona, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 31% of the population self-report as devout. The Fëstve̜chy Hik Moorland is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Fëstve̜chy Hik Moorland is predominantly economicly reiant on Kiteshield Penencila's export of Oil, and has no outstanding industries byond what is required for its role in that process.

The Fëstve̜chy Hik Moorland has an Imperial Development Index of 11. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 11 means there are 11 locations within the Fëstve̜chy Hik Moorland which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
