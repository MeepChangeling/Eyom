The Kuz Miygu Basin (/kuz/ /ˈmijgu/ Warbands [naive] [military]) is a subtropical basin on the coast of the Lower Warding Range region. The Kuz Miygu Basin is well known for being home to remarkably few animals.

Climate

The Kuz Miygu Basin has a yearly average temperature of 26°C (78°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 22°C (71°F). The Kuz Miygu Basin receives an average of 68 cm/y (26 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Kuz Miygu Basin covers an area of nearly 7423 km2 (4613 mi2), and an average elevation of 6543 m (21466 ft) above sea level.

The Kuz Miygu Basin is overseen by Potentate Umus Ethann Mandroniu, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 33% of the population self-report as devout. The Kuz Miygu Basin is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Kuz Miygu Basin is predominantly economicly reiant on Lower Warding Range's export of Undead, and has no outstanding industries byond what is required for its role in that process.
