The Paseaqisiz̄u Maquis (/ˈpase/ /ˌaqiˈsiz̚u/ Fairnoon [noon; midday] [attractive]) is a subtropical maquis within the temperate deciduous forest of the Iron Coast region. Paseaqisiz̄u Maquis is a relatively typical maquis for Iron Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Paseaqisiz̄u Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 23°C (73°F). The Paseaqisiz̄u Maquis receives an average of 144 cm/y (56 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Paseaqisiz̄u Maquis covers an area of nearly 21186 km2 (13167 mi2), and an average elevation of 1673 m (5488 ft) above sea level.
