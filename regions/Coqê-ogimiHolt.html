The Coqê-ogimi Holt (/ˈcoqɘ/ /oˈgimi/ Leatherstool [rough; raw] [accessory; fixture]) is a subtropical holt on the coast of the Thousand Plantations region. Coqê-ogimi Holt is a relatively typical holt for Thousand Plantations, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Coqê-ogimi Holt has a yearly average temperature of 19°C (66°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 11°C (51°F). The Coqê-ogimi Holt receives an average of 149 cm/y (58 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Coqê-ogimi Holt covers an area of nearly 8721 km2 (5420 mi2), and an average elevation of 4385 m (14386 ft) above sea level.
