The Mböt I̽nböō Heathland (/mbo˞t/ /i̽nˈbo˞oː/ Lavanderhalo [violet] [orbit]) is a subtropical heathland within the wetland of the Aegispoint region. Mböt I̽nböō Heathland is a relatively typical heathland for Aegispoint, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Mböt I̽nböō Heathland has a yearly average temperature of 7°C (44°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant -10°C (14°F). The Mböt I̽nböō Heathland receives an average of 210 cm/y (82 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Mböt I̽nböō Heathland covers an area of nearly 7129 km2 (4430 mi2), and an average elevation of 2497 m (8192 ft) above sea level.
