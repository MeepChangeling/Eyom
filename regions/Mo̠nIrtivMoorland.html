The Mo̠n Irtiv Moorland (/mo̠n/ /ˈirtiv/ Giantbee [tall] [bee]) is a subtropical moorland within the wetland of the Riverpack Coast region. The Mo̠n Irtiv Moorland is the home of the ocean-capable steamboat.

Climate

The Mo̠n Irtiv Moorland has a yearly average temperature of 17°C (62°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 8°C (46°F). The Mo̠n Irtiv Moorland receives an average of 217 cm/y (85 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Mo̠n Irtiv Moorland covers an area of nearly 12323 km2 (7658 mi2), and an average elevation of 955 m (3133 ft) above sea level.
