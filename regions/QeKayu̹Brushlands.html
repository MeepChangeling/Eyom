The Qe Kayu̹ Brushlands (/qe/ /ˈkaju̹/ Turquoise Patriot's [turquoise] [patriot]) are a group of subtropical brushland on the coast of the Raked Plains region. The Qe Kayu̹ Brushlands are well known for being home to remarkably few animals.

Climate

The Qe Kayu̹ Brushlands has a yearly average temperature of 20°C (68°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 12°C (53°F). The Qe Kayu̹ Brushlands receives an average of 204 cm/y (80 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Qe Kayu̹ Brushlands covers an area of nearly 9242 km2 (5743 mi2), and an average elevation of 2834 m (9297 ft) above sea level.
