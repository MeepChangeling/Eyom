The Vi̽r-en Maquis (/vi̽r/ /en/ Armoredgaze [arsenal] [look; appearance (looks)]) is a temperate maquis within the temperate rainforest of the Islestand Coast region. Vi̽r-en Maquis is a relatively typical maquis for Islestand Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Vi̽r-en Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 10°C (50°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -4°C (25°F). The Vi̽r-en Maquis receives an average of 284 cm/y (111 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Vi̽r-en Maquis covers an area of nearly 18499 km2 (11497 mi2), and an average elevation of 1775 m (5823 ft) above sea level.

The Vi̽r-en Maquis is home to 389457 people. They break down into the following demographics: 54% Wareneese, 21% Hobgoblins, 10% Wareneese, 9% Elf, 4% Kobold, and ~2% Other.

The Vi̽r-en Maquis is's peoples are culturally Wareneese, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include ethnic purity of blood and culture and dominating and possessing other people, as well as a unique folk festival and a history festival.

The Vi̽r-en Maquis is overseen by Potentate Oenar Kassandros Muirentus, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 52% of the population self-report as devout. The Vi̽r-en Maquis is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
