The Gisoqopuki Moorland (/ˈgiso/ /qoˈpuki/ Reatiner [boyfriend] [loyal]) is a subtropical moorland on the coast of the Shimmering Spires region. Gisoqopuki Moorland is a relatively typical moorland for Shimmering Spires, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Gisoqopuki Moorland has a yearly average temperature of 22°C (71°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 20°C (68°F). The Gisoqopuki Moorland receives an average of 284 cm/y (111 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Gisoqopuki Moorland covers an area of nearly 22569 km2 (14026 mi2), and an average elevation of 5790 m (18996 ft) above sea level.
