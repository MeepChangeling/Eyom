The Cancona Moor (cancona /mʊr/ Cancona [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical moor within the wetland of the Silicahilands region. Cancona Moor is a relatively typical moor for Silicahilands, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Cancona Moor has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 30°C (86°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Cancona Moor receives an average of 213 cm/y (83 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Cancona Moor covers an area of nearly 24988 km2 (15530 mi2), and an average elevation of 4694 m (15400 ft) above sea level.

The Cancona Moor is home to 229264 people. They break down into the following demographics: 64% Constructs, 11% Kitsune, 10% Gnolls, 7% Tanuki, 5% Grey Elf, 1% Kobold, and ~2% Other.

The Cancona Moor is's peoples are culturally Constructi, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include pacifism and peaceful resolution of problems and loyalty to one’s friends, family, and own, as well as a unique summer festival and a folk festival.

Construct

The Construct subculture native to the Cancona Moor are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Construct culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of color choices with social meaning to them and base their sence of individual idenity on warbands or civic military service groups. They also place greater cultural emphasus on education and knowledge-seeking and pacifism and peaceful resolution of problems.

Local Religion

The Cancona Moor is overseen by Potentate Kenrena Corairey Aphaliassan, a god serving within Covenant of Irus, the region's principal religion. Approximately 12% of the population self-report as devout. The Cancona Moor is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Cancona Moor is predominantly economicly reiant on Silicahilands's export of Construct Componants, and has no outstanding industries byond what is required for its role in that process.

The Cancona Moor has an Imperial Development Index of 9. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 9 means there are 9 locations within the Cancona Moor which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..

As a courtesy for scholars and agents, the Cancona Moor is rated M1 by Eyom’s banks. Business ventures within the Cancona Moor are not expected to return value in yearly terms.
