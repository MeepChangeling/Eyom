The Nunaqedukhe Heathland (/ˈnuna/ /qeˈduxe/ Tradeport [sale] [foreign]) is a temperate heathland within the temperate rainforest of the Shimmering Spires region. Nunaqedukhe Heathland is a relatively typical heathland for Shimmering Spires, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Nunaqedukhe Heathland has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Nunaqedukhe Heathland receives an average of 268 cm/y (105 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Nunaqedukhe Heathland covers an area of nearly 11726 km2 (7287 mi2), and an average elevation of 5689 m (18664 ft) above sea level.
