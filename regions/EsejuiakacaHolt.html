The Esejuiakaca Holt (/eˈseʤu/ /jaˈkaca/ Skyplate [plate; platter] [eclipse]) is a subtropical holt within the temperate rainforest of the Glowing Sands region. The Esejuiakaca Holt is well known for its thriving abundance of plant life.

Climate

The Esejuiakaca Holt has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Esejuiakaca Holt receives an average of 288 cm/y (113 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Esejuiakaca Holt covers an area of nearly 13190 km2 (8197 mi2), and an average elevation of 1477 m (4845 ft) above sea level.
