The Fo̠ldle̜n-bök Forest has a yearly average temperature of 10°C (50°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 22°C (71°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -2°C (29°F). The Fo̠ldle̜n-bök Forest receives an average of 293 cm/y (115 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Fo̠ldle̜n-bök Forest covers an area of nearly 8751 km2 (5438 mi2), and an average elevation of 2834 m (9297 ft) above sea level.
