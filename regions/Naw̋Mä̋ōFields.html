Climate

The Naw̋ Mä̋ō Fields has a yearly average temperature of 18°C (64°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 13°C (55°F). The Naw̋ Mä̋ō Fields receives an average of 287 cm/y (112 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Naw̋ Mä̋ō Fields covers an area of nearly 8108 km2 (5039 mi2), and an average elevation of 1494 m (4901 ft) above sea level.
