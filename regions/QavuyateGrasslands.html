The Qavuyate Grasslands (/ˈqavu/ /aʤ/ Beverpelt [owner] [brown]) are a group of subtropical grassland within the wetland of the Yastan Peninsula region. Qavuyate Grasslands are a relatively typical grassland for Yastan Peninsula, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Qavuyate Grasslands has a yearly average temperature of 12°C (53°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a cool 16°C (60°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 8°C (46°F). The Qavuyate Grasslands receives an average of 210 cm/y (82 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Qavuyate Grasslands covers an area of nearly 10242 km2 (6365 mi2), and an average elevation of 2733 m (8966 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Qavuyate Grasslands are overseen by Potentate Ityx Weandronis Siontusan, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 10% of the population self-report as devout. The Qavuyate Grasslands is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Qavuyate Grasslands is noteworthy for its economic independence from Yastan Penencila's export of Furs. Qavuyate Grasslands is particularly renound for its dyes industry. It is also has a noteworthy potions industry.

The Qavuyate Grasslands has an Imperial Development Index of 37. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 37 means there are 37 locations within the Qavuyate Grasslands which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
