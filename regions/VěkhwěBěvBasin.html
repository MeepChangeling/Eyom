The Věkhwě Běv Basin (/ˈvɛxwɛ/ /bɛv/ Blooddrinker [red] [vampire]) is a subtropical basin within the temperate deciduous forest of the Eastlake region. Věkhwě Běv Basin is a relatively typical basin for Eastlake, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Věkhwě Běv Basin has a yearly average temperature of 26°C (78°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 30°C (86°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 23°C (73°F). The Věkhwě Běv Basin receives an average of 145 cm/y (57 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Věkhwě Běv Basin covers an area of nearly 21517 km2 (13372 mi2), and an average elevation of 3152 m (10341 ft) above sea level.
