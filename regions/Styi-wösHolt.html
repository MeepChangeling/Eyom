The Styi-wös Holt (/stji/ /wo˞s/ Neverday [calendar] [laugh]) is a temperate holt within the temperate rainforest of the Icereef region. Styi-wös Holt is a relatively typical holt for Icereef, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Styi-wös Holt has a yearly average temperature of 9°C (48°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -9°C (16°F). The Styi-wös Holt receives an average of 288 cm/y (113 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Styi-wös Holt covers an area of nearly 11446 km2 (7113 mi2), and an average elevation of 3112 m (10209 ft) above sea level.
