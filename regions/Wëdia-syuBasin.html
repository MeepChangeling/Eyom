The Wëdia-syu Basin (/ˈwɛdja/ /sju/ Powerdrain [arrow (sign)] [battery]) is a subtropical basin on the coast of the Icereef region. Wëdia-syu Basin is a relatively typical basin for Icereef, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Wëdia-syu Basin has a yearly average temperature of 2°C (35°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold -23°C (-9°F). The Wëdia-syu Basin receives an average of 297 cm/y (116 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Wëdia-syu Basin covers an area of nearly 16422 km2 (10206 mi2), and an average elevation of 3312 m (10866 ft) above sea level.
