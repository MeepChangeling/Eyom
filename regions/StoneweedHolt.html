The Stoneweed Holt (stoneweed /hoʊlt/ Stoneweed [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical holt on the coast of the Ancestor's Range region. The Stoneweed Holt is the poorest local region in all of Eyom.

Climate

The Stoneweed Holt has a yearly average temperature of 30°C (86°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 35°C (95°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 26°C (78°F). The Stoneweed Holt receives an average of 202 cm/y (79 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Stoneweed Holt covers an area of nearly 9190 km2 (5711 mi2), and an average elevation of 15358 m (7437 ft) above sea level.
