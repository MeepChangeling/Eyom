Climate

The Lîpíê-ihîkê Woods has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 18°C (64°F). The Lîpíê-ihîkê Woods receives an average of 141 cm/y (55 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Lîpíê-ihîkê Woods covers an area of nearly 16154 km2 (10039 mi2), and an average elevation of 4426 m (14520 ft) above sea level.
