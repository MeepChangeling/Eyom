The Triqtkrukraqiyo Heath (/ˈqijo/ Triqtkrukra's Doubt [Proper Noun (name of person)] [skeptic; cynic]) is a temperate heath within the wetland of the Shattered Peninsula region. Triqtkrukraqiyo Heath is a relatively typical heath for Shattered Peninsula, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Triqtkrukraqiyo Heath has a yearly average temperature of 20°C (68°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 14°C (57°F). The Triqtkrukraqiyo Heath receives an average of 232 cm/y (91 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Triqtkrukraqiyo Heath covers an area of nearly 4794 km2 (2979 mi2), and an average elevation of 1140 m (3740 ft) above sea level.
