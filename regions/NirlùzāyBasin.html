The Nirlùzāy Basin (/ˈnirlʊ/ /zəˑj/ Deepwealth [bargain] [deep]) is a temperate basin within the temperate rainforest of the Substrate region. Nirlùzāy Basin is a relatively typical basin for Substrate, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Nirlùzāy Basin has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 10°C (50°F). The Nirlùzāy Basin receives an average of 315 cm/y (124 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Nirlùzāy Basin covers an area of nearly 14544 km2 (9039 mi2), and an average elevation of 1123 m (3684 ft) above sea level.
