The Mōntlo Fiako̠s Forest (/ˈmoːntlo/ /ˈfjako̠s/ Longround [gradual] [oval]) is a subtropical forest within the tropical rainforest of the Riverpack Coast region. Mōntlo Fiako̠s Forest is a relatively typical forest for Riverpack Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Mōntlo Fiako̠s Forest has a yearly average temperature of 17°C (62°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 9°C (48°F). The Mōntlo Fiako̠s Forest receives an average of 297 cm/y (116 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Mōntlo Fiako̠s Forest covers an area of nearly 10078 km2 (6263 mi2), and an average elevation of 2412 m (7913 ft) above sea level.

The Mōntlo Fiako̠s Forest is's peoples are culturally Goblin, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include submission to the collective will or culture and ethnic purity of blood and culture, as well as a unique spring festival and a relic festival.

They also place greater cultural emphasus on submission to the collective will or culture and building things in service of their posterity.

Local Religion

The Mōntlo Fiako̠s Forest is overseen by Potentate Erena Xana Siontus, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 55% of the population self-report as devout. The Mōntlo Fiako̠s Forest is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Mōntlo Fiako̠s Forest is noteworthy for its economic independence from Riverpack Coast's export of Steel. Mōntlo Fiako̠s Forest is particularly renound for its potions industry. It is also has a noteworthy leather industry.
