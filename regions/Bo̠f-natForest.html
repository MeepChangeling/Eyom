The Bo̠f-nat Forest (/bo̠f/ /nat/ Wageoner's [wagon] [message]) is a temperate forest within the wetland of the Salt Warrens region. Bo̠f-nat Forest is a relatively typical forest for Salt Warrens, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Bo̠f-nat Forest has a yearly average temperature of 15°C (59°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 6°C (42°F). The Bo̠f-nat Forest receives an average of 229 cm/y (90 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Bo̠f-nat Forest covers an area of nearly 9220 km2 (5730 mi2), and an average elevation of 1921 m (6302 ft) above sea level.
