The Gre Röt Woods (/gre/ /ro˞t/ Bankers [gold] [priority]) are a group of subtropical woods on the coast of the Timberlund region. The Gre Röt Woods are the home of the bimetal blade.

Climate

The Gre Röt Woods has a yearly average temperature of 9°C (48°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -7°C (20°F). The Gre Röt Woods receives an average of 305 cm/y (120 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Gre Röt Woods covers an area of nearly 6901 km2 (4288 mi2), and an average elevation of 4303 m (14117 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Gre Röt Woods are overseen by Potentate Ades Aphandrodi Bacint, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 29% of the population self-report as devout. The Gre Röt Woods is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Gre Röt Woods has its economic roots in Timberlund's export of Timber, but has developed its own dungeoneering industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.
