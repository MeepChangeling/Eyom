The Poisonwood Forest (poisonwood /ˈfɔrəst/ Poisonwood [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical forest on the coast of the Ancestor's Range region. Poisonwood Forest is a relatively typical forest for Ancestor's Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Poisonwood Forest has a yearly average temperature of 32°C (89°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 36°C (96°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 27°C (80°F). The Poisonwood Forest receives an average of 66 cm/y (25 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Poisonwood Forest covers an area of nearly 8342 km2 (5184 mi2), and an average elevation of 11183 m (-6260 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Poisonwood Forest is overseen by Potentate Qhohthos Oura Mnemer, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 42% of the population self-report as devout. The Poisonwood Forest is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Poisonwood Forest has its economic roots in Ancestor's Range's export of Stone, but has developed its own innovation industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.

The Poisonwood Forest has an Imperial Development Index of 18. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 18 means there are 18 locations within the Poisonwood Forest which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
