The Vîyâlà Qova Fields (/vɪːˈjɑlə/ /ˈqova/ Wounded [absent] [doctor]) are a group of subtropical fields on the coast of the Mullberry Dales region. The Vîyâlà Qova Fields are the cultural homeland of the Sylvin peoples.

Climate

The Vîyâlà Qova Fields has a yearly average temperature of 18°C (64°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 10°C (50°F). The Vîyâlà Qova Fields receives an average of 139 cm/y (54 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Vîyâlà Qova Fields covers an area of nearly 21923 km2 (13625 mi2), and an average elevation of 3427 m (11243 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Vîyâlà Qova Fields are overseen by Potentate Earis Ourawl Hecatean, a god serving within Iron Elf Shamanism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 49% of the population self-report as devout. The Vîyâlà Qova Fields is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.
