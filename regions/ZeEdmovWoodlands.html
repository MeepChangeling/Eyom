The Ze Edmov Woodlands (/ze/ /ˈedmov/ Supportive [available] [bra]) are a group of subtropical woodland on the coast of the Iron Coast region. Ze Edmov Woodlands are a relatively typical woodland for Iron Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Ze Edmov Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 26°C (78°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 22°C (71°F). The Ze Edmov Woodlands receives an average of 69 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Ze Edmov Woodlands covers an area of nearly 19234 km2 (11954 mi2), and an average elevation of 5715 m (18750 ft) above sea level.
