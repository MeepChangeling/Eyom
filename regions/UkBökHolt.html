The Uk Bök Holt (/uc/ /bo˞k/ Madwitch [crazy; insane] [witch]) is a subtropical holt within the wetland of the Icereef region. Uk Bök Holt is a relatively typical holt for Icereef, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Uk Bök Holt has a yearly average temperature of 11°C (51°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -2°C (29°F). The Uk Bök Holt receives an average of 216 cm/y (85 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Uk Bök Holt covers an area of nearly 12777 km2 (7940 mi2), and an average elevation of 1422 m (4665 ft) above sea level.
