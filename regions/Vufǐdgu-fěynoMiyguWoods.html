Climate

The Vufǐdgu-fěyno Miygu Woods has a yearly average temperature of 27°C (80°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 30°C (86°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 23°C (73°F). The Vufǐdgu-fěyno Miygu Woods receives an average of 294 cm/y (115 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Vufǐdgu-fěyno Miygu Woods covers an area of nearly 16330 km2 (10149 mi2), and an average elevation of 5303 m (17398 ft) above sea level.

The Vufǐdgu-fěyno Miygu Woods are overseen by Potentate Mogenar Eoghantho Euann, a god serving within The Council of Twelve, the region's principal religion. Approximately 47% of the population self-report as devout. The Vufǐdgu-fěyno Miygu Woods is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.
