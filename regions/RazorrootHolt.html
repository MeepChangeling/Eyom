The Razorroot Holt (razorroot /hoʊlt/ Razoroot [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical holt on the coast of the Greenrange region. Razorroot Holt is a relatively typical holt for Greenrange, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Razorroot Holt has a yearly average temperature of 29°C (84°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 34°C (93°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 25°C (77°F). The Razorroot Holt receives an average of 69 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Razorroot Holt covers an area of nearly 8988 km2 (5586 mi2), and an average elevation of 11948 m (-3750 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Razorroot Holt is overseen by Potentate Shakohr Wealia Oenthniu, a god serving within Tol's Children, the region's principal religion. Approximately 21% of the population self-report as devout. The Razorroot Holt is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Razorroot Holt is noteworthy for its economic independence from Greenrange's export of Metals. Razorroot Holt is particularly renound for its divine legal industry. It is also has a noteworthy mining industry.
