The Nō̄ Bǔ̄y Prairie (/nʊ̄/ /bǔːiː/ Scarsetimber [rare] [wood; pole]) is a subtropical prairie within the taiga of the Northlake region. Nō̄ Bǔ̄y Prairie is a relatively typical prairie for Northlake, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Nō̄ Bǔ̄y Prairie has a yearly average temperature of 27°C (80°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 32°C (89°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 23°C (73°F). The Nō̄ Bǔ̄y Prairie receives an average of 72 cm/y (28 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Nō̄ Bǔ̄y Prairie covers an area of nearly 9535 km2 (5926 mi2), and an average elevation of 2594 m (8510 ft) above sea level.

The Nō̄ Bǔ̄y Prairie is's peoples are culturally Sylvanian, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include submission to lawful authority and exploring the unknown and discovering secrets, as well as a unique history festival and a spirit festival.

The Nō̄ Bǔ̄y Prairie is overseen by Potentate Vetella Aphrodi Leoptos, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 35% of the population self-report as devout. The Nō̄ Bǔ̄y Prairie is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
