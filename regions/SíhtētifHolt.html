The Síhtētif Holt (Síhtē /tif/ Síhtē's Stock [Proper Noun (name of person)] [stock]) is a subtropical holt on the coast of the Farmbasin region. Síhtētif Holt is a relatively typical holt for Farmbasin, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Síhtētif Holt has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 7°C (44°F). The Síhtētif Holt receives an average of 147 cm/y (57 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Síhtētif Holt covers an area of nearly 16050 km2 (9975 mi2), and an average elevation of 4780 m (15682 ft) above sea level.

The Síhtētif Holt is home to 1783251 people. They break down into the following demographics: 50% Wood Elves, 12% Taurans, 23% Wareneese, 6% Wareneese, 7% Tanuki, and ~2% Other.

The Síhtētif Holt is's peoples are culturally Wood Elven, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include leadership and charisma in the group and loyalty to one’s friends, family, and own, as well as a unique food festival and a summer festival.

Wood Elf

The Wood Elf subculture native to the Síhtētif Holt are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of color choices with social meaning to them and base their sence of individual idenity on astrologically-determined birth groups. They also place greater cultural emphasus on cunning and the ability to trick others and vengeance and execution of just vendettas.

Tauran

The Tauran subculture native to the Síhtētif Holt are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of tattoos of some cultural significance and base their sence of individual idenity on having or lacking noble blood. They also place greater cultural emphasus on membership in an elite bloodline or caste and piety and devotion to the gods.

Rattu

The Rattu subculture native to the Síhtētif Holt are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of impractical or elaborate role-based clothes and base their sence of individual idenity on voluntary mutual-assistance brotherhoods. They also place greater cultural emphasus on harmony with nature and existing life and ascetic unworldliness and pious poverty.

Quenn

The Quenn subculture native to the Síhtētif Holt are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of impractical or elaborate role-based clothes and base their sence of individual idenity on far-flung clans of affiliated families. They also place greater cultural emphasus on ascetic unworldliness and pious poverty and honor and maintaining one’s integrity.

Tanuki

The Tanuki subculture native to the Síhtētif Holt are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of culturally-significant jewelry or accessories and base their sence of individual idenity on numerous castes of hereditary workers. They also place greater cultural emphasus on purging evil and expelling the wicked and prosperity and accruing material wealth.

Local Religion

The Síhtētif Holt is overseen by Potentate Brovphy Weanos Weandronw, a god serving within Macketersism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 41% of the population self-report as devout. The Síhtētif Holt is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Síhtētif Holt is predominantly economicly reiant on Farmbasin's export of Slaves, and has no outstanding industries byond what is required for its role in that process.

The Síhtētif Holt has an Imperial Development Index of 6. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 6 means there are 6 locations within the Síhtētif Holt which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..

As a courtesy for scholars and agents, the Síhtētif Holt is rated M1 by Eyom’s banks. Business ventures within the Síhtētif Holt are not expected to return value in yearly terms.
