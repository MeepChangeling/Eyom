Climate

The Qu̹hû Ràgu̽rû Steppe has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 8°C (46°F). The Qu̹hû Ràgu̽rû Steppe receives an average of 292 cm/y (114 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Qu̹hû Ràgu̽rû Steppe covers an area of nearly 19087 km2 (11862 mi2), and an average elevation of 6086 m (19967 ft) above sea level.
