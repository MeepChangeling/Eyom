The Sër-syëd Brush (/sɛr/ /sjɛd/ Mealwind [dinner] [air; weather]) is a subtropical brush on the coast of the Islestand Coast region. The Sër-syëd Brush is the coldest brush in all of Eyom.

Climate

The Sër-syëd Brush has a yearly average temperature of 8°C (46°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool -10°C (14°F). The Sër-syëd Brush receives an average of 285 cm/y (112 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Sër-syëd Brush covers an area of nearly 27886 km2 (17331 mi2), and an average elevation of 3657 m (11998 ft) above sea level.
