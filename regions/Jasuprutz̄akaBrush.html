The Jasuprutz̄aka Brush (/ˈz̚aca/ Jasuprut's Reach [Proper Noun (name of person)] [limit]) is a temperate brush within the wetland of the Diamond Basin region. Jasuprutz̄aka Brush is a relatively typical brush for Diamond Basin, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Jasuprutz̄aka Brush has a yearly average temperature of 18°C (64°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 22°C (71°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 14°C (57°F). The Jasuprutz̄aka Brush receives an average of 218 cm/y (85 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Jasuprutz̄aka Brush covers an area of nearly 11337 km2 (7045 mi2), and an average elevation of 1371 m (4498 ft) above sea level.

The Jasuprutz̄aka Brush is home to 84246 people. They break down into the following demographics: 53% Tauric, 18% Kitsune, 14% Fae, 5% Kivu, 7% Elf, 1% Imperial, and ~2% Other.

The Jasuprutz̄aka Brush is's peoples are culturally Tauranian, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include unity and elimination of group differences and social progress toward some eventual utopia, as well as a unique music festival and a comedy festival.

Tauric

The Tauric subculture native to the Jasuprutz̄aka Brush are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Tauric culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of tattoos of some cultural significance and base their sence of individual idenity on voluntary mutual-assistance brotherhoods. They also place greater cultural emphasus on sexual license and wantonness and courage and valiance in danger.

They also place greater cultural emphasus on vengeance and execution of just vendettas and sexual license and wantonness.

They also place greater cultural emphasus on excellence in one’s profession or trade and loyalty to one’s friends, family, and own.
