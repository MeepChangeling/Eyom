The Lûna Ru̹hi Râre Brush (/ˈlʊna/ /ˈru̹hi ˈrɑre/ Sleepyflume [passive] [geography]) is a temperate brush on the coast of the Raked Plains region. Lûna Ru̹hi Râre Brush is a relatively typical brush for Raked Plains, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Lûna Ru̹hi Râre Brush has a yearly average temperature of 21°C (69°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 30°C (86°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 13°C (55°F). The Lûna Ru̹hi Râre Brush receives an average of 70 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Lûna Ru̹hi Râre Brush covers an area of nearly 7516 km2 (4671 mi2), and an average elevation of 2097 m (6879 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Lûna Ru̹hi Râre Brush is overseen by Potentate Brukdia Osgaina Aiarmun, a god serving within Covenant of Irus, the region's principal religion. Approximately 53% of the population self-report as devout. The Lûna Ru̹hi Râre Brush is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
