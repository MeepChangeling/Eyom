The Màvifor Basin (Màvi /for/ Màvi's flame [Proper Noun (name of person)] [flame]) is a subtropical basin on the coast of the Foxvale region. Màvifor Basin is a relatively typical basin for Foxvale, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Màvifor Basin has a yearly average temperature of 14°C (57°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 2°C (35°F). The Màvifor Basin receives an average of 75 cm/y (29 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Màvifor Basin covers an area of nearly 9110 km2 (5661 mi2), and an average elevation of 4636 m (15209 ft) above sea level.

The Màvifor Basin is home to 648271 people. They break down into the following demographics: 53% Wood Elves, 17% Wareneese, 14% Gnolls, 1% Hobgoblins, 8% Hobgoblins, 5% Imperial, and ~2% Other.

The Màvifor Basin is's peoples are culturally Wood Elven, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include cunning and the ability to trick others and justice and fairness between people, as well as a unique science festival and a history festival.

Wood Elf

The Wood Elf subculture native to the Màvifor Basin are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of significant scent or perfume uses and base their sence of individual idenity on trade guilds specific to particular roles. They also place greater cultural emphasus on personal development and limit-pushing and vengeance and execution of just vendettas.

Rattu

The Rattu subculture native to the Màvifor Basin are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of painted skin markings that sometimes change and base their sence of individual idenity on numerous castes of hereditary workers. They also place greater cultural emphasus on sharing wealth and goods with others and unity and elimination of group differences.

Gnoll

The Gnoll subculture native to the Màvifor Basin are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of tattoos of some cultural significance and base their sence of individual idenity on educational ties to institutions or traditions. They also place greater cultural emphasus on subtlety and indirectness of action and ascetic unworldliness and pious poverty.

The Hobgoblin subculture native to the Màvifor Basin are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. They also place greater cultural emphasus on ethnic purity of blood and culture and personal indulgence and luxuriant pleasure.

Quenn

The Quenn subculture native to the Màvifor Basin are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. Local Religion

The Màvifor Basin is overseen by Potentate Yruer Aphrodi Aphrononiu, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 54% of the population self-report as devout. The Màvifor Basin is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Màvifor Basin is noteworthy for its economic independence from Foxvale's export of Steel. Màvifor Basin is particularly renound for its glass industry. It is also has a noteworthy textiles industry.

The Màvifor Basin has an Imperial Development Index of 28. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 28 means there are 28 locations within the Màvifor Basin which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..

As a courtesy for scholars and agents, the Màvifor Basin is rated I1 by Eyom’s banks. Business ventures within the Màvifor Basin are expected to return above capitol at low risk.
