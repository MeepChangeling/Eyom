The Fifkîn-tato Basin (/ˈtato/ Fifkîn's Apparatus [Proper Noun (name of person)] [appliance]) is a temperate basin on the coast of the Silicahilands region. Fifkîn-tato Basin is a relatively typical basin for Silicahilands, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Fifkîn-tato Basin has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 18°C (64°F). The Fifkîn-tato Basin receives an average of 217 cm/y (85 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Fifkîn-tato Basin covers an area of nearly 19483 km2 (12108 mi2), and an average elevation of 4314 m (14153 ft) above sea level.

The Fifkîn-tato Basin is home to 509752 people. They break down into the following demographics: 51% Constructs, 28% Constructs, 6% Iron Elves, 5% Elf, 8% Kobold, and ~2% Other.

The Fifkîn-tato Basin is's peoples are culturally Constructi, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include prosperity and accruing material wealth and pacifism and peaceful resolution of problems, as well as a unique rain festival and a summer festival.

Local Religion

The Fifkîn-tato Basin is overseen by Potentate Krekana Ælrea Ourawy, a god serving within Fawresm, the region's principal religion. Approximately 13% of the population self-report as devout. The Fifkîn-tato Basin is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Fifkîn-tato Basin is noteworthy for its economic independence from Silicahilands's export of Construct Componants. Fifkîn-tato Basin is particularly renound for its cement industry. It is also has a noteworthy ceramic industry.

The Fifkîn-tato Basin has an Imperial Development Index of 39. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 39 means there are 39 locations within the Fifkîn-tato Basin which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
