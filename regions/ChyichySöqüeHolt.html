The Chyichy Söqüe Holt has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 4°C (39°F). The Chyichy Söqüe Holt covers an area of nearly 14396 km2 (8947 mi2), and an average elevation of 2702 m (8864 ft) above sea level.
