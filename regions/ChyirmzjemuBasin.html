The Chyirmzjemu Basin (/ˈʤemu/ Chyirmz's Awe [Proper Noun (name of person)] [awe]) is a subtropical basin within the wetland of the Shattered Peninsula region. Chyirmzjemu Basin is a relatively typical basin for Shattered Peninsula, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Chyirmzjemu Basin has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 22°C (71°F). The Chyirmzjemu Basin receives an average of 216 cm/y (85 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Chyirmzjemu Basin covers an area of nearly 20066 km2 (12471 mi2), and an average elevation of 2982 m (9783 ft) above sea level.

The Chyirmzjemu Basin is home to 380808 people. They break down into the following demographics: 54% Iron Elves, 14% Gnolls, 17% Goblins, 7% Tanuki, 5% Kobold, 1% Imperial, and ~2% Other.

The Chyirmzjemu Basin is's peoples are culturally Wareneese, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include beauty and seductive charm and remembrance of the past and memorializing history, as well as a unique folk festival and a music festival.

Iron Elf

The Iron Elf subculture native to the Chyirmzjemu Basin are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Iron Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of painted skin markings that sometimes change and base their sence of individual idenity on lineages based on ancient hero-ancestors. They also place greater cultural emphasus on discipline and obedience to the law and prosperity and accruing material wealth.

They also place greater cultural emphasus on exploring the unknown and discovering secrets and building things in service of their posterity.

Imperial

Local Religion

The Chyirmzjemu Basin is overseen by Potentate Uros Frenoneus Maponeus, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 56% of the population self-report as devout. The Chyirmzjemu Basin is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Chyirmzjemu Basin is noteworthy for its economic independence from Shattered Peninsula's export of Metals. Chyirmzjemu Basin is particularly renound for its chemicals industry. It is also has a noteworthy engines industry.

The Chyirmzjemu Basin has an Imperial Development Index of 29. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 29 means there are 29 locations within the Chyirmzjemu Basin which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..

As a courtesy for scholars and agents, the Chyirmzjemu Basin is rated I1 by Eyom’s banks. Business ventures within the Chyirmzjemu Basin are expected to return above capitol at low risk.
