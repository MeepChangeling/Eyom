The Rivercaster Heath (rivercaster /hiθ/ Rivercaster [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical heath within the temperate rainforest of the Polymer Coast region. Rivercaster Heath is a relatively typical heath for Polymer Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Rivercaster Heath has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 30°C (86°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Rivercaster Heath receives an average of 293 cm/y (115 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Rivercaster Heath covers an area of nearly 13250 km2 (8234 mi2), and an average elevation of 1516 m (4973 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Rivercaster Heath is overseen by Potentate Ghukphion Nothobarmun Partheof, a god serving within The Council of Twelve, the region's principal religion. Approximately 55% of the population self-report as devout. The Rivercaster Heath is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
