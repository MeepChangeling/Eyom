The Applegamau Grasslands (applegamau /ˈgræˌslændz/ Applegamau [Translation Unavailable]) are a group of subtropical grassland on the coast of the Highpeaks region. Applegamau Grasslands are a relatively typical grassland for Highpeaks, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Applegamau Grasslands has a yearly average temperature of 28°C (82°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 32°C (89°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 23°C (73°F). The Applegamau Grasslands receives an average of 69 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Applegamau Grasslands covers an area of nearly 13525 km2 (8405 mi2), and an average elevation of 8428 m (-15298 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Applegamau Grasslands are overseen by Potentate Tuhena Desponwyda Damoklenos, a god serving within The Council of Twelve, the region's principal religion. Approximately 29% of the population self-report as devout. The Applegamau Grasslands is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
