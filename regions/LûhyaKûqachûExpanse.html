The Lûhya Kûqachû Expanse (/kʊˈqaʧʊ/ Lûhya's Behavior [Proper Noun (name of person)] [behavior]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Weardvale region. Lûhya Kûqachû Expanse is a relatively typical expanse for Weardvale, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Lûhya Kûqachû Expanse has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 7°C (44°F). The Lûhya Kûqachû Expanse receives an average of 304 cm/y (119 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Lûhya Kûqachû Expanse covers an area of nearly 16453 km2 (10225 mi2), and an average elevation of 5937 m (19478 ft) above sea level.

The Lûhya Kûqachû Expanse is's peoples are culturally Wareneese, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include ascetic unworldliness and pious poverty and harmony with nature and existing life, as well as a unique history festival and a wine festival.

Local Religion

The Lûhya Kûqachû Expanse is overseen by Potentate Ugysus Manos Muireandra, a god serving within The Apprenticeship, the region's principal religion. Approximately 53% of the population self-report as devout. The Lûhya Kûqachû Expanse is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
