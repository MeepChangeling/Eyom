The Bêfa-píêyiqë Grasslands (/ˈbɘfa/ /p˔ɘˈjiqë/ Mudfield [nasty] [ring]) are a group of subtropical grassland on the coast of the Thousand Plantations region. Bêfa-píêyiqë Grasslands are a relatively typical grassland for Thousand Plantations, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Bêfa-píêyiqë Grasslands has a yearly average temperature of 18°C (64°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 10°C (50°F). The Bêfa-píêyiqë Grasslands receives an average of 75 cm/y (29 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Bêfa-píêyiqë Grasslands covers an area of nearly 10255 km2 (6373 mi2), and an average elevation of 6236 m (20459 ft) above sea level.
