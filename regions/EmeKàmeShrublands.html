The Eme Kàme Shrublands (/ˈeme/ /ˈkəme/ Localcane [local] [cane]) are a group of subtropical shrubland on the coast of the Wyldrange region. Eme Kàme Shrublands are a relatively typical shrubland for Wyldrange, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Eme Kàme Shrublands has a yearly average temperature of 18°C (64°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 10°C (50°F). The Eme Kàme Shrublands receives an average of 66 cm/y (25 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Eme Kàme Shrublands covers an area of nearly 4658 km2 (2894 mi2), and an average elevation of 3756 m (12322 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Eme Kàme Shrublands are overseen by Potentate Qragyn Ælreya Ecgberht, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 17% of the population self-report as devout. The Eme Kàme Shrublands is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
