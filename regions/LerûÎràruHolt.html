The Lerû Îràru Holt (/ˈlerʊ/ /ɪːˈrəru/ Flirting Ice [shelf; rack; ledge] [noise; rumor; gossip]) is a subtropical holt on the coast of the Wyldrange region. The Lerû Îràru Holt is well known for being home to remarkably few animals.

Climate

The Lerû Îràru Holt has a yearly average temperature of 18°C (64°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 9°C (48°F). The Lerû Îràru Holt receives an average of 65 cm/y (25 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Lerû Îràru Holt covers an area of nearly 9485 km2 (5894 mi2), and an average elevation of 4153 m (13625 ft) above sea level.

The Lerû Îràru Holt is overseen by Potentate Gutbus Prig Eógandros, a god serving within The Council of Twelve, the region's principal religion. Approximately 31% of the population self-report as devout. The Lerû Îràru Holt is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.
