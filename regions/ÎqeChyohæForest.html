Climate

The Îqe Chyohæ Forest has a yearly average temperature of 19°C (66°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 10°C (50°F). The Îqe Chyohæ Forest covers an area of nearly 23717 km2 (14740 mi2), and an average elevation of 3708 m (12165 ft) above sea level.
