The Northgrass Woods (northgrass /wʊdz/ Northgrass [Translation Unavailable]) are a group of subtropical woods on the coast of the Greenrange region. Northgrass Woods are a relatively typical woods for Greenrange, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Northgrass Woods has a yearly average temperature of 32°C (89°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 36°C (96°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 27°C (80°F). The Northgrass Woods receives an average of 70 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Northgrass Woods covers an area of nearly 6784 km2 (4216 mi2), and an average elevation of 11759 m (-4370 ft) above sea level.

The Northgrass Woods are home to 58801 people. They break down into the following demographics: 69% Sylvin, 16% Gnolls, 5% Gnolls, 7% Elf, 1% Tanuki, and ~2% Other.

The Northgrass Woods are's peoples are culturally Sylvanian, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include submission to the collective will or culture and ascetic unworldliness and pious poverty, as well as a unique rain festival and a folk festival.

Sylvin

The Sylvin subculture native to the Northgrass Woods are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Sylvin culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of piercings, whether minor or elaborate and base their sence of individual idenity on warbands or civic military service groups. They also place greater cultural emphasus on discipline and obedience to the law and vengeance and execution of just vendettas.

Local Religion

The Northgrass Woods are overseen by Potentate Udon Pononcho Ethelm, a god serving within Fawresm, the region's principal religion. Approximately 39% of the population self-report as devout. The Northgrass Woods is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Northgrass Woods is noteworthy for its economic independence from Greenrange's export of Metals. Northgrass Woods is particularly renound for its chemicals industry. It is also has a noteworthy divine legal industry.

The Northgrass Woods has an Imperial Development Index of 27. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 27 means there are 27 locations within the Northgrass Woods which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..

As a courtesy for scholars and agents, the Northgrass Woods is rated I1 by Eyom’s banks. Business ventures within the Northgrass Woods are expected to return above capitol at low risk.
