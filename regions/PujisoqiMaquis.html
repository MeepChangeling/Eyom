The Pujisoqi Maquis (/ˈpuʤi/ /ˈsoqi/ Carbonrod [pencil] [aroused (sexually)]) is a subtropical maquis within the wetland of the Iron Coast region. Pujisoqi Maquis is a relatively typical maquis for Iron Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Pujisoqi Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 26°C (78°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 24°C (75°F). The Pujisoqi Maquis receives an average of 222 cm/y (87 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Pujisoqi Maquis covers an area of nearly 9955 km2 (6187 mi2), and an average elevation of 1886 m (6187 ft) above sea level.
