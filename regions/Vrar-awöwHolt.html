The Vrar-awöw Holt (/vrar/ /ˈawo˞w/ Sockpainter [sock] [painter]) is a subtropical holt within the wetland of the Stalfijord region. Vrar-awöw Holt is a relatively typical holt for Stalfijord, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Vrar-awöw Holt has a yearly average temperature of 6°C (42°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant -12°C (11°F). The Vrar-awöw Holt receives an average of 215 cm/y (84 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Vrar-awöw Holt covers an area of nearly 15055 km2 (9356 mi2), and an average elevation of 4542 m (14901 ft) above sea level.
