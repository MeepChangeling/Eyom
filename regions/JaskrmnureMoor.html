Climate

The Jaskrmnure Moor has a yearly average temperature of 26°C (78°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 25°C (77°F). The Jaskrmnure Moor receives an average of 277 cm/y (109 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Jaskrmnure Moor covers an area of nearly 20733 km2 (12885 mi2), and an average elevation of 1559 m (5114 ft) above sea level.
