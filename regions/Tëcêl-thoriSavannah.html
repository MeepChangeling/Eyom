The Tëcêl-thori Savannah (/ˈʃori/ Tëcêlberry [Proper Noun (name of person)] [berry; bean]) is a subtropical savanna within the temperate rainforest of the Greatbay region. Tëcêl-thori Savannah is a relatively typical savanna for Greatbay, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Tëcêl-thori Savannah has a yearly average temperature of 20°C (68°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 15°C (59°F). The Tëcêl-thori Savannah receives an average of 287 cm/y (112 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Tëcêl-thori Savannah covers an area of nearly 18250 km2 (11342 mi2), and an average elevation of 1564 m (5131 ft) above sea level.
