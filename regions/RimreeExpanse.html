The Rimree Expanse (rimree Expanse Rimree [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Ancestor's Range region. Rimree Expanse is a relatively typical expanse for Ancestor's Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Rimree Expanse has a yearly average temperature of 30°C (86°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 34°C (93°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 25°C (77°F). The Rimree Expanse receives an average of 196 cm/y (77 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Rimree Expanse covers an area of nearly 17279 km2 (10738 mi2), and an average elevation of 11672 m (-4655 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Rimree Expanse is overseen by Potentate Themdur Æþelwigid Hyacinthos, a god serving within The Council of Twelve, the region's principal religion. Approximately 23% of the population self-report as devout. The Rimree Expanse is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
