The Kiklajsuzu Heath (/ˈkiklaʤ/ /ˈsuzu/ Lazy Twister [gyroscope] [calm; sober]) is a subtropical heath within the wetland of the Yastan Peninsula region. The Kiklajsuzu Heath is the political heartlands of the City-state of Konei.

Climate

The Kiklajsuzu Heath has a yearly average temperature of -1°C (31°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant -25°C (-13°F). The Kiklajsuzu Heath receives an average of 198 cm/y (77 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Kiklajsuzu Heath covers an area of nearly 10794 km2 (6708 mi2), and an average elevation of 5091 m (16702 ft) above sea level.
