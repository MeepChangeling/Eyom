The Zdri̽k-slō Shrublands (/zdri̽k/ /sloː/ Manyparts [acronym] [bicycle]) are a group of subtropical shrubland within the taiga of the Stalfijord region. Zdri̽k-slō Shrublands are a relatively typical shrubland for Stalfijord, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Zdri̽k-slō Shrublands has a yearly average temperature of 4°C (39°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool -16°C (4°F). The Zdri̽k-slō Shrublands receives an average of 69 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Zdri̽k-slō Shrublands covers an area of nearly 7983 km2 (4961 mi2), and an average elevation of 2946 m (9665 ft) above sea level.
