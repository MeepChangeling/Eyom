The Vj-29n Fègwě Holt (/ˈfɤgwɛ/ Vj-29n's Elixir [Proper Noun (name of person)] [elixir]) is a temperate holt within the temperate rainforest of the Cedarcoast region. Vj-29n Fègwě Holt is a relatively typical holt for Cedarcoast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Vj-29n Fègwě Holt has a yearly average temperature of 29°C (84°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 32°C (89°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 25°C (77°F). The Vj-29n Fègwě Holt receives an average of 282 cm/y (111 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Vj-29n Fègwě Holt covers an area of nearly 27892 km2 (17334 mi2), and an average elevation of 2515 m (8251 ft) above sea level.
