The Canvale Brushlands (canvale brushlands Canvale [Translation Unavailable]) are a group of subtropical brushland on the coast of the Highpeaks region. Canvale Brushlands are a relatively typical brushland for Highpeaks, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Canvale Brushlands has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). The Canvale Brushlands receives an average of 71 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Canvale Brushlands covers an area of nearly 7575 km2 (4707 mi2), and an average elevation of 7627 m (-17926 ft) above sea level.
