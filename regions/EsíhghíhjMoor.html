The Esíhghíhj Moor (/ˈesɪ̞g/ /hɪ̞ʤ/ People's Voice [discussion] [cool (fashionable)]) is a subtropical moor within the temperate rainforest of the Blackstone Coast region. Esíhghíhj Moor is a relatively typical moor for Blackstone Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Esíhghíhj Moor has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 6°C (42°F). The Esíhghíhj Moor receives an average of 282 cm/y (111 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Esíhghíhj Moor covers an area of nearly 15524 km2 (9648 mi2), and an average elevation of 2202 m (7224 ft) above sea level.
