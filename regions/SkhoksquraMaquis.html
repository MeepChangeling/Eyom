The Skhoksqura Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Skhoksqura Maquis covers an area of nearly 8682 km2 (5395 mi2), and an average elevation of 2092 m (6863 ft) above sea level.
