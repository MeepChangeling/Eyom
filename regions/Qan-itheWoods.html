The Qan-ithe Woods (/ˈiθe/ Qan's Script [Proper Noun (name of person)] [page]) are a group of subtropical woods on the coast of the Irusthrone region. Qan-ithe Woods are a relatively typical woods for Irusthrone, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Qan-ithe Woods has a yearly average temperature of 22°C (71°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 17°C (62°F). The Qan-ithe Woods receives an average of 141 cm/y (55 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Qan-ithe Woods covers an area of nearly 8085 km2 (5024 mi2), and an average elevation of 6573 m (-21384 ft) above sea level.

The Qan-ithe Woods are home to 302052 people. They break down into the following demographics: 63% Wood Elves, 15% Goblins, 7% Iron Elves, 8% Grey Elf, 5% Kobold, and ~2% Other.

The Qan-ithe Woods are's peoples are culturally Wood Elven, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include social progress toward some eventual utopia and harmony with nature and existing life, as well as a unique wine festival and a sun festival.

Wood Elf

The Wood Elf subculture native to the Qan-ithe Woods are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of worn weapons, tools or trade implements and base their sence of individual idenity on warbands or civic military service groups. They also place greater cultural emphasus on building things in service of their posterity and prosperity and accruing material wealth.

They also place greater cultural emphasus on humanistic reason and “rational” religion and industry and the ability to work tirelessly.

Kobold

The Kobold subculture native to the Qan-ithe Woods are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. They also place greater cultural emphasus on unity and elimination of group differences and restoring some real or imagined glorious past.

The Qan-ithe Woods are overseen by Potentate Dredara Eoghalaea Béibniu, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 51% of the population self-report as devout. The Qan-ithe Woods is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Qan-ithe Woods is noteworthy for its economic independence from Irusthrone's export of Suffering. Qan-ithe Woods is particularly renound for its medicine industry. It is also has a noteworthy leather industry.
