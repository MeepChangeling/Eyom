The Pikuiova Brush (/ˈpiku/ /ˈjova/ Highhelm [hat; helmet] [conscious]) is a subtropical brush on the coast of the Iron Coast region. Pikuiova Brush is a relatively typical brush for Iron Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Pikuiova Brush has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 22°C (71°F). The Pikuiova Brush receives an average of 145 cm/y (57 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Pikuiova Brush covers an area of nearly 19574 km2 (12165 mi2), and an average elevation of 2136 m (7007 ft) above sea level.
