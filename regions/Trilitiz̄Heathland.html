The Trilitiz̄ Heathland (/tiz̚/ Trili's Record [Proper Noun (name of person)] [phonograph]) is a subtropical heathland within the wetland of the Iron Coast region. The Trilitiz̄ Heathland is well known for its thriving abundance of plant life.

Climate

The Trilitiz̄ Heathland has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 20°C (68°F). The Trilitiz̄ Heathland receives an average of 205 cm/y (80 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Trilitiz̄ Heathland covers an area of nearly 10275 km2 (6385 mi2), and an average elevation of 5483 m (17988 ft) above sea level.
