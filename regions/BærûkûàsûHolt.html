The Bærûkû àsû Holt has a yearly average temperature of 17°C (62°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 8°C (46°F). The Bærûkû àsû Holt is home to 121960 people. They break down into the following demographics: 66% Wareneese, 19% Taurans, 6% Grey Elf, 6% Tanuki, 1% Kobold, and ~2% Other.

The Bærûkû àsû Holt is's peoples are culturally Wareneese, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include aesthetic beauty in material goods and architecture and excellence in one’s profession or trade, as well as a unique rain festival and a science festival.

The Bærûkû àsû Holt is overseen by Potentate Gatuna Goronw Conos, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 24% of the population self-report as devout. The Bærûkû àsû Holt is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
