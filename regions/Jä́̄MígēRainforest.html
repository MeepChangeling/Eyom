The Jä́̄ Mígē Rainforest (/ɱɪ́gæ/ Jä́̄sfolk [Proper Noun (name of person)] [descendant; offspring]) is a temperate rainforest within the wetland of the Pressingstone region. Jä́̄ Mígē Rainforest is a relatively typical rainforest for Pressingstone, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Jä́̄ Mígē Rainforest has a yearly average temperature of 26°C (78°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 30°C (86°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Jä́̄ Mígē Rainforest receives an average of 210 cm/y (82 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Jä́̄ Mígē Rainforest covers an area of nearly 12642 km2 (7857 mi2), and an average elevation of 5728 m (18792 ft) above sea level.
