The Preschill Maquis (preschill maquis Preschill [Translation Unavailable]) is a temperate maquis on the coast of the Pressingstone region. The Preschill Maquis is well known for being largely barren of plant life.

Climate

The Preschill Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). The Preschill Maquis receives an average of 148 cm/y (58 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Preschill Maquis covers an area of nearly 19339 km2 (12019 mi2), and an average elevation of 5656 m (18556 ft) above sea level.
