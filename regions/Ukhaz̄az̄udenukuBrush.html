The Ukhaz̄az̄udenuku Brush (/uˈxaz̚a/ /ˌz̚udeˈnuku/ Deadend [avenue; artery] [dead]) is a subtropical brush within the temperate rainforest of the Shattered Peninsula region. Ukhaz̄az̄udenuku Brush is a relatively typical brush for Shattered Peninsula, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Ukhaz̄az̄udenuku Brush has a yearly average temperature of 22°C (71°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 18°C (64°F). The Ukhaz̄az̄udenuku Brush receives an average of 290 cm/y (114 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Ukhaz̄az̄udenuku Brush covers an area of nearly 11519 km2 (7159 mi2), and an average elevation of 2448 m (8031 ft) above sea level.
