The Tz-44j Fækhwæb Steppe has a yearly average temperature of 26°C (78°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 24°C (75°F). The Tz-44j Fækhwæb Steppe covers an area of nearly 20081 km2 (12480 mi2), and an average elevation of 2204 m (7230 ft) above sea level.
