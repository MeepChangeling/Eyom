Climate

The Moko̠ Smöt Woods has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a cool 19°C (66°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 12°C (53°F). The Moko̠ Smöt Woods covers an area of nearly 11761 km2 (7309 mi2), and an average elevation of 1286 m (4219 ft) above sea level.
