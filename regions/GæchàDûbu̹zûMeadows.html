The Gæchà Dûbu̹zû Meadows has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 7°C (44°F). The Gæchà Dûbu̹zû Meadows receives an average of 136 cm/y (53 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring.
