The Skra Srokko Brush (/skra/ /ˈsrokko/ Manyrafts [brown] [raft]) is a subtropical brush on the coast of the Filigree Coast region. Skra Srokko Brush is a relatively typical brush for Filigree Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Skra Srokko Brush has a yearly average temperature of 13°C (55°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 1°C (33°F). The Skra Srokko Brush receives an average of 155 cm/y (61 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Skra Srokko Brush covers an area of nearly 22693 km2 (14103 mi2), and an average elevation of 2315 m (7595 ft) above sea level.
