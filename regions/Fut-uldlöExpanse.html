The Fut-uldlö Expanse (/fut/ /ˈuldlo˞/ Coin [money] [medal]) is a temperate expanse on the coast of the Salt Warrens region. Fut-uldlö Expanse is a relatively typical expanse for Salt Warrens, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Fut-uldlö Expanse has a yearly average temperature of 7°C (44°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant -10°C (14°F). The Fut-uldlö Expanse receives an average of 234 cm/y (92 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Fut-uldlö Expanse covers an area of nearly 10318 km2 (6412 mi2), and an average elevation of 4642 m (15229 ft) above sea level.
