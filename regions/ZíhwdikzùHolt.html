The Zíhwdikzù Holt is home to 1327522 people. They break down into the following demographics: 55% Wood Elves, 22% Iron Elves, 8% Gnolls, 7% Kivu, 6% Imperial, and ~2% Other.

The Zíhwdikzù Holt is's peoples are culturally Wood Elven, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include personal development and limit-pushing and submission to lawful authority, as well as a unique food festival and a theater festival.

Local Religion

The Zíhwdikzù Holt is overseen by Potentate Khaglo Eoghalaukos Phintos, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 15% of the population self-report as devout. The Zíhwdikzù Holt is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Zíhwdikzù Holt has its economic roots in Farmbasin's export of Slaves, but has developed its own education industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.

The Zíhwdikzù Holt has an Imperial Development Index of 21. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 21 means there are 21 locations within the Zíhwdikzù Holt which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
