The Näz Zabehngzÿ Prairie (/nəz/ /zabɪ̘ngˈzi/ Wornshell [sick; ill; unwell] [composite]) is a subtropical prairie within the wetland of the Polymer Coast region. Näz Zabehngzÿ Prairie is a relatively typical prairie for Polymer Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Näz Zabehngzÿ Prairie has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). The Näz Zabehngzÿ Prairie receives an average of 220 cm/y (86 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Näz Zabehngzÿ Prairie covers an area of nearly 11915 km2 (7405 mi2), and an average elevation of 1921 m (6302 ft) above sea level.
