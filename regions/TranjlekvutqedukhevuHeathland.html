The Tranjlekvutqedukhevu Heathland (/ˌqeduˈxevu/ Tranjlekvut's [Proper Noun (name of person)] [foreigner]) is a temperate heathland within the wetland of the Flanged Coast region. Tranjlekvutqedukhevu Heathland is a relatively typical heathland for Flanged Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Tranjlekvutqedukhevu Heathland has a yearly average temperature of 21°C (69°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 15°C (59°F). The Tranjlekvutqedukhevu Heathland receives an average of 233 cm/y (91 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Tranjlekvutqedukhevu Heathland covers an area of nearly 28399 km2 (17650 mi2), and an average elevation of 1289 m (4229 ft) above sea level.
