The Zíhiē̼y̼ej̼ù Prairie (/zɪ̞j̼/ /ˈj̼ej̼ʊ/ Snugglefuz [wool; fur] [sexual]) is a subtropical prairie on the coast of the Foxvale region. The Zíhiē̼y̼ej̼ù Prairie is the source of many religious herbs key to the Kitsunes cultural customs and religions.

Climate

The Zíhiē̼y̼ej̼ù Prairie has a yearly average temperature of 14°C (57°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 2°C (35°F). The Zíhiē̼y̼ej̼ù Prairie receives an average of 77 cm/y (30 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Zíhiē̼y̼ej̼ù Prairie covers an area of nearly 8626 km2 (5361 mi2), and an average elevation of 4617 m (15147 ft) above sea level.

The Zíhiē̼y̼ej̼ù Prairie is overseen by Potentate Ezdall Ælfrome Weandronis, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Zíhiē̼y̼ej̼ù Prairie has its economic roots in Foxvale's export of Steel, but has developed its own medicine industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.
