The Fairchild Meadows (/ˈfɛrˌʧaɪld/ /ˈmɛˌdoʊz/ Fairchild [Translation Unavailable]) are a group of subtropical meadow on the coast of the Pressingstone region. Fairchild Meadows are a relatively typical meadow for Pressingstone, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Fairchild Meadows has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). The Fairchild Meadows receives an average of 72 cm/y (28 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Fairchild Meadows covers an area of nearly 17290 km2 (10745 mi2), and an average elevation of 6994 m (-20003 ft) above sea level.
