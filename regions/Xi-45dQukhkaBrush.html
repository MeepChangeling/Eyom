The Xi-45d Qukhka Brush (/ˈquxka/ Xi-45d's Discovery [Proper Noun (name of person)] [discovery]) is a subtropical brush on the coast of the Grayhills region. Xi-45d Qukhka Brush is a relatively typical brush for Grayhills, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Xi-45d Qukhka Brush has a yearly average temperature of 30°C (86°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 33°C (91°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 26°C (78°F). The Xi-45d Qukhka Brush receives an average of 141 cm/y (55 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Xi-45d Qukhka Brush covers an area of nearly 23082 km2 (14345 mi2), and an average elevation of 2605 m (8546 ft) above sea level.

The Xi-45d Qukhka Brush is overseen by Potentate Dredara Orphalhmær Hodelræd, a god serving within The Council of Twelve, the region's principal religion. Approximately 30% of the population self-report as devout. The Xi-45d Qukhka Brush is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.
