The Drfiējùshàsjù Woods (Drfiē /jʊˈʃəsjʊ/ Grandmother Drfiē [Proper Noun (name of person)] [grandmother]) are a group of subtropical woods on the coast of the Foxvale region. Drfiējùshàsjù Woods are a relatively typical woods for Foxvale, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Drfiējùshàsjù Woods has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 3°C (37°F). The Drfiējùshàsjù Woods receives an average of 78 cm/y (30 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Drfiējùshàsjù Woods covers an area of nearly 7868 km2 (4889 mi2), and an average elevation of 4810 m (15780 ft) above sea level.

The Drfiējùshàsjù Woods are home to 235646 people. They break down into the following demographics: 54% Wareneese, 22% Dwarves, 9% Iron Elves, 6% Elf, 7% Imperial, and ~2% Other.

Local Religion

The Drfiējùshàsjù Woods are overseen by Potentate Vetella Hecateandra Ælreyacchus, a god serving within Covenant of Irus, the region's principal religion. Approximately 56% of the population self-report as devout. The Drfiējùshàsjù Woods is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Drfiējùshàsjù Woods has its economic roots in Foxvale's export of Steel, but has developed its own archeology industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.

The Drfiējùshàsjù Woods has an Imperial Development Index of 23. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 23 means there are 23 locations within the Drfiējùshàsjù Woods which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
