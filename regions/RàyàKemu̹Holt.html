The Ràyà Kemu̹ Holt (/ˈrəjə/ /ˈkemu̹/ Hutch [formal] [nest; den]) is a subtropical holt on the coast of the Farmbasin region. Ràyà Kemu̹ Holt is a relatively typical holt for Farmbasin, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Ràyà Kemu̹ Holt has a yearly average temperature of 17°C (62°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 9°C (48°F). The Ràyà Kemu̹ Holt receives an average of 283 cm/y (111 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Ràyà Kemu̹ Holt covers an area of nearly 16188 km2 (10060 mi2), and an average elevation of 6713 m (-20925 ft) above sea level.

The Ràyà Kemu̹ Holt is home to 1808114 people. They break down into the following demographics: 62% Wood Elves, 23% Hobgoblins, 5% Grey Elf, 8% Grey Elf, and ~2% Other.

The Ràyà Kemu̹ Holt is's peoples are culturally Wood Elven, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include cunning and the ability to trick others and justice and fairness between people, as well as a unique theater festival and a history festival.

Wood Elf

The Wood Elf subculture native to the Ràyà Kemu̹ Holt are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of worn weapons, tools or trade implements and base their sence of individual idenity on regional and province-based identity. They also place greater cultural emphasus on humanistic reason and “rational” religion and submission to the collective will or culture.

Hobgoblin

The Hobgoblin subculture native to the Ràyà Kemu̹ Holt are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of role or class-specific clothing items and base their sence of individual idenity on regional and province-based identity. They also place greater cultural emphasus on eloquence and social expertise and submission to the collective will or culture.

Grey Elf

The Grey Elf subculture native to the Ràyà Kemu̹ Holt are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of patterned hair shaving or depilation and base their sence of individual idenity on having or lacking noble blood. They also place greater cultural emphasus on education and knowledge-seeking and industry and the ability to work tirelessly.

Quenn

The Quenn subculture native to the Ràyà Kemu̹ Holt are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of culturally-significant jewelry or accessories and base their sence of individual idenity on hereditary loyalty to a political group. They also place greater cultural emphasus on sexual license and wantonness and membership in an elite bloodline or caste.

Local Religion

The Ràyà Kemu̹ Holt is overseen by Potentate Agarus Akanawn Gerðandra, a god serving within Fawresm, the region's principal religion. Approximately 23% of the population self-report as devout. The Ràyà Kemu̹ Holt is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Ràyà Kemu̹ Holt has its economic roots in Farmbasin's export of Slaves, but has developed its own guns industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.

The Ràyà Kemu̹ Holt has an Imperial Development Index of 16. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 16 means there are 16 locations within the Ràyà Kemu̹ Holt which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
