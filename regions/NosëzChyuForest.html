The Nosëz Chyu Forest (/ˈnosɛz/ /cu/ Bishop's [healthy] [priest]) is a subtropical forest on the coast of the Torpenvale region. Nosëz Chyu Forest is a relatively typical forest for Torpenvale, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Nosëz Chyu Forest has a yearly average temperature of 9°C (48°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -8°C (18°F). The Nosëz Chyu Forest receives an average of 148 cm/y (58 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Nosëz Chyu Forest covers an area of nearly 17212 km2 (10697 mi2), and an average elevation of 4688 m (15380 ft) above sea level.
