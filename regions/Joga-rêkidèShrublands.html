The Joga-rêkidè Shrublands has a yearly average temperature of 19°C (66°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 14°C (57°F). The Joga-rêkidè Shrublands covers an area of nearly 9350 km2 (5811 mi2), and an average elevation of 6473 m (21236 ft) above sea level.
