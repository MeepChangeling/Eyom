The Mistglade Brush (mistglade /brʌʃ/ Mistglade [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical brush on the coast of the Ancestor's Range region. Mistglade Brush is a relatively typical brush for Ancestor's Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Mistglade Brush has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 20°C (68°F). The Mistglade Brush receives an average of 208 cm/y (81 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Mistglade Brush covers an area of nearly 19915 km2 (12377 mi2), and an average elevation of 5919 m (19419 ft) above sea level.

Mistglade Brush is noteworthy for its economic independence from Ancestor's Range's export of Stone. Mistglade Brush is particularly renound for its hardware industry. It is also has a noteworthy textiles industry.
