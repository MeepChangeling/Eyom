The Yíhtcèp Heathland (/jɪ̞t/ /cɛp/ Slowstand [stand] [gradual]) is a temperate heathland within the wetland of the Factorum region. Yíhtcèp Heathland is a relatively typical heathland for Factorum, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Yíhtcèp Heathland has a yearly average temperature of 18°C (64°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 10°C (50°F). The Yíhtcèp Heathland receives an average of 231 cm/y (90 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Yíhtcèp Heathland covers an area of nearly 13056 km2 (8114 mi2), and an average elevation of 2033 m (6669 ft) above sea level.
