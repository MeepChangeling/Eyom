The Dezojeu Basin (/ˈdezo/ /ˈjeu/ Earthshaker [earthquake] [custom]) is a subtropical basin on the coast of the Glowing Sands region. Dezojeu Basin is a relatively typical basin for Glowing Sands, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Dezojeu Basin has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 24°C (75°F). The Dezojeu Basin receives an average of 140 cm/y (55 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Dezojeu Basin covers an area of nearly 21923 km2 (13625 mi2), and an average elevation of 3415 m (11204 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Dezojeu Basin is overseen by Potentate Oa Ælfriastan Nothann, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 24% of the population self-report as devout. The Dezojeu Basin is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Dezojeu Basin is noteworthy for its economic independence from Glowing Sands's export of Glass. Dezojeu Basin is particularly renound for its armor industry. It is also has a noteworthy fertilizer industry.

The Dezojeu Basin has an Imperial Development Index of 32. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 32 means there are 32 locations within the Dezojeu Basin which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
