The Kedotaguv Moorland (/keˈdota/ /guv/ Dairy [wallet] [cream]) is a temperate moorland within the wetland of the Yastan Peninsula region. The Kedotaguv Moorland is well known for being home to remarkably few animals.

Climate

The Kedotaguv Moorland has a yearly average temperature of 5°C (41°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 19°C (66°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool -10°C (14°F). The Kedotaguv Moorland receives an average of 209 cm/y (82 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the short winter months. The Kedotaguv Moorland covers an area of nearly 3668 km2 (2279 mi2), and an average elevation of 3234 m (10610 ft) above sea level.
