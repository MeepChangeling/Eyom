The Kststēbāmā Forest (Kststē /ˈbəˑməˑ/ Kststē's Garden [Proper Noun (name of person)] [orchard; plantation]) is a subtropical forest on the coast of the Abysstro region. Kststēbāmā Forest is a relatively typical forest for Abysstro, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Kststēbāmā Forest has a yearly average temperature of 13°C (55°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing 0°C (32°F). The Kststēbāmā Forest receives an average of 145 cm/y (57 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Kststēbāmā Forest covers an area of nearly 8783 km2 (5458 mi2), and an average elevation of 4472 m (14671 ft) above sea level.
