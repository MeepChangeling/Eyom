The Piropisi Holt (/ˈpiro/ /ˈpisi/ Veiled [data] [transparent]) is a subtropical holt within the wetland of the Shattered Peninsula region. Piropisi Holt is a relatively typical holt for Shattered Peninsula, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Piropisi Holt has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 23°C (73°F). The Piropisi Holt receives an average of 203 cm/y (79 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Piropisi Holt covers an area of nearly 19769 km2 (12286 mi2), and an average elevation of 4910 m (16108 ft) above sea level.

The Piropisi Holt is home to 350900 people. They break down into the following demographics: 52% Iron Elves, 21% Wareneese, 12% Gnolls, 7% Kobold, 6% Grey Elf, and ~2% Other.

The Piropisi Holt is's peoples are culturally Wood Elven, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include loyalty to one’s friends, family, and own and personal indulgence and luxuriant pleasure, as well as a unique sun festival and a arcane festival.

Iron Elf

The Iron Elf subculture native to the Piropisi Holt are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Iron Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of impractical or elaborate role-based clothes and base their sence of individual idenity on patron-client relationships with major figures. They also place greater cultural emphasus on conquest and domination of others and individual rights and freedom of action.

Rattu

The Rattu subculture native to the Piropisi Holt are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Iron Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of piercings, whether minor or elaborate and base their sence of individual idenity on extended family out to cousins and like kin. They also place greater cultural emphasus on honesty and truthfulness in speech and honor and maintaining one’s integrity.

Gnoll

The Gnoll subculture native to the Piropisi Holt are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Iron Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of color choices with social meaning to them and base their sence of individual idenity on educational ties to institutions or traditions. They also place greater cultural emphasus on humanistic reason and “rational” religion and seeking fortune in new places or new roles.

Kobold

The Kobold subculture native to the Piropisi Holt are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Iron Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of worn weapons, tools or trade implements and base their sence of individual idenity on lineages based on ancient hero-ancestors. They also place greater cultural emphasus on pacifism and peaceful resolution of problems and hardiness and endurance before woes.

Grey Elf

The Grey Elf subculture native to the Piropisi Holt are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Iron Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of color choices with social meaning to them and base their sence of individual idenity on having or lacking noble blood. They also place greater cultural emphasus on building things in service of their posterity and conquest and domination of others.

Local Religion

The Piropisi Holt is overseen by Potentate Eraura Sioniu Alkyonemus, a god serving within The Council of Twelve, the region's principal religion. Approximately 47% of the population self-report as devout. The Piropisi Holt is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Piropisi Holt is noteworthy for its economic independence from Shattered Peninsula's export of Metals. Piropisi Holt is particularly renound for its paper industry. It is also has a noteworthy transportation industry.

The Piropisi Holt has an Imperial Development Index of 26. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 26 means there are 26 locations within the Piropisi Holt which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..

As a courtesy for scholars and agents, the Piropisi Holt is rated I1 by Eyom’s banks. Business ventures within the Piropisi Holt are expected to return above capitol at low risk.
