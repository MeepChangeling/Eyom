The Maiddon Savannah (maiddon /səˈvænə/ Maiddon [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical savanna on the coast of the Ancestor's Range region. Maiddon Savannah is a relatively typical savanna for Ancestor's Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Maiddon Savannah has a yearly average temperature of 31°C (87°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 36°C (96°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 26°C (78°F). The Maiddon Savannah receives an average of 201 cm/y (79 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Maiddon Savannah covers an area of nearly 15383 km2 (9560 mi2), and an average elevation of 16669 m (11738 ft) above sea level.

The Maiddon Savannah is home to 171671 people. They break down into the following demographics: 63% Constructs, 13% Iron Elves, 9% Hobgoblins, 9% Grey Elf, 4% Grey Elf, and ~2% Other.

The Maiddon Savannah is's peoples are culturally Constructi, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include building things in service of their posterity and conquest and domination of others, as well as a unique sun festival and a religious festival.

Construct

The Construct subculture native to the Maiddon Savannah are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Construct culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of intricate hair styles or braiding and base their sence of individual idenity on regional and province-based identity. They also place greater cultural emphasus on zealous guardianship of their own land or holy sit and dominating and possessing other people.

They also place greater cultural emphasus on justice and fairness between people and ethnic purity of blood and culture.

Grey Elf

The Grey Elf subculture native to the Maiddon Savannah are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. Local Religion

The Maiddon Savannah is overseen by Potentate Brolena Freann Mapona, a god serving within Tol's Children, the region's principal religion. Approximately 29% of the population self-report as devout. The Maiddon Savannah is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Maiddon Savannah has its economic roots in Ancestor's Range's export of Stone, but has developed its own fertilizer industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.

The Maiddon Savannah has an Imperial Development Index of 22. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 22 means there are 22 locations within the Maiddon Savannah which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..

As a courtesy for scholars and agents, the Maiddon Savannah is rated I1 by Eyom’s banks. Business ventures within the Maiddon Savannah are expected to return above capitol at low risk.
