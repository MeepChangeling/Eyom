The Schyhyo̠-ntadë Savannah (/sco̠/ /ˈntadɛ/ Mead [honey] [water]) is a subtropical savanna within the temperate rainforest of the Icereef region. Schyhyo̠-ntadë Savannah is a relatively typical savanna for Icereef, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Schyhyo̠-ntadë Savannah has a yearly average temperature of 8°C (46°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -7°C (20°F). The Schyhyo̠-ntadë Savannah receives an average of 292 cm/y (114 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Schyhyo̠-ntadë Savannah covers an area of nearly 28012 km2 (17409 mi2), and an average elevation of 1466 m (4809 ft) above sea level.

People and Culture

However, as our culture lacks a taboo for classification of peoples by their biology and such information is highly important for our medical practices amongst other institutions, we have stated the racial demographics of this region. We do not do so to sew the seeds of disunity. We do not do so to belittle or bemoan any group. Such issues are why we use the term 'peoples' rather than races, cultures, or species.

In respect to this universal cultural element, we have split racial classification by culture while keeping the name of each race for our own purposes. Should you see the same race listed more than once in the following section, this is to designate a major cultural division within a race.

The Schyhyo̠-ntadë Savannah is home to 636263 people. They break down into the following demographics: 61% Wareneese, 10% Taurans, 14% Kitsune, 8% Tanuki, 5% Elf, and ~2% Other.

The Schyhyo̠-ntadë Savannah is's peoples are culturally Wareneese, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include honor and maintaining one’s integrity and eloquence and social expertise, as well as a unique wine festival and a literary festival.

Wareneese

The Wareneese subculture native to the Schyhyo̠-ntadë Savannah are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Wareneese culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of socially-meaningful animal motif items and base their sence of individual idenity on far-flung clans of affiliated families. They also place greater cultural emphasus on vengeance and execution of just vendettas and zealous guardianship of their own land or holy sit.

Tauran

The Tauran subculture native to the Schyhyo̠-ntadë Savannah are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wareneese culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of socially-meaningful animal motif items and base their sence of individual idenity on hereditary loyalty to a political group. They also place greater cultural emphasus on beauty and seductive charm and raw strength and personal prowess.

Kitsune

The Kitsune subculture native to the Schyhyo̠-ntadë Savannah are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Wareneese culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of patterned hair shaving or depilation and base their sence of individual idenity on trade guilds specific to particular roles. They also place greater cultural emphasus on personal sacrifice for one’s causes or purposes and zealous guardianship of their own land or holy sit.

Tanuki

The Tanuki subculture native to the Schyhyo̠-ntadë Savannah are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wareneese culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of role or class-specific clothing items and base their sence of individual idenity on patron-client relationships with major figures. They also place greater cultural emphasus on sexual license and wantonness and restoring some real or imagined glorious past.

Elf

The Elf subculture native to the Schyhyo̠-ntadë Savannah are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wareneese culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of significant scent or perfume uses and base their sence of individual idenity on dynastic lineages with cadet branches. They also place greater cultural emphasus on industry and the ability to work tirelessly and honor and maintaining one’s integrity.

Local Religion

The Schyhyo̠-ntadë Savannah is overseen by Potentate Xoelia Orphelwulf Ecgber, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 24% of the population self-report as devout. The Schyhyo̠-ntadë Savannah is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Schyhyo̠-ntadë Savannah has its economic roots in Icereef's export of Fish, but has developed its own innovation industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.

The Schyhyo̠-ntadë Savannah has an Imperial Development Index of 18. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 18 means there are 18 locations within the Schyhyo̠-ntadë Savannah which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
