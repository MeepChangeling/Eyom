The Spshzwāw Holt (Spshz /wəˑw/ Spshz's Kindness [Proper Noun (name of person)] [kind; type]) is a temperate holt within the temperate rainforest of the Salt Warrens region. Spshzwāw Holt is a relatively typical holt for Salt Warrens, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Spshzwāw Holt has a yearly average temperature of 17°C (62°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 12°C (53°F). The Spshzwāw Holt receives an average of 309 cm/y (121 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Spshzwāw Holt covers an area of nearly 12090 km2 (7513 mi2), and an average elevation of 1850 m (6069 ft) above sea level.
