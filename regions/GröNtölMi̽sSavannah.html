The Grö Ntöl Mi̽s Savannah (/gro˞/ /nto˞l mi̽s/ Allkinds [different; other; distinct; various; unique] [citizen]) is a subtropical savanna within the taiga of the Stalfijord region. Grö Ntöl Mi̽s Savannah is a relatively typical savanna for Stalfijord, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Grö Ntöl Mi̽s Savannah has a yearly average temperature of 5°C (41°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool -13°C (9°F). The Grö Ntöl Mi̽s Savannah receives an average of 73 cm/y (28 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Grö Ntöl Mi̽s Savannah covers an area of nearly 12590 km2 (7824 mi2), and an average elevation of 4612 m (15131 ft) above sea level.
