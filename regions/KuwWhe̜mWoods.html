The Kuw Whe̜m Woods (/kuw/ /whe̜m/ Surginglands [sudden; abrupt; swift] [continent]) are a group of subtropical woods on the coast of the Foxvale region. The Kuw Whe̜m Woods are the cultural homeland of the Kitsune.

Climate

The Kuw Whe̜m Woods has a yearly average temperature of 11°C (51°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -2°C (29°F). The Kuw Whe̜m Woods receives an average of 277 cm/y (109 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Kuw Whe̜m Woods covers an area of nearly 16733 km2 (10399 mi2), and an average elevation of 6145 m (20160 ft) above sea level.
