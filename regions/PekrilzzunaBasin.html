The Pekrilzzuna Basin (/ˈzuna/ Pekrilz's Catch [Proper Noun (name of person)] [bass]) is a temperate basin within the wetland of the Shattered Peninsula region. Pekrilzzuna Basin is a relatively typical basin for Shattered Peninsula, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Pekrilzzuna Basin has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 17°C (62°F). The Pekrilzzuna Basin receives an average of 226 cm/y (88 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Pekrilzzuna Basin covers an area of nearly 12371 km2 (7688 mi2), and an average elevation of 1493 m (4898 ft) above sea level.

The Pekrilzzuna Basin is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Pekrilzzuna Basin is noteworthy for its economic independence from Shattered Peninsula's export of Metals. Pekrilzzuna Basin is particularly renound for its glass industry. It is also has a noteworthy machine tools industry.
