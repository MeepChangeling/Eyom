The Vahidï-senk Meadows (/senk/ Vahidï's Shadow [Proper Noun (name of person)] [eclipse]) are a group of subtropical meadow on the coast of the Golden Brooks region. Vahidï-senk Meadows are a relatively typical meadow for Golden Brooks, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Vahidï-senk Meadows has a yearly average temperature of 26°C (78°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 30°C (86°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 23°C (73°F). The Vahidï-senk Meadows receives an average of 267 cm/y (105 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Vahidï-senk Meadows covers an area of nearly 14463 km2 (8988 mi2), and an average elevation of 7538 m (-18218 ft) above sea level.

People and Culture

However, as our culture lacks a taboo for classification of peoples by their biology and such information is highly important for our medical practices amongst other institutions, we have stated the racial demographics of this region. We do not do so to sew the seeds of disunity. We do not do so to belittle or bemoan any group. Such issues are why we use the term 'peoples' rather than races, cultures, or species.

In respect to this universal cultural element, we have split racial classification by culture while keeping the name of each race for our own purposes. Should you see the same race listed more than once in the following section, this is to designate a major cultural division within a race.

The Vahidï-senk Meadows are home to 593260 people. They break down into the following demographics: 68% Dwarves, 17% Gnolls, 8% Grey Elf, 5% Tanuki, and ~2% Other.

The Vahidï-senk Meadows are's peoples are culturally Dwarven, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include courage and valiance in danger and industry and the ability to work tirelessly, as well as a unique spirit festival and a history festival.

Dwarf

The Dwarf subculture native to the Vahidï-senk Meadows are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Dwarf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of intricate hair styles or braiding and base their sence of individual idenity on regional and province-based identity. They also place greater cultural emphasus on exploring the unknown and discovering secrets and personal sacrifice for one’s causes or purposes.

Gnoll

The Gnoll subculture native to the Vahidï-senk Meadows are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Dwarf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of worn weapons, tools or trade implements and base their sence of individual idenity on inheritance of an ancient body modification. They also place greater cultural emphasus on submission to the collective will or culture and beauty and seductive charm.

Grey Elf

The Grey Elf subculture native to the Vahidï-senk Meadows are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Dwarf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of painted skin markings that sometimes change and base their sence of individual idenity on voluntary mutual-assistance brotherhoods. They also place greater cultural emphasus on scheming subtly against enemies or rivals and seeking fortune in new places or new roles.

Tanuki

The Tanuki subculture native to the Vahidï-senk Meadows are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Dwarf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of color choices with social meaning to them and base their sence of individual idenity on educational ties to institutions or traditions. They also place greater cultural emphasus on social progress toward some eventual utopia and honesty and truthfulness in speech.

Local Religion

The Vahidï-senk Meadows are overseen by Potentate Noktrix Eoghand Sioneiara, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 32% of the population self-report as devout. The Vahidï-senk Meadows is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Vahidï-senk Meadows is a negative return economy zone at this time. It has an abundence of Gold, though infrastructure to this region of Golden Brooks is not developed enough to support any major local industry.

The Vahidï-senk Meadows has an Imperial Development Index of 5. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 5 means there are 5 locations within the Vahidï-senk Meadows which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
