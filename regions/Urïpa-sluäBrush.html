The Urïpa-sluä Brush (/sluˈɑ/ Urïpa the Hag's [Proper Noun (name of person)] [hag (old woman)]) is a subtropical brush on the coast of the Highpeaks region. Urïpa-sluä Brush is a relatively typical brush for Highpeaks, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Urïpa-sluä Brush has a yearly average temperature of 27°C (80°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 31°C (87°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 23°C (73°F). The Urïpa-sluä Brush receives an average of 202 cm/y (79 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Urïpa-sluä Brush covers an area of nearly 6820 km2 (4238 mi2), and an average elevation of 9040 m (-13290 ft) above sea level.
