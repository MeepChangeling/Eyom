The Whis Kuqüe Heath (/whis/ /kuq˔/ Passings [temporary; brief] [present (time)]) is a subtropical heath within the temperate rainforest of the Riverpack Coast region. Whis Kuqüe Heath is a relatively typical heath for Riverpack Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Whis Kuqüe Heath has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 4°C (39°F). The Whis Kuqüe Heath receives an average of 297 cm/y (116 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Whis Kuqüe Heath covers an area of nearly 11721 km2 (7284 mi2), and an average elevation of 2829 m (9281 ft) above sea level.
