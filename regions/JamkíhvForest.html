The Jamkíhv Forest (/ʤam/ /kɪ̞v/ Toured [belly] [foreign]) is a temperate forest within the temperate rainforest of the Substrate region. The Jamkíhv Forest is well known for its thriving abundance of plant life.

Climate

The Jamkíhv Forest has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 8°C (46°F). The Jamkíhv Forest receives an average of 317 cm/y (124 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Jamkíhv Forest covers an area of nearly 18376 km2 (11420 mi2), and an average elevation of 1169 m (3835 ft) above sea level.
