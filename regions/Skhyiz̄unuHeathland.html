The Skhyiz̄unu Heathland (/ˈz̚unu/ Skhyi's Tail [Proper Noun (name of person)] [tail]) is a subtropical heathland on the coast of the Iron Coast region. Skhyiz̄unu Heathland is a relatively typical heathland for Iron Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Skhyiz̄unu Heathland has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Skhyiz̄unu Heathland receives an average of 222 cm/y (87 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Skhyiz̄unu Heathland covers an area of nearly 15917 km2 (9892 mi2), and an average elevation of 4837 m (15869 ft) above sea level.
