The Kuz Sæm Steppe (/kuz/ /sæm/ Foolsmist [naive] [fog; mist]) is a subtropical steppe within the temperate rainforest of the Diamond Basin region. Kuz Sæm Steppe is a relatively typical steppe for Diamond Basin, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Kuz Sæm Steppe has a yearly average temperature of 28°C (82°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 31°C (87°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 25°C (77°F). The Kuz Sæm Steppe receives an average of 279 cm/y (109 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Kuz Sæm Steppe covers an area of nearly 23315 km2 (14490 mi2), and an average elevation of 3921 m (12864 ft) above sea level.
