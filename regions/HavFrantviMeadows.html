The Hav Frantvi Meadows (/hav/ /ˈfrantvi/ Absurd Carrot [silly; absurd] [carrot]) are a group of subtropical meadow within the temperate rainforest of the Islestand Coast region. The Hav Frantvi Meadows are well known for being largely barren of plant life.

Climate

The Hav Frantvi Meadows has a yearly average temperature of 8°C (46°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold -10°C (14°F). The Hav Frantvi Meadows receives an average of 304 cm/y (119 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Hav Frantvi Meadows covers an area of nearly 10931 km2 (6793 mi2), and an average elevation of 3014 m (9888 ft) above sea level.
