The Vdrirokipi Brush (/ˈkipi/ Vdriro's Goose [Proper Noun (name of person)] [goose]) is a subtropical brush within the temperate deciduous forest of the Galloping Plains region. Vdrirokipi Brush is a relatively typical brush for Galloping Plains, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Vdrirokipi Brush has a yearly average temperature of 26°C (78°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 23°C (73°F). The Vdrirokipi Brush receives an average of 137 cm/y (53 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Vdrirokipi Brush covers an area of nearly 20060 km2 (12467 mi2), and an average elevation of 4646 m (15242 ft) above sea level.
