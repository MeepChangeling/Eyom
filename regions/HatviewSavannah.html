The Hatview Savannah (hatview /səˈvænə/ Hatview [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical savanna on the coast of the Upper Warding Range region. Hatview Savannah is a relatively typical savanna for Upper Warding Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Hatview Savannah has a yearly average temperature of 32°C (89°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 36°C (96°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 27°C (80°F). The Hatview Savannah receives an average of 68 cm/y (26 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Hatview Savannah covers an area of nearly 11335 km2 (7044 mi2), and an average elevation of 11771 m (-4330 ft) above sea level.

The Hatview Savannah is overseen by Potentate Ghukphion Clídnawyda Kastanthan, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 50% of the population self-report as devout. The Hatview Savannah is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Hatview Savannah is predominantly economicly reiant on Upper Warding Range's export of Arcana, and has no outstanding industries byond what is required for its role in that process.
