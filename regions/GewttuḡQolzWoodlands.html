The Gewttuḡ Qolz Woodlands (/qolz/ Gewttuḡport [Proper Noun (name of person)] [port]) are a group of subtropical woodland on the coast of the Anvil Badlands region. Gewttuḡ Qolz Woodlands are a relatively typical woodland for Anvil Badlands, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Gewttuḡ Qolz Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Gewttuḡ Qolz Woodlands receives an average of 77 cm/y (30 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Gewttuḡ Qolz Woodlands covers an area of nearly 9827 km2 (6107 mi2), and an average elevation of 7858 m (-17168 ft) above sea level.
