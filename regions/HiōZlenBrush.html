The Hiō Zlen Brush (/hjoː/ /zlen/ Oldhelm [past] [helmet]) is a temperate brush within the wetland of the Aegispoint region. Hiō Zlen Brush is a relatively typical brush for Aegispoint, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Hiō Zlen Brush has a yearly average temperature of 15°C (59°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 21°C (69°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 8°C (46°F). The Hiō Zlen Brush receives an average of 218 cm/y (85 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Hiō Zlen Brush covers an area of nearly 7948 km2 (4939 mi2), and an average elevation of 1353 m (4438 ft) above sea level.

The Hiō Zlen Brush is home to 251143 people. They break down into the following demographics: 67% Gnolls, 18% Goblins, 5% Kivu, 8% Tanuki, and ~2% Other.

The Hiō Zlen Brush is's peoples are culturally Gnollish, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include personal development and limit-pushing and social progress toward some eventual utopia, as well as a unique relic festival and a spring festival.

Gnoll

The Gnoll subculture native to the Hiō Zlen Brush are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Gnoll culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of painted skin markings that sometimes change and base their sence of individual idenity on educational ties to institutions or traditions. They also place greater cultural emphasus on eloquence and social expertise and discipline and obedience to the law.

Goblin

They also place greater cultural emphasus on vengeance and execution of just vendettas and seeking fortune in new places or new roles.

Kivu

The Kivu subculture native to the Hiō Zlen Brush are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. They also place greater cultural emphasus on piety and devotion to the gods and courage and valiance in danger.

Tanuki

The Tanuki subculture native to the Hiō Zlen Brush are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. They also place greater cultural emphasus on aesthetic beauty in material goods and architecture and honor and maintaining one’s integrity.

Local Religion

The Hiō Zlen Brush is overseen by Potentate Ione Cassandrona Alenthniu, a god serving within Fawresm, the region's principal religion. Approximately 50% of the population self-report as devout. The Hiō Zlen Brush is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Hiō Zlen Brush is noteworthy for its economic independence from Aegispoint's export of Ships. Hiō Zlen Brush is particularly renound for its forestry byproducts industry. It is also has a noteworthy fertilizer industry.

The Hiō Zlen Brush has an Imperial Development Index of 26. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 26 means there are 26 locations within the Hiō Zlen Brush which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
