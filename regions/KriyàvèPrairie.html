The Kriyàvè Prairie (Kriyà /vɛ/ Kriyà's Finger [Proper Noun (name of person)] [finger]) is a subtropical prairie on the coast of the Ablative region. Kriyàvè Prairie is a relatively typical prairie for Ablative, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Kriyàvè Prairie has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 5°C (41°F). The Kriyàvè Prairie receives an average of 151 cm/y (59 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Kriyàvè Prairie covers an area of nearly 15721 km2 (9770 mi2), and an average elevation of 3179 m (10429 ft) above sea level.
