The Wöd Basklo Brushlands (/wo˞d/ /ˈbasklo/ Cometspath [common; frequent; normal] [asteroid; comet]) are a group of temperate brushland within the temperate rainforest of the Riverpack Coast region. Wöd Basklo Brushlands are a relatively typical brushland for Riverpack Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Wöd Basklo Brushlands has a yearly average temperature of 15°C (59°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 3°C (37°F). The Wöd Basklo Brushlands receives an average of 282 cm/y (111 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Wöd Basklo Brushlands covers an area of nearly 16381 km2 (10180 mi2), and an average elevation of 1463 m (4799 ft) above sea level.
