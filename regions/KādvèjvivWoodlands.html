The Kādvèjviv Woodlands (/ˈkəˑdvɛʤ/ /viv/ Umberwrit [contact] [dark]) are a group of subtropical woodland within the temperate rainforest of the Factorum region. Kādvèjviv Woodlands are a relatively typical woodland for Factorum, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Kādvèjviv Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 18°C (64°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 11°C (51°F). The Kādvèjviv Woodlands receives an average of 310 cm/y (122 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Kādvèjviv Woodlands covers an area of nearly 19678 km2 (12229 mi2), and an average elevation of 1731 m (5679 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Kādvèjviv Woodlands are overseen by Potentate Uarus Osgar Tadwine, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 27% of the population self-report as devout. The Kādvèjviv Woodlands is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Kādvèjviv Woodlands has its economic roots in Factorum's export of Industrial Goods, but has developed its own fertilizer industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.
