The I̊cajbèn Brush (/ˈi̘caʤ/ /bɛn/ Cuddle [pet] [raw]) is a subtropical brush on the coast of the Redmine region. The I̊cajbèn Brush is well known for being largely barren of plant life.

Climate

The I̊cajbèn Brush has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 3°C (37°F). The I̊cajbèn Brush receives an average of 204 cm/y (80 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The I̊cajbèn Brush covers an area of nearly 11832 km2 (7353 mi2), and an average elevation of 3088 m (10131 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The I̊cajbèn Brush is overseen by Potentate Brudione Alkyonwy Koronchus, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 24% of the population self-report as devout. The I̊cajbèn Brush is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.
