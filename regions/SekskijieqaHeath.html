The Sekskijieqa Heath (/ˈʤeqa/ Sekski the Punk's [Proper Noun (name of person)] [insurgent; rebel]) is a subtropical heath within the temperate rainforest of the Illa's Panhandle region. The Sekskijieqa Heath is well known for its thriving abundance of plant life.

Climate

The Sekskijieqa Heath has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 22°C (71°F). The Sekskijieqa Heath receives an average of 277 cm/y (109 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Sekskijieqa Heath covers an area of nearly 26490 km2 (16463 mi2), and an average elevation of 4183 m (13723 ft) above sea level.
