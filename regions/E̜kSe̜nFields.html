The E̜k Se̜n Fields has a yearly average temperature of 7°C (44°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant -14°C (7°F). The E̜k Se̜n Fields covers an area of nearly 7107 km2 (4417 mi2), and an average elevation of 2916 m (9566 ft) above sea level.
