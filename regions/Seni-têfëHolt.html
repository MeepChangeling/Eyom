Climate

The Seni-têfë Holt has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). The Seni-têfë Holt receives an average of 152 cm/y (59 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Seni-têfë Holt covers an area of nearly 7389 km2 (4592 mi2), and an average elevation of 4244 m (13923 ft) above sea level.
