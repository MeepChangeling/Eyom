The Dÿyd Gafuld Woodlands (/dijd/ /gaˈfuld/ Fairivory [fuel] [lesbian]) are a group of subtropical woodland on the coast of the Highpeaks region. Dÿyd Gafuld Woodlands are a relatively typical woodland for Highpeaks, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Dÿyd Gafuld Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 34°C (93°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 37°C (98°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 30°C (86°F). The Dÿyd Gafuld Woodlands receives an average of 201 cm/y (79 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Dÿyd Gafuld Woodlands covers an area of nearly 10548 km2 (6555 mi2), and an average elevation of 10814 m (-7470 ft) above sea level.

The Dÿyd Gafuld Woodlands are home to 100086 people. They break down into the following demographics: 56% Constructs, 29% Fae, 8% Grey Elf, 5% Tanuki, and ~2% Other.

The Dÿyd Gafuld Woodlands are's peoples are culturally Constructi, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include personal sacrifice for one’s causes or purposes and raw strength and personal prowess, as well as a unique winter festival and a relic festival.

Construct

The Construct subculture native to the Dÿyd Gafuld Woodlands are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Construct culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of worn weapons, tools or trade implements and base their sence of individual idenity on far-flung clans of affiliated families. They also place greater cultural emphasus on building things in service of their posterity and raw strength and personal prowess.

Fae

The Fae subculture native to the Dÿyd Gafuld Woodlands are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Construct culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of patterned hair shaving or depilation and base their sence of individual idenity on inheritance of an ancient body modification. They also place greater cultural emphasus on sharing wealth and goods with others and industry and the ability to work tirelessly.

Grey Elf

The Grey Elf subculture native to the Dÿyd Gafuld Woodlands are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Construct culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of impractical or elaborate role-based clothes and base their sence of individual idenity on ethnic membership. They also place greater cultural emphasus on eloquence and social expertise and excellence in one’s profession or trade.

Tanuki

The Tanuki subculture native to the Dÿyd Gafuld Woodlands are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Construct culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of patterned hair shaving or depilation and base their sence of individual idenity on extended family out to cousins and like kin. They also place greater cultural emphasus on societal or ethnic superiority over all outsiders and purging evil and expelling the wicked.

Local Religion

The Dÿyd Gafuld Woodlands are overseen by Potentate Rovbris Medalthniu Æthelric, a god serving within The Council of Twelve, the region's principal religion. Approximately 29% of the population self-report as devout. The Dÿyd Gafuld Woodlands is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Dÿyd Gafuld Woodlands is a negative return economy zone at this time. It has an abundence of Elemental Crystals, though infrastructure to this region of Highpeaks is not developed enough to support any major local industry.
