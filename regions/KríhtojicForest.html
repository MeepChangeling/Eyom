The Kríhtojic Forest (Kríhto /jic/ Kríhto's Staff [Proper Noun (name of person)] [staff]) is a subtropical forest on the coast of the Abysstro region. Kríhtojic Forest is a relatively typical forest for Abysstro, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Kríhtojic Forest has a yearly average temperature of 15°C (59°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 3°C (37°F). The Kríhtojic Forest receives an average of 152 cm/y (59 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Kríhtojic Forest covers an area of nearly 12106 km2 (7523 mi2), and an average elevation of 3581 m (11748 ft) above sea level.

People and Culture

The Kríhtojic Forest is home to 1346033 people. They break down into the following demographics: 69% Wood Elves, 16% Wareneese, 5% Elf, 5% Grey Elf, 3% Tanuki, and ~2% Other.

The Kríhtojic Forest is's peoples are culturally Wood Elven, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include aesthetic beauty in material goods and architecture and justice and fairness between people, as well as a unique sun festival and a spring festival.

Wood Elf

The Wood Elf subculture native to the Kríhtojic Forest are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of worn weapons, tools or trade implements and base their sence of individual idenity on identity based on their local community. They also place greater cultural emphasus on faithfulness towards one’s chosen friends and justice and fairness between people.

Rattu

The Rattu subculture native to the Kríhtojic Forest are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of tattoos of some cultural significance and base their sence of individual idenity on professional guild or trade-specific group. They also place greater cultural emphasus on raw strength and personal prowess and magical prowess and occult ability.

Elf

The Elf subculture native to the Kríhtojic Forest are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of piercings, whether minor or elaborate and base their sence of individual idenity on dynastic lineages with cadet branches. They also place greater cultural emphasus on discipline and obedience to the law and courage and valiance in danger.

Grey Elf

The Grey Elf subculture native to the Kríhtojic Forest are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of tattoos of some cultural significance and base their sence of individual idenity on dynastic lineages with cadet branches. They also place greater cultural emphasus on prosperity and accruing material wealth and submission to the collective will or culture.

Tanuki

The Tanuki subculture native to the Kríhtojic Forest are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of culturally-significant jewelry or accessories and base their sence of individual idenity on professional guild or trade-specific group. They also place greater cultural emphasus on personal sacrifice for one’s causes or purposes and exploring the unknown and discovering secrets.

Local Religion

The Kríhtojic Forest is overseen by Potentate Agarus Osgarmus Apollo, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 13% of the population self-report as devout. The Kríhtojic Forest is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Kríhtojic Forest has its economic roots in Abysstro's export of Spirits, but has developed its own steel industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.

The Kríhtojic Forest has an Imperial Development Index of 22. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 22 means there are 22 locations within the Kríhtojic Forest which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..

As a courtesy for scholars and agents, the Kríhtojic Forest is rated I1 by Eyom’s banks. Business ventures within the Kríhtojic Forest are expected to return above capitol at low risk.
