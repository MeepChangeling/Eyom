The Whis Lönstir Heathland (/whis/ /ˈlo˞nstir/ Sagespark [temporary; brief] [university; academy]) is a subtropical heathland on the coast of the Foxvale region. Whis Lönstir Heathland is a relatively typical heathland for Foxvale, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Whis Lönstir Heathland has a yearly average temperature of 11°C (51°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -2°C (29°F). The Whis Lönstir Heathland receives an average of 292 cm/y (114 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Whis Lönstir Heathland covers an area of nearly 21617 km2 (13435 mi2), and an average elevation of 5846 m (19179 ft) above sea level.
