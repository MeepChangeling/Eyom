The Lulf Zul Sær Woods (/lulf/ /zul sær/ Colorwind [coral] [hurricane]) are a group of subtropical woods on the coast of the Hearthcoast region. Lulf Zul Sær Woods are a relatively typical woods for Hearthcoast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Lulf Zul Sær Woods has a yearly average temperature of 30°C (86°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 33°C (91°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 26°C (78°F). The Lulf Zul Sær Woods receives an average of 134 cm/y (52 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Lulf Zul Sær Woods covers an area of nearly 11675 km2 (7256 mi2), and an average elevation of 4117 m (13507 ft) above sea level.
