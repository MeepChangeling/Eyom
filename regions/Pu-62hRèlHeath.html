The Pu-62h Rèl Heath (/rɤl/ Pu-62h's Wagon [Proper Noun (name of person)] [trailer]) is a subtropical heath within the temperate deciduous forest of the Westlake region. The Pu-62h Rèl Heath is renowned for its canal dredgers, which are widely regarded as the finest in all of Eyom.

Climate

The Pu-62h Rèl Heath has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 18°C (64°F). The Pu-62h Rèl Heath receives an average of 146 cm/y (57 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Pu-62h Rèl Heath covers an area of nearly 9848 km2 (6120 mi2), and an average elevation of 1975 m (6479 ft) above sea level.
