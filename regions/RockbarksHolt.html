The Rockbarks Holt (rockbarks /hoʊlt/ Rockbarks [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical holt on the coast of the Cutmountains region. The Rockbarks Holt is well known for its thriving abundance of plant life.

Climate

The Rockbarks Holt has a yearly average temperature of 32°C (89°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 37°C (98°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 28°C (82°F). The Rockbarks Holt receives an average of 67 cm/y (26 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Rockbarks Holt covers an area of nearly 7055 km2 (4384 mi2), and an average elevation of 14255 m (3818 ft) above sea level.
