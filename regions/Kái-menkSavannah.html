The Kái-menk Savannah (/meŋ/ Káisiron [Proper Noun (name of person)] [iron]) is a subtropical savanna on the coast of the Upper Warding Range region. The Kái-menk Savannah is well known for being largely barren of plant life.

Climate

The Kái-menk Savannah has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). The Kái-menk Savannah receives an average of 66 cm/y (25 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Kái-menk Savannah covers an area of nearly 10008 km2 (6220 mi2), and an average elevation of 9573 m (-11542 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Kái-menk Savannah is overseen by Potentate Oenar Ælfrome Eastmun, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 56% of the population self-report as devout. The Kái-menk Savannah is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Kái-menk Savannah is predominantly economicly reiant on Upper Warding Range's export of Arcana, and has no outstanding industries byond what is required for its role in that process.
