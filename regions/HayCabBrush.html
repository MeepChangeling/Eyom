The Hay Cab Brush (/haʤ/ /cab/ Moon Landlord [moon] [landlord]) is a subtropical brush within the temperate rainforest of the Flanged Coast region. Hay Cab Brush is a relatively typical brush for Flanged Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Hay Cab Brush has a yearly average temperature of 21°C (69°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 15°C (59°F). The Hay Cab Brush receives an average of 303 cm/y (119 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Hay Cab Brush covers an area of nearly 12479 km2 (7755 mi2), and an average elevation of 1836 m (6023 ft) above sea level.

The Hay Cab Brush is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
