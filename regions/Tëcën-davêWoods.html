The Tëcën-davê Woods (/ˈdavɘ/ Tëcën's Vodka [Proper Noun (name of person)] [beverage]) are a group of subtropical woods on the coast of the Upper Warding Range region. Tëcën-davê Woods are a relatively typical woods for Upper Warding Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Tëcën-davê Woods has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 18°C (64°F). The Tëcën-davê Woods receives an average of 70 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Tëcën-davê Woods covers an area of nearly 12462 km2 (7745 mi2), and an average elevation of 6796 m (-20653 ft) above sea level.
