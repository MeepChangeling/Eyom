Climate

The Ehz ån Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 27°C (80°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 31°C (87°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 24°C (75°F). The Ehz ån Woodlands receives an average of 72 cm/y (28 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Ehz ån Woodlands covers an area of nearly 8455 km2 (5254 mi2), and an average elevation of 5083 m (16676 ft) above sea level.
