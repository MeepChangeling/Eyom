The Yê-magê Woodlands (/jɘ/ /ˈmagɘ/ Ambertribe [amber] [tribe; kin]) are a group of subtropical woodland on the coast of the Cutmountains region. Yê-magê Woodlands are a relatively typical woodland for Cutmountains, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Yê-magê Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 22°C (71°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 17°C (62°F). The Yê-magê Woodlands receives an average of 67 cm/y (26 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Yê-magê Woodlands covers an area of nearly 12246 km2 (7610 mi2), and an average elevation of 8398 m (-15397 ft) above sea level.
