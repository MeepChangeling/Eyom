The Cål Fapm Forest (/cɑːl/ /fapm/ Menden [selfish] [husband]) is a subtropical forest on the coast of the Northlake region. The Cål Fapm Forest is the political heartlands of the Dalandic Empire.

Climate

The Cål Fapm Forest has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). The Cål Fapm Forest receives an average of 297 cm/y (116 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Cål Fapm Forest covers an area of nearly 13989 km2 (8694 mi2), and an average elevation of 1994 m (6541 ft) above sea level.
