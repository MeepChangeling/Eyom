The Umevoz̄i Woodlands (/ˈume/ /ˈvoz̚i/ Crucialblight [plague] [major]) are a group of subtropical woodland within the wetland of the Shattered Peninsula region. Umevoz̄i Woodlands are a relatively typical woodland for Shattered Peninsula, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Umevoz̄i Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 12°C (53°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a cool 16°C (60°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 8°C (46°F). The Umevoz̄i Woodlands receives an average of 208 cm/y (81 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the short winter months. The Umevoz̄i Woodlands covers an area of nearly 6751 km2 (4195 mi2), and an average elevation of 2324 m (7624 ft) above sea level.
