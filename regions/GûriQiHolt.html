The Gûri Qi Holt (/qi/ Gûri's School [Proper Noun (name of person)] [school]) is a subtropical holt on the coast of the Bulwark region. Gûri Qi Holt is a relatively typical holt for Bulwark, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Gûri Qi Holt has a yearly average temperature of 17°C (62°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 9°C (48°F). The Gûri Qi Holt receives an average of 70 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Gûri Qi Holt covers an area of nearly 11848 km2 (7363 mi2), and an average elevation of 8122 m (-16302 ft) above sea level.
