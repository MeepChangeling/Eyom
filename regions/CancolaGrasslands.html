The Cancola Grasslands (cancola /ˈgræˌslændz/ Cancola [Translation Unavailable]) are a group of temperate grassland on the coast of the Highpeaks region. The Cancola Grasslands are well known for being largely barren of plant life.

Climate

The Cancola Grasslands has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 18°C (64°F). The Cancola Grasslands receives an average of 72 cm/y (28 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Cancola Grasslands covers an area of nearly 6011 km2 (3735 mi2), and an average elevation of 6253 m (20515 ft) above sea level.

The Cancola Grasslands are's peoples are culturally Wood Elven, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include personal sacrifice for one’s causes or purposes and raw strength and personal prowess, as well as a unique winter festival and a autumn festival.

Local Religion

The Cancola Grasslands are overseen by Potentate Qrera Hoditandros Sartha, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 49% of the population self-report as devout. The Cancola Grasslands is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Cancola Grasslands is a negative return economy zone at this time. It has an abundence of Elemental Crystals, though infrastructure to this region of Highpeaks is not developed enough to support any major local industry.
