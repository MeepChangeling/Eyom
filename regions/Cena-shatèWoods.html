Climate

The Cena-shatè Woods has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 20°C (68°F). The Cena-shatè Woods receives an average of 74 cm/y (29 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Cena-shatè Woods covers an area of nearly 8004 km2 (4974 mi2), and an average elevation of 5346 m (17539 ft) above sea level.
