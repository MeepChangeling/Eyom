The Bake Qûrû Holt (/ˈbake/ /ˈqʊrʊ/ Silkweave [recent] [clothes; clothing; garment]) is a temperate holt on the coast of the Abysstro region. Bake Qûrû Holt is a relatively typical holt for Abysstro, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Bake Qûrû Holt has a yearly average temperature of 18°C (64°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 10°C (50°F). The Bake Qûrû Holt receives an average of 152 cm/y (59 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Bake Qûrû Holt covers an area of nearly 10846 km2 (6740 mi2), and an average elevation of 2977 m (9767 ft) above sea level.
