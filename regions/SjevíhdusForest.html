The Sjevíhdus Forest (Sjevíh /dus/ Sjevíh's Cart [Proper Noun (name of person)] [taxi; cab]) is a temperate forest within the temperate rainforest of the Riverpack Coast region. Sjevíhdus Forest is a relatively typical forest for Riverpack Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Sjevíhdus Forest has a yearly average temperature of 15°C (59°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 2°C (35°F). The Sjevíhdus Forest receives an average of 281 cm/y (110 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Sjevíhdus Forest covers an area of nearly 8344 km2 (5185 mi2), and an average elevation of 1447 m (4747 ft) above sea level.
