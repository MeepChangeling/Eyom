The Mazebepa Woodlands (/ˈmaze/ /ˈbepa/ Kingsheim [penis] [noble]) are a group of subtropical woodland within the temperate rainforest of the Yastan Peninsula region. The Mazebepa Woodlands are the political heartlands of the NasdianCity-state.

Climate

The Mazebepa Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 5°C (41°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 19°C (66°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant -10°C (14°F). The Mazebepa Woodlands receives an average of 278 cm/y (109 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the short winter months. The Mazebepa Woodlands covers an area of nearly 7954 km2 (4943 mi2), and an average elevation of 2773 m (9097 ft) above sea level.
