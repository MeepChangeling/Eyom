The Tàvríhmobwav Steppe (/təˈvrɪ̞mob/ /wav/ Blacksand [bank (river)] [charcoal]) is a subtropical steppe on the coast of the Bulwark region. Tàvríhmobwav Steppe is a relatively typical steppe for Bulwark, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Tàvríhmobwav Steppe has a yearly average temperature of 15°C (59°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 6°C (42°F). The Tàvríhmobwav Steppe receives an average of 151 cm/y (59 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Tàvríhmobwav Steppe covers an area of nearly 31071 km2 (19310 mi2), and an average elevation of 4131 m (13553 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Tàvríhmobwav Steppe is overseen by Potentate Phireus Alespoleme Emer, a god serving within Tol's Children, the region's principal religion. Approximately 25% of the population self-report as devout. The Tàvríhmobwav Steppe is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Tàvríhmobwav Steppe has its economic roots in Bulwark's export of Armor, but has developed its own weapons industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.
