The Trokhyijdzumehu Shrublands (/zuˈmehu/ Trokhyijd's Flock [Proper Noun (name of person)] [herd; flock]) are a group of subtropical shrubland within the temperate deciduous forest of the Eastlake region. Trokhyijdzumehu Shrublands are a relatively typical shrubland for Eastlake, as well as Eyom as a whole.

The Trokhyijdzumehu Shrublands has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Trokhyijdzumehu Shrublands covers an area of nearly 18894 km2 (11742 mi2), and an average elevation of 6682 m (-21027 ft) above sea level.
