The Dotrie Expanse (dotrie Expanse DotrIe [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Greenrange region. Dotrie Expanse is a relatively typical expanse for Greenrange, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Dotrie Expanse has a yearly average temperature of 30°C (86°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 35°C (95°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 25°C (77°F). The Dotrie Expanse receives an average of 140 cm/y (55 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Dotrie Expanse covers an area of nearly 10281 km2 (6389 mi2), and an average elevation of 11845 m (-4088 ft) above sea level.

The Dotrie Expanse is's peoples are culturally Sylvanian, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include remembrance of the past and memorializing history and honor and maintaining one’s integrity, as well as a unique religious festival and a folk festival.

Local Religion

The Dotrie Expanse is overseen by Potentate Brovphy Paran Iapusann, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 22% of the population self-report as devout. The Dotrie Expanse is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

The Dotrie Expanse has an Imperial Development Index of 23. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 23 means there are 23 locations within the Dotrie Expanse which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
