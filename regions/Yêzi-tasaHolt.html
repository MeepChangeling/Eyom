The Yêzi-tasa Holt (/ˈjɘzi/ /ˈtasa/ Kombu [wild] [seaweed]) is a subtropical holt on the coast of the Irusthrone region. Yêzi-tasa Holt is a relatively typical holt for Irusthrone, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Yêzi-tasa Holt has a yearly average temperature of 19°C (66°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 13°C (55°F). The Yêzi-tasa Holt receives an average of 70 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Yêzi-tasa Holt covers an area of nearly 11247 km2 (6990 mi2), and an average elevation of 9091 m (-13123 ft) above sea level.
