The Ngelä-nruq Expanse (/nruq/ Ngelä's Yacht [Proper Noun (name of person)] [boat; yacht]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Lower Warding Range region. Ngelä-nruq Expanse is a relatively typical expanse for Lower Warding Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Ngelä-nruq Expanse has a yearly average temperature of 32°C (89°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 36°C (96°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 29°C (84°F). The Ngelä-nruq Expanse receives an average of 66 cm/y (25 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Ngelä-nruq Expanse covers an area of nearly 10565 km2 (6566 mi2), and an average elevation of 17357 m (13995 ft) above sea level.
