Climate

The Bāwk Rá́dgër Woods has a yearly average temperature of 26°C (78°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 31°C (87°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 22°C (71°F). The Bāwk Rá́dgër Woods receives an average of 140 cm/y (55 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Bāwk Rá́dgër Woods covers an area of nearly 15527 km2 (9650 mi2), and an average elevation of 5770 m (18930 ft) above sea level.
