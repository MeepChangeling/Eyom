The Pithêj-olekè Holt has a yearly average temperature of 20°C (68°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 15°C (59°F). The Pithêj-olekè Holt covers an area of nearly 7052 km2 (4382 mi2), and an average elevation of 6509 m (21354 ft) above sea level.
