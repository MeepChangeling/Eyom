The Jāczùf Moorland (/jəˑc/ /zʊf/ Suckle [beast] [rake; fork]) is a subtropical moorland within the temperate rainforest of the Heartland region. Jāczùf Moorland is a relatively typical moorland for Heartland, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Jāczùf Moorland has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 8°C (46°F). The Jāczùf Moorland receives an average of 285 cm/y (112 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Jāczùf Moorland covers an area of nearly 22821 km2 (14183 mi2), and an average elevation of 2674 m (8772 ft) above sea level.
