The Savanias Savannah (savanias /səˈvænə/ Savanias [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical savanna on the coast of the Ancestor's Range region. Savanias Savannah is a relatively typical savanna for Ancestor's Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Savanias Savannah has a yearly average temperature of 29°C (84°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 33°C (91°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 25°C (77°F). The Savanias Savannah receives an average of 68 cm/y (26 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Savanias Savannah covers an area of nearly 7773 km2 (4830 mi2), and an average elevation of 11800 m (-4235 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Savanias Savannah is overseen by Potentate Uklo Alkyont Aponwyda, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 10% of the population self-report as devout. The Savanias Savannah is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Savanias Savannah is a negative return economy zone at this time. It has an abundence of Stone, though infrastructure to this region of Ancestor's Range is not developed enough to support any major local industry.
