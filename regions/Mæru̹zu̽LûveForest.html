The Mæru̹zu̽ Lûve Forest covers an area of nearly 7003 km2 (4352 mi2), and an average elevation of 3395 m (11138 ft) above sea level.

The Mæru̹zu̽ Lûve Forest is overseen by Potentate Budum Ælfrig Wulfsta, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 43% of the population self-report as devout. The Mæru̹zu̽ Lûve Forest is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.
