The Zih Věz Heathland (/zih/ /vɛz/ Wargame [small; little; short; low; miniature; brief] [battle]) is a subtropical heathland within the temperate rainforest of the Northlake region. Zih Věz Heathland is a relatively typical heathland for Northlake, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Zih Věz Heathland has a yearly average temperature of 20°C (68°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 16°C (60°F). The Zih Věz Heathland receives an average of 308 cm/y (121 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Zih Věz Heathland covers an area of nearly 6333 km2 (3935 mi2), and an average elevation of 1258 m (4127 ft) above sea level.
