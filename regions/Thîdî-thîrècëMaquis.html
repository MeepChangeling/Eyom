The Thîdî-thîrècë Maquis (/ˈθɪdɪ/ /ʃɪˈrècë/ Universal [consistent; compatible] [pride]) is a subtropical maquis on the coast of the Cogdales region. The Thîdî-thîrècë Maquis is well known for its thriving abundance of plant life.

Climate

The Thîdî-thîrècë Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 19°C (66°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 11°C (51°F). The Thîdî-thîrècë Maquis receives an average of 222 cm/y (87 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Thîdî-thîrècë Maquis covers an area of nearly 18948 km2 (11776 mi2), and an average elevation of 5178 m (16988 ft) above sea level.
