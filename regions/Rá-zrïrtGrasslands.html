The Rá-zrïrt Grasslands (/rə˞/ /zrɪrt/ Grandstar [important; significant] [galaxy]) are a group of subtropical grassland within the wetland of the Flanged Coast region. Rá-zrïrt Grasslands are a relatively typical grassland for Flanged Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Rá-zrïrt Grasslands has a yearly average temperature of 27°C (80°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 31°C (87°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 23°C (73°F). The Rá-zrïrt Grasslands receives an average of 222 cm/y (87 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Rá-zrïrt Grasslands covers an area of nearly 22452 km2 (13954 mi2), and an average elevation of 3483 m (11427 ft) above sea level.
