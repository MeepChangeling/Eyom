The Kæs Lilqæ Holt (/cæs/ /ˈlilqæ/ Sweetjoy [happy] [sugar]) is a subtropical holt within the temperate rainforest of the Flanged Coast region. Kæs Lilqæ Holt is a relatively typical holt for Flanged Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Kæs Lilqæ Holt has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 17°C (62°F). The Kæs Lilqæ Holt receives an average of 301 cm/y (118 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Kæs Lilqæ Holt covers an area of nearly 8118 km2 (5045 mi2), and an average elevation of 1897 m (6223 ft) above sea level.

The Kæs Lilqæ Holt is home to 187948 people. They break down into the following demographics: 50% Constructs, 18% Constructs, 17% Constructs, 5% Constructs, 8% Grey Elf, and ~2% Other.

The Kæs Lilqæ Holt is's peoples are culturally Constructi, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include raw strength and personal prowess and eloquence and social expertise, as well as a unique science festival and a autumn festival.

Construct

The Construct subculture native to the Kæs Lilqæ Holt are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Construct culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of painted skin markings that sometimes change and base their sence of individual idenity on educational ties to institutions or traditions. They also place greater cultural emphasus on justice and fairness between people and raw strength and personal prowess.

Local Religion

The Kæs Lilqæ Holt is overseen by Potentate Shakohr Ourann Lóegarmund, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 10% of the population self-report as devout. The Kæs Lilqæ Holt is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Kæs Lilqæ Holt is predominantly economicly reiant on Flanged Coast's export of Fish, and has no outstanding industries byond what is required for its role in that process.

The Kæs Lilqæ Holt has an Imperial Development Index of 7. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 7 means there are 7 locations within the Kæs Lilqæ Holt which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..

As a courtesy for scholars and agents, the Kæs Lilqæ Holt is rated M1 by Eyom’s banks. Business ventures within the Kæs Lilqæ Holt are not expected to return value in yearly terms.
