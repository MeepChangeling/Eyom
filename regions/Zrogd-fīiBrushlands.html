The Zrogd-fīi Brushlands (/zrogd/ /fji/ Fame's Edge [famous] [edge; limit; verge]) are a group of temperate brushland within the temperate rainforest of the Cedarcoast region. Zrogd-fīi Brushlands are a relatively typical brushland for Cedarcoast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Zrogd-fīi Brushlands has a yearly average temperature of 29°C (84°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 32°C (89°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 25°C (77°F). The Zrogd-fīi Brushlands receives an average of 274 cm/y (107 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Zrogd-fīi Brushlands covers an area of nearly 24991 km2 (15532 mi2), and an average elevation of 3963 m (13001 ft) above sea level.
