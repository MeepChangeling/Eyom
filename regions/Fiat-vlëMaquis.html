The Fiat-vlë Maquis (/fjat/ /vlɛ/ Justway [way; manner; process; route; direction (way)] [justice]) is a subtropical maquis within the temperate rainforest of the Salt Warrens region. The Fiat-vlë Maquis is the political heartlands of the City-state of Kakoray.

Climate

The Fiat-vlë Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 13°C (55°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 2°C (35°F). The Fiat-vlë Maquis receives an average of 300 cm/y (118 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Fiat-vlë Maquis covers an area of nearly 14195 km2 (8822 mi2), and an average elevation of 2246 m (7368 ft) above sea level.
