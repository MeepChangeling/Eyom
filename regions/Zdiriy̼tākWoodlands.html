The Zdiriy̼tāk Woodlands (Zdiriy̼ /təˑc/ Zdiriy̼ the Zealot's [Proper Noun (name of person)] [passion]) are a group of subtropical woodland on the coast of the Granerie region. Zdiriy̼tāk Woodlands are a relatively typical woodland for Granerie, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Zdiriy̼tāk Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 12°C (53°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -2°C (29°F). The Zdiriy̼tāk Woodlands receives an average of 147 cm/y (57 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Zdiriy̼tāk Woodlands covers an area of nearly 9901 km2 (6153 mi2), and an average elevation of 4476 m (14685 ft) above sea level.

Their shared values and traditions include loyalty to one’s friends, family, and own and social progress toward some eventual utopia, as well as a unique harvest festival and a autumn festival.

They also place greater cultural emphasus on vengeance and execution of just vendettas and conquest and domination of others.

Local Religion

The Zdiriy̼tāk Woodlands are overseen by Potentate Umdione Ælfrone Apollo, a god serving within The Seven Titans, the region's principal religion. Approximately 24% of the population self-report as devout. The Zdiriy̼tāk Woodlands is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Zdiriy̼tāk Woodlands has its economic roots in Granerie's export of Agriculture, but has developed its own research industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.
