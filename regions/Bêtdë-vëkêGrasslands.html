The Bêtdë-vëkê Grasslands (/ˈvëkɘ/ Bêtdë's Chimes [Proper Noun (name of person)] [chord]) are a group of subtropical grassland on the coast of the Endless Peaks region. The Bêtdë-vëkê Grasslands are home to Eyom's purest chalk.

Climate

The Bêtdë-vëkê Grasslands has a yearly average temperature of 21°C (69°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 12°C (53°F). The Bêtdë-vëkê Grasslands receives an average of 136 cm/y (53 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Bêtdë-vëkê Grasslands covers an area of nearly 16240 km2 (10093 mi2), and an average elevation of 10257 m (-9298 ft) above sea level.
