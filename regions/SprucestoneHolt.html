The Sprucestone Holt (sprucestone /hoʊlt/ Sprucestone [Translation Unavailable]) is a temperate holt on the coast of the Pressingstone region. The Sprucestone Holt is well known for being largely barren of plant life.

Climate

The Sprucestone Holt has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). The Sprucestone Holt receives an average of 73 cm/y (28 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Sprucestone Holt covers an area of nearly 9575 km2 (5950 mi2), and an average elevation of 5604 m (18385 ft) above sea level.
