The Noqël Gra Maquis (/ˈnoqɛl/ /gra/ Greatmother [painful] [wife]) is a subtropical maquis on the coast of the Bulwark region. Noqël Gra Maquis is a relatively typical maquis for Bulwark, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Noqël Gra Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 10°C (50°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -5°C (23°F). The Noqël Gra Maquis receives an average of 293 cm/y (115 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Noqël Gra Maquis covers an area of nearly 11756 km2 (7306 mi2), and an average elevation of 3878 m (12723 ft) above sea level.

The Noqël Gra Maquis is's peoples are culturally Wareneese, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include raw strength and personal prowess and humanistic reason and “rational” religion, as well as a unique music festival and a winter festival.

Local Religion

The Noqël Gra Maquis is overseen by Potentate Ujun Alespollo Hodita, a god serving within The Council of Twelve, the region's principal religion. Approximately 52% of the population self-report as devout. The Noqël Gra Maquis is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Noqël Gra Maquis is a negative return economy zone at this time. It has an abundence of Armor, though infrastructure to this region of Bulwark is not developed enough to support any major local industry.
