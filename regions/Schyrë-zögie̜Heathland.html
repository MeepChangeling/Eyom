The Schyrë-zögie̜ Heathland (/scrɛ/ /ˈzo˞gje̜/ Northmother [north] [parent]) is a subtropical heathland within the taiga of the Stalfijord region. Schyrë-zögie̜ Heathland is a relatively typical heathland for Stalfijord, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Schyrë-zögie̜ Heathland has a yearly average temperature of 5°C (41°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold -17°C (2°F). The Schyrë-zögie̜ Heathland receives an average of 71 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Schyrë-zögie̜ Heathland covers an area of nearly 13106 km2 (8145 mi2), and an average elevation of 4691 m (15390 ft) above sea level.
