The Flirfihum Expanse (Flirfi /hum/ Flirfi's Flight [Proper Noun (name of person)] [wing]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Riverpack Coast region. Flirfihum Expanse is a relatively typical expanse for Riverpack Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Flirfihum Expanse has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 4°C (39°F). The Flirfihum Expanse receives an average of 203 cm/y (79 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Flirfihum Expanse covers an area of nearly 16790 km2 (10435 mi2), and an average elevation of 3435 m (11269 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Flirfihum Expanse is overseen by Potentate Phosoi Afrodite Alkyoniu, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 23% of the population self-report as devout. The Flirfihum Expanse is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
