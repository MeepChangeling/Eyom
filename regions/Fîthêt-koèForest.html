Climate

The Fîthêt-koè Forest has a yearly average temperature of 17°C (62°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 8°C (46°F). The Fîthêt-koè Forest receives an average of 270 cm/y (106 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Fîthêt-koè Forest covers an area of nearly 23473 km2 (14588 mi2), and an average elevation of 8656 m (-14550 ft) above sea level.
