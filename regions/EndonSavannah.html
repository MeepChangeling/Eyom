The Endon Savannah (endon /səˈvænə/ Endon [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical savanna on the coast of the Highpeaks region. Endon Savannah is a relatively typical savanna for Highpeaks, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Endon Savannah has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 20°C (68°F). The Endon Savannah receives an average of 68 cm/y (26 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Endon Savannah covers an area of nearly 10373 km2 (6446 mi2), and an average elevation of 8996 m (-13435 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Endon Savannah is overseen by Potentate Gutbus Aiapus Mnemer, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 36% of the population self-report as devout. The Endon Savannah is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Endon Savannah is a negative return economy zone at this time. It has an abundence of Elemental Crystals, though infrastructure to this region of Highpeaks is not developed enough to support any major local industry.
