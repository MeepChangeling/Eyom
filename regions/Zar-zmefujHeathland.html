The Zar-zmefuj Heathland (/zar/ /zmeˈfuʤ/ Author's [book] [dancer]) is a subtropical heathland on the coast of the Lower Warding Range region. Zar-zmefuj Heathland is a relatively typical heathland for Lower Warding Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Zar-zmefuj Heathland has a yearly average temperature of 32°C (89°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 36°C (96°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 29°C (84°F). The Zar-zmefuj Heathland receives an average of 65 cm/y (25 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Zar-zmefuj Heathland covers an area of nearly 5187 km2 (3223 mi2), and an average elevation of 14748 m (5436 ft) above sea level.

The Zar-zmefuj Heathland is home to 39007 people. They break down into the following demographics: 60% Tanuki, 25% Fae, 9% Grey Elf, 4% Imperial, and ~2% Other.

They also place greater cultural emphasus on individual rights and freedom of action and justice and fairness between people.

Local Religion

The Zar-zmefuj Heathland is overseen by Potentate Umus Frenone Gwalthniu, a god serving within Fawresm, the region's principal religion. Approximately 31% of the population self-report as devout. The Zar-zmefuj Heathland is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Zar-zmefuj Heathland is a negative return economy zone at this time. It has an abundence of Undead, though infrastructure to this region of Lower Warding Range is not developed enough to support any major local industry.
