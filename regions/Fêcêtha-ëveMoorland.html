The Fêcêtha-ëve Moorland (/fɘˈcɘθa/ /ˈëve/ Silvergoat [shiny] [goat]) is a subtropical moorland on the coast of the Copper Hills region. Fêcêtha-ëve Moorland is a relatively typical moorland for Copper Hills, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Fêcêtha-ëve Moorland has a yearly average temperature of 29°C (84°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 34°C (93°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 24°C (75°F). The Fêcêtha-ëve Moorland receives an average of 66 cm/y (25 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Fêcêtha-ëve Moorland covers an area of nearly 8678 km2 (5393 mi2), and an average elevation of 12700 m (-1283 ft) above sea level.

The Fêcêtha-ëve Moorland is overseen by Potentate Ryhelia Neofstandra Eógardwalo, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 24% of the population self-report as devout. The Fêcêtha-ëve Moorland is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
