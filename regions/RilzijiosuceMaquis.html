The Rilzijiosuce Maquis (/ʤoˈsuce/ Rilzi the Kitten [Proper Noun (name of person)] [kitten]) is a subtropical maquis within the temperate rainforest of the Equuscoast region. Rilzijiosuce Maquis is a relatively typical maquis for Equuscoast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Rilzijiosuce Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). The Rilzijiosuce Maquis receives an average of 295 cm/y (116 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Rilzijiosuce Maquis covers an area of nearly 20386 km2 (12669 mi2), and an average elevation of 2039 m (6689 ft) above sea level.
