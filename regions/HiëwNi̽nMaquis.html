The Hiëw Ni̽n Maquis (/hjɛw/ /ni̽n/ Bardpunch [arrogant] [melody; tune]) is a subtropical maquis within the wetland of the Yastan Peninsula region. Hiëw Ni̽n Maquis is a relatively typical maquis for Yastan Peninsula, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Hiëw Ni̽n Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 1°C (33°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool -23°C (-9°F). The Hiëw Ni̽n Maquis receives an average of 198 cm/y (77 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Hiëw Ni̽n Maquis covers an area of nearly 14003 km2 (8702 mi2), and an average elevation of 5532 m (18149 ft) above sea level.
