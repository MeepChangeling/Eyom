The Nar Zōv Brushlands (/nar/ /zoːv/ Tornveil [bankrupt; broke] [screen; curtain; veil; umbrella]) are a group of temperate brushland on the coast of the Riverpack Coast region. The Nar Zōv Brushlands are home to ancient arcane anomalies which appear for brief moments in random places. Should one chance upon one, they will be able to see a possible future of someone or something close to them.

Climate

The Nar Zōv Brushlands has a yearly average temperature of 14°C (57°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 1°C (33°F). The Nar Zōv Brushlands receives an average of 302 cm/y (118 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Nar Zōv Brushlands covers an area of nearly 6839 km2 (4250 mi2), and an average elevation of 3675 m (12057 ft) above sea level.
