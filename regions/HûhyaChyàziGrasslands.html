The Hûhya Chyàzi Grasslands (/ˈcəzi/ Hûhyas Small [Proper Noun (name of person)] [bit]) are a group of subtropical grassland on the coast of the Alchemists Garden region. Hûhya Chyàzi Grasslands are a relatively typical grassland for Alchemists Garden, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Hûhya Chyàzi Grasslands has a yearly average temperature of 22°C (71°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 31°C (87°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 13°C (55°F). The Hûhya Chyàzi Grasslands receives an average of 275 cm/y (108 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Hûhya Chyàzi Grasslands covers an area of nearly 12173 km2 (7565 mi2), and an average elevation of 2339 m (7673 ft) above sea level.
