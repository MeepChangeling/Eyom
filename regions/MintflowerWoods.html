The Mintflower Woods (mintflower /wʊdz/ Mintflower [Translation Unavailable]) are a group of subtropical woods on the coast of the Upper Warding Range region. Mintflower Woods are a relatively typical woods for Upper Warding Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Mintflower Woods has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). The Mintflower Woods receives an average of 140 cm/y (55 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Mintflower Woods covers an area of nearly 19222 km2 (11946 mi2), and an average elevation of 7842 m (-17221 ft) above sea level.
