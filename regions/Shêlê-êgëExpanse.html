The Shêlê-êgë Expanse (/ˈʃɘlɘ/ /ˈɘgë/ Thoughtplague [tactic] [plague]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Thunderfields region. Shêlê-êgë Expanse is a relatively typical expanse for Thunderfields, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Shêlê-êgë Expanse has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). The Shêlê-êgë Expanse receives an average of 145 cm/y (57 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Shêlê-êgë Expanse covers an area of nearly 13703 km2 (8516 mi2), and an average elevation of 4278 m (14035 ft) above sea level.

The Shêlê-êgë Expanse is's peoples are culturally Wareneese, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include dominating and possessing other people and ethnic purity of blood and culture, as well as a unique religious festival and a autumn festival.

Local Religion

The Shêlê-êgë Expanse is overseen by Potentate Udon Iapon Leald, a god serving within The Apprenticeship, the region's principal religion. Approximately 56% of the population self-report as devout. The Shêlê-êgë Expanse is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
