The Lolqaz Fef Woods (/lolˈqaz/ /fef/ Ivorymist [off white] [secret; mystery]) are a group of subtropical woods on the coast of the Golden Brooks region. Lolqaz Fef Woods are a relatively typical woods for Golden Brooks, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Lolqaz Fef Woods has a yearly average temperature of 27°C (80°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 30°C (86°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 24°C (75°F). The Lolqaz Fef Woods receives an average of 132 cm/y (51 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Lolqaz Fef Woods covers an area of nearly 17981 km2 (11175 mi2), and an average elevation of 5206 m (17080 ft) above sea level.
