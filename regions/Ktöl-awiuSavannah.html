The Ktöl-awiu Savannah (/kto˞l/ /aˈwiu/ Truthbeater [rod; shaft; bar; cylinder; stick; bat (club); pole; axle] [liar]) is a temperate savanna within the temperate rainforest of the Gnashed Coast region. The Ktöl-awiu Savannah is the political heartlands of the Pelalan City-state.

Climate

The Ktöl-awiu Savannah has a yearly average temperature of 9°C (48°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -8°C (18°F). The Ktöl-awiu Savannah receives an average of 289 cm/y (113 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Ktöl-awiu Savannah covers an area of nearly 18832 km2 (11704 mi2), and an average elevation of 2162 m (7093 ft) above sea level.
