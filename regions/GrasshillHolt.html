The Grasshill Holt (grasshill /hoʊlt/ Grasshill [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical holt on the coast of the Silicahilands region. Grasshill Holt is a relatively typical holt for Silicahilands, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Grasshill Holt has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 18°C (64°F). The Grasshill Holt receives an average of 212 cm/y (83 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Grasshill Holt covers an area of nearly 23249 km2 (14449 mi2), and an average elevation of 4854 m (15925 ft) above sea level.
