The Fèzî-rëpíêkè Expanse (/ˈfèzɪ/ /rëˈp˔ɘkè/ Follyflight [stupid; dumb; silly] [flight]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Greenrange region. Fèzî-rëpíêkè Expanse is a relatively typical expanse for Greenrange, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Fèzî-rëpíêkè Expanse has a yearly average temperature of 21°C (69°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 16°C (60°F). The Fèzî-rëpíêkè Expanse receives an average of 138 cm/y (54 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Fèzî-rëpíêkè Expanse covers an area of nearly 15799 km2 (9819 mi2), and an average elevation of 7250 m (-19163 ft) above sea level.

However, as our culture lacks a taboo for classification of peoples by their biology and such information is highly important for our medical practices amongst other institutions, we have stated the racial demographics of this region. We do not do so to sew the seeds of disunity. We do not do so to belittle or bemoan any group. Such issues are why we use the term 'peoples' rather than races, cultures, or species.

In respect to this universal cultural element, we have split racial classification by culture while keeping the name of each race for our own purposes. Should you see the same race listed more than once in the following section, this is to designate a major cultural division within a race.

The Fèzî-rëpíêkè Expanse is's peoples are culturally Sylvanian, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include justice and fairness between people and scheming subtly against enemies or rivals, as well as a unique wine festival and a literary festival.

Local Religion

The Fèzî-rëpíêkè Expanse is overseen by Potentate Ulios Eoghanthnis Andronanos, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 47% of the population self-report as devout. The Fèzî-rëpíêkè Expanse is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
