The Tuntro Hyo Grasslands (/ˈtuntro/ /hjo/ Cash [rich] [paper]) are a group of temperate grassland within the temperate rainforest of the Stalfijord region. The Tuntro Hyo Grasslands are the political heartlands of the Lallalan City-state.

Climate

The Tuntro Hyo Grasslands has a yearly average temperature of 10°C (50°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -2°C (29°F). The Tuntro Hyo Grasslands receives an average of 290 cm/y (114 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Tuntro Hyo Grasslands covers an area of nearly 10294 km2 (6397 mi2), and an average elevation of 1570 m (5150 ft) above sea level.

Tanuki

They also place greater cultural emphasus on ascetic unworldliness and pious poverty and sexual license and wantonness.

Imperial

While they follow the broader tennents of Gnoll culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of color choices with social meaning to them and base their sence of individual idenity on lineages based on ancient hero-ancestors. They also place greater cultural emphasus on aesthetic beauty in material goods and architecture and conquest and domination of others.
