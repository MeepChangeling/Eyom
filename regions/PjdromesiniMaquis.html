The Pjdromesini Maquis (/meˈsini/ Pjdro's Dunes [Proper Noun (name of person)] [dune]) is a subtropical maquis within the temperate rainforest of the Glowing Sands region. Pjdromesini Maquis is a relatively typical maquis for Glowing Sands, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Pjdromesini Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Pjdromesini Maquis receives an average of 275 cm/y (108 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Pjdromesini Maquis covers an area of nearly 17870 km2 (11106 mi2), and an average elevation of 1936 m (6351 ft) above sea level.

The Pjdromesini Maquis is overseen by Potentate Chizdos Iaponw Verðandra, a god serving within Therilism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 42% of the population self-report as devout. The Pjdromesini Maquis is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
