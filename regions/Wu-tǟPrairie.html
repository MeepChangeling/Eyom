Climate

The Wu-tǟ Prairie has a yearly average temperature of 9°C (48°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -5°C (23°F). The Wu-tǟ Prairie receives an average of 210 cm/y (82 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Wu-tǟ Prairie covers an area of nearly 9482 km2 (5893 mi2), and an average elevation of 2805 m (9202 ft) above sea level.
