The Hartcouche Shrublands (hartcouche shrublands Hartcouche [Translation Unavailable]) are a group of subtropical shrubland on the coast of the Ancestor's Range region. Hartcouche Shrublands are a relatively typical shrubland for Ancestor's Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Hartcouche Shrublands has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 20°C (68°F). The Hartcouche Shrublands receives an average of 74 cm/y (29 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Hartcouche Shrublands covers an area of nearly 13266 km2 (8244 mi2), and an average elevation of 4674 m (15334 ft) above sea level.

Imperial

They also place greater cultural emphasus on submission to the collective will or culture and ethnic purity of blood and culture.

Elf

The Elf subculture native to the Hartcouche Shrublands are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. Local Religion

The Hartcouche Shrublands are overseen by Potentate Rovbris Grodita Easta, a god serving within The Council of Twelve, the region's principal religion. Approximately 28% of the population self-report as devout. The Hartcouche Shrublands is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
