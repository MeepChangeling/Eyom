The Wolfhide Brushlands (wolfhide brushlands Wolfhide [Translation Unavailable]) are a group of subtropical brushland on the coast of the Lower Warding Range region. Wolfhide Brushlands are a relatively typical brushland for Lower Warding Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Wolfhide Brushlands has a yearly average temperature of 32°C (89°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 36°C (96°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 27°C (80°F). The Wolfhide Brushlands receives an average of 69 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Wolfhide Brushlands covers an area of nearly 13251 km2 (8235 mi2), and an average elevation of 11978 m (-3651 ft) above sea level.
