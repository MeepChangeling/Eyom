The Qinâ Kûbî Heath (/ˈqinɑ/ /ˈkʊbɪː/ Mistword [vague; fuzzy; obscure] [answer]) is a subtropical heath on the coast of the Filigree Coast region. Qinâ Kûbî Heath is a relatively typical heath for Filigree Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Qinâ Kûbî Heath has a yearly average temperature of 19°C (66°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 11°C (51°F). The Qinâ Kûbî Heath receives an average of 308 cm/y (121 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Qinâ Kûbî Heath covers an area of nearly 25794 km2 (16031 mi2), and an average elevation of 3365 m (11040 ft) above sea level.
