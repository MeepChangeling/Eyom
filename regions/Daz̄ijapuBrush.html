The Daz̄ijapu Brush (/ˈdaz̚i/ /ˈʤapu/ Greatfalls [gravity] [ugly; awful; terrible]) is a subtropical brush within the temperate rainforest of the Glowing Sands region. Daz̄ijapu Brush is a relatively typical brush for Glowing Sands, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Daz̄ijapu Brush has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 22°C (71°F). The Daz̄ijapu Brush receives an average of 274 cm/y (107 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Daz̄ijapu Brush covers an area of nearly 21448 km2 (13330 mi2), and an average elevation of 2958 m (9704 ft) above sea level.

The Daz̄ijapu Brush is's peoples are culturally Constructi, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include subtlety and indirectness of action and personal development and limit-pushing, as well as a unique food festival and a sun festival.

Local Religion

The Daz̄ijapu Brush is overseen by Potentate Roaos Nothenn Eartheus, a god serving within The Council of Twelve, the region's principal religion. Approximately 48% of the population self-report as devout. The Daz̄ijapu Brush is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Daz̄ijapu Brush has its economic roots in Glowing Sands's export of Glass, but has developed its own innovation industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.
