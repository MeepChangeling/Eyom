The Sundare Heathland (sundare heathland Sundare [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical heathland on the coast of the Ancestor's Range region. Sundare Heathland is a relatively typical heathland for Ancestor's Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Sundare Heathland has a yearly average temperature of 32°C (89°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 36°C (96°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 27°C (80°F). The Sundare Heathland receives an average of 65 cm/y (25 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Sundare Heathland covers an area of nearly 14061 km2 (8738 mi2), and an average elevation of 20048 m (-20125 ft) above sea level.

The Sundare Heathland is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Sundare Heathland has its economic roots in Ancestor's Range's export of Stone, but has developed its own cement industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.
