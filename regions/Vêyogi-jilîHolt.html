The Vêyogi-jilî Holt (/vɘˈjogi/ /ˈʤilɪ/ Tenderpot [sore] [lobster]) is a subtropical holt on the coast of the Cutmountains region. Vêyogi-jilî Holt is a relatively typical holt for Cutmountains, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Vêyogi-jilî Holt has a yearly average temperature of 30°C (86°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 35°C (95°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 26°C (78°F). The Vêyogi-jilî Holt receives an average of 66 cm/y (25 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Vêyogi-jilî Holt covers an area of nearly 8306 km2 (5162 mi2), and an average elevation of 12348 m (-2437 ft) above sea level.
