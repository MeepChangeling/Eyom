The Yc-9p Qib Maquis (/qib/ Yc-9p's Plan [Proper Noun (name of person)] [plan; program]) is a subtropical maquis on the coast of the Hammer Hills region. Yc-9p Qib Maquis is a relatively typical maquis for Hammer Hills, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Yc-9p Qib Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Yc-9p Qib Maquis receives an average of 74 cm/y (29 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Yc-9p Qib Maquis covers an area of nearly 10892 km2 (6769 mi2), and an average elevation of 9128 m (-13002 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Yc-9p Qib Maquis is overseen by Potentate Chyohr Osgaireya Kephanawn, a god serving within The Apprenticeship, the region's principal religion. Approximately 38% of the population self-report as devout. The Yc-9p Qib Maquis is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Yc-9p Qib Maquis is predominantly economicly reiant on Hammer Hills's export of Maple Syrup, and has no outstanding industries byond what is required for its role in that process.
