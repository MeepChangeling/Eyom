Climate

The Goycÿh Zhälz Woods has a yearly average temperature of 28°C (82°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 32°C (89°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 25°C (77°F). The Goycÿh Zhälz Woods receives an average of 281 cm/y (110 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Goycÿh Zhälz Woods covers an area of nearly 10457 km2 (6499 mi2), and an average elevation of 3748 m (12296 ft) above sea level.
