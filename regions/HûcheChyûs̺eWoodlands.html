The Hûche Chyûs̺e Woodlands (/ˈhʊʧe/ /ˈcʊs̺e/ Lakewood [strong; tough; solid; sturdy; robust] [flood]) are a group of subtropical woodland on the coast of the Thousand Plantations region. Hûche Chyûs̺e Woodlands are a relatively typical woodland for Thousand Plantations, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Hûche Chyûs̺e Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 18°C (64°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 9°C (48°F). The Hûche Chyûs̺e Woodlands receives an average of 149 cm/y (58 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Hûche Chyûs̺e Woodlands covers an area of nearly 18554 km2 (11531 mi2), and an average elevation of 4543 m (14904 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Hûche Chyûs̺e Woodlands are overseen by Potentate Nieus Glaean Gromedor, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 56% of the population self-report as devout. The Hûche Chyûs̺e Woodlands is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Hûche Chyûs̺e Woodlands has its economic roots in Thousand Plantations's export of Luxury Goods, but has developed its own potions industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.
