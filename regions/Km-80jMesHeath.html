The Km-80j Mes Heath (/mes/ Km-80j's Dread [Proper Noun (name of person)] [fear]) is a subtropical heath within the temperate rainforest of the Baymoulth region. Km-80j Mes Heath is a relatively typical heath for Baymoulth, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Km-80j Mes Heath has a yearly average temperature of 31°C (87°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 34°C (93°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 27°C (80°F). The Km-80j Mes Heath receives an average of 280 cm/y (110 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Km-80j Mes Heath covers an area of nearly 11052 km2 (6868 mi2), and an average elevation of 1619 m (5311 ft) above sea level.
