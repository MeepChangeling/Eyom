The Jajlzmaz̄okha Holt (/maˈz̚oxa/ Jajlz's [Proper Noun (name of person)] [hood]) is a subtropical holt within the temperate rainforest of the Galloping Plains region. Jajlzmaz̄okha Holt is a relatively typical holt for Galloping Plains, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Jajlzmaz̄okha Holt has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 23°C (73°F). The Jajlzmaz̄okha Holt receives an average of 282 cm/y (111 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Jajlzmaz̄okha Holt covers an area of nearly 24621 km2 (15302 mi2), and an average elevation of 3382 m (11095 ft) above sea level.

The Jajlzmaz̄okha Holt is home to 280305 people. They break down into the following demographics: 58% Tauric, 15% Taurans, 12% Taurans, 8% Grey Elf, 5% Elf, and ~2% Other.

The Jajlzmaz̄okha Holt is's peoples are culturally Tauranian, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include submission to lawful authority and exploring the unknown and discovering secrets, as well as a unique spring festival and a arcane festival.

Local Religion

The Jajlzmaz̄okha Holt is overseen by Potentate Drutyja Afriara Aiarawl, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 29% of the population self-report as devout. The Jajlzmaz̄okha Holt is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Jajlzmaz̄okha Holt has its economic roots in Galloping Plains's export of Ranch Products, but has developed its own canned food industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.
