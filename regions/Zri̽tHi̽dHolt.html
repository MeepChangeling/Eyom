The Zri̽t Hi̽d Holt (/zri̽t/ /hi̽d/ Sidegirl's [parallel] [chick (girl)]) is a subtropical holt within the wetland of the Westbay region. The Zri̽t Hi̽d Holt's name is derived from an old legend involving a Queen whose name has been lost to time, and her several mistresses. It is not a story for children. Except for goblin children. But even then...
