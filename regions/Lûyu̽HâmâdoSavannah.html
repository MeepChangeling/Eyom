The Lûyu̽ Hâmâdo Savannah (/hɑˈmɑdo/ Lûyu̽'s Mural [Proper Noun (name of person)] [mural]) is a temperate savanna on the coast of the Factorum region. Lûyu̽ Hâmâdo Savannah is a relatively typical savanna for Factorum, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Lûyu̽ Hâmâdo Savannah has a yearly average temperature of 20°C (68°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 12°C (53°F). The Lûyu̽ Hâmâdo Savannah receives an average of 227 cm/y (89 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Lûyu̽ Hâmâdo Savannah covers an area of nearly 9061 km2 (5631 mi2), and an average elevation of 3249 m (10659 ft) above sea level.
