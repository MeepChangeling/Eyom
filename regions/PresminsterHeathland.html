The Presminster Heathland (presminster heathland Presminster [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical heathland on the coast of the Greenrange region. Presminster Heathland is a relatively typical heathland for Greenrange, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Presminster Heathland has a yearly average temperature of 33°C (91°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 37°C (98°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 28°C (82°F). The Presminster Heathland receives an average of 69 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Presminster Heathland covers an area of nearly 11205 km2 (6963 mi2), and an average elevation of 15348 m (7404 ft) above sea level.
