The I Mënma Heathland (/i/ /ˈmɛnma/ Ivory [off white] [treasure]) is a temperate heathland on the coast of the Granerie region. I Mënma Heathland is a relatively typical heathland for Granerie, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The I Mënma Heathland has a yearly average temperature of 12°C (53°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -1°C (31°F). The I Mënma Heathland receives an average of 149 cm/y (58 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The I Mënma Heathland covers an area of nearly 13716 km2 (8524 mi2), and an average elevation of 3456 m (11338 ft) above sea level.

The I Mënma Heathland is overseen by Potentate Udon Galthniu Despolen, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 23% of the population self-report as devout. The I Mënma Heathland is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.
