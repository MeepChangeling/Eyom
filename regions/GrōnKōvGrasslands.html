The Grōn Kōv Grasslands (/groːn/ /koːv/ Whitedust [rigorous] [chalk]) are a group of temperate grassland within the wetland of the Stalfijord region. The Grōn Kōv Grasslands are the most peaceful border between any Gnollish nations.

Climate

The Grōn Kōv Grasslands has a yearly average temperature of 5°C (41°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool -15°C (5°F). The Grōn Kōv Grasslands receives an average of 213 cm/y (83 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Grōn Kōv Grasslands covers an area of nearly 13407 km2 (8332 mi2), and an average elevation of 2441 m (8008 ft) above sea level.
