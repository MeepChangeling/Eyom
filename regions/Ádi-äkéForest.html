The Ádi-äké Forest (/ə˞ˈdi/ /ɑx/ Unicorn [true] [lesbian]) is a temperate forest within the temperate rainforest of the Palebay region. Ádi-äké Forest is a relatively typical forest for Palebay, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Ádi-äké Forest has a yearly average temperature of 26°C (78°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 30°C (86°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 22°C (71°F). The Ádi-äké Forest receives an average of 271 cm/y (106 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Ádi-äké Forest covers an area of nearly 17189 km2 (10683 mi2), and an average elevation of 3216 m (10551 ft) above sea level.
