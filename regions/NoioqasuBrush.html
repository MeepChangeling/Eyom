Climate

The Noioqasu Brush has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Noioqasu Brush receives an average of 288 cm/y (113 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Noioqasu Brush covers an area of nearly 9524 km2 (5919 mi2), and an average elevation of 1781 m (5843 ft) above sea level.
