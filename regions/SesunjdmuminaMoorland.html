The Sesunjdmumina Moorland (/muˈmina/ Sesunjd's Shelf [Proper Noun (name of person)] [shelf; rack; ledge; counter (flat; elevated surface)]) is a subtropical moorland within the tropical rainforest of the Illa's Panhandle region. Sesunjdmumina Moorland is a relatively typical moorland for Illa's Panhandle, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Sesunjdmumina Moorland has a yearly average temperature of 19°C (66°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a cool 20°C (68°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). The Sesunjdmumina Moorland receives an average of 283 cm/y (111 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Sesunjdmumina Moorland covers an area of nearly 15173 km2 (9430 mi2), and an average elevation of 2139 m (7017 ft) above sea level.
