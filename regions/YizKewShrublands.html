The Yiz Kew Shrublands (/jiz/ /kew/ Lowroad [short; low] [trial]) are a group of subtropical shrubland on the coast of the Lower Warding Range region. Yiz Kew Shrublands are a relatively typical shrubland for Lower Warding Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Yiz Kew Shrublands has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 22°C (71°F). The Yiz Kew Shrublands receives an average of 68 cm/y (26 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Yiz Kew Shrublands covers an area of nearly 8833 km2 (5489 mi2), and an average elevation of 6737 m (-20846 ft) above sea level.
