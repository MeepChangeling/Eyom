The Ella Vdrëm Heathland (/ˈella/ /vdrɛm/ Sorted [nice] [file]) is a temperate heathland on the coast of the Abysstro region. The Ella Vdrëm Heathland is well known for being largely barren of plant life.

The Ella Vdrëm Heathland has a yearly average temperature of 14°C (57°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing 0°C (32°F). The Ella Vdrëm Heathland covers an area of nearly 16996 km2 (10563 mi2), and an average elevation of 2516 m (8254 ft) above sea level.
