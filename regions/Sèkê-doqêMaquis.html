The Sèkê-doqê Maquis (/ˈsèkɘ/ /ˈdoqɘ/ Sharpkey [keen] [key]) is a subtropical maquis on the coast of the Pressingstone region. Sèkê-doqê Maquis is a relatively typical maquis for Pressingstone, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Sèkê-doqê Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 22°C (71°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 18°C (64°F). The Sèkê-doqê Maquis receives an average of 74 cm/y (29 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Sèkê-doqê Maquis covers an area of nearly 19207 km2 (11937 mi2), and an average elevation of 6410 m (21030 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Sèkê-doqê Maquis is overseen by Potentate Ulios Goina Eadwulfric, a god serving within The Apprenticeship, the region's principal religion. Approximately 29% of the population self-report as devout. The Sèkê-doqê Maquis is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Sèkê-doqê Maquis has its economic roots in Pressingstone's export of Plant Oils, but has developed its own guns industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.

The Sèkê-doqê Maquis has an Imperial Development Index of 20. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 20 means there are 20 locations within the Sèkê-doqê Maquis which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
