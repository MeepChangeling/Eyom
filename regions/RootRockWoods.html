The Root Rock Woods (/rut/ /rɑk/ /wʊdz/ Root Rock [Translation Unavailable]) are a group of subtropical woods on the coast of the Greenrange region. Root Rock Woods are a relatively typical woods for Greenrange, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Root Rock Woods has a yearly average temperature of 27°C (80°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 32°C (89°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 23°C (73°F). The Root Rock Woods receives an average of 142 cm/y (55 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Root Rock Woods covers an area of nearly 10715 km2 (6659 mi2), and an average elevation of 10038 m (-10016 ft) above sea level.
