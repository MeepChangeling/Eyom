The Ecyec Eyd Moorland (/eˈkjic/ /ejd/ Droppedcycle [lazy] [moment]) is a subtropical moorland on the coast of the Highpeaks region. Ecyec Eyd Moorland is a relatively typical moorland for Highpeaks, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Ecyec Eyd Moorland has a yearly average temperature of 28°C (82°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 32°C (89°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 24°C (75°F). The Ecyec Eyd Moorland receives an average of 206 cm/y (81 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Ecyec Eyd Moorland covers an area of nearly 13777 km2 (8562 mi2), and an average elevation of 6875 m (-20393 ft) above sea level.
