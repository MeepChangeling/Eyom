The Kríhkshvag Heathland (Kríhksh /vag/ Kríhkshsight [Proper Noun (name of person)] [perception]) is a subtropical heathland on the coast of the Heartland region. Kríhkshvag Heathland is a relatively typical heathland for Heartland, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Kríhkshvag Heathland has a yearly average temperature of 17°C (62°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 8°C (46°F). The Kríhkshvag Heathland receives an average of 138 cm/y (54 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Kríhkshvag Heathland covers an area of nearly 11535 km2 (7169 mi2), and an average elevation of 3681 m (12076 ft) above sea level.

The Kríhkshvag Heathland is home to 202392 people. They break down into the following demographics: 65% Sylvin, 20% Iron Elves, 7% Imperial, 6% Kobold, and ~2% Other.

The Kríhkshvag Heathland is's peoples are culturally Sylvanian, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include honesty and truthfulness in speech and scheming subtly against enemies or rivals, as well as a unique history festival and a relic festival.

Sylvin

The Sylvin subculture native to the Kríhkshvag Heathland are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Sylvin culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of patterned hair shaving or depilation and base their sence of individual idenity on extended family out to cousins and like kin. They also place greater cultural emphasus on cunning and the ability to trick others and zealous guardianship of their own land or holy sit.

Iron Elf

The Iron Elf subculture native to the Kríhkshvag Heathland are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Sylvin culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of socially-meaningful animal motif items and base their sence of individual idenity on lineages based on ancient hero-ancestors. They also place greater cultural emphasus on harmony with nature and existing life and faithfulness towards one’s chosen friends.

Imperial

The Imperial subculture native to the Kríhkshvag Heathland are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Sylvin culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of tattoos of some cultural significance and base their sence of individual idenity on hereditary loyalty to a political group. They also place greater cultural emphasus on industry and the ability to work tirelessly and aesthetic beauty in material goods and architecture.

Kobold

The Kobold subculture native to the Kríhkshvag Heathland are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Sylvin culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of significant scent or perfume uses and base their sence of individual idenity on warbands or civic military service groups. They also place greater cultural emphasus on courage and valiance in danger and leadership and charisma in the group.

Local Religion

The Kríhkshvag Heathland is overseen by Potentate Rerborh Menthos Perðan, a god serving within Fawresm, the region's principal religion. Approximately 18% of the population self-report as devout. The Kríhkshvag Heathland is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.
