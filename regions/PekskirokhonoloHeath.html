The Pekskirokhonolo Heath (/ˈnolo/ Pekskirokho's Gaze [Proper Noun (name of person)] [eye]) is a temperate heath within the tropical rainforest of the Glowing Sands region. Pekskirokhonolo Heath is a relatively typical heath for Glowing Sands, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Pekskirokhonolo Heath has a yearly average temperature of 20°C (68°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 22°C (71°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 18°C (64°F). The Pekskirokhonolo Heath receives an average of 289 cm/y (113 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Pekskirokhonolo Heath covers an area of nearly 11381 km2 (7073 mi2), and an average elevation of 1017 m (3336 ft) above sea level.
