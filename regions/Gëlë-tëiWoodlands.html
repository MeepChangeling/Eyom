The Gëlë-tëi Woodlands (/ˈtëi/ Gëlë's Chior [Proper Noun (name of person)] [chorus]) are a group of subtropical woodland on the coast of the Thousand Plantations region. Gëlë-tëi Woodlands are a relatively typical woodland for Thousand Plantations, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Gëlë-tëi Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 19°C (66°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 10°C (50°F). The Gëlë-tëi Woodlands receives an average of 149 cm/y (58 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Gëlë-tëi Woodlands covers an area of nearly 12995 km2 (8076 mi2), and an average elevation of 4501 m (14767 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Gëlë-tëi Woodlands are overseen by Potentate Niella Alkyoncho Xandroncho, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 19% of the population self-report as devout. The Gëlë-tëi Woodlands is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Gëlë-tëi Woodlands is noteworthy for its economic independence from Thousand Plantations's export of Luxury Goods. Gëlë-tëi Woodlands is particularly renound for its engines industry. It is also has a noteworthy machine tools industry.
