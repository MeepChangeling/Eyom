The Jáxi-yike Savannah (/jiˈce/ Jáxi's Resolve [Proper Noun (name of person)] [nerve]) is a subtropical savanna within the wetland of the Accute Bay region. Jáxi-yike Savannah is a relatively typical savanna for Accute Bay, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Jáxi-yike Savannah has a yearly average temperature of 28°C (82°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 32°C (89°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 24°C (75°F). The Jáxi-yike Savannah receives an average of 224 cm/y (88 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Jáxi-yike Savannah covers an area of nearly 14677 km2 (9121 mi2), and an average elevation of 2685 m (8809 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Jáxi-yike Savannah is overseen by Potentate Qianke Eudous Glaeanos, a god serving within Covenant of Irus, the region's principal religion. Approximately 53% of the population self-report as devout. The Jáxi-yike Savannah is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Jáxi-yike Savannah is predominantly economicly reiant on Accute Bay's export of Uranium, and has no outstanding industries byond what is required for its role in that process.

The Jáxi-yike Savannah has an Imperial Development Index of 11. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 11 means there are 11 locations within the Jáxi-yike Savannah which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
