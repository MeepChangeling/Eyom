The Mo̠s-kaf Brush (/mo̠s/ /caf/ Rootball [pile; heap] [root]) is a subtropical brush on the coast of the Timberlund region. Mo̠s-kaf Brush is a relatively typical brush for Timberlund, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Mo̠s-kaf Brush has a yearly average temperature of 8°C (46°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -7°C (20°F). The Mo̠s-kaf Brush receives an average of 146 cm/y (57 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Mo̠s-kaf Brush covers an area of nearly 18494 km2 (11494 mi2), and an average elevation of 4717 m (15475 ft) above sea level.
