The Mx-34t Nělǐlna Savannah (/nɛˈlɪlna/ Mx-34t's Lust [Proper Noun (name of person)] [erection]) is a subtropical savanna within the wetland of the Flanged Coast region. Mx-34t Nělǐlna Savannah is a relatively typical savanna for Flanged Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Mx-34t Nělǐlna Savannah has a yearly average temperature of 26°C (78°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 31°C (87°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Mx-34t Nělǐlna Savannah receives an average of 226 cm/y (88 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Mx-34t Nělǐlna Savannah covers an area of nearly 13033 km2 (8100 mi2), and an average elevation of 1968 m (6456 ft) above sea level.
