Local Religion

The Bumahi àro Moorland is overseen by Potentate Bilna Osgandronos Alenthniu, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 46% of the population self-report as devout. The Bumahi àro Moorland is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Bumahi àro Moorland has its economic roots in Endless Peaks's export of Stone, but has developed its own furniture industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.
