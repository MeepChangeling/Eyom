The Nightrivers Woodlands (nightrivers /ˈwʊˌdlændz/ Nightrivers [Translation Unavailable]) are a group of temperate woodland within the temperate deciduous forest of the Palebay region. Nightrivers Woodlands are a relatively typical woodland for Palebay, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Nightrivers Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 22°C (71°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 17°C (62°F). The Nightrivers Woodlands receives an average of 139 cm/y (54 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Nightrivers Woodlands covers an area of nearly 14460 km2 (8986 mi2), and an average elevation of 3678 m (12066 ft) above sea level.
