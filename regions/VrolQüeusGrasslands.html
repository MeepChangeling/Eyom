The Vrol Qüeus Grasslands has a yearly average temperature of 7°C (44°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant -10°C (14°F). The Vrol Qüeus Grasslands receives an average of 149 cm/y (58 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Vrol Qüeus Grasslands covers an area of nearly 18412 km2 (11443 mi2), and an average elevation of 4870 m (15977 ft) above sea level.
