Cecholvo Udweht Ÿhkphÿh (/kicholˈvo/ /uˈdwɪ̘t/ [cultural] [stay]) is a subtropical Large City located in Shävncald Hold, Qättza Kingdom, within the Undermountain.

The name Cecholvo Udweht Ÿhkphÿh is derived from the Goblin language, as Cecholvo Udweht Ÿhkphÿh was founded by Hive Mind Beetle Swarm , who was culturaly Undermountain.

Cecholvo Udweht Ÿhkphÿh has a yearly average temperature of 22°C (71°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 18°C (64°F). Cecholvo Udweht Ÿhkphÿh receives an average of 273 cm/y (107 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. Cecholvo Udweht Ÿhkphÿh covers an area of nearly 84 km2 (33 mi2), and an average elevation of 1474 m (4835 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Cecholvo Udweht Ÿhkphÿh was founded durring the early 13th century in spring of the year 1242, by Hive Mind Beetle Swarm . The establishment of Cecholvo Udweht Ÿhkphÿh suffered from many setbacks, delays, and obsticles, most notably a group of Cecholvo Udweht Ÿhkphÿh which required millitary assistance exterminate before the community could finish being built.

Cecholvo Udweht Ÿhkphÿh was built using the conventions of Undermountain durring the early 13th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Cecholvo Udweht Ÿhkphÿh is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature waddle and daub construction with good timber frames and a stone foundation protected by thatched or shingled rooves. Most buildings with second floors are built in such a way as to overhang into the streets on the upper floors for more space, as building size seems to be the primary indicator of wealth within the community. Most buildings are not decorated with any integral features, but instead use ivy, flowers, and other natural elements in planters of on trellices to breathe life into the structure they grow upon.

Cecholvo Udweht Ÿhkphÿh is buildings are located arround a single narrow packed earth mainstreet which forms a clockwise spiral to give the city a over all circular shape. The city sits behind a stone-renforced palisade wall, with stone gatehouses and timber drawbridges for their trench. Cecholvo Udweht Ÿhkphÿh's would-be-castle fortifications has sufferd soem light damage, reducing its function a little in some spots, but could almsot certainly preform as expected... Though some of the worse spots could lead to the loss of defenders lives if attackers identified the weaknesses ahead of time.

Cecholvo Udweht Ÿhkphÿh has the unmistakable air of a city on its last legs. Everything is a bit slipshod and ramshackle. Everyone is at work, or drinking. No one has anything in their eyes other than fear and despair. Whatever industry once fueled Cecholvo Udweht Ÿhkphÿh ’s existence has dried up and the city is drifting down the stream of history as it dries up.

Cultural Notes

Cecholvo Udweht Ÿhkphÿh's bank was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is known primarily for its use of abstraction and simplicity. Clean lines, right angles, and primary colors characterized this aesthetic and art movement expressed via architecture and paintings. Its design ethos allows only primary colors and non-colors, only squares and rectangles, only straight and horizontal or vertical lines. Vertical and horizontal lines are positioned in layers or planes that do not intersect, thereby allowing each element to exist independently and unobstructed by other elements. These seemingly impossible principals for an architectural style coalesces into structures which most experts find hard to put into words. It is not that their geometry is impossible, but rather the style's attempt at producing works only describable visually was most successful..

The Mercane near Cecholvo Udweht Ÿhkphÿh are known to be a mutant strain of the creature.

Cecholvo Udweht Ÿhkphÿh's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in spring and involves creating small tokens to channel Chronomancy energies of tier 2 via oath swearing.
