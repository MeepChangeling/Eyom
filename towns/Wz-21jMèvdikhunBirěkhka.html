Wz-21j Mèvdikhun Birěkhka (/ob/ /mɤvˈdixun/ [famous] [spear; lance; pike; dart]) is a subtropical Town located in Lèljĭih Zone, If-70cqǐnrì District, within the Federation of Alveria.

The name Wz-21j Mèvdikhun Birěkhka is derived from the Constructi language, as Wz-21j Mèvdikhun Birěkhka was founded by Nīki̋nḱ Ya̋mé̄b 'Dixon Rosemary' Rawmé̄ Ha̋mërmē Yoīb̪f̄̄ Trtèj, who was culturaly Constructi.

Climate

Wz-21j Mèvdikhun Birěkhka has a yearly average temperature of 12°C (53°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a cool 15°C (59°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 9°C (48°F). Wz-21j Mèvdikhun Birěkhka receives an average of 224 cm/y (88 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Wz-21j Mèvdikhun Birěkhka covers an area of nearly 4 km2 (1 mi2), and an average elevation of 2068 m (6784 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Wz-21j Mèvdikhun Birěkhka was founded durring the early 12th century in winter of the year 1148, by Nīki̋nḱ Ya̋mé̄b 'Dixon Rosemary' Rawmé̄ Ha̋mërmē Yoīb̪f̄̄ Trtèj. The establishment of the new community went well, though many minor issues had to be solved as time went on. This was enough to delay construction and push back the formal opening ceramony, leading to some embarisment for Nīki̋nḱ Ya̋mé̄b 'Dixon Rosemary' Rawmé̄ Ha̋mërmē Yoīb̪f̄̄ Trtèj.

Wz-21j Mèvdikhun Birěkhka was built using the conventions of Constructi durring the early 12th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Wz-21j Mèvdikhun Birěkhka is no diffrent. The town's buildings feature waddle and daub construction with good timber frames and a stone foundation protected by thatched or shingled rooves. Most buildings with second floors are built in such a way as to overhang into the streets on the upper floors for more space, as building size seems to be the primary indicator of wealth within the community. Most buildings are not decorated with any integral features, but instead use ivy, flowers, and other natural elements in planters of on trellices to breathe life into the structure they grow upon.

Wz-21j Mèvdikhun Birěkhka is buildings are arranged arrounded highly ordered system of spacious cobblestone streets which form octogonal paterns, allowing the incides of the octagons to be common grounds for the buildings on the edges, be it for parkland, yardspace, plazas, or markets. The town is protected by a renforced stone fence which sits atop earthwork defences, for some of the best inexpencive defences a town of Wz-21j Mèvdikhun Birěkhka's size could have. The well-designed, yet cheep have not been wellmaintained over the years, and while functional are in dire need of some loving care and perhapse light renovation.

A look around Wz-21j Mèvdikhun Birěkhka makes it abundantly clear the town suffered something horrible some time ago. It's as if the town itself is depressed. Smiles are few, cheer is nowhere to be had. Everyone quietly goes about their daily business not looking anyone in the eye.

Cultural Notes

Wz-21j Mèvdikhun Birěkhka's chapel was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is best known for the combined use of arcuated and trabeated construction, employing arches and constructed with post and lintel. The arch served as the style's chief structural element, with flanking columns serving as buttresses or decorations. While the general shape of their structures could be generously described as simplistic, the use of these arches and columns created a most distinct ascetic which is easily distinguishable on sight to armatures and experts alike.

The ﻿Lynx (Cat, Great) near Wz-21j Mèvdikhun Birěkhka are known to be more aggressive than normal.

Wz-21j Mèvdikhun Birěkhka's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in autumn and involves orgies to channel Summoning energies of tier 3 via oratory performances.

In time immemorial, reportedly some time during the late 2nd century a most peculiar disaster struck Wz-21j Mèvdikhun Birěkhka, causing great chunks of ice to fall from the sky. Wz-21j Mèvdikhun Birěkhka lost 281 people, 345 livestock, and 79 buildings in the disaster.. The ice rain is generally remembered as the Horror Rain.
