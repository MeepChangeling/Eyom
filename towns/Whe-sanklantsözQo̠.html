Whe-sanklantsöz Qo̠ (/whe/ /sanˈklantso˞z/ [debt] [temperature]) is a tropical Large City located in the Nofe County of the Viceroyalty of Rosid.

The name Whe-sanklantsöz Qo̠ is derived from the Wood Elvish language, as Whe-sanklantsöz Qo̠ was founded by Dethroc Khirsa, who was culturaly Wood Elven.

Climate

Whe-sanklantsöz Qo̠ has a yearly average temperature of 13°C (55°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 1°C (33°F). Whe-sanklantsöz Qo̠ receives an average of 148 cm/y (58 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Whe-sanklantsöz Qo̠ covers an area of nearly 268 km2 (107 mi2), and an average elevation of 3958 m (12985 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Whe-sanklantsöz Qo̠ was founded durring the late 14th century in spring of the year 1285, by Dethroc Khirsa. The establishment of Whe-sanklantsöz Qo̠ was plagued by a lack of willing colonists. After attempts to pay people to resettle failed Dethroc Khirsa struck deals with nearby nations and communities to establish Whe-sanklantsöz Qo̠ as a prison colony.

Whe-sanklantsöz Qo̠ was built using the conventions of Wood Elven durring the late 14th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Whe-sanklantsöz Qo̠ is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature delicute timber framework hidden behind layer upon layer of finly ground plaster bleached to an almost glossy white sheen, with green clay tiled roofs and decorative brass-leafed trim. Even the smallest, poorest looking structures appear to be expencive thanks to the extreem elegence of the organic shapes and paterns going into their lofty, spire-y, vagly gothic designs. The more well off folks live in identicle homes, save for even shiner trim and a more whimsical appearance to their structures flowing forms.

Whe-sanklantsöz Qo̠ is buildings are built arround a single broad packed earth mainstreet which forms a counterclockwise spiral to give the city a over all circular shape. The mountain city is the proud owner of a thick set of fortified walls fashioned from querried stone blocks. While not up to snuff for a fort or castle wall, the city's walls are naturaly much larger than those of forts or castles. Therefore, the construction such a wall is most expencive. Whe-sanklantsöz Qo̠'s buget focused wall would serve its community well in battle in spite of looking unimpressive compared to castles and fortresses. Unfortuantly, these top tier civilian fortifications are in extreem disrepair, so much so that one cannot tell if they are decaying from a lack of mantance or damage incured.

A look around Whe-sanklantsöz Qo̠ gives you an uneasy feeling. Everything is just a little too worn down, a little too dirty, or both. No one makes eye contact. Kids play quietly, but happily. Occasionally a passerby glances at you out of the corner of their eye, staring just long enough for it to be uncomfortable. A second look around Whe-sanklantsöz Qo̠ makes it abundantly clear the city suffered something horrible some time ago. It's as if the town itself is depressed. Smiles are few, cheer is nowhere to be had.

Cultural Notes

Whe-sanklantsöz Qo̠'s chapel was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is known for its fluid and florid elaborate style, comprising ornate, asymmetric designs and pastel shades. It is often considered to be a playful, light style, which made exuberant use of curves and emphasized subtle asymmetry in the general shape of its structures. Walls, ceilings and moldings are decorated with numerous interlacing of curves and counter-curves based on the shapes of ‘C’ and ‘S’, along with shell forms and other naturalistic shapes.

Due to the actions of local Kami, winter is skipped in Whe-sanklantsöz Qo̠.

The ﻿Ovinnik near Whe-sanklantsöz Qo̠ are known to be more aggressive than normal.

Whe-sanklantsöz Qo̠'s citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in summer and involves long periods of drunkenness to channel Illusion energies of tier 1 via proclamations.

History

Whe-sanklantsöz Qo̠ is in secret rebellion against their ostensible liege, having cut deals with his enemies, plotted to betray him for their own gain, or bridled under his tyranny and sought a better lord. The community’s leadership is all in on this plot, and outside viceroys or representatives are being kept carefully ignorant of the reality. The common folk may be oblivious to the truth, though they’ll doubtless have felt the same motivations and promptings that convinced their leaders to turn traitor.
