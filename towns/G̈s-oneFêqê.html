G̈s-one Fêqê (/ˈcëʃo/ /ˈone/ [loose; easy] [carrot]) is a subtropical Town located in the Bëmtlaci Zone of the Union of Engineers.

The name G̈s-one Fêqê is derived from the Iron Elvish language, as G̈s-one Fêqê was founded by Tsheshêbê Garêyêv, who was culturaly Iron Elven.

Climate

G̈s-one Fêqê has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 24°C (75°F). G̈s-one Fêqê receives an average of 211 cm/y (83 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. G̈s-one Fêqê covers an area of nearly 6 km2 (2 mi2), and an average elevation of 3754 m (12316 ft) above sea level.

Overview

G̈s-one Fêqê was founded durring the early 13th century in spring of the year 1237, by Tsheshêbê Garêyêv. The establishment of G̈s-one Fêqê suffered from many setbacks, delays, and obsticles, most notably a group of G̈s-one Fêqê which required millitary assistance exterminate before the community could finish being built.

G̈s-one Fêqê was built using the conventions of Iron Elven durring the early 13th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and G̈s-one Fêqê is no diffrent. The town's buildings feature timber framed wooden shiethed or brick construction, which gives form to a very formalized, rational, expence effishent arcatectural style based on strictly symmetrical designs which universaly feature pitched roofs, shutters, and the occasional column or pilaster for a decorative touch.

G̈s-one Fêqê is buildings are arranged arrounded highly ordered system of premissive flagstone streets which form hexical paterns, allowing the incides of the octagons to be common grounds for the buildings on the edges, be it for parkland, yardspace, plazas, or markets. The town rests behind a thick wall made from clay bricks. The wall has all of the proper fortifications and is well made. Unfortuantly the nature of clay brick leaves it quite vulnerable to siege equipment, though the thickness of the wall lends it simmilar resistnace to a thinner hardrock wall. The town's millitarily questionable fortifications have recently undergone extensive repairs and renovations, such that the repairwork is imeadiently apparent and can be spotted due to the diffring ages of materials. One can't help but wonder what brought the need for those repairs to the town.

Your first impression of G̈s-one Fêqê proves to be right on the money. This town is where the rebels chose to settle. Fashion trends are quite simply the opposite of what’s popular in the other nearby communities. THis trend extends everywhere. Things that should be black are white, what would normally be square is organically curved, and what is typically unthinkable for polite society is more than welcome in this public square. Up to the logical limits, of course. There’s no flaying someone alive to eat their skin going on, but the women are dressed a little immodestly, the men are a little more emotional, and the food is all overly complex and terrible.

Civic Infrastructure

G̈s-one Fêqê has an animal control department which works to enforce local ordinances relating to the control, impoundment, and disposition of animals.

G̈s-one Fêqê has an Office of Civil Groundskeeping, which works to enforce local ordinances relating to the construction and upkeep up of all plant life, water features, and other natural decorations within G̈s-one Fêqê. They are also responsible for the maintenance of these features. Notably, the OCG is not responsible for G̈s-one Fêqê's parks.

G̈s-one Fêqê has an Office of Civil Vicary, which is responsible for providing a livelyhood for all officialy recognised religious figures within G̈s-one Fêqê.

G̈s-one Fêqê has a Gravedigger's Guild, which is responsible for collecting the dead and laying them to rest according to all applicable laws and religious customs.

G̈s-one Fêqê has a Department of Firefighters, which is responsible for organizing fire fighting efforts during a fire and enforcing local ordinances relating to fire safety.

G̈s-one Fêqê has a Highwayman's Guild, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

G̈s-one Fêqê has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain G̈s-one Fêqê's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

G̈s-one Fêqê possesses an older civil lighting system consisting of street lamps. These lights provide nighttime illumination to most city streets.

G̈s-one Fêqê has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands. They are not to be confused with the Office of Civil Groundskeeping as they do not hold authority over nor responsibility for G̈s-one Fêqê's natural decorations nor waterways.

G̈s-one Fêqê has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

G̈s-one Fêqê has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Cultural Notes

G̈s-one Fêqê's bank was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is which made use of the classical orders and mathematically precise ratios of height and width combined with a desire for symmetry, proportion, and harmony. It used columns, pediments, arches and domes are imaginatively in buildings of all types. Decorative features were seen as largely unnecessary as the sheer beauty of the structure itself was often close to art. However, many buildings with large ceiling spaces had their ceilings decorated with elaborate paintings, simply because the large flat spaces could feel wasted.

Due to the actions of local Kami, summer is skipped in G̈s-one Fêqê.

The ﻿Domovoi near G̈s-one Fêqê are known to be more aggressive than normal.

G̈s-one Fêqê's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in autumn and involves sex to channel Elven High Magic energies of tier 3 via speaking in tongues.
