Bearley Citidel (/bearley/ [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical Large City located in Shadewinter Dutchy, Sifate Kingdom, within the Sylvan.

The name Bearley Citidel is derived from the Sylvin language, as Bearley Citidel was founded by Balcheth Philipps, who was culturaly Sylvin.

Climate

Bearley Citidel has a yearly average temperature of 18°C (64°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 9°C (48°F). Bearley Citidel receives an average of 146 cm/y (57 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. Bearley Citidel covers an area of nearly 133 km2 (53 mi2), and an average elevation of 2436 m (7992 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Bearley Citidel was founded durring the late 14th century in winter of the year 1298, by Balcheth Philipps. The establishment of Bearley Citidel suffered from several major issues, resulting in the need to develop many solutions to basic problems. Problems such as a lack of fresh water, logistical support, poor quality tools, and the odd monster or two. Howeaver, these were overcome in time.

Bearley Citidel was built using the conventions of Sylvin durring the late 14th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Bearley Citidel is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature plaster covered brick construction taking the shape of large simple gemometric shapes which ate then embelished with decorative features, but retain their simple shapes in spite of the added features such as pillars, faccaddes, and covered walkways. The blocky nature gives a general impression of extreem age, as if each structure has existed since time immemorial.

Bearley Citidel is is constructed arround a series of crampt worn bedrock mainstreets which form overlapping circles, with smaller strait roads linking the circiles to eachother at varrious points. The city rests behind a thick wall made from clay bricks. The wall has all of the proper fortifications and is well made. Unfortuantly the nature of clay brick leaves it quite vulnerable to siege equipment, though the thickness of the wall lends it simmilar resistnace to a thinner hardrock wall. The city's millitarily questionable fortifications are visibly old, but also obviously well maintained. Its likly the local malishia or garrison are tasked with routine mantance of the city's defences.

Bearley Citidel is a bustling hive of activity. Everyone moves at nothing short of a jog, each convocation is a mile a minute, and there’s dedicated lanes for riding through town in the center of each street. The locals all appear to be not merely occupied, but in a true hurry for everything from drinking a pint of ale to their daily work.

Bearley Citidel has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain Bearley Citidel's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

Bearley Citidel has an Arcane Academy which provides higher education in the arcane sciences.

Bearley Citidel possesses a Galvanic Power Grid, which brings galvanic current to most if not all buildings in town, and permits a great many consumer goods to function within the Large City. Bearley Citidel's grid is powered by an arcane means.

Bearley Citidel possesses an older civil lighting system consisting of street lamps. In spite of the Galvanic Grid, these lights continue to use their old fule sources to provide nighttime illumination to all city streets.

Bearley Citidel has a first rate hospital which caters to anyone in need of long term medical care.

Bearley Citidel has a library, which keeps a large collection of books, scrolls, and archives all manner of physical items. The library is open to the public, including the Aether Link.

Bearley Citidel has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands. They are not to be confused with the Office of Civil Groundskeeping as they do not hold authority over nor responsibility for Bearley Citidel's natural decorations nor waterways.

Bearley Citidel has a Guild of Roadworkers, who are responsible for maintaining the roadways and public paths within town. They also have the duty of enforcing all civil laws relating to the roadways.

Bearley Citidel has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Bearley Citidel has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Cultural Notes

Bearley Citidel's chapel was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is famous for its stately symmetry, classical elements, and grand appearance. Columns and pillars, such as Corinthian columns, are often seen supporting open structures or porticos. Symmetry is an important feature of this style, with each half of a building mirroring the other. Domed ceilings and windows grace these buildings, with everything placed in a mathematical arrangement.

In Bearley Citidel birds speak prophesy.

The Dlurgraven near Bearley Citidel are known to be almost tame, such that they can be put to domestic use.

Bearley Citidel's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in summer and involves orgies to channel Illusion energies of tier 3 via guttural bellowing.
