Counter's Castle (/ˈkaʊntərz/ [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical Large City located in Silvergold Dutchy, Emzada Kingdom, within the Sylvan.

The name Counter's Castle is derived from the Sylvin language, as Counter's Castle was founded by Thilioril Goodgame, who was culturaly Sylvin.

Climate

Counter's Castle has a yearly average temperature of 17°C (62°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 9°C (48°F). Counter's Castle receives an average of 135 cm/y (53 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. Counter's Castle covers an area of nearly 55 km2 (22 mi2), and an average elevation of 4514 m (14809 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Counter's Castle was founded durring the early 12th century in spring of the year 1050, by Thilioril Goodgame. The establishment of Counter's Castle was somewhat plagued by a lack of willing colonists, leading to Thilioril Goodgame electing to pay people to resettle in Counter's Castle.

Counter's Castle was built using the conventions of Sylvin durring the early 12th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Counter's Castle is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature waddle and daub construction with good timber frames and a stone foundation protected by thatched or shingled rooves. Most buildings with second floors are built in such a way as to overhang into the streets on the upper floors for more space, as building size seems to be the primary indicator of wealth within the community. Most buildings are not decorated with any integral features, but instead use ivy, flowers, and other natural elements in planters of on trellices to breathe life into the structure they grow upon.

Counter's Castle is buildings are arranged within a network of restrictive split-log ties streets which form a rectangular grid, where each block verries in size given the proximity of the paralell streets forming each section. The ocasional smaller block has been used to construct a park, plaza, and other communal structures. The city has a defencive wall made from large clay bricks. The wall is constructed to the exact specifications of millitary fortifications, but the nature of its clay brick construction leaves it vulnerable to even outdated siege equipment. That said, the city is well defended against anything short of an army. Astonishigly, the brittle defences are in pristine condishion, as if they had just been finished before you laied eyes upon them.

Right off the bat Counter's Castle hits you in the face with its success. Everyone, even the peasants, are dressed in well made clothing. Every tool and implement you can see is finely made, and people will boast to you as obvious strangers of the wonders which can be found in their markets. More interestingly is a total lack of beggars, and plenty of new buildings are going up even as you speak. Somehow this city has come into quite a lot of wealth, and recently from the looks of things. The new wealth has created some worrying attitudes in many passers by, such that it’s clear new laws have recently been enacted and enforced with such extreme scrutiny the locals seem to be going about their day as if by clockwork.

Civic Infrastructure

Counter's Castle has an animal control department which works to enforce local ordinances relating to the control, impoundment, and disposition of animals.

Counter's Castle has an Office of Civil Groundskeeping, which works to enforce local ordinances relating to the construction and upkeep up of all plant life, water features, and other natural decorations within Counter's Castle. They are also responsible for the maintenance of these features. Notably, the OCG is not responsible for Counter's Castle's parks.

Counter's Castle has an Office of Civil Vicary, which is responsible for providing a livelyhood for all officialy recognised religious figures within Counter's Castle.

Counter's Castle has a government-funded child care program, overseen by the local Department of Nursemaids, which is responsible for providing childcare to working-class citizens according to local ordinances.

Counter's Castle has a Gravedigger's Guild, which is responsible for collecting the dead and laying them to rest according to all applicable laws and religious customs.

Counter's Castle has a Guild of Nurses, which is tasked with caring for the elderly and infirm in accordance with local ordinances, religious values, and customs.

Counter's Castle has a Department of Firefighters, which is responsible for organizing fire fighting efforts during a fire and enforcing local ordinances relating to fire safety.

Counter's Castle has a Highwayman's Guild, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Counter's Castle has a Hall of Slayers, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Counter's Castle has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain Counter's Castle's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

Counter's Castle has an Millitary Academy which trains military officers and specilists.

Counter's Castle possesses a Galvanic Power Grid, which brings galvanic current to most if not all buildings in town, and permits a great many consumer goods to function within the Large City. Counter's Castle's grid is powered by a boiler and turbine based power plant.

Counter's Castle's old civil lighting system was converted to Galvanic Lamps recently, and expanded to provide nighttime illumination to all city streets.

Counter's Castle has a first rate hospital which caters to anyone in need of long term medical care.

Counter's Castle has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands. They are not to be confused with the Office of Civil Groundskeeping as they do not hold authority over nor responsibility for Counter's Castle's natural decorations nor waterways.

Counter's Castle has a Guild of Roadworkers, who are responsible for maintaining the roadways and public paths within town. They also have the duty of enforcing all civil laws relating to the roadways.

Counter's Castle has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Counter's Castle has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Cultural Notes

Counter's Castle's bank was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is characterized by grandeur of scale, simplicity of geometric forms, dramatic use of columns, and a preference for blank walls. This design ethos extended to re-imagining earlier styles of structure to create them anew, with a similar overall look and feel to one another..

In Counter's Castle every night at precisely midnight every structure in town is engulfed by sailors fire until the end of the witching hour.

The Raven Swarm near Counter's Castle are known to be a mutant strain of the creature.

Counter's Castle's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in spring and involves long periods of drunkenness to channel Illusion energies of tier 1 via throat chanting.
