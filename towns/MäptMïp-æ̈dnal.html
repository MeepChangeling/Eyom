Mäpt Mïp-æ̈dnal (/mɪp/ /æ̝dˈnal/ [meat; flesh] [happiness]) is a temperate Town located in the Veláïkulk Principality of the City-state of Tano.

The name Mäpt Mïp-æ̈dnal is derived from the Gnollish language, as Mäpt Mïp-æ̈dnal was founded by Mesopedia vyim Tæ̝Lʤine, who was culturaly Gnollish.

Climate

Mäpt Mïp-æ̈dnal has a yearly average temperature of 2°C (35°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 16°C (60°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool -12°C (11°F). Mäpt Mïp-æ̈dnal receives an average of 205 cm/y (80 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Mäpt Mïp-æ̈dnal covers an area of nearly 5 km2 (2 mi2), and an average elevation of 2072 m (6797 ft) above sea level.

Civic Infrastructure

Mäpt Mïp-æ̈dnal possesses a city-wide Aethary Link which provides Aethary access anywhere within its metropolitan. This allows citizens who can afford the relevant devices access in their places of work, and rarely homes.

Mäpt Mïp-æ̈dnal has an Office of Civil Vicary, which is responsible for providing a livelyhood for all officialy recognised religious figures within Mäpt Mïp-æ̈dnal.

Mäpt Mïp-æ̈dnal has a Gravedigger's Guild, which is responsible for collecting the dead and laying them to rest according to all applicable laws and religious customs.

Mäpt Mïp-æ̈dnal has a Department of Firefighters, which is responsible for organizing fire fighting efforts during a fire and enforcing local ordinances relating to fire safety.

Mäpt Mïp-æ̈dnal has a Highwayman's Guild, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Mäpt Mïp-æ̈dnal has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain Mäpt Mïp-æ̈dnal's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

Mäpt Mïp-æ̈dnal has an Arcane Academy which provides higher education in the arcane sciences.

Mäpt Mïp-æ̈dnal has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands.

Mäpt Mïp-æ̈dnal has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Cultural Notes

Mäpt Mïp-æ̈dnal's mayor's house was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is famous for its stately symmetry, classical elements, and grand appearance. Columns and pillars, such as Corinthian columns, are often seen supporting open structures or porticos. Symmetry is an important feature of this style, with each half of a building mirroring the other. Domed ceilings and windows grace these buildings, with everything placed in a mathematical arrangement.

In Mäpt Mïp-æ̈dnal yeast remains dormant.

The Shredskin near Mäpt Mïp-æ̈dnal are known to be a mutant strain of the creature.

Mäpt Mïp-æ̈dnal's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in autumn and involves square dance to channel Truename Magic energies of tier 1 via throat singing.
