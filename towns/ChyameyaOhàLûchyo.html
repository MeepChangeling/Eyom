Chyameya Ohà Lûchyo (/ˈohə/ /ˈlʊco/ [ambiguous] [security]) is a subtropical Small City located in the Glikérád Principality of the Saborri.

The name Chyameya Ohà Lûchyo is derived from the Sylvin language, as Chyameya Ohà Lûchyo was founded by Ortólore, who was culturaly Rattu.

Climate

Chyameya Ohà Lûchyo has a yearly average temperature of 4°C (39°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -17°C (2°F). Chyameya Ohà Lûchyo receives an average of 202 cm/y (79 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Chyameya Ohà Lûchyo covers an area of nearly 19 km2 (7 mi2), and an average elevation of 4156 m (13635 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Chyameya Ohà Lûchyo was founded durring the late 16th century in fall of the year 1479, by Ortólore. The establishment of Chyameya Ohà Lûchyo was somewhat plagued by a lack of willing colonists, leading to Ortólore electing to pay people to resettle in Chyameya Ohà Lûchyo.

Chyameya Ohà Lûchyo was built using the conventions of Rattu durring the late 16th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Chyameya Ohà Lûchyo is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature waddle and daub construction with good timber frames and a stone foundation protected by thatched or shingled rooves. Most buildings with second floors are built in such a way as to overhang into the streets on the upper floors for more space, as building size seems to be the primary indicator of wealth within the community. Most buildings are not decorated with any integral features, but instead use ivy, flowers, and other natural elements in planters of on trellices to breathe life into the structure they grow upon.

Chyameya Ohà Lûchyo is buildings folow an organic layout of spacious flagstone streets whihch gives the city a shape simmilar to a tree, if one views its streets from above. The city resides behind a palisade wall complete with battlments, a moat, and timber gatehouses with drawbridges. Chyameya Ohà Lûchyo's robustly designed timber walls has sufferd soem light damage, reducing its function a little in some spots, but could almsot certainly preform as expected... Though some of the worse spots could lead to the loss of defenders lives if attackers identified the weaknesses ahead of time.

A look around Chyameya Ohà Lûchyo is like a look into a broken heart. There is no planning, no organization. Everyone here clearly goes about their own thing with little thought to anyone around them who isn’t selling something they need. It’s not malicious. Heads are hung. Eyes are empty. Something truly horrible happened here once upon a time and the city never healed.

Chyameya Ohà Lûchyo has a Highwayman's Guild, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Chyameya Ohà Lûchyo has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain Chyameya Ohà Lûchyo's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

Chyameya Ohà Lûchyo possesses a Galvanic Power Grid, which brings galvanic current to most if not all buildings in town, and permits a great many consumer goods to function within the Small City. Chyameya Ohà Lûchyo's grid is powered by hydrogalvanic generators.

Chyameya Ohà Lûchyo's old civil lighting system was converted to Galvanic Lamps recently, and expanded to provide nighttime illumination to all city streets.

Chyameya Ohà Lûchyo has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands. They are not to be confused with the Office of Civil Groundskeeping as they do not hold authority over nor responsibility for Chyameya Ohà Lûchyo's natural decorations nor waterways.

Chyameya Ohà Lûchyo has a Guild of Roadworkers, who are responsible for maintaining the roadways and public paths within town. They also have the duty of enforcing all civil laws relating to the roadways.

Chyameya Ohà Lûchyo has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Chyameya Ohà Lûchyo has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

The style used which employed abundant symbolic geometry, using pure forms such as the circle and square, and plans are based on often symmetrical layouts featuring rectangular courtyards and halls. These structures were is decorated with carved stone or stucco reliefs and made use of colorful stone mosaics..

In Chyameya Ohà Lûchyo rainbows form quite often above the Small City.
