Mǟchy Hërot Med (/maːc/ /ˈhɛrot/ [exact; precise] [pig; pork]) is a subtropical Large City located in the Yaditar Earldom of the Viceroyalty of Rosid.

The name Mǟchy Hërot Med is derived from the Wood Elvish language, as Mǟchy Hërot Med was founded by Batruc Wandec, who was culturaly Wood Elven.

Climate

Mǟchy Hërot Med has a yearly average temperature of 6°C (42°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -12°C (11°F). Mǟchy Hërot Med receives an average of 190 cm/y (74 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Mǟchy Hërot Med covers an area of nearly 530 km2 (212 mi2), and an average elevation of 3664 m (12020 ft) above sea level.
