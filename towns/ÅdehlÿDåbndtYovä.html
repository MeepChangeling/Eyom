Ådehlÿ Dåbndt Yovä (/lell/ /joˈvə/ [sorry] [garbage; trash; rubbish]) is a subtropical Town located in the Zophtevub̄ Provence of the Kingdom of Hobben.

The name Ådehlÿ Dåbndt Yovä is derived from the Dwarven language, as Ådehlÿ Dåbndt Yovä was founded by Gä̌s Sé̄̌s 'Rave Harley' Jó̄̋ Ha̋chī Yó̄́̄ Zehndtubckexu, who was culturaly Dwarvern.

Most buildings are not decorated with any integral features, but instead use ivy, flowers, and other natural elements in planters of on trellices to breathe life into the structure they grow upon.
