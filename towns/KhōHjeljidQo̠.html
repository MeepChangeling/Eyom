Khō Hjeljid Qo̠ (/khoː/ /ˈhjeljid/ [canned (food)] [heat]) is a temperate Large City located in the Erelu County of the Viceroyalty of Rosid.

The name Khō Hjeljid Qo̠ is derived from the Wood Elvish language, as Khō Hjeljid Qo̠ was founded by Zêbelè Martheeh, who was culturaly Wood Elven.

Climate

Khō Hjeljid Qo̠ has a yearly average temperature of 19°C (66°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 11°C (51°F). Khō Hjeljid Qo̠ receives an average of 150 cm/y (59 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. Khō Hjeljid Qo̠ covers an area of nearly 314 km2 (125 mi2), and an average elevation of 2948 m (9671 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Khō Hjeljid Qo̠ was founded durring the late 11th century, by Zêbelè Martheeh. The establishment of the new community went well, with no major obsticles durring construction.

Khō Hjeljid Qo̠ was built using the conventions of Wood Elven durring the late 11th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Khō Hjeljid Qo̠ is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature waddle and daub construction with good timber frames and a stone foundation protected by thatched or shingled rooves. Most buildings with second floors are built in such a way as to overhang into the streets on the upper floors for more space, as building size seems to be the primary indicator of wealth within the community. Most buildings are not decorated with any integral features, but instead use ivy, flowers, and other natural elements in planters of on trellices to breathe life into the structure they grow upon.

Cultural Notes

Khō Hjeljid Qo̠ has a long tradition of martial expertise. This may be a crisply-organized history of skilled native levies, or it may be a natural belligerence in the people that leaves them familiar with bloodshed. While their neighbors and liege doubtless respect their talents, this very aptitude might make them more willing to turn to steel than prudence would advise.

Khō Hjeljid Qo̠'s mayor's house was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is best known for the combined use of arcuated and trabeated construction, employing arches and constructed with post and lintel. The arch served as the style's chief structural element, with flanking columns serving as buttresses or decorations. While the general shape of their structures could be generously described as simplistic, the use of these arches and columns created a most distinct ascetic which is easily distinguishable on sight to armatures and experts alike.

In Khō Hjeljid Qo̠ birds speak prophesy.

The Chicken near Khō Hjeljid Qo̠ are known to be a mutant strain of the creature.

Khō Hjeljid Qo̠'s citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in spring and involves reenactments to channel Charm energies of tier 1 via divine sermons.
