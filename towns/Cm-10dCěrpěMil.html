Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil (/foː/ /ˈcɛrpɛ/ [eager] [bead]) is a temperate Large City located in Ozruf Zone, Zk-465w Governorate, within the Federation of Alveria.

The name Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil is derived from the Constructi language, as Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil was founded by Scotha Fyëm, who was culturaly Constructi.

Climate

Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil has a yearly average temperature of 19°C (66°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 13°C (55°F). Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil receives an average of 255 cm/y (100 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil covers an area of nearly 280 km2 (112 mi2), and an average elevation of 2658 m (8720 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil was founded durring the early 14th century in winter of the year 1339, by Scotha Fyëm. The establishment of Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil was somewhat plagued by a lack of willing colonists, leading to Scotha Fyëm electing to pay people to resettle in Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil.

Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil was built using the conventions of Constructi durring the early 14th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature plaster covered brickwork used to form structures with an emphasis on symmetry, proportion, geometry and the regularity of parts. Orderly arrangements of columns, pilasters and lintels, as well as the use of semicircular arches, hemispherical domes, niches and aediculae can be found everywhere such that only size of building and yard can be used to measure the general prosparity of a given building's owners due to a general wealthy feeling the style gives off.

Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil is is constructed arround a semi-circular broad paverstone mainstreet, with smaller strait roads linking the circiles to eachother at varrious points. The cliffside city is the proud owner of a thick set of fortified walls fashioned from querried stone blocks. While not up to snuff for a fort or castle wall, the city's walls are naturaly much larger than those of forts or castles. Therefore, the construction such a wall is most expencive. Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil's buget focused wall would serve its community well in battle in spite of looking unimpressive compared to castles and fortresses. Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil's top tier civilian fortifications has sufferd soem light damage, reducing its function a little in some spots, but could almsot certainly preform as expected... Though some of the worse spots could lead to the loss of defenders lives if attackers identified the weaknesses ahead of time.

Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil has the unmistakable air of a city on its last legs. Everything is a bit slipshod and ramshackle. Everyone is at work, or drinking. No one has anything in their eyes other than fear and despair. Whatever industry once fueled Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil ’s existence has dried up and the city is drifting down the stream of history as it dries up. Even with that as it is, everything is just a little too worn down, a little too dirty, or much of both. The town should be gone, not dying. No one makes eye contact. Kids play quietly, but happily. Occasionally a passerby glances at you out of the corner of their eye, staring just long enough for it to be uncomfortable.

They are not to be confused with the Office of Civil Groundskeeping as they do not hold authority over nor responsibility for Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil's natural decorations nor waterways.

Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil is home to a University which provides higher education in a variety of fields, and also serves as a research institute for those same fields.

Cultural Notes

Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil's mayor's house was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is characterized by grandeur of scale, simplicity of geometric forms, dramatic use of columns, and a preference for blank walls. This design ethos extended to re-imagining earlier styles of structure to create them anew, with a similar overall look and feel to one another..

Due to the actions of local Kami, spring is recurring in Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil.

The Gravebound near Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil are known to be a mutant strain of the creature.

Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in summer and involves creating small tokens to channel Truename Magic energies of tier 1 via oath swearing.

History

A vast influx of newcomers over the last (16378 % 6)+1 years has greatly spiked Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil's population. They may have been drawn by economic opportunities, or fled some pursuing peril, or been forcibly moved there by a ruler who wanted to dilute the existing native cohesion. The natives may not have the resources or opportunities to integrate these newcomers, and it may be that the new population has no desire to stay longer than is necessary.

In time immemorial, reportedly some time during the late 2nd century, Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil was attacked by savage duergars living nearby. The details of the conflict are hazy at best due to many conflicting accounts. What is known is Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil lost 198 people, 233 livestock, and 62 buildings. The conflict ended after roughly 158, when members of Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil's militia enacted an operation to deliver supplies to a specific prison. The operation was complicated by the officer in charge of Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil's forces was incompetent, and issue many foolish and-or unprofessional orders. The conflict ended with the defense of the prison against a siege, which ended in victory for Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil's forces. The war is remembered in legend by Cm-10d Cěrpě Mil's bards, historians, and legend keepers.
