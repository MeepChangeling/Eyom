Räz äldaph Cäntwe (/əlˈdaf/ /cənˈtwe/ [dangerous] [crust; bark (of tree)]) is a subtropical Small City located in Khrutprukranasapa Hold, Lujiasu Kingdom, within the Undermountain.

The name Räz äldaph Cäntwe is derived from the Sylvin language, as Räz äldaph Cäntwe was founded by Glêl, who was culturaly Undermountain.

Räz äldaph Cäntwe has a yearly average temperature of 32°C (89°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 36°C (96°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 28°C (82°F).
