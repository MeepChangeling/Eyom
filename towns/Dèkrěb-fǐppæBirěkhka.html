Dèkrěb-fǐppæ Birěkhka (/ˈdɤkrɛb/ /ˈfɪppæ/ [bubble] [chick (girl)]) is a subtropical Town located in the Madraměvì District of the Federation of Alveria.

The name Dèkrěb-fǐppæ Birěkhka is derived from the Constructi language, as Dèkrěb-fǐppæ Birěkhka was founded by Of-82y 'Moonracer' Zlkdshèj, who was culturaly Constructi.

Dèkrěb-fǐppæ Birěkhka has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 18°C (64°F). Dèkrěb-fǐppæ Birěkhka receives an average of 265 cm/y (104 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. Dèkrěb-fǐppæ Birěkhka covers an area of nearly 4 km2 (1 mi2), and an average elevation of 1608 m (5275 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Dèkrěb-fǐppæ Birěkhka was founded durring the early 11th century, by Of-82y 'Moonracer' Zlkdshèj. The establishment of the new community went well, though many minor issues had to be solved as time went on. This was enough to delay construction and push back the formal opening ceramony, leading to some embarisment for Of-82y 'Moonracer' Zlkdshèj.

Dèkrěb-fǐppæ Birěkhka was built using the conventions of Constructi durring the early 11th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Dèkrěb-fǐppæ Birěkhka is no diffrent. The town's buildings feature waddle and daub construction with good timber frames and a stone foundation protected by thatched or shingled rooves. Most buildings with second floors are built in such a way as to overhang into the streets on the upper floors for more space, as building size seems to be the primary indicator of wealth within the community. Most buildings are not decorated with any integral features, but instead use ivy, flowers, and other natural elements in planters of on trellices to breathe life into the structure they grow upon.

Dèkrěb-fǐppæ Birěkhka is buildings are built arround a single spacious cobblestone mainstreet which forms a counterclockwise spiral to give the town a over all circular shape. The town sits comfortably behind a palisade wall complete with a timber gatehouse and battlments. Astonishigly, the timber-based walls are in pristine condishion, as if they had just been finished before you laied eyes upon them.

Dèkrěb-fǐppæ Birěkhka has the unmistakable air of a town on its last legs. Everything is a bit slipshod and ramshackle. Everyone is at work, or drinking. No one has anything in their eyes other than fear and despair. Whatever industry once fueled Dèkrěb-fǐppæ Birěkhka ’s existence has dried up and the town is drifting down the stream of history as it dries up. The locals seem to have responded to their slow downfall by recreating Dèkrěb-fǐppæ Birěkhka as one of the strictest places imaginable. Everyone’s actions are clearly directed by laws they keep in heart and mind at all times. Orderly byond order is a phrase which the town brings to mind.

Cultural Notes

Dèkrěb-fǐppæ Birěkhka's mayor's house was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is characterized by order, symmetry, formal design, grandiosity, and elaborate ornamentation. Architectural characteristics include balustrades, balconies, columns, cornices, pilasters, and triangular pediments. Stone exteriors are massive and grandiose in their symmetry; interiors are typically polished and lavishly decorated with sculptures, swags, medallions, flowers, and shields. Interiors will often have a grand stairway and opulent ballroom..

The Atomie near Dèkrěb-fǐppæ Birěkhka are known to be almost tame, such that they can be put to domestic use.

Dèkrěb-fǐppæ Birěkhka's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in autumn and involves destroying a prepared ritual vessel to channel Enchantment energies of tier 2 via mimery.
