Ponegehiz̄i (/ˈpone/ /geˈhiz̚i/ [ginger] [internal]) is a temperate Town located in the expanse.

The name Ponegehiz̄i is derived from the Sylvin language, as Ponegehiz̄i was founded by Shêlè grw Hunefer hu Hor hrd Snaaib hrd Pothinus, who was culturaly Ancient.

Climate

Ponegehiz̄i has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 32°C (89°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 17°C (62°F). Ponegehiz̄i receives an average of 255 cm/y (100 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. Ponegehiz̄i covers an area of nearly 4 km2 (1 mi2), and an average elevation of 246 m (807 ft) above sea level.

Ponegehiz̄i is remarkably well-fortified for a site of its size and role. Tall, stout walls, strong points inside the community, concentric defenses, a strategic terrain location, or a large body of standing troops might be present. Some threat is thought to exist that makes maintaining this fortification worthwhile, though it may come at a dear cost to the locals. The community’s suzerain may be uncomfortable with these defenses, as they could just as easily be used to defy the ruler.

Due to a magical anomaly, Ponegehiz̄i is directly accessible from a nearby river, despite the lack of a physical connection between the town's pond and the river.

History

In time immemorial, reportedly some time during the early 2nd century Ponegehiz̄i was struck by a devistating earthquake. The quake brought ruin to Ponegehiz̄i, which lost 211 people, 332 livestock, and 47 buildings in the earthquake. The day of the quake is remembered by many as Terror's Day.
