Cultural Notes

Life is hard in Nenesu Nasuranasahejia. Its people are impoverished compared to their peers elsewhere. Something is making the locals stay, however, whether fear of the alternative, hope for a better future, or a stubborn attachment to their ancestral lands. Whatver the reason, living in this harsh area for generations has made the people of Nenesu Nasuranasahejia a notably resourceful and hearty people.

Nenesu Nasuranasahejia's bank was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is admittedly strange and non-linear style rooted in defiance of symmetrical shapes. It championed the creation of buildings with a unique visual appearance. the structural norms of classic buildings and deforms or moves away from elementary architectural principles. Its buildings appear out-of-the-ordinary, draw the eye in immediately and sometimes create a feeling of strangeness.
