Odíhg Rùvíhnùsij̼íhg (Rùvíh /nʊˈsij̼ɪ̞g/ [prisoner] [liar]) is a subtropical Town located in the Bombuqíh Region of the Tetbur Commune.

The name Odíhg Rùvíhnùsij̼íhg is derived from the Tauric language, as Odíhg Rùvíhnùsij̼íhg was founded by Demodea Belíh, who was culturaly Tauric.

Climate

Odíhg Rùvíhnùsij̼íhg has a yearly average temperature of 26°C (78°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 23°C (73°F). Odíhg Rùvíhnùsij̼íhg receives an average of 279 cm/y (109 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. Odíhg Rùvíhnùsij̼íhg covers an area of nearly 6 km2 (2 mi2), and an average elevation of 4344 m (14251 ft) above sea level.

Civic Infrastructure

Odíhg Rùvíhnùsij̼íhg has an Office of Civil Groundskeeping, which works to enforce local ordinances relating to the construction and upkeep up of all plant life, water features, and other natural decorations within Odíhg Rùvíhnùsij̼íhg. They are also responsible for the maintenance of these features. Notably, the OCG is not responsible for Odíhg Rùvíhnùsij̼íhg's parks.

Odíhg Rùvíhnùsij̼íhg has an Office of Civil Vicary, which is responsible for providing a livelyhood for all officialy recognised religious figures within Odíhg Rùvíhnùsij̼íhg.

Odíhg Rùvíhnùsij̼íhg has a Gravedigger's Guild, which is responsible for collecting the dead and laying them to rest according to all applicable laws and religious customs.

Odíhg Rùvíhnùsij̼íhg has a Highwayman's Guild, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Odíhg Rùvíhnùsij̼íhg has a Hall of Slayers, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Odíhg Rùvíhnùsij̼íhg has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain Odíhg Rùvíhnùsij̼íhg's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

Odíhg Rùvíhnùsij̼íhg possesses a Galvanic Power Grid, which brings galvanic current to most if not all buildings in town, and permits a great many consumer goods to function within the Town. Odíhg Rùvíhnùsij̼íhg's grid is powered by a boiler and turbine based power plant.

Odíhg Rùvíhnùsij̼íhg possesses an older civil lighting system consisting of street lamps. In spite of the Galvanic Grid, these lights continue to use their old fule sources to provide nighttime illumination to all city streets.

Odíhg Rùvíhnùsij̼íhg has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands. They are not to be confused with the Office of Civil Groundskeeping as they do not hold authority over nor responsibility for Odíhg Rùvíhnùsij̼íhg's natural decorations nor waterways.

Odíhg Rùvíhnùsij̼íhg has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Odíhg Rùvíhnùsij̼íhg has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Odíhg Rùvíhnùsij̼íhg is home to a University which provides higher education in a variety of fields, and also serves as a research institute for those same fields.

Cultural Notes

Odíhg Rùvíhnùsij̼íhg's garrison was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is known for its buildings being predominantly formed through the combination of basic geometric shapes. However, it shown in the detailing given to the structures such as its characteristic tall columns, intricate detail, symmetry, harmony, and balance in their designs to an astonishing degree of precision. Decorative elements for the buildings tended to be built into the structure itself, making great use of fluting, frescoes, inlays, and embossing.

Due to the actions of local Kami, spring is short in Odíhg Rùvíhnùsij̼íhg.

The Urdefhan near Odíhg Rùvíhnùsij̼íhg are known to be more aggressive than normal.

Odíhg Rùvíhnùsij̼íhg's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in winter and involves square dance to channel Illusion energies of tier 1 via oratory performances.
