Wæm Bp-92w Uq (/ˈmɪtʤɛ/ /uq/ [exact; precise] [coat; jacket; layer; cloak]) is a subtropical Town located in Ěkhgělmǐrìl Zone, Pinizeva District, within the Federation of Alveria.

The name Wæm Bp-92w Uq is derived from the Constructi language, as Wæm Bp-92w Uq was founded by Zlelê Treg̈me Garëm, who was culturaly Constructi.

Climate

Wæm Bp-92w Uq has a yearly average temperature of 29°C (84°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 33°C (91°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 26°C (78°F). Wæm Bp-92w Uq receives an average of 164 cm/y (64 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. Wæm Bp-92w Uq covers an area of nearly 5 km2 (2 mi2), and an average elevation of 3838 m (12591 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Wæm Bp-92w Uq was founded durring the early 15th century in fall of the year 1412, by Zlelê Treg̈me Garëm. The establishment of Wæm Bp-92w Uq suffered from many setbacks, delays, and obsticles, most notably a group of Wæm Bp-92w Uq which required millitary assistance exterminate before the community could finish being built.

Wæm Bp-92w Uq was built using the conventions of Constructi durring the early 15th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Wæm Bp-92w Uq is no diffrent. The town's buildings feature waddle and daub construction with good timber frames and a stone foundation protected by thatched or shingled rooves. Most buildings with second floors are built in such a way as to overhang into the streets on the upper floors for more space, as building size seems to be the primary indicator of wealth within the community. Most buildings are not decorated with any integral features, but instead use ivy, flowers, and other natural elements in planters of on trellices to breathe life into the structure they grow upon.

Wæm Bp-92w Uq is buildings are grouped arround an odd layout of narrow packed earth streets, which seems to be based on an overlapping squair patern such that there are small squares at the cornor of every bigger square. Sometimes buildings exist in the smaller squaires, other times they are open spaces, or occupied by temporary structures. The town emploies a series of defencive earthworks, spikes, and fences to provide some protection against wild beasts and smaller groups of intelegent foes. Wæm Bp-92w Uq's minimaly adiquite has sufferd soem light damage, reducing its function a little in some spots, but could almsot certainly preform as expected... Though some of the worse spots could lead to the loss of defenders lives if attackers identified the weaknesses ahead of time.

A look around Wæm Bp-92w Uq gives you an uneasy feeling. Everything is just a little too worn down, a little too dirty, or both. No one makes eye contact. Kids play quietly, but happily. Occasionally a passerby glances at you out of the corner of their eye, staring jsut long enough for it to be uncomfortable. People seem to be allowed to do as they please with little harmoney to anything. It feels less like a town, and more like a spot people just happened to place their homes.

Civic Infrastructure

Wæm Bp-92w Uq has an Office of Civil Groundskeeping, which works to enforce local ordinances relating to the construction and upkeep up of all plant life, water features, and other natural decorations within Wæm Bp-92w Uq. They are also responsible for the maintenance of these features. Notably, the OCG is not responsible for Wæm Bp-92w Uq's parks.

Wæm Bp-92w Uq has an Office of Civil Vicary, which is responsible for providing a livelyhood for all officialy recognised religious figures within Wæm Bp-92w Uq.

Wæm Bp-92w Uq has a Gravedigger's Guild, which is responsible for collecting the dead and laying them to rest according to all applicable laws and religious customs.

Wæm Bp-92w Uq has a Highwayman's Guild, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Wæm Bp-92w Uq has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain Wæm Bp-92w Uq's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

Wæm Bp-92w Uq possesses a Galvanic Power Grid, which brings galvanic current to most if not all buildings in town, and permits a great many consumer goods to function within the Town. Wæm Bp-92w Uq's grid is powered by a direct leyline tap.

Wæm Bp-92w Uq has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands. They are not to be confused with the Office of Civil Groundskeeping as they do not hold authority over nor responsibility for Wæm Bp-92w Uq's natural decorations nor waterways.

Wæm Bp-92w Uq has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Wæm Bp-92w Uq has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Cultural Notes

Wæm Bp-92w Uq's bank was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is characterized by grandeur of scale, simplicity of geometric forms, dramatic use of columns, and a preference for blank walls. This design ethos extended to re-imagining earlier styles of structure to create them anew, with a similar overall look and feel to one another..

In Wæm Bp-92w Uq there are no smells.

The Ovinnik near Wæm Bp-92w Uq are known to be a mutant strain of the creature.

Wæm Bp-92w Uq's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in autumn and involves line dance to channel Elven High Magic energies of tier 3 via oath swearing.
