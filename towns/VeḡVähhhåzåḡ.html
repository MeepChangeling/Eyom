Veḡ Vähhh åzåḡ (/mer/ /ɑːˈzɑːgg/ [accurate; valid] [division]) is a subtropical Large City located in Räydnäzeh Hold, Baharb Province, within the United Kingdom of Undermountain.

The name Veḡ Vähhh åzåḡ is derived from the Dwarven language, as Veḡ Vähhh åzåḡ was founded by Chodred Eghehf, who was culturaly Dwarvern.

Climate

Veḡ Vähhh åzåḡ has a yearly average temperature of 15°C (59°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 6°C (42°F). Veḡ Vähhh åzåḡ receives an average of 220 cm/y (86 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Veḡ Vähhh åzåḡ covers an area of nearly 269 km2 (107 mi2), and an average elevation of 7388 m (-18710 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Veḡ Vähhh åzåḡ was founded durring the early 12th century in spring of the year 1121, by Chodred Eghehf. The establishment of Veḡ Vähhh åzåḡ suffered from several major issues, resulting in the need to develop many solutions to basic problems. Problems such as a lack of fresh water, logistical support, poor quality tools, and the odd monster or two. Howeaver, these were overcome in time.

Veḡ Vähhh åzåḡ was built using the conventions of Dwarvern durring the early 12th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Veḡ Vähhh åzåḡ is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature waddle and daub construction with good timber frames and a stone foundation protected by thatched or shingled rooves. Most buildings with second floors are built in such a way as to overhang into the streets on the upper floors for more space, as building size seems to be the primary indicator of wealth within the community. Most buildings are not decorated with any integral features, but instead use ivy, flowers, and other natural elements in planters of on trellices to breathe life into the structure they grow upon.

Veḡ Vähhh åzåḡ is buildings are arranged arrounded a highly ordered system of broad flagstone streets which form triangular paterns, allowing the incides of the octagons to be common grounds for the buildings on the edges, be it for parkland, yardspace, plazas, or markets. The city has a set of well fortified walls, with gatehouses, watch towers, battlments, and even a moat, which are fashioned from stone and timber. Veḡ Vähhh åzåḡ's walls are, howeaver, fashioned from stone and timber. While unorthadox, the design looks to be functional to a reasonable degree. With luck, the untested design will remain untested for years to come. The unusual though effective defenses have not been wellmaintained over the years, and while functional are in dire need of some loving care and perhapse light renovation.

Before you’ve even set foot into the heart of Veḡ Vähhh åzåḡ, you can smell it. The incense. It hangs about the town like a cloud. Monks, priests, and clerics are everywhere, all dedicated to the same god, all performing the same rituals to bless and anoint buildings, streets, people, animals, you name it they are or have blessed it. The same holy symbol is everywhere too. Its on buildings, on people, and even branded into livestock. This certainly loves its god. More than it loves wealth. The city is very clearly poor. Buildings are run down in ways that are not imeadiatly obvious. The people are a bit too thin. The market is very eager to sell to newcomers, but not so eager to buy from them. There’s also a general lack of the hum and buzz of healthy industry in Veḡ Vähhh åzåḡ.

Cultural Notes

Veḡ Vähhh åzåḡ's garrison was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is known for its fluid and florid elaborate style, comprising ornate, asymmetric designs and pastel shades. It is often considered to be a playful, light style, which made exuberant use of curves and emphasized subtle asymmetry in the general shape of its structures. Walls, ceilings and moldings are decorated with numerous interlacing of curves and counter-curves based on the shapes of ‘C’ and ‘S’, along with shell forms and other naturalistic shapes.

Due to the actions of local Kami, spring is recurring in Veḡ Vähhh åzåḡ.

The Copsewight near Veḡ Vähhh åzåḡ are known to be more aggressive than normal.

Veḡ Vähhh åzåḡ's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in autumn and involves embarking on a group pilgrimage to channel Illusion energies of tier 1 via moments of science.

Points of Interest

Veḡ Vähhh åzåḡ is uncommonly rich, not only for the gentry but for the common citizens as well. They may produce a valuable good, oversee precious resource extraction, have special economic favors from the ruler, or simply have inherited a vast body of infrastructure. Their neighbors likely view them with envy, and outside raiders and exploiters find them an ideal target.

Veḡ Vähhh åzåḡ is known for its odd use of round-a-bouts, small ring roads used in place of intersections.
