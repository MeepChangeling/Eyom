Vewb Gäl Cewkez (/vuːb/ /gəl/ [wedge] [web]) is a temperate Large City located in Luzfåbn Hold, Nar Province, within the United Kingdom of Undermountain.

The name Vewb Gäl Cewkez is derived from the Dwarven language, as Vewb Gäl Cewkez was founded by Greli Yadequg, who was culturaly Dwarvern.

Climate

Vewb Gäl Cewkez has a yearly average temperature of 17°C (62°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 22°C (71°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 12°C (53°F). Vewb Gäl Cewkez receives an average of 258 cm/y (101 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. Vewb Gäl Cewkez covers an area of nearly 531 km2 (212 mi2), and an average elevation of 956 m (3136 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Vewb Gäl Cewkez was founded durring the early 11th century, by Greli Yadequg. The establishment of the new community went well, though many minor issues had to be solved as time went on. This was enough to delay construction and push back the formal opening ceramony, leading to some embarisment for Greli Yadequg.

Vewb Gäl Cewkez was built using the conventions of Dwarvern durring the early 11th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Vewb Gäl Cewkez is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature masoned stone construction which prominantly features pointed arches, pointed ribbed vault cielings, flying buttress', and window tracery all of which share a simmilar gemoetetic patern halfway between organic and inorganic in design formaing a very distinct aesthetically integrated style. BUildings tend to reach for the havens, and more expencive homes are easily identified by their floor count as well as the addition of decorative features intigrated into the building's design such as statues, gargoyals, and embelished joinery.

Vewb Gäl Cewkez is buildings are arranged arround a network of restrictive cobblestone streets which form a diamond shaped grid, where each diamond verries in size given the proximity of the paralell streets forming each section. The ocasional smaller diamond has been used to construct a park, plaza, and other communal structures. The riverside city has a defencive wall made from large clay bricks. The wall is constructed to the exact specifications of millitary fortifications, but the nature of its clay brick construction leaves it vulnerable to even outdated siege equipment. That said, the city is well defended against anything short of an army. The brittle defences has suffered a visible ammount of structural damage, leaving them effectivly useless. One can't help but wonder why the has not yet effected repairs.

A look around Vewb Gäl Cewkez gives the unmistakable impression the town is one of the strictest places imaginable. Everyone’s actions are clearly directed by laws they keep in heart and mind at all times. Orderly byond order is a phrase which Vewb Gäl Cewkez brings to mind.

Vewb Gäl Cewkez is home to a University which provides higher education in a variety of fields, and also serves as a research institute for those same fields.

Cultural Notes

Vewb Gäl Cewkez's town hall was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is known for its fluid and florid elaborate style, comprising ornate, asymmetric designs and pastel shades. It is often considered to be a playful, light style, which made exuberant use of curves and emphasized subtle asymmetry in the general shape of its structures. Walls, ceilings and moldings are decorated with numerous interlacing of curves and counter-curves based on the shapes of ‘C’ and ‘S’, along with shell forms and other naturalistic shapes.

In Vewb Gäl Cewkez rainbows form quite often above the Large City.

The Snake, Venomous near Vewb Gäl Cewkez are known to be quite timid.

Vewb Gäl Cewkez's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in autumn and involves consuming a local toxin to channel Chronomancy energies of tier 2 via divine sermons.

History

Vewb Gäl Cewkez used to be more prosperous, but something happened relatively long ago that left it a shrunken shadow of its former self. If the settlement is prosperous, the locals often lament how much more they could have had. If the settlement is not prosperous, the locals blame their ill fortunes on that event. Reminders of this better time can be found in many places within Vewb Gäl Cewkez.
