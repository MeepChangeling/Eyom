Àsu̹ Rage Gûchya (/ˈəsu̹/ /ˈrage/ [fair; just; moral] [scarf]) is a temperate Large City located in Hûche Ûkîma Kingdom, Rûlichyû Îyà Empire, within the Warren.

The name Àsu̹ Rage Gûchya is derived from the Sylvin language, as Àsu̹ Rage Gûchya was founded by Gnanvaihn, who was culturaly Rattu.
