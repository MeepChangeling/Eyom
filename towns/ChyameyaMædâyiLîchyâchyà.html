Chyameya Mædâyi Lîchyâchyà (/ke/ /lɪːˈcɑcə/ [peach] [peasant]) is a subtropical Large City located in Gliktzipu Kingdom, Kahotivupunabu Empire, within the Warren.

The name Chyameya Mædâyi Lîchyâchyà is derived from the Sylvin language, as Chyameya Mædâyi Lîchyâchyà was founded by Khèbê, who was culturaly Rattu.

Climate

Chyameya Mædâyi Lîchyâchyà has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 9°C (48°F). Chyameya Mædâyi Lîchyâchyà receives an average of 84 cm/y (33 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. Chyameya Mædâyi Lîchyâchyà covers an area of nearly 83 km2 (33 mi2), and an average elevation of 7704 m (-17674 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Chyameya Mædâyi Lîchyâchyà was founded durring the early 10th century, by Khèbê. The establishment of Chyameya Mædâyi Lîchyâchyà was somewhat plagued by a lack of willing colonists, leading to Khèbê electing to pay people to resettle in Chyameya Mædâyi Lîchyâchyà.

Chyameya Mædâyi Lîchyâchyà was built using the conventions of Rattu durring the early 10th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Chyameya Mædâyi Lîchyâchyà is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature masoned stone construction which prominantly features pointed arches, pointed ribbed vault cielings, flying buttress', and window tracery all of which share a simmilar gemoetetic patern halfway between organic and inorganic in design formaing a very distinct aesthetically integrated style. BUildings tend to reach for the havens, and more expencive homes are easily identified by their floor count as well as the addition of decorative features intigrated into the building's design such as statues, gargoyals, and embelished joinery.

Chyameya Mædâyi Lîchyâchyà is buildings are arranged arrounded highly ordered system of spacious packed earth streets which form octogonal paterns, allowing the incides of the octagons to be common grounds for the buildings on the edges, be it for parkland, yardspace, plazas, or markets. The city rests behind the absurdity that is a thick, timber braced, wall made of clay bricks. While visualy impressive and certainly an astetic, Chyameya Mædâyi Lîchyâchyà's wall provides no actual defence against siege equipment due to the choice of its cosntruction materials. Even nonexperts can tell the town is trying to impress rather than defend with its walls, towers, and gatehouses. Though admittedly, they do look nice... To primitive tribals who have never seen fortifications before. The political statment focused walls have not been wellmaintained over the years, and while functional are in dire need of some loving care and perhapse light renovation.

Chyameya Mædâyi Lîchyâchyà has the unmistakable air of a city on its last legs. Everything is a bit slipshod and ramshackle. Everyone is at work, or drinking. No one has anything in their eyes other than fear and despair. Whatever industry once fueled Chyameya Mædâyi Lîchyâchyà ’s existence has dried up and the city is drifting down the stream of history as it dries up. Likely due to this, Chyameya Mædâyi Lîchyâchyà is, in a word, disorder. People seem to be allowed to do as they please with little harmoney to anything. It feels less like a city, and more like a spot people just happened to have homes. One cannot help but wonder what Chyameya Mædâyi Lîchyâchyà was like during the glorydays.

Cultural Notes

One or more crime bosses have a powerful influence within Chyameya Mædâyi Lîchyâchyà. They may control crime within the community itself, or they may use it simply as a safe haven from which to direct their minions elsewhere. Local law enforcement may know all about them, but lack the strength to confront them and their paid or intimidated henchmen.

Chyameya Mædâyi Lîchyâchyà's bank was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is which made use of the classical orders and mathematically precise ratios of height and width combined with a desire for symmetry, proportion, and harmony. It used columns, pediments, arches and domes are imaginatively in buildings of all types. Decorative features were seen as largely unnecessary as the sheer beauty of the structure itself was often close to art. However, many buildings with large ceiling spaces had their ceilings decorated with elaborate paintings, simply because the large flat spaces could feel wasted.

Due to the actions of local Kami, autumn is long in Chyameya Mædâyi Lîchyâchyà.

The Amoeba, Giant near Chyameya Mædâyi Lîchyâchyà are known to be more aggressive than normal.

Chyameya Mædâyi Lîchyâchyà's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in winter and involves performance art to channel Wild Magic energies of tier 1 via throat chanting.

History

In time immemorial, reportedly some time during the late 2nd century, Chyameya Mædâyi Lîchyâchyà was attacked by members of a peasant revolt. The details of the conflict are hazy at best due to many conflicting accounts. What is known is Chyameya Mædâyi Lîchyâchyà lost 184 people, 198 livestock, and 32 buildings. The conflict ended after roughly 104, when members of Chyameya Mædâyi Lîchyâchyà's militia enacted an operation to train a group of civilians for a special clandestine operation. The operation was complicated by a natural disaster interrupted the operation, shattering unit cohesion. The conflict ended with the defense of the ruin against a siege, which ended in a stalemate for Chyameya Mædâyi Lîchyâchyà's forces. The war is remembered in legend by Chyameya Mædâyi Lîchyâchyà's bards, historians, and legend keepers.
