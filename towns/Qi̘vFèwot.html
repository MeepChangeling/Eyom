Qi̘v Fèwot (/fɛw/ /ot/ [buffalo] [poor; weak]) is a subtropical Large City located in Fyāzíhp County, Somyardia County, within the Dalandic Empire.

The name Qi̘v Fèwot is derived from the Tauric language, as Qi̘v Fèwot was founded by Trînm Leyshon, who was culturaly Tauric.

Climate

Qi̘v Fèwot has a yearly average temperature of 26°C (78°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 23°C (73°F). Qi̘v Fèwot receives an average of 189 cm/y (74 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. Qi̘v Fèwot covers an area of nearly 407 km2 (162 mi2), and an average elevation of 5306 m (17408 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Qi̘v Fèwot was founded durring the late 12th century in winter of the year 1068, by Trînm Leyshon. The establishment of the new community went well, with no major obsticles durring construction.

Qi̘v Fèwot was built using the conventions of Tauric durring the late 12th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Qi̘v Fèwot is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature masoned stone construction which prominantly features pointed arches, pointed ribbed vault cielings, flying buttress', and window tracery all of which share a simmilar gemoetetic patern halfway between organic and inorganic in design formaing a very distinct aesthetically integrated style. BUildings tend to reach for the havens, and more expencive homes are easily identified by their floor count as well as the addition of decorative features intigrated into the building's design such as statues, gargoyals, and embelished joinery.

Qi̘v Fèwot is buildings are arranged arround a network of crampt cobblestone streets which form a grid, where each square verries in size given the proximity of the paralell streets forming each section. The ocasional smaller square has been used to construct a park, plaza, and other communal structures. The hillside city sits behind a stone-renforced palisade wall, with stone gatehouses and timber drawbridges for their trench. Qi̘v Fèwot's would-be-castle fortifications are in an unremarkable state. To some, this is the ideal sate for defences to be in. In need of absoutly nothing, and ready to serve the city as needed.

Qi̘v Fèwot has a very calm atmosphere. People can be seen relaxing, scocilizing, and going about all manner of business other than the daily grind. Men, women, children, all can be seen enjoying life in a laid-back way in the many parks which line Qi̘v Fèwot’s streets. Yet, deep down, you know this atmosphere is a carefully created illusion. You can see the ocasional seam in their tapestry. You’re not sure what has them doing their best to act so carefree, but act carefree they do.

Qi̘v Fèwot is home to a University which provides higher education in a variety of fields, and also serves as a research institute for those same fields.
