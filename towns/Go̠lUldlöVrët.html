Go̠l Uldlö Vrët (/kli/ /ˈuldlo˞/ [yellow] [medal]) is a subtropical Town located in Diriz̄i Kingdom, Zōzqut Diocese, within the Covenent of Irus.

The name Go̠l Uldlö Vrët is derived from the Wood Elvish language, as Go̠l Uldlö Vrët was founded by Trgêrí Chebedric, who was culturaly Wood Elven.

Climate

Go̠l Uldlö Vrët has a yearly average temperature of 27°C (80°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 32°C (89°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 23°C (73°F). Go̠l Uldlö Vrët receives an average of 118 cm/y (46 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. Go̠l Uldlö Vrët covers an area of nearly 5 km2 (2 mi2), and an average elevation of 7022 m (-19911 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Go̠l Uldlö Vrët was founded durring the late 16th century in winter of the year 1558, by Trgêrí Chebedric. The establishment of Go̠l Uldlö Vrët was plagued by a lack of willing colonists. After attempts to pay people to resettle failed Trgêrí Chebedric struck deals with nearby nations and communities to establish Go̠l Uldlö Vrët as a prison colony.

Go̠l Uldlö Vrët was built using the conventions of Wood Elven durring the late 16th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Go̠l Uldlö Vrët is no diffrent. The town's buildings feature masoned stone construction which prominantly features pointed arches, pointed ribbed vault cielings, flying buttress', and window tracery all of which share a simmilar gemoetetic patern halfway between organic and inorganic in design formaing a very distinct aesthetically integrated style. BUildings tend to reach for the havens, and more expencive homes are easily identified by their floor count as well as the addition of decorative features intigrated into the building's design such as statues, gargoyals, and embelished joinery.

Go̠l Uldlö Vrët is buildings are speckled and packed arround broad packed earth streets with seemingly no patern to them. It appears as if the town's residents simply built streets as they pleased and squeazed buildings in wherever and howeave rpossible, creating an organic, frustrating to navigate, maze of a town. The town is protected by a series of wooden fences ringing the town's parimiter, which are likly intended to keep varrious beasts out of town rather than protect it from attack by any intelegent agents. Go̠l Uldlö Vrët's bare minimum defences are suffering from significent damage, so much so that examples can be pointed to no matter which section one might have within their line of site, and most of which render sections inoperable at present.

Go̠l Uldlö Vrët has a very calm atmosphere. People can be seen relaxing, scocilizing, and going about all manner of business other than the daily grind. Men, women, children, all can be seen enjoying life in a laid-back way in the many parks which line Go̠l Uldlö Vrët’s streets. A look around Go̠l Uldlö Vrët has something terribly wrong with it. It’s impossible to put one’s finger on, but something is horribly wrong. Maybe it’s the way fog blankets the ground, but only in the connors of places. Maybe it’s the vermin scutteling between shadows in the corner of your eyes. Perhaps it’s the overcast sky which seemed to creep out of nowhere, or the distant howling of wolves. Maybe it’s everything together. Regardless, you do not feel it would be wise to remain in Go̠l Uldlö Vrët long.

Civic Infrastructure

Go̠l Uldlö Vrët has an Office of Civil Groundskeeping, which works to enforce local ordinances relating to the construction and upkeep up of all plant life, water features, and other natural decorations within Go̠l Uldlö Vrët. They are also responsible for the maintenance of these features. Notably, the OCG is not responsible for Go̠l Uldlö Vrët's parks.

Go̠l Uldlö Vrët has an Office of Civil Vicary, which is responsible for providing a livelyhood for all officialy recognised religious figures within Go̠l Uldlö Vrët.

Go̠l Uldlö Vrët has a Gravedigger's Guild, which is responsible for collecting the dead and laying them to rest according to all applicable laws and religious customs.

Go̠l Uldlö Vrët has a Department of Firefighters, which is responsible for organizing fire fighting efforts during a fire and enforcing local ordinances relating to fire safety.

Go̠l Uldlö Vrët has a Highwayman's Guild, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Go̠l Uldlö Vrët has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain Go̠l Uldlö Vrët's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

Go̠l Uldlö Vrët has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands. They are not to be confused with the Office of Civil Groundskeeping as they do not hold authority over nor responsibility for Go̠l Uldlö Vrët's natural decorations nor waterways.

Go̠l Uldlö Vrët has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Cultural Notes

Go̠l Uldlö Vrët's bank was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is famous for its stately symmetry, classical elements, and grand appearance. Columns and pillars, such as Corinthian columns, are often seen supporting open structures or porticos. Symmetry is an important feature of this style, with each half of a building mirroring the other. Domed ceilings and windows grace these buildings, with everything placed in a mathematical arrangement.

In Go̠l Uldlö Vrët sometimes, near one of the seasonal solstices, the sun appears to split into three and it rains from a clear sky for several hours.

The Human Juju Zombie near Go̠l Uldlö Vrët are known to be almost tame, such that they can be put to domestic use.

Go̠l Uldlö Vrët's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in summer and involves ritual combat to channel Mysticism energies of tier 1 via throat singing.
