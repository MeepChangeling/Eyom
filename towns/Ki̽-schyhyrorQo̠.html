Ki̽-schyhyror Qo̠ (/ki̽/ /scror/ [effect] [wound]) is a temperate Large City located in the Chagheindyl Earldom of the Viceroyalty of Rosid.

The name Ki̽-schyhyror Qo̠ is derived from the Wood Elvish language, as Ki̽-schyhyror Qo̠ was founded by Vicky Khorva, who was culturaly Wood Elven.

Climate

Ki̽-schyhyror Qo̠ has a yearly average temperature of 14°C (57°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 2°C (35°F). Ki̽-schyhyror Qo̠ receives an average of 152 cm/y (59 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Ki̽-schyhyror Qo̠ covers an area of nearly 278 km2 (111 mi2), and an average elevation of 4312 m (14146 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Ki̽-schyhyror Qo̠ was founded durring the late 15th century in fall of the year 1387, by Vicky Khorva. The establishment of Ki̽-schyhyror Qo̠ was only bairly constructed. The sheer number of problems with its founding were enough to make several of the backers funding Ki̽-schyhyror Qo̠'s construction back out of the project. Vicky Khorva pushed on reguardles, and Ki̽-schyhyror Qo̠ was finished, but starts off as a terible place to live.

Ki̽-schyhyror Qo̠ was built using the conventions of Wood Elven durring the late 15th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Ki̽-schyhyror Qo̠ is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature timber framed wooden shiethed or brick construction, which gives form to a very formalized, rational, expence effishent arcatectural style based on strictly symmetrical designs which universaly feature pitched roofs, shutters, and the occasional column or pilaster for a decorative touch.

Ki̽-schyhyror Qo̠ is buildings are built arround a single crampt packed earth mainstreet which forms a counterclockwise spiral to give the city a over all circular shape. The hillside city rests behind the absurdity that is a thick, timber braced, wall made of clay bricks. While visualy impressive and certainly an astetic, Ki̽-schyhyror Qo̠'s wall provides no actual defence against siege equipment due to the choice of its cosntruction materials. Even nonexperts can tell the town is trying to impress rather than defend with its walls, towers, and gatehouses. Though admittedly, they do look nice... To primitive tribals who have never seen fortifications before. Ki̽-schyhyror Qo̠'s political statment focused walls has sufferd soem light damage, reducing its function a little in some spots, but could almsot certainly preform as expected... Though some of the worse spots could lead to the loss of defenders lives if attackers identified the weaknesses ahead of time.

The first thing you notice about Ki̽-schyhyror Qo̠ is the large sign at the entrance welcoming you to their humble city. Children come up to you and say hi, some asking if you’ve fought any big monsters, and others just zipping away after their hello. The adults are much the same. By the time you’ve made it a significant way into the town you’ve been invited to a tavern for a pint to drink in exchange for news of the world. You’ve also had no less than three older women give you a sweetroll and direct your attention to their still unmarried children. It would be creepy if it wasn't so sincere.

Cultural Notes

Two or more groups of citizens within Ki̽-schyhyror Qo̠ hate each other. Their neighbors or the local law have kept things from too-overt violence, but members of the groups will constantly interfere with their rivals and cause whatever misery they can get away with. This hate may spring from recent events, or it may be an inherited spite from old wrongs.

Ki̽-schyhyror Qo̠'s town hall was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is admittedly strange and non-linear style rooted in defiance of symmetrical shapes. It championed the creation of buildings with a unique visual appearance. the structural norms of classic buildings and deforms or moves away from elementary architectural principles. By including non-linear designs processed into its buildings and favoring fragmentation, this style expressed a form of controlled chaos. Its buildings appear out-of-the-ordinary, draw the eye in immediately and sometimes create a feeling of strangeness. These distorted shapes and structure are not reserved to the building’s outer facade, they destabilize interior elements too, favoring minimalism and play on people’s perceptions by injecting a futuristic touch.

In Ki̽-schyhyror Qo̠ birds speak prophesy.

The Mole near Ki̽-schyhyror Qo̠ are known to be almost tame, such that they can be put to domestic use.

Ki̽-schyhyror Qo̠'s citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in autumn and involves ritual combat to channel Elven High Magic energies of tier 3 via proclamations.
