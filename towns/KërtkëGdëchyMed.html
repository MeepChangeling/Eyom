Kërtkë Gdëchy Med (/ˈkɛrtkɛ/ /gdɛc/ [honest; pure; authentic; moral] [address]) is a subtropical Large City located in Aōkme̜l Schyi̽ Zone, Thardor Province, within the Federation of Alveria.

The name Kërtkë Gdëchy Med is derived from the Wood Elvish language, as Kërtkë Gdëchy Med was founded by Cyih Drorsair, who was culturaly Wood Elven.

Climate

Kërtkë Gdëchy Med has a yearly average temperature of 19°C (66°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 10°C (50°F). Kërtkë Gdëchy Med receives an average of 257 cm/y (101 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. Kërtkë Gdëchy Med covers an area of nearly 282 km2 (112 mi2), and an average elevation of 2326 m (7631 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Kërtkë Gdëchy Med was founded durring the early 9th century, by Cyih Drorsair. The establishment of Kërtkë Gdëchy Med was plagued by a lack of willing colonists. After attempts to pay people to resettle failed Cyih Drorsair struck deals with nearby nations and communities to establish Kërtkë Gdëchy Med as a prison colony.

Kërtkë Gdëchy Med was built using the conventions of Wood Elven durring the early 9th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Kërtkë Gdëchy Med is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature waddle and daub construction with good timber frames and a stone foundation protected by thatched or shingled rooves. Most buildings with second floors are built in such a way as to overhang into the streets on the upper floors for more space, as building size seems to be the primary indicator of wealth within the community. Most buildings are not decorated with any integral features, but instead use ivy, flowers, and other natural elements in planters of on trellices to breathe life into the structure they grow upon.

Kërtkë Gdëchy Med is buildings are arranged arround a single restrictive baked earthen mainstreet with many smaller streets branching off of it which gives the city a over all rectangular shape, albit one warped and twisted by the nature of the curves of the main road. The hillside city is the proud owner of a thick set of fortified walls fashioned from querried stone blocks. While not up to snuff for a fort or castle wall, the city's walls are naturaly much larger than those of forts or castles. Therefore, the construction such a wall is most expencive. Kërtkë Gdëchy Med's buget focused wall would serve its community well in battle in spite of looking unimpressive compared to castles and fortresses. Kërtkë Gdëchy Med's top tier civilian fortifications are in an unremarkable state. To some, this is the ideal sate for defences to be in. In need of absoutly nothing, and ready to serve the city as needed.

A look around Kërtkë Gdëchy Med gives you an uneasy feeling. Everything is just a little too worn down, a little too dirty, or both. No one makes eye contact. Kids play quietly, but happily. Occasionally a passerby glances at you out of the corner of their eye, staring jsut long enough for it to be uncomfortable.

Civic Infrastructure

They are also responsible for the maintenance of these features. Notably, the OCG is not responsible for Kërtkë Gdëchy Med's parks.

Kërtkë Gdëchy Med has an Office of Civil Vicary, which is responsible for providing a livelyhood for all officialy recognised religious figures within Kërtkë Gdëchy Med.

Kërtkë Gdëchy Med has a Department of Firefighters, which is responsible for organizing fire fighting efforts during a fire and enforcing local ordinances relating to fire safety.

Kërtkë Gdëchy Med has a Highwayman's Guild, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Kërtkë Gdëchy Med has a Hall of Slayers, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Kërtkë Gdëchy Med has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain Kërtkë Gdëchy Med's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

Kërtkë Gdëchy Med has an Millitary Academy which trains military officers and specilists.

Kërtkë Gdëchy Med possesses a Galvanic Power Grid, which brings galvanic current to most if not all buildings in town, and permits a great many consumer goods to function within the Large City. Kërtkë Gdëchy Med's grid is powered by a boiler and turbine based power plant.

Kërtkë Gdëchy Med's old civil lighting system was converted to Galvanic Lamps recently, and expanded to provide nighttime illumination to all city streets.

Kërtkë Gdëchy Med has a first rate hospital which caters to anyone in need of long term medical care.

Kërtkë Gdëchy Med has a library, which keeps a large collection of books, scrolls, and archives all manner of physical items. The library is open to the public, including the Aether Link.

Kërtkë Gdëchy Med has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands. They are not to be confused with the Office of Civil Groundskeeping as they do not hold authority over nor responsibility for Kërtkë Gdëchy Med's natural decorations nor waterways.

Kërtkë Gdëchy Med has a Guild of Roadworkers, who are responsible for maintaining the roadways and public paths within town. They also have the duty of enforcing all civil laws relating to the roadways.

Kërtkë Gdëchy Med has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Kërtkë Gdëchy Med has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Kërtkë Gdëchy Med is home to a University which provides higher education in a variety of fields, and also serves as a research institute for those same fields.

Cultural Notes

Kërtkë Gdëchy Med's bank was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is best known for its striking sculptural forms and often dazzling ornamental detail that characterizes the buildings general shape. The radiant colors, rich patterns, and symmetrical silhouettes employed by this style were backed up by rich decorative features including gardens, courtyards, extruded arches, domes, pointed domes, vaulted ceilings, elaborate painted and inlaid designs, and decorative sculptures.

In Kërtkë Gdëchy Med birds speak prophesy.

The Snake, Venomous near Kërtkë Gdëchy Med are known to be more aggressive than normal.

Kërtkë Gdëchy Med's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in winter and involves embarking on a group pilgrimage to channel Invocation energies of tier 3 via throat singing.
