Chyichy Gok Hadfow (/cic/ /gok/ [pink] [apology]) is a subtropical Large City located in Ëfi̽nenkwë Parish, Ke̜kzi Diocese, within the Irus.

The name Chyichy Gok Hadfow is derived from the Sylvin language, as Chyichy Gok Hadfow was founded by Bresèbrê, who was culturaly Wood Elf.

Climate

Chyichy Gok Hadfow has a yearly average temperature of 18°C (64°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 9°C (48°F). Chyichy Gok Hadfow receives an average of 222 cm/y (87 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. Chyichy Gok Hadfow covers an area of nearly 113 km2 (45 mi2), and an average elevation of 4596 m (15078 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Chyichy Gok Hadfow was founded durring the early 14th century in spring of the year 1334, by Bresèbrê. The establishment of the new community went well, though many minor issues had to be solved as time went on. This was enough to delay construction and push back the formal opening ceramony, leading to some embarisment for Bresèbrê.

Chyichy Gok Hadfow was built using the conventions of Wood Elf durring the early 14th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Chyichy Gok Hadfow is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature masoned stone construction which prominantly features pointed arches, pointed ribbed vault cielings, flying buttress', and window tracery all of which share a simmilar gemoetetic patern halfway between organic and inorganic in design formaing a very distinct aesthetically integrated style. BUildings tend to reach for the havens, and more expencive homes are easily identified by their floor count as well as the addition of decorative features intigrated into the building's design such as statues, gargoyals, and embelished joinery.

Chyichy Gok Hadfow is is constructed arround a series of narrow split-log ties mainstreets which form concentric circles, with smaller strait roads linking the circiles to each other at varrious points. The city has a fortified albit thin brick wall. The wall has most of the feeatures of a typical castle wall, just on a much smaller scale and and budget. Notably brick isn't a particuarly soild choice for resisting siege weapons. Chyichy Gok Hadfow's wall wouldn't hinder a proper army, but it is more than sufishent for bandits and other small marauding groups. The city's monster and outlaw focused fortifications are visibly old, but also obviously maintained semi-regularly. Its likly the local malishia or garrison are tasked with ocasional mantance of the citys defences.

Chyichy Gok Hadfow is not quite well. Something happened here, perhapse recently, perhapse long ago. Whatever it was, it settled into the very soul of the city like a festering wound. The people go about their day well enough, but there’s a tention in the air you can cut with a knife.

Civic Infrastructure

Chyichy Gok Hadfow possesses a Aethary Link for its accademic, government, and financial institutions. Public Aethary access is available through one or more of these intitutions.

Chyichy Gok Hadfow has an animal control department which works to enforce local ordinances relating to the control, impoundment, and disposition of animals.

Chyichy Gok Hadfow has an Office of Civil Groundskeeping, which works to enforce local ordinances relating to the construction and upkeep up of all plant life, water features, and other natural decorations within Chyichy Gok Hadfow. They are also responsible for the maintenance of these features. Notably, the OCG is not responsible for Chyichy Gok Hadfow's parks.

Chyichy Gok Hadfow has an Office of Civil Vicary, which is responsible for providing a livelyhood for all officialy recognised religious figures within Chyichy Gok Hadfow.

Chyichy Gok Hadfow has a government-funded child care program, overseen by the local Department of Nursemaids, which is responsible for providing childcare to working-class citizens according to local ordinances.

Chyichy Gok Hadfow has a Gravedigger's Guild, which is responsible for collecting the dead and laying them to rest according to all applicable laws and religious customs.

Chyichy Gok Hadfow has a Guild of Nurses, which is tasked with caring for the elderly and infirm in accordance with local ordinances, religious values, and customs.

Chyichy Gok Hadfow has a Department of Firefighters, which is responsible for organizing fire fighting efforts during a fire and enforcing local ordinances relating to fire safety.

Chyichy Gok Hadfow has a Highwayman's Guild, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Chyichy Gok Hadfow has a Hall of Slayers, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Chyichy Gok Hadfow has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain Chyichy Gok Hadfow's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

Chyichy Gok Hadfow has an Administrative Academy which trains individuals in the administrative arts.

Chyichy Gok Hadfow possesses a Galvanic Power Grid, which brings galvanic current to most if not all buildings in town, and permits a great many consumer goods to function within the Large City. Chyichy Gok Hadfow's grid is powered by a direct leyline tap.

Chyichy Gok Hadfow's old civil lighting system was converted to Galvanic Lamps recently, and expanded to provide nighttime illumination to all city streets.

Chyichy Gok Hadfow has a first rate hospital which caters to anyone in need of long term medical care.

Chyichy Gok Hadfow has a library, which keeps a large collection of books, scrolls, and archives all manner of physical items. The library is open to the public, including the Aether Link.

Chyichy Gok Hadfow has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands. They are not to be confused with the Office of Civil Groundskeeping as they do not hold authority over nor responsibility for Chyichy Gok Hadfow's natural decorations nor waterways.

Chyichy Gok Hadfow has a Guild of Roadworkers, who are responsible for maintaining the roadways and public paths within town. They also have the duty of enforcing all civil laws relating to the roadways.

Chyichy Gok Hadfow has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Chyichy Gok Hadfow has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Cultural Notes

Chyichy Gok Hadfow's mayor's house was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is characterized by grandeur of scale, simplicity of geometric forms, dramatic use of columns, and a preference for blank walls. This design ethos extended to re-imagining earlier styles of structure to create them anew, with a similar overall look and feel to one another..

In Chyichy Gok Hadfow rain occurs in reverse with water pooling up from the earth then falling into the skies to from clouds, this unsettles every visitor that sees it.

The Psyche Serpent near Chyichy Gok Hadfow are known to be almost tame, such that they can be put to domestic use.

Chyichy Gok Hadfow's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in spring and involves performance art to channel Augury energies of tier 2 via speaking in tongues.
