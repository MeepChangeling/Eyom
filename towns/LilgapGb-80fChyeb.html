Lilgap Gb-80f Chyeb (/ˈvɨpɤb/ /ceb/ [triple] [semen]) is a subtropical Large City located in Zb-64ysibæ Zone, Pz-339jnup District, within the Alveria.

The name Lilgap Gb-80f Chyeb is derived from the Goblin language, as Lilgap Gb-80f Chyeb was founded by Mp̪vó̄nḱ Mp̪f̄shv 'Roxy Aplenty' Mēkīb Sé̄s Cawmb, who was culturaly KLEO.

Climate

Lilgap Gb-80f Chyeb has a yearly average temperature of 26°C (78°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 30°C (86°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 23°C (73°F). Lilgap Gb-80f Chyeb receives an average of 211 cm/y (83 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. Lilgap Gb-80f Chyeb covers an area of nearly 199 km2 (79 mi2), and an average elevation of 5622 m (18444 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Lilgap Gb-80f Chyeb was founded durring the early 15th century in summer of the year 1444, by Mp̪vó̄nḱ Mp̪f̄shv 'Roxy Aplenty' Mēkīb Sé̄s Cawmb. The establishment of Lilgap Gb-80f Chyeb was plagued by a lack of willing colonists. After attempts to pay people to resettle failed Mp̪vó̄nḱ Mp̪f̄shv 'Roxy Aplenty' Mēkīb Sé̄s Cawmb struck deals with nearby nations and communities to establish Lilgap Gb-80f Chyeb as a prison colony.

Lilgap Gb-80f Chyeb was built using the conventions of KLEO durring the early 15th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Lilgap Gb-80f Chyeb is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature timber and earth construction, with most buildigns first floors resembling mounds of earth, with subsequent floors appearing as elaborate log cabins, with each building forming a tiered pyramid of sorts fromed from the stack of rectangular, peek roofed cabins. Most wooden bracing, support, and trim is carved with decorative knotwork, and the larger structures even feature painted trim which emphasises the knotwork.

Lilgap Gb-80f Chyeb is buildings are arranged arrounded highly ordered system of crampt packed earth streets which form octogonal paterns, allowing the incides of the octagons to be common grounds for the buildings on the edges, be it for parkland, yardspace, plazas, or markets. The city has a defencive wall made from querried stone. The wall is equipped with a full set of battlments but the nature of its construction methodology leaves it somewhat vulnerable to siege equipment. That said, the city is well defended against anything short of an army. Lilgap Gb-80f Chyeb's cost-cutting-focused defences are suffering from significent damage, so much so that examples can be pointed to no matter which section one might have within their line of site, and most of which render sections inoperable at present.

A look around Lilgap Gb-80f Chyeb is like a look into a broken heart which long ago fell to corruption. There is no planning, no organization. Everyone here clearly goes about their own thing with little thought to anyone around them who isn’t selling something they need… unless of course, they would be an easy target for some robbery. The city has truly fallen to evil.

Lilgap Gb-80f Chyeb has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Lilgap Gb-80f Chyeb is home to a University which provides higher education in a variety of fields, and also serves as a research institute for those same fields.

Cultural Notes

Lilgap Gb-80f Chyeb's town hall was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is inspired by the natural world, characterized by sinuous, sculptural, organic shapes, arches, curving lines, and sensual ornamentation. Common motifs included stylized versions of leaves, flowers, vines, insects, animals, and other natural elements. Decorative elements found on the inside and outside of buildings include intricate mosaic work, curved windows, and decorative trim work. .

In Lilgap Gb-80f Chyeb snow is warm to the touch and does not melt within city limits. Also it only happens during summer.

The Begedhi, Parasite near Lilgap Gb-80f Chyeb are known to be almost tame, such that they can be put to domestic use.

Lilgap Gb-80f Chyeb's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in autumn and involves long periods of drunkenness to channel Illusion energies of tier 3 via oath swearing.
