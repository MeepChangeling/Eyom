Qi̘v Íhmíhzíhdà (Íhmíhz /ˈɪ̞də/ [sap] [alcohol]) is a temperate Large City located in Krfievāzè County, Botbury Shire, within the Dalandic Empire.

The name Qi̘v Íhmíhzíhdà is derived from the Tauric language, as Qi̘v Íhmíhzíhdà was founded by Micropera Guynney Owens, who was culturaly Tauric.

Climate

Qi̘v Íhmíhzíhdà has a yearly average temperature of 29°C (84°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 33°C (91°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 26°C (78°F). Qi̘v Íhmíhzíhdà receives an average of 140 cm/y (55 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. Qi̘v Íhmíhzíhdà covers an area of nearly 339 km2 (135 mi2), and an average elevation of 3512 m (11522 ft) above sea level.

Qi̘v Íhmíhzíhdà was founded durring the early 11th century, by Micropera Guynney Owens. The establishment of Qi̘v Íhmíhzíhdà was somewhat plagued by a lack of willing colonists, leading to Micropera Guynney Owens electing to pay people to resettle in Qi̘v Íhmíhzíhdà.

The city's buildings feature waddle and daub construction with good timber frames and a stone foundation protected by thatched or shingled rooves. Most buildings with second floors are built in such a way as to overhang into the streets on the upper floors for more space, as building size seems to be the primary indicator of wealth within the community. Most buildings are not decorated with any integral features, but instead use ivy, flowers, and other natural elements in planters of on trellices to breathe life into the structure they grow upon.

Cultural Notes

Qi̘v Íhmíhzíhdà's town hall was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is characterized by grandeur of scale, simplicity of geometric forms, dramatic use of columns, and a preference for blank walls. This design ethos extended to re-imagining earlier styles of structure to create them anew, with a similar overall look and feel to one another..

In Qi̘v Íhmíhzíhdà every night at precisely midnight every structure in town is engulfed by sailors fire until the end of the witching hour.

The Blightspawn near Qi̘v Íhmíhzíhdà are known to be a mutant strain of the creature.

Qi̘v Íhmíhzíhdà's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in spring and involves reenactments to channel Conjuration energies of tier 2 via oratory performances.
