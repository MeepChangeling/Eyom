Năz Wk-61y Mizkæ (/ˈmoxwæ/ /ˈmizkæ/ [benign] [expression (look on someone's face)]) is a temperate Large City located in Dèhæn Zone, Wozqoěs District, within the Alveria.

The name Năz Wk-61y Mizkæ is derived from the Sylvin language, as Năz Wk-61y Mizkæ was founded by Mf-894m 'EV-9D9', who was culturaly KLEO.

Năz Wk-61y Mizkæ has a yearly average temperature of 18°C (64°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 7°C (44°F). Năz Wk-61y Mizkæ covers an area of nearly 195 km2 (78 mi2), and an average elevation of 2366 m (7762 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Năz Wk-61y Mizkæ was founded durring the late 11th century, by Mf-894m 'EV-9D9'. The establishment of Năz Wk-61y Mizkæ was plagued by a lack of willing colonists. After attempts to pay people to resettle failed Mf-894m 'EV-9D9' struck deals with nearby nations and communities to establish Năz Wk-61y Mizkæ as a prison colony.

Năz Wk-61y Mizkæ was built using the conventions of KLEO durring the late 11th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Năz Wk-61y Mizkæ is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature timber framed wooden shiethed or brick construction, which gives form to a very formalized, rational, expence effishent arcatectural style based on strictly symmetrical designs which universaly feature pitched roofs, shutters, and the occasional column or pilaster for a decorative touch.

Năz Wk-61y Mizkæ is is constructed arround a semi-circular broad paverstone mainstreet, with smaller strait roads linking the circiles to eachother at varrious points. The city is defended by arcane means. It's hard to spot at first, but there's a tell tell shimmer in the air arround Năz Wk-61y Mizkæ, and you can spot the ocasional warding glyph carved into a rock or tree all arround town. These mystical defences are ancient, unknowable, and unassailable by current means... Assuming everything is in working order. Otherwise, the wards are little more than a deathtrap. Astonishigly, the Relic of the World That Was are in pristine condishion, as if they had just been finished before you laied eyes upon them.

Năz Wk-61y Mizkæ has the unmistakable air of a city on its last legs. Everything is a bit slipshod and ramshackle. Everyone is at work, or drinking. No one has anything in their eyes other than fear and despair. Whatever industry once fueled Năz Wk-61y Mizkæ ’s existence has dried up and the city is drifting down the stream of history as it dries up.

Cultural Notes

Năz Wk-61y Mizkæ's bank was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is known primarily for its use of abstraction and simplicity. Clean lines, right angles, and primary colors characterized this aesthetic and art movement expressed via architecture and paintings. Its design ethos allows only primary colors and non-colors, only squares and rectangles, only straight and horizontal or vertical lines. Vertical and horizontal lines are positioned in layers or planes that do not intersect, thereby allowing each element to exist independently and unobstructed by other elements. These seemingly impossible principals for an architectural style coalesces into structures which most experts find hard to put into words. It is not that their geometry is impossible, but rather the style's attempt at producing works only describable visually was most successful..

Due to the actions of local Kami, autumn is skipped in Năz Wk-61y Mizkæ.

The Spider, Assassin (Giant) near Năz Wk-61y Mizkæ are known to be quite timid.

Năz Wk-61y Mizkæ's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in spring and involves sex to channel Augury energies of tier 3 via singing.

History

Năz Wk-61y Mizkæ's residents established a new industry 4 years ago, and it’s making them a great deal of profit. Old patterns of authority and wealth are being disrupted, and the old gentry are unlikely to be pleased about it. They may be trying to take over the industry, or they may have been the ones to enable it in the first place and are using it to crush the life out of any rival power bases. Outsiders might be playing a major role as well, and it could be they plot to siphon off the profits.
