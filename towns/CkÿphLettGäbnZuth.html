Ckÿph Lett Gäbn Zuth (/ˈato̠/ /gəbn zuth/ [killer] [firearm]) is a temperate Large City located in Shebähash Hold, Baharb Province, within the United Kingdom of Undermountain.

The name Ckÿph Lett Gäbn Zuth is derived from the Dwarven language, as Ckÿph Lett Gäbn Zuth was founded by Vú̄w̄b Här 'Dani Alina' Vú̄boīy Cóń̄y Yoúrmë̌ Hutchäyel Hub̄qug, who was culturaly Dwarvern.

Ckÿph Lett Gäbn Zuth has a yearly average temperature of 18°C (64°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 13°C (55°F). Ckÿph Lett Gäbn Zuth receives an average of 283 cm/y (111 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. Ckÿph Lett Gäbn Zuth covers an area of nearly 462 km2 (184 mi2), and an average elevation of 2238 m (7342 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Ckÿph Lett Gäbn Zuth was founded durring the early 12th century in summer of the year 1118, by Vú̄w̄b Här 'Dani Alina' Vú̄boīy Cóń̄y Yoúrmë̌ Hutchäyel Hub̄qug. The establishment of Ckÿph Lett Gäbn Zuth suffered from many setbacks, delays, and obsticles, most notably a group of Ckÿph Lett Gäbn Zuth which required millitary assistance exterminate before the community could finish being built.

Ckÿph Lett Gäbn Zuth was built using the conventions of Dwarvern durring the early 12th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Ckÿph Lett Gäbn Zuth is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature waddle and daub construction with good timber frames and a stone foundation protected by thatched or shingled rooves. Most buildings with second floors are built in such a way as to overhang into the streets on the upper floors for more space, as building size seems to be the primary indicator of wealth within the community. Most buildings are not decorated with any integral features, but instead use ivy, flowers, and other natural elements in planters of on trellices to breathe life into the structure they grow upon.

Ckÿph Lett Gäbn Zuth is was constructed arround several restrictive packed earth mainstreets which cross one another at certain axies, with smaller streets branching off of them to premit acess to the many buildings deeper into the road network. The overall fashion is remenessent of a circulatory system, or other organic construct, and is quite effishent in its design. The lakeside city posesses a mighty albit amaturly crafted stone wall. It was built using querried stone blocks and posesses all of the standard defencive features, including a few watch towers. While it would last against a siege, the wall's outdated and simplistic construction method percludes the possability of Ckÿph Lett Gäbn Zuth weathering a full scale bombardment durring a siege. The budget focused millitary grade defenses has suffered a visible ammount of structural damage, leaving them effectivly useless. One can't help but wonder why the has not yet effected repairs.

A look around Ckÿph Lett Gäbn Zuth seems to be home to a quite vibrant and boisterous community. Everywhere one looks they can see people going out their daily business with a smile and a spring in their step. Children play loudly in the streets, causing untold havoc as youth are want and allowed to do.

Cultural Notes

Two or more groups of citizens within Ckÿph Lett Gäbn Zuth hate each other. Their neighbors or the local law have kept things from too-overt violence, but members of the groups will constantly interfere with their rivals and cause whatever misery they can get away with. This hate may spring from recent events, or it may be an inherited spite from old wrongs.

Ckÿph Lett Gäbn Zuth's garrison was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is characterized by vertical proportions, pointed arches, external buttressing, and asymmetry in the general shape of its buildings. The decorative features of the style were key, consisting of large arched windows, pointed arches, vaulted ceilings, flying buttresses, and sculptures integrated into the structure itself. Occasionally, for very important buildings, an array of sculptures or one colossal sculpture might replace the entirety of the entrance to said building.

Due to the actions of local Kami, summer is short in Ckÿph Lett Gäbn Zuth.

The Siren near Ckÿph Lett Gäbn Zuth are known to be more aggressive than normal.

Ckÿph Lett Gäbn Zuth's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in winter and involves gestures to channel Elven High Magic energies of tier 2 via throat singing.
