Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ (/ˈvəje/ /ˈbucæ/ [probable; possible] [honor]) is a temperate Large City located in Rîra Lagohû Kingdom, Gæchà Dîrîdî Empire, within the Warren.

The name Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ is derived from the Sylvin language, as Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ was founded by Ktîbë, who was culturaly Rattu.

Climate

Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ has a yearly average temperature of 12°C (53°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -2°C (29°F). Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ receives an average of 304 cm/y (119 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ covers an area of nearly 196 km2 (78 mi2), and an average elevation of 3832 m (12572 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ was founded durring the late 14th century in fall of the year 1295, by Ktîbë. The establishment of Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ was plagued by a lack of willing colonists. After attempts to pay people to resettle failed Ktîbë struck deals with nearby nations and communities to establish Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ as a prison colony.

Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ was built using the conventions of Rattu durring the late 14th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature masoned stone construction which prominantly features pointed arches, pointed ribbed vault cielings, flying buttress', and window tracery all of which share a simmilar gemoetetic patern halfway between organic and inorganic in design formaing a very distinct aesthetically integrated style. BUildings tend to reach for the havens, and more expencive homes are easily identified by their floor count as well as the addition of decorative features intigrated into the building's design such as statues, gargoyals, and embelished joinery.

Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ is buildings are arranged arround a single broad baked earthen mainstreet with many smaller streets branching off of it which gives the city a over all rectangular shape, albit one warped and twisted by the nature of the curves of the main road. The city is the proud owner of a properly designed set of renforced walls made from mighty querried stone blocks. Their construction and material choices would make a dwarf weap with joy, for each and every part of the elaborate fortifications are purly functional and robust well byond reason. Even nonexperts can tell the walls are an excelent defencive structure. Unfortuantly, these exceptionaly well made fortifications are in extreem disrepair, so much so that one cannot tell if they are decaying from a lack of mantance or damage incured.

Right off the bat Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ hits you in the face with its success. Everyone, even the peasants, are dressed in well made clothing. Every tool and implement you can see is finely made, and people will boast to you as obvious strangers of the wonders which can be found in their markets. More interestingly is a total lack of beggars, and plenty of new buildings are going up even as you speak. Somehow this city has come into quite a lot of wealth, and recently from the looks of things. Precisely how it has gained its wealth is a mystery. Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ is, in a word, disorder. People seem to be allowed to do as they please with little harmoney to anything. It feels less like a city, and more like a spot people just happened to place their homes. Yet there are small elements here and there which show the underlying structure of the community. It’s just so complex, organic, and flowing one can only understand what is a piece of the puzzle, but not what its neighbors are.

Civic Infrastructure

Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ possesses a city-wide Aethary Link which provides Aethary access anywhere within its metropolitan. This allows citizens who can afford the relevant devices access in their places of work, and rarely homes.

Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ has an animal control department which works to enforce local ordinances relating to the control, impoundment, and disposition of animals.

Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ has an Office of Civil Groundskeeping, which works to enforce local ordinances relating to the construction and upkeep up of all plant life, water features, and other natural decorations within Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ. They are also responsible for the maintenance of these features. Notably, the OCG is not responsible for Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ's parks.

Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ has an Office of Civil Vicary, which is responsible for providing a livelyhood for all officialy recognised religious figures within Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ.

Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ has a government-funded child care program, overseen by the local Department of Nursemaids, which is responsible for providing childcare to working-class citizens according to local ordinances.

Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ has a Gravedigger's Guild, which is responsible for collecting the dead and laying them to rest according to all applicable laws and religious customs.

Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ has a Guild of Nurses, which is tasked with caring for the elderly and infirm in accordance with local ordinances, religious values, and customs.

Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ has a Department of Firefighters, which is responsible for organizing fire fighting efforts during a fire and enforcing local ordinances relating to fire safety.

Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ has a Highwayman's Guild, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ has a Hall of Slayers, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ has an Arts Academy which provides higher education in many fields including math, language arts, philosophy, engineering, and other such disciplines.

Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ possesses a Galvanic Power Grid, which brings galvanic current to most if not all buildings in town, and permits a great many consumer goods to function within the Large City. Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ's grid is powered by a boiler and turbine based power plant.

Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ's old civil lighting system was converted to Galvanic Lamps recently, and expanded to provide nighttime illumination to all city streets.

Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ has a first rate hospital which caters to anyone in need of long term medical care.

Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ has a library, which keeps a large collection of books, scrolls, and archives all manner of physical items. While not open to the public, the librarians and scholars employed by the library will assist anyone with their research needs, and wealthy individuals can purchase membership to access the library's materials themselves. In spite of being generally closed to the public, the library has a room with several Aether Linked devices available to the public during business hours.

Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands. They are not to be confused with the Office of Civil Groundskeeping as they do not hold authority over nor responsibility for Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ's natural decorations nor waterways.

Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ has a Guild of Roadworkers, who are responsible for maintaining the roadways and public paths within town. They also have the duty of enforcing all civil laws relating to the roadways.

Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ is home to a University which provides higher education in a variety of fields, and also serves as a research institute for those same fields.

Cultural Notes

Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ's chapel was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used which employed abundant symbolic geometry, using pure forms such as the circle and square, and plans are based on often symmetrical layouts featuring rectangular courtyards and halls. These structures were is decorated with carved stone or stucco reliefs and made use of colorful stone mosaics..

In Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ snow is warm to the touch and does not melt within city limits. Also it only happens during summer.

The Grimstalker near Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ are known to be a mutant strain of the creature.

Chyameya Kurahyi Buchyæ's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in spring and involves consuming a local narcotic to channel Necromancy energies of tier 1 via divine sermons.
