Àsu̹ Làbo Kâchyâ (/ˈəsu̹/ /ˈləbo/ [fair; just; moral] [car]) is a temperate City located in the Temmī̄f Principality of the Lallalo.

The name Àsu̹ Làbo Kâchyâ is derived from the Sylvin language, as Àsu̹ Làbo Kâchyâ was founded by Ůwdie, who was culturaly Rattu.

Climate

Àsu̹ Làbo Kâchyâ has a yearly average temperature of 7°C (44°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a freezing -1°C (31°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 16°C (60°F). Àsu̹ Làbo Kâchyâ receives an average of 222 cm/y (87 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Àsu̹ Làbo Kâchyâ covers an area of nearly 36 km2 (14 mi2), and an average elevation of 3950 m (12959 ft) above sea level.

Cultural Notes

One or more crime bosses have a powerful influence within Àsu̹ Làbo Kâchyâ. They may control crime within the community itself, or they may use it simply as a safe haven from which to direct their minions elsewhere. Local law enforcement may know all about them, but lack the strength to confront them and their paid or intimidated henchmen.

Àsu̹ Làbo Kâchyâ's mayor's house was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is characterized by their massive, monolithic and 'blocky' appearance with a rigid geometric style. It was best known for its rough, unfinished surfaces, unusual shapes, heavy-looking materials, straight lines, and small windows. Modular elements were often used to form masses representing specific functional zones, grouped into a unified whole.

Due to the actions of local Kami, spring is short in Àsu̹ Làbo Kâchyâ.

The Anhana near Àsu̹ Làbo Kâchyâ are known to be quite timid.

Àsu̹ Làbo Kâchyâ's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in winter and involves destroying a prepared ritual vessel to channel Truename Magic energies of tier 1 via chanting.
