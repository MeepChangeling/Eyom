Stsdièbùjā Ji̊ Qi̊v (Stsdiè /ˈbʊʤəˑ/ [interest (curiosity; concern)] [photograph]) is a subtropical City located in the Tmíhiy̼gelfàf Region of the Tetburland.

The name Stsdièbùjā Ji̊ Qi̊v is derived from the Sylvin language, as Stsdièbùjā Ji̊ Qi̊v was founded by Gith́, who was culturaly Tauran.

Climate

Stsdièbùjā Ji̊ Qi̊v has a yearly average temperature of 31°C (87°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 35°C (95°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 28°C (82°F). Stsdièbùjā Ji̊ Qi̊v receives an average of 191 cm/y (75 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. Stsdièbùjā Ji̊ Qi̊v covers an area of nearly 41 km2 (16 mi2), and an average elevation of 3152 m (10341 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Stsdièbùjā Ji̊ Qi̊v was founded durring the early 15th century in fall of the year 1417, by Gith́. The establishment of the new community went well, with no major obsticles durring construction.

Stsdièbùjā Ji̊ Qi̊v was built using the conventions of Tauran durring the early 15th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Stsdièbùjā Ji̊ Qi̊v is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature timber framed wooden shiethed or brick construction, which gives form to a very formalized, rational, expence effishent arcatectural style based on strictly symmetrical designs which universaly feature pitched roofs, shutters, and the occasional column or pilaster for a decorative touch.

Stsdièbùjā Ji̊ Qi̊v is is constructed arround a series of restrictive packed earth mainstreets which form concentric circles, with smaller strait roads linking the circiles to each other at varrious points. The city rests behind the absurdity that is a thick, timber braced, wall made of clay bricks. While visualy impressive and certainly an astetic, Stsdièbùjā Ji̊ Qi̊v's wall provides no actual defence against siege equipment due to the choice of its cosntruction materials. Even nonexperts can tell the town is trying to impress rather than defend with its walls, towers, and gatehouses. Though admittedly, they do look nice... To primitive tribals who have never seen fortifications before. Stsdièbùjā Ji̊ Qi̊v's political statment focused walls are suffering from significent damage, so much so that examples can be pointed to no matter which section one might have within their line of site, and most of which render sections inoperable at present.

Stsdièbùjā Ji̊ Qi̊v has a very calm atmosphere. People can be seen relaxing, scocilizing, and going about all manner of business other than the daily grind. Men, women, children, all can be seen enjoying life in a laid-back way in the many parks which line Stsdièbùjā Ji̊ Qi̊v’s streets. In spite of this, quite a few people can be seen reading and the city has an abundance of libraries. It’s quite clear the community values education.

Stsdièbùjā Ji̊ Qi̊v has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Cultural Notes

While Stsdièbùjā Ji̊ Qi̊v might ostensibly be ruled by some other power, real control lies with the senior members of the local craft and labor guilds. Their decisions have the practical weight of law, and much of their time and effort is spent squeezing out competitors and parceling out economic opportunities in the community. Some guilds might have little or nothing to do with their original trade, and now exist purely as shells for political influence.

Stsdièbùjā Ji̊ Qi̊v's town hall was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is known for its buildings being predominantly formed through the combination of basic geometric shapes. However, it shown in the detailing given to the structures such as its characteristic tall columns, intricate detail, symmetry, harmony, and balance in their designs to an astonishing degree of precision. Decorative elements for the buildings tended to be built into the structure itself, making great use of fluting, frescoes, inlays, and embossing.

Due to the actions of local Kami, spring is long in Stsdièbùjā Ji̊ Qi̊v.

The Wasp Swarm near Stsdièbùjā Ji̊ Qi̊v are known to be more aggressive than normal.

Stsdièbùjā Ji̊ Qi̊v's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in autumn and involves sex to channel Elven High Magic energies of tier 3 via divine sermons.

History

In time immemorial, reportedly some time during the early 2nd century, Stsdièbùjā Ji̊ Qi̊v was attacked by soldiers from another nation, waging a greater campaign. The details of the conflict are hazy at best due to many conflicting accounts. What is known is Stsdièbùjā Ji̊ Qi̊v lost 260 people, 125 livestock, and 62 buildings. The conflict ended after roughly 184, when members of Stsdièbùjā Ji̊ Qi̊v's militia enacted an operation to locate a particular solgiers group. The operation was complicated by at least some of the intelligence related to the operation was incorrect. The conflict ended with pitched battle between both forces, which ended in defeat for Stsdièbùjā Ji̊ Qi̊v's forces. The war is remembered in legend by Stsdièbùjā Ji̊ Qi̊v's bards, historians, and legend keepers.
