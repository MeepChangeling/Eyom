Eg Rächulf Nackyeq (/ogtəˈgi/ /naˈkjeq/ [sole; lone] [population]) is a subtropical Town located in the Gumada Provence of the Kingdom of Hobben.

The name Eg Rächulf Nackyeq is derived from the Dwarven language, as Eg Rächulf Nackyeq was founded by Idi Hub̄qug, who was culturaly Dwarvern.

Climate

Eg Rächulf Nackyeq has a yearly average temperature of 26°C (78°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 31°C (87°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 22°C (71°F). Eg Rächulf Nackyeq receives an average of 267 cm/y (105 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. Eg Rächulf Nackyeq covers an area of nearly 4 km2 (1 mi2), and an average elevation of 1726 m (5662 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Eg Rächulf Nackyeq was founded durring the early 11th century, by Idi Hub̄qug. The establishment of Eg Rächulf Nackyeq suffered from many setbacks, delays, and obsticles, most notably a group of Eg Rächulf Nackyeq which required millitary assistance exterminate before the community could finish being built.

Eg Rächulf Nackyeq was built using the conventions of Dwarvern durring the early 11th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Eg Rächulf Nackyeq is no diffrent. The town's buildings feature timber framed wooden shiethed or brick construction, which gives form to a very formalized, rational, expence effishent arcatectural style based on strictly symmetrical designs which universaly feature pitched roofs, shutters, and the occasional column or pilaster for a decorative touch.

Eg Rächulf Nackyeq is buildings are arranged arround a network of narrow cobblestone streets which form a diamond shaped grid, where each diamond verries in size given the proximity of the paralell streets forming each section. The ocasional smaller diamond has been used to construct a park, plaza, and other communal structures. The town is protected by a humble stone fence which is tall enough to provide adiquite cover for defenders to fire from, but no more than that. Unfortuantly, these peasent-grade are in extreem disrepair, so much so that one cannot tell if they are decaying from a lack of mantance or damage incured.

Something in your gut tells you that you may be unwelcome in Eg Rächulf Nackyeq. The town seems like it’s not showing you the side of itself it would show to others. People mostly ignore your questions. Many folks ask you to leave their establishments, even before you’ve walked inside them. You should probably listen to them, as every time you’re asked to leave the person asking you to leave has referenced the town’s patron divine in some way. The little voice in the back of your mind that tells you when you’re in danger feels like it’s just glaring at you and muttering about how stupid you are for even remaining in town.

Civic Infrastructure

Eg Rächulf Nackyeq has an Office of Civil Vicary, which is responsible for providing a livelyhood for all officialy recognised religious figures within Eg Rächulf Nackyeq.

Eg Rächulf Nackyeq has a Gravedigger's Guild, which is responsible for collecting the dead and laying them to rest according to all applicable laws and religious customs.

Eg Rächulf Nackyeq has a Highwayman's Guild, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Eg Rächulf Nackyeq has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain Eg Rächulf Nackyeq's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

Eg Rächulf Nackyeq has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands.

Eg Rächulf Nackyeq has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Cultural Notes

One or more crime bosses have a powerful influence within Eg Rächulf Nackyeq. They may control crime within the community itself, or they may use it simply as a safe haven from which to direct their minions elsewhere. Local law enforcement may know all about them, but lack the strength to confront them and their paid or intimidated henchmen.

Eg Rächulf Nackyeq's bank was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is characterized by grandeur of scale, simplicity of geometric forms, dramatic use of columns, and a preference for blank walls. This design ethos extended to re-imagining earlier styles of structure to create them anew, with a similar overall look and feel to one another..

Due to the actions of local Kami, spring is long in Eg Rächulf Nackyeq.

The Nightcaller near Eg Rächulf Nackyeq are known to be a mutant strain of the creature.

Eg Rächulf Nackyeq's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in summer and involves performance art to channel Wild Magic energies of tier 3 via recitation of scripture.

Points of Interest

Eg Rächulf Nackyeq's roads were poorly made when first laid. Rather than repairing them correctly, a series of new roads was laid atop the old, leading to the streets of modern Eg Rächulf Nackyeq suffering from potholes, cracking, and even sinkholes. The locals often repair the road by putting down wooden decking.
