Becaz Yǟqüe Qo̠ (/ˈbecaz/ /jaːq˔/ [guilty] [poem; lyrics]) is a subtropical Large City located in Vremōfzbo Parish, Shan Deanery, within the Covenent of Irus.

The name Becaz Yǟqüe Qo̠ is derived from the Wood Elvish language, as Becaz Yǟqüe Qo̠ was founded by Triesh Criebolri, who was culturaly Wood Elven.

Climate

Becaz Yǟqüe Qo̠ has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 20°C (68°F). Becaz Yǟqüe Qo̠ receives an average of 192 cm/y (75 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. Becaz Yǟqüe Qo̠ covers an area of nearly 273 km2 (109 mi2), and an average elevation of 4890 m (16043 ft) above sea level.

Cultural Notes

Becaz Yǟqüe Qo̠'s garrison was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is known primarily for its use of abstraction and simplicity. Clean lines, right angles, and primary colors characterized this aesthetic and art movement expressed via architecture and paintings. Its design ethos allows only primary colors and non-colors, only squares and rectangles, only straight and horizontal or vertical lines. Vertical and horizontal lines are positioned in layers or planes that do not intersect, thereby allowing each element to exist independently and unobstructed by other elements. These seemingly impossible principals for an architectural style coalesces into structures which most experts find hard to put into words. It is not that their geometry is impossible, but rather the style's attempt at producing works only describable visually was most successful..
