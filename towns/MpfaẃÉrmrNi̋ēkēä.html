Mpfaẃ Érmr Ni̋ēkēä (/mpʰɒ́/ /ɜ̂ːmɜː/ [thin (slender); slim] [lease]) is a subtropical Large City located in the Thḗpō̌yey Region of the Goblin Tribes.

The name Mpfaẃ Érmr Ni̋ēkēä is derived from the Goblin language, as Mpfaẃ Érmr Ni̋ēkēä was founded by Anghara Shark-Eating Crabs Goliath Stag Beetles, who was culturaly Goblin.

Climate

Mpfaẃ Érmr Ni̋ēkēä has a yearly average temperature of 20°C (68°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 11°C (51°F). Mpfaẃ Érmr Ni̋ēkēä receives an average of 270 cm/y (106 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. Mpfaẃ Érmr Ni̋ēkēä covers an area of nearly 55 km2 (22 mi2), and an average elevation of 3876 m (12716 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Mpfaẃ Érmr Ni̋ēkēä was founded durring the early 12th century in winter of the year 1108, by Anghara Shark-Eating Crabs Goliath Stag Beetles. The establishment of the new community went well, with no major obsticles durring construction.

Mpfaẃ Érmr Ni̋ēkēä was built using the conventions of Goblin durring the early 12th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Mpfaẃ Érmr Ni̋ēkēä is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature waddle and daub construction with good timber frames and a stone foundation protected by thatched or shingled rooves. Most buildings with second floors are built in such a way as to overhang into the streets on the upper floors for more space, as building size seems to be the primary indicator of wealth within the community. Most buildings are not decorated with any integral features, but instead use ivy, flowers, and other natural elements in planters of on trellices to breathe life into the structure they grow upon.

Mpfaẃ Érmr Ni̋ēkēä is is constructed arround a series of narrow paverstone mainstreets which form concentric circles, with smaller strait roads linking the circiles to each other at varrious points. The city posesses a mighty albit amaturly crafted stone wall. It was built using querried stone blocks and posesses all of the standard defencive features, including a few watch towers. While it would last against a siege, the wall's outdated and simplistic construction method percludes the possability of Mpfaẃ Érmr Ni̋ēkēä weathering a full scale bombardment durring a siege. The budget focused millitary grade defenses have not been wellmaintained over the years, and while functional are in dire need of some loving care and perhapse light renovation.

A look around Mpfaẃ Érmr Ni̋ēkēä is like a look into a broken heart. There is no planning, no organization. Everyone here clearly goes about their own thing with little thought to anyone around them who isn’t selling something they need. It’s not malicious. Heads are hung. Eyes are empty. Something truly horrible happened here once upon a time and the city never healed.

History

In time immemorial, reportedly some time during the early 2nd century, Mpfaẃ Érmr Ni̋ēkēä was attacked by soldiers from another nation, waging a greater campaign. The details of the conflict are hazy at best due to many conflicting accounts. What is known is Mpfaẃ Érmr Ni̋ēkēä lost 210 people, 206 livestock, and 95 buildings. The conflict ended after roughly 180, when members of Mpfaẃ Érmr Ni̋ēkēä's militia enacted an operation to rescue a particular irregulars group from the enemy. The operation was complicated by at least some of the intelligence related to the operation was incorrect. The conflict ended with an assault and siege on the port, which ended in a stalemate for Mpfaẃ Érmr Ni̋ēkēä's forces. The war is remembered in legend by Mpfaẃ Érmr Ni̋ēkēä's bards, historians, and legend keepers.
