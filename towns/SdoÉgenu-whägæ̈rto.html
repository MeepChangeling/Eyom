Sdo Égenu-whägæ̈rto (/ˌe̜stjaːˈfoklek/ /whɑˈgæ̝rto/ [critical] [carcass]) is a temperate Large City located in the Atonkas Governorate of the Koiapian City-state.

The name Sdo Égenu-whägæ̈rto is derived from the Gnollish language, as Sdo Égenu-whägæ̈rto was founded by Szarheihr vyim Glə˞graɑgki, who was culturaly Gnollish.

Climate

Sdo Égenu-whägæ̈rto has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 9°C (48°F). Sdo Égenu-whägæ̈rto receives an average of 242 cm/y (95 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. Sdo Égenu-whägæ̈rto covers an area of nearly 272 km2 (108 mi2), and an average elevation of 4392 m (14409 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Sdo Égenu-whägæ̈rto was founded durring the early 15th century in spring of the year 1410, by Szarheihr vyim Glə˞graɑgki. The establishment of the new community went well, with no major obsticles durring construction.

Sdo Égenu-whägæ̈rto was built using the conventions of Gnollish durring the early 15th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Sdo Égenu-whägæ̈rto is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature plaster covered brickwork used to form structures with an emphasis on symmetry, proportion, geometry and the regularity of parts. Orderly arrangements of columns, pilasters and lintels, as well as the use of semicircular arches, hemispherical domes, niches and aediculae can be found everywhere such that only size of building and yard can be used to measure the general prosparity of a given building's owners due to a general wealthy feeling the style gives off.

Sdo Égenu-whägæ̈rto is buildings are grouped arround an odd layout of restrictive baked earthen streets, which seems to be based on an overlapping squair patern such that there are small squares at the cornor of every bigger square. Sometimes buildings exist in the smaller squaires, other times they are open spaces, or occupied by temporary structures. The city rests behind a set of well fortified walls, with gatehouses, watch towers, and battlments. Sdo Égenu-whägæ̈rto's walls are, howeaver, fashioned from stone and timber. While unorthadox, the design looks to be functional to a reasonable degree. With luck, the untested design will remain untested for years to come. Sdo Égenu-whägæ̈rto's unusual yet seemingly effective fortifications has sufferd soem light damage, reducing its function a little in some spots, but could almsot certainly preform as expected... Though some of the worse spots could lead to the loss of defenders lives if attackers identified the weaknesses ahead of time.

Sdo Égenu-whägæ̈rto is, in a word, disorder. People seem to be allowed to do as they please with little harmoney to anything. It feels less like a city, and more like a spot people just happened to place their homes. Yet there are small elements here and there which show the underlying structure of the community. It’s just so complex, organic, and flowing one can only understand what is a piece of the puzzle, but not what its neighbors are.

Sdo Égenu-whägæ̈rto has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Sdo Égenu-whägæ̈rto is home to a University which provides higher education in a variety of fields, and also serves as a research institute for those same fields.

Cultural Notes

Sdo Égenu-whägæ̈rto's garrison was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used which employed abundant symbolic geometry, using pure forms such as the circle and square, and plans are based on often symmetrical layouts featuring rectangular courtyards and halls. These structures were is decorated with carved stone or stucco reliefs and made use of colorful stone mosaics..

In Sdo Égenu-whägæ̈rto sometimes, near one of the seasonal solstices, the sun appears to split into three and it rains from a clear sky for several hours.

The Spider, Ogre (Huge) near Sdo Égenu-whägæ̈rto are known to be almost tame, such that they can be put to domestic use.

Sdo Égenu-whägæ̈rto's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in spring and involves drinking to channel Abjuration energies of tier 2 via oratory performances.
