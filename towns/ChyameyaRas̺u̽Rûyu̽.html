Chyameya Ras̺u̽ Rûyu̽ (/ˈras̺u̽/ /ˈrʊju̽/ [white; blank; vacant] [rod; shaft; bar; pole; axle]) is a temperate Large City located in Makuheru Kingdom, Mijgake Empire, within the Warren.

The name Chyameya Ras̺u̽ Rûyu̽ is derived from the Sylvin language, as Chyameya Ras̺u̽ Rûyu̽ was founded by Pana, who was culturaly Rattu.

Climate

Chyameya Ras̺u̽ Rûyu̽ has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 9°C (48°F). Chyameya Ras̺u̽ Rûyu̽ receives an average of 71 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. Chyameya Ras̺u̽ Rûyu̽ covers an area of nearly 196 km2 (78 mi2), and an average elevation of 6986 m (-20029 ft) above sea level.

The establishment of Chyameya Ras̺u̽ Rûyu̽ suffered from many setbacks, delays, and obsticles, most notably a group of Chyameya Ras̺u̽ Rûyu̽ which required millitary assistance exterminate before the community could finish being built.

Chyameya Ras̺u̽ Rûyu̽ was built using the conventions of Rattu durring the early 14th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Chyameya Ras̺u̽ Rûyu̽ is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature timber framed wooden shiethed or brick construction, which gives form to a very formalized, rational, expence effishent arcatectural style based on strictly symmetrical designs which universaly feature pitched roofs, shutters, and the occasional column or pilaster for a decorative touch.

Chyameya Ras̺u̽ Rûyu̽ is buildings have been located at convienant points along the valley Chyameya Ras̺u̽ Rûyu̽ was built upon. Navigating the town is therefore a little chalanging as the distance between buildings verris greatly and the narrow paverstone streets flow where they are able to be made rather than folowing the most convienant paths. The city has a defencive wall made from querried stone. The wall is equipped with a full set of battlments but the nature of its construction methodology leaves it somewhat vulnerable to siege equipment. That said, the city is well defended against anything short of an army. The cost-cutting-focused defences has suffered a visible ammount of structural damage, leaving them effectivly useless. One can't help but wonder why the has not yet effected repairs.

Maybe it’s the way fog blankets the ground, but only in the connors of places. Maybe it’s the vermin scutteling between shadows in the corner of your eyes. It’s quite clear the city places a lot of value on education and being a learned individual. Regardless, you do not feel it would be wise to remain in Chyameya Ras̺u̽ Rûyu̽ long.

Cultural Notes

Chyameya Ras̺u̽ Rûyu̽'s mayor's house was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is which made use of the classical orders and mathematically precise ratios of height and width combined with a desire for symmetry, proportion, and harmony. It used columns, pediments, arches and domes are imaginatively in buildings of all types. Decorative features were seen as largely unnecessary as the sheer beauty of the structure itself was often close to art. However, many buildings with large ceiling spaces had their ceilings decorated with elaborate paintings, simply because the large flat spaces could feel wasted.

In Chyameya Ras̺u̽ Rûyu̽ grains of dust blow into perfectly neat rows.

The Shredskin near Chyameya Ras̺u̽ Rûyu̽ are known to be a mutant strain of the creature.

Chyameya Ras̺u̽ Rûyu̽'s citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in autumn and involves destroying a prepared ritual vessel to channel Truename Magic energies of tier 3 via speaking in tongues.
