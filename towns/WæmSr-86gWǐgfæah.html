Wæm Sr-86g Wǐgfæah (/cɪx/ /wɪgˈfæah/ [navy blue] [health]) is a subtropical Town located in Misuqaba Parish, Wv-0săbb District, within the Federation of Alveria.

The name Wæm Sr-86g Wǐgfæah is derived from the Constructi language, as Wæm Sr-86g Wǐgfæah was founded by Brtco Zepí, who was culturaly Constructi.

Cultural Notes

Wæm Sr-86g Wǐgfæah's locals despise outsiders. For some “outsiders” may be natives of foreign lands, while others might have a grudge against anyone from outside the community. These locals have an active loathing, and the outsiders allowed to trade or interact with them do so at a heavy disadvantage.

Wæm Sr-86g Wǐgfæah's mayor's house was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used makes use of a large oblong hall or building with double colonnades and a semicircular apse and symmetrical central-plan, resulting in buildings with a square central mass and four arms of equal length. Decorative features included domed rooves, arches, soaring spaces, and sumptuous decoration: marble columns and inlay, mosaics on the vaults, inlaid-stone pavements, and sometimes gold coffered ceilings.

In Wæm Sr-86g Wǐgfæah hail is always enormous, yet harmlessly plinks off people, creatures, and structures.

The Blood Hawk near Wæm Sr-86g Wǐgfæah are known to be a mutant strain of the creature.

Wæm Sr-86g Wǐgfæah's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in summer and involves ritual combat to channel Transmutation energies of tier 2 via moments of science.
