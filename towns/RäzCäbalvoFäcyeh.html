Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh (/kəbalˈvo/ /fəˈkjɪ̘/ [political] [cloak]) is a subtropical Large City located in Yåyhäy Eb Hold, Gandlacckÿrf Kingdom, within the Undermountain.

The name Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh is derived from the Sylvin language, as Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh was founded by Thedoul, who was culturaly Undermountain.

Climate

Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh has a yearly average temperature of 26°C (78°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 30°C (86°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 23°C (73°F). Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh receives an average of 166 cm/y (65 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh covers an area of nearly 133 km2 (53 mi2), and an average elevation of 3816 m (12519 ft) above sea level.

Civic Infrastructure

Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh possesses a city-wide Aethary Link which provides Aethary access anywhere within its metropolitan. This allows citizens who can afford the relevant devices access in their places of work, and rarely homes.

Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh has an animal control department which works to enforce local ordinances relating to the control, impoundment, and disposition of animals.

Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh has an Office of Civil Groundskeeping, which works to enforce local ordinances relating to the construction and upkeep up of all plant life, water features, and other natural decorations within Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh. They are also responsible for the maintenance of these features. Notably, the OCG is not responsible for Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh's parks.

Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh has an Office of Civil Vicary, which is responsible for providing a livelyhood for all officialy recognised religious figures within Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh.

Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh has a government-funded child care program, overseen by the local Department of Nursemaids, which is responsible for providing childcare to working-class citizens according to local ordinances.

Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh has a Gravedigger's Guild, which is responsible for collecting the dead and laying them to rest according to all applicable laws and religious customs.

Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh has a Guild of Nurses, which is tasked with caring for the elderly and infirm in accordance with local ordinances, religious values, and customs.

Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh has a Department of Firefighters, which is responsible for organizing fire fighting efforts during a fire and enforcing local ordinances relating to fire safety.

Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh has a Highwayman's Guild, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh has a Hall of Slayers, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh has an Arts Academy which provides higher education in many fields including math, language arts, philosophy, engineering, and other such disciplines.

Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh possesses a Galvanic Power Grid, which brings galvanic current to most if not all buildings in town, and permits a great many consumer goods to function within the Large City. Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh's grid is powered by mana accumulators.

Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh's old civil lighting system was converted to Galvanic Lamps recently, and expanded to provide nighttime illumination to all city streets.

Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh has a first rate hospital which caters to anyone in need of long term medical care.

Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh has a library, which keeps a large collection of books, scrolls, and archives all manner of physical items. While not open to the public, the librarians and scholars employed by the library will assist anyone with their research needs, and wealthy individuals can purchase membership to access the library's materials themselves. In spite of being generally closed to the public, the library has a room with several Aether Linked devices available to the public during business hours.

Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands. They are not to be confused with the Office of Civil Groundskeeping as they do not hold authority over nor responsibility for Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh's natural decorations nor waterways.

Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh has a Guild of Roadworkers, who are responsible for maintaining the roadways and public paths within town. They also have the duty of enforcing all civil laws relating to the roadways.

Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Cultural Notes

Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh's mayor's house was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is based upon new and innovative technologies of construction and the idea that form should follow function. It was an embrace of minimalism and a rejection of ornament. The style became characterized by an emphasis on volume, asymmetrical compositions, and minimal ornamentation..

In Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh sometimes, near one of the seasonal solstices, the sun appears to split into three and it rains from a clear sky for several hours.

The Blink Dog near Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh are known to be more aggressive than normal.

Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in winter and involves orgies to channel Enchantment energies of tier 3 via moments of science.

A great funnel cloud itself touched down in Räz Cäbalvo Fäcyeh, bringing twisting winds which killed 129 people, 235 livestock, and 94 buildings in the disaster..
