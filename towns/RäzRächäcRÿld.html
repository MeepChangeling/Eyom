Räz Rächäc Rÿld (/dub/ /rild/ [random] [twilight]) is a subtropical Large City located in Qangyeshÿhpm Hold, Lälhulål Kingdom, within the Undermountain.

The name Räz Rächäc Rÿld is derived from the Sylvin language, as Räz Rächäc Rÿld was founded by Dropera, who was culturaly Undermountain.

Climate

Räz Rächäc Rÿld has a yearly average temperature of 18°C (64°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 13°C (55°F). Räz Rächäc Rÿld receives an average of 178 cm/y (70 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. Räz Rächäc Rÿld covers an area of nearly 108 km2 (43 mi2), and an average elevation of 6292 m (20643 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Räz Rächäc Rÿld was founded durring the late 14th century in fall of the year 1278, by Dropera. The establishment of the new community went well, with no major obsticles durring construction.

Räz Rächäc Rÿld was built using the conventions of Undermountain durring the late 14th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Räz Rächäc Rÿld is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature waddle and daub construction with good timber frames and a stone foundation protected by thatched or shingled rooves. Most buildings with second floors are built in such a way as to overhang into the streets on the upper floors for more space, as building size seems to be the primary indicator of wealth within the community. Most buildings are not decorated with any integral features, but instead use ivy, flowers, and other natural elements in planters of on trellices to breathe life into the structure they grow upon.

Räz Rächäc Rÿld is is constructed arround a series of restrictive paverstone mainstreets which form overlapping circles, with smaller strait roads linking the circiles to eachother at varrious points. The city has a fortified albit thin brick wall. The wall has most of the feeatures of a typical castle wall, just on a much smaller scale and and budget. Notably brick isn't a particuarly soild choice for resisting siege weapons. Räz Rächäc Rÿld's wall wouldn't hinder a proper army, but it is more than sufishent for bandits and other small marauding groups. Räz Rächäc Rÿld's monster and outlaw focused fortifications has sufferd soem light damage, reducing its function a little in some spots, but could almsot certainly preform as expected... Though some of the worse spots could lead to the loss of defenders lives if attackers identified the weaknesses ahead of time.

A look around Räz Rächäc Rÿld makes it abundantly clear the city suffered something horrible some time ago. It's as if the town itself is depressed. Smiles are few, cheer is nowhere to be had. Everyone quietly goes about their daily business not looking anyone in the eye.

Civic Infrastructure

Räz Rächäc Rÿld possesses a city-wide Aethary Link which provides Aethary access anywhere within its metropolitan. This allows citizens who can afford the relevant devices access in their places of work, and rarely homes.

Räz Rächäc Rÿld has an animal control department which works to enforce local ordinances relating to the control, impoundment, and disposition of animals.

Räz Rächäc Rÿld has an Office of Civil Groundskeeping, which works to enforce local ordinances relating to the construction and upkeep up of all plant life, water features, and other natural decorations within Räz Rächäc Rÿld. They are also responsible for the maintenance of these features. Notably, the OCG is not responsible for Räz Rächäc Rÿld's parks.

Räz Rächäc Rÿld has an Office of Civil Vicary, which is responsible for providing a livelyhood for all officialy recognised religious figures within Räz Rächäc Rÿld.

Räz Rächäc Rÿld has a government-funded child care program, overseen by the local Department of Nursemaids, which is responsible for providing childcare to working-class citizens according to local ordinances.

Räz Rächäc Rÿld has a Gravedigger's Guild, which is responsible for collecting the dead and laying them to rest according to all applicable laws and religious customs.

Räz Rächäc Rÿld has a Guild of Nurses, which is tasked with caring for the elderly and infirm in accordance with local ordinances, religious values, and customs.

Räz Rächäc Rÿld has a Department of Firefighters, which is responsible for organizing fire fighting efforts during a fire and enforcing local ordinances relating to fire safety.

Räz Rächäc Rÿld has a Highwayman's Guild, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Räz Rächäc Rÿld has a Hall of Slayers, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Räz Rächäc Rÿld has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain Räz Rächäc Rÿld's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

Räz Rächäc Rÿld has an Theological Academy which trains clergy in various arcane and theological topics required for their occupations.

Räz Rächäc Rÿld possesses a Galvanic Power Grid, which brings galvanic current to most if not all buildings in town, and permits a great many consumer goods to function within the Large City. Räz Rächäc Rÿld's grid is powered by an arcane means.

Räz Rächäc Rÿld's old civil lighting system was converted to Galvanic Lamps recently, and expanded to provide nighttime illumination to all city streets.

Räz Rächäc Rÿld has a first rate hospital which caters to anyone in need of long term medical care.

Räz Rächäc Rÿld has a library, which keeps a large collection of books, scrolls, and archives all manner of physical items. While not open to the public, the librarians and scholars employed by the library will assist anyone with their research needs, and wealthy individuals can purchase membership to access the library's materials themselves. In spite of being generally closed to the public, the library has a room with several Aether Linked devices available to the public during business hours.

Räz Rächäc Rÿld has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands. They are not to be confused with the Office of Civil Groundskeeping as they do not hold authority over nor responsibility for Räz Rächäc Rÿld's natural decorations nor waterways.

Räz Rächäc Rÿld has a Guild of Roadworkers, who are responsible for maintaining the roadways and public paths within town. They also have the duty of enforcing all civil laws relating to the roadways.

Räz Rächäc Rÿld has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Räz Rächäc Rÿld has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Cultural Notes

Räz Rächäc Rÿld's chapel was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is characterized by their massive, monolithic and 'blocky' appearance with a rigid geometric style. It was best known for its rough, unfinished surfaces, unusual shapes, heavy-looking materials, straight lines, and small windows. Modular elements were often used to form masses representing specific functional zones, grouped into a unified whole.

Due to the actions of local Kami, autumn is long in Räz Rächäc Rÿld.

The Ercinee near Räz Rächäc Rÿld are known to be almost tame, such that they can be put to domestic use.

Räz Rächäc Rÿld's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in summer and involves consuming a local toxin to channel Mysticism energies of tier 3 via oath swearing.

Points of Interest

While private tutelage of worthy apprentices can be had even in most remote villages, Räz Rächäc Rÿld is home to a proper school dedicated to teaching magic. Such schools are usually small, with no more than a few dozen pupils, most of whom will fail for lack of talent or discipline. The instructors are rarely first-rate, usually serving only for the pay and status, but sometimes a genius sorcerer will find a reason to observe likely apprentices here. Given the unfortunate accident potential of the school, it’s probably isolated or well-fortified.

The center of Räz Rächäc Rÿld's town square was built around an ancient standing stone.
