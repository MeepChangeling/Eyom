Lilgap Wo-13y Bun (/bic/ /bun/ [fun] [percent]) is a subtropical Small City located in Ac-78zwìb Zone, Wækwìilbu District, within the Alveria.

The name Lilgap Wo-13y Bun is derived from the Goblin language, as Lilgap Wo-13y Bun was founded by Blodwen, who was culturaly KLEO.

Climate

Lilgap Wo-13y Bun has a yearly average temperature of 32°C (89°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 37°C (98°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 28°C (82°F). Lilgap Wo-13y Bun receives an average of 235 cm/y (92 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. Lilgap Wo-13y Bun covers an area of nearly 10 km2 (4 mi2), and an average elevation of 21810 m (-14344 ft) above sea level.

Civic Infrastructure

Lilgap Wo-13y Bun has an animal control department which works to enforce local ordinances relating to the control, impoundment, and disposition of animals.

Lilgap Wo-13y Bun has an Office of Civil Groundskeeping, which works to enforce local ordinances relating to the construction and upkeep up of all plant life, water features, and other natural decorations within Lilgap Wo-13y Bun. They are also responsible for the maintenance of these features. Notably, the OCG is not responsible for Lilgap Wo-13y Bun's parks.

Lilgap Wo-13y Bun has an Office of Civil Vicary, which is responsible for providing a livelyhood for all officialy recognised religious figures within Lilgap Wo-13y Bun.

Lilgap Wo-13y Bun has a government-funded child care program, overseen by the local Department of Nursemaids, which is responsible for providing childcare to working-class citizens according to local ordinances.

Lilgap Wo-13y Bun has a Gravedigger's Guild, which is responsible for collecting the dead and laying them to rest according to all applicable laws and religious customs.

Lilgap Wo-13y Bun has a Highwayman's Guild, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Lilgap Wo-13y Bun has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain Lilgap Wo-13y Bun's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

Lilgap Wo-13y Bun possesses a Galvanic Power Grid, which brings galvanic current to most if not all buildings in town, and permits a great many consumer goods to function within the Small City. Lilgap Wo-13y Bun's grid is powered by a boiler and turbine based power plant.

Lilgap Wo-13y Bun has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands. They are not to be confused with the Office of Civil Groundskeeping as they do not hold authority over nor responsibility for Lilgap Wo-13y Bun's natural decorations nor waterways.

Lilgap Wo-13y Bun has a Guild of Roadworkers, who are responsible for maintaining the roadways and public paths within town. They also have the duty of enforcing all civil laws relating to the roadways.

Lilgap Wo-13y Bun has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Lilgap Wo-13y Bun has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Cultural Notes

Lilgap Wo-13y Bun has a long tradition of martial expertise. This may be a crisply-organized history of skilled native levies, or it may be a natural belligerence in the people that leaves them familiar with bloodshed. While their neighbors and liege doubtless respect their talents, this very aptitude might make them more willing to turn to steel than prudence would advise.

Lilgap Wo-13y Bun's town hall was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is characterized by order, symmetry, formal design, grandiosity, and elaborate ornamentation. Architectural characteristics include balustrades, balconies, columns, cornices, pilasters, and triangular pediments. Stone exteriors are massive and grandiose in their symmetry; interiors are typically polished and lavishly decorated with sculptures, swags, medallions, flowers, and shields. Interiors will often have a grand stairway and opulent ballroom..

In Lilgap Wo-13y Bun the utterance of expletives is impossible within city limits. This has changed as the limits have changed, but has not kept up with slang, or swearing in foreign languages.

The Amoeba Swarm near Lilgap Wo-13y Bun are known to be more aggressive than normal.

Lilgap Wo-13y Bun's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in winter and involves embarking on a group pilgrimage to channel Elven High Magic energies of tier 2 via recitation of scripture.
