Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka (/ˈfə̝rxo/ /ˈnɨbɪ/ [static] [tray]) is a subtropical Large Town located in the Qisunahu District of the Federation of Alveria.

The name Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka is derived from the Constructi language, as Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka was founded by Máldes Èdshë, who was culturaly Constructi.

Climate

Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka has a yearly average temperature of 30°C (86°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 34°C (93°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 26°C (78°F). Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka receives an average of 102 cm/y (40 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka covers an area of nearly 7 km2 (2 mi2), and an average elevation of 8868 m (-13855 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka was founded durring the early 16th century in fall of the year 1533, by Máldes Èdshë. The establishment of Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka suffered from many setbacks, delays, and obsticles, most notably a group of Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka which required millitary assistance exterminate before the community could finish being built.

Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka was built using the conventions of Constructi durring the early 16th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka is no diffrent. The town's buildings feature timber framed wooden shiethed or brick construction, which gives form to a very formalized, rational, expence effishent arcatectural style based on strictly symmetrical designs which universaly feature pitched roofs, shutters, and the occasional column or pilaster for a decorative touch.

Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka is buildings are grouped arround an odd layout of narrow paverstone streets, which seems to be based on an overlapping squair patern such that there are small squares at the cornor of every bigger square. Sometimes buildings exist in the smaller squaires, other times they are open spaces, or occupied by temporary structures. The town sits behind a stone-renforced palisade wall, with stone gatehouses and timber drawbridges for their trench. Unfortuantly, these would-be-castle fortifications are in extreem disrepair, so much so that one cannot tell if they are decaying from a lack of mantance or damage incured.

Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka is not quite well. Something happened here, perhapse recently, perhapse long ago. Whatever it was, it settled into the very soul of the town like a festering wound. The people go about their day well enough, but there’s a tention in the air you can cut with a knife.

Civic Infrastructure

Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka has an Office of Civil Groundskeeping, which works to enforce local ordinances relating to the construction and upkeep up of all plant life, water features, and other natural decorations within Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka. They are also responsible for the maintenance of these features. Notably, the OCG is not responsible for Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka's parks.

Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka has an Office of Civil Vicary, which is responsible for providing a livelyhood for all officialy recognised religious figures within Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka.

Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka has a Gravedigger's Guild, which is responsible for collecting the dead and laying them to rest according to all applicable laws and religious customs.

Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka has a Highwayman's Guild, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka possesses a Galvanic Power Grid, which brings galvanic current to most if not all buildings in town, and permits a great many consumer goods to function within the Large Town. Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka's grid is powered by a boiler and turbine based power plant.

Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands. They are not to be confused with the Office of Civil Groundskeeping as they do not hold authority over nor responsibility for Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka's natural decorations nor waterways.

Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka has a Guild of Roadworkers, who are responsible for maintaining the roadways and public paths within town. They also have the duty of enforcing all civil laws relating to the roadways.

Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Cultural Notes

Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka's mayor's house was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used which employed abundant symbolic geometry, using pure forms such as the circle and square, and plans are based on often symmetrical layouts featuring rectangular courtyards and halls. These structures were is decorated with carved stone or stucco reliefs and made use of colorful stone mosaics..

In Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka rainbows form quite often above the Large Town.

The Phase Spider near Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka are known to be almost tame, such that they can be put to domestic use.

Xl-77s Nìbǐ Birěkhka's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in winter and involves orgies to channel Mysticism energies of tier 3 via recitation of poetic epics.
