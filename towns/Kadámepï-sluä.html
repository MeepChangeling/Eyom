Kad ámepï-sluä (/ə˞ˈmepɪ/ /sluˈɑ/ [civilized] [hag (old woman)]) is a temperate Large City located in the Temryont Principality of the Soheque.

The name Kad ámepï-sluä is derived from the Goblin language, as Kad ámepï-sluä was founded by Yhzarth, who was culturaly Gnoll.

Climate

Kad ámepï-sluä has a yearly average temperature of 4°C (39°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a freezing 0°C (32°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 8°C (46°F). Kad ámepï-sluä receives an average of 205 cm/y (80 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Kad ámepï-sluä covers an area of nearly 102 km2 (40 mi2), and an average elevation of 1474 m (4835 ft) above sea level.
