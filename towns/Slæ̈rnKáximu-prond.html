Cultural Notes

Slæ̈rn Káximu-prond's mayor's house was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is known for its fluid and florid elaborate style, comprising ornate, asymmetric designs and pastel shades. It is often considered to be a playful, light style, which made exuberant use of curves and emphasized subtle asymmetry in the general shape of its structures. Walls, ceilings and moldings are decorated with numerous interlacing of curves and counter-curves based on the shapes of ‘C’ and ‘S’, along with shell forms and other naturalistic shapes.

In Slæ̈rn Káximu-prond tiny harmless tornadoes plague the town.

History

In time immemorial, reportedly some time during the early 2nd century, Slæ̈rn Káximu-prond was attacked by savage cyclopss living nearby. The details of the conflict are hazy at best due to many conflicting accounts. What is known is Slæ̈rn Káximu-prond lost 106 people, 318 livestock, and 54 buildings. The conflict ended after roughly 214, when members of Slæ̈rn Káximu-prond's militia enacted an operation to train a group of civilians for a special clandestine operation. The operation was complicated by major logistical problems. The conflict ended with the defense of the fortification against a siege, which ended in victory for Slæ̈rn Káximu-prond's forces. The war is remembered in legend by Slæ̈rn Káximu-prond's bards, historians, and legend keepers.
