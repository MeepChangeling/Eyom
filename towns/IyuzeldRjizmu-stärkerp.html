Iyuzeld Rjizmu-stärkerp (/rjizˈmu/ /stæ̝rˈkerp/ [naive] [navy]) is a temperate Town located in the Temryont Principality of the City-state of Soheque.

The name Iyuzeld Rjizmu-stärkerp is derived from the Gnollish language, as Iyuzeld Rjizmu-stärkerp was founded by Rawmpf̄̌sh Da̋ń̄r 'Amy Wavy' Ga̋ch Yóch Réóchīy vyim Rovuldirdirn, who was culturaly Gnollish.

Climate

Iyuzeld Rjizmu-stärkerp has a yearly average temperature of 4°C (39°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a cold 7°C (44°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 1°C (33°F). Iyuzeld Rjizmu-stärkerp receives an average of 214 cm/y (84 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Iyuzeld Rjizmu-stärkerp covers an area of nearly 4 km2 (1 mi2), and an average elevation of 2742 m (8996 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Iyuzeld Rjizmu-stärkerp was founded durring the early 9th century, by Rawmpf̄̌sh Da̋ń̄r 'Amy Wavy' Ga̋ch Yóch Réóchīy vyim Rovuldirdirn. The establishment of Iyuzeld Rjizmu-stärkerp suffered from several major issues, resulting in the need to develop many solutions to basic problems. Problems such as a lack of fresh water, logistical support, poor quality tools, and the odd monster or two. Howeaver, these were overcome in time.

Iyuzeld Rjizmu-stärkerp was built using the conventions of Gnollish durring the early 9th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Iyuzeld Rjizmu-stärkerp is no diffrent. The town's buildings feature masoned stone construction which prominantly features pointed arches, pointed ribbed vault cielings, flying buttress', and window tracery all of which share a simmilar gemoetetic patern halfway between organic and inorganic in design formaing a very distinct aesthetically integrated style. BUildings tend to reach for the havens, and more expencive homes are easily identified by their floor count as well as the addition of decorative features intigrated into the building's design such as statues, gargoyals, and embelished joinery.

Iyuzeld Rjizmu-stärkerp is buildings are arranged arround a network of broad cobblestone streets which form a diagonal shaped grid, where each square verries in size given the proximity of the paralell streets forming each section. The ocasional smaller square has been used to construct a park, plaza, and other communal structures. The town emploies a series of defencive earthworks, spikes, and fences to provide some protection against wild beasts and smaller groups of intelegent foes. Iyuzeld Rjizmu-stärkerp's minimaly adiquite has sufferd soem light damage, reducing its function a little in some spots, but could almsot certainly preform as expected... Though some of the worse spots could lead to the loss of defenders lives if attackers identified the weaknesses ahead of time.

A look around Iyuzeld Rjizmu-stärkerp gives you a feeling of distrust and paranoia. Also greed. The locals seem to be very much into scholastic pursuits, based on the hushed, murmured conversations you hear at least. Everyone in Iyuzeld Rjizmu-stärkerp acts like they have discovered something wondrous, and are worried they will endure some horrible fate so another can take what they’ve discovered for themselves.

Iyuzeld Rjizmu-stärkerp has a Highwayman's Guild, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Iyuzeld Rjizmu-stärkerp has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain Iyuzeld Rjizmu-stärkerp's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

Iyuzeld Rjizmu-stärkerp has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands.

Iyuzeld Rjizmu-stärkerp has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Cultural Notes

Life is hard in Iyuzeld Rjizmu-stärkerp. Its people are impoverished compared to their peers elsewhere. Something is making the locals stay, however, whether fear of the alternative, hope for a better future, or a stubborn attachment to their ancestral lands. Whatver the reason, living in this harsh area for generations has made the people of Iyuzeld Rjizmu-stärkerp a notably resourceful and hearty people.

Iyuzeld Rjizmu-stärkerp's town hall was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is characterized by their massive, monolithic and 'blocky' appearance with a rigid geometric style. It was best known for its rough, unfinished surfaces, unusual shapes, heavy-looking materials, straight lines, and small windows. Modular elements were often used to form masses representing specific functional zones, grouped into a unified whole.

History

In time immemorial, reportedly some time during the early 2nd century Mount Agony, an iconic vista located neare Iyuzeld Rjizmu-stärkerp, proved to be volcanic when the mountain erupted. The eruption was isolated to the area around Iyuzeld Rjizmu-stärkerp, which was swallowed in ash, lava flows, and pyroclastic gasses. Iyuzeld Rjizmu-stärkerp lost 282 people, 110 livestock, and 56 buildings in the disaster. The event is forever remembered as the Day of Agony's Wrath.
