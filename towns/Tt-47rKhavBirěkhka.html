Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka (/ˈqɛci/ /xav/ [manual] [bunker]) is a subtropical Town located in Skrōvnil Zone, Madraměvì District, within the Federation of Alveria.

The name Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka is derived from the Constructi language, as Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka was founded by Sm-52p 'Zoid' Fyeshèj, who was culturaly Constructi.

Climate

Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka has a yearly average temperature of 20°C (68°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 16°C (60°F). Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka receives an average of 264 cm/y (103 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka covers an area of nearly 6 km2 (2 mi2), and an average elevation of 2054 m (6738 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka was founded durring the late 10th century, by Sm-52p 'Zoid' Fyeshèj. The establishment of the new community went well, with no major obsticles durring construction.

Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka was built using the conventions of Constructi durring the late 10th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka is no diffrent. The town's buildings feature waddle and daub construction with good timber frames and a stone foundation protected by thatched or shingled rooves. Most buildings with second floors are built in such a way as to overhang into the streets on the upper floors for more space, as building size seems to be the primary indicator of wealth within the community. Most buildings are not decorated with any integral features, but instead use ivy, flowers, and other natural elements in planters of on trellices to breathe life into the structure they grow upon.

Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka is buildings are arranged arround a single restrictive flagstone mainstreet with many smaller streets branching off of it which gives the town a over all rectangular shape, albit one warped and twisted by the nature of the curves of the main road. The town is the proud owner of a properly designed set of renforced walls made from mighty querried stone blocks. Their construction and material choices would make a dwarf weap with joy, for each and every part of the elaborate fortifications are purly functional and robust well byond reason. Even nonexperts can tell the walls are an excelent defencive structure. Astonishigly, the exceptionaly well made fortifications are in pristine condishion, as if they had just been finished before you laied eyes upon them.

Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka has a very calm atmosphere. People can be seen relaxing, scocilizing, and going about all manner of business other than the daily grind. Men, women, children, all can be seen enjoying life in a laid-back way in the many parks which line Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka’s streets. In spite of this, quite a few people can be seen reading and the town has an abundance of libraries. It’s quite clear the community values education.

Civic Infrastructure

Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka has an Office of Civil Groundskeeping, which works to enforce local ordinances relating to the construction and upkeep up of all plant life, water features, and other natural decorations within Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka. They are also responsible for the maintenance of these features. Notably, the OCG is not responsible for Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka's parks.

Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka has an Office of Civil Vicary, which is responsible for providing a livelyhood for all officialy recognised religious figures within Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka.

Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka has a Highwayman's Guild, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka has a Hall of Slayers, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka possesses a Galvanic Power Grid, which brings galvanic current to most if not all buildings in town, and permits a great many consumer goods to function within the Town. Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka's grid is powered by an arcane means.

Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands. They are not to be confused with the Office of Civil Groundskeeping as they do not hold authority over nor responsibility for Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka's natural decorations nor waterways.

Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka's mayor's house was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used has a sleek, linear appearance with stylized, often geometric ornamentation. The primary facade of its buildings often featured a series of set backs that create a stepped outline. Low-relief decorative panels can be found at entrances, around windows, along roof edges or as string courses. It was best known for its use of smooth finish building materials such as stucco, concrete block, glazed brick or mosaic tile. Decorative details can incorporate various artistic or exotic motifs to suit the building's function or the architect's whim. Chevrons, zigzags, and other geometrical motifs are common forms of ornament.

Due to the actions of local Kami, summer is short in Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka.

The Weedwhip near Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka are known to be almost tame, such that they can be put to domestic use.

Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in autumn and involves drinking to channel Truename Magic energies of tier 1 via speaking in tongues.

Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka is uncommonly rich, not only for the gentry but for the common citizens as well. History

In time immemorial, reportedly some time during the early 2nd century the Sagê-píêtë Woodlands was struck by a drought when Mere Bellandur grew too small to continue feeding Backwater Emerald, which reduced the available water supply nationwide, but was especially harsh in the area arround Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka. As the neighboring regions had no water so spare, Tt-47r Khav Birěkhka lost 169 people, and 130 livestock in the disaster.. The drought lasted for 4 weeks, which are remembered as the Doom Thirst.
