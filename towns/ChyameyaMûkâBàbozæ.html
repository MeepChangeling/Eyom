Chyameya Mûkâ Bàbozæ (/ˈmʊkɑ/ /bəˈbozæ/ [silence] [cult]) is a subtropical Large City located in Keyâ U̹vû Kingdom, Vu̹chi Bu̽su Empire, within the Warren.

The name Chyameya Mûkâ Bàbozæ is derived from the Sylvin language, as Chyameya Mûkâ Bàbozæ was founded by Fyèqtco, who was culturaly Rattu.

Climate

Chyameya Mûkâ Bàbozæ has a yearly average temperature of 15°C (59°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 2°C (35°F). Chyameya Mûkâ Bàbozæ receives an average of 288 cm/y (113 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Chyameya Mûkâ Bàbozæ covers an area of nearly 59 km2 (23 mi2), and an average elevation of 3188 m (10459 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Chyameya Mûkâ Bàbozæ was founded durring the late 16th century in winter of the year 1557, by Fyèqtco. The establishment of Chyameya Mûkâ Bàbozæ was somewhat plagued by a lack of willing colonists, leading to Fyèqtco electing to pay people to resettle in Chyameya Mûkâ Bàbozæ.

Chyameya Mûkâ Bàbozæ was built using the conventions of Rattu durring the late 16th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Chyameya Mûkâ Bàbozæ is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature masoned stone construction which prominantly features pointed arches, pointed ribbed vault cielings, flying buttress', and window tracery all of which share a simmilar gemoetetic patern halfway between organic and inorganic in design formaing a very distinct aesthetically integrated style. BUildings tend to reach for the havens, and more expencive homes are easily identified by their floor count as well as the addition of decorative features intigrated into the building's design such as statues, gargoyals, and embelished joinery.

Chyameya Mûkâ Bàbozæ is buildings are arranged arround a network of crampt cobblestone streets which form a diamond shaped grid, where each diamond verries in size given the proximity of the paralell streets forming each section. The ocasional smaller diamond has been used to construct a park, plaza, and other communal structures. The city rests behind the absurdity that is a thick, timber braced, wall made of clay bricks. While visualy impressive and certainly an astetic, Chyameya Mûkâ Bàbozæ's wall provides no actual defence against siege equipment due to the choice of its cosntruction materials. Even nonexperts can tell the town is trying to impress rather than defend with its walls, towers, and gatehouses. Though admittedly, they do look nice... To primitive tribals who have never seen fortifications before. Chyameya Mûkâ Bàbozæ's political statment focused walls has sufferd soem light damage, reducing its function a little in some spots, but could almsot certainly preform as expected... Though some of the worse spots could lead to the loss of defenders lives if attackers identified the weaknesses ahead of time.

Before you’ve even set foot into the heart of Chyameya Mûkâ Bàbozæ, you can smell it. The incense. It hangs about the town like a cloud. Monks, priests, and clerics are everywhere, all dedicated to the same god, all preforming the same rituals to bless and anoint building,s streets, people, animals, you name it they are or have blessed it. The same holysymbols is everywhere too. Its on buildings, on people, and even branded into livestock. This city certainly loves its god.

Cultural Notes

Chyameya Mûkâ Bàbozæ's garrison was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is admittedly strange and non-linear style rooted in defiance of symmetrical shapes. It championed the creation of buildings with a unique visual appearance. the structural norms of classic buildings and deforms or moves away from elementary architectural principles. By including non-linear designs processed into its buildings and favoring fragmentation, this style expressed a form of controlled chaos. Its buildings appear out-of-the-ordinary, draw the eye in immediately and sometimes create a feeling of strangeness. These distorted shapes and structure are not reserved to the building’s outer facade, they destabilize interior elements too, favoring minimalism and play on people’s perceptions by injecting a futuristic touch.

In Chyameya Mûkâ Bàbozæ hail is always enormous, yet harmlessly plinks off people, creatures, and structures.

The Psyche Serpent near Chyameya Mûkâ Bàbozæ are known to be a mutant strain of the creature.

Chyameya Mûkâ Bàbozæ's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in summer and involves creating small tokens to channel Augury energies of tier 1 via singing.

History

In time immemorial, reportedly some time during the late 2nd century Chyameya Mûkâ Bàbozæ was rocked by a string of powerful winter storms. The worst of the disaster struck The storm coated everything in a thick layer of ice and freezing countless animals, plants, and people to death. Chyameya Mûkâ Bàbozæ lost 116 people, and 285 livestock in the disaster.. The disaster is remembered as the Mourning Screams, after the sounds of the winds.
