Qi̘v Bùshhèj̼àm (/ˈbʊʃhɛ/ /j̼əm/ [usher] [general]) is a subtropical Large City located in the Birton Community of the Tetbur Commune.

The name Qi̘v Bùshhèj̼àm is derived from the Tauric language, as Qi̘v Bùshhèj̼àm was founded by Ǵatsa Dyfodwg, who was culturaly Tauric.

Climate

Qi̘v Bùshhèj̼àm has a yearly average temperature of 30°C (86°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 33°C (91°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 27°C (80°F). Qi̘v Bùshhèj̼àm receives an average of 243 cm/y (95 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. Qi̘v Bùshhèj̼àm covers an area of nearly 451 km2 (180 mi2), and an average elevation of 3180 m (10433 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Qi̘v Bùshhèj̼àm was founded durring the early 11th century, by Ǵatsa Dyfodwg. The establishment of Qi̘v Bùshhèj̼àm was plagued by a lack of willing colonists. After attempts to pay people to resettle failed Ǵatsa Dyfodwg struck deals with nearby nations and communities to establish Qi̘v Bùshhèj̼àm as a prison colony.

Qi̘v Bùshhèj̼àm was built using the conventions of Tauric durring the early 11th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Qi̘v Bùshhèj̼àm is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature timber framed wooden shiethed or brick construction, which gives form to a very formalized, rational, expence effishent arcatectural style based on strictly symmetrical designs which universaly feature pitched roofs, shutters, and the occasional column or pilaster for a decorative touch.

Qi̘v Bùshhèj̼àm is buildings are arranged arround a network of premissive cobblestone streets which form a grid, where each square verries in size given the proximity of the paralell streets forming each section. The ocasional smaller square has been used to construct a park, plaza, and other communal structures. The mountain city is defended by arcane means. It's hard to spot at first, but there's a tell tell shimmer in the air arround Qi̘v Bùshhèj̼àm, and you can spot the ocasional warding glyph carved into a rock or tree all arround town. These mystical defences are ancient, unknowable, and unassailable by current means... Assuming everything is in working order. Otherwise, the wards are little more than a deathtrap. The Relic of the World That Was have not been wellmaintained over the years, and while functional are in dire need of some loving care and perhapse light renovation.

A look arround Qi̘v Bùshhèj̼àm reveals an abundance of schools, libraries, and other academic structures. Locals can be overheard having academic discussions, as well as talking about scholarly subjects in general. It’s quite clear the city places a lot of value on education and being a learned individual.

Cultural Notes

Qi̘v Bùshhèj̼àm's mayor's house was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used embraces individualism and experimentation. It emerged as a movement against traditional, classical styles and sought to make buildings dynamic and fun while breaking the rules. The style incorporated elements of previous architectural styles in exaggerated and whimsical ways. Traditional, conservative leanings were void in this era, with most scholars of architecture agreeing it was a time of “anything goes.”.

In Qi̘v Bùshhèj̼àm vermin leave small tokens in payment for food taken.

The Spriggan near Qi̘v Bùshhèj̼àm are known to be more aggressive than normal.

Qi̘v Bùshhèj̼àm's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in summer and involves sacrificing an animal to channel Wild Magic energies of tier 2 via guttural bellowing.
