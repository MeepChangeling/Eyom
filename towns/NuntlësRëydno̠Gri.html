Nuntlës Rëydno̠ Gri (/ˈnuntlɛs/ /ˈrɛjdno̠/ [wrong] [void]) is a subtropical Large City located in the Rúpoůj Aysh Moot of the Rosid.

The name Nuntlës Rëydno̠ Gri is derived from the Goblin language, as Nuntlës Rëydno̠ Gri was founded by Syhly, who was culturaly Wood Elf.

Climate

Nuntlës Rëydno̠ Gri has a yearly average temperature of 15°C (59°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 2°C (35°F). Nuntlës Rëydno̠ Gri receives an average of 153 cm/y (60 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Nuntlës Rëydno̠ Gri covers an area of nearly 159 km2 (63 mi2), and an average elevation of 2210 m (7250 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Nuntlës Rëydno̠ Gri was founded durring the early 14th century in fall of the year 1325, by Syhly. The establishment of Nuntlës Rëydno̠ Gri was somewhat plagued by a lack of willing colonists, leading to Syhly electing to pay people to resettle in Nuntlës Rëydno̠ Gri.

Nuntlës Rëydno̠ Gri was built using the conventions of Wood Elf durring the early 14th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Nuntlës Rëydno̠ Gri is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature waddle and daub construction with good timber frames and a stone foundation protected by thatched or shingled rooves. Most buildings with second floors are built in such a way as to overhang into the streets on the upper floors for more space, as building size seems to be the primary indicator of wealth within the community. Most buildings are not decorated with any integral features, but instead use ivy, flowers, and other natural elements in planters of on trellices to breathe life into the structure they grow upon.

Nuntlës Rëydno̠ Gri is buildings folow an organic layout of broad paverstone streets whihch gives the city a shape simmilar to a tree, if one views its streets from above. The city resides behind a palisade wall complete with battlments, a moat, and timber gatehouses with drawbridges. The city's robustly designed timber walls are visibly old, but also obviously well maintained. Its likly the local malishia or garrison are tasked with routine mantance of the city's defences.

Nuntlës Rëydno̠ Gri has the unmistakable air of a city on its last legs. Everything is a bit slipshod and ramshackle. Everyone is at work, or drinking. No one has anything in their eyes other than fear and despair. Whatever industry once fueled Nuntlës Rëydno̠ Gri ’s existence has dried up and the city is drifting down the stream of history as it dries up.

Cultural Notes

Nuntlës Rëydno̠ Gri is home to a distinct subculture, who are either the majority here or have the dominant positions in the community. Architecture, local laws, and social customs are all tuned to suit them, and they may not be particularly forgiving or friendly to the major culture of the region. Communities that are not outright independent usually make an arm's-length submission to a local lord.

Nuntlës Rëydno̠ Gri's bank was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is characterized by their massive, monolithic and 'blocky' appearance with a rigid geometric style. It was best known for its rough, unfinished surfaces, unusual shapes, heavy-looking materials, straight lines, and small windows. Modular elements were often used to form masses representing specific functional zones, grouped into a unified whole.

In Nuntlës Rëydno̠ Gri most nights are accompanied by colorful ribbons of light in the sky.

Nuntlës Rëydno̠ Gri's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in winter and involves sex to channel Wild Magic energies of tier 1 via mimery.
