Xl-92s Jĭitbi Birěkhka (/wɛn/ /ˈʤitbi/ [tall] [leaf; flake; foliage]) is a subtropical Town located in Huzezaji Zone, Vt-48nhǐl District, within the Federation of Alveria.

The name Xl-92s Jĭitbi Birěkhka is derived from the Constructi language, as Xl-92s Jĭitbi Birěkhka was founded by Ri-44z 'Gnut' Zlkdshe, who was culturaly Constructi.

Xl-92s Jĭitbi Birěkhka has a yearly average temperature of 11°C (51°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a cool 13°C (55°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 10°C (50°F). Xl-92s Jĭitbi Birěkhka receives an average of 219 cm/y (86 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Xl-92s Jĭitbi Birěkhka covers an area of nearly 4 km2 (1 mi2), and an average elevation of 1270 m (4166 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Xl-92s Jĭitbi Birěkhka was founded durring the early 9th century, by Ri-44z 'Gnut' Zlkdshe. The establishment of the new community went well, with no major obsticles durring construction.

Xl-92s Jĭitbi Birěkhka was built using the conventions of Constructi durring the early 9th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Xl-92s Jĭitbi Birěkhka is no diffrent. The town's buildings feature plaster covered brick construction taking the shape of large simple gemometric shapes which ate then embelished with decorative features, but retain their simple shapes in spite of the added features such as pillars, faccaddes, and covered walkways. The blocky nature gives a general impression of extreem age, as if each structure has existed since time immemorial.

Xl-92s Jĭitbi Birěkhka is is constructed arround a semi-circular broad cobblestone mainstreet, with smaller strait roads linking the circiles to eachother at varrious points. The town is the proud owner of a properly designed set of renforced walls made from mighty querried stone blocks. Their construction and material choices would make a dwarf weap with joy, for each and every part of the elaborate fortifications are purly functional and robust well byond reason. Even nonexperts can tell the walls are an excelent defencive structure. Xl-92s Jĭitbi Birěkhka's exceptionaly well made fortifications are suffering from significent damage, so much so that examples can be pointed to no matter which section one might have within their line of site, and most of which render sections inoperable at present.

Right off the bat Xl-92s Jĭitbi Birěkhka hits you in the face with its success. Everyone, even the peasants, are dressed in well made clothing. Every tool and implement you can see is finely made, and people will boast to you as obvious strangers of the wonders which can be found in their markets. More interestingly is a total lack of beggars, and plenty of new buildings are going up even as you speak. Somehow this town has come into quite a lot of wealth, and recently from the looks of things. This wealth has brought the town nothing but neurosis, if you can believe your eyes. Everyone acts as if you’re out to take a piece of them. Every door is locked and every window barred. Efforts are made to make you feel welcome, but only in the public areas of the town, particularly the market.

Cultural Notes

Xl-92s Jĭitbi Birěkhka's chapel was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is best known for the combined use of arcuated and trabeated construction, employing arches and constructed with post and lintel. The arch served as the style's chief structural element, with flanking columns serving as buttresses or decorations. While the general shape of their structures could be generously described as simplistic, the use of these arches and columns created a most distinct ascetic which is easily distinguishable on sight to armatures and experts alike.

Due to the actions of local Kami, summer is short in Xl-92s Jĭitbi Birěkhka.

The Voonith near Xl-92s Jĭitbi Birěkhka are known to be a mutant strain of the creature.

Xl-92s Jĭitbi Birěkhka's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in spring and involves consuming a local narcotic to channel Invocation energies of tier 2 via chanting.

History

Xl-92s Jĭitbi Birěkhka used to be more prosperous, but something happened relatively long ago that left it a shrunken shadow of its former self. If the settlement is prosperous, the locals often lament how much more they could have had. If the settlement is not prosperous, the locals blame their ill fortunes on that event. Reminders of this better time can be found in many places within Xl-92s Jĭitbi Birěkhka.

In time immemorial, reportedly some time during the late 2nd century, Xl-92s Jĭitbi Birěkhka was attacked by soldiers from another nation, waging a greater campaign. The details of the conflict are hazy at best due to many conflicting accounts. What is known is Xl-92s Jĭitbi Birěkhka lost 196 people, 397 livestock, and 89 buildings. The conflict ended after roughly 97, when members of Xl-92s Jĭitbi Birěkhka's militia enacted an operation to capture a particular enemy resource intact. The operation was complicated by aggravated civilians, who cause problems for the militia for a host of reasons. The conflict ended with pitched battle between both forces, which ended in defeat for Xl-92s Jĭitbi Birěkhka's forces. The war is remembered in legend by Xl-92s Jĭitbi Birěkhka's bards, historians, and legend keepers.
