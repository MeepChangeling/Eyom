I̽ Vdro̠ Med (/i̽/ /vdro̠/ [white] [log; stick]) is a subtropical Large City located in the Bek'thond Earldom of the Viceroyalty of Rosid.

The name I̽ Vdro̠ Med is derived from the Wood Elvish language, as I̽ Vdro̠ Med was founded by Mp̪f̄shv Mē̌shvoī 'Harley Iris' Réó̄̄̄y Rér Vú̄ń̄s Yaezulror, who was culturaly Wood Elven.

Climate

I̽ Vdro̠ Med has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 9°C (48°F). I̽ Vdro̠ Med receives an average of 218 cm/y (85 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. I̽ Vdro̠ Med covers an area of nearly 507 km2 (202 mi2), and an average elevation of 1142 m (3746 ft) above sea level.

Overview

I̽ Vdro̠ Med was founded durring the late 12th century in fall of the year 1086, by Mp̪f̄shv Mē̌shvoī 'Harley Iris' Réó̄̄̄y Rér Vú̄ń̄s Yaezulror. The establishment of the new community went well, though many minor issues had to be solved as time went on. This was enough to delay construction and push back the formal opening ceramony, leading to some embarisment for Mp̪f̄shv Mē̌shvoī 'Harley Iris' Réó̄̄̄y Rér Vú̄ń̄s Yaezulror.

I̽ Vdro̠ Med was built using the conventions of Wood Elven durring the late 12th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and I̽ Vdro̠ Med is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature waddle and daub construction with good timber frames and a stone foundation protected by thatched or shingled rooves. Most buildings with second floors are built in such a way as to overhang into the streets on the upper floors for more space, as building size seems to be the primary indicator of wealth within the community. Most buildings are not decorated with any integral features, but instead use ivy, flowers, and other natural elements in planters of on trellices to breathe life into the structure they grow upon.

I̽ Vdro̠ Med is buildings have been located at convienant points along the valley I̽ Vdro̠ Med was built upon. Navigating the town is therefore a little chalanging as the distance between buildings verris greatly and the spacious split-log ties streets flow where they are able to be made rather than folowing the most convienant paths. The valley city rests behind the absurdity that is a thick, timber braced, wall made of clay bricks. While visualy impressive and certainly an astetic, I̽ Vdro̠ Med's wall provides no actual defence against siege equipment due to the choice of its cosntruction materials. Even nonexperts can tell the town is trying to impress rather than defend with its walls, towers, and gatehouses. Though admittedly, they do look nice... To primitive tribals who have never seen fortifications before. I̽ Vdro̠ Med's political statment focused walls has sufferd soem light damage, reducing its function a little in some spots, but could almsot certainly preform as expected... Though some of the worse spots could lead to the loss of defenders lives if attackers identified the weaknesses ahead of time.

A look around I̽ Vdro̠ Med makes you worry someone might stab you in a dark ally for your boots. It’s not filthy, or dark, but the smiles seem strained, the locals seem to glare daggers in eachothers backs a little too much, and everyone is armed at all times. You may want to keep an eye on your valuables, and make sure you don’t wind up in any position of power.

I̽ Vdro̠ Med has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

I̽ Vdro̠ Med is home to a University which provides higher education in a variety of fields, and also serves as a research institute for those same fields.

Cultural Notes

I̽ Vdro̠ Med's bank was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is inspired by the natural world, characterized by sinuous, sculptural, organic shapes, arches, curving lines, and sensual ornamentation. Common motifs included stylized versions of leaves, flowers, vines, insects, animals, and other natural elements. Decorative elements found on the inside and outside of buildings include intricate mosaic work, curved windows, and decorative trim work. .

In I̽ Vdro̠ Med there is a constant smell of overcooked presumably ethnic food.

The Skinwraith near I̽ Vdro̠ Med are known to be quite timid.

I̽ Vdro̠ Med's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in autumn and involves creating small tokens to channel Invocation energies of tier 1 via moments of science.
