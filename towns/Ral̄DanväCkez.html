Ral̄ Danvä Ckez (/rall/ /danˈvə/ [off] [alibi]) is a subtropical Large Town located in Bus̺u Bu̽yo County, Gandlacckÿrf Kingdom, within the United Kingdom of Undermountain.

The name Ral̄ Danvä Ckez is derived from the Dwarven language, as Ral̄ Danvä Ckez was founded by Mathli Hub̄qug, who was culturaly Dwarvern.

Climate

Ral̄ Danvä Ckez has a yearly average temperature of 27°C (80°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 30°C (86°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 24°C (75°F). Ral̄ Danvä Ckez receives an average of 133 cm/y (52 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. Ral̄ Danvä Ckez covers an area of nearly 6 km2 (2 mi2), and an average elevation of 4274 m (14022 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Ral̄ Danvä Ckez was founded durring the early 13th century in winter of the year 1223, by Mathli Hub̄qug. The establishment of the new community went well, with no major obsticles durring construction.

Ral̄ Danvä Ckez was built using the conventions of Dwarvern durring the early 13th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Ral̄ Danvä Ckez is no diffrent. The town's buildings feature timber framed wooden shiethed or brick construction, which gives form to a very formalized, rational, expence effishent arcatectural style based on strictly symmetrical designs which universaly feature pitched roofs, shutters, and the occasional column or pilaster for a decorative touch.

Ral̄ Danvä Ckez is buildings are arranged arround a network of spacious flagstone streets which form a grid, where each square verries in size given the proximity of the paralell streets forming each section. The ocasional smaller square has been used to construct a park, plaza, and other communal structures. The town sits behind a stone-renforced palisade wall, with stone gatehouses and timber drawbridges for their trench. The would-be-castle fortifications has suffered a visible ammount of structural damage, leaving them effectivly useless. One can't help but wonder why the has not yet effected repairs.

A look around Ral̄ Danvä Ckez makes you worry someone might stab you in a dark ally for your boots. It’s not filthy, or dark, but the smiles seem strained, the locals seem to glare daggers in eachothers backs a little too much, and everyone is armed at all times. You may want to keep an eye on your valuables, and make sure you don’t wind up in any position of power.

Cultural Notes

Ral̄ Danvä Ckez's chapel was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used embraces individualism and experimentation. It emerged as a movement against traditional, classical styles and sought to make buildings dynamic and fun while breaking the rules. The style incorporated elements of previous architectural styles in exaggerated and whimsical ways. Traditional, conservative leanings were void in this era, with most scholars of architecture agreeing it was a time of “anything goes.”.
