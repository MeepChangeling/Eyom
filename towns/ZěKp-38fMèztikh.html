Zě Kp-38f Mèztikh (/inver/ /ˈmɤztix/ [Propername] [total; sum; score]) is a subtropical Large City located in Zyìdǐn Zone, Lx-173v Province, within the Federation of Alveria.

The name Zě Kp-38f Mèztikh is derived from the Constructi language, as Zě Kp-38f Mèztikh was founded by Trëmê Fyêyèj, who was culturaly Constructi.

Climate

Zě Kp-38f Mèztikh has a yearly average temperature of 34°C (93°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 39°C (102°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 30°C (86°F). Zě Kp-38f Mèztikh receives an average of 251 cm/y (98 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. Zě Kp-38f Mèztikh covers an area of nearly 264 km2 (105 mi2), and an average elevation of 19652 m (-21424 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Zě Kp-38f Mèztikh was founded durring the late 12th century in winter of the year 1098, by Trëmê Fyêyèj. The establishment of Zě Kp-38f Mèztikh suffered from many setbacks, delays, and obsticles, most notably a group of Zě Kp-38f Mèztikh which required millitary assistance exterminate before the community could finish being built.

Zě Kp-38f Mèztikh was built using the conventions of Constructi durring the late 12th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Zě Kp-38f Mèztikh is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature waddle and daub construction with good timber frames and a stone foundation protected by thatched or shingled rooves. Most buildings with second floors are built in such a way as to overhang into the streets on the upper floors for more space, as building size seems to be the primary indicator of wealth within the community. Most buildings are not decorated with any integral features, but instead use ivy, flowers, and other natural elements in planters of on trellices to breathe life into the structure they grow upon.

Zě Kp-38f Mèztikh is is constructed arround a semi-circular crampt packed earth mainstreet, with smaller strait roads linking the circiles to eachother at varrious points. The valley city rests behind a set of well fortified walls, with gatehouses, watch towers, and battlments. Zě Kp-38f Mèztikh's walls are, howeaver, fashioned from stone and timber. While unorthadox, the design looks to be functional to a reasonable degree. With luck, the untested design will remain untested for years to come. The unusual yet seemingly effective fortifications has suffered a visible ammount of structural damage, leaving them effectivly useless. One can't help but wonder why the has not yet effected repairs.

The first thing you notice about Zě Kp-38f Mèztikh is the large sign at the entrance welcoming you to their humble city. Children come up to you and say hi, some asking if you’ve fought any big monsters, and others just zipping away after their hello. The adults are much the same. By the time you’ve made it a significant way into the town you’ve been invited to a tavern for a pint to drink in exchange for news of the world. You’ve also had no less than three older women give you a sweetroll and direct your attention to their still unmarried children. It would be creepy if it wasn't so sincere.

Cultural Notes

Zě Kp-38f Mèztikh has a long tradition of martial expertise. This may be a crisply-organized history of skilled native levies, or it may be a natural belligerence in the people that leaves them familiar with bloodshed. While their neighbors and liege doubtless respect their talents, this very aptitude might make them more willing to turn to steel than prudence would advise.

Zě Kp-38f Mèztikh's chapel was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is best known for the combined use of arcuated and trabeated construction, employing arches and constructed with post and lintel. The arch served as the style's chief structural element, with flanking columns serving as buttresses or decorations. While the general shape of their structures could be generously described as simplistic, the use of these arches and columns created a most distinct ascetic which is easily distinguishable on sight to armatures and experts alike.

Due to the actions of local Kami, autumn is long in Zě Kp-38f Mèztikh.

The Amoeba, Giant near Zě Kp-38f Mèztikh are known to be quite timid.

Zě Kp-38f Mèztikh's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in spring and involves line dance to channel Abjuration energies of tier 1 via throat chanting.
