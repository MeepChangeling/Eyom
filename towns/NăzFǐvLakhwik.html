Năz Fǐv Lakhwik (/fɪv/ /ˈlaxwic/ [tropical] [flour]) is a temperate Large City located in Zircěnuw Zone, Ot-61esìb District, within the Alveria.

The name Năz Fǐv Lakhwik is derived from the Sylvin language, as Năz Fǐv Lakhwik was founded by Ýåhie, who was culturaly KLEO.

Climate

Năz Fǐv Lakhwik has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 30°C (86°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 20°C (68°F). Năz Fǐv Lakhwik receives an average of 226 cm/y (88 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. Năz Fǐv Lakhwik covers an area of nearly 115 km2 (46 mi2), and an average elevation of 6472 m (21233 ft) above sea level.

Năz Fǐv Lakhwik possesses a Galvanic Power Grid, which brings galvanic current to most if not all buildings in town, and permits a great many consumer goods to function within the Large City. Năz Fǐv Lakhwik's grid is powered by hydrogalvanic generators.

Năz Fǐv Lakhwik's old civil lighting system was converted to Galvanic Lamps recently, and expanded to provide nighttime illumination to all city streets.

While not open to the public, the librarians and scholars employed by the library will assist anyone with their research needs, and wealthy individuals can purchase membership to access the library's materials themselves. Năz Fǐv Lakhwik has a Guild of Roadworkers, who are responsible for maintaining the roadways and public paths within town. They also have the duty of enforcing all civil laws relating to the roadways.

Năz Fǐv Lakhwik has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Năz Fǐv Lakhwik has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Cultural Notes

Năz Fǐv Lakhwik's locals despise outsiders. For some “outsiders” may be natives of foreign lands, while others might have a grudge against anyone from outside the community. These locals have an active loathing, and the outsiders allowed to trade or interact with them do so at a heavy disadvantage.

Năz Fǐv Lakhwik's garrison was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is known for its fluid and florid elaborate style, comprising ornate, asymmetric designs and pastel shades. It is often considered to be a playful, light style, which made exuberant use of curves and emphasized subtle asymmetry in the general shape of its structures. Walls, ceilings and moldings are decorated with numerous interlacing of curves and counter-curves based on the shapes of ‘C’ and ‘S’, along with shell forms and other naturalistic shapes.

Due to the actions of local Kami, summer is long in Năz Fǐv Lakhwik.

The Horsefly, Giant near Năz Fǐv Lakhwik are known to be quite timid.

Năz Fǐv Lakhwik's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in autumn and involves creating small tokens to channel Chronomancy energies of tier 3 via oratory performances.
