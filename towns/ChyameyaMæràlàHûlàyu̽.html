Chyameya Mæràlà Hûlàyu̽ (/mæˈrələ/ /hʊˈləju̽/ [reasonable] [moisture]) is a subtropical Large City located in the Glikérád Principality of the Saborri.

The name Chyameya Mæràlà Hûlàyu̽ is derived from the Sylvin language, as Chyameya Mæràlà Hûlàyu̽ was founded by Glkêbêbm, who was culturaly Rattu.

Climate

Chyameya Mæràlà Hûlàyu̽ has a yearly average temperature of 4°C (39°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold -15°C (5°F). Chyameya Mæràlà Hûlàyu̽ receives an average of 139 cm/y (54 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Chyameya Mæràlà Hûlàyu̽ covers an area of nearly 107 km2 (42 mi2), and an average elevation of 4740 m (15551 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Chyameya Mæràlà Hûlàyu̽ was founded durring the early 17th century in late summer of the year 1613, by Glkêbêbm. The establishment of Chyameya Mæràlà Hûlàyu̽ suffered from several major issues, resulting in the need to develop many solutions to basic problems. Problems such as a lack of fresh water, logistical support, poor quality tools, and the odd monster or two. Howeaver, these were overcome in time.

Chyameya Mæràlà Hûlàyu̽ was built using the conventions of Rattu durring the early 17th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Chyameya Mæràlà Hûlàyu̽ is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature masoned stone construction which prominantly features pointed arches, pointed ribbed vault cielings, flying buttress', and window tracery all of which share a simmilar gemoetetic patern halfway between organic and inorganic in design formaing a very distinct aesthetically integrated style. BUildings tend to reach for the havens, and more expencive homes are easily identified by their floor count as well as the addition of decorative features intigrated into the building's design such as statues, gargoyals, and embelished joinery.

Chyameya Mæràlà Hûlàyu̽ is buildings are arranged arround a single crampt flagstone mainstreet with many smaller streets branching off of it which gives the city a over all rectangular shape, albit one warped and twisted by the nature of the curves of the main road. The city has a set of well fortified walls, with gatehouses, watch towers, battlments, and even a moat, which are fashioned from stone and timber. Chyameya Mæràlà Hûlàyu̽'s walls are, howeaver, fashioned from stone and timber. While unorthadox, the design looks to be functional to a reasonable degree. With luck, the untested design will remain untested for years to come. The unusual though effective defenses have not been wellmaintained over the years, and while functional are in dire need of some loving care and perhapse light renovation.

A look around Chyameya Mæràlà Hûlàyu̽ shows Chyameya Mæràlà Hûlàyu̽ as a den of corruption. Birbes can be seen changing hands openly, such that it must be customary to do so and must have been for a long time. The locals have no fear, no annoyance at the state of things, it simply is. The city has another layer to it as well. Locals can be overheard having academic discussions, as well as talking about scholarly subjects in general. It’s quite clear Chyameya Mæràlà Hûlàyu̽ places a lot of value on education and being a learned individual, or at least, faking it.

Cultural Notes

Chyameya Mæràlà Hûlàyu̽'s town hall was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used embraces individualism and experimentation. It emerged as a movement against traditional, classical styles and sought to make buildings dynamic and fun while breaking the rules. The style incorporated elements of previous architectural styles in exaggerated and whimsical ways. Traditional, conservative leanings were void in this era, with most scholars of architecture agreeing it was a time of “anything goes.”.

In Chyameya Mæràlà Hûlàyu̽ most nights are accompanied by colorful ribbons of light in the sky.

The Domovoi near Chyameya Mæràlà Hûlàyu̽ are known to be more aggressive than normal.

Chyameya Mæràlà Hûlàyu̽'s citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in summer and involves orgies to channel Conjuration energies of tier 2 via throat chanting.

Points of Interest

Chyameya Mæràlà Hûlàyu̽ is remarkably well-fortified for a site of its size and role. Tall, stout walls, strong points inside the community, concentric defenses, a strategic terrain location, or a large body of standing troops might be present. Some threat is thought to exist that makes maintaining this fortification worthwhile, though it may come at a dear cost to the locals. The community’s suzerain may be uncomfortable with these defenses, as they could just as easily be used to defy the ruler.

Chyameya Mæràlà Hûlàyu̽'s is something of a geological and arcane anomaly, as neither physical nor magical law entirely explains its formation.
