Qÿhtvehb Räct Cessh Nägbehqa (/rɪ̘v/ /cis̼ nəgbɪ̘ˈqa/ [safe] [underwear]) is a subtropical Large City located in Phägäbn Hold, Yuttul̄ Kingdom, within the Undermountain.

The name Qÿhtvehb Räct Cessh Nägbehqa is derived from the Sylvin language, as Qÿhtvehb Räct Cessh Nägbehqa was founded by Dyênmtco, who was culturaly Undermountain.

Climate

Qÿhtvehb Räct Cessh Nägbehqa has a yearly average temperature of 28°C (82°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 32°C (89°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 24°C (75°F). Qÿhtvehb Räct Cessh Nägbehqa receives an average of 272 cm/y (107 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. Qÿhtvehb Räct Cessh Nägbehqa covers an area of nearly 78 km2 (31 mi2), and an average elevation of 4258 m (13969 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Qÿhtvehb Räct Cessh Nägbehqa was founded durring the early 13th century in fall of the year 1203, by Dyênmtco. The establishment of the new community went well, though many minor issues had to be solved as time went on. This was enough to delay construction and push back the formal opening ceramony, leading to some embarisment for Dyênmtco.

Qÿhtvehb Räct Cessh Nägbehqa was built using the conventions of Undermountain durring the early 13th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Qÿhtvehb Räct Cessh Nägbehqa is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature masoned stone construction which prominantly features pointed arches, pointed ribbed vault cielings, flying buttress', and window tracery all of which share a simmilar gemoetetic patern halfway between organic and inorganic in design formaing a very distinct aesthetically integrated style. BUildings tend to reach for the havens, and more expencive homes are easily identified by their floor count as well as the addition of decorative features intigrated into the building's design such as statues, gargoyals, and embelished joinery.

Qÿhtvehb Räct Cessh Nägbehqa is buildings have been located at convienant points along the mountain Qÿhtvehb Räct Cessh Nägbehqa was built upon. Navigating the town is therefore a little chalanging as the distance between buildings verris greatly and the spacious packed earth streets flow where they are able to be made rather than folowing the most convienant paths. The city resides behind a palisade wall complete with battlments, a moat, and timber gatehouses with drawbridges. The robustly designed timber walls have not been wellmaintained over the years, and while functional are in dire need of some loving care and perhapse light renovation.

A look around Qÿhtvehb Räct Cessh Nägbehqa gives the unmistakable impression the town is one of the strictest places imaginable. Everyone’s actions are clearly directed by laws they keep in heart and mind at all times. Orderly byond order is a phrase which Qÿhtvehb Räct Cessh Nägbehqa brings to mind.

Cultural Notes

Qÿhtvehb Räct Cessh Nägbehqa's town hall was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is known for its fluid and florid elaborate style, comprising ornate, asymmetric designs and pastel shades. It is often considered to be a playful, light style, which made exuberant use of curves and emphasized subtle asymmetry in the general shape of its structures. Walls, ceilings and moldings are decorated with numerous interlacing of curves and counter-curves based on the shapes of ‘C’ and ‘S’, along with shell forms and other naturalistic shapes.

In Qÿhtvehb Räct Cessh Nägbehqa all of the cats speak in a dead language.

The Twigjack near Qÿhtvehb Räct Cessh Nägbehqa are known to be a mutant strain of the creature.

Qÿhtvehb Räct Cessh Nägbehqa's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in summer and involves drinking to channel Chronomancy energies of tier 2 via divine sermons.
