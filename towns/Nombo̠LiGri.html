Nombo̠ Li Gri (/ˈnombo̠/ /li/ [messy] [valley]) is a subtropical City located in Vdrijenjetusu Zone, Asu Pamo Nopozopa District, within the Alveria.

The name Nombo̠ Li Gri is derived from the Sylvin language, as Nombo̠ Li Gri was founded by Gucun, who was culturaly Wood Elf.

Climate

Nombo̠ Li Gri has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 20°C (68°F). Nombo̠ Li Gri receives an average of 74 cm/y (29 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. Nombo̠ Li Gri covers an area of nearly 37 km2 (14 mi2), and an average elevation of 3722 m (12211 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Nombo̠ Li Gri was founded durring the early 13th century in fall of the year 1201, by Gucun. The establishment of Nombo̠ Li Gri was only bairly constructed. The sheer number of problems with its founding were enough to make several of the backers funding Nombo̠ Li Gri's construction back out of the project. Gucun pushed on reguardles, and Nombo̠ Li Gri was finished, but starts off as a terible place to live.

Nombo̠ Li Gri was built using the conventions of Wood Elf durring the early 13th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Nombo̠ Li Gri is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature waddle and daub construction with good timber frames and a stone foundation protected by thatched or shingled rooves. Most buildings with second floors are built in such a way as to overhang into the streets on the upper floors for more space, as building size seems to be the primary indicator of wealth within the community. Most buildings are not decorated with any integral features, but instead use ivy, flowers, and other natural elements in planters of on trellices to breathe life into the structure they grow upon.

Nombo̠ Li Gri is buildings are built arround a single narrow cobblestone mainstreet which forms a counterclockwise spiral to give the city a over all circular shape. The city sits behind a stone-renforced palisade wall, with stone gatehouses and timber drawbridges for their trench. Unfortuantly, these would-be-castle fortifications are in extreem disrepair, so much so that one cannot tell if they are decaying from a lack of mantance or damage incured.

A look around Nombo̠ Li Gri makes you wonder how anything ever gets done. The locals have clear, obvious rivalries with one another, as seen through clenched teeth and fake smiles. What’s more, there is a serious lack of any organization and planning in Nombo̠ Li Gri’s layout as well as the local’s behaviors. The city seems to be a den of chaos where tongues are forked and local customs do not exist.

Nombo̠ Li Gri has a Department of Firefighters, which is responsible for organizing fire fighting efforts during a fire and enforcing local ordinances relating to fire safety.

Nombo̠ Li Gri has a Highwayman's Guild, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Nombo̠ Li Gri has a Hall of Slayers, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Nombo̠ Li Gri has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain Nombo̠ Li Gri's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

Nombo̠ Li Gri has an Theological Academy which trains clergy in various arcane and theological topics required for their occupations.

Nombo̠ Li Gri possesses a Galvanic Power Grid, which brings galvanic current to most if not all buildings in town, and permits a great many consumer goods to function within the City. Nombo̠ Li Gri's grid is powered by a god's will and kindness.

Nombo̠ Li Gri's old civil lighting system was converted to Galvanic Lamps recently, and expanded to provide nighttime illumination to all city streets.

Nombo̠ Li Gri has a library, which keeps a large collection of books, scrolls, and archives all manner of physical items. While not open to the public, the librarians and scholars employed by the library will assist anyone with their research needs, and wealthy individuals can purchase membership to access the library's materials themselves. In spite of being generally closed to the public, the library has a room with several Aether Linked devices available to the public during business hours.

Nombo̠ Li Gri has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands. They are not to be confused with the Office of Civil Groundskeeping as they do not hold authority over nor responsibility for Nombo̠ Li Gri's natural decorations nor waterways.

Nombo̠ Li Gri has a Guild of Roadworkers, who are responsible for maintaining the roadways and public paths within town. They also have the duty of enforcing all civil laws relating to the roadways.

Nombo̠ Li Gri has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Nombo̠ Li Gri has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Cultural Notes

A substantial minority of the locals are descended from foreigners alien to their local neighbors. They may have been religious exiles, economic migrants, indigenous locals surrounded by the existing polity, or a foreign settlement conquered within the relatively recent past. The locals may not be enthusiastic about being ruled by others not of their kind, and their neighbors may look askance at the way foreign customs or even laws may be maintained.

Nombo̠ Li Gri's bank was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is based upon new and innovative technologies of construction and the idea that form should follow function. It was an embrace of minimalism and a rejection of ornament. The style became characterized by an emphasis on volume, asymmetrical compositions, and minimal ornamentation..

In Nombo̠ Li Gri sometimes, near one of the seasonal solstices, the sun appears to split into three and it rains from a clear sky for several hours.

The Griffon near Nombo̠ Li Gri are known to be more aggressive than normal.

Nombo̠ Li Gri's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in spring and involves consuming a local toxin to channel Elven High Magic energies of tier 1 via guttural bellowing.

History

In time immemorial, reportedly some time during the late 2nd century, Nombo̠ Li Gri was attacked by soldiers from another nation, waging a greater campaign. The details of the conflict are hazy at best due to many conflicting accounts. What is known is Nombo̠ Li Gri lost 100 people, 291 livestock, and 51 buildings. The conflict ended after roughly 61, when members of Nombo̠ Li Gri's militia enacted an operation to escort a specific expert to a safe location. The operation was complicated by extremely dangerous terrain, including unfamiliar and poisonous plants which many ate while foraging. The conflict ended with pitched battle between both forces, which ended in victory for Nombo̠ Li Gri's forces. The war is remembered in legend by Nombo̠ Li Gri's bards, historians, and legend keepers.
