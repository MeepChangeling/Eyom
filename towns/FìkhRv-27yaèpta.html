Fìkh Rv-27y aèpta (/ˈhæi/ /aˈɤpta/ [friendly] [germ; bacteria]) is a subtropical Town located in the Pinizeva District of the Federation of Alveria.

The name Fìkh Rv-27y aèpta is derived from the Constructi language, as Fìkh Rv-27y aèpta was founded by Shêj Trêtëm, who was culturaly Constructi.

Climate

Fìkh Rv-27y aèpta has a yearly average temperature of 31°C (87°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 35°C (95°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 27°C (80°F). Fìkh Rv-27y aèpta receives an average of 220 cm/y (86 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. Fìkh Rv-27y aèpta covers an area of nearly 5 km2 (2 mi2), and an average elevation of 2514 m (8248 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Fìkh Rv-27y aèpta was founded durring the early 13th century in summer of the year 1218, by Shêj Trêtëm. The establishment of Fìkh Rv-27y aèpta suffered from many setbacks, delays, and obsticles, most notably a group of Fìkh Rv-27y aèpta which required millitary assistance exterminate before the community could finish being built.

Fìkh Rv-27y aèpta was built using the conventions of Constructi durring the early 13th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Fìkh Rv-27y aèpta is no diffrent. The town's buildings feature timber framed wooden shiethed or brick construction, which gives form to a very formalized, rational, expence effishent arcatectural style based on strictly symmetrical designs which universaly feature pitched roofs, shutters, and the occasional column or pilaster for a decorative touch.

Fìkh Rv-27y aèpta is buildings are built arround a single narrow baked earthen mainstreet which forms a counterclockwise spiral to give the town a over all circular shape. The town is the proud owner of a thick set of fortified walls fashioned from querried stone blocks. While not up to snuff for a fort or castle wall, the town's walls are naturaly much larger than those of forts or castles. Therefore, the construction such a wall is most expencive. Fìkh Rv-27y aèpta's buget focused wall would serve its community well in battle in spite of looking unimpressive compared to castles and fortresses. Fìkh Rv-27y aèpta's top tier civilian fortifications are suffering from significent damage, so much so that examples can be pointed to no matter which section one might have within their line of site, and most of which render sections inoperable at present.

A look around Fìkh Rv-27y aèpta seems to be home to a quite vibrant and boisterous community. Everywhere one looks they can see people going out their daily business with a smile and a spring in their step. Children play loudly in the streets, causing untold havoc as youth are want and allowed to do. On second glance, that chaos continues into adulthood. People seem to be allowed to do as they please with little harmoney to anything. It feels less like a town, and more like a spot people just happened to place their homes. Yet there are small elements here and there which show the underlying structure of the community. It’s just so complex, organic, and flowing one can only understand what is a piece of the puzzle, but not what its neighbors are.

Cultural Notes

Fìkh Rv-27y aèpta's bank was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is characterized by order, symmetry, formal design, grandiosity, and elaborate ornamentation. Architectural characteristics include balustrades, balconies, columns, cornices, pilasters, and triangular pediments. Stone exteriors are massive and grandiose in their symmetry; interiors are typically polished and lavishly decorated with sculptures, swags, medallions, flowers, and shields. Interiors will often have a grand stairway and opulent ballroom..

In Fìkh Rv-27y aèpta hail is always enormous, yet harmlessly plinks off people, creatures, and structures.

The Trollkin near Fìkh Rv-27y aèpta are known to be a mutant strain of the creature.

Fìkh Rv-27y aèpta's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in spring and involves consuming a local narcotic to channel Charm energies of tier 2 via recitation of poetic epics.
