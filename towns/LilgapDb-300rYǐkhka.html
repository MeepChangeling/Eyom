Lilgap Db-300r Yǐkhka (/qiv/ /ˈjɪxka/ [poor] [mountain; cliff]) is a temperate Large City located in Khìftsfep Zone, Rubivælǐ District, within the Alveria.

The name Lilgap Db-300r Yǐkhka is derived from the Goblin language, as Lilgap Db-300r Yǐkhka was founded by Ts-70g 'Marvin', who was culturaly KLEO.

Climate

Lilgap Db-300r Yǐkhka has a yearly average temperature of 29°C (84°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 32°C (89°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 26°C (78°F). Lilgap Db-300r Yǐkhka receives an average of 281 cm/y (110 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. Lilgap Db-300r Yǐkhka covers an area of nearly 227 km2 (90 mi2), and an average elevation of 4016 m (13175 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Lilgap Db-300r Yǐkhka was founded durring the late 14th century in winter of the year 1282, by Ts-70g 'Marvin'. The establishment of Lilgap Db-300r Yǐkhka suffered from many setbacks, delays, and obsticles, most notably a group of Lilgap Db-300r Yǐkhka which required millitary assistance exterminate before the community could finish being built.

Lilgap Db-300r Yǐkhka was built using the conventions of KLEO durring the late 14th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Lilgap Db-300r Yǐkhka is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature waddle and daub construction with good timber frames and a stone foundation protected by thatched or shingled rooves. Most buildings with second floors are built in such a way as to overhang into the streets on the upper floors for more space, as building size seems to be the primary indicator of wealth within the community. Most buildings are not decorated with any integral features, but instead use ivy, flowers, and other natural elements in planters of on trellices to breathe life into the structure they grow upon.

Lilgap Db-300r Yǐkhka is is constructed arround a series of spacious baked earthen mainstreets which form overlapping circles, with smaller strait roads linking the circiles to eachother at varrious points. The city is defended by arcane means. It's hard to spot at first, but there's a tell tell shimmer in the air arround Lilgap Db-300r Yǐkhka, and you can spot the ocasional warding glyph carved into a rock or tree all arround town. These mystical defences are ancient, unknowable, and unassailable by current means... Assuming everything is in working order. Otherwise, the wards are little more than a deathtrap. Astonishigly, the Relic of the World That Was are in pristine condishion, as if they had just been finished before you laied eyes upon them.

A look arround Lilgap Db-300r Yǐkhka reveals an abundance of schools, libraries, and other academic structures. Locals can be overheard having academic discussions, as well as talking about scholarly subjects in general. It’s quite clear the city places a lot of value on education and being a learned individual.

Cultural Notes

There’s a relatively new religion in Lilgap Db-300r Yǐkhka which is rapidly gaining power. It might be a sectarian offshoot of a major faith, the unique product of a new prophet, or an outside faith backed by wealthy and powerful foreign supporters. Depending on the demands made on believers, the new faith may be a matter of concern only to the existing clergy, or it might be a major flashpoint for conflict in the community.

Lilgap Db-300r Yǐkhka's town hall was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is known for its fluid and florid elaborate style, comprising ornate, asymmetric designs and pastel shades. It is often considered to be a playful, light style, which made exuberant use of curves and emphasized subtle asymmetry in the general shape of its structures. Walls, ceilings and moldings are decorated with numerous interlacing of curves and counter-curves based on the shapes of ‘C’ and ‘S’, along with shell forms and other naturalistic shapes.

Due to the actions of local Kami, spring is recurring in Lilgap Db-300r Yǐkhka.

The Cloud Dragon, Wyrmling near Lilgap Db-300r Yǐkhka are known to be quite timid.

Lilgap Db-300r Yǐkhka's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in summer and involves drinking to channel Conjuration energies of tier 3 via proclamations.
