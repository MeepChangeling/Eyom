Chyameya Vahâ Qâs̺û (/huˈjəhə/ /ˈqɑs̺ʊ/ [rigorous] [cause]) is a temperate Large City located in the Qàdurû Vu̽yûchû Principality of the Kakoray.

The name Chyameya Vahâ Qâs̺û is derived from the Sylvin language, as Chyameya Vahâ Qâs̺û was founded by Dyëkêbê, who was culturaly Rattu.

Climate

Chyameya Vahâ Qâs̺û has a yearly average temperature of 10°C (50°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a cold 7°C (44°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 14°C (57°F). Chyameya Vahâ Qâs̺û receives an average of 261 cm/y (102 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the short winter months. Chyameya Vahâ Qâs̺û covers an area of nearly 217 km2 (86 mi2), and an average elevation of 1574 m (5164 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Chyameya Vahâ Qâs̺û was founded durring the late 12th century in summer of the year 1063, by Dyëkêbê. The establishment of Chyameya Vahâ Qâs̺û was somewhat plagued by a lack of willing colonists, leading to Dyëkêbê electing to pay people to resettle in Chyameya Vahâ Qâs̺û.

Chyameya Vahâ Qâs̺û was built using the conventions of Rattu durring the late 12th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Chyameya Vahâ Qâs̺û is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature delicute timber framework hidden behind layer upon layer of finly ground plaster bleached to an almost glossy white sheen, with green clay tiled roofs and decorative brass-leafed trim. Even the smallest, poorest looking structures appear to be expencive thanks to the extreem elegence of the organic shapes and paterns going into their lofty, spire-y, vagly gothic designs. The more well off folks live in identicle homes, save for even shiner trim and a more whimsical appearance to their structures flowing forms.

Chyameya Vahâ Qâs̺û is buildings are arranged arrounded highly ordered system of crampt cobblestone streets which form hexical paterns, allowing the incides of the octagons to be common grounds for the buildings on the edges, be it for parkland, yardspace, plazas, or markets. The city rests behind a set of well fortified walls, with gatehouses, watch towers, and battlments. Chyameya Vahâ Qâs̺û's walls are, howeaver, fashioned from stone and timber. While unorthadox, the design looks to be functional to a reasonable degree. With luck, the untested design will remain untested for years to come. Unfortuantly, these unusual yet seemingly effective fortifications are in extreem disrepair, so much so that one cannot tell if they are decaying from a lack of mantance or damage incured.

A look around Chyameya Vahâ Qâs̺û makes it abundantly clear the city suffered something horrible some time ago. It's as if the town itself is depressed. Smiles are few, cheer is nowhere to be had. Everyone quietly goes about their daily business not looking anyone in the eye.

Chyameya Vahâ Qâs̺û has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Chyameya Vahâ Qâs̺û has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Chyameya Vahâ Qâs̺û is home to a University which provides higher education in a variety of fields, and also serves as a research institute for those same fields.

Cultural Notes

Chyameya Vahâ Qâs̺û's bank was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is best known for its striking sculptural forms and often dazzling ornamental detail that characterizes the buildings general shape. The radiant colors, rich patterns, and symmetrical silhouettes employed by this style were backed up by rich decorative features including gardens, courtyards, extruded arches, domes, pointed domes, vaulted ceilings, elaborate painted and inlaid designs, and decorative sculptures.

In Chyameya Vahâ Qâs̺û it is impossible to directly or indirectly lie.

The Cloud Dragon, Wyrmling near Chyameya Vahâ Qâs̺û are known to be more aggressive than normal.

Chyameya Vahâ Qâs̺û's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in winter and involves sacrificing an animal to channel Chronomancy energies of tier 2 via mimery.
