Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp (/doˈfɪnu/ /qə̝p/ [general; generic] [premiere]) is a subtropical Town located in the Jĭìlkìwvǐ District of the Federation of Alveria.

The name Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp is derived from the Constructi language, as Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp was founded by Belegiel Gadsë, who was culturaly Constructi.

Climate

Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp has a yearly average temperature of 20°C (68°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 13°C (55°F). Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp receives an average of 236 cm/y (92 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp covers an area of nearly 6 km2 (2 mi2), and an average elevation of 3398 m (11148 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp was founded durring the early 15th century in summer of the year 1418, by Belegiel Gadsë. The establishment of Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp was somewhat plagued by a lack of willing colonists, leading to Belegiel Gadsë electing to pay people to resettle in Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp.

Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp was built using the conventions of Constructi durring the early 15th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp is no diffrent. The town's buildings feature delicute timber framework hidden behind layer upon layer of finly ground plaster bleached to an almost glossy white sheen, with green clay tiled roofs and decorative brass-leafed trim. Even the smallest, poorest looking structures appear to be expencive thanks to the extreem elegence of the organic shapes and paterns going into their lofty, spire-y, vagly gothic designs. The more well off folks live in identicle homes, save for even shiner trim and a more whimsical appearance to their structures flowing forms.

Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp is buildings folow an organic layout of restrictive cobblestone streets whihch gives the town a shape simmilar to a tree, if one views its streets from above. The town is the proud owner of a properly designed set of renforced walls made from mighty querried stone blocks. Their construction and material choices would make a dwarf weap with joy, for each and every part of the elaborate fortifications are purly functional and robust well byond reason. Even nonexperts can tell the walls are an excelent defencive structure. The town's exceptionaly well made fortifications are visibly old, but also obviously maintained semi-regularly. Its likly the local malishia or garrison are tasked with ocasional mantance of the towns defences.

A look around Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp gives you an uneasy feeling. Everything is just a little too worn down, a little too dirty, or both. No one makes eye contact. Kids play quietly, but happily. Occasionally a passerby glances at you out of the corner of their eye, staring jsut long enough for it to be uncomfortable. People seem to be allowed to do as they please with little harmoney to anything. It feels less like a town, and more like a spot people just happened to place their homes.

Civic Infrastructure

Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp has an Office of Civil Groundskeeping, which works to enforce local ordinances relating to the construction and upkeep up of all plant life, water features, and other natural decorations within Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp. They are also responsible for the maintenance of these features. Notably, the OCG is not responsible for Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp's parks.

Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp has an Office of Civil Vicary, which is responsible for providing a livelyhood for all officialy recognised religious figures within Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp.

Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp has a Gravedigger's Guild, which is responsible for collecting the dead and laying them to rest according to all applicable laws and religious customs.

Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp has a Highwayman's Guild, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp possesses a Galvanic Power Grid, which brings galvanic current to most if not all buildings in town, and permits a great many consumer goods to function within the Town. Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp's grid is powered by mana accumulators.

Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands. They are not to be confused with the Office of Civil Groundskeeping as they do not hold authority over nor responsibility for Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp's natural decorations nor waterways.

Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp has a Guild of Roadworkers, who are responsible for maintaining the roadways and public paths within town. They also have the duty of enforcing all civil laws relating to the roadways.

Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Cultural Notes

Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp is home to a distinct subculture, who are either the majority here or have the dominant positions in the community. Architecture, local laws, and social customs are all tuned to suit them, and they may not be particularly forgiving or friendly to the major culture of the region. Communities that are not outright independent usually make an arm's-length submission to a local lord.

Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp's bank was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is which made use of the classical orders and mathematically precise ratios of height and width combined with a desire for symmetry, proportion, and harmony. It used columns, pediments, arches and domes are imaginatively in buildings of all types. Decorative features were seen as largely unnecessary as the sheer beauty of the structure itself was often close to art. However, many buildings with large ceiling spaces had their ceilings decorated with elaborate paintings, simply because the large flat spaces could feel wasted.

Due to the actions of local Kami, winter is skipped in Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp.

The Armadillo near Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp are known to be a mutant strain of the creature.

Fìkh Cd-27a Qăp's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in summer and involves embarking on a group pilgrimage to channel Transmutation energies of tier 3 via singing.
