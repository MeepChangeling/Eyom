Kb-90v Be Birěkhka (/ˈhepmæg/ /be/ [technological] [right; entitlement]) is a subtropical Town located in Qudoqudez̄u Zone, Yăvajĭkǐkh District, within the Federation of Alveria.

The name Kb-90v Be Birěkhka is derived from the Constructi language, as Kb-90v Be Birěkhka was founded by Lumbiel Shêyê, who was culturaly Constructi.

Climate

Kb-90v Be Birěkhka has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). Kb-90v Be Birěkhka receives an average of 208 cm/y (81 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. Kb-90v Be Birěkhka covers an area of nearly 5 km2 (2 mi2), and an average elevation of 6106 m (20032 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Kb-90v Be Birěkhka was founded durring the late 11th century, by Lumbiel Shêyê. The establishment of the new community went well, though many minor issues had to be solved as time went on. This was enough to delay construction and push back the formal opening ceramony, leading to some embarisment for Lumbiel Shêyê.

Kb-90v Be Birěkhka was built using the conventions of Constructi durring the late 11th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Kb-90v Be Birěkhka is no diffrent. The town's buildings feature plaster covered brickwork used to form structures with an emphasis on symmetry, proportion, geometry and the regularity of parts. Orderly arrangements of columns, pilasters and lintels, as well as the use of semicircular arches, hemispherical domes, niches and aediculae can be found everywhere such that only size of building and yard can be used to measure the general prosparity of a given building's owners due to a general wealthy feeling the style gives off.

Kb-90v Be Birěkhka is buildings are arranged arround a network of premissive cobblestone streets which form a diamond shaped grid, where each diamond verries in size given the proximity of the paralell streets forming each section. The ocasional smaller diamond has been used to construct a park, plaza, and other communal structures. The town is protected by a renforced stone fence which sits atop earthwork defences, for some of the best inexpencive defences a town of Kb-90v Be Birěkhka's size could have. Though some of the worse spots could lead to the loss of defenders lives if attackers identified the weaknesses ahead of time.

Right off the bat Kb-90v Be Birěkhka hits you in the face with its success. Everyone, even the peasants, are dressed in well made clothing. Every tool and implement you can see is finely made, and people will boast to you as obvious strangers of the wonders which can be found in their markets. More interestingly is a total lack of beggars, and plenty of new buildings are going up even as you speak. Somehow this town has come into quite a lot of wealth, and recently from the looks of things. The influx of wealth has brought with it an influx of relaxation. Everywhere you look people are enjoying their prosperity in a slow, casual, and deliberate manner. There’s no rush for anything anywhere you look.

Kb-90v Be Birěkhka has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain Kb-90v Be Birěkhka's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

Kb-90v Be Birěkhka possesses a Galvanic Power Grid, which brings galvanic current to most if not all buildings in town, and permits a great many consumer goods to function within the Town. Kb-90v Be Birěkhka's grid is powered by mana accumulators.

Kb-90v Be Birěkhka has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands. They are not to be confused with the Office of Civil Groundskeeping as they do not hold authority over nor responsibility for Kb-90v Be Birěkhka's natural decorations nor waterways.

Kb-90v Be Birěkhka has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Cultural Notes

Kb-90v Be Birěkhka's garrison was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used has a sleek, linear appearance with stylized, often geometric ornamentation. The primary facade of its buildings often featured a series of set backs that create a stepped outline. Low-relief decorative panels can be found at entrances, around windows, along roof edges or as string courses. It was best known for its use of smooth finish building materials such as stucco, concrete block, glazed brick or mosaic tile. Decorative details can incorporate various artistic or exotic motifs to suit the building's function or the architect's whim. Chevrons, zigzags, and other geometrical motifs are common forms of ornament.

In Kb-90v Be Birěkhka every night at precisely midnight every structure in town is engulfed by sailors fire until the end of the witching hour.

The Ifrit near Kb-90v Be Birěkhka are known to be a mutant strain of the creature.

Kb-90v Be Birěkhka's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in winter and involves orgies to channel Elven High Magic energies of tier 3 via recitation of scripture.
