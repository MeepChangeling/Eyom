Kstíhjec Ji̊ Qi̊v (Kstíh /ʤec/ [metro] [health]) is a subtropical City located in the Kririsi̊mù Region of the Tetburland.

The name Kstíhjec Ji̊ Qi̊v is derived from the Sylvin language, as Kstíhjec Ji̊ Qi̊v was founded by Shovnoh, who was culturaly Tauran.

Climate

Kstíhjec Ji̊ Qi̊v has a yearly average temperature of 29°C (84°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 32°C (89°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 26°C (78°F). Kstíhjec Ji̊ Qi̊v receives an average of 289 cm/y (113 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. Kstíhjec Ji̊ Qi̊v covers an area of nearly 41 km2 (16 mi2), and an average elevation of 2202 m (7224 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Kstíhjec Ji̊ Qi̊v was founded durring the early 18th century in late winter of the year 1730, by Shovnoh. The establishment of Kstíhjec Ji̊ Qi̊v was plagued by a lack of willing colonists. After attempts to pay people to resettle failed Shovnoh struck deals with nearby nations and communities to establish Kstíhjec Ji̊ Qi̊v as a prison colony.

Kstíhjec Ji̊ Qi̊v was built using the conventions of Tauran durring the early 18th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Kstíhjec Ji̊ Qi̊v is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature masoned stone construction which prominantly features pointed arches, pointed ribbed vault cielings, flying buttress', and window tracery all of which share a simmilar gemoetetic patern halfway between organic and inorganic in design formaing a very distinct aesthetically integrated style. BUildings tend to reach for the havens, and more expencive homes are easily identified by their floor count as well as the addition of decorative features intigrated into the building's design such as statues, gargoyals, and embelished joinery.

Kstíhjec Ji̊ Qi̊v is buildings folow an organic layout of spacious cobblestone streets whihch gives the city a shape simmilar to a tree, if one views its streets from above. The city is the proud owner of a thick set of fortified walls fashioned from querried stone blocks. While not up to snuff for a fort or castle wall, the city's walls are naturaly much larger than those of forts or castles. Therefore, the construction such a wall is most expencive. Kstíhjec Ji̊ Qi̊v's buget focused wall would serve its community well in battle in spite of looking unimpressive compared to castles and fortresses. Astonishigly, the top tier civilian fortifications are in pristine condishion, as if they had just been finished before you laied eyes upon them.

Kstíhjec Ji̊ Qi̊v has a very calm atmosphere. People can be seen relaxing, scocilizing, and going about all manner of business other than the daily grind. Men, women, children, all can be seen enjoying life in a laid-back way in the many parks which line Kstíhjec Ji̊ Qi̊v’s streets.

Cultural Notes

Kstíhjec Ji̊ Qi̊v's locals despise outsiders. For some “outsiders” may be natives of foreign lands, while others might have a grudge against anyone from outside the community. These locals have an active loathing, and the outsiders allowed to trade or interact with them do so at a heavy disadvantage.

Kstíhjec Ji̊ Qi̊v's town hall was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is known primarily for its use of abstraction and simplicity. Clean lines, right angles, and primary colors characterized this aesthetic and art movement expressed via architecture and paintings. Its design ethos allows only primary colors and non-colors, only squares and rectangles, only straight and horizontal or vertical lines. Vertical and horizontal lines are positioned in layers or planes that do not intersect, thereby allowing each element to exist independently and unobstructed by other elements. These seemingly impossible principals for an architectural style coalesces into structures which most experts find hard to put into words. It is not that their geometry is impossible, but rather the style's attempt at producing works only describable visually was most successful..

In Kstíhjec Ji̊ Qi̊v snow is warm to the touch and does not melt within city limits. Also it only happens during summer.

The Ooze, Amber near Kstíhjec Ji̊ Qi̊v are known to be quite timid.

Kstíhjec Ji̊ Qi̊v's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in winter and involves creating small tokens to channel Chronomancy energies of tier 3 via recitation of poetic epics.
