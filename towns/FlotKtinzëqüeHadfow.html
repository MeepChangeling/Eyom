Flot Ktinzëqüe Hadfow (/flot/ /ˈktinzɛq˔/ [funny] [theater; arena]) is a subtropical City located in the Ko̠ktugttö Moot of the Rosid.

The name Flot Ktinzëqüe Hadfow is derived from the Sylvin language, as Flot Ktinzëqüe Hadfow was founded by Có̄ńnvō Yātḩlärm 'Bullet Belle' Goīshv Rä̌ Vúrm, who was culturaly Wood Elf.

Climate

Flot Ktinzëqüe Hadfow has a yearly average temperature of 13°C (55°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 1°C (33°F). Flot Ktinzëqüe Hadfow receives an average of 147 cm/y (57 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Flot Ktinzëqüe Hadfow covers an area of nearly 45 km2 (18 mi2), and an average elevation of 4866 m (15964 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Flot Ktinzëqüe Hadfow was founded durring the late 15th century in fall of the year 1458, by Có̄ńnvō Yātḩlärm 'Bullet Belle' Goīshv Rä̌ Vúrm. The establishment of the new community went well, with no major obsticles durring construction.

Flot Ktinzëqüe Hadfow was built using the conventions of Wood Elf durring the late 15th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Flot Ktinzëqüe Hadfow is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature timber framed wooden shiethed or brick construction, which gives form to a very formalized, rational, expence effishent arcatectural style based on strictly symmetrical designs which universaly feature pitched roofs, shutters, and the occasional column or pilaster for a decorative touch.

Flot Ktinzëqüe Hadfow is buildings are arranged arrounded a highly ordered system of restrictive baked earthen streets which form triangular paterns, allowing the incides of the octagons to be common grounds for the buildings on the edges, be it for parkland, yardspace, plazas, or markets. The city sits comfortably behind a palisade wall complete with a timber gatehouse and battlments. Flot Ktinzëqüe Hadfow's timber-based walls has sufferd soem light damage, reducing its function a little in some spots, but could almsot certainly preform as expected... Though some of the worse spots could lead to the loss of defenders lives if attackers identified the weaknesses ahead of time.

A look around Flot Ktinzëqüe Hadfow gives the unmistakable impression the town is one of the strictest places imaginable. Everyone’s actions are clearly directed by laws they keep in heart and mind at all times. Orderly byond order is a phrase which Flot Ktinzëqüe Hadfow brings to mind.

Cultural Notes

Flot Ktinzëqüe Hadfow's garrison was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is characterized by vertical proportions, pointed arches, external buttressing, and asymmetry in the general shape of its buildings. The decorative features of the style were key, consisting of large arched windows, pointed arches, vaulted ceilings, flying buttresses, and sculptures integrated into the structure itself. Occasionally, for very important buildings, an array of sculptures or one colossal sculpture might replace the entirety of the entrance to said building.

In Flot Ktinzëqüe Hadfow every night at precisely midnight every structure in town is engulfed by sailors fire until the end of the witching hour.

The Vukodlak near Flot Ktinzëqüe Hadfow are known to be more aggressive than normal.

Flot Ktinzëqüe Hadfow's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in spring and involves square dance to channel Abjuration energies of tier 3 via chanting.
