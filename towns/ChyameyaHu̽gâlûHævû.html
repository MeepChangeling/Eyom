Chyameya Hu̽gâlû Hævû (/hu̽ˈgɑlʊ/ /ˈhævʊ/ [pragmatic] [fabric; textile]) is a temperate Small City located in Ochyàlà U̽gà Kingdom, Chyûyu̽ Lu̹sû Empire, within the Warren.

The name Chyameya Hu̽gâlû Hævû is derived from the Sylvin language, as Chyameya Hu̽gâlû Hævû was founded by Strekka, who was culturaly Rattu.

Climate

Chyameya Hu̽gâlû Hævû has a yearly average temperature of 11°C (51°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -2°C (29°F). Chyameya Hu̽gâlû Hævû receives an average of 208 cm/y (81 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Chyameya Hu̽gâlû Hævû covers an area of nearly 15 km2 (6 mi2), and an average elevation of 3446 m (11305 ft) above sea level.

Sometimes buildings exist in the smaller squaires, other times they are open spaces, or occupied by temporary structures. The city is the proud owner of a properly designed set of renforced walls made from mighty querried stone blocks. Their construction and material choices would make a dwarf weap with joy, for each and every part of the elaborate fortifications are purly functional and robust well byond reason. Even nonexperts can tell the walls are an excelent defencive structure. Astonishigly, the exceptionaly well made fortifications are in pristine condishion, as if they had just been finished before you laied eyes upon them.

Men, women, children, all can be seen enjoying life in a laid-back way in the many parks which line Chyameya Hu̽gâlû Hævû’s streets. Cultural Notes

Chyameya Hu̽gâlû Hævû's garrison was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is known for its fluid and florid elaborate style, comprising ornate, asymmetric designs and pastel shades. It is often considered to be a playful, light style, which made exuberant use of curves and emphasized subtle asymmetry in the general shape of its structures. Walls, ceilings and moldings are decorated with numerous interlacing of curves and counter-curves based on the shapes of ‘C’ and ‘S’, along with shell forms and other naturalistic shapes.

Due to the actions of local Kami, winter is recurring in Chyameya Hu̽gâlû Hævû.

The Human Juju Zombie near Chyameya Hu̽gâlû Hævû are known to be almost tame, such that they can be put to domestic use.

Chyameya Hu̽gâlû Hævû's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in summer and involves performance art to channel Enchantment energies of tier 3 via proclamations.

Points of Interest

Chyameya Hu̽gâlû Hævû is cursed with recurrent spells of some troublesome disease. The affliction isn’t so fatal as to make living there impossible, but it adds suffering and expense to local lives. The plague might be the product of an ancient curse, the results of long lost toxic remains, or an unavoidable byproduct of whatever industry or purpose justifies the city. It’s probably not overly contagious, but visitors may be in some peril all the same.

Chyameya Hu̽gâlû Hævû's is something of a geological and arcane anomaly, as neither physical nor magical law entirely explains its formation.
