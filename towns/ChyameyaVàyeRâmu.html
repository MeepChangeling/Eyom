Chyameya Vàye Râmu (/ˈvəje/ /ˈrɑmu/ [probable; possible] [tattoo]) is a temperate City located in Kuqe S̺eyî Kingdom, S̺âho Chyæmû Empire, within the Warren.

The name Chyameya Vàye Râmu is derived from the Sylvin language, as Chyameya Vàye Râmu was founded by Syntoma, who was culturaly Rattu.

Climate

Chyameya Vàye Râmu has a yearly average temperature of 14°C (57°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 6°C (42°F). Chyameya Vàye Râmu receives an average of 138 cm/y (54 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Chyameya Vàye Râmu covers an area of nearly 30 km2 (12 mi2), and an average elevation of 9744 m (-10981 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Chyameya Vàye Râmu was founded durring the late 18th century in early winter of the year 1686, by Syntoma. The establishment of Chyameya Vàye Râmu was only bairly constructed. The sheer number of problems with its founding were enough to make several of the backers funding Chyameya Vàye Râmu's construction back out of the project. Syntoma pushed on reguardles, and Chyameya Vàye Râmu was finished, but starts off as a terible place to live.

Chyameya Vàye Râmu was built using the conventions of Rattu durring the late 18th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Chyameya Vàye Râmu is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature masoned stone construction which prominantly features pointed arches, pointed ribbed vault cielings, flying buttress', and window tracery all of which share a simmilar gemoetetic patern halfway between organic and inorganic in design formaing a very distinct aesthetically integrated style. BUildings tend to reach for the havens, and more expencive homes are easily identified by their floor count as well as the addition of decorative features intigrated into the building's design such as statues, gargoyals, and embelished joinery.

Chyameya Vàye Râmu is buildings are arranged arround a network of crampt paverstone streets which form a diagonal shaped grid, where each square verries in size given the proximity of the paralell streets forming each section. The ocasional smaller square has been used to construct a park, plaza, and other communal structures. The city is protected by a well-crafted cobblestone fence tall enough to provide adiquite cover for defenders to fire from, but no more than that. The city's frontieer-style defences are visibly old, but also obviously well maintained. Its likly the local malishia or garrison are tasked with routine mantance of the city's defences.

A look around Chyameya Vàye Râmu makes it abundantly clear the city suffered something horrible some time ago. It’s a quiet city filled with closed scriptoriums, people quietly yet dejectedly reading in isolated places around town, and the occasional book laying in a heap of garbage.

Cultural Notes

Chyameya Vàye Râmu's bank was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is known for its fluid and florid elaborate style, comprising ornate, asymmetric designs and pastel shades. It is often considered to be a playful, light style, which made exuberant use of curves and emphasized subtle asymmetry in the general shape of its structures. Walls, ceilings and moldings are decorated with numerous interlacing of curves and counter-curves based on the shapes of ‘C’ and ‘S’, along with shell forms and other naturalistic shapes.

Due to the actions of local Kami, autumn is recurring in Chyameya Vàye Râmu.

The Alseid near Chyameya Vàye Râmu are known to be more aggressive than normal.

Chyameya Vàye Râmu's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in summer and involves embarking on a group pilgrimage to channel Illusion energies of tier 1 via oath swearing.
