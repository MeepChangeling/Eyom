Lilgap Tl-45t Voyge (/ʤɤr/ /ˈvojge/ [weak; sick; fragile] [branch (tree part)]) is a temperate Large City located in Ki-42cěwǐ Zone, Yăvajĭkǐkh District, within the Alveria.

The name Lilgap Tl-45t Voyge is derived from the Goblin language, as Lilgap Tl-45t Voyge was founded by Mp̪voī Bé̄̄̌s 'Skye Alyssa' Réchvé̄r Jō̄ Caw̋chīs, who was culturaly KLEO.

Climate

Lilgap Tl-45t Voyge has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). Lilgap Tl-45t Voyge receives an average of 212 cm/y (83 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. Lilgap Tl-45t Voyge covers an area of nearly 225 km2 (90 mi2), and an average elevation of 3804 m (12480 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Lilgap Tl-45t Voyge was founded durring the early 14th century in fall of the year 1317, by Mp̪voī Bé̄̄̌s 'Skye Alyssa' Réchvé̄r Jō̄ Caw̋chīs. The establishment of Lilgap Tl-45t Voyge was plagued by a lack of willing colonists. After attempts to pay people to resettle failed Mp̪voī Bé̄̄̌s 'Skye Alyssa' Réchvé̄r Jō̄ Caw̋chīs struck deals with nearby nations and communities to establish Lilgap Tl-45t Voyge as a prison colony.

Lilgap Tl-45t Voyge was built using the conventions of KLEO durring the early 14th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Lilgap Tl-45t Voyge is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature timber framed wooden shiethed or brick construction, which gives form to a very formalized, rational, expence effishent arcatectural style based on strictly symmetrical designs which universaly feature pitched roofs, shutters, and the occasional column or pilaster for a decorative touch.

Lilgap Tl-45t Voyge is buildings are arranged arrounded a highly ordered system of premissive baked earthen streets which form triangular paterns, allowing the incides of the octagons to be common grounds for the buildings on the edges, be it for parkland, yardspace, plazas, or markets. The city has a defencive wall made from large clay bricks. The wall is constructed to the exact specifications of millitary fortifications, but the nature of its clay brick construction leaves it vulnerable to even outdated siege equipment. That said, the city is well defended against anything short of an army. Lilgap Tl-45t Voyge's brittle defences are suffering from significent damage, so much so that examples can be pointed to no matter which section one might have within their line of site, and most of which render sections inoperable at present.

Lilgap Tl-45t Voyge is not quite well. Something happened here, perhapse recently, perhapse long ago. Whatever it was, it settled into the very soul of the city like a festering wound. The people go about their day well enough, but there’s a tention in the air you can cut with a knife. You get the terrible feeling that whatever it was, the wound it left will simply never heal. This city is as a necropolice.

In spite of being generally closed to the public, the library has a room with several Aether Linked devices available to the public during business hours.

Lilgap Tl-45t Voyge has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands. They are not to be confused with the Office of Civil Groundskeeping as they do not hold authority over nor responsibility for Lilgap Tl-45t Voyge's natural decorations nor waterways.

Lilgap Tl-45t Voyge has a Guild of Roadworkers, who are responsible for maintaining the roadways and public paths within town. They also have the duty of enforcing all civil laws relating to the roadways.

Lilgap Tl-45t Voyge has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Lilgap Tl-45t Voyge has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Lilgap Tl-45t Voyge is home to a University which provides higher education in a variety of fields, and also serves as a research institute for those same fields.

Cultural Notes

Lilgap Tl-45t Voyge's chapel was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is characterized by their massive, monolithic and 'blocky' appearance with a rigid geometric style. It was best known for its rough, unfinished surfaces, unusual shapes, heavy-looking materials, straight lines, and small windows. Modular elements were often used to form masses representing specific functional zones, grouped into a unified whole.

In Lilgap Tl-45t Voyge the stars are always right.

The Lurker in Light near Lilgap Tl-45t Voyge are known to be almost tame, such that they can be put to domestic use.

Lilgap Tl-45t Voyge's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in winter and involves ritual combat to channel Wild Magic energies of tier 2 via guttural bellowing.

History

In time immemorial, reportedly some time during the late 2nd century after an anomalously snowy winter, runoff from snow-melt caused Mere Monoun to overflow its banks and spill into Backwater Choral. The resulting flood was isolated to the area around Lilgap Tl-45t Voyge, which was swallowed by the waters for several days. Lilgap Tl-45t Voyge lost 101 people, 396 livestock, and 28 buildings in the disaster. The disaster is remembered as the Horror Drownings.
