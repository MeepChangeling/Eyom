Eg Ed Fapm (/ed/ /fapm/ [foreign; external] [husband]) is a subtropical Town located in Morfalcon Dutchy, Qånehgdab Kingdom, within the United Kingdom of Undermountain.

The name Eg Ed Fapm is derived from the Dwarven language, as Eg Ed Fapm was founded by Trèqtho Zehndtubckexu, who was culturaly Dwarvern.

Climate

Eg Ed Fapm has a yearly average temperature of 15°C (59°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a cool 19°C (66°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 12°C (53°F). Eg Ed Fapm receives an average of 258 cm/y (101 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Eg Ed Fapm covers an area of nearly 5 km2 (2 mi2), and an average elevation of 2034 m (6673 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Eg Ed Fapm was founded durring the late 16th century in winter of the year 1496, by Trèqtho Zehndtubckexu. The establishment of Eg Ed Fapm was only bairly constructed. The sheer number of problems with its founding were enough to make several of the backers funding Eg Ed Fapm's construction back out of the project. Trèqtho Zehndtubckexu pushed on reguardles, and Eg Ed Fapm was finished, but starts off as a terible place to live.

Eg Ed Fapm was built using the conventions of Dwarvern durring the late 16th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Eg Ed Fapm is no diffrent. The town's buildings feature waddle and daub construction with good timber frames and a stone foundation protected by thatched or shingled rooves. Most buildings with second floors are built in such a way as to overhang into the streets on the upper floors for more space, as building size seems to be the primary indicator of wealth within the community. Most buildings are not decorated with any integral features, but instead use ivy, flowers, and other natural elements in planters of on trellices to breathe life into the structure they grow upon.

Eg Ed Fapm is buildings are arranged arround a network of spacious packed earth streets which form a grid, where each square verries in size given the proximity of the paralell streets forming each section. The ocasional smaller square has been used to construct a park, plaza, and other communal structures. The town has a fortified albit thin brick wall. The wall has most of the feeatures of a typical castle wall, just on a much smaller scale and and budget. Notably brick isn't a particuarly soild choice for resisting siege weapons. Eg Ed Fapm's wall wouldn't hinder a proper army, but it is more than sufishent for bandits and other small marauding groups. Eg Ed Fapm's monster and outlaw focused fortifications are in an unremarkable state. To some, this is the ideal sate for defences to be in. In need of absoutly nothing, and ready to serve the town as needed.

A look around Eg Ed Fapm makes you wonder how anything ever gets done. The locals have clear, obvious rivalries with one another, as seen through clenched teeth and fake smiles. What’s more, there is a serious lack of any organization and planning in Eg Ed Fapm’s layout as well as the local’s behaviors. The town seems to be a den of chaos where tongues are forked and local customs do not exist.

Civic Infrastructure

Eg Ed Fapm has an Office of Civil Vicary, which is responsible for providing a livelyhood for all officialy recognised religious figures within Eg Ed Fapm.

Eg Ed Fapm has a Gravedigger's Guild, which is responsible for collecting the dead and laying them to rest according to all applicable laws and religious customs.

Eg Ed Fapm has a Guild of Nurses, which is tasked with caring for the elderly and infirm in accordance with local ordinances, religious values, and customs.

Eg Ed Fapm has a Highwayman's Guild, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Eg Ed Fapm has a Hall of Slayers, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Eg Ed Fapm has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain Eg Ed Fapm's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

Eg Ed Fapm possesses a Galvanic Power Grid, which brings galvanic current to most if not all buildings in town, and permits a great many consumer goods to function within the Town. Eg Ed Fapm's grid is powered by an arcane means.

Eg Ed Fapm has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands.

Eg Ed Fapm has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Cultural Notes

Life is hard in Eg Ed Fapm. Its people are impoverished compared to their peers elsewhere. Something is making the locals stay, however, whether fear of the alternative, hope for a better future, or a stubborn attachment to their ancestral lands. Whatver the reason, living in this harsh area for generations has made the people of Eg Ed Fapm a notably resourceful and hearty people.

Eg Ed Fapm's garrison was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is which made use of the classical orders and mathematically precise ratios of height and width combined with a desire for symmetry, proportion, and harmony. It used columns, pediments, arches and domes are imaginatively in buildings of all types. Decorative features were seen as largely unnecessary as the sheer beauty of the structure itself was often close to art. However, many buildings with large ceiling spaces had their ceilings decorated with elaborate paintings, simply because the large flat spaces could feel wasted.

In Eg Ed Fapm snow is warm to the touch and does not melt within city limits. Also it only happens during summer.

The Pudding, Blood near Eg Ed Fapm are known to be almost tame, such that they can be put to domestic use.

Eg Ed Fapm's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in spring and involves gestures to channel Enchantment energies of tier 2 via oath swearing.
