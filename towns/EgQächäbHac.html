Eg Qächäb Hac (/quˈjla/ /hac/ [extinct] [chart; graph; diagram]) is a subtropical Village located in Huíhmwuf County, Yuttul̄ Kingdom, within the United Kingdom of Undermountain.

The name Eg Qächäb Hac is derived from the Dwarven language, as Eg Qächäb Hac was founded by Êyêbrê Åxruqedrayd Rebckezqug, who was culturaly Dwarvern.

Climate

Eg Qächäb Hac has a yearly average temperature of 17°C (62°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 12°C (53°F). Eg Qächäb Hac receives an average of 258 cm/y (101 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. Eg Qächäb Hac covers an area of nearly 3 km2 (1 mi2), and an average elevation of 1166 m (3825 ft) above sea level.

History

In time immemorial, reportedly some time during the early 2nd century, Eg Qächäb Hac was attacked by savage hagss living nearby. The details of the conflict are hazy at best due to many conflicting accounts. What is known is Eg Qächäb Hac lost 237 people, 301 livestock, and 99 buildings. The conflict ended after roughly 58, when members of Eg Qächäb Hac's militia enacted an operation to escort a particular vault to a safe location. The operation was complicated by a trusted officer who turned traitor and defects. The conflict ended with an assault and siege on the vault, which ended in a stalemate for Eg Qächäb Hac's forces. The war is remembered in legend by Eg Qächäb Hac's bards, historians, and legend keepers.
