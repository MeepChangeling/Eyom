Uv-maqüe Ner (/uv/ /maq˔/ [court (of law); tribunal] [biology]) is a subtropical Large Town located in Tj-46qjĭæn Zone, Vrǟmnde̜wi̽fto Diocese, within the Covenent of Irus.

The name Uv-maqüe Ner is derived from the Wood Elvish language, as Uv-maqüe Ner was founded by Tasnora Dhando, who was culturaly Wood Elven.

Climate

Uv-maqüe Ner has a yearly average temperature of 17°C (62°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 9°C (48°F). Uv-maqüe Ner receives an average of 222 cm/y (87 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. Uv-maqüe Ner covers an area of nearly 6 km2 (2 mi2), and an average elevation of 6410 m (21030 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Uv-maqüe Ner was founded durring the late 12th century in winter of the year 1072, by Tasnora Dhando. The establishment of Uv-maqüe Ner suffered from many setbacks, delays, and obsticles, most notably a group of Uv-maqüe Ner which required millitary assistance exterminate before the community could finish being built.

Uv-maqüe Ner was built using the conventions of Wood Elven durring the late 12th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Uv-maqüe Ner is no diffrent. The town's buildings feature waddle and daub construction with good timber frames and a stone foundation protected by thatched or shingled rooves. Most buildings with second floors are built in such a way as to overhang into the streets on the upper floors for more space, as building size seems to be the primary indicator of wealth within the community. Most buildings are not decorated with any integral features, but instead use ivy, flowers, and other natural elements in planters of on trellices to breathe life into the structure they grow upon.

Uv-maqüe Ner is buildings are arranged arround a network of broad canals streets which form a grid, where each square verries in size given the proximity of the paralell streets forming each section. The ocasional smaller square has been used to construct a park, plaza, and other communal structures. The town rests behind a thick wall made from clay bricks. The wall has all of the proper fortifications and is well made. Unfortuantly the nature of clay brick leaves it quite vulnerable to siege equipment, though the thickness of the wall lends it simmilar resistnace to a thinner hardrock wall. Uv-maqüe Ner's millitarily questionable fortifications are in an unremarkable state. To some, this is the ideal sate for defences to be in. In need of absoutly nothing, and ready to serve the town as needed.

Uv-maqüe Ner is, in a word, disorder. People seem to be allowed to do as they please with little harmoney to anything. It feels less like a town, and more like a spot people just happened to place their homes. Yet there are small elements here and there which show the underlying structure of the community. It’s just so complex, organic, and flowing one can only understand what is a piece of the puzzle, but not what its neighbors are.

Civic Infrastructure

Uv-maqüe Ner possesses a city-wide Aethary Link which provides Aethary access anywhere within its metropolitan. This allows citizens who can afford the relevant devices access in their places of work, and rarely homes.

Uv-maqüe Ner has an Office of Civil Groundskeeping, which works to enforce local ordinances relating to the construction and upkeep up of all plant life, water features, and other natural decorations within Uv-maqüe Ner. They are also responsible for the maintenance of these features. Notably, the OCG is not responsible for Uv-maqüe Ner's parks.

Uv-maqüe Ner has an Office of Civil Vicary, which is responsible for providing a livelyhood for all officialy recognised religious figures within Uv-maqüe Ner.

Uv-maqüe Ner has a Gravedigger's Guild, which is responsible for collecting the dead and laying them to rest according to all applicable laws and religious customs.

Uv-maqüe Ner has a Highwayman's Guild, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Uv-maqüe Ner has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain Uv-maqüe Ner's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

Uv-maqüe Ner possesses a Galvanic Power Grid, which brings galvanic current to most if not all buildings in town, and permits a great many consumer goods to function within the Large Town. Uv-maqüe Ner's grid is powered by hydrogalvanic generators.

Uv-maqüe Ner has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands. They are not to be confused with the Office of Civil Groundskeeping as they do not hold authority over nor responsibility for Uv-maqüe Ner's natural decorations nor waterways.

Uv-maqüe Ner has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Uv-maqüe Ner has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Cultural Notes

Uv-maqüe Ner's bank was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is known for its buildings being predominantly formed through the combination of basic geometric shapes. However, it shown in the detailing given to the structures such as its characteristic tall columns, intricate detail, symmetry, harmony, and balance in their designs to an astonishing degree of precision. Decorative elements for the buildings tended to be built into the structure itself, making great use of fluting, frescoes, inlays, and embossing.

In Uv-maqüe Ner there is a constant smell of overcooked presumably ethnic food.

The Amphisbaena near Uv-maqüe Ner are known to be almost tame, such that they can be put to domestic use.

Uv-maqüe Ner's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in autumn and involves bloodletting to channel Wild Magic energies of tier 1 via divine sermons.
