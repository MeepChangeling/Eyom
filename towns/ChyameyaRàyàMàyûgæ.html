Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ (/ˈrəjə/ /məˈjʊgæ/ [formal] [bureaucrat]) is a temperate Large City located in Mædame Hûbâ Kingdom, Vu̹chi Bu̽su Empire, within the Warren.

The name Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ is derived from the Sylvin language, as Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ was founded by Rob, who was culturaly Rattu.

Climate

Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ has a yearly average temperature of 13°C (55°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a cold 9°C (48°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 17°C (62°F). Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ receives an average of 258 cm/y (101 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the short winter months. Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ covers an area of nearly 107 km2 (42 mi2), and an average elevation of 1668 m (5472 ft) above sea level.

Civic Infrastructure

Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ possesses a city-wide Aethary Link which provides Aethary access anywhere within its metropolitan. This allows citizens who can afford the relevant devices access in their places of work, and rarely homes.

Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ has an animal control department which works to enforce local ordinances relating to the control, impoundment, and disposition of animals.

Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ has an Office of Civil Groundskeeping, which works to enforce local ordinances relating to the construction and upkeep up of all plant life, water features, and other natural decorations within Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ. They are also responsible for the maintenance of these features. Notably, the OCG is not responsible for Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ's parks.

Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ has an Office of Civil Vicary, which is responsible for providing a livelyhood for all officialy recognised religious figures within Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ.

Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ has a government-funded child care program, overseen by the local Department of Nursemaids, which is responsible for providing childcare to working-class citizens according to local ordinances.

Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ has a Gravedigger's Guild, which is responsible for collecting the dead and laying them to rest according to all applicable laws and religious customs.

Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ has a Guild of Nurses, which is tasked with caring for the elderly and infirm in accordance with local ordinances, religious values, and customs.

Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ has a Department of Firefighters, which is responsible for organizing fire fighting efforts during a fire and enforcing local ordinances relating to fire safety.

Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ has a Highwayman's Guild, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ has a Hall of Slayers, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ has an Arts Academy which provides higher education in many fields including math, language arts, philosophy, engineering, and other such disciplines.

Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ possesses a Galvanic Power Grid, which brings galvanic current to most if not all buildings in town, and permits a great many consumer goods to function within the Large City. Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ's grid is powered by a direct leyline tap.

Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ's old civil lighting system was converted to Galvanic Lamps recently, and expanded to provide nighttime illumination to all city streets.

Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ has a first rate hospital which caters to anyone in need of long term medical care.

Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ has a library, which keeps a large collection of books, scrolls, and archives all manner of physical items. While not open to the public, the librarians and scholars employed by the library will assist anyone with their research needs, and wealthy individuals can purchase membership to access the library's materials themselves. In spite of being generally closed to the public, the library has a room with several Aether Linked devices available to the public during business hours.

Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands. They are not to be confused with the Office of Civil Groundskeeping as they do not hold authority over nor responsibility for Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ's natural decorations nor waterways.

Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ has a Guild of Roadworkers, who are responsible for maintaining the roadways and public paths within town. They also have the duty of enforcing all civil laws relating to the roadways.

Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Cultural Notes

Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ's garrison was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used which employed abundant symbolic geometry, using pure forms such as the circle and square, and plans are based on often symmetrical layouts featuring rectangular courtyards and halls. These structures were is decorated with carved stone or stucco reliefs and made use of colorful stone mosaics..

In Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ rainbows form quite often above the Large City.

The ﻿Pegasus near Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ are known to be almost tame, such that they can be put to domestic use.

Chyameya Ràyà Màyûgæ's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in spring and involves square dance to channel Abjuration energies of tier 2 via singing.
