Gi̽ Chyhyiōn Med (/gi̽/ /ˈcioːn/ [violent] [trail]) is a subtropical Large City located in the Menertahasha County of the Viceroyalty of Rosid.

The name Gi̽ Chyhyiōn Med is derived from the Wood Elvish language, as Gi̽ Chyhyiōn Med was founded by Eluned Ghibulduc, who was culturaly Wood Elven.

Climate

Gi̽ Chyhyiōn Med has a yearly average temperature of 15°C (59°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 6°C (42°F). Gi̽ Chyhyiōn Med receives an average of 148 cm/y (58 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Gi̽ Chyhyiōn Med covers an area of nearly 467 km2 (186 mi2), and an average elevation of 4208 m (13805 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Gi̽ Chyhyiōn Med was founded durring the late 14th century in fall of the year 1255, by Eluned Ghibulduc. The establishment of Gi̽ Chyhyiōn Med was only bairly constructed. The sheer number of problems with its founding were enough to make several of the backers funding Gi̽ Chyhyiōn Med's construction back out of the project. Eluned Ghibulduc pushed on reguardles, and Gi̽ Chyhyiōn Med was finished, but starts off as a terible place to live.

Gi̽ Chyhyiōn Med was built using the conventions of Wood Elven durring the late 14th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Gi̽ Chyhyiōn Med is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature timber framed wooden shiethed or brick construction, which gives form to a very formalized, rational, expence effishent arcatectural style based on strictly symmetrical designs which universaly feature pitched roofs, shutters, and the occasional column or pilaster for a decorative touch.

Gi̽ Chyhyiōn Med is buildings are arranged arround a network of premissive paverstone streets which form a diagonal shaped grid, where each square verries in size given the proximity of the paralell streets forming each section. The ocasional smaller square has been used to construct a park, plaza, and other communal structures. The cliffside city rests behind a thick wall made from clay bricks. The wall has all of the proper fortifications and is well made. Unfortuantly the nature of clay brick leaves it quite vulnerable to siege equipment, though the thickness of the wall lends it simmilar resistnace to a thinner hardrock wall. Gi̽ Chyhyiōn Med's millitarily questionable fortifications are suffering from significent damage, so much so that examples can be pointed to no matter which section one might have within their line of site, and most of which render sections inoperable at present.

Gi̽ Chyhyiōn Med is not quite well. Something happened here, perhapse recently, perhapse long ago. Whatever it was, it settled into the very soul of the city like a festering wound. The people go about their day well enough, but there’s a tention in the air you can cut with a knife.
