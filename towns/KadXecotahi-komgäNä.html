Kad Xecotahi-komgä Nä (/kjipt/ /comˈgɑ nɑ/ [cannon] [triangle]) is a temperate Large City located in the Temryont Principality of the Soheque.

The name Kad Xecotahi-komgä Nä is derived from the Goblin language, as Kad Xecotahi-komgä Nä was founded by Hrihzarh, who was culturaly Gnoll.

Climate

Kad Xecotahi-komgä Nä has a yearly average temperature of -1°C (31°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 10°C (50°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -12°C (11°F). Kad Xecotahi-komgä Nä receives an average of 246 cm/y (96 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Kad Xecotahi-komgä Nä covers an area of nearly 86 km2 (34 mi2), and an average elevation of 2186 m (7171 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Kad Xecotahi-komgä Nä was founded durring the late 16th century in winter of the year 1556, by Hrihzarh. The establishment of Kad Xecotahi-komgä Nä suffered from several major issues, resulting in the need to develop many solutions to basic problems. Problems such as a lack of fresh water, logistical support, poor quality tools, and the odd monster or two. Howeaver, these were overcome in time.

Kad Xecotahi-komgä Nä was built using the conventions of Gnoll durring the late 16th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Kad Xecotahi-komgä Nä is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature masoned stone construction which prominantly features pointed arches, pointed ribbed vault cielings, flying buttress', and window tracery all of which share a simmilar gemoetetic patern halfway between organic and inorganic in design formaing a very distinct aesthetically integrated style. BUildings tend to reach for the havens, and more expencive homes are easily identified by their floor count as well as the addition of decorative features intigrated into the building's design such as statues, gargoyals, and embelished joinery.

Kad Xecotahi-komgä Nä is is constructed arround a semi-circular narrow packed earth mainstreet, with smaller strait roads linking the circiles to eachother at varrious points. The city rests behind the absurdity that is a thick, timber braced, wall made of clay bricks. While visualy impressive and certainly an astetic, Kad Xecotahi-komgä Nä's wall provides no actual defence against siege equipment due to the choice of its cosntruction materials. Even nonexperts can tell the town is trying to impress rather than defend with its walls, towers, and gatehouses. Though admittedly, they do look nice... To primitive tribals who have never seen fortifications before. Astonishigly, the political statment focused walls are in pristine condishion, as if they had just been finished before you laied eyes upon them.

Kad Xecotahi-komgä Nä has a very calm atmosphere. People can be seen relaxing, scocilizing, and going about all manner of business other than the daily grind. Men, women, children, all can be seen enjoying life in a laid-back way in the many parks which line Kad Xecotahi-komgä Nä’s streets. A look around Kad Xecotahi-komgä Nä has something terribly wrong with it. It’s impossible to put one’s finger on, but something is horribly wrong. Maybe it’s the way fog blankets the ground, but only in the connors of places. Maybe it’s the vermin scutteling between shadows in the corner of your eyes. Perhaps it’s the overcast sky which seemed to creep out of nowhere, or the distant howling of wolves. Maybe it’s everything together. Regardless, you do not feel it would be wise to remain in Kad Xecotahi-komgä Nä long.

Kad Xecotahi-komgä Nä has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Kad Xecotahi-komgä Nä has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Kad Xecotahi-komgä Nä is home to a University which provides higher education in a variety of fields, and also serves as a research institute for those same fields.

Cultural Notes

Kad Xecotahi-komgä Nä's mayor's house was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is best known for its functional shapes, abstract shapes used sparingly for decor, simple color schemes, holistic design, and basic industrial materials. Its simple designs were created to be beautiful, functional, and mass-producible. The style used little to no embellishment or ornamentation, instead drawing attention to the streamlined design, such as flat roofs to create a simple, geometric look. The simplicity masks the style's nearly sinister functionality, as every last feature is designed to guide the people living in the building in how to make the most efficient use of the structure.

In Kad Xecotahi-komgä Nä there is no wind.

The Blood Hawk near Kad Xecotahi-komgä Nä are known to be almost tame, such that they can be put to domestic use.

Kad Xecotahi-komgä Nä's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in spring and involves embarking on a group pilgrimage to channel Elven High Magic energies of tier 2 via recitation of scripture.
