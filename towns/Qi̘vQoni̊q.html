Qi̘v Qoni̊q (/qon/ /i̘q/ [coal] [awake; aware; conscious]) is a subtropical Large City located in the Westcland Community of the Tetbur Commune.

The name Qi̘v Qoni̊q is derived from the Tauric language, as Qi̘v Qoni̊q was founded by Sheg̈m Llywelyn Pritchard Leyshon, who was culturaly Tauric.

Climate

Qi̘v Qoni̊q has a yearly average temperature of 21°C (69°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 17°C (62°F). Qi̘v Qoni̊q receives an average of 262 cm/y (103 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. Qi̘v Qoni̊q covers an area of nearly 420 km2 (168 mi2), and an average elevation of 1848 m (6062 ft) above sea level.

Cultural Notes

Qi̘v Qoni̊q's chapel was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is admittedly strange and non-linear style rooted in defiance of symmetrical shapes. It championed the creation of buildings with a unique visual appearance. the structural norms of classic buildings and deforms or moves away from elementary architectural principles. By including non-linear designs processed into its buildings and favoring fragmentation, this style expressed a form of controlled chaos. Its buildings appear out-of-the-ordinary, draw the eye in immediately and sometimes create a feeling of strangeness. These distorted shapes and structure are not reserved to the building’s outer facade, they destabilize interior elements too, favoring minimalism and play on people’s perceptions by injecting a futuristic touch.

Due to the actions of local Kami, spring is long in Qi̘v Qoni̊q.

The Urdefhan near Qi̘v Qoni̊q are known to be more aggressive than normal.

Qi̘v Qoni̊q's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in summer and involves performance art to channel Mysticism energies of tier 2 via recitation of poetic epics.
