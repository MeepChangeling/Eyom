Bæru̹be Qâyî Kîchyà (/mu̹/ /ˈqɑjɪː/ [brown] [milk]) is a temperate Town located in Nogreftö Parish, Bàze Logu Empire, within the Warrenese Shogunate.

The name Bæru̹be Qâyî Kîchyà is derived from the Wareneese language, as Bæru̹be Qâyî Kîchyà was founded by Gwyneth Gadsë, who was culturaly Wareneese.

Bæru̹be Qâyî Kîchyà has a yearly average temperature of 8°C (46°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -10°C (14°F). Bæru̹be Qâyî Kîchyà receives an average of 289 cm/y (113 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Bæru̹be Qâyî Kîchyà covers an area of nearly 4 km2 (1 mi2), and an average elevation of 4626 m (15177 ft) above sea level.

Cultural Notes

Bæru̹be Qâyî Kîchyà 's garrison was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is which made use of the classical orders and mathematically precise ratios of height and width combined with a desire for symmetry, proportion, and harmony. It used columns, pediments, arches and domes are imaginatively in buildings of all types. Decorative features were seen as largely unnecessary as the sheer beauty of the structure itself was often close to art. However, many buildings with large ceiling spaces had their ceilings decorated with elaborate paintings, simply because the large flat spaces could feel wasted.
