Áedla-ogtär Xluwuf (/ə˞ˈedla/ /ogˈtɑr/ [friendly] [weight]) is a temperate Large City located in the Trērgslij Principality of the Nika.

The name Áedla-ogtär Xluwuf is derived from the Sylvin language, as Áedla-ogtär Xluwuf was founded by Ardeitulus, who was culturaly Gnoll.

Climate

Áedla-ogtär Xluwuf has a yearly average temperature of 1°C (33°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a cold 8°C (46°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -5°C (23°F). Áedla-ogtär Xluwuf receives an average of 233 cm/y (91 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Áedla-ogtär Xluwuf covers an area of nearly 84 km2 (33 mi2), and an average elevation of 1844 m (6049 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Áedla-ogtär Xluwuf was founded durring the early 16th century in summer of the year 1503, by Ardeitulus. The establishment of Áedla-ogtär Xluwuf suffered from several major issues, resulting in the need to develop many solutions to basic problems. Problems such as a lack of fresh water, logistical support, poor quality tools, and the odd monster or two. Howeaver, these were overcome in time.

Áedla-ogtär Xluwuf was built using the conventions of Gnoll durring the early 16th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Áedla-ogtär Xluwuf is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature plaster covered brickwork used to form structures with an emphasis on symmetry, proportion, geometry and the regularity of parts. Orderly arrangements of columns, pilasters and lintels, as well as the use of semicircular arches, hemispherical domes, niches and aediculae can be found everywhere such that only size of building and yard can be used to measure the general prosparity of a given building's owners due to a general wealthy feeling the style gives off.

Áedla-ogtär Xluwuf is buildings are arranged arround a network of broad paverstone streets which form a grid, where each square verries in size given the proximity of the paralell streets forming each section. The ocasional smaller square has been used to construct a park, plaza, and other communal structures. The city is the proud owner of a thick set of fortified walls fashioned from querried stone blocks. While not up to snuff for a fort or castle wall, the city's walls are naturaly much larger than those of forts or castles. Therefore, the construction such a wall is most expencive. Áedla-ogtär Xluwuf's buget focused wall would serve its community well in battle in spite of looking unimpressive compared to castles and fortresses. Áedla-ogtär Xluwuf's top tier civilian fortifications has sufferd soem light damage, reducing its function a little in some spots, but could almsot certainly preform as expected... Though some of the worse spots could lead to the loss of defenders lives if attackers identified the weaknesses ahead of time.

Áedla-ogtär Xluwuf is a bustling hive of activity. Everyone moves at nothing short of a jog, each convocation is a mile a minute, and there’s dedicated lanes for riding through town in the center of each street. The locals all appear to be not merely occupied, but in a true hurry for everything from drinking a pint of ale to their daily work.

Cultural Notes

Áedla-ogtär Xluwuf's garrison was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is known for its buildings being predominantly formed through the combination of basic geometric shapes. However, it shown in the detailing given to the structures such as its characteristic tall columns, intricate detail, symmetry, harmony, and balance in their designs to an astonishing degree of precision. Decorative elements for the buildings tended to be built into the structure itself, making great use of fluting, frescoes, inlays, and embossing.

Due to the actions of local Kami, winter is skipped in Áedla-ogtär Xluwuf.

The Leopard, Snow (Cat, Great) near Áedla-ogtär Xluwuf are known to be more aggressive than normal.

Áedla-ogtär Xluwuf's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in autumn and involves reenactments to channel Invocation energies of tier 2 via chanting.
