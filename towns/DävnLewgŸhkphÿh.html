Dävn Lewg Ÿhkphÿh (/ojˈvjib/ /luːg/ [beautiful] [prison; jail]) is a temperate Large City located in Lewÿqded Hold, Dähechanxya Kingdom, within the Undermountain.

The name Dävn Lewg Ÿhkphÿh is derived from the Sylvin language, as Dävn Lewg Ÿhkphÿh was founded by Zêbelè, who was culturaly Undermountain.

Climate

Dävn Lewg Ÿhkphÿh has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 7°C (44°F). Dävn Lewg Ÿhkphÿh receives an average of 239 cm/y (94 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. Dävn Lewg Ÿhkphÿh covers an area of nearly 164 km2 (65 mi2), and an average elevation of 2878 m (9442 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Dävn Lewg Ÿhkphÿh was founded durring the early 18th century in late summer of the year 1703, by Zêbelè. The establishment of Dävn Lewg Ÿhkphÿh was only bairly constructed. The sheer number of problems with its founding were enough to make several of the backers funding Dävn Lewg Ÿhkphÿh's construction back out of the project. Zêbelè pushed on reguardles, and Dävn Lewg Ÿhkphÿh was finished, but starts off as a terible place to live.

Dävn Lewg Ÿhkphÿh was built using the conventions of Undermountain durring the early 18th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Dävn Lewg Ÿhkphÿh is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature waddle and daub construction with good timber frames and a stone foundation protected by thatched or shingled rooves. Most buildings with second floors are built in such a way as to overhang into the streets on the upper floors for more space, as building size seems to be the primary indicator of wealth within the community. Most buildings are not decorated with any integral features, but instead use ivy, flowers, and other natural elements in planters of on trellices to breathe life into the structure they grow upon.

Dävn Lewg Ÿhkphÿh is buildings are arranged arround a network of spacious baked earthen streets which form a diagonal shaped grid, where each square verries in size given the proximity of the paralell streets forming each section. The ocasional smaller square has been used to construct a park, plaza, and other communal structures. The city has a set of well fortified walls, with gatehouses, watch towers, battlments, and even a moat, which are fashioned from stone and timber. Dävn Lewg Ÿhkphÿh's walls are, howeaver, fashioned from stone and timber. While unorthadox, the design looks to be functional to a reasonable degree. With luck, the untested design will remain untested for years to come. Unfortuantly, these unusual though effective defenses are in extreem disrepair, so much so that one cannot tell if they are decaying from a lack of mantance or damage incured.

Dävn Lewg Ÿhkphÿh is not quite well. Something happened here, perhapse recently, perhapse long ago. Whatever it was, it settled into the very soul of the city like a festering wound. The people go about their day well enough, but there’s a tention in the air you can cut with a knife. You get the terrible feeling that whatever it was, the wound it left will simply never heal. This city is as a necropolice.

Cultural Notes

Dävn Lewg Ÿhkphÿh's mayor's house was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is known for its buildings being predominantly formed through the combination of basic geometric shapes. However, it shown in the detailing given to the structures such as its characteristic tall columns, intricate detail, symmetry, harmony, and balance in their designs to an astonishing degree of precision. Decorative elements for the buildings tended to be built into the structure itself, making great use of fluting, frescoes, inlays, and embossing.

In Dävn Lewg Ÿhkphÿh grains of dust blow into perfectly neat rows.

The Thoqqua near Dävn Lewg Ÿhkphÿh are known to be almost tame, such that they can be put to domestic use.

Dävn Lewg Ÿhkphÿh's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in autumn and involves sex to channel Wild Magic energies of tier 2 via recitation of poetic epics.
