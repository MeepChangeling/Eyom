The name Rÿrtrÿh Väl Ckez is derived from the Dwarven language, as Rÿrtrÿh Väl Ckez was founded by Yakdraetrude Zacwafqehgbävle, who was culturaly Dwarvern.

Cultural Notes

Rÿrtrÿh Väl Ckez's chapel was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is known for its fluid and florid elaborate style, comprising ornate, asymmetric designs and pastel shades. It is often considered to be a playful, light style, which made exuberant use of curves and emphasized subtle asymmetry in the general shape of its structures. Walls, ceilings and moldings are decorated with numerous interlacing of curves and counter-curves based on the shapes of ‘C’ and ‘S’, along with shell forms and other naturalistic shapes.

The Mud Elemental, Medium near Rÿrtrÿh Väl Ckez are known to be almost tame, such that they can be put to domestic use.

Rÿrtrÿh Väl Ckez's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in summer and involves ritual combat to channel Truename Magic energies of tier 2 via throat chanting.
