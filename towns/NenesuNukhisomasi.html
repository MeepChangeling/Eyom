Nenesu Nukhisomasi (/ˈnuxi/ /soˈmasi/ [gauge] [drunk (inebriated)]) is a subtropical Town located in the Ůch Ḿåwa Moot of the Viceroyalty of Rosid.

The name Nenesu Nukhisomasi is derived from the Faeish language, as Nenesu Nukhisomasi was founded by Groukwaline H́åchú, who was culturaly Fae.

Climate

Nenesu Nukhisomasi has a yearly average temperature of 8°C (46°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -7°C (20°F). Nenesu Nukhisomasi receives an average of 146 cm/y (57 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Nenesu Nukhisomasi covers an area of nearly 5 km2 (2 mi2), and an average elevation of 4298 m (14101 ft) above sea level.

Civic Infrastructure

Nenesu Nukhisomasi has an Office of Civil Vicary, which is responsible for providing a livelyhood for all officialy recognised religious figures within Nenesu Nukhisomasi.

Nenesu Nukhisomasi has a Gravedigger's Guild, which is responsible for collecting the dead and laying them to rest according to all applicable laws and religious customs.

Nenesu Nukhisomasi has a Highwayman's Guild, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Nenesu Nukhisomasi has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain Nenesu Nukhisomasi's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

Nenesu Nukhisomasi possesses a Galvanic Power Grid, which brings galvanic current to most if not all buildings in town, and permits a great many consumer goods to function within the Town. Nenesu Nukhisomasi's grid is powered by a god's will and kindness.

Nenesu Nukhisomasi has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands.

Nenesu Nukhisomasi has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Cultural Notes

Nenesu Nukhisomasi's garrison was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is known for its fluid and florid elaborate style, comprising ornate, asymmetric designs and pastel shades. It is often considered to be a playful, light style, which made exuberant use of curves and emphasized subtle asymmetry in the general shape of its structures. Walls, ceilings and moldings are decorated with numerous interlacing of curves and counter-curves based on the shapes of ‘C’ and ‘S’, along with shell forms and other naturalistic shapes.

In Nenesu Nukhisomasi there is a constant smell of overcooked presumably ethnic food.

The Witchcrow near Nenesu Nukhisomasi are known to be a mutant strain of the creature.

Nenesu Nukhisomasi's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in summer and involves consuming a local toxin to channel Wild Magic energies of tier 3 via mimery.

Nenesu Nukhisomasi is uncommonly rich, not only for the gentry but for the common citizens as well.
