Groqüe Nuzvǟ Qo̠ (/groq˔/ /ˈnuzvaː/ [blue; dark blue] [remains; remnant]) is a subtropical Large City located in Vdrustri Parish, Vaajha Deanery, within the Covenent of Irus.

The name Groqüe Nuzvǟ Qo̠ is derived from the Wood Elvish language, as Groqüe Nuzvǟ Qo̠ was founded by Rhobudve Ziezamrad, who was culturaly Wood Elven.

Climate

Groqüe Nuzvǟ Qo̠ has a yearly average temperature of 21°C (69°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 17°C (62°F). Groqüe Nuzvǟ Qo̠ receives an average of 220 cm/y (86 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. Groqüe Nuzvǟ Qo̠ covers an area of nearly 333 km2 (133 mi2), and an average elevation of 6506 m (21345 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Groqüe Nuzvǟ Qo̠ was founded durring the late 17th century in early summer of the year 1579, by Rhobudve Ziezamrad. The establishment of Groqüe Nuzvǟ Qo̠ was only bairly constructed. The sheer number of problems with its founding were enough to make several of the backers funding Groqüe Nuzvǟ Qo̠'s construction back out of the project. Rhobudve Ziezamrad pushed on reguardles, and Groqüe Nuzvǟ Qo̠ was finished, but starts off as a terible place to live.

Groqüe Nuzvǟ Qo̠ was built using the conventions of Wood Elven durring the late 17th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Groqüe Nuzvǟ Qo̠ is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature masoned stone construction which prominantly features pointed arches, pointed ribbed vault cielings, flying buttress', and window tracery all of which share a simmilar gemoetetic patern halfway between organic and inorganic in design formaing a very distinct aesthetically integrated style. BUildings tend to reach for the havens, and more expencive homes are easily identified by their floor count as well as the addition of decorative features intigrated into the building's design such as statues, gargoyals, and embelished joinery.

Groqüe Nuzvǟ Qo̠ is was constructed arround several restrictive packed earth mainstreets which cross one another at certain axies, with smaller streets branching off of them to premit acess to the many buildings deeper into the road network. The overall fashion is remenessent of a circulatory system, or other organic construct, and is quite effishent in its design. The hillside city is the proud owner of a thick set of fortified walls fashioned from querried stone blocks. While not up to snuff for a fort or castle wall, the city's walls are naturaly much larger than those of forts or castles. Therefore, the construction such a wall is most expencive. Groqüe Nuzvǟ Qo̠'s buget focused wall would serve its community well in battle in spite of looking unimpressive compared to castles and fortresses. Groqüe Nuzvǟ Qo̠'s top tier civilian fortifications are in an unremarkable state. To some, this is the ideal sate for defences to be in. In need of absoutly nothing, and ready to serve the city as needed.

Before you’ve even set foot into the heart of Groqüe Nuzvǟ Qo̠, you can smell it. The incense. It hangs about the town like a cloud. Monks, priests, and clerics are everywhere, all dedicated to the same god, all preforming the same rituals to bless and anoint building,s streets, people, animals, you name it they are or have blessed it. The same holysymbols is everywhere too. Its on buildings, on people, and even branded into livestock. This city certainly loves its god.

They are not to be confused with the Office of Civil Groundskeeping as they do not hold authority over nor responsibility for Groqüe Nuzvǟ Qo̠'s natural decorations nor waterways.

Groqüe Nuzvǟ Qo̠ has a Guild of Roadworkers, who are responsible for maintaining the roadways and public paths within town. They also have the duty of enforcing all civil laws relating to the roadways.

Groqüe Nuzvǟ Qo̠ has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Groqüe Nuzvǟ Qo̠ has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Groqüe Nuzvǟ Qo̠ is home to a University which provides higher education in a variety of fields, and also serves as a research institute for those same fields.

Cultural Notes

Groqüe Nuzvǟ Qo̠'s chapel was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is characterized by their massive, monolithic and 'blocky' appearance with a rigid geometric style. It was best known for its rough, unfinished surfaces, unusual shapes, heavy-looking materials, straight lines, and small windows. Modular elements were often used to form masses representing specific functional zones, grouped into a unified whole.

The Mud Elemental, Medium near Groqüe Nuzvǟ Qo̠ are known to be more aggressive than normal.

Groqüe Nuzvǟ Qo̠'s citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in spring and involves line dance to channel Transmutation energies of tier 1 via oath swearing.

The locals often repair the road by putting down wooden decking.
