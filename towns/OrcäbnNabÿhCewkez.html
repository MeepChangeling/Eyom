Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez ([root words not on file] [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical Metropolis located in Ckonx̄rävebehrt Hold, Kizir Province, within the United Kingdom of Undermountain.

The name Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez is derived from the Tauric language, as Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez was founded by Nrê Prodger, who was culturaly Tauric.

Climate

Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez has a yearly average temperature of 9°C (48°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -4°C (25°F). Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez receives an average of 182 cm/y (71 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez covers an area of nearly 28 km2 (11 mi2), and an average elevation of 2292 m (7519 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez was founded durring the early 12th century in summer of the year 1141, by Nrê Prodger. The establishment of Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez was only bairly constructed. The sheer number of problems with its founding were enough to make several of the backers funding Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez's construction back out of the project. Nrê Prodger pushed on reguardles, and Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez was finished, but starts off as a terible place to live.

Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez was built using the conventions of Tauric durring the early 12th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez is no diffrent. The metropolis's buildings feature timber and earth construction, with most buildigns first floors resembling mounds of earth, with subsequent floors appearing as elaborate log cabins, with each building forming a tiered pyramid of sorts fromed from the stack of rectangular, peek roofed cabins. Most wooden bracing, support, and trim is carved with decorative knotwork, and the larger structures even feature painted trim which emphasises the knotwork.

Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez is buildings are arranged arround a single restrictive paverstone mainstreet with many smaller streets branching off of it which gives the metropolis a over all rectangular shape, albit one warped and twisted by the nature of the curves of the main road. The lakeside metropolis is the proud owner of a thick set of fortified walls fashioned from querried stone blocks. While not up to snuff for a fort or castle wall, the metropolis's walls are naturaly much larger than those of forts or castles. Therefore, the construction such a wall is most expencive. Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez's buget focused wall would serve its community well in battle in spite of looking unimpressive compared to castles and fortresses. Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez's top tier civilian fortifications has sufferd soem light damage, reducing its function a little in some spots, but could almsot certainly preform as expected... Though some of the worse spots could lead to the loss of defenders lives if attackers identified the weaknesses ahead of time.

Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez has the unmistakable air of a metropolis on its last legs. Everything is a bit slipshod and ramshackle. Everyone is at work, or drinking. No one has anything in their eyes other than fear and despair. Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez ’s existence was once fed by scholarly pursuits. But the income once drawn in by the works of scribes and academics has dried up and the metropolis is drifting down the stream of history as it dries up. Everywhere about the metropolis one can see abandoned schools, libraries, and other academic structures. Locals can be overheard having academic discussions, as well as talking about scholarly subjects in general. It’s quite clear the metropolis places a lot of value on education and being a learned individual. Even in these times.

Civic Infrastructure

Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez possesses a Aethary Link for its accademic, government, and financial institutions. Public Aethary access is available through one or more of these intitutions.

Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez has an animal control department which works to enforce local ordinances relating to the control, impoundment, and disposition of animals.

Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez has an Office of Civil Groundskeeping, which works to enforce local ordinances relating to the construction and upkeep up of all plant life, water features, and other natural decorations within Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez. They are also responsible for the maintenance of these features. Notably, the OCG is not responsible for Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez's parks.

Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez has an Office of Civil Vicary, which is responsible for providing a livelyhood for all officialy recognised religious figures within Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez.

Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez has a government-funded child care program, overseen by the local Department of Nursemaids, which is responsible for providing childcare to working-class citizens according to local ordinances.

Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez has a Gravedigger's Guild, which is responsible for collecting the dead and laying them to rest according to all applicable laws and religious customs.

Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez has a Guild of Nurses, which is tasked with caring for the elderly and infirm in accordance with local ordinances, religious values, and customs.

Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez has a Department of Firefighters, which is responsible for organizing fire fighting efforts during a fire and enforcing local ordinances relating to fire safety.

Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez has a Highwayman's Guild, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez has a Hall of Slayers, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez has an Arts Academy which provides higher education in many fields including math, language arts, philosophy, engineering, and other such disciplines.

Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez possesses a Galvanic Power Grid, which brings galvanic current to most if not all buildings in town, and permits a great many consumer goods to function within the Metropolis. Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez's grid is powered by a god's will and kindness.

Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez possesses an older civil lighting system consisting of street lamps. In spite of the Galvanic Grid, these lights continue to use their old fule sources to provide nighttime illumination to all city streets.

Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez has a first rate hospital which caters to anyone in need of long term medical care.

Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez has a library, which keeps a large collection of books, scrolls, and archives all manner of physical items. The library is open to the public, including the Aether Link.

Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands. They are not to be confused with the Office of Civil Groundskeeping as they do not hold authority over nor responsibility for Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez's natural decorations nor waterways.

Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez has a Guild of Roadworkers, who are responsible for maintaining the roadways and public paths within town. They also have the duty of enforcing all civil laws relating to the roadways.

Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez is home to a University which provides higher education in a variety of fields, and also serves as a research institute for those same fields.

Cultural Notes

One or more crime bosses have a powerful influence within Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez. They may control crime within the community itself, or they may use it simply as a safe haven from which to direct their minions elsewhere. Local law enforcement may know all about them, but lack the strength to confront them and their paid or intimidated henchmen.

Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez's town hall was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used embraces individualism and experimentation. It emerged as a movement against traditional, classical styles and sought to make buildings dynamic and fun while breaking the rules. The style incorporated elements of previous architectural styles in exaggerated and whimsical ways. Traditional, conservative leanings were void in this era, with most scholars of architecture agreeing it was a time of “anything goes.”.

In Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez yeast remains dormant.

The Gremlin near Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez are known to be quite timid.

Orcäbn Nabÿh Cewkez's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in spring and involves gestures to channel Augury energies of tier 1 via recitation of poetic epics.
