Zě Jn-59k Hǐb (/waɪt/ /hɪb/ [Propername] [jewel; gem]) is a subtropical Large City located in Yǐslomæběs Zone, Bh-732fia Province, within the Federation of Alveria.

The name Zě Jn-59k Hǐb is derived from the Constructi language, as Zě Jn-59k Hǐb was founded by Baradeth Brtconkdo, who was culturaly Constructi.

Climate

Zě Jn-59k Hǐb has a yearly average temperature of 31°C (87°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 36°C (96°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 27°C (80°F). Zě Jn-59k Hǐb receives an average of 205 cm/y (80 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. Zě Jn-59k Hǐb covers an area of nearly 269 km2 (107 mi2), and an average elevation of 10140 m (-9681 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Zě Jn-59k Hǐb was founded durring the late 14th century in summer of the year 1352, by Baradeth Brtconkdo. The establishment of Zě Jn-59k Hǐb was somewhat plagued by a lack of willing colonists, leading to Baradeth Brtconkdo electing to pay people to resettle in Zě Jn-59k Hǐb.

Zě Jn-59k Hǐb was built using the conventions of Constructi durring the late 14th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Zě Jn-59k Hǐb is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature timber framed wooden shiethed or brick construction, which gives form to a very formalized, rational, expence effishent arcatectural style based on strictly symmetrical designs which universaly feature pitched roofs, shutters, and the occasional column or pilaster for a decorative touch.

Zě Jn-59k Hǐb is buildings are arranged arrounded a highly ordered system of restrictive cobblestone streets which form triangular paterns, allowing the incides of the octagons to be common grounds for the buildings on the edges, be it for parkland, yardspace, plazas, or markets. The city is the proud owner of a thick set of fortified walls fashioned from querried stone blocks. While not up to snuff for a fort or castle wall, the city's walls are naturaly much larger than those of forts or castles. Therefore, the construction such a wall is most expencive. Zě Jn-59k Hǐb's buget focused wall would serve its community well in battle in spite of looking unimpressive compared to castles and fortresses. Zě Jn-59k Hǐb's top tier civilian fortifications are in an unremarkable state. To some, this is the ideal sate for defences to be in. In need of absoutly nothing, and ready to serve the city as needed.

Zě Jn-59k Hǐb has a very calm atmosphere. People can be seen relaxing, scocilizing, and going about all manner of business other than the daily grind. Men, women, children, all can be seen enjoying life in a laid-back way in the many parks which line Zě Jn-59k Hǐb’s streets.

Civic Infrastructure

They are also responsible for the maintenance of these features. Notably, the OCG is not responsible for Zě Jn-59k Hǐb's parks.

Zě Jn-59k Hǐb has an Office of Civil Vicary, which is responsible for providing a livelyhood for all officialy recognised religious figures within Zě Jn-59k Hǐb.

Zě Jn-59k Hǐb has a Gravedigger's Guild, which is responsible for collecting the dead and laying them to rest according to all applicable laws and religious customs.

Zě Jn-59k Hǐb has a Guild of Nurses, which is tasked with caring for the elderly and infirm in accordance with local ordinances, religious values, and customs.

Zě Jn-59k Hǐb has a Department of Firefighters, which is responsible for organizing fire fighting efforts during a fire and enforcing local ordinances relating to fire safety.

Zě Jn-59k Hǐb has a Highwayman's Guild, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Zě Jn-59k Hǐb has a Hall of Slayers, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Zě Jn-59k Hǐb has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain Zě Jn-59k Hǐb's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

Zě Jn-59k Hǐb has an Arts Academy which provides higher education in many fields including math, language arts, philosophy, engineering, and other such disciplines.

Zě Jn-59k Hǐb possesses a Galvanic Power Grid, which brings galvanic current to most if not all buildings in town, and permits a great many consumer goods to function within the Large City. Zě Jn-59k Hǐb's grid is powered by hydrogalvanic generators.

Zě Jn-59k Hǐb possesses an older civil lighting system consisting of street lamps. In spite of the Galvanic Grid, these lights continue to use their old fule sources to provide nighttime illumination to all city streets.

Zě Jn-59k Hǐb has a first rate hospital which caters to anyone in need of long term medical care.

Zě Jn-59k Hǐb has a library, which keeps a large collection of books, scrolls, and archives all manner of physical items. While not open to the public, the librarians and scholars employed by the library will assist anyone with their research needs, and wealthy individuals can purchase membership to access the library's materials themselves. In spite of being generally closed to the public, the library has a room with several Aether Linked devices available to the public during business hours.

Zě Jn-59k Hǐb has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands. They are not to be confused with the Office of Civil Groundskeeping as they do not hold authority over nor responsibility for Zě Jn-59k Hǐb's natural decorations nor waterways.

Zě Jn-59k Hǐb has a Guild of Roadworkers, who are responsible for maintaining the roadways and public paths within town. They also have the duty of enforcing all civil laws relating to the roadways.

Zě Jn-59k Hǐb has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Zě Jn-59k Hǐb has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Zě Jn-59k Hǐb is home to a University which provides higher education in a variety of fields, and also serves as a research institute for those same fields.

Cultural Notes

Zě Jn-59k Hǐb's mayor's house was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is best known for its striking sculptural forms and often dazzling ornamental detail that characterizes the buildings general shape. The radiant colors, rich patterns, and symmetrical silhouettes employed by this style were backed up by rich decorative features including gardens, courtyards, extruded arches, domes, pointed domes, vaulted ceilings, elaborate painted and inlaid designs, and decorative sculptures.
