Yache Zehbaph Eb Ÿhkphÿh (/dujc/ /zɪ̘ˈbaf eb/ [blank] [upper class]) is a subtropical Large City located in the Ekÿckyewl̄ärt Provence of the Hobben.

The name Yache Zehbaph Eb Ÿhkphÿh is derived from the Goblin language, as Yache Zehbaph Eb Ÿhkphÿh was founded by Hydronodon, who was culturaly Undermountain.

Yache Zehbaph Eb Ÿhkphÿh has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 20°C (68°F). Yache Zehbaph Eb Ÿhkphÿh receives an average of 137 cm/y (53 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. Yache Zehbaph Eb Ÿhkphÿh covers an area of nearly 95 km2 (38 mi2), and an average elevation of 3236 m (10616 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Yache Zehbaph Eb Ÿhkphÿh was founded durring the early 11th century, by Hydronodon. The establishment of the new community went well, with no major obsticles durring construction.

Yache Zehbaph Eb Ÿhkphÿh was built using the conventions of Undermountain durring the early 11th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Yache Zehbaph Eb Ÿhkphÿh is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature timber framed wooden shiethed or brick construction, which gives form to a very formalized, rational, expence effishent arcatectural style based on strictly symmetrical designs which universaly feature pitched roofs, shutters, and the occasional column or pilaster for a decorative touch.

Yache Zehbaph Eb Ÿhkphÿh is buildings are located arround a single broad baked earthen mainstreet which forms a clockwise spiral to give the city a over all circular shape. The city resides behind a palisade wall complete with battlments, a moat, and timber gatehouses with drawbridges. The robustly designed timber walls has suffered a visible ammount of structural damage, leaving them effectivly useless. One can't help but wonder why the has not yet effected repairs.

A look around Yache Zehbaph Eb Ÿhkphÿh seems to be home to a quite vibrant and boisterous community. Everywhere one looks they can see people going out their daily business with a smile and a spring in their step. Children play loudly in the streets, causing untold havoc as youth are want and allowed to do. On second glance, that chaos continues into adulthood. People seem to be allowed to do as they please with little harmoney to anything. It feels less like a city, and more like a spot people just happened to place their homes. Yet there are small elements here and there which show the underlying structure of the community. It’s just so complex, organic, and flowing one can only understand what is a piece of the puzzle, but not what its neighbors are.

Cultural Notes

Yache Zehbaph Eb Ÿhkphÿh's town hall was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is characterized by vertical proportions, pointed arches, external buttressing, and asymmetry in the general shape of its buildings. The decorative features of the style were key, consisting of large arched windows, pointed arches, vaulted ceilings, flying buttresses, and sculptures integrated into the structure itself. Occasionally, for very important buildings, an array of sculptures or one colossal sculpture might replace the entirety of the entrance to said building.

In Yache Zehbaph Eb Ÿhkphÿh rainbows form quite often above the Large City.

The Frog, Poisonous Swarm near Yache Zehbaph Eb Ÿhkphÿh are known to be almost tame, such that they can be put to domestic use.

Yache Zehbaph Eb Ÿhkphÿh's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in autumn and involves bloodletting to channel Enchantment energies of tier 3 via throat singing.

History

In time immemorial, reportedly some time during the late 2nd century, Yache Zehbaph Eb Ÿhkphÿh was attacked by soldiers from another nation, waging a greater campaign. The details of the conflict are hazy at best due to many conflicting accounts. What is known is Yache Zehbaph Eb Ÿhkphÿh lost 217 people, 131 livestock, and 47 buildings. The conflict ended after roughly 101, when members of Yache Zehbaph Eb Ÿhkphÿh's militia enacted an operation to locate a particular irregulars group. The operation was complicated by a trusted officer who turned traitor and defects. The conflict ended with pitched battle between both forces, which ended in victory for Yache Zehbaph Eb Ÿhkphÿh's forces. The war is remembered in legend by Yache Zehbaph Eb Ÿhkphÿh's bards, historians, and legend keepers.
