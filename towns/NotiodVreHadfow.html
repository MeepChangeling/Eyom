Notiod Vre Hadfow (/ˈnotjod/ /vre/ [sexual] [fat (bodily substance); fat (component in food)]) is a subtropical Large City located in the Nogrehie̜w Moot of the Rosid.

The name Notiod Vre Hadfow is derived from the Sylvin language, as Notiod Vre Hadfow was founded by Dyënkdo, who was culturaly Wood Elf.

Climate

Notiod Vre Hadfow has a yearly average temperature of 10°C (50°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a cool 12°C (53°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 9°C (48°F). Notiod Vre Hadfow receives an average of 229 cm/y (90 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Notiod Vre Hadfow covers an area of nearly 76 km2 (30 mi2), and an average elevation of 2822 m (9258 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Notiod Vre Hadfow was founded durring the late 16th century in summer of the year 1467, by Dyënkdo. The establishment of Notiod Vre Hadfow was only bairly constructed. The sheer number of problems with its founding were enough to make several of the backers funding Notiod Vre Hadfow's construction back out of the project. Dyënkdo pushed on reguardles, and Notiod Vre Hadfow was finished, but starts off as a terible place to live.

Notiod Vre Hadfow was built using the conventions of Wood Elf durring the late 16th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Notiod Vre Hadfow is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature timber framed wooden shiethed or brick construction, which gives form to a very formalized, rational, expence effishent arcatectural style based on strictly symmetrical designs which universaly feature pitched roofs, shutters, and the occasional column or pilaster for a decorative touch.

Notiod Vre Hadfow is buildings are arranged within a network of broad packed earth streets which form a rectangular grid, where each block verries in size given the proximity of the paralell streets forming each section. The ocasional smaller block has been used to construct a park, plaza, and other communal structures. The city has a set of well fortified walls, with gatehouses, watch towers, battlments, and even a moat, which are fashioned from stone and timber. Notiod Vre Hadfow's walls are, howeaver, fashioned from stone and timber. While unorthadox, the design looks to be functional to a reasonable degree. With luck, the untested design will remain untested for years to come. The city's unusual though effective defenses are visibly old, but also obviously maintained semi-regularly. Its likly the local malishia or garrison are tasked with ocasional mantance of the citys defences.

A look around Notiod Vre Hadfow seems to be home to a quite vibrant and boisterous community. Everywhere one looks they can see people going out their daily business with a smile and a spring in their step. Children play loudly in the streets, causing untold havoc as youth are want and allowed to do.

History

In time immemorial, reportedly some time during the early 2nd century the Māshzsíhv Woodlands was struck by a drought when Tarn Saron grew too small to continue feeding Brook Boundless, which reduced the available water supply nationwide, but was especially harsh in the area arround Notiod Vre Hadfow. As the neighboring regions had no water so spare, Notiod Vre Hadfow lost 163 people, and 379 livestock in the disaster.. The drought lasted for 6 weeks, which are remembered as the Horror Thirst.
