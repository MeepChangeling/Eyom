Qÿhtvehb Odnotvopmaf Eq (/odˌnotvopˈmaf/ /iq/ [buxom] [shape]) is a temperate Large City located in Ewntewhäny Hold, Yazcarfeh Kingdom, within the Undermountain.

The name Qÿhtvehb Odnotvopmaf Eq is derived from the Sylvin language, as Qÿhtvehb Odnotvopmaf Eq was founded by Tsmê, who was culturaly Undermountain.

Climate

Qÿhtvehb Odnotvopmaf Eq has a yearly average temperature of 17°C (62°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 10°C (50°F). Qÿhtvehb Odnotvopmaf Eq receives an average of 279 cm/y (109 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. Qÿhtvehb Odnotvopmaf Eq covers an area of nearly 188 km2 (75 mi2), and an average elevation of 2788 m (9146 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Qÿhtvehb Odnotvopmaf Eq was founded durring the late 11th century, by Tsmê. The establishment of Qÿhtvehb Odnotvopmaf Eq was somewhat plagued by a lack of willing colonists, leading to Tsmê electing to pay people to resettle in Qÿhtvehb Odnotvopmaf Eq.

Qÿhtvehb Odnotvopmaf Eq was built using the conventions of Undermountain durring the late 11th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Qÿhtvehb Odnotvopmaf Eq is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature waddle and daub construction with good timber frames and a stone foundation protected by thatched or shingled rooves. Most buildings with second floors are built in such a way as to overhang into the streets on the upper floors for more space, as building size seems to be the primary indicator of wealth within the community. Most buildings are not decorated with any integral features, but instead use ivy, flowers, and other natural elements in planters of on trellices to breathe life into the structure they grow upon.

Qÿhtvehb Odnotvopmaf Eq is is constructed arround a series of broad baked earthen mainstreets which form overlapping circles, with smaller strait roads linking the circiles to eachother at varrious points. The city is defended by arcane means. It's hard to spot at first, but there's a tell tell shimmer in the air arround Qÿhtvehb Odnotvopmaf Eq, and you can spot the ocasional warding glyph carved into a rock or tree all arround town. These mystical defences are ancient, unknowable, and unassailable by current means... Assuming everything is in working order. Otherwise, the wards are little more than a deathtrap. Qÿhtvehb Odnotvopmaf Eq's Relic of the World That Was has sufferd soem light damage, reducing its function a little in some spots, but could almsot certainly preform as expected... Though some of the worse spots could lead to the loss of defenders lives if attackers identified the weaknesses ahead of time.

A look around Qÿhtvehb Odnotvopmaf Eq is like looking into a university’s plaza on the day before an exam. People rush about to-and-fro. Discarded scrolls and quills are dotted around city. In spite of many locals discussing academic topics, there’s little sign of any organized groups for any of that talk. Infact, the city seems disordered in general with everything scattered helter skelter about.

Cultural Notes

Qÿhtvehb Odnotvopmaf Eq's locals are divided into several castes. They may be organized by social role, by imputed nobility of birth, by ethnic origins, or any other dividing principle, but they cannot imagine any other way of organizing themselves. A hierarchy of castes is not inevitable, but there will be social and legal limits applied to ensure that each caste remains fixed in its function. The outside world may or may not respect these distinctions when dealing with the locals.

Qÿhtvehb Odnotvopmaf Eq's mayor's house was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is characterized by grandeur of scale, simplicity of geometric forms, dramatic use of columns, and a preference for blank walls. This design ethos extended to re-imagining earlier styles of structure to create them anew, with a similar overall look and feel to one another..

In Qÿhtvehb Odnotvopmaf Eq the water is caffeinated.

The Owl near Qÿhtvehb Odnotvopmaf Eq are known to be a mutant strain of the creature.

Qÿhtvehb Odnotvopmaf Eq's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in summer and involves square dance to channel Abjuration energies of tier 1 via chanting.
