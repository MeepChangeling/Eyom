Iyuzeld Tmurn-sæ̈t (/tmurn/ /sæ̝t/ [frequent] [soil; clay]) is a temperate Small Town located in the Ftuzmïlärtä Region of the Confederation of Goblin Tribes.

The name Iyuzeld Tmurn-sæ̈t is derived from the Gnollish language, as Iyuzeld Tmurn-sæ̈t was founded by Adelen vyim Roxlendnreys, who was culturaly Gnollish.

Climate

Iyuzeld Tmurn-sæ̈t has a yearly average temperature of 10°C (50°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a cold 5°C (41°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 16°C (60°F). Iyuzeld Tmurn-sæ̈t receives an average of 254 cm/y (99 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Iyuzeld Tmurn-sæ̈t covers an area of nearly 4 km2 (1 mi2), and an average elevation of 3510 m (11515 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Iyuzeld Tmurn-sæ̈t was founded durring the late 9th century, by Adelen vyim Roxlendnreys. The establishment of Iyuzeld Tmurn-sæ̈t suffered from many setbacks, delays, and obsticles, most notably a group of Iyuzeld Tmurn-sæ̈t which required millitary assistance exterminate before the community could finish being built.

Iyuzeld Tmurn-sæ̈t was built using the conventions of Gnollish durring the late 9th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Iyuzeld Tmurn-sæ̈t is no diffrent. The town's buildings feature timber framed wooden shiethed or brick construction, which gives form to a very formalized, rational, expence effishent arcatectural style based on strictly symmetrical designs which universaly feature pitched roofs, shutters, and the occasional column or pilaster for a decorative touch.

Iyuzeld Tmurn-sæ̈t is buildings are arranged arrounded highly ordered system of premissive paverstone streets which form hexical paterns, allowing the incides of the octagons to be common grounds for the buildings on the edges, be it for parkland, yardspace, plazas, or markets. The town has a defencive wall made from large clay bricks. The wall is constructed to the exact specifications of millitary fortifications, but the nature of its clay brick construction leaves it vulnerable to even outdated siege equipment. That said, the town is well defended against anything short of an army. Iyuzeld Tmurn-sæ̈t's brittle defences are in an unremarkable state. To some, this is the ideal sate for defences to be in. In need of absoutly nothing, and ready to serve the town as needed.

A look around Iyuzeld Tmurn-sæ̈t makes it abundantly clear the town suffered something horrible some time ago. It's as if the town itself is depressed. Smiles are few, cheer is nowhere to be had. Everyone quietly goes about their daily business not looking anyone in the eye.

Cultural Notes

Iyuzeld Tmurn-sæ̈t's bank was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is characterized by grandeur of scale, simplicity of geometric forms, dramatic use of columns, and a preference for blank walls. This design ethos extended to re-imagining earlier styles of structure to create them anew, with a similar overall look and feel to one another..

Due to the actions of local Kami, spring is recurring in Iyuzeld Tmurn-sæ̈t.

The Marble Snake near Iyuzeld Tmurn-sæ̈t are known to be more aggressive than normal.

Iyuzeld Tmurn-sæ̈t's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in autumn and involves gestures to channel Abjuration energies of tier 2 via proclamations.
