Chyameya Chyibû S̺æqû (/ˈcibʊ/ /ˈs̺æqʊ/ [sophisticated] [brothel]) is a subtropical Large City located in Bu̹yu Bàyâ Kingdom, Rûlichyû Îyà Empire, within the Warren.

The name Chyameya Chyibû S̺æqû is derived from the Sylvin language, as Chyameya Chyibû S̺æqû was founded by Mb̪f̄̋sh Cānvó̄ 'Martin Dazzle' Cōkī́̄ Vúchīń̄ Mbó̄̌s, who was culturaly Rattu.

Climate

Chyameya Chyibû S̺æqû has a yearly average temperature of 18°C (64°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 11°C (51°F). Chyameya Chyibû S̺æqû receives an average of 161 cm/y (63 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. Chyameya Chyibû S̺æqû covers an area of nearly 235 km2 (94 mi2), and an average elevation of 1402 m (4599 ft) above sea level.

Cultural Notes

Chyameya Chyibû S̺æqû's town hall was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is best known for the combined use of arcuated and trabeated construction, employing arches and constructed with post and lintel. The arch served as the style's chief structural element, with flanking columns serving as buttresses or decorations. While the general shape of their structures could be generously described as simplistic, the use of these arches and columns created a most distinct ascetic which is easily distinguishable on sight to armatures and experts alike.

In Chyameya Chyibû S̺æqû most nights are accompanied by colorful ribbons of light in the sky.

The Flea, Giant near Chyameya Chyibû S̺æqû are known to be almost tame, such that they can be put to domestic use.

Chyameya Chyibû S̺æqû's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in summer and involves consuming a local narcotic to channel Necromancy energies of tier 3 via oath swearing.
