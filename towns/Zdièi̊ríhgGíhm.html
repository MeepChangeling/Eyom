Zdièi̊ríhg Gíhm (Zdiè /ˈi̘rɪ̞g/ [maze] [service]) is a subtropical Large City located in the Hujdàj Region of the Tetburland.

The name Zdièi̊ríhg Gíhm is derived from the Goblin language, as Zdièi̊ríhg Gíhm was founded by Tsèb, who was culturaly Tauran.

Climate

Zdièi̊ríhg Gíhm has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 30°C (86°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 18°C (64°F). Zdièi̊ríhg Gíhm receives an average of 290 cm/y (114 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. Zdièi̊ríhg Gíhm covers an area of nearly 247 km2 (98 mi2), and an average elevation of 1260 m (4133 ft) above sea level.
