Fåt Sheckyä Cewkez (/fɑːt/ /s̼eˈkjə/ [sudden; abrupt; swift] [raid]) is a subtropical Large City located in the Nazah Shire of the Kingdom of Hobben.

The name Fåt Sheckyä Cewkez is derived from the Dwarven language, as Fåt Sheckyä Cewkez was founded by Tshêthèdo Åxruqedrayd, who was culturaly Dwarvern.

Climate

Fåt Sheckyä Cewkez has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). Fåt Sheckyä Cewkez receives an average of 155 cm/y (61 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. Fåt Sheckyä Cewkez covers an area of nearly 339 km2 (135 mi2), and an average elevation of 7146 m (-19504 ft) above sea level.

Fåt Sheckyä Cewkez was founded durring the early 15th century in fall of the year 1431, by Tshêthèdo Åxruqedrayd. The establishment of Fåt Sheckyä Cewkez was plagued by a lack of willing colonists. After attempts to pay people to resettle failed Tshêthèdo Åxruqedrayd struck deals with nearby nations and communities to establish Fåt Sheckyä Cewkez as a prison colony.

Fåt Sheckyä Cewkez has the unmistakable air of a city on its last legs. Everything is a bit slipshod and ramshackle. Everyone is at work, or drinking. Cultural Notes

The locals are in a state of despair and dull apathy. They've lost the things that used to give them pride and hope, with the best among them carrying on out of habitual duty and the worst giving ready hands to shameful deeds and ignoble acts. No one really believes the future can be better, and most seek only to satisfy immediate appetites.

Fåt Sheckyä Cewkez's bank was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is which made use of the classical orders and mathematically precise ratios of height and width combined with a desire for symmetry, proportion, and harmony. It used columns, pediments, arches and domes are imaginatively in buildings of all types. Decorative features were seen as largely unnecessary as the sheer beauty of the structure itself was often close to art. However, many buildings with large ceiling spaces had their ceilings decorated with elaborate paintings, simply because the large flat spaces could feel wasted.

In Fåt Sheckyä Cewkez the stars are always right.

The Copsewight near Fåt Sheckyä Cewkez are known to be a mutant strain of the creature.

Fåt Sheckyä Cewkez's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in autumn and involves gestures to channel Charm energies of tier 2 via recitation of scripture.
