Qÿhtvehb Cä Rewd (/kə/ /ruːd/ [thin )slender); slim] [bell]) is a subtropical Large City located in Qäncäq Hold, Dähechanxya Kingdom, within the Undermountain.

The name Qÿhtvehb Cä Rewd is derived from the Sylvin language, as Qÿhtvehb Cä Rewd was founded by Theddu, who was culturaly Undermountain.

Qÿhtvehb Cä Rewd has a yearly average temperature of 15°C (59°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 6°C (42°F). Qÿhtvehb Cä Rewd receives an average of 107 cm/y (42 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Qÿhtvehb Cä Rewd covers an area of nearly 107 km2 (42 mi2), and an average elevation of 10950 m (-7024 ft) above sea level.

After attempts to pay people to resettle failed Theddu struck deals with nearby nations and communities to establish Qÿhtvehb Cä Rewd as a prison colony.

Orderly arrangements of columns, pilasters and lintels, as well as the use of semicircular arches, hemispherical domes, niches and aediculae can be found everywhere such that only size of building and yard can be used to measure the general prosparity of a given building's owners due to a general wealthy feeling the style gives off.

The city has a set of well fortified walls, with gatehouses, watch towers, battlments, and even a moat, which are fashioned from stone and timber. Qÿhtvehb Cä Rewd's walls are, howeaver, fashioned from stone and timber. While unorthadox, the design looks to be functional to a reasonable degree. With luck, the untested design will remain untested for years to come. The city's unusual though effective defenses are visibly old, but also obviously maintained semi-regularly. Cultural Notes

Qÿhtvehb Cä Rewd's bank was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is characterized by vertical proportions, pointed arches, external buttressing, and asymmetry in the general shape of its buildings. The decorative features of the style were key, consisting of large arched windows, pointed arches, vaulted ceilings, flying buttresses, and sculptures integrated into the structure itself. Occasionally, for very important buildings, an array of sculptures or one colossal sculpture might replace the entirety of the entrance to said building.

In Qÿhtvehb Cä Rewd rain occurs in reverse with water pooling up from the earth then falling into the skies to from clouds, this unsettles every visitor that sees it.

The Bumblebee, Giant near Qÿhtvehb Cä Rewd are known to be quite timid.

Qÿhtvehb Cä Rewd's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in summer and involves sacrificing an animal to channel Wild Magic energies of tier 3 via throat singing.

History

Qÿhtvehb Cä Rewd is still bloodied by a recent violent conflict. A crushing bandit raid, a lost siege, getting caught at the periphery of a major battle, or some other calamity has inflicted severe damage on the place. Some communities may suffer a longer-term version of this, their youths lost in a grinding, endless battle against some perpetual threat.

In time immemorial, reportedly some time during the early 2nd century heavy spring rains following an uncharacteristically dry year and a minor earthquake resulted in the collapse of the north side of Toluca Valley. the landslide struck Qÿhtvehb Cä Rewd, and devastated the community. 154 people, 346 livestock, and 59 buildings were lost to the calamity. The disaster is recorded in history as Terror's Fall.
