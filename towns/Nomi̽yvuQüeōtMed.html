Nomi̽yvu Qüeōt Med (/noˈmi̽jvu/ /q˔oːt/ [bright] [experiment]) is a subtropical Large City located in the Drelwen Earldom of the Viceroyalty of Rosid.

The name Nomi̽yvu Qüeōt Med is derived from the Wood Elvish language, as Nomi̽yvu Qüeōt Med was founded by Mog̈î Nizanrel, who was culturaly Wood Elven.

Climate

Nomi̽yvu Qüeōt Med has a yearly average temperature of 13°C (55°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 1°C (33°F). Nomi̽yvu Qüeōt Med receives an average of 149 cm/y (58 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Nomi̽yvu Qüeōt Med covers an area of nearly 273 km2 (109 mi2), and an average elevation of 4604 m (15104 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Nomi̽yvu Qüeōt Med was founded durring the late 11th century, by Mog̈î Nizanrel. The establishment of Nomi̽yvu Qüeōt Med suffered from many setbacks, delays, and obsticles, most notably a group of Nomi̽yvu Qüeōt Med which required millitary assistance exterminate before the community could finish being built.

Nomi̽yvu Qüeōt Med was built using the conventions of Wood Elven durring the late 11th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Nomi̽yvu Qüeōt Med is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature timber and earth construction, with most buildigns first floors resembling mounds of earth, with subsequent floors appearing as elaborate log cabins, with each building forming a tiered pyramid of sorts fromed from the stack of rectangular, peek roofed cabins. Most wooden bracing, support, and trim is carved with decorative knotwork, and the larger structures even feature painted trim which emphasises the knotwork.

Nomi̽yvu Qüeōt Med is buildings are built arround a single narrow paverstone mainstreet which forms a counterclockwise spiral to give the city a over all circular shape. The cliffside city has a defencive wall made from large clay bricks. The wall is constructed to the exact specifications of millitary fortifications, but the nature of its clay brick construction leaves it vulnerable to even outdated siege equipment. That said, the city is well defended against anything short of an army. Astonishigly, the brittle defences are in pristine condishion, as if they had just been finished before you laied eyes upon them.

Right off the bat Nomi̽yvu Qüeōt Med hits you in the face with its success. Everyone, even the peasants, are dressed in well made clothing. Every tool and implement you can see is finely made, and people will boast to you as obvious strangers of the wonders which can be found in their markets. More interestingly is a total lack of beggars, and plenty of new buildings are going up even as you speak. Somehow this city has come into quite a lot of wealth, and recently from the looks of things. The city is very clearly a joyfull place as well as wealthy. Music can be heard often, as well as laughter. The smell of food and drink permiates the air. You can’t help but smile.

Nomi̽yvu Qüeōt Med has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Nomi̽yvu Qüeōt Med is home to a University which provides higher education in a variety of fields, and also serves as a research institute for those same fields.

Cultural Notes

Nomi̽yvu Qüeōt Med's garrison was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is characterized by their massive, monolithic and 'blocky' appearance with a rigid geometric style. It was best known for its rough, unfinished surfaces, unusual shapes, heavy-looking materials, straight lines, and small windows. Modular elements were often used to form masses representing specific functional zones, grouped into a unified whole.

Due to the actions of local Kami, autumn is short in Nomi̽yvu Qüeōt Med.

The Ice Elemental, Medium near Nomi̽yvu Qüeōt Med are known to be a mutant strain of the creature.

Nomi̽yvu Qüeōt Med's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in winter and involves embarking on a group pilgrimage to channel Charm energies of tier 2 via guttural bellowing.
