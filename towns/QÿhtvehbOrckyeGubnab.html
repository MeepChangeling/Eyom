Qÿhtvehb Orckye Gubnab (/dujc/ /gubˈnab/ [blank] [artist]) is a subtropical Small City located in Rävnewyȳ̈hhe Hold, Halähÿhvwu Kingdom, within the Undermountain.

The name Qÿhtvehb Orckye Gubnab is derived from the Sylvin language, as Qÿhtvehb Orckye Gubnab was founded by Shëgê, who was culturaly Undermountain.
