Dewltålvo Dänzle Ÿhkphÿh (/duːltɑːlˈvo/ /dənˈzli/ [religious] [post]) is a temperate Small City located in Ckÿchäckewck Hold, Qättza Kingdom, within the Undermountain.

The name Dewltålvo Dänzle Ÿhkphÿh is derived from the Sylvin language, as Dewltålvo Dänzle Ÿhkphÿh was founded by Trêtconhe, who was culturaly Undermountain.

Dewltålvo Dänzle Ÿhkphÿh was founded durring the late 12th century in spring of the year 1054, by Trêtconhe. The establishment of the new community went well, though many minor issues had to be solved as time went on. This was enough to delay construction and push back the formal opening ceramony, leading to some embarisment for Trêtconhe.

Dewltålvo Dänzle Ÿhkphÿh was built using the conventions of Undermountain durring the late 12th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Dewltålvo Dänzle Ÿhkphÿh is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature waddle and daub construction with good timber frames and a stone foundation protected by thatched or shingled rooves. Most buildings with second floors are built in such a way as to overhang into the streets on the upper floors for more space, as building size seems to be the primary indicator of wealth within the community. Most buildings are not decorated with any integral features, but instead use ivy, flowers, and other natural elements in planters of on trellices to breathe life into the structure they grow upon.

Dewltålvo Dänzle Ÿhkphÿh is buildings are speckled and packed arround broad split-log ties streets with seemingly no patern to them. It appears as if the city's residents simply built streets as they pleased and squeazed buildings in wherever and howeave rpossible, creating an organic, frustrating to navigate, maze of a city. The city is the proud owner of a properly designed set of renforced walls made from mighty querried stone blocks. Their construction and material choices would make a dwarf weap with joy, for each and every part of the elaborate fortifications are purly functional and robust well byond reason. Even nonexperts can tell the walls are an excelent defencive structure. Dewltålvo Dänzle Ÿhkphÿh's exceptionaly well made fortifications are in an unremarkable state. To some, this is the ideal sate for defences to be in. In need of absoutly nothing, and ready to serve the city as needed.

Before you’ve even set foot into the heart of Dewltålvo Dänzle Ÿhkphÿh, you can smell it. The incense. It hangs about the town like a cloud. Monks, priests, and clerics are everywhere, all dedicated to the same divine, all performing the same rituals to bless and anoint buildings, streets, people, animals, you name it they are or have blessed it. The same holy symbol is everywhere too. Its on buildings, on people, and even branded into livestock. This certainly loves its divine. Asside form the revrent worship, you can see the citizens are preparing for something, and that something isn’t good. Weapons are worn openly. Defensive structures have been recently constructed to guard less used areas of the city. Merchants seem very eager to sell their wares at discount prices. Something is coming.

Cultural Notes

Dewltålvo Dänzle Ÿhkphÿh's locals despise outsiders. For some “outsiders” may be natives of foreign lands, while others might have a grudge against anyone from outside the community. These locals have an active loathing, and the outsiders allowed to trade or interact with them do so at a heavy disadvantage.

Dewltålvo Dänzle Ÿhkphÿh's mayor's house was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is known for its buildings being predominantly formed through the combination of basic geometric shapes. However, it shown in the detailing given to the structures such as its characteristic tall columns, intricate detail, symmetry, harmony, and balance in their designs to an astonishing degree of precision. Decorative elements for the buildings tended to be built into the structure itself, making great use of fluting, frescoes, inlays, and embossing.

In Dewltålvo Dänzle Ÿhkphÿh birds speak prophesy.

The Mercane near Dewltålvo Dänzle Ÿhkphÿh are known to be quite timid.

Dewltålvo Dänzle Ÿhkphÿh's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in summer and involves sex to channel Augury energies of tier 2 via throat chanting.

History

In time immemorial, reportedly some time during the early 2nd century, Dewltålvo Dänzle Ÿhkphÿh was attacked by soldiers from another nation, with orders to raid Dewltålvo Dänzle Ÿhkphÿh. The details of the conflict are hazy at best due to many conflicting accounts. What is known is Dewltålvo Dänzle Ÿhkphÿh lost 163 people, 362 livestock, and 95 buildings. The conflict ended after roughly 186, when members of Dewltålvo Dänzle Ÿhkphÿh's militia enacted an operation to scout a specific location for information about the enemy. The operation was complicated by the army's activities draw the wrath of formerly neutral parties. The conflict ended with an assault and siege on the strategic location, which ended in a stalemate for Dewltålvo Dänzle Ÿhkphÿh's forces. The war is remembered in legend by Dewltålvo Dänzle Ÿhkphÿh's bards, historians, and legend keepers.
