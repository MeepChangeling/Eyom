Jaríhvviy Se (/ˈjarɪ̞v/ /vij/ [corn] [absurd]) is a subtropical Town located in the Luvjash County of the Picham Community.

The name Jaríhvviy Se is derived from the Tauric language, as Jaríhvviy Se was founded by Cú̄ń Góch 'Kylie Alexa' Bēs Mp̪f̄ Cä̌sh Collins, who was culturaly Tauric.

Climate

Jaríhvviy Se has a yearly average temperature of 26°C (78°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 30°C (86°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 22°C (71°F). Jaríhvviy Se receives an average of 258 cm/y (101 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. Jaríhvviy Se covers an area of nearly 6 km2 (2 mi2), and an average elevation of 2352 m (7716 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Jaríhvviy Se was founded durring the early 12th century in spring of the year 1049, by Cú̄ń Góch 'Kylie Alexa' Bēs Mp̪f̄ Cä̌sh Collins. The establishment of Jaríhvviy Se was only bairly constructed. The sheer number of problems with its founding were enough to make several of the backers funding Jaríhvviy Se's construction back out of the project. Cú̄ń Góch 'Kylie Alexa' Bēs Mp̪f̄ Cä̌sh Collins pushed on reguardles, and Jaríhvviy Se was finished, but starts off as a terible place to live.

Jaríhvviy Se was built using the conventions of Tauric durring the early 12th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Jaríhvviy Se is no diffrent. The town's buildings feature timber framed wooden shiethed or brick construction, which gives form to a very formalized, rational, expence effishent arcatectural style based on strictly symmetrical designs which universaly feature pitched roofs, shutters, and the occasional column or pilaster for a decorative touch.

Jaríhvviy Se is is constructed arround a semi-circular crampt packed earth mainstreet, with smaller strait roads linking the circiles to eachother at varrious points. The town is protected by a well-crafted cobblestone fence tall enough to provide adiquite cover for defenders to fire from, but no more than that. The frontieer-style defences has suffered a visible ammount of structural damage, leaving them effectivly useless. One can't help but wonder why the has not yet effected repairs.

A look around Jaríhvviy Se is like looking into a university’s plaza on the day before an exam. People rush about to-and-fro. Discarded scrolls and quills are dotted around town. In spite of many locals discussing academic topics, there’s little sign of any organized groups for any of that talk. Infact, the town seems disordered in general with everything scattered helter skelter about.

Civic Infrastructure

Jaríhvviy Se has an Office of Civil Groundskeeping, which works to enforce local ordinances relating to the construction and upkeep up of all plant life, water features, and other natural decorations within Jaríhvviy Se. They are also responsible for the maintenance of these features. Notably, the OCG is not responsible for Jaríhvviy Se's parks.

Jaríhvviy Se has an Office of Civil Vicary, which is responsible for providing a livelyhood for all officialy recognised religious figures within Jaríhvviy Se.

Jaríhvviy Se has a Gravedigger's Guild, which is responsible for collecting the dead and laying them to rest according to all applicable laws and religious customs.

Jaríhvviy Se has a Highwayman's Guild, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Jaríhvviy Se has a Hall of Slayers, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Jaríhvviy Se has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain Jaríhvviy Se's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

Jaríhvviy Se has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands. They are not to be confused with the Office of Civil Groundskeeping as they do not hold authority over nor responsibility for Jaríhvviy Se's natural decorations nor waterways.

Jaríhvviy Se has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Jaríhvviy Se has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Cultural Notes

Jaríhvviy Se's citizens locals enjoy many vices and lustful appetites. They may have religious sanction for their deeds, or neighbors might trade with them for such things, or they could be followers of some ideology that blesses such pursuits. Their economy or their social organization is usually heavily reliant on such traffic, and to ensure its continuance they may have made bargains with various mortal and immortal powers.

Jaríhvviy Se's town hall was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is inspired by the natural world, characterized by sinuous, sculptural, organic shapes, arches, curving lines, and sensual ornamentation. Common motifs included stylized versions of leaves, flowers, vines, insects, animals, and other natural elements. Decorative elements found on the inside and outside of buildings include intricate mosaic work, curved windows, and decorative trim work. .

In Jaríhvviy Se yeast remains dormant.

The Raven near Jaríhvviy Se are known to be a mutant strain of the creature.

Jaríhvviy Se's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in spring and involves reenactments to channel Charm energies of tier 1 via recitation of poetic epics.
