Krö Nto̠n Vrët (/kro˞/ /nto̠n/ [ill; unwell] [lava]) is a subtropical Town located in Gduzqut Parish, Moko̠ë Empire, within the Warrenese Shogunate.

The name Krö Nto̠n Vrët is derived from the Wood Elvish language, as Krö Nto̠n Vrët was founded by Rrimvesh Ghuldrash, who was culturaly Wood Elven.

Climate

Krö Nto̠n Vrët has a yearly average temperature of 14°C (57°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 5°C (41°F). Krö Nto̠n Vrët receives an average of 154 cm/y (60 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Krö Nto̠n Vrët covers an area of nearly 6 km2 (2 mi2), and an average elevation of 4986 m (16358 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Krö Nto̠n Vrët was founded durring the early 9th century, by Rrimvesh Ghuldrash. The establishment of Krö Nto̠n Vrët suffered from many setbacks, delays, and obsticles, most notably a group of Krö Nto̠n Vrët which required millitary assistance exterminate before the community could finish being built.

Krö Nto̠n Vrët was built using the conventions of Wood Elven durring the early 9th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Krö Nto̠n Vrët is no diffrent. The town's buildings feature timber framed wooden shiethed or brick construction, which gives form to a very formalized, rational, expence effishent arcatectural style based on strictly symmetrical designs which universaly feature pitched roofs, shutters, and the occasional column or pilaster for a decorative touch.

Krö Nto̠n Vrët is buildings are arranged arround a single narrow cobblestone mainstreet with many smaller streets branching off of it which gives the town a over all rectangular shape, albit one warped and twisted by the nature of the curves of the main road. The town posesses a fortified albit thin wall of querried stone. This wall posesses most of the features of a castle wall, though it is constructed from cheeper inferior stone. It would pose a minor chalange for an attacking army, though it's clear the wall's true purpose is to crush the hopes of bandits and marauders. The town's failry decent fortifications are visibly old, but also obviously well maintained. Its likly the local malishia or garrison are tasked with routine mantance of the town's defences.

A look around Krö Nto̠n Vrët makes it abundantly clear the town suffered something horrible some time ago. It's as if the town itself is depressed. Smiles are few, cheer is nowhere to be had. Everyone quietly goes about their daily business not looking anyone in the eye.

Civic Infrastructure

Krö Nto̠n Vrët has an Office of Civil Groundskeeping, which works to enforce local ordinances relating to the construction and upkeep up of all plant life, water features, and other natural decorations within Krö Nto̠n Vrët. They are also responsible for the maintenance of these features. Notably, the OCG is not responsible for Krö Nto̠n Vrët's parks.

Krö Nto̠n Vrët has an Office of Civil Vicary, which is responsible for providing a livelyhood for all officialy recognised religious figures within Krö Nto̠n Vrët.

Krö Nto̠n Vrët has a Gravedigger's Guild, which is responsible for collecting the dead and laying them to rest according to all applicable laws and religious customs.

Krö Nto̠n Vrët has a Highwayman's Guild, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Krö Nto̠n Vrët has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain Krö Nto̠n Vrët's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

Krö Nto̠n Vrët has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands. They are not to be confused with the Office of Civil Groundskeeping as they do not hold authority over nor responsibility for Krö Nto̠n Vrët's natural decorations nor waterways.

Krö Nto̠n Vrët has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Krö Nto̠n Vrët has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.
