Vähäq Ÿqurv Ÿhkphÿh (/lɪ̘ˈs̼o/ /iˈqurv/ [practical; pragmatic] [tribunal]) is a temperate Large City located in Lewÿqded Hold, Dähechanxya Kingdom, within the Undermountain.

The name Vähäq Ÿqurv Ÿhkphÿh is derived from the Sylvin language, as Vähäq Ÿqurv Ÿhkphÿh was founded by Hurum, who was culturaly Undermountain.

Climate

Vähäq Ÿqurv Ÿhkphÿh has a yearly average temperature of 18°C (64°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 11°C (51°F). Vähäq Ÿqurv Ÿhkphÿh receives an average of 277 cm/y (109 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. Vähäq Ÿqurv Ÿhkphÿh covers an area of nearly 72 km2 (28 mi2), and an average elevation of 4302 m (14114 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Vähäq Ÿqurv Ÿhkphÿh was founded durring the late 11th century, by Hurum. The establishment of Vähäq Ÿqurv Ÿhkphÿh was plagued by a lack of willing colonists. After attempts to pay people to resettle failed Hurum struck deals with nearby nations and communities to establish Vähäq Ÿqurv Ÿhkphÿh as a prison colony.

Vähäq Ÿqurv Ÿhkphÿh was built using the conventions of Undermountain durring the late 11th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Vähäq Ÿqurv Ÿhkphÿh is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature waddle and daub construction with good timber frames and a stone foundation protected by thatched or shingled rooves. Most buildings with second floors are built in such a way as to overhang into the streets on the upper floors for more space, as building size seems to be the primary indicator of wealth within the community. Most buildings are not decorated with any integral features, but instead use ivy, flowers, and other natural elements in planters of on trellices to breathe life into the structure they grow upon.

Vähäq Ÿqurv Ÿhkphÿh is buildings are located arround a single premissive baked earthen mainstreet which forms a clockwise spiral to give the city a over all circular shape. The city resides behind a palisade wall complete with battlments, a moat, and timber gatehouses with drawbridges. Vähäq Ÿqurv Ÿhkphÿh's robustly designed timber walls are in an unremarkable state. To some, this is the ideal sate for defences to be in. In need of absoutly nothing, and ready to serve the city as needed.

Vähäq Ÿqurv Ÿhkphÿh has a very calm atmosphere. People can be seen relaxing, scocilizing, and going about all manner of business other than the daily grind. Men, women, children, all can be seen enjoying life in a laid-back way in the many parks which line Vähäq Ÿqurv Ÿhkphÿh’s streets. In spite of this, quite a few people can be seen reading and the city has an abundance of libraries. It’s quite clear the community values education.

Cultural Notes

Vähäq Ÿqurv Ÿhkphÿh's town hall was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is best known for its striking sculptural forms and often dazzling ornamental detail that characterizes the buildings general shape. The radiant colors, rich patterns, and symmetrical silhouettes employed by this style were backed up by rich decorative features including gardens, courtyards, extruded arches, domes, pointed domes, vaulted ceilings, elaborate painted and inlaid designs, and decorative sculptures.

Due to the actions of local Kami, winter is skipped in Vähäq Ÿqurv Ÿhkphÿh.

The ﻿Marsupial, Kangaroo near Vähäq Ÿqurv Ÿhkphÿh are known to be quite timid.

Vähäq Ÿqurv Ÿhkphÿh's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in spring and involves line dance to channel Mysticism energies of tier 3 via throat chanting.
