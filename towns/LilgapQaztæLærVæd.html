Lilgap Qaztæ Lær Væd (/ˈqaztæ/ /lær væd/ [long] [drought]) is a subtropical Large City located in the Pz-339jnup District of the Alveria.

The name Lilgap Qaztæ Lær Væd is derived from the Goblin language, as Lilgap Qaztæ Lær Væd was founded by Mboidī Joīnvé̄ 'Lianna Shanti' Nībó̄r Sé̄nv Cawmé, who was culturaly KLEO.

Climate

Lilgap Qaztæ Lær Væd has a yearly average temperature of 14°C (57°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a cool 20°C (68°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 9°C (48°F). Lilgap Qaztæ Lær Væd receives an average of 215 cm/y (84 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Lilgap Qaztæ Lær Væd covers an area of nearly 77 km2 (30 mi2), and an average elevation of 1206 m (3956 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Lilgap Qaztæ Lær Væd was founded durring the early 13th century in summer of the year 1243, by Mboidī Joīnvé̄ 'Lianna Shanti' Nībó̄r Sé̄nv Cawmé. The establishment of Lilgap Qaztæ Lær Væd was plagued by a lack of willing colonists. After attempts to pay people to resettle failed Mboidī Joīnvé̄ 'Lianna Shanti' Nībó̄r Sé̄nv Cawmé struck deals with nearby nations and communities to establish Lilgap Qaztæ Lær Væd as a prison colony.

Lilgap Qaztæ Lær Væd was built using the conventions of KLEO durring the early 13th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Lilgap Qaztæ Lær Væd is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature masoned stone construction which prominantly features pointed arches, pointed ribbed vault cielings, flying buttress', and window tracery all of which share a simmilar gemoetetic patern halfway between organic and inorganic in design formaing a very distinct aesthetically integrated style. BUildings tend to reach for the havens, and more expencive homes are easily identified by their floor count as well as the addition of decorative features intigrated into the building's design such as statues, gargoyals, and embelished joinery.

Lilgap Qaztæ Lær Væd is buildings are located arround a single spacious baked earthen mainstreet which forms a clockwise spiral to give the city a over all circular shape. The city posesses a fortified albit thin wall of querried stone. This wall posesses most of the features of a castle wall, though it is constructed from cheeper inferior stone. It would pose a minor chalange for an attacking army, though it's clear the wall's true purpose is to crush the hopes of bandits and marauders. Though some of the worse spots could lead to the loss of defenders lives if attackers identified the weaknesses ahead of time.
