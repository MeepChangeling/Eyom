Slæ̈rn Ilēp-mïgē (/iˈlæ̝p/ /mɪˈgæ̝/ [oral] [harvest]) is a temperate City located in the Gïtarejé Principality of the Nasdi.

The name Slæ̈rn Ilēp-mïgē is derived from the Sylvin language, as Slæ̈rn Ilēp-mïgē was founded by Qrimken, who was culturaly Gnoll.

Climate

Slæ̈rn Ilēp-mïgē has a yearly average temperature of 1°C (33°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a cool 12°C (53°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -9°C (16°F). Slæ̈rn Ilēp-mïgē receives an average of 258 cm/y (101 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Slæ̈rn Ilēp-mïgē covers an area of nearly 30 km2 (12 mi2), and an average elevation of 2372 m (7782 ft) above sea level.
