Räz Un Hoph (/un/ /hof/ [cool )fashionable)] [bird; penis (slang)]) is a subtropical Large City located in Genephfurn Hold, Gÿtgesheyd Kingdom, within the Undermountain.

The name Räz Un Hoph is derived from the Sylvin language, as Räz Un Hoph was founded by Cave Scorpions, who was culturaly Undermountain.

Climate

Räz Un Hoph has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 20°C (68°F). Räz Un Hoph receives an average of 192 cm/y (75 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. Räz Un Hoph covers an area of nearly 216 km2 (86 mi2), and an average elevation of 4248 m (13937 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Räz Un Hoph was founded durring the early 12th century in fall of the year 1127, by Cave Scorpions. The establishment of the new community went well, though many minor issues had to be solved as time went on. This was enough to delay construction and push back the formal opening ceramony, leading to some embarisment for Cave Scorpions.

Räz Un Hoph was built using the conventions of Undermountain durring the early 12th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Räz Un Hoph is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature timber framed wooden shiethed or brick construction, which gives form to a very formalized, rational, expence effishent arcatectural style based on strictly symmetrical designs which universaly feature pitched roofs, shutters, and the occasional column or pilaster for a decorative touch.

Räz Un Hoph is buildings are arranged arrounded highly ordered system of restrictive split-log ties streets which form octogonal paterns, allowing the incides of the octagons to be common grounds for the buildings on the edges, be it for parkland, yardspace, plazas, or markets. The city is the proud owner of a properly designed set of renforced walls made from mighty querried stone blocks. Their construction and material choices would make a dwarf weap with joy, for each and every part of the elaborate fortifications are purly functional and robust well byond reason. Even nonexperts can tell the walls are an excelent defencive structure. Räz Un Hoph's exceptionaly well made fortifications are suffering from significent damage, so much so that examples can be pointed to no matter which section one might have within their line of site, and most of which render sections inoperable at present.

Looking around Räz Un Hoph you immediately realise that the locals are looking back at you. All of them. Everywhere you look somone is staring back at you analytically, looking over every inch of you, your gear, and your companions. As you get close to people, their hands move closer to their belt knife, or dagger. You may want to watch where you go and what you say...

Civic Infrastructure

Räz Un Hoph possesses a Aethary Link for its accademic, government, and financial institutions. Public Aethary access is available through one or more of these intitutions.

Räz Un Hoph has an animal control department which works to enforce local ordinances relating to the control, impoundment, and disposition of animals.

Räz Un Hoph has an Office of Civil Groundskeeping, which works to enforce local ordinances relating to the construction and upkeep up of all plant life, water features, and other natural decorations within Räz Un Hoph. They are also responsible for the maintenance of these features. Notably, the OCG is not responsible for Räz Un Hoph's parks.

Räz Un Hoph has an Office of Civil Vicary, which is responsible for providing a livelyhood for all officialy recognised religious figures within Räz Un Hoph.

Räz Un Hoph has a government-funded child care program, overseen by the local Department of Nursemaids, which is responsible for providing childcare to working-class citizens according to local ordinances.

Räz Un Hoph has a Gravedigger's Guild, which is responsible for collecting the dead and laying them to rest according to all applicable laws and religious customs.

Räz Un Hoph has a Guild of Nurses, which is tasked with caring for the elderly and infirm in accordance with local ordinances, religious values, and customs.

Räz Un Hoph has a Department of Firefighters, which is responsible for organizing fire fighting efforts during a fire and enforcing local ordinances relating to fire safety.

Räz Un Hoph has a Highwayman's Guild, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Räz Un Hoph has a Hall of Slayers, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Räz Un Hoph has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain Räz Un Hoph's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

Räz Un Hoph has an Scientific Academy which provides higher education in the natural sciences.

Räz Un Hoph possesses a Galvanic Power Grid, which brings galvanic current to most if not all buildings in town, and permits a great many consumer goods to function within the Large City. Räz Un Hoph's grid is powered by mana accumulators.

Räz Un Hoph possesses an older civil lighting system consisting of street lamps. In spite of the Galvanic Grid, these lights continue to use their old fule sources to provide nighttime illumination to all city streets.

Räz Un Hoph has a first rate hospital which caters to anyone in need of long term medical care.

Räz Un Hoph has a library, which keeps a large collection of books, scrolls, and archives all manner of physical items. The library is open to the public, including the Aether Link.

Räz Un Hoph has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands. They are not to be confused with the Office of Civil Groundskeeping as they do not hold authority over nor responsibility for Räz Un Hoph's natural decorations nor waterways.

Räz Un Hoph has a Guild of Roadworkers, who are responsible for maintaining the roadways and public paths within town. They also have the duty of enforcing all civil laws relating to the roadways.

Räz Un Hoph has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Räz Un Hoph has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Räz Un Hoph is home to a University which provides higher education in a variety of fields, and also serves as a research institute for those same fields.

Cultural Notes

Räz Un Hoph's mayor's house was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is famous for its stately symmetry, classical elements, and grand appearance. Columns and pillars, such as Corinthian columns, are often seen supporting open structures or porticos. Symmetry is an important feature of this style, with each half of a building mirroring the other. Domed ceilings and windows grace these buildings, with everything placed in a mathematical arrangement.

In Räz Un Hoph leaves and flower petals never touch the ground.

The Corpse Rook near Räz Un Hoph are known to be a mutant strain of the creature.

Räz Un Hoph's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in spring and involves consuming a local toxin to channel Charm energies of tier 2 via proclamations.
