Boke Ùqdèzkíh (/ʊq/ /ˈdɛzkɪ̞/ [ash] [stupid; dumb; dull; blunt]) is a subtropical Large City located in the Aldäbn Region of the Tetburland.

The name Boke Ùqdèzkíh is derived from the Sylvin language, as Boke Ùqdèzkíh was founded by Tshêl, who was culturaly Tauran.

Climate

Boke Ùqdèzkíh has a yearly average temperature of 29°C (84°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 33°C (91°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 26°C (78°F). Boke Ùqdèzkíh receives an average of 205 cm/y (80 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. Boke Ùqdèzkíh covers an area of nearly 225 km2 (90 mi2), and an average elevation of 4006 m (13143 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Boke Ùqdèzkíh was founded durring the late 14th century in summer of the year 1357, by Tshêl. The establishment of Boke Ùqdèzkíh suffered from several major issues, resulting in the need to develop many solutions to basic problems. Problems such as a lack of fresh water, logistical support, poor quality tools, and the odd monster or two. Howeaver, these were overcome in time.

Boke Ùqdèzkíh was built using the conventions of Tauran durring the late 14th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Boke Ùqdèzkíh is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature plaster covered brickwork used to form structures with an emphasis on symmetry, proportion, geometry and the regularity of parts. Orderly arrangements of columns, pilasters and lintels, as well as the use of semicircular arches, hemispherical domes, niches and aediculae can be found everywhere such that only size of building and yard can be used to measure the general prosparity of a given building's owners due to a general wealthy feeling the style gives off.

Boke Ùqdèzkíh is buildings are grouped arround an odd layout of broad paverstone streets, which seems to be based on an overlapping squair patern such that there are small squares at the cornor of every bigger square. Sometimes buildings exist in the smaller squaires, other times they are open spaces, or occupied by temporary structures. The city is the proud owner of a thick set of fortified walls fashioned from querried stone blocks. While not up to snuff for a fort or castle wall, the city's walls are naturaly much larger than those of forts or castles. Therefore, the construction such a wall is most expencive. Boke Ùqdèzkíh's buget focused wall would serve its community well in battle in spite of looking unimpressive compared to castles and fortresses. The city's top tier civilian fortifications are visibly old, but also obviously well maintained. Its likly the local malishia or garrison are tasked with routine mantance of the city's defences.

Looking around Boke Ùqdèzkíh you immediately realise that the locals are looking back at you. All of them. Everywhere you look somone is staring back at you analytically, looking over every inch of you, your gear, and your companions. As you get close to people, their hands move closer to their belt knife, or dagger. You may want to watch where you go and what you say...

Boke Ùqdèzkíh is home to a University which provides higher education in a variety of fields, and also serves as a research institute for those same fields.

Cultural Notes

Boke Ùqdèzkíh's garrison was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is best known for its functional shapes, abstract shapes used sparingly for decor, simple color schemes, holistic design, and basic industrial materials. Its simple designs were created to be beautiful, functional, and mass-producible. The style used little to no embellishment or ornamentation, instead drawing attention to the streamlined design, such as flat roofs to create a simple, geometric look. The simplicity masks the style's nearly sinister functionality, as every last feature is designed to guide the people living in the building in how to make the most efficient use of the structure.

In Boke Ùqdèzkíh most nights are accompanied by colorful ribbons of light in the sky.

The Ice Elemental, Large near Boke Ùqdèzkíh are known to be almost tame, such that they can be put to domestic use.

Boke Ùqdèzkíh's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in winter and involves performance art to channel Enchantment energies of tier 2 via moments of science.

History

Boke Ùqdèzkíh is still bloodied by a recent violent conflict. A crushing bandit raid, a lost siege, getting caught at the periphery of a major battle, or some other calamity has inflicted severe damage on the place. Some communities may suffer a longer-term version of this, their youths lost in a grinding, endless battle against some perpetual threat.

In time immemorial, reportedly some time during the late 2nd century the Kami granted the town a great harvest. One of Boke Ùqdèzkíh's local festivals commemorates this miracle.
