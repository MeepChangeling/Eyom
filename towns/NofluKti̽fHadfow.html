Noflu Kti̽f Hadfow (/ˈnoflu/ /kti̽f/ [sad] [acquaintance]) is a subtropical Large City located in the Zbëkfenfyë Moot of the Rosid.

The name Noflu Kti̽f Hadfow is derived from the Sylvin language, as Noflu Kti̽f Hadfow was founded by Celenil, who was culturaly Wood Elf.

Noflu Kti̽f Hadfow receives an average of 195 cm/y (76 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Noflu Kti̽f Hadfow covers an area of nearly 58 km2 (23 mi2), and an average elevation of 4056 m (13307 ft) above sea level.

Decorative features included domed rooves, arches, soaring spaces, and sumptuous decoration: marble columns and inlay, mosaics on the vaults, inlaid-stone pavements, and sometimes gold coffered ceilings.
