Wæm Xq-48o Ǐfmǐ (/xɨˈgɛxka/ /ˈɪfmɪ/ [loss] [shell]) is a subtropical Town located in Potozari Zone, If-70cqǐnrì District, within the Federation of Alveria.

The name Wæm Xq-48o Ǐfmǐ is derived from the Constructi language, as Wæm Xq-48o Ǐfmǐ was founded by Glkêtê Dyêg̈m, who was culturaly Constructi.

Wæm Xq-48o Ǐfmǐ receives an average of 264 cm/y (103 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. Wæm Xq-48o Ǐfmǐ covers an area of nearly 4 km2 (1 mi2), and an average elevation of 2708 m (8884 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Wæm Xq-48o Ǐfmǐ was founded durring the late 17th century in late fall of the year 1597, by Glkêtê Dyêg̈m. The establishment of Wæm Xq-48o Ǐfmǐ was plagued by a lack of willing colonists. After attempts to pay people to resettle failed Glkêtê Dyêg̈m struck deals with nearby nations and communities to establish Wæm Xq-48o Ǐfmǐ as a prison colony.

Wæm Xq-48o Ǐfmǐ was built using the conventions of Constructi durring the late 17th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Wæm Xq-48o Ǐfmǐ is no diffrent. The town's buildings feature timber framed wooden shiethed or brick construction, which gives form to a very formalized, rational, expence effishent arcatectural style based on strictly symmetrical designs which universaly feature pitched roofs, shutters, and the occasional column or pilaster for a decorative touch.

Wæm Xq-48o Ǐfmǐ is buildings are arranged arround a network of broad cobblestone streets which form a diamond shaped grid, where each diamond verries in size given the proximity of the paralell streets forming each section. The ocasional smaller diamond has been used to construct a park, plaza, and other communal structures. The town sits comfortably behind a palisade wall complete with a timber gatehouse and battlments. Cultural Notes

Wæm Xq-48o Ǐfmǐ is home to a distinct subculture, who are either the majority here or have the dominant positions in the community. Architecture, local laws, and social customs are all tuned to suit them, and they may not be particularly forgiving or friendly to the major culture of the region. Communities that are not outright independent usually make an arm's-length submission to a local lord.

Wæm Xq-48o Ǐfmǐ's chapel was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is which made use of the classical orders and mathematically precise ratios of height and width combined with a desire for symmetry, proportion, and harmony. It used columns, pediments, arches and domes are imaginatively in buildings of all types. Decorative features were seen as largely unnecessary as the sheer beauty of the structure itself was often close to art. However, many buildings with large ceiling spaces had their ceilings decorated with elaborate paintings, simply because the large flat spaces could feel wasted.

Due to the actions of local Kami, spring is recurring in Wæm Xq-48o Ǐfmǐ.

The Ice Elemental, Small near Wæm Xq-48o Ǐfmǐ are known to be almost tame, such that they can be put to domestic use.

Wæm Xq-48o Ǐfmǐ's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in winter and involves sacrificing an animal to channel Summoning energies of tier 2 via recitation of poetic epics.
