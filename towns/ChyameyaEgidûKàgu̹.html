Chyameya Egidû Kàgu̹ (/eˈgidʊ/ /ˈkəgu̹/ [backward] [flaw; defect; scar]) is a temperate Large City located in S̺u̹zû Màbæ Kingdom, Vu̹chi Bu̽su Empire, within the Warren.

The name Chyameya Egidû Kàgu̹ is derived from the Sylvin language, as Chyameya Egidû Kàgu̹ was founded by Hadil̄, who was culturaly Rattu.

Climate

Chyameya Egidû Kàgu̹ has a yearly average temperature of 15°C (59°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a cool 18°C (64°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 12°C (53°F). Chyameya Egidû Kàgu̹ receives an average of 200 cm/y (78 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the short winter months. Chyameya Egidû Kàgu̹ covers an area of nearly 97 km2 (38 mi2), and an average elevation of 1738 m (5702 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Chyameya Egidû Kàgu̹ was founded durring the late 14th century in summer of the year 1296, by Hadil̄. The establishment of Chyameya Egidû Kàgu̹ was only bairly constructed. The sheer number of problems with its founding were enough to make several of the backers funding Chyameya Egidû Kàgu̹'s construction back out of the project. Hadil̄ pushed on reguardles, and Chyameya Egidû Kàgu̹ was finished, but starts off as a terible place to live.

Chyameya Egidû Kàgu̹ was built using the conventions of Rattu durring the late 14th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Chyameya Egidû Kàgu̹ is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature waddle and daub construction with good timber frames and a stone foundation protected by thatched or shingled rooves. Most buildings with second floors are built in such a way as to overhang into the streets on the upper floors for more space, as building size seems to be the primary indicator of wealth within the community. Most buildings are not decorated with any integral features, but instead use ivy, flowers, and other natural elements in planters of on trellices to breathe life into the structure they grow upon.

Chyameya Egidû Kàgu̹ is buildings are arranged arrounded highly ordered system of restrictive flagstone streets which form octogonal paterns, allowing the incides of the octagons to be common grounds for the buildings on the edges, be it for parkland, yardspace, plazas, or markets. The city is defended by arcane means. It's hard to spot at first, but there's a tell tell shimmer in the air arround Chyameya Egidû Kàgu̹, and you can spot the ocasional warding glyph carved into a rock or tree all arround town. These mystical defences are ancient, unknowable, and unassailable by current means... Assuming everything is in working order. Otherwise, the wards are little more than a deathtrap. Chyameya Egidû Kàgu̹'s Relic of the World That Was has sufferd soem light damage, reducing its function a little in some spots, but could almsot certainly preform as expected... Though some of the worse spots could lead to the loss of defenders lives if attackers identified the weaknesses ahead of time.

A look around Chyameya Egidû Kàgu̹ is like a look into a broken heart. There is no planning, no organization. Everyone here clearly goes about their own thing with little thought to anyone around them who isn’t selling something they need. It’s not malicious. Heads are hung. Eyes are empty. Something truly horrible happened here once upon a time and the city never healed.

Chyameya Egidû Kàgu̹ has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Cultural Notes

Chyameya Egidû Kàgu̹'s bank was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is best known for the combined use of arcuated and trabeated construction, employing arches and constructed with post and lintel. The arch served as the style's chief structural element, with flanking columns serving as buttresses or decorations. While the general shape of their structures could be generously described as simplistic, the use of these arches and columns created a most distinct ascetic which is easily distinguishable on sight to armatures and experts alike.

Due to the actions of local Kami, winter is long in Chyameya Egidû Kàgu̹.

The Flea, Giant near Chyameya Egidû Kàgu̹ are known to be a mutant strain of the creature.

Chyameya Egidû Kàgu̹'s citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in autumn and involves long periods of drunkenness to channel Elven High Magic energies of tier 2 via speaking in tongues.
