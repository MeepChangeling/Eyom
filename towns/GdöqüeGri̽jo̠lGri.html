Gdöqüe Gri̽jo̠l Gri (/gdo˞q˔/ /ˈgri̽jo̠l/ [automatic] [winery]) is a subtropical Large City located in the Skruvnōk Moot of the Rosid.

The name Gdöqüe Gri̽jo̠l Gri is derived from the Goblin language, as Gdöqüe Gri̽jo̠l Gri was founded by Nrèsè, who was culturaly Wood Elf.

Climate

Gdöqüe Gri̽jo̠l Gri has a yearly average temperature of 6°C (42°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a cold 6°C (42°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 7°C (44°F). Gdöqüe Gri̽jo̠l Gri receives an average of 228 cm/y (89 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Gdöqüe Gri̽jo̠l Gri covers an area of nearly 89 km2 (35 mi2), and an average elevation of 1418 m (4652 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Gdöqüe Gri̽jo̠l Gri was founded durring the late 13th century in winter of the year 1251, by Nrèsè. The establishment of Gdöqüe Gri̽jo̠l Gri suffered from many setbacks, delays, and obsticles, most notably a group of Gdöqüe Gri̽jo̠l Gri which required millitary assistance exterminate before the community could finish being built.

Gdöqüe Gri̽jo̠l Gri was built using the conventions of Wood Elf durring the late 13th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Gdöqüe Gri̽jo̠l Gri is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature timber framed wooden shiethed or brick construction, which gives form to a very formalized, rational, expence effishent arcatectural style based on strictly symmetrical designs which universaly feature pitched roofs, shutters, and the occasional column or pilaster for a decorative touch.

Gdöqüe Gri̽jo̠l Gri is buildings are arranged arrounded a highly ordered system of restrictive cobblestone streets which form triangular paterns, allowing the incides of the octagons to be common grounds for the buildings on the edges, be it for parkland, yardspace, plazas, or markets. The city is defended by arcane means. It's hard to spot at first, but there's a tell tell shimmer in the air arround Gdöqüe Gri̽jo̠l Gri, and you can spot the ocasional warding glyph carved into a rock or tree all arround town. These mystical defences are ancient, unknowable, and unassailable by current means... Assuming everything is in working order. Otherwise, the wards are little more than a deathtrap. The Relic of the World That Was has suffered a visible ammount of structural damage, leaving them effectivly useless. One can't help but wonder why the has not yet effected repairs.

Gdöqüe Gri̽jo̠l Gri has a very calm atmosphere. People can be seen relaxing, scocilizing, and going about all manner of business other than the daily grind. Men, women, children, all can be seen enjoying life in a laid-back way in the many parks which line Gdöqüe Gri̽jo̠l Gri’s streets. Yet, deep down, you know this atmosphere is a carefully created illusion. You can see the ocasional seam in their tapestry. You’re not sure what has them doing their best to act so carefree, but act carefree they do.

Cultural Notes

Gdöqüe Gri̽jo̠l Gri's chapel was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is which made use of the classical orders and mathematically precise ratios of height and width combined with a desire for symmetry, proportion, and harmony. It used columns, pediments, arches and domes are imaginatively in buildings of all types. Decorative features were seen as largely unnecessary as the sheer beauty of the structure itself was often close to art. However, many buildings with large ceiling spaces had their ceilings decorated with elaborate paintings, simply because the large flat spaces could feel wasted.

Due to the actions of local Kami, winter is skipped in Gdöqüe Gri̽jo̠l Gri.

The Chaneque near Gdöqüe Gri̽jo̠l Gri are known to be almost tame, such that they can be put to domestic use.

Gdöqüe Gri̽jo̠l Gri's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in summer and involves square dance to channel Enchantment energies of tier 3 via throat singing.
