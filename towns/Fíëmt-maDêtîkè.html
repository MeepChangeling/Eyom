Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè (/ˈvɘvè/ /ma/ [fuzzy] [spectrocope]) is a temperate Town located in Sekvutponuna Parish, Glenjetmuro District, within the Federation of Alveria.

The name Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè is derived from the Iron Elvish language, as Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè was founded by Brilêj Glkdonhêv, who was culturaly Iron Elven.

Climate

Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè receives an average of 72 cm/y (28 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè covers an area of nearly 5 km2 (2 mi2), and an average elevation of 7536 m (-18225 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè was founded durring the early 10th century, by Brilêj Glkdonhêv. The establishment of Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè was only bairly constructed. The sheer number of problems with its founding were enough to make several of the backers funding Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè's construction back out of the project. Brilêj Glkdonhêv pushed on reguardles, and Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè was finished, but starts off as a terible place to live.

Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè was built using the conventions of Iron Elven durring the early 10th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè is no diffrent. The town's buildings feature plaster covered brickwork used to form structures with an emphasis on symmetry, proportion, geometry and the regularity of parts. Orderly arrangements of columns, pilasters and lintels, as well as the use of semicircular arches, hemispherical domes, niches and aediculae can be found everywhere such that only size of building and yard can be used to measure the general prosparity of a given building's owners due to a general wealthy feeling the style gives off.

Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè is buildings are built arround a single narrow paverstone mainstreet which forms a counterclockwise spiral to give the town a over all circular shape. The town resides behind a palisade wall complete with battlments, a moat, and timber gatehouses with drawbridges. Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè's robustly designed timber walls has sufferd soem light damage, reducing its function a little in some spots, but could almsot certainly preform as expected... Though some of the worse spots could lead to the loss of defenders lives if attackers identified the weaknesses ahead of time.

Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè has a very calm atmosphere. People can be seen relaxing, scocilizing, and going about all manner of business other than the daily grind. Men, women, children, all can be seen enjoying life in a laid-back way in the many parks which line Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè’s streets. A look around Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè has something terribly wrong with it. It’s impossible to put one’s finger on, but something is horribly wrong. Maybe it’s the way fog blankets the ground, but only in the connors of places. Maybe it’s the vermin scutteling between shadows in the corner of your eyes. Perhaps it’s the overcast sky which seemed to creep out of nowhere, or the distant howling of wolves. Maybe it’s everything together. Regardless, you do not feel it would be wise to remain in Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè long.

Civic Infrastructure

Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè has an Office of Civil Groundskeeping, which works to enforce local ordinances relating to the construction and upkeep up of all plant life, water features, and other natural decorations within Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè. They are also responsible for the maintenance of these features. Notably, the OCG is not responsible for Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè's parks.

Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè has an Office of Civil Vicary, which is responsible for providing a livelyhood for all officialy recognised religious figures within Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè.

Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè has a Gravedigger's Guild, which is responsible for collecting the dead and laying them to rest according to all applicable laws and religious customs.

Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè has a Highwayman's Guild, which is tasked with maintaining the roads and highways leading into town as well as keeping them safe for travelers.

Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè has a monistary of an order of Civil Monks, who provide divine-related services to the general public and maintain Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè's public wards, blessings, and other arcane systems.

Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè possesses a Galvanic Power Grid, which brings galvanic current to most if not all buildings in town, and permits a great many consumer goods to function within the Town. Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè's grid is powered by mana accumulators.

Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè has a Parks and Recreation Department, which is responsible for the construction, management, and usage rights for all of its parks and parklands. They are not to be confused with the Office of Civil Groundskeeping as they do not hold authority over nor responsibility for Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè's natural decorations nor waterways.

Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè has a Guild of Roadworkers, who are responsible for maintaining the roadways and public paths within town. They also have the duty of enforcing all civil laws relating to the roadways.

Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Cultural Notes

Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè's garrison was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is best known for its striking sculptural forms and often dazzling ornamental detail that characterizes the buildings general shape. The radiant colors, rich patterns, and symmetrical silhouettes employed by this style were backed up by rich decorative features including gardens, courtyards, extruded arches, domes, pointed domes, vaulted ceilings, elaborate painted and inlaid designs, and decorative sculptures.

Due to the actions of local Kami, autumn is long in Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè.

The Raven Swarm near Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè are known to be more aggressive than normal.

Fíëmt-ma Dêtîkè's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in spring and involves sacrificing an animal to channel Invocation energies of tier 3 via singing.
