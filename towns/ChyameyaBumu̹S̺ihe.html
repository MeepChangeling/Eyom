Chyameya Bumu̹ S̺ihe (/ˈeba/ /ˈs̺ihe/ [popular] [spike]) is a temperate Large City located in the Qàdurû Vu̽yûchû Principality of the Kakoray.

The name Chyameya Bumu̹ S̺ihe is derived from the Sylvin language, as Chyameya Bumu̹ S̺ihe was founded by Trëmèqth, who was culturaly Rattu.

Climate

Chyameya Bumu̹ S̺ihe has a yearly average temperature of 14°C (57°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 2°C (35°F). Chyameya Bumu̹ S̺ihe receives an average of 246 cm/y (96 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Chyameya Bumu̹ S̺ihe covers an area of nearly 118 km2 (47 mi2), and an average elevation of 2144 m (7034 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Chyameya Bumu̹ S̺ihe was founded durring the late 15th century in summer of the year 1366, by Trëmèqth. The establishment of Chyameya Bumu̹ S̺ihe was only bairly constructed. The sheer number of problems with its founding were enough to make several of the backers funding Chyameya Bumu̹ S̺ihe's construction back out of the project. Trëmèqth pushed on reguardles, and Chyameya Bumu̹ S̺ihe was finished, but starts off as a terible place to live.

Chyameya Bumu̹ S̺ihe was built using the conventions of Rattu durring the late 15th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Chyameya Bumu̹ S̺ihe is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature waddle and daub construction with good timber frames and a stone foundation protected by thatched or shingled rooves. Most buildings with second floors are built in such a way as to overhang into the streets on the upper floors for more space, as building size seems to be the primary indicator of wealth within the community. Most buildings are not decorated with any integral features, but instead use ivy, flowers, and other natural elements in planters of on trellices to breathe life into the structure they grow upon.

Chyameya Bumu̹ S̺ihe is buildings are arranged arround a single restrictive flagstone mainstreet with many smaller streets branching off of it which gives the city a over all rectangular shape, albit one warped and twisted by the nature of the curves of the main road. The city is the proud owner of a proper castle-style stone wall complete with all of the trimmings. It has towers, a moat, gatehouses, drawbridges, and even merticulationsshortsizeleadershipname.. Chyameya Bumu̹ S̺ihe's perhapse unnessisarily well built defences are in an unremarkable state. To some, this is the ideal sate for defences to be in. In need of absoutly nothing, and ready to serve the city as needed.

Chyameya Bumu̹ S̺ihe has a very calm atmosphere. People can be seen relaxing, scocilizing, and going about all manner of business other than the daily grind. Men, women, children, all can be seen enjoying life in a laid-back way in the many parks which line Chyameya Bumu̹ S̺ihe’s streets.

Chyameya Bumu̹ S̺ihe has a public schooling program overseen by the Hall of Sages who has the responsibility of ensuring access to affordable high-quality education in all basic classes (Reading, Writing, Mathmatics, General Sciences, General Arcana, and Social Education) is made available to all citizens.

Chyameya Bumu̹ S̺ihe has a public septic system, which allows its citizens to have indoor bathrooms. The septic system is overseen by the League of Sewerkeepers, who posses the legal authority to enforce all laws relating to the septic system, and are also tasked with its maintenance and upkeep.

Cultural Notes

Chyameya Bumu̹ S̺ihe's bank was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is characterized by grandeur of scale, simplicity of geometric forms, dramatic use of columns, and a preference for blank walls. This design ethos extended to re-imagining earlier styles of structure to create them anew, with a similar overall look and feel to one another..

Due to the actions of local Kami, summer is short in Chyameya Bumu̹ S̺ihe.

The Kami, Kodama near Chyameya Bumu̹ S̺ihe are known to be quite timid.

Chyameya Bumu̹ S̺ihe's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in winter and involves reenactments to channel Enchantment energies of tier 2 via recitation of poetic epics.

History

Chyameya Bumu̹ S̺ihe used to be more prosperous, but something happened relatively long ago that left it a shrunken shadow of its former self. If the settlement is prosperous, the locals often lament how much more they could have had. If the settlement is not prosperous, the locals blame their ill fortunes on that event. Reminders of this better time can be found in many places within Chyameya Bumu̹ S̺ihe.
