Chyameya Bigàbâ Gækû (/biˈgəbɑ/ /ˈgækʊ/ [relevant] [lip]) is a subtropical City located in Kûyî Bàyâ Kingdom, I Qûhu̹ Empire, within the Warren.

The name Chyameya Bigàbâ Gækû is derived from the Sylvin language, as Chyameya Bigàbâ Gækû was founded by Casey, who was culturaly Rattu.

Climate

Chyameya Bigàbâ Gækû has a yearly average temperature of 8°C (46°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -9°C (16°F). Chyameya Bigàbâ Gækû receives an average of 297 cm/y (116 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Chyameya Bigàbâ Gækû covers an area of nearly 27 km2 (10 mi2), and an average elevation of 3204 m (10511 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Chyameya Bigàbâ Gækû was founded durring the late 17th century in early spring of the year 1598, by Casey. The establishment of Chyameya Bigàbâ Gækû was only bairly constructed. The sheer number of problems with its founding were enough to make several of the backers funding Chyameya Bigàbâ Gækû's construction back out of the project. Casey pushed on reguardles, and Chyameya Bigàbâ Gækû was finished, but starts off as a terible place to live.

Chyameya Bigàbâ Gækû was built using the conventions of Rattu durring the late 17th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Chyameya Bigàbâ Gækû is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature plaster covered brickwork used to form structures with an emphasis on symmetry, proportion, geometry and the regularity of parts. Orderly arrangements of columns, pilasters and lintels, as well as the use of semicircular arches, hemispherical domes, niches and aediculae can be found everywhere such that only size of building and yard can be used to measure the general prosparity of a given building's owners due to a general wealthy feeling the style gives off.

Chyameya Bigàbâ Gækû is is constructed arround a series of broad packed earth mainstreets which form overlapping circles, with smaller strait roads linking the circiles to eachother at varrious points. The city sits comfortably behind a palisade wall complete with a timber gatehouse and battlments. Chyameya Bigàbâ Gækû's timber-based walls has sufferd soem light damage, reducing its function a little in some spots, but could almsot certainly preform as expected... Though some of the worse spots could lead to the loss of defenders lives if attackers identified the weaknesses ahead of time.

Chyameya Bigàbâ Gækû has the unmistakable air of a city on its last legs. Everything is a bit slipshod and ramshackle. Everyone is at work, or drinking. No one has anything in their eyes other than fear and despair. Whatever industry once fueled Chyameya Bigàbâ Gækû ’s existence has dried up and the city is drifting down the stream of history as it dries up. The locals seem to have responded to their slow downfall by recreating Chyameya Bigàbâ Gækû as one of the strictest places imaginable. Everyone’s actions are clearly directed by laws they keep in heart and mind at all times. Orderly byond order is a phrase which the city brings to mind.

Cultural Notes

Chyameya Bigàbâ Gækû's garrison was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used has a sleek, linear appearance with stylized, often geometric ornamentation. The primary facade of its buildings often featured a series of set backs that create a stepped outline. Low-relief decorative panels can be found at entrances, around windows, along roof edges or as string courses. It was best known for its use of smooth finish building materials such as stucco, concrete block, glazed brick or mosaic tile. Decorative details can incorporate various artistic or exotic motifs to suit the building's function or the architect's whim. Chevrons, zigzags, and other geometrical motifs are common forms of ornament.

In Chyameya Bigàbâ Gækû grains of dust blow into perfectly neat rows.

The Giant cockroach near Chyameya Bigàbâ Gækû are known to be a mutant strain of the creature.

Chyameya Bigàbâ Gækû's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in spring and involves reenactments to channel Chronomancy energies of tier 3 via guttural bellowing.
