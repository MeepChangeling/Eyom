Fìkh Zv-67g Jĭěd (/ˈhɪkɨ/ /ʤɛd/ [neat; fine (okay)] [engine; motor]) is a subtropical Town located in Wh-22asev Zone, Ěslorvogwin District, within the Federation of Alveria.

The name Fìkh Zv-67g Jĭěd is derived from the Constructi language, as Fìkh Zv-67g Jĭěd was founded by Zeshêrî Trepí, who was culturaly Constructi.

Climate

Fìkh Zv-67g Jĭěd has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 30°C (86°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). Fìkh Zv-67g Jĭěd receives an average of 221 cm/y (87 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. Fìkh Zv-67g Jĭěd covers an area of nearly 5 km2 (2 mi2), and an average elevation of 2506 m (8221 ft) above sea level.

History

In time immemorial, reportedly some time during the late 2nd century after an anomalously snowy winter, runoff from snow-melt caused Lake Mistake to overflow its banks and spill into Stream Buxly. The resulting flood was isolated to the area around Fìkh Zv-67g Jĭěd, which was swallowed by the waters for several days. Fìkh Zv-67g Jĭěd lost 180 people, 265 livestock, and 65 buildings in the disaster. The disaster is remembered as the Heartache Drownings.
