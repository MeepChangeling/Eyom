Zdo̠ Zdet Gri (/zdo̠/ /zdet/ [boring] [mule]) is a subtropical Large City located in I̽vli̽la Parish, Zröntsökid Diocese, within the Irus.

The name Zdo̠ Zdet Gri is derived from the Goblin language, as Zdo̠ Zdet Gri was founded by Cora, who was culturaly Wood Elf.

Climate

Zdo̠ Zdet Gri has a yearly average temperature of 22°C (71°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 18°C (64°F). Zdo̠ Zdet Gri receives an average of 157 cm/y (61 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. Zdo̠ Zdet Gri covers an area of nearly 67 km2 (26 mi2), and an average elevation of 7038 m (-19859 ft) above sea level.

Overview

Zdo̠ Zdet Gri was founded durring the early 12th century in winter of the year 1118, by Cora. The establishment of the new community went well, though many minor issues had to be solved as time went on. This was enough to delay construction and push back the formal opening ceramony, leading to some embarisment for Cora.

Zdo̠ Zdet Gri was built using the conventions of Wood Elf durring the early 12th century. Naturaly, all settlmentss have their own look to them, and Zdo̠ Zdet Gri is no diffrent. The city's buildings feature timber framed wooden shiethed or brick construction, which gives form to a very formalized, rational, expence effishent arcatectural style based on strictly symmetrical designs which universaly feature pitched roofs, shutters, and the occasional column or pilaster for a decorative touch.

Zdo̠ Zdet Gri is is constructed arround a series of narrow baked earthen mainstreets which form concentric circles, with smaller strait roads linking the circiles to each other at varrious points. The city is defended by arcane means. It's hard to spot at first, but there's a tell tell shimmer in the air arround Zdo̠ Zdet Gri, and you can spot the ocasional warding glyph carved into a rock or tree all arround town. These mystical defences are ancient, unknowable, and unassailable by current means... Assuming everything is in working order. Otherwise, the wards are little more than a deathtrap. Zdo̠ Zdet Gri's Relic of the World That Was has sufferd soem light damage, reducing its function a little in some spots, but could almsot certainly preform as expected... Though some of the worse spots could lead to the loss of defenders lives if attackers identified the weaknesses ahead of time.

Zdo̠ Zdet Gri has the unmistakable air of a city on its last legs. Everything is a bit slipshod and ramshackle. Everyone is at work, or drinking. No one has anything in their eyes other than fear and despair. Whatever industry once fueled Zdo̠ Zdet Gri ’s existence has dried up and the city is drifting down the stream of history as it dries up.

Cultural Notes

Zdo̠ Zdet Gri's garrison was built using a different architectural style from the rest of the town. The style used is characterized by their massive, monolithic and 'blocky' appearance with a rigid geometric style. It was best known for its rough, unfinished surfaces, unusual shapes, heavy-looking materials, straight lines, and small windows. Modular elements were often used to form masses representing specific functional zones, grouped into a unified whole.

In Zdo̠ Zdet Gri rain occurs in reverse with water pooling up from the earth then falling into the skies to from clouds, this unsettles every visitor that sees it.

The ﻿Ifrit near Zdo̠ Zdet Gri are known to be more aggressive than normal.

Zdo̠ Zdet Gri's citizens partake in a curious ritual relating to their local kami. It takes place in spring and involves line dance to channel Wild Magic energies of tier 3 via singing.
