Hûchâ Magu̽ Kâchyâ (/ˈhʊʧɑ/ /ˈmagu̽/ [exotic] [percent]) is a temperate City located in the Temmī̄f Principality of the Lallalo.

The name Hûchâ Magu̽ Kâchyâ is derived from the Sylvin language, as Hûchâ Magu̽ Kâchyâ was founded by Cä̂f̄nḱ Yó̄nḱ 'Amethyst Gina' Rē̋ Béchīs Gawm, who was culturaly Rattu.

Hûchâ Magu̽ Kâchyâ's roads were poorly made when first laid. The locals often repair the road by putting down wooden decking.

History

In time immemorial, reportedly some time during the early 2nd century, Hûchâ Magu̽ Kâchyâ was attacked by savage trollss living nearby. The details of the conflict are hazy at best due to many conflicting accounts. What is known is Hûchâ Magu̽ Kâchyâ lost 185 people, 209 livestock, and 23 buildings. The conflict ended after roughly 66, when members of Hûchâ Magu̽ Kâchyâ's militia enacted an operation to defend a particular expert, and ensure they remain out of enemy hands. The operation was complicated by the officers in charge fight among themselves, weakening the army as a whole. The conflict ended with an assault and siege on the strategic location, which ended in defeat for Hûchâ Magu̽ Kâchyâ's forces. The war is remembered in legend by Hûchâ Magu̽ Kâchyâ's bards, historians, and legend keepers.
