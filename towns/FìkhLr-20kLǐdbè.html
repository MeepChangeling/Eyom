Fìkh Lr-20k Lǐdbè (/ˈmidɛl/ /ˈlɪdbɤ/ [blind] [rice]) is a subtropical Town located in the Khǐnèzhog District of the Federation of Alveria.

The name Fìkh Lr-20k Lǐdbè is derived from the Constructi language, as Fìkh Lr-20k Lǐdbè was founded by Trthêg̈kdo Zlêg̈, who was culturaly Constructi.

Fìkh Lr-20k Lǐdbè receives an average of 223 cm/y (87 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. Fìkh Lr-20k Lǐdbè covers an area of nearly 5 km2 (2 mi2), and an average elevation of 2062 m (6765 ft) above sea level.

In time immemorial, reportedly some time during the late 2nd century, Fìkh Lr-20k Lǐdbè was attacked by soldiers from another nation, waging a greater campaign. The details of the conflict are hazy at best due to many conflicting accounts. What is known is Fìkh Lr-20k Lǐdbè lost 118 people, 353 livestock, and 45 buildings. The conflict ended after roughly 152, when members of Fìkh Lr-20k Lǐdbè's militia enacted an operation to destroy or capture a particular enemy artifact. The operation was complicated by an outbreak of plague which struck the ranks, killing many and laying low many more. The conflict ended with an assault and siege on the research site, which ended in a stalemate for Fìkh Lr-20k Lǐdbè's forces. The war is remembered in legend by Fìkh Lr-20k Lǐdbè's bards, historians, and legend keepers.
