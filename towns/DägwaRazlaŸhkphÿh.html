Dägwa Razla Ÿhkphÿh (/dəˈgwa/ /raˈzla/ [tomorrow] [dot; spot]) is a subtropical Large City located in the Nahan Provence of the Hobben.

The name Dägwa Razla Ÿhkphÿh is derived from the Goblin language, as Dägwa Razla Ÿhkphÿh was founded by Byocthi, who was culturaly Undermountain.

Climate

Dägwa Razla Ÿhkphÿh has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 30°C (86°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). Dägwa Razla Ÿhkphÿh receives an average of 217 cm/y (85 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. Dägwa Razla Ÿhkphÿh covers an area of nearly 172 km2 (68 mi2), and an average elevation of 2946 m (9665 ft) above sea level.

History

In time immemorial, reportedly some time during the early 2nd century, Dägwa Razla Ÿhkphÿh was attacked by savage tribes living nearby Dägwa Razla Ÿhkphÿh. The details of the conflict are hazy at best due to many conflicting accounts. What is known is Dägwa Razla Ÿhkphÿh lost 271 people, 271 livestock, and 66 buildings. The conflict ended after roughly 164, when members of Dägwa Razla Ÿhkphÿh's militia enacted an operation to escort a particular elites group to a safe location. The operation was complicated by the officer in charge of Dägwa Razla Ÿhkphÿh's forces was incompetent, and issue many foolish and-or unprofessional orders. The conflict ended with pitched battle between both forces, which ended in a crushing defeat for Dägwa Razla Ÿhkphÿh's forces. The war is remembered in legend by Dägwa Razla Ÿhkphÿh's bards, historians, and legend keepers.
