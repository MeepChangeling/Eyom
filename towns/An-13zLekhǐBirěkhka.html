An-13z Lekhǐ Birěkhka (/wə̝x/ /ˈlexɪ/ [loose; easy] [rust]) is a subtropical Town located in Quasiuw Parish, Rubivælǐ District, within the Federation of Alveria.

The name An-13z Lekhǐ Birěkhka is derived from the Constructi language, as An-13z Lekhǐ Birěkhka was founded by Mraez Êtco, who was culturaly Constructi.

Climate

An-13z Lekhǐ Birěkhka has a yearly average temperature of 28°C (82°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 32°C (89°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 25°C (77°F). An-13z Lekhǐ Birěkhka receives an average of 280 cm/y (110 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. An-13z Lekhǐ Birěkhka covers an area of nearly 6 km2 (2 mi2), and an average elevation of 5316 m (17440 ft) above sea level.
