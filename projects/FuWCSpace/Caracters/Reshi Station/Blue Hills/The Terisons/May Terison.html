Overview

May is the twin brother of Kay Terison. Many people erroneously believe they are a couple, but the two merely have an extremely close sibling bond. Albeit one where they are comfortable helping each other with things most people would only feel comfortable doing with a partner. The two are faternal twins, and due to their mother's biosculpted modifications were born at the same time, coming into the world cuddled up and peacefully resting.

This dynamic continued through life, with the two seemingly only happy when they could spend plenty of time with each other. This caused extreme tension during their teenage years when their parents believed they were in a sexual relationship (Primarily due to catching May tailoring a pair of panties for Kay so they'd fit nicely, while he was wearing them (Purely for the prefect fit).). This tension was never resolved, leading to the two leaving home at the first opportunity and electing to move somewhere where if people did think they were a couple, no one would care.

Kay is an athletic tomboy with an unending love for zero-g sports. She hates to watch games, but loves to play them, and has recently become a professional 0G-Lacross player with a position as the new goalie for the Reshi Silver Hawks. She couldn't be happier.
