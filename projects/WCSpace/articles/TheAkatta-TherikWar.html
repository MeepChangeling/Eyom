The Akatta-Therik War was a pivotal conflict in galactic history, marking a significant shift in political and economic power within the Steller Empire Intercoperation Organization (SEIO aka the Galactic Bureaucracy / the Bureaucracy). The war not only devastated the involved regions but also led to the relocation of the Galactic Bureaucracy's capital, profoundly affecting sectors such as the H-99 Resort Sector.

Before the war, the Bureaucrasy was focuse dprimarily on the economic development of Prime and Urban sectors. This focus led to a reduction of other priorities in other regions, including conflict resolution. Growing tensions between the Akatta hive intelligence and the neighboring Therik civilization were overlooked due to SEIO's then current priorities.

The Akatta, a Class III hive intelligence, had long maintained a tenuous peace with the neighboring Therik Republic under a non-aggression pact. The Therik, a technologically advanced and culturally rich species, relied on this pact to focus on their internal development and external trade. As a species somewhat byond the Galatic Standard at the time, the Therik became exceptionaly wealthy (particuarly in terms of GDP per capita). Their neirbors, meanwhile, were constrained by the Hive Intelegence Quarintine and Restriction Act.

Unable to even begin approching their neighbor's prosparity, the Akatta chose to raid the Therik Republic for resources and to take out its frustrations. The initial assaults were devastating for the Therik. The Akatta, utilizing their collective intelligence and coordinated strategies, overwhelmed several key Therik outposts and colonies. The hive's relentless and unified attacks caught the Therik off guard, leading to significant early losses. The invasion quickly escalated into a full-scale war, drawing in various allies and sympathizers on both sides, quickly plunging a twelth of the Orion Arm into the conflict. The Galactic Bureaucracy, initially hesitant to intervene, soon realized the potential for the conflict to destabilize the entire region and threaten the broader galactic peace.

Major Battles

  • The Battle of Korriban V: One of the earliest and most brutal battles, where the Akatta forces decimated a major Therik colony, leading to a significant loss of life and resources.
  • Siege of Lyris: A prolonged siege where the Therik, leveraging their technological prowess, managed to hold off Akatta forces for months, buying time for a strategic counteroffensive.
  • The Standoff at Myrna: A turning point where combined Therik and allied forces managed to push back the Akatta, showcasing the resilience and tactical ingenuity of the Therik.

Galactic Bureaucracy's Intervention

Recognizing the threat of the conflict spiraling out of control, the Galactic Bureaucracy intervened, both diplomatically and militarily. As a part of the nogotiation with allied factions, the Bureaucracy agreed to relocate their capital (and thus naval yards) to a new more strategicaly relevent location in the Kellis Sector. The relocation of the capital proved to be key, allowing for the rapid deployment of peacekeeping forces and more efficient diplomatic negotiations.

After years of brutal fighting, the war culminated in the Treaty of Solas. This treaty, brokered by the Bureaucracy, mandated a cessation of hostilities and outlined reparations and territorial adjustments. The Akatta were forced to retreat from several occupied territories and make reparations to the Therik. Tentions remain high, but with the Bureaucracy's current location, their responce time is simply too fast for any side to take millitary action without also being ready to fight the Galatic Navy.

Relocation of the Galactic Capital

The relocation of the Galactic Bureaucracy's capital had profound implications. While the move was strategically sound, enabling quicker response times for diplomats and the Galactic Navy, it also led to the economic decline of sectors like the H-99 Resort Sector. Once bustling with trade and tourism, H-99 saw a mass exodus of corporations and infrastructure, leading to an ongoing period of hardship and decline.

The Akatta-Therik War also led to political realignments within the Galactic Bureaucracy. The need for a more centralized and efficient response to conflicts became evident, leading to reforms in how the Bureaucracy managed its military and diplomatic resources. With these reforms came new leaders within SEIO's many departments, resulting in shifts in policy and direction across far more than the defence sector. Hardly any aspect of the Bureaucracy's operations were uneffected by the move.

The Akatta-Therik War was a watershed moment in galactic history, highlighting the fragility of peace and the far-reaching consequences of conflict. The war's impact on regions like H-99 Resort Sector serve as a stark reminder of how quickly fortunes can change in the ever-shifting landscape of interstellar politics.
