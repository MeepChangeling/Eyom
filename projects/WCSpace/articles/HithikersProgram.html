Program Overview

The Galactic Hitchhiker's Program is a volunteer-based effort run by a small but dedicated group of me, Axxani (I'm a hive mind. Don't worry, I'm not an all consuming swarm. Unless you're made of honey or something tasty like aphids.). Our program revolves around randomly selected locations on Earth where we discreetly establish designated pickup points. These points are chosen for their relative obscurity and low likelihood of immediate detection, ensuring that only those truly eager for a cosmic adventure are likely to find them.

Individual humans who arrive at a designated point at a pickup time will be shuttled off world and assigned a host family or individual who will be your guide to the galixy. A smaller number of points are reserved for humans looking for romance with an alien partner. Plenty of us are also into xenos, so a small matchmaking service has been established for this purpose.

How It Works

  1. Volunteer Recruitment
    • We recruit volunteers from various species across the galaxy (Mostly within Quiet Space) who are keen on meeting humans. These volunteers range from curious scholars and cultural enthusiasts to those seeking companionship or even potential romantic partners. (Also me.   2. Pickup Points
    • We establish hidden pickup points at random spots around the world. These locations are carefully selected to avoid detection by Earth’s governments and to ensure the safety of both our volunteers and the participating humans.
  3. Arrival
    • On a predetermined schedule (Current locations and times can be found on this page.), our volunteers arrive at these pickup points, ready to welcome any human who has discovered the location and wishes to embark on an interstellar journey. (Including MIB agents, provided you've quit working for those assholes.) Fair warning, if you panic on seeing one of us we might leave you behind. The pickup windows are very tight and we may not have time to calm you down, so prepare yourself to see non-human persons before you arrive. Also we do most of the pickups, so if you're scared of insects or aurthropods this program may not be for you.
  4. Embarking
    • Humans who arrive at these points and choose to join the program are swiftly transported to our vessels, where they begin their journey across the stars. Due to the secrecy imposed by Earth’s governments, these humans must remain off-planet until the day when the existence of extraterrestrial life is publicly acknowledged. This is for your protection as various agencies across the world will detain you uppon your return if they discover you've been offworld.
  5. Integration and Exchange
    • Once aboard, humans are introduced to their alien hosts, who guide them through various cultural exchanges, educational programs, and adventures across different worlds. Special care is taken to ensure their well-being and to facilitate meaningful connections between species. (Unless you're me, apparently...     • Among our pickup points, a select few are specifically designated for humans interested in exploring romantic relationships with aliens. These locations are marked with unique symbols, and volunteers with similar interests are assigned to these points to increase the likelihood of forming deeper connections. Our Mission and Vision

The Galactic Hitchhiker's Program aims to build a foundation of trust and friendship between humans and extraterrestrial beings. By bringing adventurous humans into the fold, we hope to create ambassadors who will one day bridge the gap between Earth and the galaxy at large (and also have friends of your species, which is you know, kinda cool. Also you make the best entertainment in the sector.).

Our ultimate vision is a future where interstellar travel and cooperation are not simply commonplace but just "the way it is". A future where the people of Earth can freely explore the wonders of the universe, and help us take this dump from a one to a ten, like it was in the good old days. Until that day arrives, we remain committed to ensuring that every human who joins our program has a transformative and enriching experience.
