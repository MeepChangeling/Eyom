Incident Report

MIB-01: Battle of Los Angeles
Date: February 25, 1942
Classification: Top Secret
Prepared by: Agent A1
Archival Reference: MIB/IncidentReports/1942/BLA/01

Summary:

On February 25, 1942, an extraterrestrial incursion occurred over the city of Los Angeles, California. This incident, later referred to as the Battle of Los Angeles, involved hostile actions by a UFO identified as a vessel of extraterrestrial origin (Later revealed to be under the operation of pirates). The event resulted in the first recorded extraterrestrial engagement by Earthâ€™s military forces and subsequently led to the establishment of the Men in Black (MiB) organization.

Timeline of Events:

00:15 PST: Initial radar contacts detected an unidentified object approximately 120 miles west of Los Angeles. Object traveling at an estimated speed of 2000 mph.

00:32 PST: Coastal anti-aircraft batteries are placed on high alert following a series of confirmed visual sightings of a luminous object.

01:44 PST: Object enters Los Angeles airspace. Civilian air raid sirens activated, causing widespread panic and blackout procedures across the city.

02:21 PST: Anti-aircraft artillery opens fire on the object, expending over 1,400 shells over a 58-minute period. Object demonstrates advanced evasion capabilities, sustaining minimal damage.

03:18 PST: A squadron of six P-51 Mustang fighter aircraft dispatched from March Field engage the object. After a prolonged aerial skirmish, the Mustangs successfully incapacitate the UFO utilizing concentrated machine gun fire and tactical maneuvers. Alien craft's energy shields (Later revealed to be Class III Generation 8 Technology) were unable to endure prolonged bombardment by .50 caliber machinegun fire, resulting in an overload of the power core. This tatic remained effective untill 1996 with the releace of Generation 10 shield systems.

03:48 PST: UFO crash lands in an uninhabited area near the Santa Monica Mountains. Ground forces secure the crash site under the cover of darkness.

Analysis and Findings:

Object Description:

Diameter: Approximately 50 feet.

Composition: Unknown metallic alloy, exhibiting properties of extreme durability and advanced thermal resistance.

Markings: Indications of pirate insignia consistent with extraterrestrial groups identified in later MiB intelligence. Exact affiliation remains unknown.

Crew:

Recovery teams discovered three extraterrestrial entities within the wreckage. All occupants exhibited humanoid characteristics with notable biological differences, including bioluminescent skin and ocular adaptations for low-light environments. Species unknown, related to Grays.

Technology:

The craft contained advanced propulsion systems and weaponry far surpassing current Earth technologies. Analysis is ongoing to reverse-engineer key components despite friendly contact with other groups.

Casualties and Damage:

Civilian: No fatalities reported. Minor injuries and property damage due to anti-aircraft fire and debris fallout.

Military: Seven casualties (2 gunners, 5 pilots). Minor damage to military equipment.

Subsequent Actions:

Cover Story Implementation:

Public narrative controlled through a combination of misinformation and suppression of eyewitness accounts. The incident officially attributed to "war nerves" and misidentification of weather balloons and commercial aircraft.

Formation of MiB:

Due to the nature and potential threat of extraterrestrial activities, Executive Order 9926 authorized the creation of the Men in Black (MiB) to monitor, manage, and mitigate extraterrestrial threats.

MiB operational protocols established, including rapid response teams, technological research divisions, and global surveillance networks.

Conclusion:

The Battle of Los Angeles represents a significant milestone in human-extraterrestrial relations, marking the first verified encounter and engagement with a hostile extraterrestrial force. The actions taken by the U.S. military, in conjunction with subsequent MiB initiatives, have laid the groundwork for ongoing defense and intelligence operations concerning extraterrestrial phenomena.

Clandestine contact made with Jir resulted from the battle in the form of an appology for failing to safeguard Earth. Their views clash with Galatic Law, resulting in them paying a small fine each year to maintain discrete contact with their "neighbors".
