Texan Brotherhood

The Texan Brotherhood is a splinter faction of the Brotherhood of Steel that is located in the former state of Texas. Unlike their counterparts in the West Coast, the Texan Brotherhood has embraced a more flexible and adaptable approach to their mission of preserving and advancing technology.

The current leader of the Texan Brotherhood is Elder Jackson, a charismatic and visionary leader who has focused on expanding the Brotherhood's influence and working with other factions to rebuild civilization in the region.

The Texan Brotherhood has a diverse industrial base that includes manufacturing, farming, and resource extraction. They have established several major settlements, including their headquarters at the Alamo, which serves as a hub for research, development, and training.

The Texan Brotherhood has a well-developed infrastructure that includes a sophisticated transportation network and communication lines. They have also established alliances and trade relationships with other factions, which has helped them to secure resources and expand their influence.

In terms of military capabilities, the Texan Brotherhood has a highly-trained and well-equipped army that includes a mix of traditional soldiers and power armored knights. They also have a number of advanced weapons and technology at their disposal, including Vertibirds, laser weapons, and robotics.

The Texan Brotherhood is widely regarded as one of the most powerful factions in the region, and has played a major role in several major conflicts. Despite this, they are generally respected by other factions due to their commitment to preserving technology and rebuilding civilization.
