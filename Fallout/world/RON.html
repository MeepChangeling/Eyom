Ronto

Ronto is a faction located in the former Canadian province of Ontario. Led by a High Council of elected representatives, Ronto is a democratic nation that is dedicated to rebuilding and improving the lives of its citizens in the aftermath of the nuclear apocalypse.

Ronto's industrial base is focused on agriculture and resource extraction. They have a robust farming industry and control many mines and oil fields. They also possess advanced manufacturing capabilities, but these are limited compared to the other factions.

In terms of infrastructure, Ronto has established a network of settlements and cities throughout Ontario, with well-maintained roads and transportation systems connecting them. They also possess a well-trained and equipped police force, and have established a system of laws and regulations to maintain order and protect their citizens.

Ronto's military forces are composed of highly-trained soldiers who are equipped with a mix of advanced and conventional weaponry. They are known for their defensive capabilities, utilizing sophisticated fortifications and tactics to repel attacks.

The High Council is the governing body of Ronto, and is responsible for making important decisions and setting policies for the faction. They are highly respected by the citizens of Ronto and are known for their commitment to democracy and the well-being of their people.

Overall, Ronto is a stable and prosperous nation that commands respect throughout the wasteland. While they may not have the military might of some of the other factions, their commitment to democracy and rebuilding society makes them a valuable ally for any group that shares their values.
