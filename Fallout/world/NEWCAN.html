The Republic of Texas is a powerful and independent nation that has emerged in the aftermath of the Great War. With a focus on agriculture, oil production, and advanced manufacturing, the Republic has become a major economic power in the southern United States.

Under the leadership of President James Bowie, the Republic has established a strong military force and invested heavily in infrastructure and technology. The Republic's capital is located in the city of Houston, which has been rebuilt as a modern metropolis with skyscrapers, highways, and advanced infrastructure.

The Republic's industrial base is focused on advanced manufacturing, with factories producing everything from robots and computers to weapons and vehicles. The Republic is also a major producer of oil, with vast reserves of petroleum that have fueled its economic growth.

In terms of infrastructure, the Republic has invested heavily in transportation, building a network of highways, railways, and airfields that connect its major cities and military bases. The Republic also has a robust telecommunications network, including satellite communication and high-speed internet access.

The Republic's military is well-trained and well-equipped, with a strong focus on defensive capabilities and border security. The Republic's soldiers are equipped with advanced weapons and armor, including power armor, laser weapons, and missile launchers.

The current leader of the Republic of Texas is President James Bowie, a charismatic and respected leader who has overseen the Republic's continued growth and prosperity. Bowie is a strong believer in the Republic's independence and has emphasized the importance of maintaining a strong military and defensive capabilities.
