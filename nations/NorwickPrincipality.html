The Norwick Principality (also known as Norwick) is a an independent principality located in central Eyom. Its predominantly Sylvin citizens live within its 269,590 km2, and primarily speak Sylvin.

Government and Politics

The Norwick Principality is ruled by a Prince of Princess who serves as the nation's Sovereign. They are functionally the King or Queen of their nation, but Norwik's constitution prohibits any sovereign from claiming the title of King or Queen unless Norwik has taken the throne of the Sylvanian Empire.
In Norwick, the sovereign is chosen by the common folk and serves at their pleasure, and supported by the banks.

The ruling class of Norwick are the most elite citizens of Oldden, with few exceptions. Their authority is stable enough endure even fierce shocks, due to their current greatest asset, the popular support of the populace. The largest issue currently facing Norwick is the existing political elites are demanding more power.

Millitary

The Norwick Principality's millitary is average for Eyom with a few boons and banes. It favors a mixture of armored calvery and infantry, while using airships for close support and command centers. Norwick's millitary is known for its embrasure of traditional combat methods, and being slow to adopt any new aracana or tech.

The millitary favors a doctrine of trench warfare in its millitary operations, but is willing and able to use others should the need arise. Norwick's armed forces are mostly of volenteers with some conscripts in times of war, which generaly view themselves as protectors of the region who serve the intrests of the nobility, by applying violence as needed.
