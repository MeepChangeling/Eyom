The City-state of Kakoray (also known as Kakoray) is a a member of the League of Gnollish Cities, a loose alliance of nations in north western Eyom which loosly function as part of a greater whole. Its predominantly Gnollish citizens live within its 141,660 km2, and primarily speak Zräysian.

Government and Politics

As with all of the gnollish city states, the City-state of Kakoray is ruled by a King or Queen. In Kakoray, the sovereign is the most prominent citizen within Dahivu̽Î Mudâ (regardless of their former station), and supported by subordinate lords pledged to them.

The ruling class of Kakoray are people who are a part of magically empowered bloodlines, with few exceptions. Their authority is stable enough endure even fierce shocks, due to their current greatest asset, their diplomats' awe inspiring cunning. The largest issue currently facing Kakoray is the sovereign's attempts to crush an insubordinate who has obtained too much power.

Millitary

The City-state of Kakoray's millitary is below average for Eyom, with a few disadvantages and not very many banes. It favors a mixture of armored calvery and infantry, while using airships for close support and command centers. Kakoray's millitary makes use of advanced arcana above all else.

The millitary favors a doctrine of defence diplomacy in its millitary operations, but is willing and able to use others should the need arise. Kakoray's armed forces are mostly of volenteers with some conscripts in times of war, which generaly view themselves as a caste of warriors appart from civilians who are more of an orniment for their nation to wear than a sword or shield, by bringing war to the deserving.
