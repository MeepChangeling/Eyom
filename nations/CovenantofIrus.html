The Covenant of Irus (also known as The Covenant) is a a member of the League of Gnollish Cities, a loose alliance of nations in north western Eyom which loosly function as part of a greater whole. Its predominantly Wood Elven citizens live within its 1,231,816 km2, and primarily speak Zdaalish.

Government and Politics

The Covenant of Irus is a theocratic state ruled by the Church of Irus which appoints the head of their church as the head of state. This individuals title is Alderpriest ^(also Grand Patriarch^) and they serve as an absolute monarch. In The Covenant, the sovereign is supported by a powerful circle of mages, as well as the enritity of the church.

The ruling class of The Covenant are the most skilled of mages, with few exceptions. Their authority is stable enough endure even fierce shocks, due to their current greatest asset, their zelitiously loyal populace. The largest issue currently facing The Covenant is a diplomat has bungled something proper fierce.

Millitary

The Covenant of Irus's millitary is ahead of the curve for Eyom, with few problems and a few serious advantages. It favors a mixture of armored calvory and special forces, leaving its infantry as a rear guard. The Covenant uses airships in the conventional fashion, as troop transport and commandposts, but has been known to attempt to develop air-based artillery platforms from time to time. The Covenant's millitary is known for its embrasure of traditional combat methods, and being slow to adopt any new aracana or tech.

The millitary favors a doctrine of deep operation in its millitary operations, but is willing and able to use others should the need arise. The Covenant's armed forces are mostly composed of members of a dedicated warrior caste, with volenteers padding out the ranks, which generaly view themselves as a caste of warriors above from civilians who serve the intrests of the nobility, by applying violence as needed.
