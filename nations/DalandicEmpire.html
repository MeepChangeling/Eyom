The Dalandic Empire (also known as Daland) is a a trade empire located within central Eyom. Its predominantly Tauric citizens live within its 1,774,147 km2, and primarily speak Wèshèti.

Government and Politics

The Dalandic Empire is ruled by an Emperor or Empress. In Daland, the sovereign is whomever is chosen by a particular artifact upon the death of the prior sovereign, and supported by the banks.

The ruling class of Daland are the most elite citizens of Dap Utiewhāg, with few exceptions. Their authority is stable enough endure even fierce shocks, due to their current greatest asset, their nobles arcane powers. The largest issue currently facing Daland is one of the nation's grand schemes has gone disastrously wrong.

Millitary

The Dalandic Empire's millitary is average for Eyom with a few boons and banes. It favors a mixture of armored calvory and special forces, leaving its infantry as a rear guard. Daland uses airships in the conventional fashion, as troop transport and commandposts, but has been known to attempt to develop air-based artillery platforms from time to time. Daland's armed forces heavily favor advanced technology.

The millitary favors a doctrine of shock and awe in its millitary operations, but is willing and able to use others should the need arise. Daland's armed forces are a fully conscripted force kept in line by an officer's corpse, which generaly view themselves as protectors of the people who defend the state more than the people, by applying violence as needed.
