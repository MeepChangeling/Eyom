The Koiapian City-state (also known as Koiapi) is a a member of the League of Gnollish Cities, a loose alliance of nations in north western Eyom which loosly function as part of a greater whole. Its predominantly Gnollish citizens live within its 159,040 km2, and primarily speak Zräysian.

Government and Politics

As with all of the gnollish city states, the Koiapian City-state is ruled by a King or Queen. In Koiapi, the sovereign is whomever is chosen by a particular artifact upon the death of the prior sovereign, and supported by subordinate lords pledged to them.

The ruling class of Koiapi are individuals with substantial military power, with few exceptions. Their authority is relatively stable, with strong legitimacy, due to their current greatest asset, their extremely efficient bureaucracy. The largest issue currently facing Koiapi is the previous sovereign's incompetence is still damaging Koiapi.

Millitary

The Koiapian City-state's millitary is average for Eyom with a few boons and banes. It favors an antiquated millitary compossion with its troops being almost entirly infantry. It makes up for these shortfalls with mandatory conscription of sourcerors, and other mages. Koiapi's millitary is known for its embrasure of traditional combat methods, and being slow to adopt any new aracana or tech.

The millitary favors a doctrine of trench warfare in its millitary operations, but is willing and able to use others should the need arise. Koiapi's armed forces are entirly of volenteers, which generaly view themselves as protectors of the region who work to expand their nation's reach, by bringing war to the deserving.
