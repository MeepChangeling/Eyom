Before Combat Unless directed by a master, thorn dogs will spread out and try to position themselves to be able to attack.

During Combat Thorn dogs will try to pack attack those who are isolated from their allies and spellcasters first. Preferentially, they will concentrate on one target at a time, disabling them before moving on.

Morale Thorn dogs are implacable foes, they will not flee combat unless it serves some tactical or strategic purpose. They are breed as warriors and they will fight to the death if needed.
