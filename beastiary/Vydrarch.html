The terrifying creature known as the vydrarch was originally thought to be an entirely unique creature, but in recent years, reports from sailors have hinted at the existence of other such beasts.

Vydrarchs live solely for the thrill of destruction and chaos, but what elder force could have created such a monster is unknown. Their dual heads act in tandem to destroy vessels and devour cargo, but survivors of such attacks claim that the heads can act independently of one another if need be, occasionally talking or even arguing among themselves.

A vydrarch is 40 feet long from tail to head and weighs 10 tons.
