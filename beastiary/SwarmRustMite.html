Quick Rust (Ex)

Any metal object in a fiendish rust mite swarm is destroyed. Masterwork and magic items that fail a DC 17 Reflex save gain the broken condition (destroyed on a second failed save). The DC for this ability is Dexterity-based.

Environment any (cold, mountains, underground)
Organization solitary, pair, or rust field (3-10)
Treasure none

It is thought that the demons that originally came to Pradjna brought these mites with them to aid in the extraction of ore, but they, like the other demons, became trapped in the frozen tundra. Occasionally, a swarm is released and creates havoc until it is destroyed.
