Witherstench

This creature resembles a putrid yellow skunk with very little fur and whose body is splotched with tiny purple spots. Patches of darker fur are sprinkled about its body.

Witherstench CR 1

XP 400
N Small magical beast
hp 13 (2d10+2)
Fort +4, Ref +5, Will +0

Base Atk +2; CMB +0; CMD 12 (16 vs. trip)
Feats Weapon Finesse

Environment temperate forests
Organization pack (3-12)
Treasure standard

A witherstench (also called skunk beast) is a mutated relative of the common skunk. It is about 4 feet long and weighs around 50 pounds. Its diet consists of carrion, and the creature is always found in areas where such “food” is plentiful.
