Though called riotblood, these nasty little undead can congeal anywhere large amounts of blood are spilled in anger. A street riot might produce a few Riotblood, while the grand melee of a gladiatorial arena might spawn dozens, and a horde of hundreds might congeal in the wake of an especially horrific battle.

Riotblood are masses of blood, saliva, broken bones, spit teeth and other ichors animated by a dim and malicious instinct. Riotblood exist only to spread violence, the more random and unprovoked the better.

The vile slicks of bloody tissue can ooze beneath doors and turn a peaceful dinner hour in a crowded tavern or the most serene high church service into a savage free-for-all.
