Cotton Candy Beard (Su)

(1/1d4 rounds, DC 17)

The faerhle shoots of a gob of its cotton-candy-like beard at a target within 30 feet. The target must succeed on a Fortitude saving throw or become entangled. While entangled, it is anchored and takes 1d6 acid damage at the beginning of each of its turns.

Weapon Evoker Mastery

As a swift action, weapon dealing extra elemental damage adds +1d4 of same energy type.

Faerhle are unique demons that bear a strong resemblance to dwarves. Faerhles have three fingers on each hand, and their large beards are made up of a fluffy, sticky substance that grows in a variety of bright colors.
