Carrion Sense (Ex)

Angheuvores gain the scent ability, but only for corpses and badly wounded creatures (creatures with 25% or fewer hit points).

Flesh Consumption (Ex)

If an angheuvore consumes at least 1 pound of humanoid flesh, it gains a number of temporary hit points equal to double its Hit Dice for 24 hours. These temporary hit points stack with other sources of temporary hit points, but not with other uses of this ability.

Paralytic Saliva (Su)

Three times per day, as an immediate action an angheuvore can affect a living creature it bites with paralytic saliva. An affected creature is paralyzed for 1 round unless it succeeds at a DC 13 Fortitude save. Elves are immune to this effect. The save DC is Constitution-based.
