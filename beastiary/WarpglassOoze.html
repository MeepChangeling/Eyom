Warpglass oozes lurk in marshes and forests, masquerading as a simple puddle or stretching across the surface of a larger body of water. Barely sentient, warpglass oozes primarily concern themselves with the basics of survival. Warpglass oozes can subsist on nearly any sort of organic material, but they strongly prefer fresh meat to vegetation or carrion. Habitat and Society

Though warpglass oozes thrive in nearly any environment, they frequently drift toward rural, stony areas where standing water is common.
