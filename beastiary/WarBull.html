These enormous bovines are 15 feet tall, measure nearly 30 feet from horns to tail, and weigh approximately 16 tons.

Bred for war and violence, war bulls are aggressive and trained for combat. They charge most anything that stands in their way and gore whatever is left. Skilled drovers oversee and train them for warfare, driving them into enemy formations to break the front line or crush foot archers and other lightly armored targets.
