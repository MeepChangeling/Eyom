Eel, Electric (Dire)

Dire electric eels have elongated, cylindrical bodies. Their coloration is dark gray-brown on the back and yellow or orange on the belly. Mature males have a darker color on the belly. They have no scales. The mouth is square, and positioned right at the end of the snout.

Jolt (Ex)

Once every 1d6 rounds, a dire electric eel can generate a powerful jolt of electricity. Creatures within 5 feet of the eel suffer 4d8 points of electricity damage and are stunned for 1d6 rounds. Those within 10 feet suffer 2d8 points of electricity damage and are stunned for 1d3 rounds. Creatures no farther away than 15 feet suffer 1d8 points of electricity damage and are stunned for 1 round. A DC 13 Fortitude save halves this damage and negates stunning. The save DC is Constitution-based. Public domain image from WP Clipart .
