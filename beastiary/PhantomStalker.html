Phantom Stalker

This creature resembles a tall, powerfully muscled humanoid with fiery red flesh. Its face is flattish and oval with a large wide mouth and round, inset eyes of orange.

Phantom Stalker CR 4

XP 1,200
N Medium outsider (elemental, extraplanar, fire, shapechanger)
hp 45 (6d10+12)
Fort +7, Ref +7, Will +2
Defensive Abilities flames of healing; Immune fire, elemental traits
Melee 2 burning claws +10 (1d4+4 plus 1d4 fire and grab)
Base Atk +6; CMB +10 (+14 to grapple); CMD 22
Feats Improved Initiative, Power Attack, Skill Focus (Perception)
Skills Fly +11, Perception +12, Survival +9
Languages Common, Ignan

Environment Elemental Planes of Fire
Organization solitary or pair
Treasure none

Phantom stalkers live in the Elemental Planes of Fire. In its true form, a phantom stalker appears as a column of fire. It can take a humanshaped form, appearing as a muscular, fiery red humanoid about 8 feet tall. A phantom stalker rarely enters the Material Plane on its own. If encountered, it is usually in the employ of a spellcaster that has summoned it there to do his bidding. A phantom stalker can be summoned using a summon monster IV spell.

A phantom stalker is 6 feet tall and weighs about 180 pounds.

A phantom stalker attacks with its claws, attempting to grab its opponent and pull it into its body.
