In the tropical rainforests and the Jungle lurk animalistic offshoots of the cyclopes that once dominated the region. Known as ngogas, these half-orangutan, half-cyclops hybrids are infamous for their unpredictability and unnatural agility. In cyclops legends, the ngogas were a gift from otherworldly patrons, magical beings infused with the brutality and insight afforded cyclopes, but lacking the intelligence that would make them viable competitors.
