Half-fiend dryads are cursed guardians of nature that take to their duties with a gruesome zeal that often inspires lumberjacks and the like to stay far from their territory. It is quite easy to recognize an area frequented by a half-fiend dryad, as corpses of trespassers are creatively displayed throughout the branches above to ward away any other curious travelers. Druids, rangers, and other friends of nature receive a single warning to leave the realm of one of these infernal creatures, and all others are immediately attacked when detected.

A half-fiend dryad nears 7 ft. in height and weighs 720 pounds.
