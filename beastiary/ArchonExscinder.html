Censor Text (Su)

An exscinder can attempt to magically steal or modify any text within 100 feet as a standard action.

Against an unattended text, it automatically succeeds. A creature in possession of a text can attempt a DC 22 Will save to negate this ability. A stolen text teleports directly into the exscinder’s hand.
