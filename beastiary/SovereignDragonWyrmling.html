Sovereign Dragon, Wyrmling

This magnificent dragon is covered in splendid gold scales, and horns jut from its head like a crown.

Wyrmling Sovereign Dragon CR 7

XP 3,200
N Small dragon
Init +6; Senses dragon senses; Perception +13

AC 20, touch 13, flat-footed 18 (+2 Dex, +7 natural, +1 size)
hp 68 (8d12+16)
Fort +8, Ref +10, Will +10
Immune paralysis, sleep

Speed 50 ft., fly 150 ft. (average)
Melee bite +13 (1d6+4), 2 claws +12 (1d4+3)
Special Attacks breath weapon (20 ft. cone, 2d6 sonic, DC 16)

Str 17, Dex 14, Con 15, Int 14, Wis 15, Cha 14
Base Atk +8; CMB +10; CMD 22 (26 vs. trip)
Feats Improved Initiative, Iron Will, Lightning Reflexes, Weapon Focus (bite)
Skills Appraise +13, Bluff +13, Diplomacy +13, Fly +4, Intimidate +13, Knowledge (arcana, history) +13, Perception +13, Sense Motive +13
Languages Celestial, Common, Draconic
SQ dogmatic discordance

Dogmatic Discordance (Su)

Good or evil creatures take a -2 penalty when making saving throws against a sovereign dragon’s spells, spell-like abilities, breath weapon, and aura.

Environment any mountains
Organization solitary
Treasure triple

Guardians of balance, sovereign dragons, or lungwangs as they are also known, were placed in the skies by the gods themselves to safeguard harmony in the world.
