Flame-spawned trolls walk upright but hunched forward with sagging shoulders. Their gait is uneven, and when they run, their arms dangle and drag along the ground. For all this seeming awkwardness, flame-spawned trolls are very agile.

A typical adult flame-spawned troll stands 9 feet tall and weighs 500 pounds. Females are slightly larger than males. A flame-spawned trollâs rubbery hide is brownish-red, mottled green and gray, or putrid gray. The hair is usually greenish black or iron gray.

Flame-spawned trolls have no fear of death: They launch themselves into combat without hesitation, flailing wildly at the closest opponent. Even when confronted with cold, they try to attack.
