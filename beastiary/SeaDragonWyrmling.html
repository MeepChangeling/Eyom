Sea Dragon, Wyrmling

The long, undulating body of this serpentine dragon rolls and bends with scales the color of the ocean’s waves.

Wyrmling Sea Dragon CR 4

XP 1,200
CG Small dragon (water)
Init +6; Senses dragon senses; Perception +4

AC 18, touch 13, flat-footed 16 (+2 Dex, +5 natural, +1 size)
hp 37 (5d12+5)
Fort +7, Ref +6, Will +6
Immune electricity, paralysis, sleep

Speed 40 ft., fly 150 ft. (avr.), swim 60 ft.
Melee bite +7 (1d6+1), 2 claws +7 (1d4+1)
Space 5 ft.; Reach 5 ft. (10 ft. with bite)
Special Attacks breath weapon (20-ft. cone, d6 fire damage, DC 13)

Str 13, Dex 14, Con 13, Int 14, Wis 15, Cha 14
Base Atk +5; CMB +5; CMD 17 (21 vs. trip)
Feats Alertness, Great Fortitude, Improved Initiative
Skills Diplomacy +10, Fly +12, Intimidate +10, Knowledge (nature) +10, Perception +4, Sense Motive +12, Stealth +14, Survival +10, Swim +17
Languages Aquan, Common, Draconic
SQ water breathing

Environment any water
Organization solitary
Treasure triple

Infused with the power of waves and storms, sea dragons or jiaolungs, as they are known in many lands are draconic protectors of oceans and their creatures. Possessing tempestuous natures, sea dragons wander widely, sometimes claiming thousands of miles of ocean and coastlines as their protectorates.
