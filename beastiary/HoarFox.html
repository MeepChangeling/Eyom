Hoar Fox

This creature appears to be a silvery-gray fox with sapphire colored eyes.

hoar fox CR 2

XP 600
N Small magical beast (cold)
Init +3; Senses darkvision 60 ft., low-light vision, scent; Perception +8

hp 24 (3d10+6)
Fort +5; Ref +6; Will +2
Immune cold
Weaknesses vulnerability to fire

Melee bite +5 (1d6)
Special Attacks breath weapon (cold)

Base Atk +3; CMB +2; CMD 15 (19 vs. trip)
Feats Skill Focus (perception), Weapon Finesse
Skills Perception +8, Stealth +11 (+15 in natural environment), Survival +2; Racial Modifiers +4 Stealth in natural environment

Environment cold hills
Organization solitary, pair, or pack (3-6)
Treasure none (pelt is worth 200 gp)

Hoar foxes hunt in packs and can often be found lairing near settled areas. Hoar foxes are often hunted for their fur as it brings a handsome sum on the open market. Hoar foxes are timid creatures and rarely aggressive unless threatened. They bite an attacker to try to scare it away, but if that tactic fails, they use their breath weapon. While attacking a hoar fox with fire seems like a sensible solution (given their vulnerability to it), such an attack destroys its pelt and renders it worthless.
