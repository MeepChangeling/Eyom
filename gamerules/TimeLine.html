General Timeline of Eyom

The Tyrant's Era

Events occuring durring the first age of Zo, when it was under the dominion of The Tyrant.

  • -14m FC
    Zo is Created

    A divine known only as "The Tyrant" creates Zo along with the other contents of Zo's Crystal Sphere. They are not partiuruly skilled at bringing their visions to life, and punish their creations for failing to live up to expectations rather than improving their own skills.

  • -13.2m FC
    Birth of the Primordials

    The Tyrant creates lesser divines to manage his creation for him. These entities are named "The Primordials" and exist more as anthropomorphic Personifications than people. The Primordials are able to keep The Tyrant's vision of reality working, but only just. Tol was created at this time.

  • -10.6m FC
    The First Tyranical War

    The Tyrant, unable to achieve his exact vision, begins to steal creations of other gods from all actross the multiverce. His thefts are aided and abeded by a means of reaching through the phlogistion which even divines can use. This method has been eraced from time.

    The multiverce's major divines track the thefts back to The Tyrant and begin a war against him and his influence. The war ravages the multiverce for an age, ending only when The Tyrant's means of travel is destroyed and he is confined to his own sphere. The majority of his stolen items are not recovered.

  • -8.6m FC
    The First Divines

    Using stolen knolage, items, and mortals from across the unierce The Tyrant is able to create traditional deities, beings needing the worship of mortals to survive, to further manage his creation. These goes have defined personalities, apperances, and goals, and do a very quick job of getting mortal kind to heed the Tyrant's will with near prefection. Yet, this is not enough for the Tyrant, who begins to stew in his guilded cage.

    Hospitlar and Thekle were created at this time.
