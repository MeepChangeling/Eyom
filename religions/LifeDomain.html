Domain: Life

Life Domain

You are attuned with the cellular truth of mortality. You have dominion over the rythems of the body and the threashold of death.

Granted Powers

Halt Death (Su): You gain access to the spell gentel repose as a spell-like ability at-will.

Restore Life (Su): At 6th level, spells which restore the dead to life may ignore the requirments for time and body state once per day.

Domain Spells: 1st—Restore Corpse, 2nd—Life Pact, 3rd—Animate Dead, 4th—Persistent Vigor, 5th—Breath of Life, 6th—Undeath to Death, 7th—Regenerate, 8th—Death Clutch, 9th—True Resurrection.
