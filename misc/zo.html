Eyom is an unusual contenent as it is surrounded by a divine energy barrier commonly called the God Wall. The God Wall prevents easy passage (and up untill recently all passage) too and from Eyom. The otehr 9 contenents have no such walls and can be travled too and from without difficulty.

Zo is a sphere, and if one could safly sail across the deep oceans, you could sail in a straight line arround the entire world and come back to where you started.
