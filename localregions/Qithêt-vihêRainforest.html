The Qithêt-vihê Rainforest (/ˈvihɘ/ Qithêt the Gay's [Proper Noun (name of person)] [lesbian]) is a temperate rainforest on the coast of the Ancestor's Range region. Qithêt-vihê Rainforest is a relatively typical rainforest for Ancestor's Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Qithêt-vihê Rainforest has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). The Qithêt-vihê Rainforest receives an average of 71 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Qithêt-vihê Rainforest covers an area of nearly 7904 km2 (4912 mi2), and an average elevation of 5392 m (17690 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Qithêt-vihê Rainforest is overseen by Potentate Zaune Bacinn Kassana, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 54% of the population self-report as devout. The Qithêt-vihê Rainforest is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
