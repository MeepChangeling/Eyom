The Glijijdrasumo Moor (/ˈsumo/ Glijijdra's Shrine [Proper Noun (name of person)] [shrine]) is a subtropical moor within the temperate deciduous forest of the Glowing Sands region. Glijijdrasumo Moor is a relatively typical moor for Glowing Sands, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Glijijdrasumo Moor has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 22°C (71°F). The Glijijdrasumo Moor receives an average of 132 cm/y (51 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Glijijdrasumo Moor covers an area of nearly 10648 km2 (6617 mi2), and an average elevation of 5071 m (16637 ft) above sea level.
