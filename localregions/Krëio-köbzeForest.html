Climate

The Krëio-köbze Forest has a yearly average temperature of 17°C (62°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 11°C (51°F). The Krëio-köbze Forest receives an average of 214 cm/y (84 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Krëio-köbze Forest covers an area of nearly 16006 km2 (9947 mi2), and an average elevation of 4609 m (15121 ft) above sea level.
