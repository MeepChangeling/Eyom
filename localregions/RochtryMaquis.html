The Rochtry Maquis (rochtry maquis Rochtry [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical maquis on the coast of the Ancestor's Range region. The Rochtry Maquis is well known for its thriving abundance of plant life.

Climate

The Rochtry Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 34°C (93°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 38°C (100°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 29°C (84°F). The Rochtry Maquis receives an average of 199 cm/y (78 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Rochtry Maquis covers an area of nearly 9188 km2 (5710 mi2), and an average elevation of 16011 m (9579 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Rochtry Maquis is overseen by Potentate Qikzotz Andros Orphelræd, a god serving within The Council of Twelve, the region's principal religion. Approximately 44% of the population self-report as devout. The Rochtry Maquis is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Rochtry Maquis has its economic roots in Ancestor's Range's export of Stone, but has developed its own arcana industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.
