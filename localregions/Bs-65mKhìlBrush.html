Climate

The Bs-65m Khìl Brush has a yearly average temperature of 26°C (78°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 23°C (73°F). The Bs-65m Khìl Brush covers an area of nearly 10223 km2 (6353 mi2), and an average elevation of 3082 m (10111 ft) above sea level.
