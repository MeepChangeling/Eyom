The Ned Ol Holt (/ned/ /ol/ Rose [anxious] [plant]) is a temperate holt within the wetland of the Gnashed Coast region. The Ned Ol Holt is well known for its thriving abundance of plant life.

Climate

The Ned Ol Holt has a yearly average temperature of 13°C (55°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 3°C (37°F). The Ned Ol Holt receives an average of 212 cm/y (83 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Ned Ol Holt covers an area of nearly 8215 km2 (5105 mi2), and an average elevation of 1954 m (6410 ft) above sea level.
