The Sodugija Heath (/ˈsodu/ /ˈgiʤa/ Smooth [bump] [exempt]) is a subtropical heath on the coast of the Brownmarsh region. Sodugija Heath is a relatively typical heath for Brownmarsh, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Sodugija Heath has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 22°C (71°F). The Sodugija Heath receives an average of 134 cm/y (52 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Sodugija Heath covers an area of nearly 18957 km2 (11781 mi2), and an average elevation of 6141 m (20147 ft) above sea level.
