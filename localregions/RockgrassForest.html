The Rockgrass Forest (rockgrass /ˈfɔrəst/ Rockgrass [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical forest on the coast of the Cutmountains region. Rockgrass Forest is a relatively typical forest for Cutmountains, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Rockgrass Forest has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 30°C (86°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Rockgrass Forest receives an average of 69 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Rockgrass Forest covers an area of nearly 7572 km2 (4706 mi2), and an average elevation of 8704 m (-14393 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Rockgrass Forest is overseen by Potentate Thalldir Hemed Osgaraid, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 21% of the population self-report as devout. The Rockgrass Forest is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Rockgrass Forest is predominantly economicly reiant on Cutmountains's export of Stone, and has no outstanding industries byond what is required for its role in that process.

The Rockgrass Forest has an Imperial Development Index of 6. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 6 means there are 6 locations within the Rockgrass Forest which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..

As a courtesy for scholars and agents, the Rockgrass Forest is rated M1 by Eyom’s banks. Business ventures within the Rockgrass Forest are not expected to return value in yearly terms.
