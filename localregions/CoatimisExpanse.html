The Coatimis Expanse (coatimis Expanse Coatimis [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Fractal Range region. Coatimis Expanse is a relatively typical expanse for Fractal Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Coatimis Expanse has a yearly average temperature of 34°C (93°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 39°C (102°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 30°C (86°F). The Coatimis Expanse receives an average of 68 cm/y (26 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Coatimis Expanse covers an area of nearly 14933 km2 (9280 mi2), and an average elevation of 15377 m (7499 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Coatimis Expanse is overseen by Potentate Phosoi Eadwalia Perawn, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 16% of the population self-report as devout. The Coatimis Expanse is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.
