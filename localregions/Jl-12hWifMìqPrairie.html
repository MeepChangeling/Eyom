The Jl-12h Wif Mìq Prairie (/wif mɨq/ Jl-12h's Dictation [Proper Noun (name of person)] [psychology]) is a subtropical prairie on the coast of the Anvil Badlands region. Jl-12h Wif Mìq Prairie is a relatively typical prairie for Anvil Badlands, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Jl-12h Wif Mìq Prairie has a yearly average temperature of 22°C (71°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). The Jl-12h Wif Mìq Prairie receives an average of 276 cm/y (108 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Jl-12h Wif Mìq Prairie covers an area of nearly 12836 km2 (7977 mi2), and an average elevation of 6057 m (19872 ft) above sea level.
