The Löt-vak Expanse (/lo˞t/ /vak/ Tigerelf [tiger] [elf]) is a subtropical expanse within the wetland of the Kiteshield Peninsula region. Löt-vak Expanse is a relatively typical expanse for Kiteshield Peninsula, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Löt-vak Expanse has a yearly average temperature of 12°C (53°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 22°C (71°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 2°C (35°F). The Löt-vak Expanse receives an average of 208 cm/y (81 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Löt-vak Expanse covers an area of nearly 12419 km2 (7718 mi2), and an average elevation of 4771 m (15652 ft) above sea level.

The Löt-vak Expanse is's peoples are culturally Gnollish, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include sharing wealth and goods with others and social progress toward some eventual utopia, as well as a unique science festival and a rain festival.

Local Religion

The Löt-vak Expanse is overseen by Potentate Bystrix Hyacchusa Siononiu, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 58% of the population self-report as devout. The Löt-vak Expanse is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
