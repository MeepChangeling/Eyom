The Vrōqüe-moz Holt (/vroːq˔/ /moz/ Tarantula [desk] [spider]) is a subtropical holt on the coast of the Torpenvale region. Vrōqüe-moz Holt is a relatively typical holt for Torpenvale, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Vrōqüe-moz Holt has a yearly average temperature of 8°C (46°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant -11°C (13°F). The Vrōqüe-moz Holt receives an average of 159 cm/y (62 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Vrōqüe-moz Holt covers an area of nearly 30592 km2 (19013 mi2), and an average elevation of 3866 m (12683 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Vrōqüe-moz Holt is overseen by Potentate Mogenar Ponwyllotus Xanditan, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 57% of the population self-report as devout. The Vrōqüe-moz Holt is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Vrōqüe-moz Holt has its economic roots in Torpenvale's export of Coal, but has developed its own legal industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.
