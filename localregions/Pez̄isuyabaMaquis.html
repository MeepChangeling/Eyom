The Pez̄isuyaba Maquis (/peˈz̚isu/ /ˈjaba/ Simplify [suggestion] [backward]) is a subtropical maquis within the temperate rainforest of the Iron Coast region. The Pez̄isuyaba Maquis is the political heartlands of the Union of Engineers.

Climate

The Pez̄isuyaba Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 22°C (71°F). The Pez̄isuyaba Maquis receives an average of 301 cm/y (118 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Pez̄isuyaba Maquis covers an area of nearly 11967 km2 (7437 mi2), and an average elevation of 1393 m (4570 ft) above sea level.
