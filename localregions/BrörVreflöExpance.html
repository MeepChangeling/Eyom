The Brör Vreflö Expance (/bro˞r/ /ˈvreflo˞/ Thundering [extreme; intense; severe; radical] [hearing]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Timberlund region. The Brör Vreflö Expance is home to the last free Faefolk court in Eyom.

Climate

The Brör Vreflö Expance has a yearly average tempature of 9°C (48°F), with its average tempature durring the summer being a pleasent 25°C (77°F) and its average tempature durring the winter being a freezing -6°C (22°F). The Brör Vreflö Expance recieves an average of 148 cm/y (58 in/y) of percipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow durring the pleasently short winter months. The Brör Vreflö Expance covers an area of nearly 12145 km2 (7548 mi2), and an average elevation of 4523 m (14839 ft) above sealevel.

In respect to this universal cultural element, we have split racial classification by culture while keeping the name of each race for our own purposes. Should you see the same race listed more than once in the following section, this is to desinate a major cultural division within a race.

The Brör Vreflö Expance is's peoples are culturaly Fae, but have their own local customs and traditions. Their shared vaules and traditions include piety and devotion to the gods and individual rights and freedom of action, as well as a unique winter festival and a arcane festival.

Fae

The Fae subculture native to the Brör Vreflö Expance are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Fae culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of significant scent or perfume uses and base their sence of individual idenity on ethnic membership. They also place greater cultural emphasus on ethnic purity of blood and culture and conquest and domination of others.

Local Religion

The Brör Vreflö Expance is overseen by Potentate Bilna Euandra Eardwawl, a god serving within Covenent of Irus, the region's pricipal religion. Aproximently 24% of the population self-report as devout. The Brör Vreflö Expance is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevent to their portfolio.

Settlments and Economy

Brör Vreflö Expance has its economic roots in Timberlund's export of Timber, but has developed its own ceramic industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.

The Brör Vreflö Expance has an Imperial Development Index of 23. For the refrence of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 23 means there are 23 locations within the Brör Vreflö Expance which the Empire can securly and regurly transport matrials and personell..
