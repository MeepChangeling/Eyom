The Ávïji-yäk Woodlands (/ə˞ˈvɪji/ /jɑk/ Fortune's Rest [lucky] [leisure; recreation]) are a group of subtropical woodland on the coast of the Highpeaks region. Ávïji-yäk Woodlands are a relatively typical woodland for Highpeaks, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Ávïji-yäk Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 29°C (84°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 32°C (89°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 25°C (77°F). The Ávïji-yäk Woodlands receives an average of 68 cm/y (26 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Ávïji-yäk Woodlands covers an area of nearly 8775 km2 (5453 mi2), and an average elevation of 8415 m (-15341 ft) above sea level.
