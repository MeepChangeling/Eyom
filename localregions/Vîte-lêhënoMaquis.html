The Vîte-lêhëno Maquis (/ˈvɪte/ /lɘˈhëno/ Wisedrain [wise] [drain]) is a temperate maquis on the coast of the Mullberry Dales region. Vîte-lêhëno Maquis is a relatively typical maquis for Mullberry Dales, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Vîte-lêhëno Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 21°C (69°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 12°C (53°F). The Vîte-lêhëno Maquis receives an average of 207 cm/y (81 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Vîte-lêhëno Maquis covers an area of nearly 9822 km2 (6104 mi2), and an average elevation of 3584 m (11758 ft) above sea level.
