The Gàqidjig Expance (/gə/ /ˈqidjig/ Whiptongue [word; language; phrase] [sharp]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Abysstro region. Gàqidjig Expance is a relativly typical expanse for Abysstro, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Gàqidjig Expance has a yearly average tempature of 15°C (59°F), with its average tempature durring the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average tempature durring the winter being a cold 3°C (37°F). The Gàqidjig Expance recieves an average of 151 cm/y (59 in/y) of percipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow durring the pleasently short winter months. The Gàqidjig Expance covers an area of nearly 16736 km2 (10401 mi2), and an average elevation of 3356 m (11010 ft) above sealevel.

The Gàqidjig Expance is overseen by Potentate Ugysus Osber Béibhintora, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's pricipal religion. Aproximently 28% of the population self-report as devout.
