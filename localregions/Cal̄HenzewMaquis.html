The Cal̄ Henzew Maquis (/kall/ /henˈzuː/ Purpleprose [maroon] [accent (of language); dialect]) is a subtropical maquis on the coast of the Silverhills region. Cal̄ Henzew Maquis is a relatively typical maquis for Silverhills, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Cal̄ Henzew Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Cal̄ Henzew Maquis receives an average of 146 cm/y (57 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Cal̄ Henzew Maquis covers an area of nearly 23529 km2 (14623 mi2), and an average elevation of 8028 m (-16611 ft) above sea level.

The Cal̄ Henzew Maquis is home to 837167 people. They break down into the following demographics: 67% Iron Elves, 15% Wareneese, 3% Kitsune, 7% Kitsune, 6% Kivu, and ~2% Other.

The Cal̄ Henzew Maquis is's peoples are culturally Sylvanian, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include vengeance and execution of just vendettas and industry and the ability to work tirelessly, as well as a unique music festival and a food festival.

Iron Elf

The Iron Elf subculture native to the Cal̄ Henzew Maquis are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Iron Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of color choices with social meaning to them and base their sence of individual idenity on extended family out to cousins and like kin. They also place greater cultural emphasus on hardiness and endurance before woes and seeking fortune in new places or new roles.

Rattu

The Rattu subculture native to the Cal̄ Henzew Maquis are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Iron Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of piercings, whether minor or elaborate and base their sence of individual idenity on lineages based on ancient hero-ancestors. They also place greater cultural emphasus on membership in an elite bloodline or caste and vengeance and execution of just vendettas.

Kitsune

The Kitsune subculture native to the Cal̄ Henzew Maquis are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Iron Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of color choices with social meaning to them and base their sence of individual idenity on voluntary mutual-assistance brotherhoods. They also place greater cultural emphasus on conquest and domination of others and vengeance and execution of just vendettas.

Quenn

The Quenn subculture native to the Cal̄ Henzew Maquis are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Iron Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of tattoos of some cultural significance and base their sence of individual idenity on inheritance of an ancient body modification. They also place greater cultural emphasus on discipline and obedience to the law and seeking fortune in new places or new roles.

Kivu

The Kivu subculture native to the Cal̄ Henzew Maquis are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Iron Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of significant scent or perfume uses and base their sence of individual idenity on voluntary mutual-assistance brotherhoods. They also place greater cultural emphasus on individual rights and freedom of action and submission to lawful authority.

Local Religion

The Cal̄ Henzew Maquis is overseen by Potentate Thalldir Klothnis Xantheofsta, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 41% of the population self-report as devout. The Cal̄ Henzew Maquis is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Cal̄ Henzew Maquis is predominantly economicly reiant on Silverhills's export of Iron, and has no outstanding industries byond what is required for its role in that process.

The Cal̄ Henzew Maquis has an Imperial Development Index of 6. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 6 means there are 6 locations within the Cal̄ Henzew Maquis which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..

As a courtesy for scholars and agents, the Cal̄ Henzew Maquis is rated M1 by Eyom’s banks. Business ventures within the Cal̄ Henzew Maquis are not expected to return value in yearly terms.
