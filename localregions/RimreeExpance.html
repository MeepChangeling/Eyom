The Rimree Expance (rimree expance Rimree [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Ancestor's Range region. Rimree Expance is a relativly typical expanse for Ancestor's Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Rimree Expance has a yearly average tempature of 30°C (86°F), with its average tempature durring the summer being a blistering 34°C (93°F) and its average tempature durring the winter being a pleasent 25°C (77°F). The Rimree Expance recieves an average of 196 cm/y (77 in/y) of percipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain durring the fall. The Rimree Expance covers an area of nearly 17279 km2 (10738 mi2), and an average elevation of 11672 m (-4655 ft) above sealevel.

The Rimree Expance is's peoples are culturaly Constructi, but have their own local customs and traditions. Their shared vaules and traditions include industry and the ability to work tirelessly and sexual license and wantonness, as well as a unique fire festival and a arcane festival.

Construct

The Construct subculture native to the Rimree Expance are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Construct culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of piercings, whether minor or elaborate and base their sence of individual idenity on dynastic lineages with cadet branches. They also place greater cultural emphasus on beauty and seductive charm and filial devotion to family and parents.

Goblin

The Goblin subculture native to the Rimree Expance are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Construct culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of culturally-significant jewelry or accessories and base their sence of individual idenity on inheritance of an ancient body modification. They also place greater cultural emphasus on submission to lawful authority and societal or ethnic superiority over all outsiders.

Kivu

The Kivu subculture native to the Rimree Expance are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Construct culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of painted skin markings that sometimes change and base their sence of individual idenity on regional and province-based identity. They also place greater cultural emphasus on ethnic purity of blood and culture and purging evil and expelling the wicked.

Local Religion

The Rimree Expance is overseen by Potentate Kitara Æþelenthos Galatea, a god serving within The Council of Twelve, the region's pricipal religion. Aproximently 32% of the population self-report as devout. The Rimree Expance is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevent to their portfolio.

Settlments and Economy

Rimree Expance is noteworthy for its economic independence from Ancestor's Range's export of Stone. Rimree Expance is particularly renound for its potions industry. It is also has a noteworthy machines industry.

The Rimree Expance has an Imperial Development Index of 37. For the refrence of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 37 means there are 37 locations within the Rimree Expance which the Empire can securly and regurly transport matrials and personell..
