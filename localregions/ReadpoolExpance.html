The Readpool Expance (readpool expance Readpool [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Highpeaks region. Readpool Expance is a relativly typical expanse for Highpeaks, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Readpool Expance has a yearly average tempature of 32°C (89°F), with its average tempature durring the summer being a blistering 36°C (96°F) and its average tempature durring the winter being a warm 27°C (80°F). The Readpool Expance recieves an average of 68 cm/y (26 in/y) of percipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain durring the spring. The Readpool Expance covers an area of nearly 6726 km2 (4180 mi2), and an average elevation of 17112 m (13192 ft) above sealevel.
