Climate

The Mûyû Miya Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 19°C (66°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 14°C (57°F). The Mûyû Miya Woodlands covers an area of nearly 7849 km2 (4878 mi2), and an average elevation of 940 m (3083 ft) above sea level.
