The Fō Yud Woods (/foː/ /jud/ Reset [eager] [recession]) are a group of subtropical woods within the temperate rainforest of the Substrate region. At 477 km2 (296 mi2), Fō Yud Woods is is the smallest wood in Eyom, and also the smallest local region in general.

Climate

The Fō Yud Woods has a yearly average temperature of 14°C (57°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 22°C (71°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 6°C (42°F). The Fō Yud Woods receives an average of 268 cm/y (105 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Fō Yud Woods covers an area of nearly 477 km2 (296 mi2), and an average elevation of 3911 m (12831 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Fō Yud Woods are overseen by Potentate Alotl Prias Lóegairey, a god serving within Covenant of Irus, the region's principal religion. Approximately 16% of the population self-report as devout. The Fō Yud Woods is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Fō Yud Woods is noteworthy for its economic independence from Substrate's export of Agriculture. Fō Yud Woods is particularly renound for its leather industry. It is also has a noteworthy cement industry.

The Fō Yud Woods has an Imperial Development Index of 28. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 28 means there are 28 locations within the Fō Yud Woods which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
