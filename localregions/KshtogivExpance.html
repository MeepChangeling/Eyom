The Kshtogiv Expance (Kshto /giv/ Kshto’s Bane [Proper Noun (name of person)] [hell]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Westbay region. Kshtogiv Expance is a relativly typical expanse for Westbay, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Kshtogiv Expance has a yearly average tempature of 15°C (59°F), with its average tempature durring the summer being a pleasent 25°C (77°F) and its average tempature durring the winter being a cold 6°C (42°F). The Kshtogiv Expance recieves an average of 227 cm/y (89 in/y) of percipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow durring the pleasently short winter months. The Kshtogiv Expance covers an area of nearly 6638 km2 (4125 mi2), and an average elevation of 630 m (2066 ft) above sealevel.

They also place greater cultural emphasus on hardiness and endurance before woes and personal sacrifice for one’s causes or purposes.

The Kshtogiv Expance is overseen by Potentate Drione Klotus Leopeto, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's pricipal religion. Aproximently 22% of the population self-report as devout. The Kshtogiv Expance is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevent to their portfolio.
