The Fë́̂ Yāer Woods (/jɑ̄ɜː/ Soul of Fë́̂ [Proper Noun (name of person)] [soul]) are a group of subtropical woods on the coast of the Fractal Range region. The Fë́̂ Yāer Woods are well known for its thriving abundance of plant life.

Climate

The Fë́̂ Yāer Woods has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Fë́̂ Yāer Woods receives an average of 280 cm/y (110 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Fë́̂ Yāer Woods covers an area of nearly 10692 km2 (6645 mi2), and an average elevation of 8551 m (-14895 ft) above sea level.

The Fë́̂ Yāer Woods are home to 157587 people. They break down into the following demographics: 54% Sylvin, 29% Gnolls, 2% Hobgoblins, 9% Grey Elf, 4% Imperial, and ~2% Other.

The Fë́̂ Yāer Woods are's peoples are culturally Sylvanian, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include humanistic reason and “rational” religion and personal development and limit-pushing, as well as a unique spirit festival and a literary festival.

Sylvin

The Sylvin subculture native to the Fë́̂ Yāer Woods are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Sylvin culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of patterned hair shaving or depilation and base their sence of individual idenity on having or lacking noble blood. They also place greater cultural emphasus on individual rights and freedom of action and harmony with nature and existing life.

Gnoll

While they follow the broader tennents of Sylvin culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of piercings, whether minor or elaborate and base their sence of individual idenity on religious factions or faith alliances. They also place greater cultural emphasus on societal or ethnic superiority over all outsiders and personal indulgence and luxuriant pleasure.

Grey Elf

The Grey Elf subculture native to the Fë́̂ Yāer Woods are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. Imperial

The Imperial subculture native to the Fë́̂ Yāer Woods are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Sylvin culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of impractical or elaborate role-based clothes and base their sence of individual idenity on inheritance of an ancient body modification. They also place greater cultural emphasus on discipline and obedience to the law and societal or ethnic superiority over all outsiders.

Local Religion

The Fë́̂ Yāer Woods are overseen by Potentate Yxtar Sarmusan Emerainthos, a god serving within Tol's Children, the region's principal religion. Approximately 35% of the population self-report as devout. The Fë́̂ Yāer Woods is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
