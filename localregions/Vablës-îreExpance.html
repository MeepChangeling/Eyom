The Vablës-îre Expance (/ˈɪre/ Vablës' Goose Roost [Proper Noun (name of person)] [goose]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Cutmountains region. The Vablës-îre Expance is well known for its thriving abundance of plant life.

Climate

The Vablës-îre Expance has a yearly average tempature of 23°C (73°F), with its average tempature durring the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average tempature durring the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). The Vablës-îre Expance recieves an average of 70 cm/y (27 in/y) of percipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain durring the spring. The Vablës-îre Expance covers an area of nearly 12893 km2 (8013 mi2), and an average elevation of 8193 m (-16069 ft) above sealevel.
