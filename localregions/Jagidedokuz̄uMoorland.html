The Jagidedokuz̄u Moorland (/jaˈgide/ /doˈcuz̚u/ Septicrain [shower] [offensive]) is a subtropical moorland within the wetland of the Illa's Panhandle region. Jagidedokuz̄u Moorland is a relatively typical moorland for Illa's Panhandle, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Jagidedokuz̄u Moorland has a yearly average temperature of 19°C (66°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a cool 20°C (68°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). The Jagidedokuz̄u Moorland receives an average of 216 cm/y (85 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Jagidedokuz̄u Moorland covers an area of nearly 10082 km2 (6266 mi2), and an average elevation of 2113 m (6932 ft) above sea level.
