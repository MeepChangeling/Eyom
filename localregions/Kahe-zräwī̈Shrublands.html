The Kahe-zräwī̈ Shrublands (/zrɑwˈʤɪ/ Kahe's Shakes [Proper Noun (name of person)] [seizure]) are a group of subtropical shrubland within the temperate rainforest of the Polymer Coast region. Kahe-zräwī̈ Shrublands are a relatively typical shrubland for Polymer Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Kahe-zräwī̈ Shrublands has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). The Kahe-zräwī̈ Shrublands receives an average of 297 cm/y (116 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Kahe-zräwī̈ Shrublands covers an area of nearly 12306 km2 (7648 mi2), and an average elevation of 1097 m (3599 ft) above sea level.
