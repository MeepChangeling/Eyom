The Vabîl-vêpíî Forest (/ˈvɘp˔ɪ/ Vabîl's Beacon [Proper Noun (name of person)] [light]) is a temperate forest on the coast of the Bulwark region. Vabîl-vêpíî Forest is a relatively typical forest for Bulwark, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Vabîl-vêpíî Forest has a yearly average temperature of 19°C (66°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 11°C (51°F). The Vabîl-vêpíî Forest receives an average of 145 cm/y (57 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Vabîl-vêpíî Forest covers an area of nearly 8925 km2 (5546 mi2), and an average elevation of 4649 m (15252 ft) above sea level.

The Vabîl-vêpíî Forest is home to 244537 people. They break down into the following demographics: 61% Wareneese, 24% Wareneese, 8% Grey Elf, 5% Elf, and ~2% Other.

The Vabîl-vêpíî Forest is's peoples are culturally Wareneese, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include faithfulness towards one’s chosen friends and discipline and obedience to the law, as well as a unique harvest festival and a history festival.

Wareneese

The Wareneese subculture native to the Vabîl-vêpíî Forest are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wareneese culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of patterned hair shaving or depilation and base their sence of individual idenity on lineages based on ancient hero-ancestors. They also place greater cultural emphasus on industry and the ability to work tirelessly and personal development and limit-pushing.

Elf

The Elf subculture native to the Vabîl-vêpíî Forest are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wareneese culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of intricate hair styles or braiding and base their sence of individual idenity on educational ties to institutions or traditions. They also place greater cultural emphasus on justice and fairness between people and vengeance and execution of just vendettas.

Local Religion

The Vabîl-vêpíî Forest is overseen by Potentate Theheyar Goibhina Parthelþryð, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 39% of the population self-report as devout. The Vabîl-vêpíî Forest is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Vabîl-vêpíî Forest has its economic roots in Bulwark's export of Armor, but has developed its own furniture industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.

The Vabîl-vêpíî Forest has an Imperial Development Index of 24. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 24 means there are 24 locations within the Vabîl-vêpíî Forest which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
