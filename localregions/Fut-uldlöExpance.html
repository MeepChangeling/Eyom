The Fut-uldlö Expance (/fut/ /ˈuldlo˞/ Coin [money] [medal]) is a temperate expanse on the coast of the Salt Warrens region. Fut-uldlö Expance is a relativly typical expanse for Salt Warrens, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Fut-uldlö Expance has a yearly average tempature of 7°C (44°F), with its average tempature durring the summer being an icy 25°C (77°F) and its average tempature durring the winter being a pleasent -10°C (14°F). The Fut-uldlö Expance recieves an average of 234 cm/y (92 in/y) of percipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow durring the pleasently short winter months. The Fut-uldlö Expance covers an area of nearly 10318 km2 (6412 mi2), and an average elevation of 4642 m (15229 ft) above sealevel.

The Fut-uldlö Expance is overseen by Potentate Qhuzphy Feiassa Simoklecate, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's pricipal religion. Aproximently 12% of the population self-report as devout. Fut-uldlö Expance is predominantly economicly reiant on Salt Warrens's export of Salt, and has no outstanding industries byond what is required for its role in that process.
