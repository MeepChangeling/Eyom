The Dÿhv Oyvyebÿh Expance (/dɪ̘v/ /ojvjiˈbɪ̘/ Everfare [always; all the time; forever] [beauty]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Flanged Coast region. Dÿhv Oyvyebÿh Expance is a relativly typical expanse for Flanged Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Dÿhv Oyvyebÿh Expance has a yearly average tempature of 27°C (80°F), with its average tempature durring the summer being a hot 31°C (87°F) and its average tempature durring the winter being a pleasent 24°C (75°F). The Dÿhv Oyvyebÿh Expance recieves an average of 216 cm/y (85 in/y) of percipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain durring the fall. The Dÿhv Oyvyebÿh Expance covers an area of nearly 13424 km2 (8343 mi2), and an average elevation of 5506 m (18064 ft) above sealevel.
