The Pjiqtsktsgi Maquis (/gi/ Pjiqtskts the Lumberjack [Proper Noun (name of person)] [lumber]) is a subtropical maquis within the tropical rainforest of the Illa's Panhandle region. Pjiqtsktsgi Maquis is a relatively typical maquis for Illa's Panhandle, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Pjiqtsktsgi Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 22°C (71°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Pjiqtsktsgi Maquis receives an average of 285 cm/y (112 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Pjiqtsktsgi Maquis covers an area of nearly 8057 km2 (5007 mi2), and an average elevation of 2785 m (9137 ft) above sea level.
