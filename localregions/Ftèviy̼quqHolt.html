The Ftèviy̼quq Holt has a yearly average temperature of 19°C (66°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 11°C (51°F). The Ftèviy̼quq Holt covers an area of nearly 11782 km2 (7322 mi2), and an average elevation of 3139 m (10298 ft) above sea level.
