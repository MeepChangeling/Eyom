The Shḡ Leyvyu Prairie (/lejˈvju/ Shḡ's College [Proper Noun (name of person)] [university]) is a subtropical prairie on the coast of the Lower Warding Range region. Shḡ Leyvyu Prairie is a relatively typical prairie for Lower Warding Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Shḡ Leyvyu Prairie has a yearly average temperature of 32°C (89°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 35°C (95°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 28°C (82°F). The Shḡ Leyvyu Prairie receives an average of 66 cm/y (25 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Shḡ Leyvyu Prairie covers an area of nearly 15559 km2 (9669 mi2), and an average elevation of 11378 m (-5620 ft) above sea level.

The Shḡ Leyvyu Prairie is home to 117950 people. They break down into the following demographics: 67% Imperial, 14% Goblins, 4% Iron Elves, 5% Elf, 8% Tanuki, and ~2% Other.

The Shḡ Leyvyu Prairie is's peoples are culturally Z̚oman, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include beauty and seductive charm and courage and valiance in danger, as well as a unique theater festival and a food festival.

Imperial

The Imperial subculture native to the Shḡ Leyvyu Prairie are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Imperial culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of patterned hair shaving or depilation and base their sence of individual idenity on lineages based on ancient hero-ancestors. They also place greater cultural emphasus on ascetic unworldliness and pious poverty and personal sacrifice for one’s causes or purposes.

Goblin

The Goblin subculture native to the Shḡ Leyvyu Prairie are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Imperial culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of impractical or elaborate role-based clothes and base their sence of individual idenity on inheritance of an ancient body modification. They also place greater cultural emphasus on building things in service of their posterity and sexual license and wantonness.

Elf

The Elf subculture native to the Shḡ Leyvyu Prairie are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. The Tanuki subculture native to the Shḡ Leyvyu Prairie are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. Local Religion

The Shḡ Leyvyu Prairie is overseen by Potentate Godione Eadwintus Ecgberseus, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 14% of the population self-report as devout. The Shḡ Leyvyu Prairie is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Shḡ Leyvyu Prairie is a negative return economy zone at this time. It has an abundence of Undead, though infrastructure to this region of Lower Warding Range is not developed enough to support any major local industry.

The Shḡ Leyvyu Prairie has an Imperial Development Index of 5. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 5 means there are 5 locations within the Shḡ Leyvyu Prairie which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
