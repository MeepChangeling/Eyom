The Nël Zde Heath (/nɛl/ /zde/ Big Mistake [bad] [fluke]) is a subtropical heath within the taiga of the Bulwark region. Nël Zde Heath is a relatively typical heath for Bulwark, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Nël Zde Heath has a yearly average temperature of 9°C (48°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -8°C (18°F). The Nël Zde Heath receives an average of 73 cm/y (28 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Nël Zde Heath covers an area of nearly 18336 km2 (11395 mi2), and an average elevation of 3596 m (11797 ft) above sea level.

The Nël Zde Heath is home to 276244 people. They break down into the following demographics: 51% Wareneese, 27% Goblins, 7% Hobgoblins, 9% Kobold, 4% Imperial, and ~2% Other.

The Nël Zde Heath is's peoples are culturally Wareneese, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include membership in an elite bloodline or caste and exploring the unknown and discovering secrets, as well as a unique science festival and a harvest festival.

Wareneese

The Wareneese subculture native to the Nël Zde Heath are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wareneese culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of painted skin markings that sometimes change and base their sence of individual idenity on educational ties to institutions or traditions. They also place greater cultural emphasus on submission to lawful authority and education and knowledge-seeking.

Goblin

The Goblin subculture native to the Nël Zde Heath are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Wareneese culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of significant scent or perfume uses and base their sence of individual idenity on inheritance of an ancient body modification. They also place greater cultural emphasus on loyalty to one’s friends, family, and own and pacifism and peaceful resolution of problems.

Hobgoblin

The Hobgoblin subculture native to the Nël Zde Heath are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wareneese culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of color choices with social meaning to them and base their sence of individual idenity on dynastic lineages with cadet branches. They also place greater cultural emphasus on vengeance and execution of just vendettas and exploring the unknown and discovering secrets.

Kobold

The Kobold subculture native to the Nël Zde Heath are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wareneese culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of worn weapons, tools or trade implements and base their sence of individual idenity on inheritance of an ancient body modification. They also place greater cultural emphasus on humanistic reason and “rational” religion and societal or ethnic superiority over all outsiders.

Imperial

The Imperial subculture native to the Nël Zde Heath are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Wareneese culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of worn weapons, tools or trade implements and base their sence of individual idenity on ethnic membership. They also place greater cultural emphasus on membership in an elite bloodline or caste and sexual license and wantonness.

Local Religion

The Nël Zde Heath is overseen by Potentate Uros Gwalthniu Goraina, a god serving within Covenant of Irus, the region's principal religion. Approximately 23% of the population self-report as devout. The Nël Zde Heath is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Nël Zde Heath is noteworthy for its economic independence from Bulwark's export of Armor. Nël Zde Heath is particularly renound for its ceramic industry. It is also has a noteworthy research industry.

The Nël Zde Heath has an Imperial Development Index of 37. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 37 means there are 37 locations within the Nël Zde Heath which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..

As a courtesy for scholars and agents, the Nël Zde Heath is rated C1 by Eyom’s banks. Business ventures within the Nël Zde Heath are expected to return market rate at the lowest risk.
