The Jasktppoma Woodlands (/ˈpoma/ Jask's Patron [Proper Noun (name of person)] [customer; patron]) are a group of subtropical woodland within the temperate rainforest of the Illa's Panhandle region. Jasktppoma Woodlands are a relatively typical woodland for Illa's Panhandle, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Jasktppoma Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 23°C (73°F). The Jasktppoma Woodlands receives an average of 287 cm/y (112 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Jasktppoma Woodlands covers an area of nearly 6698 km2 (4162 mi2), and an average elevation of 2763 m (9064 ft) above sea level.

The Jasktppoma Woodlands are overseen by Potentate Kitara Clídnant Muireandi, a god serving within The Council of Twelve, the region's principal religion.
