Climate

The Gèdkùj̼ Moor has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 6°C (42°F). The Gèdkùj̼ Moor covers an area of nearly 10160 km2 (6314 mi2), and an average elevation of 1974 m (6476 ft) above sea level.
