The Yöt-nrem Moor (/jo˞t/ /nrem/ Poorsoil [garbage; trash; rubbish; sewage] [bulb (plant root)]) is a subtropical moor on the coast of the Rivereach region. Yöt-nrem Moor is a relatively typical moor for Rivereach, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Yöt-nrem Moor has a yearly average temperature of 8°C (46°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold -10°C (14°F). The Yöt-nrem Moor receives an average of 304 cm/y (119 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Yöt-nrem Moor covers an area of nearly 17428 km2 (10831 mi2), and an average elevation of 4292 m (14081 ft) above sea level.
