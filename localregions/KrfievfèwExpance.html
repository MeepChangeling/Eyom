Climate

The Krfievfèw Expance has a yearly average tempature of 16°C (60°F), with its average tempature durring the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average tempature durring the winter being a cold 3°C (37°F). The Krfievfèw Expance recieves an average of 290 cm/y (114 in/y) of percipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain durring the summer. The Krfievfèw Expance covers an area of nearly 6686 km2 (4155 mi2), and an average elevation of 2817 m (9242 ft) above sealevel.
