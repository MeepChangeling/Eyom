The Vdriqtpoyayuja Heathland (/jaˈjuʤa/ Drik's Scratch [Proper Noun (name of person)] [record]) is a subtropical heathland within the wetland of the Shattered Peninsula region. Vdriqtpoyayuja Heathland is a relatively typical heathland for Shattered Peninsula, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Vdriqtpoyayuja Heathland has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 22°C (71°F). The Vdriqtpoyayuja Heathland receives an average of 209 cm/y (82 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Vdriqtpoyayuja Heathland covers an area of nearly 16664 km2 (10356 mi2), and an average elevation of 4452 m (14606 ft) above sea level.
