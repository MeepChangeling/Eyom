The Noftaf Kren Fōn Woodlands (/ˈnoftaf/ /kren foːn/ Sportsman's [greedy] [zoo]) are a group of temperate woodland on the coast of the Foxvale region. Noftaf Kren Fōn Woodlands are a relatively typical woodland for Foxvale, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Noftaf Kren Fōn Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 12°C (53°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -1°C (31°F). The Noftaf Kren Fōn Woodlands receives an average of 154 cm/y (60 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Noftaf Kren Fōn Woodlands covers an area of nearly 9615 km2 (5975 mi2), and an average elevation of 4229 m (13874 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Noftaf Kren Fōn Woodlands are overseen by Potentate Qedaos Æþelrea Goronwy, a god serving within Therilism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 11% of the population self-report as devout. The Noftaf Kren Fōn Woodlands is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Noftaf Kren Fōn Woodlands has its economic roots in Foxvale's export of Steel, but has developed its own arcana industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.
