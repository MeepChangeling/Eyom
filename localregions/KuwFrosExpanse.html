The Kuw Fros Expanse (/kuw/ /fros/ Caning [sudden; abrupt; swift] [painting]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Timberlund region. Kuw Fros Expanse is a relatively typical expanse for Timberlund, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Kuw Fros Expanse has a yearly average temperature of 11°C (51°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -3°C (27°F). The Kuw Fros Expanse receives an average of 153 cm/y (60 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Kuw Fros Expanse covers an area of nearly 7468 km2 (4641 mi2), and an average elevation of 3472 m (11391 ft) above sea level.

The Kuw Fros Expanse is's peoples are culturally Wood Elven, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include exploring the unknown and discovering secrets and purging evil and expelling the wicked, as well as a unique storytelling festival and a winter festival.

Local Religion

The Kuw Fros Expanse is overseen by Potentate Thetneas Cassandrona Gronchus, a god serving within Therilism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 14% of the population self-report as devout. The Kuw Fros Expanse is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
