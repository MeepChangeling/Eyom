The Terrenburn Prairie (terrenburn /ˈprɛri/ Terrenburn [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical prairie within the wetland of the Polymer Coast region. Terrenburn Prairie is a relatively typical prairie for Polymer Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Terrenburn Prairie has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). The Terrenburn Prairie receives an average of 218 cm/y (85 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Terrenburn Prairie covers an area of nearly 10371 km2 (6445 mi2), and an average elevation of 1891 m (6204 ft) above sea level.
