The Vnör Bruk Woods has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 4°C (39°F). The Vnör Bruk Woods covers an area of nearly 10395 km2 (6460 mi2), and an average elevation of 1844 m (6049 ft) above sea level.
