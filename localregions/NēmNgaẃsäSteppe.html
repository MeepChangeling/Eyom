The Nēm Ngaẃsä Steppe (/nīːm/ /ŋgɒ́sæ/ Lockheart [exempt] [heart]) is a subtropical steppe within the glacier of the Pressingstone region. Nēm Ngaẃsä Steppe is a relatively typical steppe for Pressingstone, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Nēm Ngaẃsä Steppe has a yearly average temperature of 26°C (78°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 31°C (87°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 22°C (71°F). The Nēm Ngaẃsä Steppe receives an average of 71 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Nēm Ngaẃsä Steppe covers an area of nearly 9356 km2 (5814 mi2), and an average elevation of 4617 m (15147 ft) above sea level.
