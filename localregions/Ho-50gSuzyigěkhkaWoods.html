Climate

The Ho-50g Suzyigěkhka Woods has a yearly average temperature of 30°C (86°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 33°C (91°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 26°C (78°F). The Ho-50g Suzyigěkhka Woods covers an area of nearly 9240 km2 (5742 mi2), and an average elevation of 4197 m (13769 ft) above sea level.

Their shared values and traditions include vengeance and execution of just vendettas and seeking fortune in new places or new roles, as well as a unique science festival and a fire festival.
