The Y̼ùwiēusrè Expance has a yearly average tempature of 16°C (60°F), with its average tempature durring the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average tempature durring the winter being a cold 7°C (44°F). The Y̼ùwiēusrè Expance recieves an average of 152 cm/y (59 in/y) of percipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain durring the fall. The Y̼ùwiēusrè Expance covers an area of nearly 15061 km2 (9360 mi2), and an average elevation of 3477 m (11407 ft) above sealevel.

The Y̼ùwiēusrè Expance is's peoples are culturaly Wood Elven, but have their own local customs and traditions. Their shared vaules and traditions include education and knowledge-seeking and building things in service of their posterity, as well as a unique spirit festival and a literary festival.

They also place greater cultural emphasus on personal development and limit-pushing and cunning and the ability to trick others.
