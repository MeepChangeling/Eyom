The Thîmîqê-done Woods has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). The Thîmîqê-done Woods covers an area of nearly 16550 km2 (10285 mi2), and an average elevation of 5583 m (18316 ft) above sea level.
