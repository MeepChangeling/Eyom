The Skywood Holt (skywood /hoʊlt/ Skywood [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical holt on the coast of the Pressingstone region. Skywood Holt is a relatively typical holt for Pressingstone, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Skywood Holt has a yearly average temperature of 22°C (71°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 18°C (64°F). The Skywood Holt receives an average of 72 cm/y (28 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Skywood Holt covers an area of nearly 16716 km2 (10389 mi2), and an average elevation of 7525 m (-18261 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Skywood Holt is overseen by Potentate Gyvses Kastanthan Waliape, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 55% of the population self-report as devout. The Skywood Holt is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Skywood Holt is predominantly economicly reiant on Pressingstone's export of Plant Oils, and has no outstanding industries byond what is required for its role in that process.
