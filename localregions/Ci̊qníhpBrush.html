The Ci̊qníhp Brush has a yearly average temperature of 18°C (64°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 9°C (48°F). The Ci̊qníhp Brush covers an area of nearly 12237 km2 (7605 mi2), and an average elevation of 3275 m (10744 ft) above sea level.
