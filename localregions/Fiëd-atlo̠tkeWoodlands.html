The Fiëd-atlo̠tke Woodlands (/fjɛd/ /aˈtlo̠tke/ Shatteredpeak [array; spectrum] [rubble; debris]) are a group of temperate woodland on the coast of the Rivereach region. The Fiëd-atlo̠tke Woodlands are well known for its thriving abundance of plant life.

Climate

The Fiëd-atlo̠tke Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 7°C (44°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold -11°C (13°F). The Fiëd-atlo̠tke Woodlands receives an average of 316 cm/y (124 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Fiëd-atlo̠tke Woodlands covers an area of nearly 18137 km2 (11272 mi2), and an average elevation of 3967 m (13015 ft) above sea level.

Wareneese

The Wareneese subculture native to the Fiëd-atlo̠tke Woodlands are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wareneese culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of tattoos of some cultural significance and base their sence of individual idenity on having or lacking noble blood. They also place greater cultural emphasus on leadership and charisma in the group and courage and valiance in danger.

Hobgoblin

The Hobgoblin subculture native to the Fiëd-atlo̠tke Woodlands are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Wareneese culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of color choices with social meaning to them and base their sence of individual idenity on hereditary loyalty to a political group. They also place greater cultural emphasus on restoring some real or imagined glorious past and leadership and charisma in the group.

Iron Elf

The Iron Elf subculture native to the Fiëd-atlo̠tke Woodlands are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Wareneese culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of color choices with social meaning to them and base their sence of individual idenity on inheritance of an ancient body modification. Imperial

The Imperial subculture native to the Fiëd-atlo̠tke Woodlands are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Wareneese culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of painted skin markings that sometimes change and base their sence of individual idenity on religious factions or faith alliances. They also place greater cultural emphasus on ethnic purity of blood and culture and raw strength and personal prowess.

Quenn

The Quenn subculture native to the Fiëd-atlo̠tke Woodlands are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wareneese culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of worn weapons, tools or trade implements and base their sence of individual idenity on warbands or civic military service groups. They also place greater cultural emphasus on social progress toward some eventual utopia and piety and devotion to the gods.

Local Religion

The Fiëd-atlo̠tke Woodlands are overseen by Potentate Dinja Grodelm Eadwalhmær, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 18% of the population self-report as devout.
