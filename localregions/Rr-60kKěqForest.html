Climate

The Rr-60k Kěq Forest has a yearly average temperature of 22°C (71°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 17°C (62°F). The Rr-60k Kěq Forest receives an average of 290 cm/y (114 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Rr-60k Kěq Forest covers an area of nearly 9128 km2 (5673 mi2), and an average elevation of 1944 m (6377 ft) above sea level.
