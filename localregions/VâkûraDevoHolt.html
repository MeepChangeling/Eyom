Climate

The Vâkûra Devo Holt has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 31°C (87°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 14°C (57°F). The Vâkûra Devo Holt receives an average of 68 cm/y (26 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Vâkûra Devo Holt covers an area of nearly 9530 km2 (5922 mi2), and an average elevation of 14203 m (3648 ft) above sea level.
