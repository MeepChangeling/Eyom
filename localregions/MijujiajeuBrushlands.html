The Mijujiajeu Brushlands (/miˈjuʤa/ /ˈjeu/ Cold Dish [revenge] [custom]) are a group of temperate brushland within the temperate rainforest of the Iron Coast region. The Mijujiajeu Brushlands are well known for its thriving abundance of plant life.

Climate

The Mijujiajeu Brushlands has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 22°C (71°F). The Mijujiajeu Brushlands receives an average of 300 cm/y (118 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Mijujiajeu Brushlands covers an area of nearly 23501 km2 (14605 mi2), and an average elevation of 2355 m (7726 ft) above sea level.
