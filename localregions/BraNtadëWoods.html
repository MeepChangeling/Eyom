The Bra Ntadë Woods (/bra/ /ˈntadɛ/ Maridriver [hot (attractive)] [water]) are a group of subtropical woods on the coast of the Pantza region. Bra Ntadë Woods are a relatively typical woods for Pantza, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Bra Ntadë Woods has a yearly average temperature of 13°C (55°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 1°C (33°F). The Bra Ntadë Woods receives an average of 148 cm/y (58 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Bra Ntadë Woods covers an area of nearly 15055 km2 (9356 mi2), and an average elevation of 4079 m (13382 ft) above sea level.
