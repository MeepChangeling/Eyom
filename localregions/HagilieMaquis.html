The Hagilie Maquis (/ˈhagi/ /ˈlie/ Yesterward [controversy] [late]) is a subtropical maquis within the wetland of the Glowing Sands region. Hagilie Maquis is a relatively typical maquis for Glowing Sands, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Hagilie Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 14°C (57°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a cool 17°C (62°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 11°C (51°F). The Hagilie Maquis receives an average of 229 cm/y (90 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the long winter months. The Hagilie Maquis covers an area of nearly 10770 km2 (6693 mi2), and an average elevation of 534 m (1751 ft) above sea level.

The Hagilie Maquis is's peoples are culturally Iron ELvish, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include ethnic purity of blood and culture and purging evil and expelling the wicked, as well as a unique literary festival and a arcane festival.

Local Religion

The Hagilie Maquis is overseen by Potentate Zaune Aponwydan Eógaineid, a god serving within Tol's Children, the region's principal religion. Approximately 10% of the population self-report as devout. The Hagilie Maquis is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Hagilie Maquis is noteworthy for its economic independence from Glowing Sands's export of Glass. Hagilie Maquis is particularly renound for its dungeoneering industry. It is also has a noteworthy forestry byproducts industry.

The Hagilie Maquis has an Imperial Development Index of 28. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 28 means there are 28 locations within the Hagilie Maquis which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
