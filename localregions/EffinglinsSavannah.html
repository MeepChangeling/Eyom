The Effinglins Savannah (effinglins /səˈvænə/ Effinglins [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical savanna on the coast of the Highpeaks region. Effinglins Savannah is a relatively typical savanna for Highpeaks, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Effinglins Savannah has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 30°C (86°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Effinglins Savannah receives an average of 68 cm/y (26 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Effinglins Savannah covers an area of nearly 9870 km2 (6134 mi2), and an average elevation of 7004 m (-19970 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Effinglins Savannah is overseen by Potentate Cazdona Notho Ales, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 52% of the population self-report as devout. The Effinglins Savannah is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Effinglins Savannah is a negative return economy zone at this time. It has an abundence of Elemental Crystals, though infrastructure to this region of Highpeaks is not developed enough to support any major local industry.

The Effinglins Savannah has an Imperial Development Index of 2. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 2 means there are 2 locations within the Effinglins Savannah which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
