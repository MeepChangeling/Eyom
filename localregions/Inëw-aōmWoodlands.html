The Inëw-aōm Woodlands (/ˈinɛw/ /ˈaoːm/ Tradersend [morning; dawn; birth] [trader]) are a group of subtropical woodland within the tropical rainforest of the Riverpack Coast region. Inëw-aōm Woodlands are a relatively typical woodland for Riverpack Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Inëw-aōm Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 15°C (59°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 4°C (39°F). The Inëw-aōm Woodlands receives an average of 293 cm/y (115 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Inëw-aōm Woodlands covers an area of nearly 15079 km2 (9371 mi2), and an average elevation of 3574 m (11725 ft) above sea level.
