The Irdirn-glokīē Maquis (/irˈdirn/ /gloˈkjæ̝/ Bodega [short; low] [drugstore]) is a subtropical maquis on the coast of the Fractal Range region. Irdirn-glokīē Maquis is a relatively typical maquis for Fractal Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Irdirn-glokīē Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 20°C (68°F). The Irdirn-glokīē Maquis receives an average of 68 cm/y (26 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Irdirn-glokīē Maquis covers an area of nearly 10811 km2 (6719 mi2), and an average elevation of 9261 m (-12565 ft) above sea level.
