The Yet Nëqüe Holt (/jet/ /nɛq˔/ Pumping Iron [gay; homosexual] [pump]) is a subtropical holt on the coast of the Torpenvale region. Yet Nëqüe Holt is a relatively typical holt for Torpenvale, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Yet Nëqüe Holt has a yearly average temperature of 7°C (44°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant -10°C (14°F). The Yet Nëqüe Holt receives an average of 155 cm/y (61 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Yet Nëqüe Holt covers an area of nearly 14136 km2 (8785 mi2), and an average elevation of 4250 m (13943 ft) above sea level.
