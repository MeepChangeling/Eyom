The Iv-62b Qun Fields (/qun/ Iv-62b's University [Proper Noun (name of person)] [education]) are a group of temperate fields within the wetland of the Polymer Coast region. Iv-62b Qun Fields are a relatively typical fields for Polymer Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Iv-62b Qun Fields has a yearly average temperature of 19°C (66°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 13°C (55°F). The Iv-62b Qun Fields receives an average of 219 cm/y (86 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Iv-62b Qun Fields covers an area of nearly 17110 km2 (10633 mi2), and an average elevation of 1498 m (4914 ft) above sea level.
