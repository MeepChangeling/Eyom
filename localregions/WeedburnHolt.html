The Weedburn Holt (weedburn /hoʊlt/ Weedburn [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical holt on the coast of the Ancestor's Range region. Weedburn Holt is a relatively typical holt for Ancestor's Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Weedburn Holt has a yearly average temperature of 28°C (82°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 33°C (91°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 24°C (75°F). The Weedburn Holt receives an average of 67 cm/y (26 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Weedburn Holt covers an area of nearly 9223 km2 (5732 mi2), and an average elevation of 10325 m (-9074 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Weedburn Holt is overseen by Potentate Ityx Coraired Medoron, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 49% of the population self-report as devout. The Weedburn Holt is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Weedburn Holt has its economic roots in Ancestor's Range's export of Stone, but has developed its own forestry byproducts industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.

The Weedburn Holt has an Imperial Development Index of 20. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 20 means there are 20 locations within the Weedburn Holt which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
