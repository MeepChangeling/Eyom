The Flirfihum Expance (Flirfi /hum/ Flirfi's Flight [Proper Noun (name of person)] [wing]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Riverpack Coast region. Flirfihum Expance is a relativly typical expanse for Riverpack Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Flirfihum Expance has a yearly average tempature of 16°C (60°F), with its average tempature durring the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average tempature durring the winter being a cold 4°C (39°F). The Flirfihum Expance recieves an average of 203 cm/y (79 in/y) of percipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain durring the fall. The Flirfihum Expance covers an area of nearly 16790 km2 (10435 mi2), and an average elevation of 3435 m (11269 ft) above sealevel.

People and Culture

In defrence to the traditions of Eyomic peoples, we have forgon town-specific racial cencus, howeaver, as our culture lacks a taboo for classification of peoples by their biology and such infomration is highly important for our medical practices amongst other institutions, we have stated the racial demographics of this region. In respect to this universal cultural element, we have split racial classification by culture while keeping the name of each race for our own purposes. Should you see the same race listed more than once in the following section, this is to desinate a major cultural division within a race.

The Flirfihum Expance is's peoples are culturaly Gnollish, but have their own local customs and traditions. Their shared vaules and traditions include discipline and obedience to the law and ethnic purity of blood and culture, as well as a unique folk festival and a music festival.

Gnoll

The Gnoll subculture native to the Flirfihum Expance are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Gnoll culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of tattoos of some cultural significance and base their sence of individual idenity on hereditary loyalty to a political group. They also place greater cultural emphasus on exploring the unknown and discovering secrets and personal indulgence and luxuriant pleasure.

Goblin

The Goblin subculture native to the Flirfihum Expance are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Gnoll culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of color choices with social meaning to them and base their sence of individual idenity on dynastic lineages with cadet branches. They also place greater cultural emphasus on subtlety and indirectness of action and submission to the collective will or culture.

Hobgoblin

The Hobgoblin subculture native to the Flirfihum Expance are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Gnoll culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of worn weapons, tools or trade implements and base their sence of individual idenity on far-flung clans of affiliated families. They also place greater cultural emphasus on justice and fairness between people and remembrance of the past and memorializing history.

Rattu

The Rattu subculture native to the Flirfihum Expance are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Gnoll culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of patterned hair shaving or depilation and base their sence of individual idenity on extended family out to cousins and like kin. They also place greater cultural emphasus on humanistic reason and “rational” religion and subtlety and indirectness of action.

Kobold

The Kobold subculture native to the Flirfihum Expance are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Gnoll culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of tattoos of some cultural significance and base their sence of individual idenity on religious factions or faith alliances. They also place greater cultural emphasus on aesthetic beauty in material goods and architecture and filial devotion to family and parents.

Tanuki

The Tanuki subculture native to the Flirfihum Expance are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Gnoll culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of color choices with social meaning to them and base their sence of individual idenity on educational ties to institutions or traditions. They also place greater cultural emphasus on piety and devotion to the gods and vengeance and execution of just vendettas.

Local Religion

The Flirfihum Expance is overseen by Potentate Dronta Æðelwineus Eastmund, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's pricipal religion. Aproximently 16% of the population self-report as devout. The Flirfihum Expance is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevent to their portfolio.

Settlments and Economy

Flirfihum Expance has its economic roots in Riverpack Coast's export of Steel, but has developed its own innovation industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.

The Flirfihum Expance has an Imperial Development Index of 17. For the refrence of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 17 means there are 17 locations within the Flirfihum Expance which the Empire can securly and regurly transport matrials and personell..

As a courticy for scholars and agents, the Flirfihum Expance is rated I2 by Eyoms banks. Buisness ventures within the Flirfihum Expance are expected to return above capitol with some volitility.
