The Exsea Fields (exsea /fildz/ Exsea [Translation Unavailable]) are a group of subtropical fields on the coast of the Highpeaks region. Exsea Fields are a relatively typical fields for Highpeaks, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Exsea Fields has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 20°C (68°F). The Exsea Fields receives an average of 70 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Exsea Fields covers an area of nearly 10533 km2 (6546 mi2), and an average elevation of 9366 m (-12221 ft) above sea level.

The Exsea Fields has an MDI of 15, as such it has been classified as Deadly by Imperial insurance adjusters. Travel through Exsea Fields is not advised. If travel through the Exsea Fields cannot be avoided, travelers are advised to be heavily armed at all times and travel by the most expedient route and method of transportation available. Local Religion

The Exsea Fields are overseen by Potentate Beotl Damokles Apoibnis, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 45% of the population self-report as devout. The Exsea Fields is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Exsea Fields is a negative return economy zone at this time. It has an abundence of Elemental Crystals, though infrastructure to this region of Highpeaks is not developed enough to support any major local industry.

The Exsea Fields has an Imperial Development Index of 3. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 3 means there are 3 locations within the Exsea Fields which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
