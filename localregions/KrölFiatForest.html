The Kröl Fiat Forest (/kro˞l/ /fjat/ Tradeway [right; correct] [way; manner; process; route; direction (way)]) is a subtropical forest on the coast of the Bulwark region. Kröl Fiat Forest is a relatively typical forest for Bulwark, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Kröl Fiat Forest has a yearly average temperature of 8°C (46°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -8°C (18°F). The Kröl Fiat Forest receives an average of 70 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Kröl Fiat Forest covers an area of nearly 13293 km2 (8261 mi2), and an average elevation of 5061 m (16604 ft) above sea level.
