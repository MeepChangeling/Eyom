The Skikvungzhona Holt (/ˈhona/ Skikvungz's Maiden [Proper Noun (name of person)] [slave]) is a subtropical holt within the temperate rainforest of the Shimmering Spires region. Skikvungzhona Holt is a relatively typical holt for Shimmering Spires, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Skikvungzhona Holt has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 22°C (71°F). The Skikvungzhona Holt receives an average of 284 cm/y (111 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Skikvungzhona Holt covers an area of nearly 30961 km2 (19242 mi2), and an average elevation of 2840 m (9317 ft) above sea level.

The Skikvungzhona Holt is overseen by Potentate Etorr Nothusandi Eudora, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 39% of the population self-report as devout. The Skikvungzhona Holt is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Skikvungzhona Holt has its economic roots in Shimmering Spires's export of Precious Metals, but has developed its own mercenary industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.
