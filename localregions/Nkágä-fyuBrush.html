The Nkágä-fyu Brush (/fju/ Nkágä's Proof [Proper Noun (name of person)] [evidence]) is a subtropical brush on the coast of the Upper Warding Range region. Nkágä-fyu Brush is a relatively typical brush for Upper Warding Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Nkágä-fyu Brush has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Nkágä-fyu Brush receives an average of 129 cm/y (50 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Nkágä-fyu Brush covers an area of nearly 12212 km2 (7589 mi2), and an average elevation of 9390 m (-12142 ft) above sea level.
