The Vi̽ Miw Brushlands (/vi̽/ /miw/ Drooping [stupid; dumb; dull] [bracket]) are a group of temperate brushland on the coast of the Farmbasin region. Vi̽ Miw Brushlands are a relatively typical brushland for Farmbasin, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Vi̽ Miw Brushlands has a yearly average temperature of 15°C (59°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 3°C (37°F). The Vi̽ Miw Brushlands receives an average of 149 cm/y (58 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Vi̽ Miw Brushlands covers an area of nearly 16509 km2 (10260 mi2), and an average elevation of 3719 m (12201 ft) above sea level.

The Vi̽ Miw Brushlands are's peoples are culturally Wood Elven, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include education and knowledge-seeking and exploring the unknown and discovering secrets, as well as a unique autumn festival and a arcane festival.

Local Religion

The Vi̽ Miw Brushlands are overseen by Potentate Ises Apon Cassandrona, a god serving within Covenant of Irus, the region's principal religion. Approximately 18% of the population self-report as devout. The Vi̽ Miw Brushlands is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Vi̽ Miw Brushlands has its economic roots in Farmbasin's export of Slaves, but has developed its own monster hunting industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.
