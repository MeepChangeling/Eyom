The Coatimis Expance (coatimis expance Coatimis [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Fractal Range region. Coatimis Expance is a relativly typical expanse for Fractal Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Coatimis Expance has a yearly average tempature of 34°C (93°F), with its average tempature durring the summer being a blistering 39°C (102°F) and its average tempature durring the winter being a warm 30°C (86°F). The Coatimis Expance recieves an average of 68 cm/y (26 in/y) of percipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain durring the spring. The Coatimis Expance covers an area of nearly 14933 km2 (9280 mi2), and an average elevation of 15377 m (7499 ft) above sealevel.
