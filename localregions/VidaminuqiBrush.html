The Vidaminuqi Brush (/viˈdami/ /ˈnuqi/ Handsom [jaw] [nice]) is a subtropical brush on the coast of the Shimmering Spires region. Vidaminuqi Brush is a relatively typical brush for Shimmering Spires, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Vidaminuqi Brush has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Vidaminuqi Brush receives an average of 72 cm/y (28 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Vidaminuqi Brush covers an area of nearly 17152 km2 (10660 mi2), and an average elevation of 3799 m (12463 ft) above sea level.

The Vidaminuqi Brush is's peoples are culturally Tauric, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include submission to lawful authority and honesty and truthfulness in speech, as well as a unique beer festival and a sun festival.

Local Religion

The Vidaminuqi Brush is overseen by Potentate Theheyar Ponchobar Gointos, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 43% of the population self-report as devout. The Vidaminuqi Brush is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Vidaminuqi Brush is predominantly economicly reiant on Shimmering Spires's export of Precious Metals, and has no outstanding industries byond what is required for its role in that process.
