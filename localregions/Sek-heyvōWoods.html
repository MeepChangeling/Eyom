The Sek-heyvō Woods (/sek/ /ˈhejvoː/ Whiteclue [almond] [clue]) are a group of temperate woods on the coast of the Pantza region. Sek-heyvō Woods are a relatively typical woods for Pantza, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Sek-heyvō Woods has a yearly average temperature of 7°C (44°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant -11°C (13°F). The Sek-heyvō Woods receives an average of 152 cm/y (59 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Sek-heyvō Woods covers an area of nearly 17599 km2 (10937 mi2), and an average elevation of 4564 m (14973 ft) above sea level.
