The Linobà Hâs̺imî Woodlands (/liˈnobə/ /hɑˈs̺imɪː/ Shinyspring [shiny] [spring (coil)]) are a group of temperate woodland within the tropical rainforest of the Alchemists Garden region. Linobà Hâs̺imî Woodlands are a relatively typical woodland for Alchemists Garden, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Linobà Hâs̺imî Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 20°C (68°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 10°C (50°F). The Linobà Hâs̺imî Woodlands receives an average of 284 cm/y (111 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Linobà Hâs̺imî Woodlands covers an area of nearly 16474 km2 (10238 mi2), and an average elevation of 2286 m (7500 ft) above sea level.
