The Noftaf I̽nböō Expanse (/ˈnoftaf/ /i̽nˈbo˞oː/ Merchantscircle [greedy] [orbit]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Foxvale region. Noftaf I̽nböō Expanse is a relatively typical expanse for Foxvale, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Noftaf I̽nböō Expanse has a yearly average temperature of 8°C (46°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -7°C (20°F). The Noftaf I̽nböō Expanse receives an average of 291 cm/y (114 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Noftaf I̽nböō Expanse covers an area of nearly 23021 km2 (14307 mi2), and an average elevation of 6287 m (20626 ft) above sea level.
