The Rilzinulesu Brushlands (/nuˈlesu/ Rilzisfaith [Proper Noun (name of person)] [perception]) are a group of subtropical brushland within the temperate rainforest of the Illa's Panhandle region. Rilzinulesu Brushlands are a relatively typical brushland for Illa's Panhandle, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Rilzinulesu Brushlands has a yearly average temperature of 22°C (71°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 20°C (68°F). The Rilzinulesu Brushlands receives an average of 282 cm/y (111 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Rilzinulesu Brushlands covers an area of nearly 19708 km2 (12248 mi2), and an average elevation of 3370 m (11056 ft) above sea level.
