The Jevepema Heathland (/ˈjeve/ /ˈpema/ Blueruins [rubble; debris] [blue]) is a subtropical heathland within the wetland of the Shattered Peninsula region. Jevepema Heathland is a relatively typical heathland for Shattered Peninsula, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Jevepema Heathland has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Jevepema Heathland receives an average of 219 cm/y (86 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Jevepema Heathland covers an area of nearly 17083 km2 (10617 mi2), and an average elevation of 1856 m (6089 ft) above sea level.

The Jevepema Heathland is home to 371991 people. They break down into the following demographics: 54% Iron Elves, 24% Dwarves, 7% Dwarves, 7% Tanuki, 6% Imperial, and ~2% Other.

The Jevepema Heathland is's peoples are culturally Wareneese, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include seeking fortune in new places or new roles and personal indulgence and luxuriant pleasure, as well as a unique comedy festival and a religious festival.

Iron Elf

The Iron Elf subculture native to the Jevepema Heathland are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Iron Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of piercings, whether minor or elaborate and base their sence of individual idenity on far-flung clans of affiliated families. They also place greater cultural emphasus on discipline and obedience to the law and submission to lawful authority.

Dwarf

The Dwarf subculture native to the Jevepema Heathland are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Iron Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of tattoos of some cultural significance and base their sence of individual idenity on numerous castes of hereditary workers. They also place greater cultural emphasus on sexual license and wantonness and membership in an elite bloodline or caste.

Tanuki

The Tanuki subculture native to the Jevepema Heathland are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Iron Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of piercings, whether minor or elaborate and base their sence of individual idenity on ideological groups or philosophical sects. They also place greater cultural emphasus on social progress toward some eventual utopia and courage and valiance in danger.

Imperial

The Imperial subculture native to the Jevepema Heathland are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Iron Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of color choices with social meaning to them and base their sence of individual idenity on numerous castes of hereditary workers. They also place greater cultural emphasus on excellence in one’s profession or trade and ethnic purity of blood and culture.

Local Religion

The Jevepema Heathland is overseen by Potentate Noktrix Orphalthniu Mapoint, a god serving within Covenant of Irus, the region's principal religion. Approximately 55% of the population self-report as devout. The Jevepema Heathland is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Jevepema Heathland is noteworthy for its economic independence from Shattered Peninsula's export of Metals. Jevepema Heathland is particularly renound for its transportation industry. It is also has a noteworthy potions industry.

The Jevepema Heathland has an Imperial Development Index of 27. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 27 means there are 27 locations within the Jevepema Heathland which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..

As a courtesy for scholars and agents, the Jevepema Heathland is rated I1 by Eyom’s banks. Business ventures within the Jevepema Heathland are expected to return above capitol at low risk.
