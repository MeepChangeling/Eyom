The Dëog̈ëvî-thêbëla Forest has a yearly average temperature of 21°C (69°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 16°C (60°F). The Dëog̈ëvî-thêbëla Forest covers an area of nearly 15068 km2 (9364 mi2), and an average elevation of 4448 m (14593 ft) above sea level.
