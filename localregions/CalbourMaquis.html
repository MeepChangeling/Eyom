The Calbour Maquis (calbour maquis Calbour [Translation Unavailable]) is a temperate maquis on the coast of the Silicahilands region. Calbour Maquis is a relatively typical maquis for Silicahilands, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Calbour Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 20°C (68°F). The Calbour Maquis receives an average of 219 cm/y (86 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Calbour Maquis covers an area of nearly 20702 km2 (12866 mi2), and an average elevation of 5140 m (16863 ft) above sea level.
