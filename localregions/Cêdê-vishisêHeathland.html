The Cêdê-vishisê Heathland (/ˈcɘdɘ/ /viˈʃisɘ/ Lavander [sweet] [laundry]) is a temperate heathland on the coast of the Cogdales region. The Cêdê-vishisê Heathland is well known for its thriving abundance of plant life.

Climate

The Cêdê-vishisê Heathland has a yearly average temperature of 20°C (68°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 15°C (59°F). The Cêdê-vishisê Heathland receives an average of 215 cm/y (84 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Cêdê-vishisê Heathland covers an area of nearly 7596 km2 (4720 mi2), and an average elevation of 6506 m (21345 ft) above sea level.
