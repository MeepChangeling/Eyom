The Zdin Vlo Forest (/zdin/ /vlo/ Skyhope [light blue] [confidence]) is a subtropical forest on the coast of the Timberlund region. The Zdin Vlo Forest is well known for being largely barren of plant life.

Climate

The Zdin Vlo Forest has a yearly average temperature of 11°C (51°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -4°C (25°F). The Zdin Vlo Forest receives an average of 231 cm/y (90 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Zdin Vlo Forest covers an area of nearly 6715 km2 (4173 mi2), and an average elevation of 2882 m (9455 ft) above sea level.
