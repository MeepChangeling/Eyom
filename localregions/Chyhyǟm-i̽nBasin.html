The Chyhyǟm-i̽n Basin (/caːm/ /i̽n/ Watchtower [sign; signal; symptom; alarm] [eye]) is a subtropical basin within the taiga of the Breeze Corridor region. Chyhyǟm-i̽n Basin is a relatively typical basin for Breeze Corridor, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Chyhyǟm-i̽n Basin has a yearly average temperature of 4°C (39°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant -15°C (5°F). The Chyhyǟm-i̽n Basin receives an average of 72 cm/y (28 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Chyhyǟm-i̽n Basin covers an area of nearly 7608 km2 (4728 mi2), and an average elevation of 5591 m (18343 ft) above sea level.
