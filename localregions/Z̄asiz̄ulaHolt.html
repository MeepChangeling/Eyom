The Z̄asiz̄ula Holt (/ˈz̚asi/ /ˈz̚ula/ Graveyard [urn] [quiet; silent]) is a subtropical holt within the wetland of the Shattered Peninsula region. The Z̄asiz̄ula Holt is well known for its thriving abundance of plant life.

Climate

The Z̄asiz̄ula Holt has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Z̄asiz̄ula Holt receives an average of 224 cm/y (88 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Z̄asiz̄ula Holt covers an area of nearly 24185 km2 (15031 mi2), and an average elevation of 1657 m (5436 ft) above sea level.
