Local Religion

The Ntël Bo̠nsko Grasslands are overseen by Potentate Bomos Alespoll Medous, a god serving within Covenant of Irus, the region's principal religion. Approximately 17% of the population self-report as devout. The Ntël Bo̠nsko Grasslands is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
