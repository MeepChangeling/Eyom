The Ekyetttt Däsh Basin (/dəs̼/ Ekyetttt's Bow [Proper Noun (name of person)] [bow (weapon)]) is a subtropical basin within the temperate rainforest of the Greatbay region. Ekyetttt Däsh Basin is a relatively typical basin for Greatbay, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Ekyetttt Däsh Basin has a yearly average temperature of 22°C (71°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 18°C (64°F). The Ekyetttt Däsh Basin receives an average of 309 cm/y (121 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Ekyetttt Däsh Basin covers an area of nearly 9835 km2 (6112 mi2), and an average elevation of 902 m (2959 ft) above sea level.
