The Piblên-like Shrublands (/ˈlike/ Piblênlodge [Proper Noun (name of person)] [cabin; hut; inn; lodge]) are a group of subtropical shrubland on the coast of the Thousand Plantations region. Piblên-like Shrublands are a relatively typical shrubland for Thousand Plantations, as well as Eyom as a whole.

The Piblên-like Shrublands has a yearly average temperature of 17°C (62°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 9°C (48°F). The Piblên-like Shrublands covers an area of nearly 12348 km2 (7674 mi2), and an average elevation of 5435 m (17831 ft) above sea level.
