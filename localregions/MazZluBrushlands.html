The Maz Zlu Brushlands (/maz/ /zlu/ Nana [miniature] [grandmother]) are a group of subtropical brushland on the coast of the Nowhere region. The Maz Zlu Brushlands are remarkable for the suspicious lack of anything interesting ever having occurred there.

Climate

The Maz Zlu Brushlands has a yearly average temperature of 10°C (50°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -5°C (23°F). The Maz Zlu Brushlands receives an average of 221 cm/y (87 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Maz Zlu Brushlands covers an area of nearly 15488 km2 (9625 mi2), and an average elevation of 4235 m (13894 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Maz Zlu Brushlands are overseen by Potentate Bilna Eastan Verðandros, a god serving within Covenant of Irus, the region's principal religion. Approximately 48% of the population self-report as devout. The Maz Zlu Brushlands is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Maz Zlu Brushlands is a negative return economy zone at this time. It has an abundence of Radium, though infrastructure to this region of Nowhere is not developed enough to support any major local industry.
