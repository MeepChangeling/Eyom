The Pjlenzine Brushlands (/ˈzine/ Pjlen's Dope Hut [Proper Noun (name of person)] [shelter; shack]) are a group of temperate brushland on the coast of the Illa's Panhandle region. Pjlenzine Brushlands are a relatively typical brushland for Illa's Panhandle, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Pjlenzine Brushlands has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Pjlenzine Brushlands receives an average of 269 cm/y (105 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Pjlenzine Brushlands covers an area of nearly 15804 km2 (9822 mi2), and an average elevation of 4602 m (15098 ft) above sea level.

The Pjlenzine Brushlands are overseen by Potentate Veraos Afronchus Helre, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. The Pjlenzine Brushlands is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
