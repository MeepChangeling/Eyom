The Ekoqequja Holt (/ˈeko/ /qeˈquja/ Biter [default] [job; career]) is a subtropical holt within the wetland of the Yastan Peninsula region. Ekoqequja Holt is a relatively typical holt for Yastan Peninsula, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Ekoqequja Holt has a yearly average temperature of 4°C (39°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 20°C (68°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold -13°C (9°F). The Ekoqequja Holt receives an average of 211 cm/y (83 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Ekoqequja Holt covers an area of nearly 12416 km2 (7716 mi2), and an average elevation of 2840 m (9317 ft) above sea level.

The Ekoqequja Holt is home to 945491 people. They break down into the following demographics: 67% Gnolls, 18% Taurans, 8% Kobold, 5% Elf, and ~2% Other.

The Ekoqequja Holt is's peoples are culturally Gnollish, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include honor and maintaining one’s integrity and harmony with nature and existing life, as well as a unique comedy festival and a fire festival.

Gnoll

The Gnoll subculture native to the Ekoqequja Holt are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Gnoll culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of patterned hair shaving or depilation and base their sence of individual idenity on hereditary loyalty to a political group. They also place greater cultural emphasus on dominating and possessing other people and pacifism and peaceful resolution of problems.

Tauran

The Tauran subculture native to the Ekoqequja Holt are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Gnoll culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of tattoos of some cultural significance and base their sence of individual idenity on ethnic membership. They also place greater cultural emphasus on humanistic reason and “rational” religion and ethnic purity of blood and culture.

Kobold

The Kobold subculture native to the Ekoqequja Holt are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Gnoll culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of socially-meaningful animal motif items and base their sence of individual idenity on numerous castes of hereditary workers. They also place greater cultural emphasus on aesthetic beauty in material goods and architecture and personal sacrifice for one’s causes or purposes.

Elf

The Elf subculture native to the Ekoqequja Holt are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Gnoll culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of intricate hair styles or braiding and base their sence of individual idenity on astrologically-determined birth groups. They also place greater cultural emphasus on education and knowledge-seeking and leadership and charisma in the group.

Local Religion

The Ekoqequja Holt is overseen by Potentate Qikzotz Kephros Akantor, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 53% of the population self-report as devout. The Ekoqequja Holt is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Ekoqequja Holt is noteworthy for its economic independence from Yastan Penencila's export of Furs. Ekoqequja Holt is particularly renound for its fertilizer industry. It is also has a noteworthy transportation industry.

The Ekoqequja Holt has an Imperial Development Index of 34. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 34 means there are 34 locations within the Ekoqequja Holt which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..

As a courtesy for scholars and agents, the Ekoqequja Holt is rated C2 by Eyom’s banks. Business ventures within the Ekoqequja Holt are expected to return market rate with some volatility.
