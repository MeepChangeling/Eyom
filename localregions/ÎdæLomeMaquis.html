The Îdæ Lome Maquis (/ˈɪːdæ/ /ˈlome/ Quietward [dumb (speechless)] [boss]) is a temperate maquis on the coast of the Fargarden region. Îdæ Lome Maquis is a relatively typical maquis for Fargarden, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Îdæ Lome Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 19°C (66°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 11°C (51°F). The Îdæ Lome Maquis receives an average of 201 cm/y (79 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Îdæ Lome Maquis covers an area of nearly 9079 km2 (5642 mi2), and an average elevation of 2801 m (9189 ft) above sea level.
