The Nrow-atuqüe Prairie (/nrow/ /ˈatuq˔/ Barrister's [friction] [lawyer]) is a subtropical prairie within the temperate rainforest of the Filigree Coast region. Nrow-atuqüe Prairie is a relatively typical prairie for Filigree Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Nrow-atuqüe Prairie has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 22°C (71°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 11°C (51°F). The Nrow-atuqüe Prairie receives an average of 312 cm/y (122 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Nrow-atuqüe Prairie covers an area of nearly 22752 km2 (14140 mi2), and an average elevation of 1367 m (4484 ft) above sea level.
