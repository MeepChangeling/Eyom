The Zoz̄okepo Brush (/ˈzoz̚o/ /ˈkepo/ Commongood [community] [debt]) is a subtropical brush on the coast of the Equuscoast region. Zoz̄okepo Brush is a relatively typical brush for Equuscoast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Zoz̄okepo Brush has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Zoz̄okepo Brush receives an average of 145 cm/y (57 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Zoz̄okepo Brush covers an area of nearly 13099 km2 (8141 mi2), and an average elevation of 2498 m (8195 ft) above sea level.

The Zoz̄okepo Brush is home to 389578 people. They break down into the following demographics: 53% Tauric, 13% Dwarves, 19% Taurans, 6% Kobold, 5% Grey Elf, 2% Imperial, and ~2% Other.

The Zoz̄okepo Brush is's peoples are culturally Tauric, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include personal indulgence and luxuriant pleasure and discipline and obedience to the law, as well as a unique food festival and a sun festival.

Local Religion

The Zoz̄okepo Brush is overseen by Potentate Kuana Euanos Parmus, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 14% of the population self-report as devout. The Zoz̄okepo Brush is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Zoz̄okepo Brush has its economic roots in Equuscoast's export of Machine Parts, but has developed its own agriculture industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.
