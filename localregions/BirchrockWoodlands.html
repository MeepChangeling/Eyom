The Birchrock Woodlands (birchrock /ˈwʊˌdlændz/ Birchrock [Translation Unavailable]) are a group of subtropical woodland on the coast of the Upper Warding Range region. Birchrock Woodlands are a relatively typical woodland for Upper Warding Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Birchrock Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). The Birchrock Woodlands receives an average of 70 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Birchrock Woodlands covers an area of nearly 14014 km2 (8709 mi2), and an average elevation of 9098 m (-13100 ft) above sea level.
