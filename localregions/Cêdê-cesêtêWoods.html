The Cêdê-cesêtê Woods has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 7°C (44°F). The Cêdê-cesêtê Woods receives an average of 305 cm/y (120 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Cêdê-cesêtê Woods covers an area of nearly 13544 km2 (8417 mi2), and an average elevation of 4775 m (15666 ft) above sea level.
