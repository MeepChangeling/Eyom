Climate

The Hedë-hyo Heath has a yearly average temperature of 15°C (59°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 2°C (35°F). The Hedë-hyo Heath receives an average of 156 cm/y (61 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Hedë-hyo Heath covers an area of nearly 19149 km2 (11901 mi2), and an average elevation of 3105 m (10187 ft) above sea level.
