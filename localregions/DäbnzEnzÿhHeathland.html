The Däbnz Enzÿh Heathland (/inˈzɪ̘/ Däbnz's Arrow [Proper Noun (name of person)] [arrow (weapon); arrow (sign)]) is a subtropical heathland within the temperate deciduous forest of the Greatbay region. Däbnz Enzÿh Heathland is a relatively typical heathland for Greatbay, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Däbnz Enzÿh Heathland has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 22°C (71°F). The Däbnz Enzÿh Heathland receives an average of 142 cm/y (55 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Däbnz Enzÿh Heathland covers an area of nearly 9453 km2 (5875 mi2), and an average elevation of 2225 m (7299 ft) above sea level.

The Däbnz Enzÿh Heathland is home to 431683 people. They break down into the following demographics: 53% Tauric, 27% Fae, 5% Dwarves, 8% Imperial, 5% Grey Elf, and ~2% Other.

The Däbnz Enzÿh Heathland is's peoples are culturally Tauric, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include piety and devotion to the gods and loyalty to one’s friends, family, and own, as well as a unique music festival and a rain festival.

Tauric

The Tauric subculture native to the Däbnz Enzÿh Heathland are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Tauric culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of significant scent or perfume uses and base their sence of individual idenity on trade guilds specific to particular roles. They also place greater cultural emphasus on justice and fairness between people and seeking fortune in new places or new roles.

Fae

The Fae subculture native to the Däbnz Enzÿh Heathland are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Tauric culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of culturally-significant jewelry or accessories and base their sence of individual idenity on extended family out to cousins and like kin. They also place greater cultural emphasus on sexual license and wantonness and education and knowledge-seeking.

Dwarf

The Dwarf subculture native to the Däbnz Enzÿh Heathland are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Tauric culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of impractical or elaborate role-based clothes and base their sence of individual idenity on identity based on their local community. They also place greater cultural emphasus on excellence in one’s profession or trade and aesthetic beauty in material goods and architecture.

Imperial

The Imperial subculture native to the Däbnz Enzÿh Heathland are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Tauric culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of worn weapons, tools or trade implements and base their sence of individual idenity on trade guilds specific to particular roles. They also place greater cultural emphasus on conquest and domination of others and hardiness and endurance before woes.

Grey Elf

The Grey Elf subculture native to the Däbnz Enzÿh Heathland are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Tauric culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of painted skin markings that sometimes change and base their sence of individual idenity on identity based on their local community. They also place greater cultural emphasus on justice and fairness between people and ethnic purity of blood and culture.

Local Religion

The Däbnz Enzÿh Heathland is overseen by Potentate Grera Walaukos Ælfric, a god serving within The Council of Twelve, the region's principal religion. Approximately 24% of the population self-report as devout. The Däbnz Enzÿh Heathland is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Däbnz Enzÿh Heathland has its economic roots in Greatbay's export of Luxury Goods, but has developed its own armor industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.
