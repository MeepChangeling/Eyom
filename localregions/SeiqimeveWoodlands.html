The Seiqimeve Woodlands (/ˈsei/ /qiˈmeve/ Dawning [site] [imminent; pending]) are a group of subtropical woodland within the wetland of the Glowing Sands region. Seiqimeve Woodlands are a relatively typical woodland for Glowing Sands, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Seiqimeve Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 23°C (73°F). The Seiqimeve Woodlands receives an average of 204 cm/y (80 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Seiqimeve Woodlands covers an area of nearly 13634 km2 (8473 mi2), and an average elevation of 3525 m (11564 ft) above sea level.

The Seiqimeve Woodlands are's peoples are culturally Sylvanian, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include honor and maintaining one’s integrity and justice and fairness between people, as well as a unique rain festival and a comedy festival.

Local Religion

The Seiqimeve Woodlands are overseen by Potentate Bystrix Eoghandrodi Wealaukos, a god serving within The Seven Titans, the region's principal religion. Approximately 54% of the population self-report as devout. The Seiqimeve Woodlands is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Seiqimeve Woodlands has its economic roots in Glowing Sands's export of Glass, but has developed its own glass industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.

The Seiqimeve Woodlands has an Imperial Development Index of 23. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 23 means there are 23 locations within the Seiqimeve Woodlands which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
