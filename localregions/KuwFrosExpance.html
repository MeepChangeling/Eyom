The Kuw Fros Expance (/kuw/ /fros/ Caning [sudden; abrupt; swift] [painting]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Timberlund region. Kuw Fros Expance is a relativly typical expanse for Timberlund, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Kuw Fros Expance has a yearly average tempature of 11°C (51°F), with its average tempature durring the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average tempature durring the winter being a freezing -3°C (27°F). The Kuw Fros Expance recieves an average of 153 cm/y (60 in/y) of percipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow durring the pleasently short winter months. The Kuw Fros Expance covers an area of nearly 7468 km2 (4641 mi2), and an average elevation of 3472 m (11391 ft) above sealevel.

In respect to this universal cultural element, we have split racial classification by culture while keeping the name of each race for our own purposes. Should you see the same race listed more than once in the following section, this is to desinate a major cultural division within a race.

The Kuw Fros Expance is's peoples are culturaly Wood Elven, but have their own local customs and traditions. Their shared vaules and traditions include zealous guardianship of their own land or holy sit and purging evil and expelling the wicked, as well as a unique relic festival and a summer festival.

Wood Elf

The Wood Elf subculture native to the Kuw Fros Expance are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of worn weapons, tools or trade implements and base their sence of individual idenity on hereditary loyalty to a political group. They also place greater cultural emphasus on societal or ethnic superiority over all outsiders and discipline and obedience to the law.

Kitsune

The Kitsune subculture native to the Kuw Fros Expance are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of patterned hair shaving or depilation and base their sence of individual idenity on ethnic membership. They also place greater cultural emphasus on exploring the unknown and discovering secrets and justice and fairness between people.

Imperial

The Imperial subculture native to the Kuw Fros Expance are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of socially-meaningful animal motif items and base their sence of individual idenity on religious factions or faith alliances. They also place greater cultural emphasus on eloquence and social expertise and dominating and possessing other people.

Kivu

The Kivu subculture native to the Kuw Fros Expance are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of patterned hair shaving or depilation and base their sence of individual idenity on identity based on their local community. They also place greater cultural emphasus on building things in service of their posterity and prosperity and accruing material wealth.

Local Religion

The Kuw Fros Expance is overseen by Potentate Rovbris Freald Galotheusa, a god serving within Therilism, the region's pricipal religion. Aproximently 48% of the population self-report as devout. The Kuw Fros Expance is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevent to their portfolio.

Settlments and Economy

Kuw Fros Expance has its economic roots in Timberlund's export of Timber, but has developed its own medicine industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.

The Kuw Fros Expance has an Imperial Development Index of 24. For the refrence of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 24 means there are 24 locations within the Kuw Fros Expance which the Empire can securly and regurly transport matrials and personell..

As a courticy for scholars and agents, the Kuw Fros Expance is rated I1 by Eyoms banks. Buisness ventures within the Kuw Fros Expance are expected to return above capitol at low risk.
