The Jîn-teyê Expanse (/ˈtejɘ/ Jîn's Knowlage [Proper Noun (name of person)] [knowledge]) is a temperate expanse within the temperate rainforest of the Greatbay region. Jîn-teyê Expanse is a relatively typical expanse for Greatbay, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Jîn-teyê Expanse has a yearly average temperature of 20°C (68°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 16°C (60°F). The Jîn-teyê Expanse receives an average of 293 cm/y (115 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Jîn-teyê Expanse covers an area of nearly 25252 km2 (15694 mi2), and an average elevation of 1354 m (4442 ft) above sea level.

However, as our culture lacks a taboo for classification of peoples by their biology and such information is highly important for our medical practices amongst other institutions, we have stated the racial demographics of this region. In respect to this universal cultural element, we have split racial classification by culture while keeping the name of each race for our own purposes. Should you see the same race listed more than once in the following section, this is to designate a major cultural division within a race.

The Jîn-teyê Expanse is's peoples are culturally Sylvanian, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include restoring some real or imagined glorious past and harmony with nature and existing life, as well as a unique religious festival and a autumn festival.

Local Religion

The Jîn-teyê Expanse is overseen by Potentate Fua Brig Osgaid, a god serving within The Council of Twelve, the region's principal religion. Approximately 32% of the population self-report as devout. The Jîn-teyê Expanse is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

The Jîn-teyê Expanse has an Imperial Development Index of 38. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 38 means there are 38 locations within the Jîn-teyê Expanse which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..

As a courtesy for scholars and agents, the Jîn-teyê Expanse is rated C1 by Eyom’s banks. Business ventures within the Jîn-teyê Expanse are expected to return market rate at the lowest risk.
