The Nuqicozupi Brushlands (/nuˈqico/ /ˈzupi/ Noblecrest [item] [elite]) are a group of subtropical brushland within the wetland of the Shattered Peninsula region. Nuqicozupi Brushlands are a relatively typical brushland for Shattered Peninsula, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Nuqicozupi Brushlands has a yearly average temperature of 20°C (68°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 17°C (62°F). The Nuqicozupi Brushlands receives an average of 224 cm/y (88 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Nuqicozupi Brushlands covers an area of nearly 21106 km2 (13117 mi2), and an average elevation of 949 m (3113 ft) above sea level.
