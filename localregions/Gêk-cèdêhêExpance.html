The Gêk-cèdêhê Expance (/cèˈdɘhɘ/ Gêk's Beard [Proper Noun (name of person)] [hair (of body)]) is a temperate expanse on the coast of the Southlake region. Gêk-cèdêhê Expance is a relativly typical expanse for Southlake, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Gêk-cèdêhê Expance has a yearly average tempature of 21°C (69°F), with its average tempature durring the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average tempature durring the winter being a cool 15°C (59°F). The Gêk-cèdêhê Expance recieves an average of 292 cm/y (114 in/y) of percipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain durring the summer. The Gêk-cèdêhê Expance covers an area of nearly 15273 km2 (9492 mi2), and an average elevation of 1647 m (5403 ft) above sealevel.
