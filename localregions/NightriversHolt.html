The Nightrivers Holt (nightrivers /hoʊlt/ Nightrivers [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical holt on the coast of the Ancestor's Range region. Nightrivers Holt is a relatively typical holt for Ancestor's Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Nightrivers Holt has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 20°C (68°F). The Nightrivers Holt receives an average of 71 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Nightrivers Holt covers an area of nearly 10819 km2 (6724 mi2), and an average elevation of 4565 m (14977 ft) above sea level.
