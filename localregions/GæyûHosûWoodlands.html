The Gæyû Hosû Woodlands (/ˈgæjʊ/ /ˈhosʊ/ Fairmist [gorgeous] [hangover]) are a group of subtropical woodland on the coast of the Flashpan region. Gæyû Hosû Woodlands are a relatively typical woodland for Flashpan, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Gæyû Hosû Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 7°C (44°F). The Gæyû Hosû Woodlands receives an average of 137 cm/y (53 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Gæyû Hosû Woodlands covers an area of nearly 13493 km2 (8385 mi2), and an average elevation of 5625 m (18454 ft) above sea level.
