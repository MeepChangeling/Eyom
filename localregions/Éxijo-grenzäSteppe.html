The Éxijo-grenzä Steppe (/greŋˈzɑ/ Éxijo's Gown [Proper Noun (name of person)] [garment]) is a subtropical steppe on the coast of the Allpine Hills region. Éxijo-grenzä Steppe is a relatively typical steppe for Allpine Hills, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Éxijo-grenzä Steppe has a yearly average temperature of 27°C (80°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 32°C (89°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 23°C (73°F). The Éxijo-grenzä Steppe receives an average of 142 cm/y (55 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Éxijo-grenzä Steppe covers an area of nearly 18441 km2 (11461 mi2), and an average elevation of 3454 m (11332 ft) above sea level.
