The Ngäpahi-jog Expance (/ʤog/ Ngäpahi's Heart [Proper Noun (name of person)] [nucleus]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Highpeaks region. Ngäpahi-jog Expance is a relativly typical expanse for Highpeaks, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Ngäpahi-jog Expance has a yearly average tempature of 33°C (91°F), with its average tempature durring the summer being a blistering 36°C (96°F) and its average tempature durring the winter being a warm 29°C (84°F). The Ngäpahi-jog Expance recieves an average of 66 cm/y (25 in/y) of percipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain durring the spring. The Ngäpahi-jog Expance covers an area of nearly 12583 km2 (7820 mi2), and an average elevation of 11352 m (-5705 ft) above sealevel.
