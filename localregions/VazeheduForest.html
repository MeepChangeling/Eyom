The Vazehedu Forest (/ˈvaze/ /ˈhedu/ Dewdrop [style; tone] [wet]) is a subtropical forest within the temperate rainforest of the Equuscoast region. The Vazehedu Forest is the political heartlands of the Tauranian United Provinces.

Climate

The Vazehedu Forest has a yearly average temperature of 19°C (66°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 15°C (59°F). The Vazehedu Forest receives an average of 296 cm/y (116 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Vazehedu Forest covers an area of nearly 3802 km2 (2362 mi2), and an average elevation of 1311 m (4301 ft) above sea level.
