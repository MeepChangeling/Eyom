The Khib-ăh Brush (/xib/ /ə̝h/ Elderwrit [license; patent] [hag (old woman)]) is a temperate brush within the wetland of the Flanged Coast region. The Khib-ăh Brush is well known for being home to remarkably few animals.

Climate

The Khib-ăh Brush has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). The Khib-ăh Brush receives an average of 217 cm/y (85 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Khib-ăh Brush covers an area of nearly 10042 km2 (6241 mi2), and an average elevation of 3676 m (12060 ft) above sea level.
