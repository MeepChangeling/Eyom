The Kt-38v Bæq Woodlands (/bæq/ Kt-38v the Tempest's [Proper Noun (name of person)] [storm]) are a group of subtropical woodland on the coast of the Anvil Badlands region. Kt-38v Bæq Woodlands are a relatively typical woodland for Anvil Badlands, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Kt-38v Bæq Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 22°C (71°F). The Kt-38v Bæq Woodlands receives an average of 71 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Kt-38v Bæq Woodlands covers an area of nearly 8195 km2 (5093 mi2), and an average elevation of 7930 m (-16932 ft) above sea level.
