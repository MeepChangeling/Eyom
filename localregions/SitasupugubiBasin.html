The Sitasupugubi Basin (/siˈtasu/ /puˈgubi/ Unconquered [conquest] [absent]) is a subtropical basin within the wetland of the Shattered Peninsula region. The Sitasupugubi Basin is well known for its thriving abundance of plant life.

Climate

The Sitasupugubi Basin has a yearly average temperature of 15°C (59°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 21°C (69°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 10°C (50°F). The Sitasupugubi Basin receives an average of 206 cm/y (81 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the long winter months. The Sitasupugubi Basin covers an area of nearly 16757 km2 (10414 mi2), and an average elevation of 3882 m (12736 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Sitasupugubi Basin is overseen by Potentate Uneas Ethobara Oenthniu, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 44% of the population self-report as devout. The Sitasupugubi Basin is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Sitasupugubi Basin has its economic roots in Shattered Peninsula's export of Metals, but has developed its own engines industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.
