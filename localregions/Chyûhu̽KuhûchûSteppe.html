The Chyûhu̽ Kuhûchû Steppe (/ˈcʊhu̽/ /kuˈhʊʧʊ/ Nostalgia [sweet; delicious] [memory]) is a temperate steppe within the wetland of the Substrate region. The Chyûhu̽ Kuhûchû Steppe is well known for its thriving abundance of plant life.

Climate

The Chyûhu̽ Kuhûchû Steppe has a yearly average temperature of 19°C (66°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 11°C (51°F). The Chyûhu̽ Kuhûchû Steppe receives an average of 219 cm/y (86 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Chyûhu̽ Kuhûchû Steppe covers an area of nearly 14533 km2 (9032 mi2), and an average elevation of 1977 m (6486 ft) above sea level.

The Chyûhu̽ Kuhûchû Steppe is home to 150209 people. They break down into the following demographics: 51% Constructs, 20% Gnolls, 14% Wareneese, 6% Tanuki, 7% Imperial, and ~2% Other.

The Chyûhu̽ Kuhûchû Steppe is's peoples are culturally Constructi, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include faithfulness towards one’s chosen friends and vengeance and execution of just vendettas, as well as a unique rain festival and a beer festival.

Local Religion

The Chyûhu̽ Kuhûchû Steppe is overseen by Potentate Shogius Frealate Androne, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 25% of the population self-report as devout. The Chyûhu̽ Kuhûchû Steppe is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Chyûhu̽ Kuhûchû Steppe has its economic roots in Substrate's export of Agriculture, but has developed its own medicine industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.
