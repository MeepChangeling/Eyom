The Gêk-cèdêhê Expanse (/cèˈdɘhɘ/ Gêk's Beard [Proper Noun (name of person)] [hair (of body)]) is a temperate expanse on the coast of the Southlake region. Gêk-cèdêhê Expanse is a relatively typical expanse for Southlake, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Gêk-cèdêhê Expanse has a yearly average temperature of 21°C (69°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 15°C (59°F). The Gêk-cèdêhê Expanse receives an average of 292 cm/y (114 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Gêk-cèdêhê Expanse covers an area of nearly 15273 km2 (9492 mi2), and an average elevation of 1647 m (5403 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Gêk-cèdêhê Expanse is overseen by Potentate Boslotl Euandrodite Nothentus, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 56% of the population self-report as devout. The Gêk-cèdêhê Expanse is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
