The Hārsi̊shāv Holt (/həˑr/ /ˈsi̘ʃəˑv/ Kingsbane [dagger] [political]) is a subtropical holt on the coast of the Foxvale region. Hārsi̊shāv Holt is a relatively typical holt for Foxvale, as well as Eyom as a whole.

The Hārsi̊shāv Holt has a yearly average temperature of 13°C (55°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing 0°C (32°F). The Hārsi̊shāv Holt receives an average of 154 cm/y (60 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Hārsi̊shāv Holt covers an area of nearly 19647 km2 (12210 mi2), and an average elevation of 4911 m (16112 ft) above sea level.
