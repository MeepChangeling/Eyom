The Kasdèbi Shrublands (/cas/ /ˈdɛbi/ Fluffybridge [traffic] [fuzzy]) are a group of subtropical shrubland within the temperate rainforest of the Substrate region. Kasdèbi Shrublands are a relatively typical shrubland for Substrate, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Kasdèbi Shrublands has a yearly average temperature of 17°C (62°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 10°C (50°F). The Kasdèbi Shrublands receives an average of 311 cm/y (122 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Kasdèbi Shrublands covers an area of nearly 21669 km2 (13467 mi2), and an average elevation of 1992 m (6535 ft) above sea level.
