The Nu Gewrcht Brushlands (/nu/ /guːrkt/ Nobs [green] [boss]) are a group of subtropical brushland on the coast of the Lower Warding Range region. Nu Gewrcht Brushlands are a relatively typical brushland for Lower Warding Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Nu Gewrcht Brushlands has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 22°C (71°F). The Nu Gewrcht Brushlands receives an average of 67 cm/y (26 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Nu Gewrcht Brushlands covers an area of nearly 9527 km2 (5921 mi2), and an average elevation of 9035 m (-13307 ft) above sea level.
