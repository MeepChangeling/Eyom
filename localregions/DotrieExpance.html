The Dotrie Expance (dotrie expance DotrIe [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Greenrange region. Dotrie Expance is a relativly typical expanse for Greenrange, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Dotrie Expance has a yearly average tempature of 30°C (86°F), with its average tempature durring the summer being a blistering 35°C (95°F) and its average tempature durring the winter being a pleasent 25°C (77°F). The Dotrie Expance recieves an average of 140 cm/y (55 in/y) of percipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain durring the spring. The Dotrie Expance covers an area of nearly 10281 km2 (6389 mi2), and an average elevation of 11845 m (-4088 ft) above sealevel.

People and Culture

Imperial Warning: The following section of this document deals with biological race. In defrence to the traditions of Eyomic peoples, we have forgon town-specific racial cencus, howeaver, as our culture lacks a taboo for classification of peoples by their biology and such infomration is highly important for our medical practices amongst other institutions, we have stated the racial demographics of this region. We do not do so to sew the seeds of disunity. We do not do so to belittle or bemoan any group. Such issues are why we use the term "peoples" rather than races, cultures, or species.

In respect to this universal cultural element, we have split racial classification by culture while keeping the name of each race for our own purposes. Should you see the same race listed more than once in the following section, this is to desinate a major cultural division within a race.

The Dotrie Expance is's peoples are culturaly Sylvanian, but have their own local customs and traditions. Their shared vaules and traditions include submission to the collective will or culture and filial devotion to family and parents, as well as a unique spring festival and a wine festival.

Sylvin

The Sylvin subculture native to the Dotrie Expance are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Sylvin culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of color choices with social meaning to them and base their sence of individual idenity on identity based on their local community. They also place greater cultural emphasus on membership in an elite bloodline or caste and submission to lawful authority.

Kitsune

The Kitsune subculture native to the Dotrie Expance are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Sylvin culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of culturally-significant jewelry or accessories and base their sence of individual idenity on professional guild or trade-specific group. They also place greater cultural emphasus on building things in service of their posterity and personal indulgence and luxuriant pleasure.

Kivu

The Kivu subculture native to the Dotrie Expance are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Sylvin culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of impractical or elaborate role-based clothes and base their sence of individual idenity on astrologically-determined birth groups. They also place greater cultural emphasus on leadership and charisma in the group and excellence in one’s profession or trade.

Kobold

The Kobold subculture native to the Dotrie Expance are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Sylvin culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of tattoos of some cultural significance and base their sence of individual idenity on patron-client relationships with major figures. They also place greater cultural emphasus on sexual license and wantonness and magical prowess and occult ability.

Elf

The Elf subculture native to the Dotrie Expance are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Sylvin culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of socially-meaningful animal motif items and base their sence of individual idenity on regional and province-based identity. They also place greater cultural emphasus on sharing wealth and goods with others and loyalty to one’s friends, family, and own.

Local Religion

The Dotrie Expance is overseen by Potentate Qhivx Wealiasta Aglaukos, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's pricipal religion. Aproximently 22% of the population self-report as devout. The Dotrie Expance is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevent to their portfolio.

Settlments and Economy

Dotrie Expance has its economic roots in Greenrange's export of Metals, but has developed its own forestry byproducts industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.

The Dotrie Expance has an Imperial Development Index of 23. For the refrence of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 23 means there are 23 locations within the Dotrie Expance which the Empire can securly and regurly transport matrials and personell..

As a courticy for scholars and agents, the Dotrie Expance is rated I1 by Eyoms banks. Buisness ventures within the Dotrie Expance are expected to return above capitol at low risk.
