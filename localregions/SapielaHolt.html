The Sapiela Holt (/ˈsapi/ /ˈela/ Lastunion [wedding] [last (only remaining)]) is a subtropical holt within the temperate rainforest of the Illa's Panhandle region. Sapiela Holt is a relatively typical holt for Illa's Panhandle, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Sapiela Holt has a yearly average temperature of 22°C (71°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Sapiela Holt receives an average of 285 cm/y (112 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Sapiela Holt covers an area of nearly 21619 km2 (13436 mi2), and an average elevation of 2475 m (8120 ft) above sea level.
