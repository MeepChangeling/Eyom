Climate

The Mûgu̹ Hûhiga Forest has a yearly average temperature of 15°C (59°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 6°C (42°F). The Mûgu̹ Hûhiga Forest receives an average of 146 cm/y (57 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Mûgu̹ Hûhiga Forest covers an area of nearly 14372 km2 (8932 mi2), and an average elevation of 7252 m (-19157 ft) above sea level.
