The Hiyàbumwùy Fields (Hiyà /ˈbumwʊj/ Hiyà's Gun [Proper Noun (name of person)] [pistol]) are a group of subtropical fields within the temperate deciduous forest of the Darkrass Plains region. The Hiyàbumwùy Fields are the birthplace of the The Council of Twelve, a major Sylvin religion.

Climate

The Hiyàbumwùy Fields has a yearly average temperature of 14°C (57°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 2°C (35°F). The Hiyàbumwùy Fields receives an average of 138 cm/y (54 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Hiyàbumwùy Fields covers an area of nearly 17995 km2 (11183 mi2), and an average elevation of 4247 m (13933 ft) above sea level.
