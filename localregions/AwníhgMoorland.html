The Awníhg Moorland (/aw/ /nɪ̞g/ Mercanary [rebel; guerrilla] [private]) is a subtropical moorland on the coast of the Bountyrange region. Awníhg Moorland is a relatively typical moorland for Bountyrange, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Awníhg Moorland has a yearly average temperature of 19°C (66°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 11°C (51°F). The Awníhg Moorland receives an average of 274 cm/y (107 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Awníhg Moorland covers an area of nearly 14652 km2 (9106 mi2), and an average elevation of 3695 m (12122 ft) above sea level.

The Awníhg Moorland is home to 415622 people. They break down into the following demographics: 69% Sylvin, 16% Goblins, 8% Elf, 5% Tanuki, and ~2% Other.

The Awníhg Moorland is's peoples are culturally Sylvanian, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include seeking fortune in new places or new roles and membership in an elite bloodline or caste, as well as a unique spring festival and a sun festival.

Sylvin

The Sylvin subculture native to the Awníhg Moorland are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Sylvin culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of patterned hair shaving or depilation and base their sence of individual idenity on ideological groups or philosophical sects. They also place greater cultural emphasus on ethnic purity of blood and culture and justice and fairness between people.

Local Religion

The Awníhg Moorland is overseen by Potentate Yruer Æthenope Mapoles, a god serving within Covenant of Irus, the region's principal religion. Approximately 30% of the population self-report as devout. The Awníhg Moorland is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Awníhg Moorland has its economic roots in Bountyrange's export of Metals, but has developed its own innovation industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.

The Awníhg Moorland has an Imperial Development Index of 24. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 24 means there are 24 locations within the Awníhg Moorland which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
