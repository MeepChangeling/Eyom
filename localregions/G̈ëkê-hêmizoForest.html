The G̈ëkê-hêmizo Forest is overseen by Potentate Unlotl Ponthus Waltheus, a god serving within The Council of Twelve, the region's principal religion. Approximately 53% of the population self-report as devout. The G̈ëkê-hêmizo Forest is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
