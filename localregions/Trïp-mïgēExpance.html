The Trïp-mïgē Expance has a yearly average tempature of 34°C (93°F), with its average tempature durring the summer being a blistering 38°C (100°F) and its average tempature durring the winter being a hot 31°C (87°F). The Trïp-mïgē Expance recieves an average of 69 cm/y (27 in/y) of percipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain durring the spring. The Trïp-mïgē Expance covers an area of nearly 12418 km2 (7717 mi2), and an average elevation of 19219 m (20104 ft) above sealevel.

The Trïp-mïgē Expance is overseen by Potentate Hinia Korawn Simonchusa, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's pricipal religion. Aproximently 25% of the population self-report as devout.
