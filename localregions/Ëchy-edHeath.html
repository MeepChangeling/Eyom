The Ëchy-ed Heath (/ɛc/ /ed/ Paleale [pub; bar (pub)] [anxiety]) is a subtropical heath on the coast of the Abysstro region. Ëchy-ed Heath is a relatively typical heath for Abysstro, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Ëchy-ed Heath has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 4°C (39°F). The Ëchy-ed Heath receives an average of 152 cm/y (59 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Ëchy-ed Heath covers an area of nearly 16964 km2 (10543 mi2), and an average elevation of 2747 m (9012 ft) above sea level.

The Ëchy-ed Heath is home to 1390918 people. They break down into the following demographics: 59% Wood Elves, 17% Gnolls, 9% Hobgoblins, 7% Hobgoblins, 6% Kobold, and ~2% Other.

The Ëchy-ed Heath is's peoples are culturally Wood Elven, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include honesty and truthfulness in speech and submission to the collective will or culture, as well as a unique spirit festival and a winter festival.

Wood Elf

The Wood Elf subculture native to the Ëchy-ed Heath are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of patterned hair shaving or depilation and base their sence of individual idenity on extended family out to cousins and like kin. They also place greater cultural emphasus on membership in an elite bloodline or caste and prosperity and accruing material wealth.

Hobgoblin

The Hobgoblin subculture native to the Ëchy-ed Heath are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. Kobold

The Kobold subculture native to the Ëchy-ed Heath are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. Local Religion

The Ëchy-ed Heath is overseen by Potentate Ghulena Æthen Ælfstandros, a god serving within Pagan, the region's principal religion. Approximately 57% of the population self-report as devout. The Ëchy-ed Heath is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Ëchy-ed Heath has its economic roots in Abysstro's export of Spirits, but has developed its own legal industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.
