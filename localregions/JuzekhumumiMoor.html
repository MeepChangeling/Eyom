The Juzekhumumi Moor (/juˈzexu/ /ˈmumi/ Stardrop [shooting star] [lethal; fatal]) is a subtropical moor within the wetland of the Shattered Peninsula region. Juzekhumumi Moor is a relatively typical moor for Shattered Peninsula, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Juzekhumumi Moor has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 23°C (73°F). The Juzekhumumi Moor receives an average of 200 cm/y (78 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Juzekhumumi Moor covers an area of nearly 13979 km2 (8688 mi2), and an average elevation of 4136 m (13569 ft) above sea level.
