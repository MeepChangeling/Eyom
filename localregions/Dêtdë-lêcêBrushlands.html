The Dêtdë-lêcê Brushlands (/ˈlɘcɘ/ Dêtdë's Outhouse [Proper Noun (name of person)] [bathroom]) are a group of subtropical brushland on the coast of the Ancestor's Range region. Dêtdë-lêcê Brushlands are a relatively typical brushland for Ancestor's Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Dêtdë-lêcê Brushlands has a yearly average temperature of 24°C (75°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 20°C (68°F). The Dêtdë-lêcê Brushlands receives an average of 74 cm/y (29 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Dêtdë-lêcê Brushlands covers an area of nearly 11221 km2 (6973 mi2), and an average elevation of 4252 m (13950 ft) above sea level.

The Dêtdë-lêcê Brushlands are home to 1217315 people. They break down into the following demographics: 67% Wood Elves, 18% Wareneese, 5% Tanuki, 8% Kobold, and ~2% Other.

The Dêtdë-lêcê Brushlands are's peoples are culturally Wood Elven, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include beauty and seductive charm and raw strength and personal prowess, as well as a unique folk festival and a spring festival.

Wood Elf

The Wood Elf subculture native to the Dêtdë-lêcê Brushlands are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of color choices with social meaning to them and base their sence of individual idenity on having or lacking noble blood. They also place greater cultural emphasus on beauty and seductive charm and exploring the unknown and discovering secrets.

Rattu

The Rattu subculture native to the Dêtdë-lêcê Brushlands are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of patterned hair shaving or depilation and base their sence of individual idenity on lineages based on ancient hero-ancestors. They also place greater cultural emphasus on filial devotion to family and parents and ascetic unworldliness and pious poverty.

Tanuki

The Tanuki subculture native to the Dêtdë-lêcê Brushlands are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of significant scent or perfume uses and base their sence of individual idenity on extended family out to cousins and like kin. They also place greater cultural emphasus on vengeance and execution of just vendettas and vengeance and execution of just vendettas.

Kobold

The Kobold subculture native to the Dêtdë-lêcê Brushlands are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of culturally-significant jewelry or accessories and base their sence of individual idenity on lineages based on ancient hero-ancestors. They also place greater cultural emphasus on dominating and possessing other people and ethnic purity of blood and culture.

Local Religion

The Dêtdë-lêcê Brushlands are overseen by Potentate Xerena Iapon Hecatean, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's principal religion. Approximately 38% of the population self-report as devout. The Dêtdë-lêcê Brushlands is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Dêtdë-lêcê Brushlands has its economic roots in Ancestor's Range's export of Stone, but has developed its own musical instruments industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.
