The Ewf Laz Heathland (/juːf/ /laz/ Wisering [wise] [ring]) is a subtropical heathland within the wetland of the Flanged Coast region. The Ewf Laz Heathland is well known for being largely barren of plant life.

Climate

The Ewf Laz Heathland has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 30°C (86°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 20°C (68°F). The Ewf Laz Heathland receives an average of 226 cm/y (88 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Ewf Laz Heathland covers an area of nearly 10513 km2 (6533 mi2), and an average elevation of 2327 m (7634 ft) above sea level.
