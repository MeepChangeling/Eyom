The Ntiqüe Növ Heath (/ntiq˔/ /no˞v/ Firstearth [main] [surface]) is a temperate heath within the tropical rainforest of the Kiteshield Peninsula region. Ntiqüe Növ Heath is a relatively typical heath for Kiteshield Peninsula, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Ntiqüe Növ Heath has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 9°C (48°F). The Ntiqüe Növ Heath receives an average of 295 cm/y (116 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Ntiqüe Növ Heath covers an area of nearly 5471 km2 (3400 mi2), and an average elevation of 2287 m (7503 ft) above sea level.
