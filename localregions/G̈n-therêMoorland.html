The G̈n-therê Moorland (/ˈʃerɘ/ G̈nshome [Proper Noun (name of person)] [origin; root]) is a subtropical moorland on the coast of the Cutmountains region. G̈n-therê Moorland is a relatively typical moorland for Cutmountains, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The G̈n-therê Moorland has a yearly average temperature of 30°C (86°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 35°C (95°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 26°C (78°F). The G̈n-therê Moorland receives an average of 70 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The G̈n-therê Moorland covers an area of nearly 11743 km2 (7298 mi2), and an average elevation of 11233 m (-6095 ft) above sea level.
