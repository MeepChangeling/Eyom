The Hampgami Moorland (hampgami /ˈmʊrˌlænd/ Hampgami [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical moorland on the coast of the Lower Warding Range region. The Hampgami Moorland is well known for its thriving abundance of plant life.

Climate

The Hampgami Moorland has a yearly average temperature of 32°C (89°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 36°C (96°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 27°C (80°F). The Hampgami Moorland receives an average of 69 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Hampgami Moorland covers an area of nearly 12803 km2 (7957 mi2), and an average elevation of 10460 m (-8632 ft) above sea level.
