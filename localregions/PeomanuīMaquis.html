The Peomanuī Maquis (/peˈoma/ /ˈnuji/ Onispub [drug; medicine] [devil]) is a subtropical maquis within the wetland of the Shattered Peninsula region. Peomanuī Maquis is a relatively typical maquis for Shattered Peninsula, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Peomanuī Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 20°C (68°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 13°C (55°F). The Peomanuī Maquis receives an average of 223 cm/y (87 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Peomanuī Maquis covers an area of nearly 7010 km2 (4356 mi2), and an average elevation of 1380 m (4527 ft) above sea level.
