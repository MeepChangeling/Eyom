The Natqíhw Expance (/nat/ /qɪ̞w/ Shortnose [nose; beak; nostril] [short; low]) is a temperate expanse on the coast of the Ablative region. Natqíhw Expance is a relativly typical expanse for Ablative, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Natqíhw Expance has a yearly average tempature of 14°C (57°F), with its average tempature durring the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average tempature durring the winter being a cold 2°C (35°F). The Natqíhw Expance recieves an average of 150 cm/y (59 in/y) of percipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow durring the pleasently short winter months. The Natqíhw Expance covers an area of nearly 16432 km2 (10212 mi2), and an average elevation of 4282 m (14048 ft) above sealevel.

The Natqíhw Expance is's peoples are culturaly Wood Elven, but have their own local customs and traditions. Their shared vaules and traditions include social progress toward some eventual utopia and honor and maintaining one’s integrity, as well as a unique history festival and a harvest festival.

Wood Elf

The Wood Elf subculture native to the Natqíhw Expance are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of color choices with social meaning to them and base their sence of individual idenity on having or lacking noble blood. They also place greater cultural emphasus on pacifism and peaceful resolution of problems and eloquence and social expertise.

Rattu

The Rattu subculture native to the Natqíhw Expance are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of socially-meaningful animal motif items and base their sence of individual idenity on astrologically-determined birth groups. They also place greater cultural emphasus on piety and devotion to the gods and vengeance and execution of just vendettas.

Kivu

The Kivu subculture native to the Natqíhw Expance are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of socially-meaningful animal motif items and base their sence of individual idenity on warbands or civic military service groups. They also place greater cultural emphasus on restoring some real or imagined glorious past and conquest and domination of others.

Imperial

The Imperial subculture native to the Natqíhw Expance are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of culturally-significant jewelry or accessories and base their sence of individual idenity on far-flung clans of affiliated families. They also place greater cultural emphasus on remembrance of the past and memorializing history and magical prowess and occult ability.

Local Religion

The Natqíhw Expance is overseen by Potentate Anrus Leof Weandite, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's pricipal religion. Aproximently 24% of the population self-report as devout. The Natqíhw Expance is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevent to their portfolio.

Natqíhw Expance is noteworthy for its economic independence from Ablative's export of Wool. Natqíhw Expance is particularly renound for its research industry. It is also has a noteworthy steel industry.
