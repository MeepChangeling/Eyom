The Lehsho Härt Brushlands (/lɪ̘ˈs̼o/ /hərt/ Mightybronze [practical; pragmatic] [copper; bronze]) are a group of subtropical brushland on the coast of the Flanged Coast region. Lehsho Härt Brushlands are a relatively typical brushland for Flanged Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Lehsho Härt Brushlands has a yearly average temperature of 27°C (80°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 31°C (87°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 24°C (75°F). The Lehsho Härt Brushlands receives an average of 284 cm/y (111 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Lehsho Härt Brushlands covers an area of nearly 9161 km2 (5693 mi2), and an average elevation of 4563 m (14970 ft) above sea level.

The Lehsho Härt Brushlands are home to 240467 people. They break down into the following demographics: 54% Constructs, 26% Fae, 5% Hobgoblins, 9% Imperial, 4% Imperial, and ~2% Other.

The Lehsho Härt Brushlands are's peoples are culturally Constructi, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include submission to the collective will or culture and personal development and limit-pushing, as well as a unique summer festival and a food festival.

Construct

The Construct subculture native to the Lehsho Härt Brushlands are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Construct culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of worn weapons, tools or trade implements and base their sence of individual idenity on hereditary loyalty to a political group. They also place greater cultural emphasus on courage and valiance in danger and remembrance of the past and memorializing history.

The Fae subculture native to the Lehsho Härt Brushlands are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Construct culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of socially-meaningful animal motif items and base their sence of individual idenity on inheritance of an ancient body modification. They also place greater cultural emphasus on industry and the ability to work tirelessly and scheming subtly against enemies or rivals.

Hobgoblin

The Hobgoblin subculture native to the Lehsho Härt Brushlands are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Construct culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of color choices with social meaning to them and base their sence of individual idenity on lineages based on ancient hero-ancestors. They also place greater cultural emphasus on filial devotion to family and parents and submission to lawful authority.

The Quenn subculture native to the Lehsho Härt Brushlands are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Construct culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of impractical or elaborate role-based clothes and base their sence of individual idenity on extended family out to cousins and like kin. Local Religion

The Lehsho Härt Brushlands are overseen by Potentate Theion Diassa Goina, a god serving within Covenant of Irus, the region's principal religion. Approximately 57% of the population self-report as devout. The Lehsho Härt Brushlands is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Lehsho Härt Brushlands is a negative return economy zone at this time. It has an abundence of Fish, though infrastructure to this region of Flanged Coast is not developed enough to support any major local industry.

The Lehsho Härt Brushlands has an Imperial Development Index of 5. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 5 means there are 5 locations within the Lehsho Härt Brushlands which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
