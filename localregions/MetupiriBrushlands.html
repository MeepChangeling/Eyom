The Metupiri Brushlands (/ˈmetu/ /ˈpiri/ Emptyhearth [fire; flame] [empty; vacant; naked; nude; tired (needing rest)]) are a group of subtropical brushland within the temperate rainforest of the Equuscoast region. Metupiri Brushlands are a relatively typical brushland for Equuscoast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Metupiri Brushlands has a yearly average temperature of 22°C (71°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 18°C (64°F). The Metupiri Brushlands receives an average of 307 cm/y (120 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Metupiri Brushlands covers an area of nearly 9317 km2 (5790 mi2), and an average elevation of 1085 m (3559 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Metupiri Brushlands are overseen by Potentate Ades Orphandros Frealhmær, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 14% of the population self-report as devout. The Metupiri Brushlands is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Metupiri Brushlands is noteworthy for its economic independence from Equuscoast's export of Machine Parts. Metupiri Brushlands is particularly renound for its tools industry. It is also has a noteworthy divine legal industry.
