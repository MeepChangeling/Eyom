The Ze-34n Věz Expance (/vɛz/ Ze-34n's Battlefield [Proper Noun (name of person)] [battle]) is a temperate expanse on the coast of the Westlake region. Ze-34n Věz Expance is a relativly typical expanse for Westlake, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Ze-34n Věz Expance has a yearly average tempature of 27°C (80°F), with its average tempature durring the summer being a hot 31°C (87°F) and its average tempature durring the winter being a pleasent 24°C (75°F). The Ze-34n Věz Expance recieves an average of 142 cm/y (55 in/y) of percipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain durring the spring. The Ze-34n Věz Expance covers an area of nearly 14901 km2 (9261 mi2), and an average elevation of 3829 m (12562 ft) above sealevel.

People and Culture

In defrence to the traditions of Eyomic peoples, we have forgon town-specific racial cencus, howeaver, as our culture lacks a taboo for classification of peoples by their biology and such infomration is highly important for our medical practices amongst other institutions, we have stated the racial demographics of this region. In respect to this universal cultural element, we have split racial classification by culture while keeping the name of each race for our own purposes. Should you see the same race listed more than once in the following section, this is to desinate a major cultural division within a race.

The Ze-34n Věz Expance is's peoples are culturaly Tauranian, but have their own local customs and traditions. Their shared vaules and traditions include pacifism and peaceful resolution of problems and unity and elimination of group differences, as well as a unique rain festival and a history festival.

Tauran

The Tauran subculture native to the Ze-34n Věz Expance are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Tauran culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of piercings, whether minor or elaborate and base their sence of individual idenity on voluntary mutual-assistance brotherhoods. They also place greater cultural emphasus on remembrance of the past and memorializing history and cunning and the ability to trick others.

Iron Elf

The Iron Elf subculture native to the Ze-34n Věz Expance are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Tauran culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of piercings, whether minor or elaborate and base their sence of individual idenity on dynastic lineages with cadet branches. They also place greater cultural emphasus on unity and elimination of group differences and cunning and the ability to trick others.

Kobold

The Kobold subculture native to the Ze-34n Věz Expance are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Tauran culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of color choices with social meaning to them and base their sence of individual idenity on numerous castes of hereditary workers. They also place greater cultural emphasus on membership in an elite bloodline or caste and scheming subtly against enemies or rivals.

Elf

The Elf subculture native to the Ze-34n Věz Expance are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Tauran culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of culturally-significant jewelry or accessories and base their sence of individual idenity on regional and province-based identity. They also place greater cultural emphasus on prosperity and accruing material wealth and personal development and limit-pushing.

Local Religion

The Ze-34n Věz Expance is overseen by Potentate Phireus Wealate Eoghaliapus, a god serving within Therilism, the region's pricipal religion. Aproximently 49% of the population self-report as devout. The Ze-34n Věz Expance is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevent to their portfolio.

Settlments and Economy

Ze-34n Věz Expance has its economic roots in Westlake's export of Hemp, but has developed its own tools industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.

The Ze-34n Věz Expance has an Imperial Development Index of 19. For the refrence of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 19 means there are 19 locations within the Ze-34n Věz Expance which the Empire can securly and regurly transport matrials and personell..

As a courticy for scholars and agents, the Ze-34n Věz Expance is rated I2 by Eyoms banks. Buisness ventures within the Ze-34n Věz Expance are expected to return above capitol with some volitility.
