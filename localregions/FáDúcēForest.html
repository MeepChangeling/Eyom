The Fá Dúcē Forest (/fɑ́/ /dʌ́kæ/ Damned [evil] [incident]) is a subtropical forest within the wetland of the Silicahilands region. Fá Dúcē Forest is a relatively typical forest for Silicahilands, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Fá Dúcē Forest has a yearly average temperature of 26°C (78°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 30°C (86°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Fá Dúcē Forest receives an average of 217 cm/y (85 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Fá Dúcē Forest covers an area of nearly 20094 km2 (12488 mi2), and an average elevation of 4483 m (14708 ft) above sea level.
