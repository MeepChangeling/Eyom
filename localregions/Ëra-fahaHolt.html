Climate

The Ëra-faha Holt has a yearly average temperature of 17°C (62°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 9°C (48°F). The Ëra-faha Holt covers an area of nearly 10652 km2 (6620 mi2), and an average elevation of 6800 m (-20639 ft) above sea level.
