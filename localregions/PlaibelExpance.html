The Plaibel Expance (plaibel expance Plaibel [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Upper Warding Range region. Plaibel Expance is a relativly typical expanse for Upper Warding Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Plaibel Expance has a yearly average tempature of 31°C (87°F), with its average tempature durring the summer being a blistering 36°C (96°F) and its average tempature durring the winter being a warm 27°C (80°F). The Plaibel Expance recieves an average of 66 cm/y (25 in/y) of percipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain durring the spring. The Plaibel Expance covers an area of nearly 15573 km2 (9678 mi2), and an average elevation of 12770 m (-1053 ft) above sealevel.
