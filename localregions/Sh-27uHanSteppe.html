The Sh-27u Han Steppe (/han/ Sh-27u's Prize [Proper Noun (name of person)] [reward; award; profit]) is a subtropical steppe on the coast of the Brownmarsh region. Sh-27u Han Steppe is a relatively typical steppe for Brownmarsh, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Sh-27u Han Steppe has a yearly average temperature of 26°C (78°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 23°C (73°F). The Sh-27u Han Steppe receives an average of 139 cm/y (54 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Sh-27u Han Steppe covers an area of nearly 14950 km2 (9291 mi2), and an average elevation of 4015 m (13172 ft) above sea level.

The Sh-27u Han Steppe is home to 386472 people. They break down into the following demographics: 56% Iron Elves, 23% Hobgoblins, 6% Wareneese, 7% Elf, 6% Imperial, and ~2% Other.

The Sh-27u Han Steppe is's peoples are culturally Goblin, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include prosperity and accruing material wealth and excellence in one’s profession or trade, as well as a unique history festival and a folk festival.

Local Religion

The Sh-27u Han Steppe is overseen by Potentate Oenar Etheof Mapollos, a god serving within Fawresm, the region's principal religion. Approximately 18% of the population self-report as devout. The Sh-27u Han Steppe is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Sh-27u Han Steppe has its economic roots in Brownmarsh's export of Coffee, but has developed its own dyes industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.

The Sh-27u Han Steppe has an Imperial Development Index of 18. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 18 means there are 18 locations within the Sh-27u Han Steppe which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
