The Dog̈i-sëri Basin (/ˈdog̃i/ /ˈsëri/ Fairmire [beauty] [swamp]) is a subtropical basin on the coast of the Ancestor's Range region. The Dog̈i-sëri Basin is well known for its thriving abundance of plant life.

Climate

The Dog̈i-sëri Basin has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 17°C (62°F). The Dog̈i-sëri Basin receives an average of 209 cm/y (82 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Dog̈i-sëri Basin covers an area of nearly 18031 km2 (11206 mi2), and an average elevation of 5244 m (17204 ft) above sea level.
