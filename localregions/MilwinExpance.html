The Milwin Expance (milwin expance Milwin [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical expanse within the temperate rainforest of the Palebay region. The Milwin Expance is the cultural homeland of the Dwarvern peoples.

Climate

The Milwin Expance has a yearly average tempature of 23°C (73°F), with its average tempature durring the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average tempature durring the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). The Milwin Expance recieves an average of 284 cm/y (111 in/y) of percipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain durring the summer. The Milwin Expance covers an area of nearly 19668 km2 (12223 mi2), and an average elevation of 3669 m (12037 ft) above sealevel.

The Milwin Expance is's peoples are culturaly Dwarvern, but have their own local customs and traditions. Their shared vaules and traditions include zealous guardianship of their own land or holy sit and leadership and charisma in the group, as well as a unique relic festival and a harvest festival.

Dwarf

The Dwarf subculture native to the Milwin Expance are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Dwarf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of impractical or elaborate role-based clothes and base their sence of individual idenity on far-flung clans of affiliated families. They also place greater cultural emphasus on remembrance of the past and memorializing history and ethnic purity of blood and culture.

They also place greater cultural emphasus on subtlety and indirectness of action and magical prowess and occult ability.

Gnoll

Grey Elf

The Grey Elf subculture native to the Milwin Expance are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. They also place greater cultural emphasus on ethnic purity of blood and culture and exploring the unknown and discovering secrets.

Elf

While they follow the broader tennents of Dwarf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of color choices with social meaning to them and base their sence of individual idenity on identity based on their local community. Grey Elf

The Grey Elf subculture native to the Milwin Expance are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Dwarf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of culturally-significant jewelry or accessories and base their sence of individual idenity on numerous castes of hereditary workers. They also place greater cultural emphasus on sharing wealth and goods with others and submission to lawful authority.

Local Religion

The Milwin Expance is overseen by Potentate Adite Aina Briassan, a god serving within The Council of Twelve, the region's pricipal religion. Aproximently 34% of the population self-report as devout. The Milwin Expance is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevent to their portfolio.

Settlments and Economy

Milwin Expance is noteworthy for its economic independence from Palebay's export of Bauxite. Milwin Expance is particularly renound for its arcana industry. It is also has a noteworthy finance industry.

The Milwin Expance has an Imperial Development Index of 26. For the refrence of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 26 means there are 26 locations within the Milwin Expance which the Empire can securly and regurly transport matrials and personell..
