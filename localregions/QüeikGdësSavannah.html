The Qüeik Gdës Savannah (/q˔ik/ /gdɛs/ Everfeccund [aroused (sexually)] [century]) is a subtropical savannah within the wetland of the Heartland region. The Qüeik Gdës Savannah is home to an anomaly which places the plant and animal populations in a permanent breeding season. Strangely, neither plant nor animal outbreeds its available food supply, resulting in one of the highest densities of flora and fauna in all of Eyom. This anomaly also affects people, though it does not increase their fertility, preventing the Qüeik Gdës Savannah from being the most populous region in Eyom. Note: The Qüeik Gdës Savannah was discovered and named by a particularly traditional Goblin tribe. Its true name is typically regarded as obscene, hence its official name of Qüeik Gdës Savannah.

Climate

The Qüeik Gdës Savannah has a yearly average temperature of 14°C (57°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 5°C (41°F). The Qüeik Gdës Savannah receives an average of 216 cm/y (85 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Qüeik Gdës Savannah covers an area of nearly 18042 km2 (11213 mi2), and an average elevation of 1786 m (5859 ft) above sea level.
