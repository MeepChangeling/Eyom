The Z̄asunele Savannah (/ˈz̚asu/ /ˈnele/ Tinkersteed [mule] [mobile; portable]) is a subtropical savanna within the wetland of the Stalfijord region. Z̄asunele Savannah is a relatively typical savanna for Stalfijord, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Z̄asunele Savannah has a yearly average temperature of 14°C (57°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a cool 19°C (66°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 10°C (50°F). The Z̄asunele Savannah receives an average of 209 cm/y (82 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Z̄asunele Savannah covers an area of nearly 8921 km2 (5544 mi2), and an average elevation of 2637 m (8651 ft) above sea level.
