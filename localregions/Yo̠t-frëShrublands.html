Climate

The Yo̠t-frë Shrublands has a yearly average temperature of 13°C (55°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 2°C (35°F). The Yo̠t-frë Shrublands receives an average of 297 cm/y (116 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Yo̠t-frë Shrublands covers an area of nearly 12262 km2 (7620 mi2), and an average elevation of 1116 m (3661 ft) above sea level.
