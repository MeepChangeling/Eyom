The Frëtbë Fëldnōd Woodlands (/ˈfrɛtbɛ/ /ˈfɛldnoːd/ Direwood [lethal; fatal] [percent]) are a group of subtropical woodland on the coast of the Timberlund region. Frëtbë Fëldnōd Woodlands are a relatively typical woodland for Timberlund, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Frëtbë Fëldnōd Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 5°C (41°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold -13°C (9°F). The Frëtbë Fëldnōd Woodlands receives an average of 222 cm/y (87 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Frëtbë Fëldnōd Woodlands covers an area of nearly 8509 km2 (5288 mi2), and an average elevation of 5407 m (17739 ft) above sea level.
