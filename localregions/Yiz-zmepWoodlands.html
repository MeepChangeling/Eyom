The Yiz-zmep Woodlands (/jiz/ /zmep/ Mark III [mobile; portable] [version]) are a group of subtropical woodland on the coast of the Palebay region. Yiz-zmep Woodlands are a relatively typical woodland for Palebay, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Yiz-zmep Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 28°C (82°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 31°C (87°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 24°C (75°F). The Yiz-zmep Woodlands receives an average of 135 cm/y (53 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Yiz-zmep Woodlands covers an area of nearly 19729 km2 (12261 mi2), and an average elevation of 4251 m (13946 ft) above sea level.
