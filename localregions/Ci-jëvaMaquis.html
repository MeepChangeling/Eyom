The Ci-jëva Maquis (/ci/ /ˈʤëva/ Royaldye [navy blue] [member]) is a subtropical maquis on the coast of the Mullberry Dales region. The Ci-jëva Maquis is well known for its thriving abundance of plant life.

Climate

The Ci-jëva Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 30°C (86°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 17°C (62°F). The Ci-jëva Maquis receives an average of 290 cm/y (114 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Ci-jëva Maquis covers an area of nearly 9485 km2 (5894 mi2), and an average elevation of 2493 m (8179 ft) above sea level.

The Ci-jëva Maquis is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
