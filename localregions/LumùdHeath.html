The Lumùd Heath (/lum/ /ʊd/ Guildedgrass [lawn] [heavy; serious]) is a subtropical heath on the coast of the Foxvale region. Lumùd Heath is a relatively typical heath for Foxvale, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Lumùd Heath has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 5°C (41°F). The Lumùd Heath receives an average of 77 cm/y (30 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Lumùd Heath covers an area of nearly 8521 km2 (5295 mi2), and an average elevation of 4229 m (13874 ft) above sea level.
