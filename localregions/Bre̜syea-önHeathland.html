The Bre̜syea-ön Heathland (/bre̜ˈsjea/ /o˞n/ Elbow [skin] [angle]) is a subtropical heathland on the coast of the Foxvale region. The Bre̜syea-ön Heathland is well known for being largely barren of plant life.

Climate

The Bre̜syea-ön Heathland has a yearly average temperature of 12°C (53°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 1°C (33°F). The Bre̜syea-ön Heathland receives an average of 296 cm/y (116 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Bre̜syea-ön Heathland covers an area of nearly 10891 km2 (6768 mi2), and an average elevation of 5047 m (16558 ft) above sea level.
