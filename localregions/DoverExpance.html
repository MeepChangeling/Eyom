The Dover Expance (/ˈdoʊvər/ expance Dover [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Ancestor's Range region. Dover Expance is a relativly typical expanse for Ancestor's Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Dover Expance has a yearly average tempature of 24°C (75°F), with its average tempature durring the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average tempature durring the winter being a cool 20°C (68°F). The Dover Expance recieves an average of 67 cm/y (26 in/y) of percipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain durring the spring. The Dover Expance covers an area of nearly 11082 km2 (6887 mi2), and an average elevation of 9977 m (-10216 ft) above sealevel.

Local Religion

The Dover Expance is overseen by Potentate Qikzotz Xandra Androdita, a god serving within The Apprenticeship, the region's pricipal religion. Aproximently 31% of the population self-report as devout. The Dover Expance is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevent to their portfolio.

Dover Expance has its economic roots in Ancestor's Range's export of Stone, but has developed its own musical instruments industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.
