The Dono Mâhi Holt (/ˈdono/ /ˈmɑhi/ Wakemarch [funeral] [match (game); contest]) is a subtropical holt on the coast of the Endless Peaks region. Dono Mâhi Holt is a relatively typical holt for Endless Peaks, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Dono Mâhi Holt has a yearly average temperature of 17°C (62°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 9°C (48°F). The Dono Mâhi Holt receives an average of 128 cm/y (50 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Dono Mâhi Holt covers an area of nearly 13354 km2 (8299 mi2), and an average elevation of 8588 m (-14773 ft) above sea level.
