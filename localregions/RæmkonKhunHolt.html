The Ræmkon Khun Holt (/ˈræmkon/ /xun/ Strangemud [funny] [mud; swamp; cement]) is a subtropical holt on the coast of the Westlake region. Ræmkon Khun Holt is a relatively typical holt for Westlake, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Ræmkon Khun Holt has a yearly average temperature of 28°C (82°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 32°C (89°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 24°C (75°F). The Ræmkon Khun Holt receives an average of 143 cm/y (56 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Ræmkon Khun Holt covers an area of nearly 11622 km2 (7223 mi2), and an average elevation of 2472 m (8110 ft) above sea level.

The Ræmkon Khun Holt is overseen by Potentate Gruyja Despont Waliar, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 22% of the population self-report as devout. The Ræmkon Khun Holt is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Ræmkon Khun Holt has its economic roots in Westlake's export of Hemp, but has developed its own weapons industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.
