The Setkraqusu Mama Maquis (/ˈqusu ˈmama/ Setkra's Tower [Proper Noun (name of person)] [tower]) is a subtropical maquis on the coast of the Kindling Hills region. Setkraqusu Mama Maquis is a relatively typical maquis for Kindling Hills, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Setkraqusu Mama Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 21°C (69°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 20°C (68°F). The Setkraqusu Mama Maquis receives an average of 134 cm/y (52 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Setkraqusu Mama Maquis covers an area of nearly 11708 km2 (7276 mi2), and an average elevation of 5608 m (18398 ft) above sea level.
