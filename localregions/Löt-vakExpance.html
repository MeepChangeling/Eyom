The Löt-vak Expance (/lo˞t/ /vak/ Tigerelf [tiger] [elf]) is a subtropical expanse within the wetland of the Kiteshield Penencila region. Löt-vak Expance is a relativly typical expanse for Kiteshield Penencila, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Löt-vak Expance has a yearly average tempature of 12°C (53°F), with its average tempature durring the summer being a pleasent 22°C (71°F) and its average tempature durring the winter being a cold 2°C (35°F). The Löt-vak Expance recieves an average of 208 cm/y (81 in/y) of percipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow durring the pleasently short winter months. The Löt-vak Expance covers an area of nearly 12419 km2 (7718 mi2), and an average elevation of 4771 m (15652 ft) above sealevel.

In respect to this universal cultural element, we have split racial classification by culture while keeping the name of each race for our own purposes. Should you see the same race listed more than once in the following section, this is to desinate a major cultural division within a race.

The Löt-vak Expance is's peoples are culturaly Gnollish, but have their own local customs and traditions. Their shared vaules and traditions include remembrance of the past and memorializing history and vengeance and execution of just vendettas, as well as a unique comedy festival and a comedy festival.

Gnoll

The Gnoll subculture native to the Löt-vak Expance are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Gnoll culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of impractical or elaborate role-based clothes and base their sence of individual idenity on ethnic membership. They also place greater cultural emphasus on seeking fortune in new places or new roles and personal sacrifice for one’s causes or purposes.

Goblin

The Goblin subculture native to the Löt-vak Expance are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Gnoll culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of patterned hair shaving or depilation and base their sence of individual idenity on voluntary mutual-assistance brotherhoods. They also place greater cultural emphasus on purging evil and expelling the wicked and justice and fairness between people.

Sylvian

The Sylvian subculture native to the Löt-vak Expance are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Gnoll culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of intricate hair styles or braiding and base their sence of individual idenity on far-flung clans of affiliated families. They also place greater cultural emphasus on purging evil and expelling the wicked and courage and valiance in danger.

Elf

The Elf subculture native to the Löt-vak Expance are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Gnoll culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of role or class-specific clothing items and base their sence of individual idenity on dynastic lineages with cadet branches. They also place greater cultural emphasus on aesthetic beauty in material goods and architecture and purging evil and expelling the wicked.

Grey Elf

The Grey Elf subculture native to the Löt-vak Expance are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Gnoll culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of piercings, whether minor or elaborate and base their sence of individual idenity on extended family out to cousins and like kin. They also place greater cultural emphasus on building things in service of their posterity and piety and devotion to the gods.

Local Religion

The Löt-vak Expance is overseen by Potentate Gatuna Sardwalhmær Pwyll, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's pricipal religion. Aproximently 47% of the population self-report as devout. The Löt-vak Expance is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevent to their portfolio.

Settlments and Economy

Löt-vak Expance is predominantly economicly reiant on Kiteshield Penencila's export of Oil, and has no outstanding industries byond what is required for its role in that process.

The Löt-vak Expance has an Imperial Development Index of 12. For the refrence of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 12 means there are 12 locations within the Löt-vak Expance which the Empire can securly and regurly transport matrials and personell..

As a courticy for scholars and agents, the Löt-vak Expance is rated M1 by Eyoms banks. Buisness ventures within the Löt-vak Expance are not expected to return value in yearly terms.
