The Ipahenu-atlärd Holt (/aˈtlɑrd/ Ipahenu's Creamery [Proper Noun (name of person)] [butter]) is a subtropical holt on the coast of the Highpeaks region. Ipahenu-atlärd Holt is a relatively typical holt for Highpeaks, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Ipahenu-atlärd Holt has a yearly average temperature of 34°C (93°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 38°C (100°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 30°C (86°F). The Ipahenu-atlärd Holt receives an average of 66 cm/y (25 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Ipahenu-atlärd Holt covers an area of nearly 13800 km2 (8576 mi2), and an average elevation of 23272 m (-9547 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Ipahenu-atlärd Holt is overseen by Potentate Civa Paraine Geraintho, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 36% of the population self-report as devout. The Ipahenu-atlärd Holt is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Ipahenu-atlärd Holt is a negative return economy zone at this time. It has an abundence of Elemental Crystals, though infrastructure to this region of Highpeaks is not developed enough to support any major local industry.
