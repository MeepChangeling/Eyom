The Khrutajī Heathland (/ˈaʤi/ Khrut's World [Proper Noun (name of person)] [world; planet; universe]) is a subtropical heathland within the wetland of the Illa's Panhandle region. The Khrutajī Heathland is well known for its thriving abundance of plant life.

Climate

The Khrutajī Heathland has a yearly average temperature of 18°C (64°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a cool 19°C (66°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 18°C (64°F). The Khrutajī Heathland receives an average of 214 cm/y (84 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Khrutajī Heathland covers an area of nearly 9287 km2 (5771 mi2), and an average elevation of 3369 m (11053 ft) above sea level.
