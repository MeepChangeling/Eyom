The Pía-píêbano Maquis (/p˔a/ /p˔ɘˈbano/ Butterfly [social] [sailor]) is a temperate maquis on the coast of the Cutmountains region. Pía-píêbano Maquis is a relatively typical maquis for Cutmountains, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Pía-píêbano Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 22°C (71°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 17°C (62°F). The Pía-píêbano Maquis receives an average of 69 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Pía-píêbano Maquis covers an area of nearly 8322 km2 (5172 mi2), and an average elevation of 5833 m (19137 ft) above sea level.

The Pía-píêbano Maquis is overseen by Potentate Aotl Pwydandronw Béibniu, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 36% of the population self-report as devout.
