The Oph Yovä Moor (/of/ /joˈvə/ Recyclebin [space (empty area); room (space)] [garbage; trash; rubbish]) is a subtropical moor on the coast of the Accute Bay region. The Oph Yovä Moor is well known for its thriving abundance of plant life.

Climate

The Oph Yovä Moor has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 18°C (64°F). The Oph Yovä Moor receives an average of 295 cm/y (116 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Oph Yovä Moor covers an area of nearly 10978 km2 (6822 mi2), and an average elevation of 3174 m (10413 ft) above sea level.
