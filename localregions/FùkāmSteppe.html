The Fùkām Steppe (/fʊ/ /cəˑm/ Barley [plant] [famous]) is a subtropical steppe on the coast of the Farmbasin region. Fùkām Steppe is a relatively typical steppe for Farmbasin, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Fùkām Steppe has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 5°C (41°F). The Fùkām Steppe receives an average of 147 cm/y (57 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Fùkām Steppe covers an area of nearly 13837 km2 (8599 mi2), and an average elevation of 3368 m (11049 ft) above sea level.
