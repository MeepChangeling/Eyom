The Qiyè-cagêg̈ê Brush (/ˈqijè/ /caˈgɘg̃ɘ/ Ordered Chaos [aesthetic] [pile; heap]) is a subtropical brush within the wetland of the Polymer Coast region. Qiyè-cagêg̈ê Brush is a relatively typical brush for Polymer Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Qiyè-cagêg̈ê Brush has a yearly average temperature of 15°C (59°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 22°C (71°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 9°C (48°F). The Qiyè-cagêg̈ê Brush receives an average of 208 cm/y (81 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Qiyè-cagêg̈ê Brush covers an area of nearly 3140 km2 (1951 mi2), and an average elevation of 1987 m (6519 ft) above sea level.
