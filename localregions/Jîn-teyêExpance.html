The Jîn-teyê Expance (/ˈtejɘ/ Jîn's Knowlage [Proper Noun (name of person)] [knowledge]) is a temperate expanse within the temperate rainforest of the Greatbay region. Jîn-teyê Expance is a relativly typical expanse for Greatbay, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Jîn-teyê Expance has a yearly average tempature of 20°C (68°F), with its average tempature durring the summer being a pleasent 25°C (77°F) and its average tempature durring the winter being a cool 16°C (60°F). The Jîn-teyê Expance recieves an average of 293 cm/y (115 in/y) of percipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain durring the summer. The Jîn-teyê Expance covers an area of nearly 25252 km2 (15694 mi2), and an average elevation of 1354 m (4442 ft) above sealevel.

The Jîn-teyê Expance is's peoples are culturaly Sylvanian, but have their own local customs and traditions. Their shared vaules and traditions include submission to the collective will or culture and honesty and truthfulness in speech, as well as a unique sun festival and a science festival.

Sylvin

The Sylvin subculture native to the Jîn-teyê Expance are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Sylvin culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of impractical or elaborate role-based clothes and base their sence of individual idenity on extended family out to cousins and like kin. They also place greater cultural emphasus on membership in an elite bloodline or caste and leadership and charisma in the group.

Local Religion

The Jîn-teyê Expance is overseen by Potentate Zaune Damona Tadwulf, a god serving within The Council of Twelve, the region's pricipal religion. Aproximently 25% of the population self-report as devout. The Jîn-teyê Expance is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevent to their portfolio.

Jîn-teyê Expance is noteworthy for its economic independence from Greatbay's export of Luxury Goods. Jîn-teyê Expance is particularly renound for its monster hunting industry. It is also has a noteworthy engines industry.
