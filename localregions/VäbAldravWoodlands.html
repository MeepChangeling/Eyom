The Väb Aldrav Woodlands (/alˈdrav/ Väb the Cold's [Proper Noun (name of person)] [ice]) are a group of subtropical woodland on the coast of the Marshreach region. Väb Aldrav Woodlands are a relatively typical woodland for Marshreach, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Väb Aldrav Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 28°C (82°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 32°C (89°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 25°C (77°F). The Väb Aldrav Woodlands receives an average of 286 cm/y (112 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Väb Aldrav Woodlands covers an area of nearly 22204 km2 (13799 mi2), and an average elevation of 3335 m (10941 ft) above sea level.

The Väb Aldrav Woodlands are's peoples are culturally Tauric, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include industry and the ability to work tirelessly and vengeance and execution of just vendettas, as well as a unique fire festival and a relic festival.

Local Religion

The Väb Aldrav Woodlands are overseen by Potentate Godione Æþelecatea Goina, a god serving within The Council of Twelve, the region's principal religion. Approximately 55% of the population self-report as devout. The Väb Aldrav Woodlands is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.
