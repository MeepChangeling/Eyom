Climate

The Zëchy Awën Meadows has a yearly average temperature of 14°C (57°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 5°C (41°F). The Zëchy Awën Meadows receives an average of 213 cm/y (83 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Zëchy Awën Meadows covers an area of nearly 11869 km2 (7376 mi2), and an average elevation of 4054 m (13300 ft) above sea level.
