The Khěn-kæq Heathland (/xɛn/ /kæq/ Drybrush [camel] [lawn]) is a subtropical heathland on the coast of the Anvil Badlands region. The Khěn-kæq Heathland is well known for being home to remarkably few animals.

Climate

The Khěn-kæq Heathland has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Khěn-kæq Heathland receives an average of 209 cm/y (82 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Khěn-kæq Heathland covers an area of nearly 10231 km2 (6358 mi2), and an average elevation of 5558 m (18234 ft) above sea level.
