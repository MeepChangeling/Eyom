Climate

The Mêlëmt-dèhi Grasslands has a yearly average temperature of 18°C (64°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 11°C (51°F). The Mêlëmt-dèhi Grasslands receives an average of 78 cm/y (30 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Mêlëmt-dèhi Grasslands covers an area of nearly 14818 km2 (9209 mi2), and an average elevation of 5745 m (18848 ft) above sea level.
