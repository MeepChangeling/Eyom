The Pef-ijusdusazd Fields (/pef/ /iˈjusduˌsazd/ Beatdown [classic] [treatment]) are a group of subtropical fields on the coast of the Lower Warding Range region. Pef-ijusdusazd Fields are a relatively typical fields for Lower Warding Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Pef-ijusdusazd Fields has a yearly average temperature of 34°C (93°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a blistering 38°C (100°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a hot 31°C (87°F). The Pef-ijusdusazd Fields receives an average of 67 cm/y (26 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Pef-ijusdusazd Fields covers an area of nearly 13062 km2 (8118 mi2), and an average elevation of 10723 m (-7769 ft) above sea level.
