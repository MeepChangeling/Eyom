The Vra-fyoz Grasslands (/vra/ /fjoz/ Pride [gender] [coral]) are a group of temperate grassland within the temperate rainforest of the Gnashed Coast region. The Vra-fyoz Grasslands are the political heartlands of the City-state of Tano.

Climate

The Vra-fyoz Grasslands has a yearly average temperature of 10°C (50°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 23°C (73°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -3°C (27°F). The Vra-fyoz Grasslands receives an average of 283 cm/y (111 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Vra-fyoz Grasslands covers an area of nearly 16894 km2 (10499 mi2), and an average elevation of 2085 m (6840 ft) above sea level.
