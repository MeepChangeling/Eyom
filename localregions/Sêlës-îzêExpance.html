The Sêlës-îzê Expance (/ˈɪzɘ/ Sêlës' Countertop [Proper Noun (name of person)] [counter (flat; elevated surface)]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Greatbay region. Sêlës-îzê Expance is a relativly typical expanse for Greatbay, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Sêlës-îzê Expance has a yearly average tempature of 18°C (64°F), with its average tempature durring the summer being a pleasent 22°C (71°F) and its average tempature durring the winter being a cool 14°C (57°F). The Sêlës-îzê Expance recieves an average of 283 cm/y (111 in/y) of percipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain durring the summer. The Sêlës-îzê Expance covers an area of nearly 18300 km2 (11373 mi2), and an average elevation of 3308 m (10853 ft) above sealevel.

People and Culture

In defrence to the traditions of Eyomic peoples, we have forgon town-specific racial cencus, howeaver, as our culture lacks a taboo for classification of peoples by their biology and such infomration is highly important for our medical practices amongst other institutions, we have stated the racial demographics of this region. We do not do so to sew the seeds of disunity. We do not do so to belittle or bemoan any group. Such issues are why we use the term "peoples" rather than races, cultures, or species.

In respect to this universal cultural element, we have split racial classification by culture while keeping the name of each race for our own purposes. Should you see the same race listed more than once in the following section, this is to desinate a major cultural division within a race.

The Sêlës-îzê Expance is's peoples are culturaly Sylvanian, but have their own local customs and traditions. Their shared vaules and traditions include individual rights and freedom of action and sexual license and wantonness, as well as a unique history festival and a history festival.

Sylvin

The Sylvin subculture native to the Sêlës-îzê Expance are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Sylvin culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of impractical or elaborate role-based clothes and base their sence of individual idenity on ethnic membership. They also place greater cultural emphasus on submission to the collective will or culture and pacifism and peaceful resolution of problems.

Goblin

The Goblin subculture native to the Sêlës-îzê Expance are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Sylvin culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of patterned hair shaving or depilation and base their sence of individual idenity on hereditary loyalty to a political group. They also place greater cultural emphasus on industry and the ability to work tirelessly and membership in an elite bloodline or caste.

Kitsune

The Kitsune subculture native to the Sêlës-îzê Expance are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Sylvin culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of patterned hair shaving or depilation and base their sence of individual idenity on warbands or civic military service groups. They also place greater cultural emphasus on cunning and the ability to trick others and personal development and limit-pushing.

They also place greater cultural emphasus on honesty and truthfulness in speech and exploring the unknown and discovering secrets.

Kivu

The Kivu subculture native to the Sêlës-îzê Expance are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Sylvin culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of worn weapons, tools or trade implements and base their sence of individual idenity on identity based on their local community. The Tanuki subculture native to the Sêlës-îzê Expance are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Sylvin culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of piercings, whether minor or elaborate and base their sence of individual idenity on trade guilds specific to particular roles. Local Religion

The Sêlës-îzê Expance is overseen by Potentate Aeyar Eógaina Eoghanonwy, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's pricipal religion. Aproximently 25% of the population self-report as devout. The Sêlës-îzê Expance is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevent to their portfolio.

Settlments and Economy

Sêlës-îzê Expance is a negative return economy zone at this time. It has an abundence of Luxury Goods, though infrastructure to this region of Greatbay is not developed enough to support any major local industry.

The Sêlës-îzê Expance has an Imperial Development Index of 5. For the refrence of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 5 means there are 5 locations within the Sêlës-îzê Expance which the Empire can securly and regurly transport matrials and personell..
