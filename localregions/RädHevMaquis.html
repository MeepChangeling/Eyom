The Räd Hev Maquis (/rəd/ /hev/ Sharpspire [keen; eager] [architecture]) is a subtropical maquis on the coast of the Lower Warding Range region. Räd Hev Maquis is a relatively typical maquis for Lower Warding Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Räd Hev Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 29°C (84°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 33°C (91°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a warm 26°C (78°F). The Räd Hev Maquis receives an average of 65 cm/y (25 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Räd Hev Maquis covers an area of nearly 11594 km2 (7205 mi2), and an average elevation of 10674 m (-7929 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Räd Hev Maquis is overseen by Potentate Qhuzphy Conthos Orphen, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 13% of the population self-report as devout. The Räd Hev Maquis is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Räd Hev Maquis is a negative return economy zone at this time. It has an abundence of Undead, though infrastructure to this region of Lower Warding Range is not developed enough to support any major local industry.

The Räd Hev Maquis has an Imperial Development Index of 2. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 2 means there are 2 locations within the Räd Hev Maquis which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
