The Chrokigimave Maquis (/giˈmave/ Chroki's Mill [Proper Noun (name of person)] [dust; flour]) is a temperate maquis on the coast of the Illa's Panhandle region. Chrokigimave Maquis is a relatively typical maquis for Illa's Panhandle, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Chrokigimave Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 22°C (71°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 19°C (66°F). The Chrokigimave Maquis receives an average of 135 cm/y (53 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Chrokigimave Maquis covers an area of nearly 18285 km2 (11364 mi2), and an average elevation of 5950 m (19520 ft) above sea level.

The Chrokigimave Maquis is home to 541763 people. They break down into the following demographics: 67% Iron Elves, 18% Goblins, 6% Tanuki, 5% Kobold, 2% Kivu, and ~2% Other.

The Chrokigimave Maquis is's peoples are culturally Gnollish, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include seeking fortune in new places or new roles and exploring the unknown and discovering secrets, as well as a unique storytelling festival and a food festival.

While they follow the broader tennents of Iron Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of color choices with social meaning to them and base their sence of individual idenity on professional guild or trade-specific group. They also place greater cultural emphasus on industry and the ability to work tirelessly and membership in an elite bloodline or caste.

They also place greater cultural emphasus on exploring the unknown and discovering secrets and eloquence and social expertise.

Local Religion

The Chrokigimave Maquis is overseen by Potentate Shogius Hectoros Alespoleme, a god serving within The Council of Twelve, the region's principal religion. Approximately 21% of the population self-report as devout. The Chrokigimave Maquis is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Settlements and Economy

Chrokigimave Maquis has its economic roots in Illa's Panhandle's export of Rubber, but has developed its own education industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.

The Chrokigimave Maquis has an Imperial Development Index of 17. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 17 means there are 17 locations within the Chrokigimave Maquis which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
