The Qûyæ Amu Expance (/ˈqʊjæ/ /ˈamu/ Goblinvale [boot] [lamb; virgin]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Darkrass Planes region. Qûyæ Amu Expance is a relativly typical expanse for Darkrass Planes, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Qûyæ Amu Expance has a yearly average tempature of 17°C (62°F), with its average tempature durring the summer being a pleasent 25°C (77°F) and its average tempature durring the winter being a cold 9°C (48°F). The Qûyæ Amu Expance recieves an average of 133 cm/y (52 in/y) of percipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain durring the spring. The Qûyæ Amu Expance covers an area of nearly 14135 km2 (8784 mi2), and an average elevation of 5283 m (17332 ft) above sealevel.

The Qûyæ Amu Expance is overseen by Potentate Thugyja Akanawl Neofsta, a god serving within Tol's Children, the region's pricipal religion. Aproximently 29% of the population self-report as devout. The Qûyæ Amu Expance is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevent to their portfolio.
