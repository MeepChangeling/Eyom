The Sprupozage Moor (/ˈzage/ Sprupo's [Proper Noun (name of person)] [sea; ocean]) is a subtropical moor on the coast of the Iron Coast region. Sprupozage Moor is a relatively typical moor for Iron Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Sprupozage Moor has a yearly average temperature of 22°C (71°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 20°C (68°F). The Sprupozage Moor receives an average of 295 cm/y (116 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Sprupozage Moor covers an area of nearly 20717 km2 (12875 mi2), and an average elevation of 4675 m (15337 ft) above sea level.
