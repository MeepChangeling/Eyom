Climate

The Tas Yǟz Woods has a yearly average temperature of 7°C (44°F), with its average temperature during the summer being an icy 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold -11°C (13°F). The Tas Yǟz Woods receives an average of 208 cm/y (81 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Tas Yǟz Woods covers an area of nearly 11100 km2 (6898 mi2), and an average elevation of 3382 m (11095 ft) above sea level.
