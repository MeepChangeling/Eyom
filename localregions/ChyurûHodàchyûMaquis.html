The Chyurû Hodàchyû Maquis (/hoˈdəcʊ/ Chyurûsmill [Proper Noun (name of person)] [dust; flour]) is a subtropical maquis on the coast of the Wyldrange region. Chyurû Hodàchyû Maquis is a relatively typical maquis for Wyldrange, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Chyurû Hodàchyû Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 21°C (69°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 13°C (55°F). The Chyurû Hodàchyû Maquis receives an average of 67 cm/y (26 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Chyurû Hodàchyû Maquis covers an area of nearly 12286 km2 (7635 mi2), and an average elevation of 2619 m (8592 ft) above sea level.

The Chyurû Hodàchyû Maquis is overseen by Potentate Kenrena Apoine Clídnawn, a god serving within The Council of Twelve, the region's principal religion. Approximately 49% of the population self-report as devout. The Chyurû Hodàchyû Maquis is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.
