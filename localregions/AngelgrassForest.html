The Angelgrass Forest (angelgrass /ˈfɔrəst/ Angelgrass [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical forest on the coast of the Valley of Wheat region. The Angelgrass Forest is well known for its thriving abundance of plant life.

Climate

The Angelgrass Forest has a yearly average temperature of 26°C (78°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 30°C (86°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Angelgrass Forest receives an average of 284 cm/y (111 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Angelgrass Forest covers an area of nearly 24921 km2 (15488 mi2), and an average elevation of 3150 m (10334 ft) above sea level.

The Angelgrass Forest is home to 866957 people. They break down into the following demographics: 68% Dwarves, 11% Wareneese, 6% Kitsune, 8% Imperial, 5% Imperial, and ~2% Other.

The Angelgrass Forest is's peoples are culturally Dwarven, but have their local customs and traditions. Their shared values and traditions include restoring some real or imagined glorious past and hardiness and endurance before woes, as well as a unique spring festival and a history festival.

Dwarf

The Dwarf subculture native to the Angelgrass Forest are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Dwarf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of impractical or elaborate role-based clothes and base their sence of individual idenity on lineages based on ancient hero-ancestors. They also place greater cultural emphasus on vengeance and execution of just vendettas and education and knowledge-seeking.

Rattu

The Rattu subculture native to the Angelgrass Forest are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Dwarf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of tattoos of some cultural significance and base their sence of individual idenity on hereditary loyalty to a political group. They also place greater cultural emphasus on pacifism and peaceful resolution of problems and industry and the ability to work tirelessly.

Kitsune

The Kitsune subculture native to the Angelgrass Forest are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Dwarf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of significant scent or perfume uses and base their sence of individual idenity on extended family out to cousins and like kin. They also place greater cultural emphasus on industry and the ability to work tirelessly and filial devotion to family and parents.

Imperial

The Imperial subculture native to the Angelgrass Forest are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Dwarf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of color choices with social meaning to them and base their sence of individual idenity on extended family out to cousins and like kin. They also place greater cultural emphasus on unity and elimination of group differences and personal development and limit-pushing.

Quenn

The Quenn subculture native to the Angelgrass Forest are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Dwarf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of color choices with social meaning to them and base their sence of individual idenity on voluntary mutual-assistance brotherhoods. They also place greater cultural emphasus on submission to the collective will or culture and magical prowess and occult ability.

Local Religion

The Angelgrass Forest is overseen by Potentate Aotl Despontora Goroditan, a god serving within The Amazons, the region's principal religion. Approximately 10% of the population self-report as devout. The Angelgrass Forest is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Angelgrass Forest is predominantly economicly reiant on Valley of Wheat's export of Grain, and has no outstanding industries byond what is required for its role in that process.

The Angelgrass Forest has an Imperial Development Index of 12. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 12 means there are 12 locations within the Angelgrass Forest which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..

As a courtesy for scholars and agents, the Angelgrass Forest is rated M1 by Eyom’s banks. Business ventures within the Angelgrass Forest are not expected to return value in yearly terms.
