The Nurebulo Shrublands (/ˈnure/ /ˈbulo/ Lightmark [tattoo] [soft; smooth; quiet; gentle; sleek]) are a group of subtropical shrubland within the temperate rainforest of the Glowing Sands region. Nurebulo Shrublands are a relatively typical shrubland for Glowing Sands, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Nurebulo Shrublands has a yearly average temperature of 16°C (60°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a cool 19°C (66°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 14°C (57°F). The Nurebulo Shrublands receives an average of 293 cm/y (115 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Nurebulo Shrublands covers an area of nearly 7121 km2 (4425 mi2), and an average elevation of 1124 m (3687 ft) above sea level.

The Nurebulo Shrublands are overseen by Potentate Noktrix Goroditana Aiardwintha, a god serving within Tol's Children, the region's principal religion. Approximately 45% of the population self-report as devout. The Nurebulo Shrublands is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevant to their portfolio.

Nurebulo Shrublands has its economic roots in Glowing Sands's export of Glass, but has developed its own textiles industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.
