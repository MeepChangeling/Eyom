The Vêvê-thëfe Forest (/ˈvɘvɘ/ /ˈʃëfe/ Thousand Tunnels [long] [basement; cellar]) is a subtropical forest within the temperate deciduous forest of the Southlake region. Vêvê-thëfe Forest is a relatively typical forest for Southlake, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Vêvê-thëfe Forest has a yearly average temperature of 23°C (73°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 18°C (64°F). The Vêvê-thëfe Forest receives an average of 161 cm/y (63 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the fall. The Vêvê-thëfe Forest covers an area of nearly 9654 km2 (6000 mi2), and an average elevation of 481 m (1578 ft) above sea level.
