The Kāvge Woodlands (/kəˑv/ /ge/ University [school; academy] [ready]) are a group of subtropical woodland within the temperate rainforest of the Salt Warrens region. The Kāvge Woodlands are the birthplace of the Labdsism, the largetst religion in Eyom, followed by countless Rattu, Kitsune, Tanuki, Taurs, and Gnolls.
