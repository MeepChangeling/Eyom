The Ngelä-nruq Expance (/nruq/ Ngelä's Yacht [Proper Noun (name of person)] [boat; yacht]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Lower Warding Range region. Ngelä-nruq Expance is a relativly typical expanse for Lower Warding Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Ngelä-nruq Expance has a yearly average tempature of 32°C (89°F), with its average tempature durring the summer being a blistering 36°C (96°F) and its average tempature durring the winter being a warm 29°C (84°F). The Ngelä-nruq Expance recieves an average of 66 cm/y (25 in/y) of percipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain durring the spring. The Ngelä-nruq Expance covers an area of nearly 10565 km2 (6566 mi2), and an average elevation of 17357 m (13995 ft) above sealevel.

People and Culture

Imperial Warning: The following section of this document deals with biological race. In defrence to the traditions of Eyomic peoples, we have forgon town-specific racial cencus, howeaver, as our culture lacks a taboo for classification of peoples by their biology and such infomration is highly important for our medical practices amongst other institutions, we have stated the racial demographics of this region. We do not do so to sew the seeds of disunity. We do not do so to belittle or bemoan any group. Such issues are why we use the term "peoples" rather than races, cultures, or species.

In respect to this universal cultural element, we have split racial classification by culture while keeping the name of each race for our own purposes. Should you see the same race listed more than once in the following section, this is to desinate a major cultural division within a race.

The Ngelä-nruq Expance is's peoples are culturaly Z̚oman, but have their own local customs and traditions. Their shared vaules and traditions include justice and fairness between people and cunning and the ability to trick others, as well as a unique religious festival and a sun festival.

Elf

The Elf subculture native to the Ngelä-nruq Expance are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of painted skin markings that sometimes change and base their sence of individual idenity on numerous castes of hereditary workers. They also place greater cultural emphasus on eloquence and social expertise and unity and elimination of group differences.

Hobgoblin

The Hobgoblin subculture native to the Ngelä-nruq Expance are noticably same height and weight as the neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of socially-meaningful animal motif items and base their sence of individual idenity on lineages based on ancient hero-ancestors. They also place greater cultural emphasus on discipline and obedience to the law and faithfulness towards one’s chosen friends.

Tauran

The Tauran subculture native to the Ngelä-nruq Expance are noticably much bigger and bulkier than neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of tattoos of some cultural significance and base their sence of individual idenity on lineages based on ancient hero-ancestors. They also place greater cultural emphasus on subtlety and indirectness of action and cunning and the ability to trick others.

Imperial

The Imperial subculture native to the Ngelä-nruq Expance are noticably smaller and slighter than their neighbors. While they follow the broader tennents of Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of significant scent or perfume uses and base their sence of individual idenity on patron-client relationships with major figures. They also place greater cultural emphasus on social progress toward some eventual utopia and humanistic reason and “rational” religion.

Imperial

The Imperial subculture native to the Ngelä-nruq Expance are noticably either short and stocky or tall and slender. While they follow the broader tennents of Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of impractical or elaborate role-based clothes and base their sence of individual idenity on regional and province-based identity. They also place greater cultural emphasus on remembrance of the past and memorializing history and loyalty to one’s friends, family, and own.

Local Religion

The Ngelä-nruq Expance is overseen by Potentate Uneas Orphros Simonawl, a god serving within The Apprenticeship, the region's pricipal religion. Aproximently 11% of the population self-report as devout. The Ngelä-nruq Expance is concidered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlments and Economy

Ngelä-nruq Expance is a negative return economy zone at this time. It has an abundence of Undead, though infrastructure to this region of Lower Warding Range is not developed enough to support any major local industry.

The Ngelä-nruq Expance has an Imperial Development Index of 2. For the refrence of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 2 means there are 2 locations within the Ngelä-nruq Expance which the Empire can securly and regurly transport matrials and personell..
