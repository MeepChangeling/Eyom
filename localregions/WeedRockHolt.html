The Weed Rock Holt (/wid/ /rɑk/ /hoʊlt/ Weed Rock [Translation Unavailable]) is a subtropical holt on the coast of the Palebay region. Weed Rock Holt is a relatively typical holt for Palebay, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Weed Rock Holt has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 20°C (68°F). The Weed Rock Holt receives an average of 69 cm/y (27 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Weed Rock Holt covers an area of nearly 7774 km2 (4831 mi2), and an average elevation of 7770 m (-17457 ft) above sea level.

The Weed Rock Holt is overseen by Potentate Qhuzphy Æðelreya Gros, a god serving within Therilism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 30% of the population self-report as devout. The Weed Rock Holt is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Weed Rock Holt is predominantly economicly reiant on Palebay's export of Bauxite, and has no outstanding industries byond what is required for its role in that process.
