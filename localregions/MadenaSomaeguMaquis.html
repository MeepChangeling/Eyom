The Madena Somaegu Maquis (/maˈdena ˈsoma/ /ˈegu/ Pheonixblood [potion] [live]) is a subtropical maquis within the wetland of the Shattered Peninsula region. Madena Somaegu Maquis is a relatively typical maquis for Shattered Peninsula, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Madena Somaegu Maquis has a yearly average temperature of 18°C (64°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 25°C (77°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 11°C (51°F). The Madena Somaegu Maquis receives an average of 220 cm/y (86 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Madena Somaegu Maquis covers an area of nearly 8077 km2 (5019 mi2), and an average elevation of 1214 m (3982 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Madena Somaegu Maquis is overseen by Potentate Denera Eoghald Korairedous, a god serving within The Apprenticeship, the region's principal religion. Approximately 35% of the population self-report as devout. The Madena Somaegu Maquis is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Madena Somaegu Maquis has its economic roots in Shattered Peninsula's export of Metals, but has developed its own archeology industry, which has a marked impact on the local regional economy.

The Madena Somaegu Maquis has an Imperial Development Index of 21. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 21 means there are 21 locations within the Madena Somaegu Maquis which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..
