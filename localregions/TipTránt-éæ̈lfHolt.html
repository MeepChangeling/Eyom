The Tip Tránt-éæ̈lf Holt has a yearly average temperature of 27°C (80°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 30°C (86°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 23°C (73°F). The Tip Tránt-éæ̈lf Holt covers an area of nearly 16204 km2 (10070 mi2), and an average elevation of 4818 m (15807 ft) above sea level.
