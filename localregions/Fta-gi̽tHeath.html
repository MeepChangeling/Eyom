The Fta-gi̽t Heath (/fta/ /gi̽t/ Warmingpassion [oven] [emotion; passion]) is a subtropical heath within the tropical rainforest of the Lin Forest region. Fta-gi̽t Heath is a relatively typical heath for Lin Forest, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Fta-gi̽t Heath has a yearly average temperature of 17°C (62°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a pleasant 24°C (75°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 9°C (48°F). The Fta-gi̽t Heath receives an average of 303 cm/y (119 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Fta-gi̽t Heath covers an area of nearly 16196 km2 (10065 mi2), and an average elevation of 2299 m (7542 ft) above sea level.
