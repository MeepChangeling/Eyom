The Mop Zim Woodlands (/mop/ /zim/ Comic Couch [comic] [lounge; couch; sofa]) are a group of subtropical woodland on the coast of the Anvil Badlands region. Mop Zim Woodlands are a relatively typical woodland for Anvil Badlands, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Mop Zim Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 25°C (77°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 28°C (82°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Mop Zim Woodlands receives an average of 75 cm/y (29 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Mop Zim Woodlands covers an area of nearly 7453 km2 (4632 mi2), and an average elevation of 8045 m (-16555 ft) above sea level.
