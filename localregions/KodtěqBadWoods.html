The Kodtěq Bad Woods (/ˈɨgbo/ /bad/ Bonzi [purple] [virus; germ]) are a group of subtropical woods within the wetland of the Flanged Coast region. Kodtěq Bad Woods are a relatively typical woods for Flanged Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Kodtěq Bad Woods has a yearly average temperature of 26°C (78°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a hot 31°C (87°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a pleasant 21°C (69°F). The Kodtěq Bad Woods receives an average of 220 cm/y (86 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the summer. The Kodtěq Bad Woods covers an area of nearly 17961 km2 (11162 mi2), and an average elevation of 2558 m (8392 ft) above sea level.

Local Religion

The Kodtěq Bad Woods are overseen by Potentate Alotl Simonchos Coront, a god serving within Labdsism, the region's principal religion. Approximately 26% of the population self-report as devout. The Kodtěq Bad Woods is considered underserved by the divines despite shrines in all settled areas.

Settlements and Economy

Kodtěq Bad Woods is predominantly economicly reiant on Flanged Coast's export of Fish, and has no outstanding industries byond what is required for its role in that process.

The Kodtěq Bad Woods has an Imperial Development Index of 7. For the reference of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 7 means there are 7 locations within the Kodtěq Bad Woods which the Empire can securely and regularly transport materials and personnel..

As a courtesy for scholars and agents, the Kodtěq Bad Woods is rated M1 by Eyom’s banks. Business ventures within the Kodtěq Bad Woods are not expected to return value in yearly terms.
