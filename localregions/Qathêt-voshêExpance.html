The Qathêt-voshê Expance (/ˈvoʃɘ/ Qathêt's Vace [Proper Noun (name of person)] [vase; urn]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Upper Warding Range region. Qathêt-voshê Expance is a relativly typical expanse for Upper Warding Range, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Qathêt-voshê Expance has a yearly average tempature of 22°C (71°F), with its average tempature durring the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average tempature durring the winter being a cool 17°C (62°F). The Qathêt-voshê Expance recieves an average of 68 cm/y (26 in/y) of percipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain durring the spring. The Qathêt-voshê Expance covers an area of nearly 12067 km2 (7499 mi2), and an average elevation of 8535 m (-14947 ft) above sealevel.

The Qathêt-voshê Expance is's peoples are culturaly Wood Elven, but have their own local customs and traditions. Their shared vaules and traditions include eloquence and social expertise and beauty and seductive charm, as well as a unique harvest festival and a spring festival.

While they follow the broader tennents of Wood Elf culture, they have diffrenciated themselves by adopting a tradition of piercings, whether minor or elaborate and base their sence of individual idenity on regional and province-based identity. They also place greater cultural emphasus on ethnic purity of blood and culture and membership in an elite bloodline or caste.

Local Religion

The Qathêt-voshê Expance is overseen by Potentate Xerena Hyacineus Idalaukos, a god serving within Way of Ashlin, the region's pricipal religion. Aproximently 49% of the population self-report as devout. The Qathêt-voshê Expance is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevent to their portfolio.

Settlments and Economy

Qathêt-voshê Expance is predominantly economicly reiant on Upper Warding Range's export of Arcana, and has no outstanding industries byond what is required for its role in that process.

The Qathêt-voshê Expance has an Imperial Development Index of 10. For the refrence of Eyom's native peoples, a IDI of 10 means there are 10 locations within the Qathêt-voshê Expance which the Empire can securly and regurly transport matrials and personell..
