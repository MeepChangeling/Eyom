The Fèzî-rëpíêkè Expance (/ˈfèzɪ/ /rëˈp˔ɘkè/ Follyflight [stupid; dumb; silly] [flight]) is a subtropical expanse on the coast of the Greenrange region. Fèzî-rëpíêkè Expance is a relativly typical expanse for Greenrange, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Fèzî-rëpíêkè Expance has a yearly average tempature of 21°C (69°F), with its average tempature durring the summer being a pleasent 25°C (77°F) and its average tempature durring the winter being a cool 16°C (60°F). The Fèzî-rëpíêkè Expance recieves an average of 138 cm/y (54 in/y) of percipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain durring the spring. The Fèzî-rëpíêkè Expance covers an area of nearly 15799 km2 (9819 mi2), and an average elevation of 7250 m (-19163 ft) above sealevel.

The Fèzî-rëpíêkè Expance is overflowing with shrines and holy sites, with most noteworthy divines having one or more shrines in any location relevent to their portfolio.

Fèzî-rëpíêkè Expance is noteworthy for its economic independence from Greenrange's export of Metals. Fèzî-rëpíêkè Expance is particularly renound for its dungeoneering industry. It is also has a noteworthy mining industry.
