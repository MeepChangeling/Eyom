The Dèci-jêthi Woodlands has a yearly average temperature of 18°C (64°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 10°C (50°F). The Dèci-jêthi Woodlands covers an area of nearly 7285 km2 (4527 mi2), and an average elevation of 5248 m (17217 ft) above sea level.
