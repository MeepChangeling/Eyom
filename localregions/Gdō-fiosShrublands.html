The Gdō-fios Shrublands (/gdoː/ /fjos/ Cream [shade; shadow; hue] [dairy]) are a group of subtropical shrubland within the wetland of the Riverpack Coast region. Gdō-fios Shrublands are a relatively typical shrubland for Riverpack Coast, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Gdō-fios Shrublands has a yearly average temperature of 14°C (57°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 27°C (80°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cold 1°C (33°F). The Gdō-fios Shrublands receives an average of 221 cm/y (87 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Gdō-fios Shrublands covers an area of nearly 7545 km2 (4689 mi2), and an average elevation of 3264 m (10708 ft) above sea level.
