The Wàki̊qāv Woods (/wə/ /ˈci̘qəˑv/ Tinker's [prostitute; whore] [technological]) are a group of temperate woods on the coast of the Orchard Garden region. Wàki̊qāv Woods are a relatively typical woods for Orchard Garden, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Wàki̊qāv Woods has a yearly average temperature of 12°C (53°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 26°C (78°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a freezing -1°C (31°F). The Wàki̊qāv Woods receives an average of 299 cm/y (117 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of snow during the pleasantly short winter months. The Wàki̊qāv Woods covers an area of nearly 7114 km2 (4421 mi2), and an average elevation of 3934 m (12906 ft) above sea level.
