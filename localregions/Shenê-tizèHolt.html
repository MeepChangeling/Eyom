The Shenê-tizè Holt (/ˈʃenɘ/ /ˈtizè/ Sagelodge [main] [research]) is a subtropical holt on the coast of the Raked Plains region. Shenê-tizè Holt is a relatively typical holt for Raked Plains, as well as Eyom as a whole.

Climate

The Shenê-tizè Holt has a yearly average temperature of 20°C (68°F), with its average temperature during the summer being a warm 29°C (84°F) and its average temperature during the winter being a cool 12°C (53°F). The Shenê-tizè Holt receives an average of 72 cm/y (28 in/y) of precipitation, most of which comes in the form of rain during the spring. The Shenê-tizè Holt covers an area of nearly 7492 km2 (4656 mi2), and an average elevation of 3178 m (10426 ft) above sea level.
