Sioughau Ash

Sioughau Ash is a rough light grey wood with a dark grain. Sioughau Ash's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in the milling of various grains. Sioughau Ash was first discovered by the military leader Ca̋nvé̄y Härm 'Kitty Amy' Rēkīkī Nīńoī Rawm vyim Yuvnonreys within the Qovuzeta Forest durring the late 18th century in early winter of the year 1793.
