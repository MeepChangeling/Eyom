Oorqwsite is a rough septagonal rose crystal. Oorqwsite's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the creation of rope. Oorqwsite was first discovered by the alchemist Heblu Loder within the Mêfit-mëcê Basin in time immemorial. Heblu Loder's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 2nd century.
