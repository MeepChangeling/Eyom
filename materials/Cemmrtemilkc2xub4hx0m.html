Cemmrte Milk

Cemmrte milk is a syrupy gray milk with a turquoise sheen which smells like freshly cut grass and tastes like orc spit. . Cemmrte Milk's properties are mostly magical in nature, but do make use of its natural makeup and is uniquly useful in the process of making textiles.
