Sekdimene

Sekdimene is a smooth rectangular yellow crystal which glows dimly. Sekdimene's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the creation of paper and paper-like materials. Sekdimene was first discovered by the alchemist Izsrang within the Readwich Brushlands in time immemorial. Izsrang's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 4th century.
