Mbstaline is a striated matte predominalty turquoise sedimentary rock with lime green spots and small glints of blue. Mbstaline's properties are mostly magical in nature, but do make use of its natural makeup and is uniquly useful in the creation of glass. Mbstaline was first discovered by the guard Zepí Sheshesmèj within the Lezyabí-thizè Forest durring the early 19th century in late summer of the year 1833.
