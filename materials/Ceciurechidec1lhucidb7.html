Ceciurec Hide

The Ceciurec is a tiny packs-based carnivore. A Ceciurec looks like nothing else, posessing a broad head with two large horns, two legs ending in hooves, and a long feline-like tail. The creature's hide holds special properties. Ceciurec Hide's properties are mostly magical in nature, but do make use of its natural makeup and is uniquly useful to miners. Ceciurec Hide was first discovered by the mage Trêrí within the Kuz Miygu Basin durring the late 18th century in early fall of the year 1786.
