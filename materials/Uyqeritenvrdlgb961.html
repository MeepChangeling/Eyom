Uyqerite

Uyqerite is a striated glossey predominalty tan metamorphic rock with burnt orange streaks and small glints of dark red. Uyqerite's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the creation of paper and paper-like materials. Uyqerite was first discovered by the farmer Trêgepí Birimo within the Êfêdê-race Holt durring the late 14th century in spring of the year 1387.
