Edairtch Pine

Edairtch Pine is a smooth lime green wood with a dark grain. Edairtch Pine's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the construction of armor. Edairtch Pine was first discovered by the weaver Shnih within the Endon Heath in time immemorial. Shnih's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 2nd century.
