Upph Hide

The Upph is a tiny herd-based omnivore. They are most easily described as a melding of a Colossal Squid, a Water Vole, and a Moray Eel. The passive's hide holds special properties. Upph Hide's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the creation of glass. Upph Hide was first discovered by the inventor Kthëi within the Hudworth Brush in time immemorial. Kthëi's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 8th century.
