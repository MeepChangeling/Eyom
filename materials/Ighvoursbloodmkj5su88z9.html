Ighvours Blood

The Ighvours is a small herd-based herbavore. A Ighvours looks like nothing else, posessing a flat-faced head with a single horn, four legs long and slender ending in claws, and a long feline-like tail. The creature's blood holds special properties. Ighvours Blood's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the creation of glass.
