Cymene

Cymene is a ribbed rectangular dark purple crystal which glimmers in the light. Cymene's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in the milling of various grains. Cymene was first discovered by the miner Gîbê Shruzraneil within the Bâmeya Ku̹chyi Woodlands durring the early 16th century in summer of the year 1538.
