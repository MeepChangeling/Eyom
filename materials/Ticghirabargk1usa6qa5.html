Ticghirabar

Ticghirabar is a gritty dark blue ore with splotches of cyan. Ticghirabar's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in the creation of glass. Ticghirabar was first discovered by the soldier Balcheth Latymer within the G̈cêk-vazê Prairie in time immemorial. Balcheth Latymer's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 3rd century.
