Ghoughmene

Ghoughmene is a rough rhomboid charcoal crystal which glows dimly. Ghoughmene's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in the milling of various grains. Ghoughmene was first discovered by the miner Glèshê within the Nuqicozupi Brushlands in time immemorial. Glèshê's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the late 2nd century.
