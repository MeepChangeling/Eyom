Teooosh

Teooosh is a glassey smooth prefectly matte predominalty dark blue metamorphic rock with blue streaks and small glints of peach. Teooosh's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in pottery. Teooosh was first discovered by the mage Shëkê Gruffudd within the Metunuru Heath in time immemorial. Shëkê Gruffudd's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 5th century.
