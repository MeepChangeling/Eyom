Urney Maple

Urney Maple was first discovered by the scholar Bēnḱ Nīń̄s 'Heaven Xandy' Härmé̄ Hä̋ch Mboīs Gakēè within the Éimu-sdá Holt in time immemorial. Bēnḱ Nīń̄s 'Heaven Xandy' Härmé̄ Hä̋ch Mboīs Gakēè's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 2nd century.
