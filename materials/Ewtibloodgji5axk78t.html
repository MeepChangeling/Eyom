Ewti Blood

The Ewti is a diminutive packs-based omnivore. A Ewti looks like nothing else, posessing a pointed head with a single horn, two legs ending in cloven hooves, and a long feline-like tail. The creature's blood holds special properties. Ewti Blood's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the construction of weapons. Ewti Blood was first discovered by the rancher Trëkêv within the Nujosepete Moor in time immemorial. Trëkêv's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the late 7th century.
