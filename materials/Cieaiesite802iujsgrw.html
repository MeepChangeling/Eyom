Cieaiesite

Cieaiesite is a rough satin predominalty charcoal sedimentary rock with dark red flecks and small glints of orange. Cieaiesite's properties are entirly magical in nature and is uniquly useful in the process of making textiles. Cieaiesite was first discovered by the inventor Snocth Tshêyêgë within the Cêhara-lotê Heathland in time immemorial. Snocth Tshêyêgë's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the late 6th century.
