Ieriisalite

Ieriisalite is a ribbed square cyan crystal with a noticable slate shimmer when it catches the light. Ieriisalite's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in goldsmithing. Ieriisalite was first discovered by the miner Nomick hu Hsekiu within the Rockgrass Forest in time immemorial. Nomick hu Hsekiu's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the late 5th century.
