Toyssmene was first discovered by the smith Béch Séchvé̄ 'Wavy Lilith' Joīkī̌ Vú̄w̄̌ Vúnḱ Crosrebu within the Nanline Prairie in time immemorial. Béch Séchvé̄ 'Wavy Lilith' Joīkī̌ Vú̄w̄̌ Vúnḱ Crosrebu's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 4th century.
