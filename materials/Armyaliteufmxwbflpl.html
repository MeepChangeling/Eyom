Armyalite

Armyalite is a lumpy satin predominalty blue metamorphic rock with black spots. Armyalite's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful for making dye. Armyalite was first discovered by the trader Cākīb Rérmé̄ 'Chloe Belle' Cúnvé Joīnvé̄ Mp̪ftór Owen within the Okhnǐ Sijĭti Meadows durring the early 12th century.
