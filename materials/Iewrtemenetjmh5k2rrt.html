Iewrtemene is a glassey smooth dark blue ore with splotches of cream, and flecks of rose Iewrtemene's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in pottery. Iewrtemene was first discovered by the rancher Trîbeg̈mê Glëg̈ Treshe within the Hûhya Chyàzi Grasslands in time immemorial. Trîbeg̈mê Glëg̈ Treshe's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 9th century.
