Oetiu Beech

Oetiu Beech is a rigged green wood with a dark grain. Oetiu Beech's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the construction of weapons. Oetiu Beech was first discovered by the miner Grêbêv Krazron within the Kiyu Donîs̺æ Brushlands in time immemorial. Grêbêv Krazron's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 8th century.
