Eatuerite

Eatuerite is a powdery prefectly matte predominalty amber igneous rock with silver spots and small glints of black. Eatuerite's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in agriculture. Eatuerite was first discovered by the farmer Lessil Zëmêpí within the Hö Fëu Meadows durring the early 19th century in late spring of the year 1810.
