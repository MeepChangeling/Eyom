Tchougherite

Tchougherite is a powdery satin predominalty crimson igneous rock with black spots and small glints of brown. Tchougherite's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in stone cutting and masonry. Tchougherite was first discovered by the weaver Zeshêrî hrd Nikare within the Xecotá-ken Holt durring the early 11th century.
