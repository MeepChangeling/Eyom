Eighthveq Hide

The Eighthveq is a huge herd-based omnivore. A Eighthveq looks like nothing else, posessing a blunt head covered in thick bone-armor plates, two legs muscular ending in hooves, and a short tail. The creature's hide holds special properties. Eighthveq Hide's properties are mostly magical in nature, but do make use of its natural makeup and is uniquly useful in pressing oils. Eighthveq Hide was first discovered by the ranger Garê within the Hubonio Holt in time immemorial. Garê's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the late 6th century.
