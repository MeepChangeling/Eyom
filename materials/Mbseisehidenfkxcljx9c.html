Mbseise Hide

The Mbseise is a huge herd-based carnivore. A Mbseise looks like nothing else, posessing a dog-like head with a single horn, four legs long and slender ending in talons, and a short tail. The creature's hide holds special properties. Mbseise Hide's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful for making dye. Mbseise Hide was first discovered by the explorer Nerys within the Sekskijieqa Heath durring the late 10th century.
