Oeteena is a rough cream ore with splotches of silver, and flecks of brown Oeteena's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the process of making textiles. Oeteena was first discovered by the carpenter Nik within the Nkágä-fyu Brush durring the late 14th century in summer of the year 1398.
