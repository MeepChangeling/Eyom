Zeeygeerite was first discovered by the soldier Bēs Ra̋nvé̄ 'Lisso Blacksey' Bérméch Mbōy Jóoī Gowler within the Fitëvî-sîqê Forest in time immemorial. Bēs Ra̋nvé̄ 'Lisso Blacksey' Bérméch Mbōy Jóoī Gowler's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 8th century.
