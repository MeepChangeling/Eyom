Whormene

Whormene is a gloopy cream herb with light blue ribbons running through it which smells odd and tastes like blackberries.. Whormene's properties are mostly magical in nature, but do make use of its natural makeup and is uniquly useful in the construction of armor. Whormene was first discovered by the rancher Szarheihr within the Vujoqale Woodlands durring the late 10th century.
