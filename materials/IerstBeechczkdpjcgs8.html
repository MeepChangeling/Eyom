Ierst Beech

Ierst Beech is a ripply magenta wood with a pale grain. Ierst Beech's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in carpentry. Ierst Beech was first discovered by the scholar Cúnḱ Ya̋ch 'Pie Butternut' Da̋nvē Mbó̄̄ Bé̄shīs Barnes Dyeg̈mênm within the Lumùd Heath in time immemorial. Cúnḱ Ya̋ch 'Pie Butternut' Da̋nvē Mbó̄̄ Bé̄shīs Barnes Dyeg̈mênm's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 7th century.
