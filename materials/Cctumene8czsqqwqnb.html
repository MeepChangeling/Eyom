Cctumene

Cctumene is a smooth light blue ore. Cctumene's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in the creation of paper and paper-like materials. Cctumene was first discovered by the scholar Tresê Trêrêga within the Schyhyo̠-ntadë Savannah in time immemorial. Tresê Trêrêga's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the late 8th century.
