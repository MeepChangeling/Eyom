Oll Hide

The Oll is a huge herd-based herbavore. They are most easily described as a melding of a Vicuña, a Abyssinian, and a Xoloitzcuintli. The dosscile's hide holds special properties. Oll Hide's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in pressing oils. Oll Hide was first discovered by the military leader Rhiannon Bhineiris within the Sek-heyvō Woods in time immemorial. Rhiannon Bhineiris's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the late 7th century.
