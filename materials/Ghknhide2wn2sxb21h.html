Ghkn Hide

The Ghkn is a small packs-based omnivore. A Ghkn looks like nothing else, posessing a pointed head covered in thick bone-armor plates, four legs bent ending in talons, and a blunt tail. The creature's hide holds special properties. Ghkn Hide's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in the process of making textiles. Ghkn Hide was first discovered by the traveler Dog Batton within the Fliyàvom Woods durring the late 19th century in late winter of the year 1879.
