The Ewng is a colossal packs-based herbavore. A Ewng looks like nothing else, posessing a stone-like head with two large horns, six legs long and slender ending in claws, and a long feline-like tail. The creature's hide holds special properties. Ewng Hide's properties are entirly magical in nature and is uniquly useful to the arcane arts. Ewng Hide was first discovered by the guard Khrabai Bregfëm within the Urïpa-sluä Brush in time immemorial.
