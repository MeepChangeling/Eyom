Aezeksite

Aezeksite is a rough rhomboid turquoise crystal with a noticable cream shimmer when it catches the light. Aezeksite's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in the creation of paper and paper-like materials. Aezeksite was first discovered by the adventurer Ammahilde within the Win Keslè Forest in time immemorial. Ammahilde's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 4th century.
