Eellwmene is a gritty dark purple ore with splotches of pink, and flecks of light blue Eellwmene's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in the creation of glass. Eellwmene was first discovered by the teacher Pagunos Ghuldrash within the Dachyû Chyudû Forest in time immemorial.
