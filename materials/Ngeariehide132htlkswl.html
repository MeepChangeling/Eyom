Ngearie Hide

The Ngearie is a small packs-based carnivore. They are most easily described as a melding of a Salmon and a Green Frog. The passive's hide holds special properties. Ngearie Hide's properties are mostly magical in nature, but do make use of its natural makeup and is uniquly useful as a light source. Ngearie Hide was first discovered by the adventurer Riknai Raeldireth within the Skra Fratno Moor in time immemorial.
