Tcksite

Tcksite is a smooth triangular black crystal which glimmers in the light. Tcksite's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the creation of rope. Tcksite was first discovered by the alchemist Amdirbarad Raeldireth Zlêgfê Glënho within the Nenyë Stiak Grasslands in time immemorial. Amdirbarad Raeldireth Zlêgfê Glënho's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the late 2nd century.
