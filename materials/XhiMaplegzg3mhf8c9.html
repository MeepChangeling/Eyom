Xhi Maple

Xhi Maple is a smooth dark purple wood with rose grain. Xhi Maple's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in goldsmithing. Xhi Maple was first discovered by the traveler Zlêli Pizai within the Munska Nëbës Woods durring the late 19th century in early summer of the year 1866.
