Oewed Hide

The Oewed is a small packs-based omnivore. A Oewed looks like nothing else, posessing a head covered in thick bone-armor plates, four legs long and slender ending in cloven hooves, and a rabbit-like tail. The creature's hide holds special properties. Oewed Hide's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in the creation of glass. Oewed Hide was first discovered by the rancher Ditela hu Paanchi within the Líhàwi̊ Vadrā Moorland in time immemorial. Ditela hu Paanchi's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the late 5th century.
