Arttsite's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the creation of glass. Arttsite was first discovered by the scholar Falassiel Shêbêv within the Lûhya Kûqachû Expance in time immemorial. Falassiel Shêbêv's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 6th century.
