Seserite

Seserite is a lumpy prefectly matte predominalty charcoal sedimentary rock with orange streaks and small glints of tan. Seserite's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in the creation of paper and paper-like materials. Seserite was first discovered by the ranger Davre Tununzic within the Frëtbë Fëldnōd Woodlands in time immemorial. Davre Tununzic's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 4th century.
