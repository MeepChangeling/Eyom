Ygh Bone

The Ygh is a giant herd-based herbavore. A Ygh looks like nothing else, posessing a sharp head adorned with a crest-like ridge, four legs ending in cloven hooves, and a blunt tail. The creature's bones holds special properties. Ygh Bone's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the construction of armor. Ygh Bone was first discovered by the guard Zlkarí Phillips McMorrow within the Gæyû Hosû Woodlands in time immemorial. Zlkarí Phillips McMorrow's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the late 5th century.
