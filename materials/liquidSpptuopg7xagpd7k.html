Liquid Spptuo

Liquid Spptuo's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the process of bookbinding. Liquid Spptuo was first discovered by the scholar Munvin Unzabu within the Dachyû Chyudû Forest in time immemorial.
