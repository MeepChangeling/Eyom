Lurlm

Lurlm is a thin peach fluid with a dark purple sheen which smells like pepper and tastes like medicine.. Lurlm's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in the process of making textiles. Lurlm was first discovered by the teacher Alyar Criebolri within the Wolfhide Brushlands durring the late 15th century in winter of the year 1497.
