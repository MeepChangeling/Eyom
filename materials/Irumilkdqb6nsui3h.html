Iru milk is a syrupy auburn milk which smells like pinecones and tastes like death. . Iru Milk's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful for making dye. Iru Milk was first discovered by the rancher Fadhisa Vimeni within the Meyû Chyàve Grasslands durring the late 13th century in winter of the year 1252.
