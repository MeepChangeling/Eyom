Iewh Pear

Iewh Pear is a rough red wood with a dark grain. Iewh Pear's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in creating perfumes. Iewh Pear was first discovered by the shepherd Trepîthëm Trënhêpî Brêgfêbe within the Vo-ni̽nwenste̜ Forest in time immemorial. Trepîthëm Trënhêpî Brêgfêbe's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 4th century.
