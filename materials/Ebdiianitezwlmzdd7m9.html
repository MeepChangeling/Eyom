Ebdiianite

Ebdiianite is a gritty dark purple ore. Ebdiianite's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in pressing oils. Ebdiianite was first discovered by the weaver Trèbêbrí Vanzadur within the Dèhêl-qëthî Holt in time immemorial. Trèbêbrí Vanzadur's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 4th century.
