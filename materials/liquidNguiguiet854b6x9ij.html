Liquid Nguiguie

liquid Nguiguie is a thin light blue fluid which smells somewhat like rum and tastes like caramel.. Liquid Nguiguie's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the creation of paper and paper-like materials. Liquid Nguiguie was first discovered by the historian Trtêbê Tshê within the Gdës-flës Woods in time immemorial. Trtêbê Tshê's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 2nd century.
