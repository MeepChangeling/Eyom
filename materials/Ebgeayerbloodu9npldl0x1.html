Ebgeayer Blood

The Ebgeayer is a colossal solitary carnivore. A Ebgeayer looks like nothing else, posessing a blunt head with a single horn, four legs short ending in claws, and a short tail. The creature's blood holds special properties. Ebgeayer Blood's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in agriculture. Ebgeayer Blood was first discovered by the farmer Drake within the Gillow Grasslands durring the early 16th century in spring of the year 1521.
