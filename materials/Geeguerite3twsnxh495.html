Geeguerite

Geeguerite is a oily turquoise ore with splotches of burnt orange. Geeguerite's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in the process of making textiles. Geeguerite was first discovered by the scholar Gaw̄̄b Haw̄b 'Melissa Lisso' Vúoīy Yór Mbōkīkī Ya̋nvē Sēb within the Wāttoq Shrublands durring the late 10th century.
