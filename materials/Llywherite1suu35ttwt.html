Llywherite

Llywherite is a gritty light blue ore with splotches of magenta. Llywherite's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in the process of making textiles. Llywherite was first discovered by the guard Coralone Ignun within the Firemoss Woods durring the early 15th century in winter of the year 1440.
