The Aighth is a diminutive solitary carnivore. A Aighth looks like nothing else, posessing a dog-like head with two large horns, two legs ending in paws, and a blunt tail. The creature's blood holds special properties. Aighth Blood's properties are mostly magical in nature, but do make use of its natural makeup and is uniquly useful to miners. Aighth Blood was first discovered by the ranger Velneash grw Usermare within the Stair Steppe in time immemorial.
