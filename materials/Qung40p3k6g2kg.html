Qung

Qung is a ribbed pentagonal dark purple crystal with a noticable silver shimmer when it catches the light. Qung's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful for making dye. Qung was first discovered by the military leader Dhan Shuzoreth within the Kivûri Bâbâ Woods in time immemorial. Dhan Shuzoreth's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the late 8th century.
