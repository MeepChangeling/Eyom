Kchuresmene is a rough gray ore with splotches of gold. Kchuresmene's properties are mostly magical in nature, but do make use of its natural makeup and is uniquly useful in pottery. Kchuresmene was first discovered by the trader Tizrin hu Senebhenaf within the Rockbloom Holt durring the early 13th century in spring of the year 1250.
