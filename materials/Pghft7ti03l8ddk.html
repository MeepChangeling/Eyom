Pghft

Pghft is a smooth charcoal wood with a pale grain. Pghft's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in stone cutting and masonry. Pghft was first discovered by the military leader Zraz Garthesí Garthê within the Nenyë Stiak Grasslands in time immemorial. Zraz Garthesí Garthê's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the late 4th century.
