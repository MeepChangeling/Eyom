Usciieabar

Usciieabar is a oily auburn ore with splotches of magenta, and flecks of pink Usciieabar's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in the construction of watercraft. Usciieabar was first discovered by the shepherd Seletela Connocanus Mesodata within the Qe Kayu̹ Brushlands durring the late 14th century in spring of the year 1398.
