Iemnci Milk

Iemnci milk is a thick gray milk with a amber sheen which smells nightmareish and tastes like fruit.. Iemnci Milk's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the creation of paper and paper-like materials. Iemnci Milk was first discovered by the soldier Casuanidae within the Gleskraiku Woodlands durring the early 11th century.
