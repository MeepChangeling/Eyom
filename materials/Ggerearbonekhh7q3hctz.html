The Ggerear is a small solitary herbavore. A Ggerear looks like nothing else, posessing a blunt head covered in thick bone-armor plates, four legs muscular ending in broad paws, and a long feline-like tail. The creature's bones holds special properties. Ggerear Bone's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in pottery. Ggerear Bone was first discovered by the guard Buscoce Drimorlas within the Firemoss Woods durring the early 20th century in early summer of the year 1931.
