Cillanite

Cillanite is a smooth septagonal green crystal with a noticable orange shimmer when it catches the light. Cillanite's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in pottery. Cillanite was first discovered by the smith Ghadunra Phiegror within the Êqolê-ihê Êfê Brush durring the late 13th century in fall of the year 1278.
