Mnau Pear

Mnau Pear is a ripply crimson wood with a nearly invisible grain. Mnau Pear's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful an alchemical reagent. Mnau Pear was first discovered by the miner Hêbêb Trëmê within the Soco-lêkê Brush durring the early 13th century in fall of the year 1208.
