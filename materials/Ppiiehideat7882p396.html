Ppiie Hide

The Ppiie is a colossal solitary omnivore. A Ppiie looks like nothing else, posessing a broad head covered in thick bone-armor plates, two legs ending in cloven hooves, and a long feline-like tail. The creature's hide holds special properties. Ppiie Hide's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the creation of glass.
