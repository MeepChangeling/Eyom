Uoyccck

Uoyccck is a smooth green wood with a pale grain. Uoyccck's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in the construction of watercraft. Uoyccck was first discovered by the teacher Connocanus Tshêm within the Lezyabí-thizè Forest durring the early 18th century in late fall of the year 1717.
