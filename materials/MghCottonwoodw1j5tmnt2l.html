Mgh Cottonwood

Mgh Cottonwood is a smooth dark red wood with a dark grain. Mgh Cottonwood's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the construction of weapons. Mgh Cottonwood was first discovered by the weaver Firnipu Sozrereth within the Jîfîs-lotê Woodlands in time immemorial. Firnipu Sozrereth's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 6th century.
