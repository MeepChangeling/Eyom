Up

Up is a thick pink fluid with a dark purple sheen which smells odd and tastes like blackberries.. Up's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in the process of bookbinding. Up was first discovered by the teacher Ya̋chvóń Ha̋nvé̄ 'Calvin Zoin' Cawmē Ré̄̋nv Mp̪vēshī Trîbm within the Dige-qêpíê Forest durring the late 11th century.
