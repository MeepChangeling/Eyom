Guyuie is a gritty lime green ore with splotches of charcoal and dark blue Guyuie's properties are mostly magical in nature, but do make use of its natural makeup and is uniquly useful in the process of making textiles. Guyuie was first discovered by the scholar Trêbè Trtêbëm within the Fifkîn-tato Basin in time immemorial. Trêbè Trtêbëm's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 7th century.
