Edcalite

Edcalite is a rough rhomboid peach crystal which glimmers in the light. Edcalite's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the creation of rope. Edcalite was first discovered by the military leader Zêthëm Drimorlas within the Nrot Tus Heath durring the late 9th century.
