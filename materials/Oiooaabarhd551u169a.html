Oiooaabar is a oily gray ore with splotches of cream. Oiooaabar's properties are entirly mundane and is uniquly useful in the creation of paper and paper-like materials. Oiooaabar was first discovered by the trader Carandoliel Tuvno within the Coqê-ogimi Holt durring the early 16th century in fall of the year 1506.
