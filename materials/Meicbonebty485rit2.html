Meic Bone

The Meic is a giant solitary herbavore. A Meic looks like nothing else, posessing a broad head with two large horns, four legs bent ending in cloven hooves, and a long feline-like tail. The creature's bones holds special properties. Meic Bone's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in agriculture. Meic Bone was first discovered by the adventurer Ghrinshiy Wright-Phillips within the Gæyû Hosû Woodlands in time immemorial. Ghrinshiy Wright-Phillips's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 7th century.
