Eausefterite

Eausefterite is a gritty dark brown ore with splotches of gray. Eausefterite's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the creation of glass. Eausefterite was first discovered by the explorer Méchī̄nḱ Yoúr 'Hit Nina' Ya̋chīy Sēbóch Joi̋nvénv nis Inenek-Inti within the Hartcouche Moorland durring the early 13th century in fall of the year 1241.
