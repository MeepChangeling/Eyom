Imbgn Cottonwood

Imbgn Cottonwood is a smooth cyan wood with a nearly invisible grain. Imbgn Cottonwood's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the creation of rope. Imbgn Cottonwood was first discovered by the military leader Trèsêj within the Gleskraiku Woodlands in time immemorial. Trèsêj's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 6th century.
