Tuguemmaline

Tuguemmaline is a ribbed trapazoidal light blue crystal. Tuguemmaline's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the milling of various grains. Tuguemmaline was first discovered by the adventurer Firnipu Tuvno within the Binilà Hari Savannah in time immemorial. Firnipu Tuvno's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the late 2nd century.
