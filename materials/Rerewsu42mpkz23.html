Rerew is a lumpy satin light grey sedimentary rock Rerew's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in goldsmithing. Rerew was first discovered by the explorer Coī̋nḱ Ya̋chvó̄b 'Depth Ebony' Vú̄rméch Yoīy Ha̋ńoī Èbmesîpí within the Mbo̠n-qōl Brush durring the late 9th century.
