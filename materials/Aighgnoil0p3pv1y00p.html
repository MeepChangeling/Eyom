Aighgn Oil

Aighgn oil is a thick dark blue oil which smells like oranges and tastes like candy. . Aighgn Oil's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful for making dye. Aighgn Oil was first discovered by the scholar Dyêbesesí nis Sekhemrekhutawy within the G̈ogë-yafitê Holt in time immemorial. Dyêbesesí nis Sekhemrekhutawy's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 3rd century.
