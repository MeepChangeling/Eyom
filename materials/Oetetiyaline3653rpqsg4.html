Oetetiyaline

Oetetiyaline is a streaky square cream crystal with a noticable dark blue shimmer when it catches the light. Oetetiyaline's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in carpentry. Oetetiyaline was first discovered by the miner Tsêlè within the Sehiiukho Moor durring the early 20th century in late spring of the year 1926.
