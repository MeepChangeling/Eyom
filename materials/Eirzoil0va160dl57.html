Eirz Oil

Eirz oil is a syrupy gray oil with a dark red sheen which smells like fruit and tastes like ambrosia. . Eirz Oil's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in pottery. Eirz Oil was first discovered by the farmer Klehn Palzic within the Gëlë-tëi Woodlands durring the early 11th century.
