The Nnlm is a small packs-based carnivore. A Nnlm looks like nothing else, posessing a dog-like head with two large horns, four legs ending in broad paws, and a short tail. The creature's blood holds special properties. Nnlm Blood's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the process of making textiles.
