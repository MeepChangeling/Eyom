Edeaho Hide

The Edeaho is a tiny packs-based omnivore. A Edeaho looks like nothing else, posessing a pointed head with a single horn, two legs ending in cloven hooves, and a rabbit-like tail. The creature's hide holds special properties. Edeaho Hide's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in agriculture. Edeaho Hide was first discovered by the adventurer Tshelî within the Vyilenjinuna Maquis durring the early 18th century in late summer of the year 1749.
