The Uremf is a medium herd-based omnivore. A Uremf looks like nothing else, posessing a pointed head with a single horn, six legs ending in cloven hooves, and a rabbit-like tail. The creature's blood holds special properties. Uremf Blood's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the creation of paper and paper-like materials.
