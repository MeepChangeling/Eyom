Yryze

Yryze is a rough dark purple wood with a pale grain. Yryze's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in the creation of rope. Yryze was first discovered by the weaver Cephalotilia Zaevnes Zêpí within the Qîngí-cësêno Shrublands in time immemorial. Cephalotilia Zaevnes Zêpí's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the late 1st century.
