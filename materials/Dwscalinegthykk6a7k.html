Dwscaline

Dwscaline is a rough square gray crystal with a noticable dark green shimmer when it catches the light. Dwscaline's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in pottery. Dwscaline was first discovered by the scholar Garêpí Trepîbrë within the Shêlê-êgë Expance durring the late 12th century in spring of the year 1152.
