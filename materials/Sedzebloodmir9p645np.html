Sedze Blood

The Sedze is a giant herd-based carnivore. A Sedze looks like nothing else, posessing a sharp head with a single horn, four legs powerful ending in hooves, and a short tail. The creature's blood holds special properties. Sedze Blood's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in the milling of various grains. Sedze Blood was first discovered by the ranger Vilwiel within the Qapubupujago Shrublands durring the late 14th century in winter of the year 1355.
