Yecew Hide

The Yecew is a medium solitary carnivore. A Yecew looks like nothing else, posessing a head with two large horns, eight legs long and slender ending in claws, and a round backside lacking a tail. The creature's hide holds special properties. Yecew Hide's properties are mostly magical in nature, but do make use of its natural makeup and is uniquly useful in the construction of weapons. Yecew Hide was first discovered by the ranger Veahl Gando Sheihl within the Utopùfàlnù Steppe in time immemorial.
