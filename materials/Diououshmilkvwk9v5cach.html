Diouoush Milk

Diouoush milk is a gloopy green milk with a rose sheen which smells like ooze and tastes like fresh dirt.. Diouoush Milk's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful to the arcane arts. Diouoush Milk was first discovered by the mage Chudvose Zamezat within the Kûgû Obû Fields in time immemorial. Chudvose Zamezat's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the late 1st century.
