Oeshckoy Offal

The Oeshckoy is a medium herd-based herbavore. A Oeshckoy looks like nothing else, posessing a flat-faced head with two large horns, four legs bent ending in cloven hooves, and a rabbit-like tail. The creature's organs holds special properties. Oeshckoy Offal's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the milling of various grains. Oeshckoy Offal was first discovered by the weaver Mocth hu Djer within the Razorroot Holt durring the early 10th century.
