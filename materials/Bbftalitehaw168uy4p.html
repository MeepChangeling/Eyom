Bbftalite

Bbftalite is a streaky triangular dark green crystal which glimmers in the light. Bbftalite's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the process of making textiles. Bbftalite was first discovered by the rancher Viola Sheshë within the Cê-dêgêg̈ê Woodlands in time immemorial. Viola Sheshë's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the late 5th century.
