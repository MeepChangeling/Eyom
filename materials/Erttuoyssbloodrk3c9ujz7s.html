Erttuoyss Blood

The Erttuoyss is a giant herd-based carnivore. A Erttuoyss looks like nothing else, posessing a stone-like head adorned with a crest-like ridge, four legs long and slender ending in cloven hooves, and a round backside lacking a tail. The creature's blood holds special properties. Erttuoyss Blood's properties are mostly magical in nature, but do make use of its natural makeup and is uniquly useful in the construction of weapons.
