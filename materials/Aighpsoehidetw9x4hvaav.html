Aighpsoe Hide

The Aighpsoe is a medium packs-based carnivore. A Aighpsoe looks like nothing else, posessing a flat-faced head with a single horn, four legs powerful ending in broad paws, and a short tail. The creature's hide holds special properties. Aighpsoe Hide's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in goldsmithing. Aighpsoe Hide was first discovered by the weaver Zêre within the Duwärd-nardäjs Woodlands in time immemorial. Zêre's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 4th century.
