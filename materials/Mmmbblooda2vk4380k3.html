The Mmmb is a medium solitary omnivore. A Mmmb looks like nothing else, posessing a broad head with a single horn, four legs short ending in claws, and a round backside lacking a tail. The creature's blood holds special properties. Mmmb Blood's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in pressing oils. Mmmb Blood was first discovered by the soldier Skafida hu Kanefer within the Qithêt-vihê Rainforest durring the late 20th century in early summer of the year 1975.
