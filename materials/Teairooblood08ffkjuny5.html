Teairoo Blood

The Teairoo is a diminutive solitary herbavore. A Teairoo looks like nothing else, posessing a dog-like head with two large horns, four legs short ending in broad paws, and a blunt tail. The creature's blood holds special properties. Teairoo Blood's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the process of bookbinding. Teairoo Blood was first discovered by the explorer Ranmyl Gheengiabash within the Ëdêsê-inë Heathland in time immemorial. Ranmyl Gheengiabash's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 7th century.
