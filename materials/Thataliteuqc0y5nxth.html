Thatalite is a gritty dark red ore with splotches of dark brown. Thatalite's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the creation of rope. Thatalite was first discovered by the weaver Hëîl within the Izosimu Moorland in time immemorial. Hëîl's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the late 3rd century.
