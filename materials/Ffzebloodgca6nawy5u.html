Ffze Blood

The Ffze is a giant herd-based herbavore. They are most easily described as a melding of a False Cobra, a Pudelpointer, and a Budgerigar. The passive's blood holds special properties. Ffze Blood's properties are mostly magical in nature, but do make use of its natural makeup and is uniquly useful in the process of making textiles. Ffze Blood was first discovered by the alchemist Ezaenzo Phrugremen within the Balbriand Heath in time immemorial. Ezaenzo Phrugremen's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 6th century.
