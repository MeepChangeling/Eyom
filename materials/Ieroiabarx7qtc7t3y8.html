Ieroiabar is a rough green ore with splotches of turquoise. Ieroiabar's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in the creation of rope. Ieroiabar was first discovered by the inventor Aglarchiw Zêthëm within the Äncwe Udweht Woodlands in time immemorial. Aglarchiw Zêthëm's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the late 3rd century.
