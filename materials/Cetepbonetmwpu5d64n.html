The Cetep is a small packs-based herbavore. A Cetep looks like nothing else, posessing a flat-faced head with two large horns, two legs short ending in cloven hooves, and a round backside lacking a tail. The creature's bones holds special properties. Cetep Bone's properties are mostly magical in nature, but do make use of its natural makeup and is uniquly useful in the milling of various grains.
