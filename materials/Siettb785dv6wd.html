Siet

Siet is a rough purple wood with a nearly invisible grain. Siet's properties are mostly magical in nature, but do make use of its natural makeup and is uniquly useful in the construction of watercraft. Siet was first discovered by the traveler Joīb Më̋mē̋ 'Lianna Glaze' Yawmē̄ Gä̋chvē Cā̌shī Phiegror within the Enê Fîna-ëyêthê Forest durring the early 17th century in spring of the year 1623.
