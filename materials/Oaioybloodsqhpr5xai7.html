Oaioy Blood

The Oaioy is a giant herd-based carnivore. A Oaioy looks like nothing else, posessing a stone-like head adorned with a crest-like ridge, six legs bent ending in paws, and a round backside lacking a tail. The creature's blood holds special properties. Oaioy Blood's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in creating perfumes. Oaioy Blood was first discovered by the farmer Tshëd grw Siatum within the Hartcouche Moorland in time immemorial. Tshëd grw Siatum's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 4th century.
