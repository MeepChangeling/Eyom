The Pneil is a medium solitary carnivore. A Pneil looks like nothing else, posessing a dog-like head with a single horn, two legs short ending in claws, and a short tail. The creature's blood holds special properties. Pneil Blood's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in agriculture.
