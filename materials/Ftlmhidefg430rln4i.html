Ftlm Hide

The Ftlm is a medium herd-based herbavore. A Ftlm looks like nothing else, posessing a pointed head with two large horns, eight legs lankey ending in talons, and a blunt tail. The creature's hide holds special properties. Ftlm Hide's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful for making dye. Ftlm Hide was first discovered by the adventurer Dyegfe Spenceley within the Qûyæ Amu Expance durring the early 14th century in summer of the year 1316.
