Orngue Beech

Orngue Beech is a rigged light grey wood with a pale grain. Orngue Beech's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in carpentry. Orngue Beech was first discovered by the smith Gersel Brêyêv within the Sihî-jêsêno Maquis in time immemorial. Gersel Brêyêv's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the late 8th century.
