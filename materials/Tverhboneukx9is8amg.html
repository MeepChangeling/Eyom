The Tverh is a medium solitary carnivore. A Tverh looks like nothing else, posessing a flat-faced head adorned with a crest-like ridge, four legs long and slender ending in hooves, and a rabbit-like tail. The creature's bones holds special properties. Tverh Bone's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the construction of armor.
