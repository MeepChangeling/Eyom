The Eauwh is a tiny packs-based omnivore. A Eauwh looks like nothing else, posessing a blunt head with a single horn, four legs long and slender ending in paws, and a round backside lacking a tail. The creature's hide holds special properties. Eauwh Hide's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in the construction of weapons.
