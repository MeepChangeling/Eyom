Cczsciabar

Cczsciabar is a smooth charcoal ore with splotches of turquoise. Cczsciabar's properties are mostly magical in nature, but do make use of its natural makeup and is uniquly useful in the creation of rope. Cczsciabar was first discovered by the miner Eler Vanzadur within the Mbö Bök Woodlands durring the early 19th century in late spring of the year 1812.
