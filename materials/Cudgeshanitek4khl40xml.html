Cudgeshanite

Cudgeshanite is a smooth satin predominalty white igneous rock with cream spots and small glints of tan. Cudgeshanite's properties are entirly magical in nature and is uniquly useful in the process of making textiles. Cudgeshanite was first discovered by the teacher Jóō Sé̄ 'Skyla Ella' Vú̄nḱ Ca̋mp̪f̄nḱ Mēs Dyêyêm within the Yo̠t-frë Shrublands durring the early 11th century.
