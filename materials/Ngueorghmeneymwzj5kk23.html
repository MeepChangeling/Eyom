Ngueorghmene

Ngueorghmene is a oily charcoal ore with splotches of gold and white Ngueorghmene's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the construction of armor. Ngueorghmene was first discovered by the rancher Brêbê nis Nubwenet within the Dèci-jêthi Woodlands in time immemorial. Brêbê nis Nubwenet's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 8th century.
