Ngueppuerh

Ngueppuerh is a rough red ore with splotches of light grey, and flecks of gray Ngueppuerh's properties are entirly magical in nature and is uniquly useful in carpentry. Ngueppuerh was first discovered by the shepherd Dyèqtco Bye within the Hæmàbà Iyu̽ Grasslands in time immemorial. Dyèqtco Bye's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 2nd century.
