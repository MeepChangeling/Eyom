Lldd Blood

The Lldd is a giant solitary carnivore. A Lldd looks like nothing else, posessing a head with a single horn, two legs bent ending in hooves, and a short tail. The creature's blood holds special properties. Lldd Blood's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in pottery. Lldd Blood was first discovered by the farmer Pn-54j 'Peridot' Glkadsmthë within the Sebsíhr Holt in time immemorial. Pn-54j 'Peridot' Glkadsmthë's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the late 6th century.
