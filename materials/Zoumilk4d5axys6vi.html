Zou Milk

Zou milk is a thin pink milk with lime green ribbons running through it which smells like garlic and tastes like candy.. Zou Milk's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in the process of bookbinding. Zou Milk was first discovered by the adventurer Madog Dhuzono within the Gatigamau Maquis durring the early 14th century in spring of the year 1321.
