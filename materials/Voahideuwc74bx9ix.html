Voa Hide

The Voa is a huge solitary carnivore. A Voa looks like nothing else, posessing a blunt head covered in thick bone-armor plates, four legs long and slender ending in cloven hooves, and a rabbit-like tail. The creature's hide holds special properties. Voa Hide's properties are mostly magical in nature, but do make use of its natural makeup and is uniquly useful in pressing oils. Voa Hide was first discovered by the shepherd Glêli within the Kabiqa Holt durring the late 10th century.
