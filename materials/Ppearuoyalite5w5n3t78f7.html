Ppearuoyalite

Ppearuoyalite is a rough burnt orange ore with splotches of peach, and flecks of tan Ppearuoyalite's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the creation of paper and paper-like materials. Ppearuoyalite was first discovered by the smith Mlêl Vimeni within the Bachibà Danû Holt in time immemorial. Mlêl Vimeni's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 6th century.
