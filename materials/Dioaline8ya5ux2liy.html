Dioaline

Dioaline is a striated very glossey predominalty turquoise igneous rock with rose spots and small glints of charcoal. Dioaline's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the process of making textiles. Dioaline was first discovered by the historian Cúnḱ Coida̋sh 'Wavy Pride' Mērm Rē̋ Ra̋chvoīs Bēboīy Jō̋g within the Ku̹chæda Qamà Moor durring the early 13th century in fall of the year 1205.
