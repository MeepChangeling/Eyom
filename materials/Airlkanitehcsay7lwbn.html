Airlkanite

Airlkanite is a rough light grey ore with splotches of auburn. Airlkanite's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in carpentry. Airlkanite was first discovered by the historian Tshêtco Trtegepí within the Dêqë-mênêye Heathland durring the late 11th century.
