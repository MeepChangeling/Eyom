Wreargue Bone

The Wreargue is a small herd-based herbavore. They are most easily described as a melding of a American Hairless Terrier, a Beetle, and a Red-winged blackbird. The passive's bones holds special properties. Wreargue Bone's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful to miners. Wreargue Bone was first discovered by the traveler Dyêtconm Every within the Qusigej Shrublands in time immemorial. Dyêtconm Every's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 4th century.
