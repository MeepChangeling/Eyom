Hsemmabar

Hsemmabar is a glassey smooth slate ore with splotches of rose. Hsemmabar's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the process of bookbinding. Hsemmabar was first discovered by the weaver Bresí Ningi within the Zud-yös Prairie in time immemorial. Bresí Ningi's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the late 7th century.
