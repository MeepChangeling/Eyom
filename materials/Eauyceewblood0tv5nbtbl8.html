The creature's blood holds special properties. Eauyceew Blood's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful an alchemical reagent. Eauyceew Blood was first discovered by the carpenter Ghadunra Tastri within the Meyû Chyàve Grasslands in time immemorial.
