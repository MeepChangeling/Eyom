The Jgusc is a diminutive herd-based carnivore. A Jgusc looks like nothing else, posessing a sharp head covered in thick bone-armor plates, two legs long and slender ending in claws, and a blunt tail. The creature's blood holds special properties. Jgusc Blood's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in the milling of various grains.
