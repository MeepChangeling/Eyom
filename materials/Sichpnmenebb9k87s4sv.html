Sichpnmene

Sichpnmene is a smooth light grey ore with splotches of burnt orange. Sichpnmene's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in pressing oils. Sichpnmene was first discovered by the priest Dhukwaehilda hu Ahhotep within the Qithêt-vihê Rainforest in time immemorial. Dhukwaehilda hu Ahhotep's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 8th century.
