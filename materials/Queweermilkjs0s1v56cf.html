Queweer Milk

Queweer milk is a thick light grey milk with a burnt orange sheen which smells like cave rock and tastes like lime. . Queweer Milk's properties are mostly magical in nature, but do make use of its natural makeup and is uniquly useful in goldsmithing. Queweer Milk was first discovered by the teacher Zlîb Trgêrí within the Dêqë-mênêye Heathland durring the early 11th century.
