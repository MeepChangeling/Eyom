Urchft Oil

Urchft oil is a syrupy red oil with a dark purple sheen which smells like ooze and tastes like fresh dirt.. Urchft Oil's properties are mostly magical in nature, but do make use of its natural makeup and is uniquly useful in the construction of watercraft. Urchft Oil was first discovered by the knight Byocthî within the Kosaite Holt in time immemorial. Byocthî's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the late 1st century.
