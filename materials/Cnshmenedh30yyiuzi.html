Cnshmene

Cnshmene is a oily burgundy ore. Cnshmene's properties are mostly magical in nature, but do make use of its natural makeup and is uniquly useful in the creation of paper and paper-like materials. Cnshmene was first discovered by the shepherd Ermpfē Cúnvēsh 'Elodie Elite' Mboidī Vúoīy Cúńó̄s nis Paweraa within the Shḡ Leyvyu Prairie durring the early 11th century.
