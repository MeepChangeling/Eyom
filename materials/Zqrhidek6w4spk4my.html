Zqr Hide

The Zqr is a colossal herd-based omnivore. A Zqr looks like nothing else, posessing a flat-faced head covered in thick bone-armor plates, four legs long and slender ending in cloven hooves, and a short tail. The creature's hide holds special properties. Zqr Hide's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in carpentry.
