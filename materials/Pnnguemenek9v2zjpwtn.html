Pnnguemene

Pnnguemene is a powdery prefectly matte predominalty rose igneous rock with orange spots and small glints of burgundy. Pnnguemene's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful for making dye. Pnnguemene was first discovered by the military leader Glêtesho Garê within the Îshê-qëtê Meadows durring the late 16th century in winter of the year 1593.
