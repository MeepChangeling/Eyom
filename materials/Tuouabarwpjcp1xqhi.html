Tuouabar

Tuouabar is a oily cream ore with splotches of light grey, and flecks of dark blue Tuouabar's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in brewing alcoholic beverages. Tuouabar was first discovered by the carpenter Sēbō Ha̋s 'Anabelle Thymessi' Ha̋chvēsh Méchī̄nḱ Ca̋ch Dhando within the E̜styǟgiqüe Bu Meadows durring the early 10th century.
