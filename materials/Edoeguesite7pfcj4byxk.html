Edoeguesite

Edoeguesite is a powdery glossey predominalty purple metamorphic rock with rose flecks and small glints of burgundy. Edoeguesite's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in creating perfumes. Edoeguesite was first discovered by the explorer Ardhel Shuzoreth within the Wulk-mirv Basin durring the early 10th century.
