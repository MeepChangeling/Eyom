Dgnabar

Dgnabar is a rough white ore with splotches of yellow, and flecks of brown Dgnabar's properties are entirly mundane and is uniquly useful in the creation of rope. Dgnabar was first discovered by the weaver Ya̋chvóń Mbóchī 'Coco Ophelia' Ya̋sh Vú̄s Vú̄boīy Dhando within the Letî-bîrîfî Forest durring the early 11th century.
