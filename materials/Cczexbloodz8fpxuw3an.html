Cczex Blood

The Cczex is a tiny herd-based carnivore. A Cczex looks like nothing else, posessing a sharp head covered in thick bone-armor plates, two legs ending in broad paws, and a round backside lacking a tail. The creature's blood holds special properties. Cczex Blood's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in pressing oils. Cczex Blood was first discovered by the trader Gasafri Warai within the Dachyû Chyudû Forest durring the late 11th century.
