Tieir Hide

The Tieir is a small packs-based omnivore. A Tieir looks like nothing else, posessing a stone-like head covered in thick bone-armor plates, six legs long and slender ending in claws, and a long feline-like tail. The creature's hide holds special properties. Tieir Hide's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the process of bookbinding. Tieir Hide was first discovered by the trader Grothnah vyim Roxlendrusg within the Löt-vak Expance in time immemorial. Grothnah vyim Roxlendrusg's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 5th century.
