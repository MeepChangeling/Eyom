Nnmm Blood

The Nnmm is a tiny solitary omnivore. A Nnmm looks like nothing else, posessing a dog-like head covered in thick bone-armor plates, four legs long and slender ending in broad paws, and a round backside lacking a tail. The creature's blood holds special properties. Nnmm Blood's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the milling of various grains. Nnmm Blood was first discovered by the farmer Emerthelil Powys within the Kuz Sæm Steppe durring the early 10th century.
