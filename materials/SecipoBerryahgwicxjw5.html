Secipo Berry is a shrub simmilar to Forsythia and Flannel Bush. Secipo Berry's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the construction of armor. Secipo Berry was first discovered by the soldier Colleen Griffith within the Rockmoss Woodlands durring the early 16th century in summer of the year 1530.
