Kveau Blood

The Kveau is a small packs-based omnivore. A Kveau looks like nothing else, posessing a flat-faced head with two large horns, eight legs bent ending in broad paws, and a long reptilian-like. The creature's blood holds special properties. Kveau Blood's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the construction of weapons. Kveau Blood was first discovered by the priest Thu Kirby Ferguson within the Yê-magê Woodlands in time immemorial. Thu Kirby Ferguson's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 1st century.
