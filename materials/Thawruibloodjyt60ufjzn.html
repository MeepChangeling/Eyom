Thawrui Blood

The Thawrui is a huge herd-based carnivore. A Thawrui looks like nothing else, posessing a stone-like head adorned with a crest-like ridge, four legs muscular ending in broad paws, and a rabbit-like tail. The creature's blood holds special properties. Thawrui Blood's properties are mostly magical in nature, but do make use of its natural makeup and is uniquly useful in stone cutting and masonry. Thawrui Blood was first discovered by the traveler Mlêlê Tibe within the Flibip Woodlands durring the early 12th century.
