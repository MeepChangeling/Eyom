Plfsci Hide

The Plfsci is a tiny herd-based carnivore. A Plfsci looks like nothing else, posessing a flat-faced head with two large horns, four legs ending in talons, and a rabbit-like tail. The creature's hide holds special properties. Plfsci Hide's properties are mostly magical in nature, but do make use of its natural makeup and is uniquly useful in the process of bookbinding. Plfsci Hide was first discovered by the weaver Shtin Garthêpîv within the Rivercaster Heath in time immemorial. Shtin Garthêpîv's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 5th century.
