Dgeffo Blood

The Dgeffo is a small herd-based herbavore. A Dgeffo looks like nothing else, posessing a flat-faced head covered in thick bone-armor plates, eight legs short ending in claws, and a rabbit-like tail. The creature's blood holds special properties. Dgeffo Blood's properties are entirly mundane and is uniquly useful in the milling of various grains. Dgeffo Blood was first discovered by the teacher Sēkī Nīb 'Moon Dubarry' Ma̋chī Dā̌ Ga̋chvēb Eenzin within the Bachibà Danû Holt durring the late 17th century in late winter of the year 1671.
