Tegubb Hide

The Tegubb is a medium herd-based herbavore. A Tegubb looks like nothing else, posessing a stone-like head adorned with a crest-like ridge, six legs short ending in cloven hooves, and a long feline-like tail. The creature's hide holds special properties. Tegubb Hide's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful for energy production. Tegubb Hide was first discovered by the teacher Fyèdsepí grw Yanhamu within the Readpool Expance in time immemorial. Fyèdsepí grw Yanhamu's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 5th century.
