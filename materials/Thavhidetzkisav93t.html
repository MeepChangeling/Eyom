Thav Hide

The Thav is a tiny herd-based herbavore. A Thav looks like nothing else, posessing a broad head covered in thick bone-armor plates, six legs ending in talons, and a long feline-like tail. The creature's hide holds special properties. Thav Hide's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the creation of rope. Thav Hide was first discovered by the traveler Crezzink Zlkêpî within the Iē̼iiēi̊m Woods in time immemorial. Crezzink Zlkêpî's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the late 2nd century.
