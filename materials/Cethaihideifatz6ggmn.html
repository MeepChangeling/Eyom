Cethai Hide

The Cethai is a huge herd-based herbavore. They are most easily described as a melding of a Chinese Crested Dog, a Spider-Tailed Horned Viper, and a Star-nosed mole. The dosscile's hide holds special properties. Cethai Hide's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful to the arcane arts.
