Ggch

Ggch is a gloopy dark brown fluid which glows black and smells very mellow and tastes like the sea.. Ggch's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the process of bookbinding. Ggch was first discovered by the teacher Mbónvóch Mb̪f̄nvé 'Coco Guild' Ermē Së̌ Sēbō hu Sobeknakht Shegarêv within the Zíhiē̼y̼ej̼ù Prairie in time immemorial. Mbónvóch Mb̪f̄nvé 'Coco Guild' Ermē Së̌ Sēbō hu Sobeknakht Shegarêv's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 5th century.
