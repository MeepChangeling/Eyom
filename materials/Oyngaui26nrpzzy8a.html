Oyngaui

Oyngaui is a ripply dark red wood with a dark grain. Oyngaui's properties are entirly magical in nature and is uniquly useful in the milling of various grains. Oyngaui was first discovered by the miner Ga̋chī Séchīy 'Pumpkin Ella' Mpfēbónḱ Ca̋chīrmb Vúń̄ within the Jîhe-yiyèna Holt in time immemorial. Ga̋chī Séchīy 'Pumpkin Ella' Mpfēbónḱ Ca̋chīrmb Vúń̄'s discover was believed to have occured some time durring the late 6th century.
