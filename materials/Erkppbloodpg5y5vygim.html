Erkpp Blood

The Erkpp is a small herd-based herbavore. A Erkpp looks like nothing else, posessing a blunt head with a single horn, six legs long and slender ending in cloven hooves, and a short tail. The creature's blood holds special properties. Erkpp Blood's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in brewing alcoholic beverages. Erkpp Blood was first discovered by the mage Ty-27n 'Jenny' Zlêyêpí within the Sebsíhr Holt durring the late 17th century in early fall of the year 1659.
