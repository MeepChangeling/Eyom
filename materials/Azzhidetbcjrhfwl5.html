The Azz is a giant herd-based herbavore. A Azz looks like nothing else, posessing a flat-faced head with a single horn, six legs powerful ending in paws, and a long feline-like tail. The creature's hide holds special properties. Azz Hide's properties are entirly magical in nature and is uniquly useful in goldsmithing. Azz Hide was first discovered by the scholar Sarcotoma Polson within the Ho-damê Shrublands in time immemorial. Sarcotoma Polson's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 8th century.
