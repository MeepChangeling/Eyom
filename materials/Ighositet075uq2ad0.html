Ighosite is a lumpy prefectly matte predominalty light grey metamorphic rock with dark brown ribbons and small glints of dark blue. Ighosite's properties are a blend of its material nature and magical makeup and is uniquly useful in pressing oils. Ighosite was first discovered by the carpenter Brukzarn Chenvriu within the Bloodmoss Forest in time immemorial. Brukzarn Chenvriu's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the early 3rd century.
