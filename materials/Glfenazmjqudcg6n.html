Glfena is a rough dark purple ore with splotches of peach. Glfena's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the milling of various grains. Glfena was first discovered by the farmer Brilêj Nrëd Khêd within the Khō Kot Woodlands durring the early 20th century in late summer of the year 1908.
