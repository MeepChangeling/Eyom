Uoyguairck

Uoyguairck is a ripply light grey wood with a dark grain. Uoyguairck's properties are mostly mundane but augmented with natural magic and is uniquly useful in the milling of various grains. Uoyguairck was first discovered by the guard Tsîthê Rutherford within the Lêfîn-kêqë Woodlands in time immemorial. Tsîthê Rutherford's discover was believed to have occured some time durring the late 5th century.
